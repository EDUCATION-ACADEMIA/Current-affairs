Which state Announces Outstanding Power Bill Waiver Scheme?

(A) ** Madhya Pradesh

(B) Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -OTS 2022 has started on 01-jun-2022 and the last date of ots is 30-jun-2022.

Concept note-2: -Even if any consumer does not pay the bill, his supply can be cut only after serving him a notice and after giving a period of 15 days to make the payment. Thus a non – paying consumer can use electricity for about 2 months.

Concept note-3: -If you fail to pay the amount within due date, your service connection is liable for disconnection. However if not disconnected, you can pay through online along with belated surcharges. In case if the service is disconnected, kindly approach the section office for getting reconnected.

Concept note-4: -There are certain rules that come into play. You can avail electricity subsidy when: Your consumption of electricity is within 200 units during the billing period. You can get a subsidy of Rs 800 for consuming 201 to 400 units in a month.
