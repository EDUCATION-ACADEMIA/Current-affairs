From which date Indian Railways UTS app to book unreserved tickets online?

(A) ** November 1

(B) December 3rd

(C) February 2nd

(D) March 5th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The UTS app promotes paperless economy. This mobile ticketing app enables booking unreserved paperless journey ticket, issue/renew season ticket and platform ticket. This application is available for download on google play store and windows play store for free.

Concept note-2: -You can book travel tickets with the UTS app easily for train travel. The app is easy to use and convenient for each passenger. With its ticket, you can travel on any Indian train, which includes general coaches.

Concept note-3: -“Now, passengers planning a journey at the last-minute can buy tickets from unreserved ticket system (UTS) counters and travel in the unreserved coaches. In SECR, unreserved ticket facility has been restored with immediate effect in 16 trains, ” said SECR officials.

Concept note-4: -UTS TICKET BOOKING DISTANCE 2022) that Ministry of Railways have decided to extend the facility to book unreserved tickets on Unreserved Ticketing system (UTS) mobile application from a distance of upto 20 km for non-suburban sections. Meanwhile, in suburban areas, the distance has been enhanced to 5 km.
