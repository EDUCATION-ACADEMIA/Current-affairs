According to the World Bank, businesses and individuals pay more than _________ in bribes every year.

(A) $75 billion

(B) $2 trillion

(C) $50 billion

(D) ** $1 trillion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to the United Nations, every year, an estimated US$1 trillion is paid in bribes and US$2.6 trillion is stolen through corruption.

Concept note-2: -Finding Fraud Event Replay According to the United Nations every year an estimated US$1 trillion is paid in bribes and US$2.6 trillion stolen through corruption. Together, this sum represents five (5) percent of annual global GDP.

Concept note-3: -The most popular and simplest definition of corruption is that it is the abuse of public. power for private benefit. This is the definition used by the World Bank."

Concept note-4: -Corruption, bribery, theft and tax evasion cost some US $1.26 trillion for developing countries per year.
