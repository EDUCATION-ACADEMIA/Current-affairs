When World Bicycle Day is observed as?

(A) ** June 3

(B) May 26

(C) March 6

(D) April 26

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first bicycle day was celebrated on June 3, 2018. Professor Leszek Sibilski, a Polish social scientist working in the United States, led a grassroots campaign with his sociology class to promote a UN Resolution for World Bicycle Day.

Concept note-2: -Since its establishment in 2018, World Bicycle Day has been marked annually on 3 June by advocates in many countries.

Concept note-3: -The United Nations officially designates June 3 as World Bicycle Day.
