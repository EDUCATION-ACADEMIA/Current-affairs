Which day is celebrated as 15 May recently?

(A) ** International Day Of Families

(B) International Day for Women

(C) World Heart Day

(D) World Ocean Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1993, the General Assembly decided in a resolution (A/RES/47/237) that 15 May of every year should be observed as The International Day of Families.

Concept note-2: -15 May – International Day of Families International Day of Families is observed every year on 15 May.

Concept note-3: -When is International Day of Families in 2022? International Day of Families in 2022 is on the Sunday, 15th of May (5/15/2022). International Day of Families is on the 135th day of 2022. There are 230 days left in the year.

Concept note-4: -International Family Day – May 15, 2023. U.S. Founded by the United Nations (UN) in 1994, the International Day of Families is observed every May 15 to honor the importance of families. Families-both traditional and non-traditional-are the foundation of society.

Concept note-5: -About. The International Day of Light will be held on May 16th every year, the anniversary of the first successful operation of the laser in 1960 by physicist and engineer, Theodore Maiman.
