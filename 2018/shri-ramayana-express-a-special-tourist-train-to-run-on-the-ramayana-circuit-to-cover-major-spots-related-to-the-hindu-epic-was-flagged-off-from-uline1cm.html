Shri Ramayana Express, a special tourist train to run on the Ramayana circuit to cover major spots related to the Hindu epic was flagged off from _________

(A) ** Safdarjung Railway Station

(B) Char Bagh Railway Station

(C) Chandan Nagar Railway Station

(D) Lakhimpur Railway Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From Janakpur, the train will cross into India to reach Sitamarhi in Bihar, from where it would go to Varanasi, where people will visit various temples in the city and will go to Shringverpur, the place where Ram, Sita, and Laxman crossed Ganga, and Chitrakoot by road.

Concept note-2: -Tour Price: Rs 62370/-per person on Double Occupancy. Catch the glimpses of the pilgrim tour covering the significant sacred places curated for you. The Shri Ramayana Yatra tour package departs from Delhi as you embark on your religious journey and tap on the opportunity for a soul-cleansing journey.

Concept note-3: -Bharat Gaurav Trains is Union Government initiative under “Dekho Apna Desh” to promote Domestic Tourism. Bharat Gaurav scheme also aims to leverage the core strengths of the professionals of the tourism sector to run theme-based trains to tap the vast tourist potential of India.

Concept note-4: -Speciality of Bharat Gaurav tourist train: The total cost for the first trip on Ramayana Circuit, is around Rs 62, 370 per person for 18 days and is all inclusive.
