Which state Hosts 4-Day International Dance Festival?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Gwalior

(C) Mumbai

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Khajuraho Dance Festival (IAST:Khajuraho Nritya Samaroh), organised by the Madhya Pradesh Kala Parishad, is a one-week festival of classical dances held annually beside the Khajuraho temples in Chhatarpur district of Madhya Pradesh state in central India.

Concept note-2: -The festival will be held at the Shore Temple in Mamallapuram from December 23rd, 2022, to January 12th, 2023.

Concept note-3: -The Konark Dance Festival (KDF) has been held in the poetic sands of Konark, since the last 32 years unabated, when it commenced in 1986.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Haldwani. A three-day International Honey Bee Festival kicked off, for the first time, at Haldwani, Uttarakhand on 18 December 2021. The aim of the event is to provide a global platform to hivers to display their products.
