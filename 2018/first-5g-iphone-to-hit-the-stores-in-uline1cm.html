First 5G iPhone to hit the stores in _________?

(A) ** 2020

(B) 2018

(C) 2025

(D) 2026

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -iPhone SE (2nd generation): It came as replacement for the iPhone 8 in 2020. It featured the same iPhone 8 design but upgraded internals of A13 chip same as the iPhone 11 series. In 2020, Apple launched the first iPhone with 5G connectivity.

Concept note-2: -Apple in October 2020 unveiled the iPhone 12, 12 mini, 12 Pro, and 12 Pro Max, the first iPhones to support 5G connectivity, and then followed them up with the iPhone 13 and iPhone 14 models.

Concept note-3: -Since the iPhone 11 series are limited to 4G, the current 5G compatible iPhones are only within the iPhone 12 series and the iPhone 13 series. To summarise, youâ€™ll never be able to use the 5G network on an iPhone 11, 11 Pro, or 11 Pro Max.

Concept note-4: -With the new iOS 16.2, the company said that 5G cellular support has been enabled for new iPhone 14, iPhone 13, iPhone SE and iPhone 12 lineups in India, which users can avail through Airtel and Jio 5G services.

Concept note-5: -iPhone 12 models or later work with the 5G mobile networks of certain network providers.
