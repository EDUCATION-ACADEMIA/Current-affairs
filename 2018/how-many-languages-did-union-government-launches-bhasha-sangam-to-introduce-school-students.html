How many languages did Union Government launches Bhasha Sangam to introduce school students?

(A) ** 22

(B) 25

(C) 32

(D) 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To introduce school students to all the 22 Indian Languages of Schedule VIII of the Constitution of India. To enhance linguistic tolerance and respect, and promote national integration.

Concept note-2: -Bhasha Certificate Selfie initiative aims to promote Bhasha Sangam mobile app, developed by Ministry of Education and MyGov India. Using the app, people can learn 100+ sentences of daily use in as many as 22 scheduled Indian languages.

Concept note-3: -As of today, the Indian constitution recognizes 22 major languages of India in what is known as “the 8th Schedule” of the Constitution.

Concept note-4: -Bhasha Sangam is an initiative of the Ministry of Education under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat, to teach basic sentences of everyday usage in 22 Indian languages.

Concept note-5: -Subhas Sarkar announced the launch of Bhasha Sangam, a mobile app that can teach more than 100+ sentences in 22 languages.
