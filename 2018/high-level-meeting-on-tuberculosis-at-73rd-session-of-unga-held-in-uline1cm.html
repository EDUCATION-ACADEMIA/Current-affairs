High-Level Meeting On Tuberculosis At 73rd Session Of UNGA Held In _________?

(A) ** New york

(B) Singapore

(C) Washington

(D) Venice

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On Sept 26, 2018, the first ever UN General Assembly (UNGA) High-Level Meeting on Tuberculosis (UNGA-HLM-TB), with more than 1000 participants from across the world, assembled in New York. A/RES/73/3. Political declaration of the high-level meeting of the General Assembly on the fight against tuberculosis. A/RES/73/3.

Concept note-2: -From 21 to 23 September 2022, the SDG Action Zone will take place during the High-level Week of the UNGA. It will champion the activists and allies accelerating progress towards the SDGs, through candid, honest and powerful conversations.

Concept note-3: -This year marked the 76th session of the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA 76), opening in New York on September 14, 2021.
