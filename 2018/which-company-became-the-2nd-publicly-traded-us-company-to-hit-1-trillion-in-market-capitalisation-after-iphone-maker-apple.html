Which company became the 2nd publicly traded US company to hit $1 trillion in market capitalisation after iPhone maker Apple?

(A) Walt Disney

(B) Microsoft

(C) Facebook

(D) ** Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple became the first publicly traded U.S. company to hit a $1 trillion market cap during intraday trading on Aug.

Concept note-2: -Apple became the world’s first $3 trillion company for a brief time during intraday trading on Jan. 3, 2022.
