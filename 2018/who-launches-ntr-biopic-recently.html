Who launches NTR biopic recently?

(A) ** Shri M Venkaiah Naidu

(B) Shri Dinesh Karthick

(C) Suresh Yadav

(D) Naresh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice President M Venkaiah Naidu launched the highly-anticipated NTR biopic, based on the life of late actor and former chief minister of Andhra Pradesh NT Rama Rao, on Thursday at Ramakrishna Studio in Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Box office. The film ended up as one of the biggest commercial failures in Telugu cinema and the biggest commercial failure in Nandamuri Balakrishna’s career. It was released with reduced expectations due to the commercial failure of its prequel NTR: Kathanayakudu.

Concept note-3: -NTR KATHANAYAKUDU (2019) NTR Kathanayakudu earned nearly Rs 15 crore during its full run. It is regarded as a commercial failure as its pre-release business was around Rs 54 crore.

Concept note-4: -Jr NTR will be following seen the tentpole RRR, where he will share the screen with Ram Charan, Alia Bhatt & Ajay Devgn. Directed by Baahubali filmmaker SS Rajamouli, the movie is a fictionalised account of Telugu tribe leaders Komaram Bheem (Jr NTR) and Alluri Sitarama Raju (Charan).

Concept note-5: -As per the new announcement, the shooting for the movie will begin next month and NTR30 is scheduled for release on April 5, 2024. The film is JT NTR’s collaboration with Koratala Siva. NTR30 was supposed to go on floors in June 2022 but it was delayed, reportedly due to the debacle of Siva’s directorial, Acharya.
