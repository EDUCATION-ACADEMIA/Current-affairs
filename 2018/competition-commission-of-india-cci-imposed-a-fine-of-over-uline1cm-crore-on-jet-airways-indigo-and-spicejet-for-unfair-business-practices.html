Competition Commission of India (CCI) imposed a fine of over _________ crore on Jet Airways, IndiGo, and SpiceJet, for unfair business practices.

(A) ** Rs. 54 crore

(B) Rs. 45 crore

(C) Rs. 62 crore

(D) Rs. 71 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CCI fines Jet Airways, IndiGo, SpiceJet for unfair business practices. New Delhi: The Competition Commission of India (CCI) on Wednesday imposed a total fine of more than Rs54 crore on Jet Airways, InterGlobe Aviation and SpiceJet for unfair business practices with respect to fixing fuel surcharge on cargo transport.

Concept note-2: -Non-compliance of CCI directions issued under Section 36(2)/(4) or non-adherence to the directions issued by a Director General (without sufficient cause for the same) is punishable with a fine of Rs. 1,00,000 for each day of default, subject to a maximum of Rs. 10 million.

Concept note-3: -In the case of anti-competitive agreements and abuse of dominance, the CCI may impose fines of up to 10 percent of the average turnover for the last three preceding financial years upon each of such persons or enterprises that are parties to such agreements or abuse.
