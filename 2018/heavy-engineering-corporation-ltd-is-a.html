Heavy Engineering Corporation Ltd. is a

(A) Non-government Organization

(B) Foreign government Organization

(C) Non-profit Organization

(D) ** Public Sector Unit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rs. 453.24 crore (100% Govt.) HECL a Public Sector Undertaking, under Ministry of Heavy Industry, Government of India, is a leading organization in India. HEC was incorporated on 31.12.

Concept note-2: -Heavy Engineering Corporation Ltd.

Concept note-3: -Average annual salary in Heavy Engineering Corporation is INR 7.9 lakhs . Salary estimates are based on 635 Heavy Engineering Corporation latest salaries received from various employees of Heavy Engineering Corporation.
