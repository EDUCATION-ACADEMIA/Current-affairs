Who is the chairman of Financial Stability and Development Council (FSDC)?

(A) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(B) Shri Naresh Iyer

(C) ** Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Ramesh Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Chairman of the Council is the Finance Minister and its members include the heads of financial sector Regulators (RBI, SEBI, PFRDA, IRDA & FMC) Finance Secretary and/or Secretary, Department of Economic Affairs, Secretary, Department of Financial Services, and Chief Economic Adviser.

Concept note-2: -It is headed by the Union Finance Minister. It monitors macro-prudential supervision of the economy.

Concept note-3: -A meeting of the Sub-Committee of the Financial Stability and Development Council (FSDC-SC) was held today at Reserve Bank of India, Mumbai. Shri Shaktikanta Das, Governor, Reserve Bank of India, chaired the meeting.

Concept note-4: -Chairperson: The Indian Union Finance Minister (Present-Nirmala Sitharaman).

Concept note-5: -The FSDC sub-committee headed by Reserve Bank Governor Shaktikanta Das on Thursday reviewed the economic situation in the backdrop of the COVID-19 pandemic and resolved to keep a close watch on the unfolding developments with a view to ensure financial stability.
