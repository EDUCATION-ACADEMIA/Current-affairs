Indian International Science Festival Begins In _________?

(A) ** Lucknow

(B) New Delhi

(C) Chennai

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhopal, Jan 20 (PTI) The 8th India International Science Festival-2022 (IISF) will be held from January 21 to 24 at the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) in Bhopal in Madhya Pradesh, an official said on Friday.

Concept note-2: -More than 8,000 delegates from across the country will participate in this science festival from January 21 to 24. The main theme of this festival being held in the Amrit kaal of independence is ‘Country moving towards Amrit kaal with science, technology and innovation’. Dr.

Concept note-3: -The eighth India International Science Festival is to be held in Bhopal. The Union Minister Jitendra Singh inaugurated the festival. According to the Government of India, the festival is a step in moving towards “AMRIT KAAL”.

Concept note-4: -The Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh Shivraj Singh Chauhan and Union Minister for Science & Technology, Dr. Jitendra Singh today jointly inaugurated “India International Science Festival, IISF-2022” in Bhopal.
