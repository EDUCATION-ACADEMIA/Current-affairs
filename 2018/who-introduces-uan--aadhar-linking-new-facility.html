Who Introduces UAN- Aadhar Linking New Facility?

(A) ** EPFO

(B) CBI

(C) SBI

(D) Dena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Employees’ Provident Fund Organisation (EPFO) has made provision for employees to link their Aadhaar with their Universal Account Number (UAN). A person can link Aadhaar with EPF account both online and offline. Let us have a look at how we can do it easily.

Concept note-2: -The UAN mandate was brought into being on October 1, 2014, by Prime Minister Narendra Modi to create a single umbrella under which all your PF accounts may be easily and conveniently accessed by you.

Concept note-3: -If the UAN is not linked with Aadhaar number, then even the withdrawals from EPF account be restricted.

Concept note-4: -The due date to link UAN with Aadhaar for employees was up to 31st December 2021.

Concept note-5: -Step 1: Go to https://iwu.epfindia.gov.in/eKYC/. Step 2: Next, under “EPFO Members, ” choose “Link with Aadhaar.” Step 3: To generate an OTP, enter your UAN and registered cellphone number. Step 4: Now, in the “Confirm OTP” box, enter the OTP obtained on your cell number.
