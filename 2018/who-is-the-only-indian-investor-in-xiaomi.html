Who is the only Indian Investor in Xiaomi?

(A) Lakshmi Mittal

(B) Mukesh Ambani

(C) ** Ratan Tata

(D) Gautam Adani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ratan Tata, chairman Emeritus of Tata Sons, has made an investment in Xiaomi. This is the first investment by any Indian in Xiaomi. Lei Jun, founder and CEO of Xiaomi, is happy to say: â€œMr. Tata is one of the most well-respected business leaders in the world.

Concept note-2: -Ratan Tata is the only Indian investor in Xiaomi. Ratan Tata, the Chairman Emeritus of Tata Sons, is the only Indian investor in Chinese smartphone maker Xiaomi, according to its IPO filings. Tata picked a 0.0024% stake (49, 583 shares) in 2015 through his Singapore-based company RNT Associates International.
