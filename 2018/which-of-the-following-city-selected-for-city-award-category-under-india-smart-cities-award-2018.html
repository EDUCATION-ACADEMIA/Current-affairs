Which of the following city selected for “City Award” category under “India Smart Cities Award 2018”?

(A) Indore

(B) ** Surat

(C) Kochi

(D) Udaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Surat Smart City Selected for ‘City Award’ for showing great momentum in Implementation of Projects Under ‘India Smart Cities Award’ 2018.

Concept note-2: -Under ‘India Smart Cities Award’ 2018, Surat Smart City has been selected for ‘City Award’ for showing great momentum in implementation of projects especially in the categories of urban environment, transport and mobility and sustainable integrated development.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Surat. Surat has been awarded for showcasing “great momentum” in the implementation of projects under the Smart Cities Mission, the Housing and Urban Affairs (HUA) Ministry.

Concept note-4: -How many smart cities are there in India? The Government has selected 100 smart cities as part of the Smart Cities Mission. These include cities from across the states, selected in the proportion of the urban population.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is 10. The no. of cities in U.P. which are covered under the ‘Smart City’ Scheme are 10.
