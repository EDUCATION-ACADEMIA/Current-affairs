(A) ** Thirty Second

(B) Forty Four

(C) Fifty two

(D) Sixty Five

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mela is organized by the Surajkund Mela Authority & Haryana Tourism in collaboration with Union Ministries of Tourism, Textiles, Culture and External Affairs. For the 36th Surajkund International Crafts Mela-2022, the state of has been chosen to be the Theme State.
