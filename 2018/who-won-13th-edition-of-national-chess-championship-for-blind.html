Who Won 13th Edition Of National Chess Championship For Blind?

(A) ** GM Marcin Tazbir

(B) Kishan Gangolli

(C) Darpan Irani

(D) Charudatta Jadhav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kishan Gangolli has won the 13 th edition of National Chess Championship for blind.

Concept note-2: -MPL 58th Senior National Chess Championship-2022 : Arjun Erigaisi crowned national champion – All India Chess Federation.

Concept note-3: -The Indian National Senior Chess Championship takes place February 25-March 3, 2022 in Kanpur, India.

Concept note-4: -The 44th Chess Olympiad (also known as the Indian Chess Olympiad), was organised by the Fédération Internationale des Échecs (FIDE) in Chennai, the state capital of Tamil Nadu, India from 28 July to 10 August 2022.

Concept note-5: -After Abhijeet Gupta’s five-hour search for a win ended in a draw against Sayantan Das, overnight leader Karthik Venkataraman became the National chess champion at the Indira Gandhi Stadium on Tuesday.
