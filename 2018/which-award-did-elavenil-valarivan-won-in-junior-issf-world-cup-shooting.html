Which award did Elavenil Valarivan won In Junior ISSF World Cup Shooting?

(A) Silver

(B) ** Gold

(C) Certificate

(D) Bronze

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -She won her first-ever medal in shooting at school in a team event which sparked her interest in the sport. Valarivan won her first gold in an ISSF Junior World Cup in Sydney with a score of 249.8 in the finals.

Concept note-2: -In Shooting, two-time Olympian Mairaj Ahmad Khan created history by becoming the first Indian shooter to win the gold medal in the men’s skeet event at the ISSF World Cup in Changwon, South Korea, yesterday. Mairaj Ahmad Khan recorded 37 hits out of a possible 40 in the final to win gold.

Concept note-3: -Indian shooters gave a stellar account of themselves at the I****SSF Rifle/Pistol World Championship 2022 in Cairo, Egypt and won 34 medals – 12 golds, nine silvers and 13 bronze – to finish second on the medals tally behind China.

Concept note-4: -The Indian men’s 25m rapid fire pistol team comprising Anish Bhanwala, Vijayveer Sidhu and Sameer won the silver medal on the last day of the ISSF World Cup Changwon 2022 in South Korea, on Wednesday. The medal helped India retain the top spot on the medals table with five gold, six silver and four bronze.

Concept note-5: -"India won a total of medals (6 gold, 3 silver, 1 bronze) in this World Cup Well Done Champs. Proud of You All, ” added SAI Media in another tweet. The Indian contingent won two more silvers on Day 4 on Monday.
