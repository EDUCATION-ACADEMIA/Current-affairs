Which city International Yoga Festival Held recently?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) ** Rishikesh

(D) Secunderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The eagerly awaited International Yoga Festival is organized at Parmarth Niketan Ashram in Rishikesh.

Concept note-2: -Changlang, June 21, 2022: The International Yoga Day 2022 was celebrated in Changlang district along with rest of the country. Since 2015, the International Yoga Day has been celebrating on June 21 every year.

Concept note-3: -Origin and History of International Yoga Festival Rishikesh This festival continued for many years from the same venue and at the same time. In 1999, Uttar Pradesh Tourism asked Parmarth Niketan ashram in Rishikesh to start hosting the International Yoga festival.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Saudi Arabia. Saudi Arabia’s first yoga festival took place on 29 January 2022 in the commercial center of Jeddah.

Concept note-5: -The theme for International Day of Yoga 2022 is “Yoga for humanity”.
