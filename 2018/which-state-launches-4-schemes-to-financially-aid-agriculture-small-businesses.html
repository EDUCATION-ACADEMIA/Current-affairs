Which state launches 4 schemes to financially aid agriculture, small businesses?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Maharashtra

(C) Bihar

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government has rolled out a number of new initiatives like Soil Health Card Scheme, Neem Coated Urea, Paramparagat Krishi Vikas Yojana (PKVY), Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchayee Yojana (PMKSY), National Agriculture Market (e-NAM), Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana (PMFBY) and Interest Subvention Scheme.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana (PMKSY) : PMKSY was approved by the Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs on 01.07.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Kisan Samman Nidhi (PM-KISAN), a flagship scheme of Government of India, is an outcome of the continued commitment of Prime Minister of India, Sri Narendra Modi to initiate policy actions and implement public programs for an inclusive and productive agricultural sector.

Concept note-4: -Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana – This is a scheme to take care of irrigation requirements of farming.
