Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated India’s first Multi-Modal Terminal on _________ river.

(A) ** Ganga

(B) Brahmaputra

(C) Godavari

(D) Krishna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VARANASI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Monday inaugurated India’s first multi-modal terminal on the Ganga river in his parliamentary constituency here and received the country’s first container cargo transported on inland waterways from Kolkata.

Concept note-2: -Varanasi Multi-Modal Terminal or Varanasi Port is an Inland river port situated in the city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The port is located on the Ganges river.

Concept note-3: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi flagged off the World’s Longest River Cruise-MV Ganga Vilas and inaugurated the Tent City at Varanasi via video conferencing today. He also inaugurated and laid the foundation stones for several other inland waterways projects worth more than Rs. 1000 crores during the event.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Sunday inaugurated Mopa International Airport in Goa, named after former chief minister and defence minister Manohar Parrikar. The airport’s foundation stone was laid by PM Modi in November 2016. This will be the second airport in Goa, the first one being located at Dabolim.

Concept note-5: -It was constituted under IWAI Act-1985 by the Parliament of India.
