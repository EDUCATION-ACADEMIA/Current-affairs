Under ‘India Smart Cities Award’ 2018, Joint winners in ‘Innovative Idea’ category were Bhopal and

(A) ** Ahmedabad

(B) Jabalpur

(C) Chennai

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Joint winners in this category are Bhopal for their Integrated Command and Control Centre (ICCC) and Ahmedabad for Safe and Secure Ahmedabad (SASA) Project. The Project awards are being given to most innovative and successful projects in seven categories that have been completed by April 1, 2018.

Concept note-2: -Indore (Madhya Pradesh) and Surat (Gujarat) won the award jointly for their overall development.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Surat. Surat has been awarded for showcasing “great momentum” in the implementation of projects under the Smart Cities Mission, the Housing and Urban Affairs (HUA) Ministry.

Concept note-4: -2nd Round winners – Selection of 13 Smart Cities.
