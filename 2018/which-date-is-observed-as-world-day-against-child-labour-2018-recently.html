Which date is observed as World Day against Child Labour 2018 recently?

(A) ** June 12

(B) May 18

(C) March 16

(D) January 20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It has been 19 years since 2002, and the World Day Against Child Labour is observed on 12 June every year.

Concept note-2: -Each year on 12 June, the World Day brings together governments, employers and workers organizations, civil society, as well as millions of people from around the world to highlight the plight of child labourers and what can be done to help them.

Concept note-3: -Labor Day Theme 2022 The theme for Labor Day, 2022, is Universal Social Protection to End Child Labor. It urges the protection of children from forced child labor and attracts investments into the social safety nets.

Concept note-4: -World Day Against Child Labour 2022: The theme for this year is “Universal Social Protection to End Child Labour”. It calls for investments into social safety nets and programs to protect children from being forced into child labour. Every year, June 12 is marked as the World Day Against Child Labour.
