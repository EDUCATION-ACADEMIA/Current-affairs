Which day is celebrated as 15th January 2018 recently?

(A) ** Army Day

(B) National Teacher’s Day

(C) National Youth Day

(D) National Teacher’s Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Army Day is celebrated on 15 January every year in India, in recognition of Lieutenant General Kodandera M.

Concept note-2: -India celebrates Army Day on January 15 every year to commemorate the achievements of the first Indian Commander in Chief of the Indian Army-General (later Field Marshal) K.M. Cariappa.

Concept note-3: -Makar Sankranti falls on January 14 or January 15 in certain years. Makar Sankranti is the very first major festival to be celebrated in India and is one of the universally celebrated Hindu festivals.

Concept note-4: -In 2022, the Indian Army’s theme for the event was “In Stride with the Future”. It was seen as an acknowledgement of the “increasingly critical role played by niche and disruptive technologies in modern warfare”.
