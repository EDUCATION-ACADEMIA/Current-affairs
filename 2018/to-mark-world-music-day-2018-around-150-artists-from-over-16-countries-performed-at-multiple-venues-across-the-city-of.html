To mark “World Music Day 2018”, around 150 artists from over 16 countries performed at multiple venues across the city of

(A) Mumbai

(B) ** Udaipur

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) Bhubaneswar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To mark the day, around 150 artists from over 16 countries, including France, USA, Nepal, Spain, Italy, Thailand and India, will be performing at multiple venues across the city of Udaipur this year.

Concept note-2: -More than 120 countries celebrate World Music Day. Celebrations see a variety of free public concerts and events being held across over 700 cities, in the streets, parks, museums and other public spaces. Here, musicians gather to play their instruments and perform for large crowds.

Concept note-3: -Ever since, the festival has become an international phenomenon, celebrated on the same day in more than 700 cities in 120 countries, including India, Germany, Italy, Greece, Russia, Australia, Peru, Brazil, Ecuador, Mexico, Canada, the United States, the UK, and Japan.

Concept note-4: -World Music Day is celebrated on 21st June every year. The day originated in France in 1982, and since then, it has been celebrated all over the world with music enthusiasm. World Music Day is about coming together and rejoicing in the joy of making or listening to music.

Concept note-5: -World Music Day is celebrated on June 21. The day cherishes the spirit of music, the vibrancy of vocals and the melody of instruments. The day was first celebrated as Fete de la Musique in France in the year 1982.
