Which small finance bank launches personal loan facility for salaried individuals?

(A) ** Ujjivan Small Finance Bank

(B) Equitas Small Finance Bank

(C) Small Indus Development Finance Bank

(D) Jana Small Finance Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jana Small Finance offers Personal Loan for Salaried Employees at attractive rates of interest.

Concept note-2: -However, most banks and NBFCs limit a personal loan at Rs. 35 lakh to an individual. Lenders evaluate the monthly income of loan applicants and the potential growth in it before approving a loan. In most cases, individuals are eligible for a personal loan amount of up to 30 times their monthly income.

Concept note-3: -Capital Small Finance Bank offers personal loans at competitive rates of interest. You can avail a loan of up to Rs. 5 lakh (on the basis of your eligibility) to meet your personal expenses. Rate of Interest-15.85% p.a.
