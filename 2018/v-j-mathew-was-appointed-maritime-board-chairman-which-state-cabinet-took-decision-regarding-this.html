V J Mathew was appointed Maritime Board Chairman. Which state cabinet took decision regarding this?

(A) Telangana

(B) ** Kerala

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -V J Mathew had been appointed as the new Chairman of Kerala Maritime Board (KMB). The decision in this regard was taken by the Kerala cabinet. Mathew was the legal adviser to Vizhinjam International Seaport Limited and co-president of the Indian Maritime Association (IMA).

Concept note-2: -The present Kerala Maritime Board, constituted in 2018, is still in place but “we are open to any government decision”, V. J. Mathew, chairman, has said.

Concept note-3: -Avantika Singh Aulakh, Gujarat Maritime Board: Profile and Biography-Bloomberg Markets.

Concept note-4: -Pursuant to enactment of Gujarat Maritime Board Act, 1981, Gujarat Maritime Board (GMB) was established for administration, control and management of all minor ports in the State of Gujarat.
