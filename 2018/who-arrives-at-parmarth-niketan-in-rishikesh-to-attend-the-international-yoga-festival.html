Who arrives at Parmarth Niketan in Rishikesh to attend the International Yoga Festival?

(A) ** Shri M Venkaiah Naidu

(B) Shri Dinesh Karthik

(C) Shri Arun jaitley

(D) Shri Ramesh menon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Changlang, June 21, 2022: The International Yoga Day 2022 was celebrated in Changlang district along with rest of the country. Since 2015, the International Yoga Day has been celebrating on June 21 every year.

Concept note-2: -International Yoga Festival Rishikesh 2023.

Concept note-3: -’ Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led a mass Yoga demonstration at Chandigarh, where more than 30,000 people participated. The performance of Yoga by 150 Divyang Jan along with the Prime Minister was one of the highlights. Around the world, 192 United Nations Member States observed IDY- 2016.

Concept note-4: -Recognizing its universal appeal, on 11 December 2014, the United Nations proclaimed 21 June as the International Day of Yoga by resolution 69/131.
