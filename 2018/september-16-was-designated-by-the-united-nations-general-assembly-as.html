September 16 was designated by the United Nations General Assembly as

(A) World Cancer Day

(B) World Water Day

(C) ** World Ozone Day

(D) World Refugee Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In 1994, the United Nations General Assembly proclaimed 16 September the International Day for the Preservation of the Ozone Layer, commemorating the date of the signing, in 1987, of the Montreal Protocol on Substances that Deplete the Ozone Layer (resolution 49/114).

Concept note-2: -Theme for International Day for the Preservation of the Ozone Layer 2022. This year the theme announced by the UN Environment Programme for International Day for the Preservation of the Ozone Layer 2022 is ‘Global Cooperation Protecting Life on Earth.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is 16th September. 16th September 1994 is designated as World Ozone Day by the U.N. General Assembly.

Concept note-4: -Every year, International Ozone Day is recognised with a specific theme. This year, the theme of World Ozone Day 2022 is ‘Global Cooperation to Protect Life on Earth’.
