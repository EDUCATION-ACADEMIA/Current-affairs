What is the capital of Costa Rica?

(A) Managua

(B) Belmopan

(C) ** San Jose

(D) Bogota

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -San José, capital and largest city of Costa Rica. Situated in the broad, fertile Valle Central 3, 800 feet (1, 160 metres) above sea level, it was called Villa Nueva when it was settled in 1736. San José developed slowly as a tobacco centre in the Spanish colonial era.

Concept note-2: -San José, capital and largest city of Costa Rica. Situated in the broad, fertile Valle Central 3, 800 feet (1, 160 metres) above sea level, it was called Villa Nueva when it was settled in 1736. San José developed slowly as a tobacco centre in the Spanish colonial era.

Concept note-3: -Costa Rica is known for its incredible national parks, where tourists can enjoy some thrilling activities like river rafting, canyoning, cave tubing, and zip lining. It’s also one of the best places for animal lovers to discover some interesting wildlife like macaws, sea turtles, and adorable sloths.
