What initiative has the UP Police introduced to restrict the fake news on social media?

(A) Cyber crime special squad

(B) Techno hackers

(C) ** Digital army

(D) Black hat hackers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Blue. The Offical Flag of Uttar Pradesh Police has two colors, Red and Blue. Uttar Pradesh has the distinction of being the largest single Police force not only in the country but of the entire world.

Concept note-2: -The Uttar Pradesh Police is headed by the Director General of Police (DGP) for the state of Uttar Pradesh, who is the highest-ranking (DG) IPS officer of the state cadre.

Concept note-3: -Ashutosh Pandey - Inspector General of Police - Government of U.P.

Concept note-4: -The Director General of UP Police commands a force of approx. 2.5 Lacs aprox. personnel spread over 75 districts, 33 armed Battalions and other specialised wings/ branches relating to Intelligence, Investigation, Anti-corruption, Technical, Training, Forensic Science etc.
