Which social media has admitted it shared its users’ data with 52 technology companies?

(A) WhatsApp

(B) Instagram

(C) ** Facebook

(D) Pinterest

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We do not share information that personally identifies you (personally identifiable information is information like name or email address that can by itself be used to contact you or identifies who you are) with advertising, measurement or analytics partners unless you give us permission.

Concept note-2: -According to Facebook’s Data Policy, the company may share information about you with third-party apps, advertisers, other partners (like vendors and service providers), and any companies that it owns.

Concept note-3: -Messages you send and receive, including their content, subject to applicable law. Metadata about content and messages, subject to applicable law.
