How many stop centres Union Government to set up additional for women safety?

(A) 10

(B) 25

(C) 40

(D) ** 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Buoyed by the success of the One Stop Centres (OSC) to support women affected by violence, the Centre is planning to open 300 more such centres.

Concept note-2: -234 One Stop Centers (OSCs) currently functional in the country; 1, 90, 527 women affected by violence offered support.

Concept note-3: -One-Stop Centre scheme is a sub-scheme of the National Mission for Empowerment of Women which also includes the Indira Gandhi Matritva Sahayog Yojana. The scheme is funded by the Nirbhaya fund. Under this scheme, complete financial assistance will be provided by the Centre to the States and the Union territories.

Concept note-4: -The Government of India is implementing One Stop Centre (OSC) scheme with effect from 1st April, 2015.

Concept note-5: -Schemes that were introduced under the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme includes Ladli scheme, Kanyashree Prakalpa Yojana, Sukanya Samridhi Yojana, Balika Samridhi Yojana, Ladli Laxmi Yojana, Dhanalakshmi scheme and many more.
