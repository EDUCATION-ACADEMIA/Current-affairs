How many percentage of Indian Railway catering services attract a uniform GST in trains and stations?

(A) ** 5 %

(B) 15 %

(C) 25 %

(D) 35 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -5% Uniform rate of GST to apply in all railway catering services in trains or on stations.

Concept note-2: -5% GST to apply on food on train, platform; newspaper supply to be exempt: AAAR.

Concept note-3: -For Lunch and dinner during the travel, passengers will require to pay ₹294 instead of ₹244 if the passengers did not opt for the meal during the booking of the ticket.

Concept note-4: -Thus, Service Tax is required to be paid 12.36% (current tax rate) on maintenance services rendered by Railways.
