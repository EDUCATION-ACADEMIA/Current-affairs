What is the overall investment in the second edition of ‘Make in Odisha Conclave 2018 (MIO 2018)?

(A) ** Rs.4, 19, 574 crore

(B) Rs.7, 89, 574 crore

(C) Rs.6, 87, 574 crore

(D) Rs.10, 35, 574 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The state, which had received over 120 investment intents valued at Rs 2 lakh crore in its first edition of the MIO in 2016 and later Rs 4 lakh crore in the second edition of the MIO in 2018, is hopeful to get a good response this time from the business summit.

Concept note-2: -The Make in Odisha Conclave was launched in 2016 to showcase the manufacturing prowess of the state and its investment opportunities. A leading mineral producer in the country, Odisha, has traditionally been a hub for mining and metal units.

Concept note-3: -The thematic session of Make in Odisha to be held at the plenary hall will be conducted by Startup Odisha with the theme ‘Start-ups’ Paradigm Shift and The Road Ahead’. Shri. Naveen Patnaik, Hon. Chief Minister, Government of Odisha is expected to grace the event.

Concept note-4: -The Make in Odisha Conclave 2022 (3rd edition), a biennial global investors’ meet and the flagship investor summit of the Government of Odisha, took place in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, from November 30 to December 4, 2022.

Concept note-5: -The Make In Odisha Conclave’22 is the flagship investor summit of the Government of Odisha. After the resounding success of the event in 2016 and 2018, the state has decided to organize the third edition of the marquee event at Bhubaneswar from November 30 to December 04, 2022.
