Which place Union Cabinet approves establishment of new AIIMS recently?

(A) Jaipur

(B) Raipur

(C) Jodhpur

(D) ** Bilaspur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Cabinet chaired by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has approved establishment of a new All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) at Darbhanga, Bihar.

Concept note-2: -On September 15, 2020, the Union cabinet had granted approval to Bihar’s second AIIMS at Darbhanga, after the one in Patna, to be built within four years at a cost of ₹1, 264 crore on 200 acres of land belonging to the Darbhanga Medical College and Hospital (DMCH).

Concept note-3: -There are now 19 AIIMS in India that are operational as of January 2022. By the end of 2025, five additional AIIMS are anticipated to open in India. According to the National Institute of Ranking Framework, the AIIMS in New Delhi is the best of all the AIIMS institutions in India (NIRF).

Concept note-4: -AIIMS Bathinda started operation in 2019. AIIMS Bilaspur, AIIMS Guwahati and AIIMS Vijaypur became operational in 2020.

Concept note-5: -The Centre had granted cabinet approval for the construction of the 750-bed AIIMS in Darbhanga on September 15, 2020. In November 2021, the Bihar cabinet cleared the transfer of 200 acres of land for the project out of the total 227 acres that comes under DMCH.
