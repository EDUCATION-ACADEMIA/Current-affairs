In which year was Swachhata Pakhwada scheme launched?

(A) 2015

(B) ** 2016

(C) 2017

(D) 2018

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The initiative was started in April 2016 with the objective of bringing a fortnight of intense focus on the issues and practices of Swachhata by engaging various ministries/departments of the Government of India. This is one of the key programmes under the Swachh Bharat Mission.

Concept note-2: -Swachhata Pakhwada launched in Cochin Port Trust-Read basic current affairs facts here!-GKToday.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Tourism (MoT), with the aim of maintaining cleanliness at tourist destinations across the country has observed awareness campaign on cleanliness by undertaking a fortnightly awareness drive called “Swachhta Pakhwada”.

Concept note-4: -Swachhta Pakhwada Action Plan & Activities for Schools 2023: Observance of Swachhta Pakhwada during the period from 1st to 15th September. Regarding observance of Swachhta Pakhwada during the period from 1st to 15th September (Teachers Day on 5th September).
