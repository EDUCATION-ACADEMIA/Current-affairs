Which day is observed on 26th November?

(A) ** Indian Constitution Day

(B) World Environment Day

(C) World Wildlife Day

(D) No Smoking Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Constitution Day also known as ‘Samvidhan Divas’, is celebrated in our country on 26th November every year to commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of India. On 26th November 1949, the Constituent Assembly of India adopted the Constitution of India, which came into effect from 26th January 1950.

Concept note-2: -Every year on November 26th, India observes Constitution Day, also known as Law Day or Samwidhan Divas. On November 26, 1949, India’s Constituent Assembly adopted the Indian Constitution. This took effect on January 26, 1950.

Concept note-3: -Mumbai terrorist attacks of 2008, multiple terrorist attacks that occurred on November 26–29, 2008, in Mumbai (Bombay), Maharashtra, India.

Concept note-4: -Republic Day is the day when India marks and celebrates the date on which the Constitution of India came into effect on 26 January 1950. This replaced the Government of India Act 1935 as the governing document of India, thus turning the nation into a republic separate from British Raj.

Concept note-5: -Constitution Day marks the day when India got independence whereas Republic day marks the day when India demanded for independence. No worries!
