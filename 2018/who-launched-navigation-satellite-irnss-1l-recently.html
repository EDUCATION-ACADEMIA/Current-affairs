Who Launched Navigation Satellite IRNSS-1L recently?

(A) ** ISRO

(B) NASA

(C) Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency

(D) Space Studies Institute (California)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ISRO has successfully launched navigation satellite IRNSS-1l into the orbit from which launch vehicle ?

Concept note-2: -ISRO successfully demonstrated new technology with Inflatable Aerodynamic Decelerator (IAD) – a game changer with multiple applications for future missions. Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-3: -NVS-01 is a replacement for IRNSS-1G satellite and will launch on GSLV in 2023.

Concept note-4: -PSLV-C41 Successfully Launches IRNSS-1I Navigation Satellite.

Concept note-5: -IRNSS was renamed Navigation Indian Constellation (NAVIC). 3. IRNSS will be an independent and autonomous regional navigation system aiming a service area of about 1500 kilometers around India.
