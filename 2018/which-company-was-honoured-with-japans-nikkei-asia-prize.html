Which company was Honoured With Japan’s Nikkei Asia Prize?

(A) ** Sulabh Company

(B) Ashok Leyland

(C) Reliance Industries

(D) Larsen And Toubro Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Bindeshwar Pathak, the noted social reformer and founder of Sulabh International, has been honoured with Japan’s prestigious Nikkei Asia Prize for his significant work in tackling poor hygiene and discrimination.

Concept note-2: -It was Kristin Kagetsu’s passion to work for a sustainable environment and underprivileged women that brought the American of Chinese and Japanese descent to India.

Concept note-3: -The Nikkei Asia Prize (Japanese: ) is an award which recognizes the achievements of people and organizations that have improved the lives of people throughout Asia. The awards were created and presented by Nikkei Inc, one of the largest media corporations in Japan.

Concept note-4: -Ownership. Nikkei Asia is owned by Nikkei, Inc., the same Japan-based holding company that also owns the London-based Financial Times.

Concept note-5: -Key Takeaways. The Nikkei is Japan’s leading stock index comprised of the country’s top 225 blue-chip stocks. The Nikkei is a price-weighted index, which means the index is an average of the share prices of all the companies listed.
