Which of the following Indian Navy ship is participating in RIMPAC 2018?

(A) ** INS Sahyadri

(B) INS Astradharani

(C) INS Satpura

(D) INS Tihayu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INDIAN NAVY’S INS SATPURA AND P8I PARTICIPATE IN THE RIMPAC HARBOUR PHASE. Indian Navy’s indigenous Frigate INS Satpura and P8I LRMRASW aircraft are at Pearl Harbour in Hawaii taking part in one of the largest multilateral Naval Exercises, the Rim of the Pacific exercise, also known as RIMPAC.

Concept note-2: -Indian Navy’s indigenous Frigate INS Satpura and P8I LRMRASW aircraft participated in the 2022 Rim of the Pacific exercise-Rimpac.

Concept note-3: -Nine nations participated in the RIMPAC Amphibious Assault (Australia, Chile, Indonesia, Malaysia, Mexico, Republic of Korea, Sri Lanka, Tonga and U.S.).

Concept note-4: -RIMPAC 2022: Indian Navy participates in the world’s largest maritime exercise.

Concept note-5: -Twenty-six nations, 38 surface ships, three submarines, nine national land forces, more than 30 unmanned systems, approximately 170 aircraft and over 25, 000 personnel participated in the 28th edition of the biennial Rim of the Pacific (RIMPAC).
