Who signed MoU With Tamil Nadu E-Governance Agency?

(A) ** IIT-Madras

(B) IIT - Bombay

(C) IIT-Roorkee

(D) IIT- Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thiru. T. Mano Thangaraj, Hon’ble Minister for Information Technology & Digital Services, Government of Tamil Nadu, on behalf of the ICT Academy of Tamil Nadu, exchanged the #MoU with Tally Solutions at the Bengaluru edition of the ICT Academy Bridge 2022, held on 19 Oct 2022.

Concept note-2: -National e-Governance Plan (NeGP) Tamil Nadu has in fact progressively aligned its entire e-Governance plan in consonance with NeGP, ever since its formulation in May 2006 by the Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Tamil Nadu Government launched an “e-governance policy – 2017” that would enable public, government and commercial establishments get all it’s government services through digital mode.
