Arunachal Pradesh got its 23rd district with the formal inauguration of _________ district.

(A) ** Shi Yomi

(B) Pakke-Kesang

(C) Lepa Rada

(D) Anjaw

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shi Yomi formally became 23rd district of Arunachal Pradesh after it was inaugurated by Chief Minister Pema Khandu in presence of Union Minister of State for Home Kiren Rijiju. It was created by bifurcating West Siang district with its headquarters at Tato.

Concept note-2: -Pakke Kessang 25th district of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Kamle district, also Khamle, is one of the 25 administrative districts (26th including Itanagar capital complex) of Arunachal Pradesh in northeastern India. The district headquarters are at Raga.

Concept note-4: -The 24th district of Arunachal Pradesh–Lepa Rada–was formally inaugurated on Monday, a day after Shi Yomi became a new district, officials said. Lepa Rada is carved out of the Lower Siang district, comprising four administrative circles-Tirbin, Dari, Sago and Basar.
