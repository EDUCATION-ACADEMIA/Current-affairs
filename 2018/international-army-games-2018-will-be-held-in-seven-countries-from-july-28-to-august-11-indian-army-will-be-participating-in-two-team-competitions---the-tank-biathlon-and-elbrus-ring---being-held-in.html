International Army Games 2018 will be held in seven countries from July 28 to August 11. Indian Army will be participating in two team competitions - the Tank Biathlon and Elbrus Ring - being held in

(A) ** Russia

(B) China

(C) Iran

(D) Kazakhstan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2018 International Army Games-Was held from July 28 to August 11, 2018. The competitions were held in Armenia and Iran for the first time.

Concept note-2: -This year, the Chinese military sent nine teams abroad to participate in competitions in Russia, Iran, Kazakhstan, Algeria, and Uzbekistan. The International Army Games, initiated by the Russian Defense Ministry, have served as an international platform for militaries to exchange experience and strengthen cooperation.

Concept note-3: -Team China Friday ranked the highest in the survival trail event of the International Army Games 2018 “Seaborne Assault.” The PLA led the International Army Games 2018 after it opened on Monday, ranking first in two of the three contests held in Korla, Xinjiang Uygur autonomous region.

Concept note-4: -The Russian Ministry of Defense is the host nation for these international military sports competitions. 275 teams representing 37 nations will compete in this year’s games from August 13–17 at various venues across the world.
