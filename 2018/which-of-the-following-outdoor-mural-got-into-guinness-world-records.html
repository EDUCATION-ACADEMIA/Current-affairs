Which of the following outdoor mural got into Guinness World Records?

(A) ** Dreary grain silo

(B) Mother Earth

(C) Abandoned and desolate poor woman

(D) Nature

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Him Chatterjee, the art consultant to the Pragati Maidan project, said, “In the Guinness World Records, the biggest recorded outdoor public artwork till date is in Incheon, South Korea, a 23, 688 sq metre painting on a grain silo.

Concept note-2: -The largest painting in the world entered the Guinness World Records this week when Qatar’s university football field was draped with an enormous art canvas. 9, 652 square metres of the football field was covered by the painting called “The story of the ball, ” by Iranian artist Emad Salehi.

Concept note-3: -The project is titled ‘Miradas Sobre Bilbao’, and was made using acrylic paint and finished with acrylic varnish. The mural runs alongside the main road leading into the city of Bilbao, in the community of Enécuri.

Concept note-4: -This beautiful mural on the north Wichita grain elevator is not only the largest mural in the world painted by a single artist, but also holds the Guinness World Record.
