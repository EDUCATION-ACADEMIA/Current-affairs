Who has been awarded as Best Actor in Indian International Film Awards (IIFA) 2018?

(A) ** Irrfan Khan

(B) Akshay Kumar

(C) Ranbir Kapoor

(D) Adil Hussain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IIFA Awards 2018: Irrfan Khan won the Best Actor prize for his role in Hindi Medium.

Concept note-2: -The IIFA Award for Best Actor recognizes leading male actor who has delivered an outstanding performance in a leading role. The recipient is chosen by viewers and the winner is announced at the ceremony. With 4 awards each, Hrithik Roshan and Shahrukh Khan share the record for most wins in this category.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The ‘Best Actress Award’ was given to the late actress ‘Sridevi’ for her film ‘Mom’. The 65th National Film Awards was given by the President Ramnath Kovind in 2018.

Concept note-4: -Vicky Kaushal and Kriti Sanon with their IIFA awards.
