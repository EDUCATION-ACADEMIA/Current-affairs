Which bank To Be Renamed as IDFC First Bank?

(A) ** IDFC Bank

(B) SBI Bank

(C) Yes Bank

(D) Dena Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IDFC FIRST Bank was founded by the merger of Erstwhile IDFC Bank and Erstwhile Capital First on December 18, 2018.

Concept note-2: -The merger will give IDFC First bank access to nearly ₹4, 000 crore of capital, a bulk of which came from IDFC Ltd selling its mutual fund business to Bandhan Bank. IDFC Ltd shareholders are likely to benefit from the clean-up of the bank’s balance sheet and the groundwork laid for the retail loans growth take-off.

Concept note-3: -IDFC and IDFC First Bank Merger details: In a regulatory filing on December 30, 2021, IDFC First Bank had given approval for the merger of IDFC and IDFC First Bank.
