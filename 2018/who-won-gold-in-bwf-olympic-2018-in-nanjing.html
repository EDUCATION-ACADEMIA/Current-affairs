Who won gold in BWF Olympic 2018 in Nanjing?

(A) P.V.Sindhu

(B) Tai Tzu-ying

(C) Saina Nehwal

(D) ** Carolina Marin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Kento Momota . Kento Momota became the first Japanese man to win the Badminton World Championships . BWF World Championships 2018 is held at the Nanjing Youth Olympic Sports Park from July 30 to August 5. It is one of the top tournaments in the world.

Concept note-2: -Akane Yamaguchi Yamaguchi currently holds the top spot in the world rankings for female badminton singles players. She comes from Japan and is 25 years old. Yamaguchi has already achieved quite a lot in the sport and will only be looking to add to her championships in the coming years.

Concept note-3: -Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara. Nozomi Okuhara is from Japan. It was the BWF World Championships of 2019.

Concept note-4: -Denmark’s Viktor Axelsen won his second badminton world title on Sunday, beating Thailand’s Kunlavut Vitidsarn 21-5, 21-16 in the final in Tokyo.
