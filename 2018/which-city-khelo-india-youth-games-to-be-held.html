Which city Khelo India Youth Games to be held?

(A) ** Pune

(B) Chennai

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Secunderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Khelo India Youth Games 2022 is all set to kick off on January 30, 2023, with a Grand Opening ceremony at the TT Nagar Stadium in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Prime Minister Narendra Modi is scheduled to be present during the opening ceremony, ahead of the tournament.

Concept note-2: -Khelo India Youth Games 2023: Where to watch live streaming, all the venues and full schedule. KIYG 2023 is being held at 11 venues across eight cities in Madhya Pradesh. Over 5, 000 athletes will compete in 27 sports for 1, 936 medals. Get full schedule.

Concept note-3: -Watch live! Khelo India Winter Games 2023, the premier grassroots-level multi-sport competition of India, will take place in Gulmarg, the popular hill station in Jammu and Kashmir from February 10 to 14.

Concept note-4: -The fifth edition of the Khelo India Youth Games is to start in January end. The State Government of Madhya Pradesh is to host the event.
