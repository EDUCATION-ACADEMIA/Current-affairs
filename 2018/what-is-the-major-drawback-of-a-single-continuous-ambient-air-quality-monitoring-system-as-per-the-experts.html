What is the major drawback of a single Continuous Ambient Air Quality Monitoring System as per the experts?

(A) ** It presents less accuracy of the air pollution rate

(B) It is of high cost

(C) The system fails to calculate the pollution level

(D) The system is not available in India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Air Pollution Monitoring Equipment As consequences, these instruments are typically with high cost, high power consumption, large volume, and heavy weight.

Concept note-2: -It is also known as an automatic monitoring station. It will monitor the air quality of a city on a real time basis. Its ratings will be a benchmark of air quality measurement. It will continuously measure air pollutants throughout the year in a continuous manner.

Concept note-3: -Disadvantages: As this devices are interconnected via internet there are possibilities that they can get hacked or monitored by malicious users or can be tracked by other systems as well. So the security of the recorded data can be an issue using this type of devices.
