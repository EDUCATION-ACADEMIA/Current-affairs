Concept note-1: -Gorakhpur (Hindi: , Urdu: ) is a city of 675, 000 people (2011) in the eastern part of the state of Uttar Pradesh, India. Near the Nepalese border, it is a way station on a popular overland route from India to Nepal. It is the city of great shaayar (" "). Gorakhnath mandir.
