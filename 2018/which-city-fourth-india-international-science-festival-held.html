Which city Fourth India International Science Festival held?

(A) ** Lucknow

(B) New Delhi

(C) Bihar

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first and second IISF were held in New Delhi, the third in Chennai, the fourth one in Lucknow, fifth in Kolkata, sixth through virtual mode and the last one IISF was held in Goa which witnessed a footfall from over 5 lakh people worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Bhopal, Jan 20 (PTI) The 8th India International Science Festival-2022 (IISF) will be held from January 21 to 24 at the Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology (MANIT) in Bhopal in Madhya Pradesh, an official said on Friday.

Concept note-3: -Celebrating Science In The City Of Lakes: India International Science Festival To Be Held In Bhopal In January. India International Science Festival 2022 will be held in Bhopal in January 2023.

Concept note-4: -The eighth India International Science Festival is to be held in Bhopal. The Union Minister Jitendra Singh inaugurated the festival. According to the Government of India, the festival is a step in moving towards “AMRIT KAAL”.

Concept note-5: -Chief Minister of Madhya Pradesh Shivraj Singh Chauhan and Union Minister for Science & Technology, Dr Jitendra Singh on Saturday jointly inaugurated India International Science Festival, IISF-2022 in Bhopal with the theme, “Marching towards Amrit Kaal with Science Technology and Innovation”.
