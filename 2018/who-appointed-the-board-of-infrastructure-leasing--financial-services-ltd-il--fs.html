Who appointed the board of Infrastructure Leasing & Financial Services Ltd. (IL & FS)

(A) ** National Company Law Tribunal (NCLT)

(B) National and Corporate Law Tribunal (NCLT)

(C) National Green Tribunal (NGT)

(D) National Company Tribunal (NCT)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India, through Ministry of Corporate Affairs, took management control of IL&FS and appointed a new Board in October 2018 in an effort to control defaults and restore confidence and financial stability in Capital Markets.
