How much did the TATA Chemicals Limited recently approve for its Mithapur facility?

(A) Rs.2, 000 crore

(B) Rs.2, 200 crore

(C) ** Rs.2, 400 crore

(D) Rs.2, 500 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The commissioning of a new 200 crore (US$40million) salt plant in February has brought the annual capacity to 800, 000 tonnes. This is a step towards the goal of increasing production to one million tonnes in the next 3–4 years. Mithapur has the capacity to manufacture more than 900, 000 tonnes of soda ash each year.

Concept note-2: -The average Tata Chemicals salary ranges from approximately ₹5, 66, 636 per year for a Graduate Engineer Trainee to ₹7, 29, 508 per year for an Assistant Manager. Tata Chemicals employees rate the overall compensation and benefits package 3.8/5 stars.

Concept note-3: -Power requirement will be increased from 62.97 MW to 92.60 MW proposed to be met from own Captive Co-generation Power Plant. Existing unit has 9 DG sets of 8318 KVA capacity which will be used as standby during power failure.

Concept note-4: -Pursuant to the Scheme, each shareholder of TCL will get 1.14 new equity shares of TGBL for every 1 equity share held in TCL i.e. a shareholder holding 100 shares in TCL will receive 114 shares in TGBL.

Concept note-5: -We are a leading global chemical player with over 4, 138 KT of soda ash and 236 KT of sodium bicarbonate capacities.
