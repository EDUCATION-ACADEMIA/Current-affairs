Under the ’Mukhya Mantri Yuva Nestham’ scheme, an allowance of _________ rupees per month will be provided to unemployed youth.

(A) Rs 2000

(B) Rs 1500

(C) Rs 500

(D) ** Rs 1000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The program’s goal is to provide unemployed youth with enough money to support themselves or their families. An allowance of ₹1, 000 is given to you each month.

Concept note-2: -The Pradhanmantri Rojgar Yojana or Pradhan Mantri Rozgar Yojana as it is known has been initiated by the Government of India with a view towards ensuring self-employment opportunities that are sustainable, for 10 lakh educated and unemployed women and urban youth in the country.

Concept note-3: -Berojgari Bhatta Yojana 2023-The Prime Minister of our state, Narendra Modi, had started a plan to benefit the educated unemployed youth of the country, whose name is Berojgari Bhatta Yojana 2023. The purpose of the central government behind starting this plan is unemployment allowance to the school unemployed youth.

Concept note-4: -The Pradhan Mantri Rozgar Yojana or PMRY is a Central Government initiative that aims to provide self-employment opportunities to educated youths who are unemployed. The scheme, launched in 1993, offers an unemployed loan to youth and women.

Concept note-5: -The Prime Minister Rozgar Yojana or the PMRY was launched in 1993. It aims at providing self-employment opportunities to educated and unemployed youth in rural areas and small towns.
