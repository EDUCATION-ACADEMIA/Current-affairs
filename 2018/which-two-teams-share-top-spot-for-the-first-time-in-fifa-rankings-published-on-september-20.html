Which two teams share top spot for the first time in FIFA rankings, published on September 20?

(A) ** Portugal and Belgium

(B) Belgium and France

(C) France and Brazil

(D) Brazil and Spain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If we talk about the world ranking under FIFA Football Ranking 2022, then Brazil is at the top and after Brazil, Belgium, Argentina, France, England, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Portugal and Denmark are making their own rankings.

Concept note-2: -The FIFA Men’s World Ranking is a ranking system for men’s national teams in association football, led by Brazil as of 2022.

Concept note-3: -While Brazil and Argentina, the World Cup 2022 winners, are first and second, respectively. The Blue Tigers have been ranked 106th for quite sometime now.

Concept note-4: -This is Full List of FIFA World Men’s Ranking as of 22 December 2022. As per the FIFA World Rankings 2022, Brazil stands in the first position with 1, 841 points. Belgium and Argentina rank as the second and third in FIFA rankings with 1, 817 and 1, 774 points respectively.
