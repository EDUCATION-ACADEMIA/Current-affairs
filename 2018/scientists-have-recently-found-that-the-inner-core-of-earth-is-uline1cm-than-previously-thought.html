Scientists have recently found that the inner core of earth is _________ than previously thought.

(A) ** softer

(B) harder

(C) hotter

(D) colder

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Scientists have discovered evidence that the Earth’s inner core oscillates, contradicting previously held beliefs that it consistently rotates at a faster rate than the planet’s surface.

Concept note-2: -Scientists have long thought that our planet’s inner core, located 3, 200 miles beneath the Earth’s surface, was solid – in contrast to the liquid metal region surrounding it.

Concept note-3: -USC scientists have found evidence that the Earth’s inner core oscillates, contradicting previously accepted models that suggested it consistently rotates at a faster rate than the planet’s surface.

Concept note-4: -The inner core is the most metal place on Earth-even more so than the outer core. Both are made mostly of iron, along with a smattering of nickel. But the iron is thought to also contain traces of lighter elements like oxygen, carbon, and silicon.

Concept note-5: -For decades, scientists have studied the behavior of Earth’s inner core, a solid iron ball at the heart of our planet that spans about 1, 500 miles wide-nearly 70 percent of the moon’s size.
