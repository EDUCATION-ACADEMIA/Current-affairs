What is the currency of Seychelles?

(A) Dollar

(B) Seychellois euro

(C) ** Seychellois rupee

(D) Seychellois pound

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Goods and services are paid for in Seychelles rupees (SCR). It isn’t common to buy goods or pay for services in any other currency, although some guesthouses and hotels may accept euros or US dollars.

Concept note-2: -Goods and services are paid for in Seychelles rupees (SCR). It isn’t common to buy goods or pay for services in any other currency, although some guesthouses and hotels may accept euros or US dollars. You should check before travelling.

Concept note-3: -Currency to Carry to Seychelles accept payment only in EUR or USD from foreigners and not SCR! However, transactions in EUR could be costlier so using SCR where possible would be better.

Concept note-4: -Understanding the Seychelles Rupee Seychelles established rupees as the national currency in 1914 while it was still a British colony. The country also accepted currency from its neighbor, the Mauritian rupee. The British Board of Commissioners of Currency continued to issue banknotes in 1918, 1928, and 1951.

Concept note-5: -It is the law in the Seychelles that tourists pay for hotels–including meals and drinks had there–with cash in the form of major foreign currency or credit card. Many activities and attractions, car rental companies and car services also fall under this rule.
