Which union minister aim to ease doing business in India?

(A) ** Shri Arun Jaitley

(B) Shri Prakash Javadekar

(C) Shri Ramesh Gupta

(D) Shri Vivek Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that for enhancing of ease of doing business, more than 39, 000 compliances have been reduced. In order to boost ease of doing business, over 34, 000 legal provisions have been decriminalized said FM Sitharaman in her budget speech.

Concept note-2: -Ministry Of Corporate Affairs-Ease Of Doing Business.

Concept note-3: -The Ease of Doing Business (EoDB) index is a ranking system established by the World Bank Group.

Concept note-4: -Definition: Ease of doing business is an index published by the World Bank. It is an aggregate figure that includes different parameters which define the ease of doing business in a country.

Concept note-5: -According to the World Bank, India ranked 63rd in 2022 in ease of doing business across the world among 190 countries, improving its rank from 142 in 2014.
