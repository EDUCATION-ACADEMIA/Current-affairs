HAL becomes first public sector enterprise to transact on TReDS. TReDS is related to _________

(A) Insurance Companies

(B) ** MSME

(C) Public Sector Banks

(D) Foreign Companies

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -State-owned aerospace and defence manufacturer Hindustan Aeronautics (HAL) became the first public sector enterprise (PSU) to make transaction on RXIL TReDS platform.

Concept note-2: -With this approval, C2FO becomes only the 4th platform to get the license. Currently, there are three operating TReDS platforms in India, namely Invoicemart, M1xchange and RXIL.

Concept note-3: -TReDS is an electronic platform for facilitating the financing / discounting of trade receivables of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) through multiple financiers. These receivables can be due from corporates and other buyers, including Government Departments and Public Sector Undertakings (PSUs).

Concept note-4: -Who can participate as a buyer in TReDS? Ans. Corporates, Government Departments, PSUs and any other entity can participate as buyers in TReDS.

Concept note-5: -The Reserve Bank introduced TReDS in 2014 to facilitate financials of trade receivables of the MSMEs. The MSME sector has been hardpressed for funds, with many units facing an acute resource crunch and shutdown during Covid.
