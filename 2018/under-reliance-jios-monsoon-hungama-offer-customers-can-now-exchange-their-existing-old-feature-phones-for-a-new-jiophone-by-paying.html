Under Reliance Jio’s ’Monsoon Hungama’ offer, customers can now exchange their existing old feature phones for a new JioPhone by paying

(A) Rupee.851

(B) Rupee.401

(C) Rupee.701

(D) ** Rupee.501

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Well, the new JioPhone Next offer allows you to exchange your phone, whether it’s working or not, for a value of Rs 2,000.

Concept note-2: -Any repairs/replacement of parts of JioPhone not covered by warranty may be carried out by the Company on payment of charges by the Recipient as stipulated by the Company from time to time.

Concept note-3: -For that visit your nearest Jio Store. Take your phone and charger along with you. They will tell you if you are eligible for the refund or not. Only the registered person i.e. on whose name the SIM is registered can return it as they will be asking for your ID card when you visit.

Concept note-4: -Yes, your existing Jio SIM will work in a JioPhone2.
