Which Indian Film opens the 2018 London Indian Film Festival?

(A) Sanju

(B) Race 3

(C) ** Love Sonia

(D) Raazi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Anurag Kashyap’s “Dobaaraa” has been set as the opening night film of the London Indian Film Festival.

Concept note-2: -First IFFI The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi, Calcutta and Trivandrum.

Concept note-3: -The 2nd edition of the ASEAN-India Workshop on Blue Economy, jointly hosted with the Socialist Republic of Viet Nam, was held on 18 July 2018 in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Mega Star Chiranjeevi receives Indian Film Personality of the Year Award for 2022 at the closing ceremony of 53rd edition of IFFI.

Concept note-5: -The 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India, IFFI, will be hosted in Goa from November 20 to 28, 2022.
