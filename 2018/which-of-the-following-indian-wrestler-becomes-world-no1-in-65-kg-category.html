Which of the following Indian Wrestler becomes World No.1 in 65 kg category?

(A) ** Bajrang Punia

(B) Vinesh Phogat

(C) Sandeep Tomar

(D) Sakshi Malik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Option 4. Indian wrestler, Bajrang Punia became the ‘World number 1’ ranking in the 65 kg category.

Concept note-2: -1. Sushil Kumar. Sushil Kumar is arguably the greatest Indian wrestler of the modern era and also the most controversial one. He won India’s first Olympic medal since 1952 at the 2008 Beijing Olympics.

Concept note-3: -Indian wrestler Bajrang Punia won a bronze medal in the men’s 65kg on the final day of the World Wrestling Championships 2022 in Belgrade, Serbia on Sunday.
