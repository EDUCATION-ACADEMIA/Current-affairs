Which team won FIFA 2018 World Cup Finals?

(A) England

(B) Croatia

(C) ** France

(D) Belgium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the final, France played Croatia on 15 July at the Luzhniki Stadium in Moscow. France won the match 4–2, claiming their second World Cup and becoming the fourth consecutive title won by a European team, after Italy in 2006, Spain in 2010, and Germany in 2014.

Concept note-2: -Brazil extended its lead over second-placed Belgium in the latest FIFA rankings released on 27 October 2023 to take the field as the top-ranked team at this year’s World Cup in Qatar, starting 20 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -It is scheduled to take place in Russia from 14 June to 15 July, 2018. Q. Which country hosted the 17th FIFA U-17 World Cup? Q.

Concept note-4: -A third place play-off was also played between the two losing teams of the semi-finals. France won the final 4–2 against Croatia for their second title, while Belgium won the third place play-off 2–0 over England.
