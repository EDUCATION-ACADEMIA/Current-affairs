How many crores did National Mission for Clean Ganga (NMCG) approved projects for Cleaning the River and its banks?

(A) ** 150 crores

(B) 125 crores

(C) 130 crores

(D) 145 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The executive committee of the National Mission for Clean Ganga approved several projects for Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, Jharkhand and West Bengal, collectively amounting to nearly Rs 2700 crore, aimed at rejuvenating Ganga river.

Concept note-2: -Prior to the launch of Namami Gange programme in 2014, only 28 projects existed with a sewage treatment capacity of 462.85 MLD. Under Namami Gange Programme, so far, a total of 364 projects have been sanctioned at an estimated cost of Rs 30, 853 Cr and 183 projects have been completed and made operational.

So far, 313 projects worth Rs. 25, 000 crore have been sanctioned under the mission.
