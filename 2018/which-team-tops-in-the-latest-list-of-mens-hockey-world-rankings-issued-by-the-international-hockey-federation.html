Which team tops in the latest list of Men’s hockey world rankings issued by the International Hockey Federation?

(A) India

(B) ** Australia

(C) Argentina

(D) Belgium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Men’s World Ranking is done by the International Hockey Federation, aka FIH or Fédération Internationale de Hockey, as it was founded in French around 99 years ago. With Germany winning the World Cup 2023, it tops the list. Check other countries as well.

Concept note-2: -In the men’s section, the team of Australia (2842.258) topped. The team of Belgium (2764.735) got the second position and Netherlands (2465.707) got the third position by defeating India (2366.990). Germany (2308.156) ranked fifth in this ranking.

Concept note-3: -The Boston Bruins top the NHL Power Rankings as of Feb. 8.
