Which of following reserve did the Union Ministry of Environment, Forests and Climate Change declare as an eco-sensitive zone (ESZ)?

(A) ** Mudumalai Tiger Reserve

(B) Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve

(C) Anamalai Tiger Reserve

(D) Nilgiris Biosphere Reserve

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On January 28, the MoEFCC published a draft notification to declare an Eco-Sensitive Zone (ESZ) of 118.59 sq km around the Wayanad Wildlife Sanctuary in Kerala.

Concept note-2: -The new ESZ guidelines, declared by the ministry on February 9, 2011 would also ensure that these areas act as “shock absorbers” to the protected areas by regulating and managing the activities around such areas. The guidelines were updated on the ministry website today.

Concept note-3: -Ecologically sensitive zones (ESZ) are intended to protect ‘protected areas’ – national parks and wildlife sanctuaries – by transitioning from an area of lower protection to an area of higher protection. ESZs are effectively insulating layers where humans and nature can be at peace with each other.
