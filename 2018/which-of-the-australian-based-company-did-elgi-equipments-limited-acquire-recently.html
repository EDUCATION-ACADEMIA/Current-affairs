(A) Harvey Norman

(B) Macquarie Group

(C) Orbital Corporation

(D) ** Pulford Air and Gas

Concept note-1: -Air-compressor maker Elgi Equipments today announced that it has acquired a 100 per cent shareholding of Sydney-headquartered F R Pulford and Son Pty Ltd for 11.04 million (Australian Dollars), about Rs 56 crore, in an “all cash deal.”
