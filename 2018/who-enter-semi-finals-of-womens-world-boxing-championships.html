Who enter semi-finals of Women’s World Boxing Championships?

(A) ** Mary Kom

(B) Ri Jong Hyang

(C) Hasibe Erkog

(D) Sarita Devi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On her way to becoming the undisputed champ, Claressa Shields unleashes a flurry of punches in Round 1. Shields, ESPN’s current women’s pound-for-pound No. 1 boxer, has carried the sport in the U.S. and continues her dominance as she enters the prime of her career.

Concept note-2: -Shiva Thapa and Mohammad Hussamuddin won gold medals at the Men’s National Boxing Championships 2022 in Hisar on Friday. Record six-time Asian medal-winning Indian boxer Shiva Thapa easily defeated the 2021 world youth championships bronze medallist Ankit Narwal by unanimous decision in the 63.5kg final.

Concept note-3: -Zareen became the fifth Indian women’s boxer to win a gold medal at the World Championships, joining Mary Kom, Laishram Sarita Devi, Jenny R. L., and Lekha K. C. She was only the second Indian boxer to win a World Championships Gold Medal abroad (outside India) after M.C.
