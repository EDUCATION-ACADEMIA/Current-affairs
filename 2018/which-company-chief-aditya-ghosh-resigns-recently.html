Which company Chief Aditya Ghosh Resigns recently?

(A) ** Indigo

(B) Ashok leyland

(C) L and T limited

(D) Maruthi Suzuki

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. Indigo President Aditya Ghosh Resigns, Rahul Bhatia Named Interim CEO.

Concept note-2: -Far worse was the incident of IndiGo ground staff manhandling a passenger on the tarmac. The airline sacked the whistle-blower on the pretext that he had instigated the assault and refused to tender an apology. Ghosh relented later, but the damage was already done.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rahul Bhatia. InterGlobe Aviation has announced the appointment of its Co-founder Rahul Bhatia as the company’s Managing Director with immediate effect. The company is the parent of the country’s largest airline IndiGo.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Pieter Elbers is an airline business leader with over 30 years’ experience in aviation. Prior to joining IndiGo in early September 2022, he was the President & Chief Executive Officer of KLM. He started his career at KLM in 1992 as Manager Aircraft loading.
