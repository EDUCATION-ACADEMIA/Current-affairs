In which State does the Pulicat lake located?

(A) ** Andhra Pradesh & Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala & Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala & Andhra Pradesh

(D) Andhra Pradesh & Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Tamil Nadu, the city Pulicat is located in the district of Tiruvallur . It is situated almost 60kms north of Chennai city particularly on the seaward side of Sri Harikota island blockade that segregates Pulicat Lake from the Bay of Bengal.

Concept note-2: -Pulicat Lake, saltwater lagoon on the Coromandel Coast of Andhra Pradesh state, southern India. It extends from the extreme southeastern portion of Andhra Pradesh into the adjacent portion of Tamil Nadu state and has a length of about 30 miles (50 km) and a width of 3 to 10 miles (5 to 16 km).

Concept note-3: -The Pulicat lake is a backwater lagoon which is located off the shore of the Tamil Nadu and Andhra Pradesh states which jointly host the Pulicat Lake. Pulicat as a town is totally in Tamil Nadu whereas the state of Andhra Pradesh houses 95% of the area of the Pulicat Lake.

Concept note-4: -Pulicat Lake, Nellore District, Andhra Pradesh | Film Facilitation Office. Pulicat Lake: Located 110 km from Nellore & 10 km from Sulurpeta, the Pulicat Lake is the second largest brackish water lake in the country. It is famous for its wonderful flora and fauna.

Concept note-5: -The lagoon and its river basins are located both in Andhra Pradesh and Tamil Nadu states.
