With which firm did Reliance Jio Infocomm Ltd. partner to launch VoLTE-based inbound international roaming?

(A) US based-AT & T Inc.

(B) China based-NTT-Docomo

(C) ** Japan-based KDDI Corporation

(D) German based-Deutsche Telekom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Jio has launched the VOLTE-based services with Japan’s KDDI Corporation . Reliance Jio on Tuesday launched VoLTE-based inbound international roaming services between India and Japan, based on services.

Concept note-2: -International Roaming(IR) is a service which allows you to use your Jio SIM while travelling to another country outside India. With your Jio SIM you can travel 100+ countries and enjoy seamless voice, data and SMS service experience. Was this helpful for you?

Concept note-3: -Reliance Jio was the first company in India to introduce the Voice over LTE technology, followed by Airtel and Vodafone Idea. Reliance Jio has become India’s first telecom operator to offer the 4G Voice over LTE (VoLTE) services on international roaming.

Concept note-4: -The first network to provide 4G LTE and VoLTE services was Jio. On September 5, 2016, Jio made these services available to all consumers and unveiled its LYF smartphone line. Instead of emphasizing voice and SMS, Reliance Jio Infocom Ltd focused on high-speed internet.

Concept note-5: -In the current scenario, Reliance Jio is the only cellular provider that is offering the 4G VoLTE services in India.
