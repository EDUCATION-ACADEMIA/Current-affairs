(A) ** Exam Warriors

(B) A journey - Poem

(C) Abode of love

(D) Aahwan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Paradoxical Prime Minister: Narendra Modi And His India is a 2018 nonfiction book written by the senior leader of the Indian National Congress, Shashi Tharoor, about the Prime Minister of India, Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -The main of the book is to help with exam stress and anxiety. Prime Minister Narendra Modi has updated his book, “Exam Warriors” ahead of the Pariksha Pe Charcha 2023.
