Which month will be observed as National Nutrition Month by Union Ministry of Women and Child Development?

(A) ** September

(B) August

(C) March

(D) July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -September 2022 is being observed as “National Nutrition Month” on the directives of the Government of India, said chief development officer Sudhir Kumar.

Concept note-2: -National Nutrition Month – March.

Concept note-3: -The theme of National Nutrition week 2022 is ‘Celebrate a World of Flavours’. The government introduces a special theme every year that primarily focuses on that year’s theme. Last year, the theme for nutrition week was ‘feeding smart right from the start.

Concept note-4: -National Nutrition Week, a 7-day-long event is organized by the Ministry of Women and Child Development. A healthy and balanced diet must contain nutrients like Carbohydrates, Proteins, Fats, and Vitamins. In this article, we have discussed the History, Significance, and Theme of National Nutrition Week 2022.

Concept note-5: -With the focus on inculcating good nutrition habits and positive behaviors that help pregnant women, lactating mothers and adolescent girls combat malnutrition, the Ministry of Women and Child Development is celebrating the 5th Rashtriya Poshan Maah 2022 from 1st to 30th September 2022.
