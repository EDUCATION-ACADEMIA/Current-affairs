Who has been appointed as new Chairman of Payments Council of India?

(A) Nakul Chopra

(B) ** Vishwas Patel

(C) Naveen Surya

(D) Loney Antony

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Vishwas Patel.

Concept note-2: -Vishwas Patel, chairman, Payments Council of India, shares key takeaways from #Budget2023 https://t.co/rasdfMdXYT” / Twitter.

Concept note-3: -Payments Council of India re-elects Vishwas Patel as chairman.

Vishwas has founded CCAvenue.com payment gateway in the year 2001 and it today provides payment processing services to over hundred thousand plus e-Merchants across the world.

Concept note-5: -Vijay Shekhar Sharma, is the Chairman in the Paytm Payments Bank. He spearheads the Payment Bank’s strategy and vision. A strong believer in emerging technologies, Vijay has played a major role in the evolution of mobile payments in India.
