Who is the CEO of Adani Ports and Special Economic Zones?

(A) ** Karan Adani

(B) Malay Mahadevia

(C) Gopal Krishna Pillai

(D) Radhika Haribhakti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -D Muthukumaran - Chief Financial Officer - Adani Ports and SEZ | LinkedIn.

Concept note-2: -Anil Kishore Singh - Chief Executive Officer - Adani Hazira and Dahej Ports | LinkedIn.

Concept note-3: -The company’s board on Thursday (7 July 2022) approved the appointment of D Muthukumaran as the chief financial officer and key managerial personnel of the company with effect from 25 July 2022.

Concept note-4: -Mr Ennarasu Karunesan has assumed charge as CEO – Adani Ports & SEZ (Southern Ports), based out of Chennai. He took over charge from Mr G. J. Rao who played a key role for more than two years in the development of Adani Kattupalli, Adani Ennore Container Terminal and Adani Murmugao Port Terminal.
