Which position did India obtain Global Environment Performance Index 2018 recently?

(A) ** 119

(B) 125

(C) 185

(D) 250

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has been ranked 177th among 180 nations at the 2018 Global Environment Performance Index (EPI) rankings. As per State of India’s Environment (SoE) 2018 in Figures, India scored 5.75 out of 100 in air quality.

Concept note-2: -In 2018, according to the just released State of India’s Environment (SoE) 2018 in Figures, it has slipped to the 177th position.

Concept note-3: -India ranked the lowest among 180 countries, after Vietnam (178), Bangladesh (177), and Pakistan (176). With an overall score of 18.9, India ranks at the bottom of all countries in the 2022 EPI with low scores across a range of critical issues.

Concept note-4: -India Ranking in Different Indexes 2022: Latest Reports Recently, the Global Innovation Index (GII) 2022 was announced by the World Intellectual Property Organization (WIPO). India is ranked 40th in the index.

Concept note-5: -India has slipped three spots to rank 120 on the 17 Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) adopted as a part of the 2030 agenda by 192 United Nations member states in 2015. In 2021 India ranked 117 among 192 nations. India’s overall SDG score was 66 out of 100.
