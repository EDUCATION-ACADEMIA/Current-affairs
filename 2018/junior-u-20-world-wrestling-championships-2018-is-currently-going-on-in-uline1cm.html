Junior (U-20) World Wrestling Championships 2018 is currently going on in _________

(A) ** Slovakia

(B) Finland

(C) Denmark

(D) Luxembourg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The U20 World Championship is a yearly event that brings together the best wrestlers from each category (Greco-Roman, Freestyle and Women’s), aged between 18 and 20 years old.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Japan won the Women’s freestyle Wrestling Championship 2018 conducted by United World Wrestling at Budapest, Hungary in October 2018.

Concept note-3: -India has so far won 22 medals including 1 gold, 5 silver and 16 bronze at the World Wrestling Championships, a global event organized by the United World Wrestling (UWW).
