Which ministry signs MoU with Madame Tussauds Wax Museum?

(A) ** Ministry of Railways

(B) Ministry of Defence

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) Ministry of Finance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Rail Museum (NRM) signed an MoU with Madame Tussauds Wax Museum to foster tourism in Delhi NCR. National Rail Museum (NRM):

Concept note-2: -Former Prime Minister Indira Gandhi is one of the first Indians to have a wax statue at Madame Tussauds. Fans pose along side Mahatma Gandhi’s statue. Amitabh Bachchan stands next to his wax statue at Madame Tussauds. Big B is the first Bollywood actor to have a statue at Tussauds London.

Concept note-3: -’Shehanshah’ of Bollywood, Amitabh Bachchan was the first Indian actor to get his wax statute at the famous Madame Tussauds London museum. The actor was, in fact, the first Asian actor to find a place at the London museum.

Concept note-4: -Madame Tussauds India, Noida The museum is open 11.30am to 7.30pm (with last entry at 7pm). Tickets may be booked online. Address: 4th floor, DLF Mall of India, Noida.

Concept note-5: -The wax museum, earlier located at Connaught Place’s Regal Building, features close to 50 prominent figures from various fields, including history, sports, music, film, and television.
