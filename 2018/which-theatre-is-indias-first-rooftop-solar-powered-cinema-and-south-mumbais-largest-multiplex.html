Which theatre is India’s First Rooftop Solar Powered Cinema and South Mumbai’s Largest Multiplex?

(A) ** INOX

(B) Sathyam

(C) AGS

(D) Luxe Cinemas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai’s iconic Metro Cinema too has recently joined this league of innovators. Metro Cinema in partnership with Inox has added two new 100% solar-powered screens to the establishment.

Concept note-2: -This makes Mary Pickford Theater the largest privately owned commercial solar energy with battery storage plant in the country.

Concept note-3: -The premium shopping mall unveiled by Reliance, is geared to provide a world-class retail experience to customers in Mumbai with technology, style and cutting-edge innovation at its core, the release said.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Pavan Jain, Chairman of the INOX Group, is a Chemical Engineer from IIT, New Delhi, and an industrialist with over 45 years of experience.
