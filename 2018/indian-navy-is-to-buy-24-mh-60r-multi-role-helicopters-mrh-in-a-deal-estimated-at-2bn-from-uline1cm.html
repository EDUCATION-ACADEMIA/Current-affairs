Indian Navy is to buy 24 MH-60R Multi-Role Helicopters (MRH) in a deal estimated at $2bn from _________

(A) Russia

(B) ** US

(C) Germany

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“The Government issued the Letter of Request (LoR) on November 15 for procurement of 24 MRH from the U.S. government under the Foreign Military Sales (FMS) route, ” a defence official said.

Concept note-2: -"The United States Department of State has approved the sale of 24 MH-60R multi-mission helicopters to India under its Foreign Military Sales (FMS) program.

Concept note-3: -More about the MH-60R Romeo multi-mission helicopter The Indian Navy has ordered 24 multi-mission helicopters from the US based Lockheed Martin, which is considered to be the most advanced maritime helicopter in the world.

Concept note-4: -“The delivery of all the 24 MH-60R multirole helicopters will be completed by 2025. The induction of these helicopters will significantly boost the integral Anti-Submarine Warfare (ASW) capability of the Indian Navy, ” said the Indian Navy officials.

Concept note-5: -The acquisitions are part of a 24-helicopter, USD2. 6 billion Foreign Military Sales (FMS) deal, which was jointly announced by India and the United States in February 2020. Delivery of the fleet is expected to be completed by 2025.
