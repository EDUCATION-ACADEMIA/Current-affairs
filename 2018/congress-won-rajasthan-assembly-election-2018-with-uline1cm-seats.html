Congress won Rajasthan Assembly Election 2018 with _________ seats.

(A) 107

(B) 84

(C) ** 99

(D) 78

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Legislative Assembly elections were held in the Indian state of Rajasthan on 7 December 2018. The Indian National Congress became the single largest party with 100 seats, short of majority by 1 seat.

Concept note-2: -Presently, the legislative assembly consists of 200 members.

Concept note-3: -BJP got 163 seats and Congress got only 21 seats out of 200 seats. Vasundhara Raje became the Chief Minister for second time. But in 2018 Indian National Congress flipped the state again with a big majority and Ashok Gehlot became the Chief Minister for the third time.

Concept note-4: -Rajasthan Pradesh Congress Committee (RPCC) is the Pradesh Congress Committee (state wing) of the Indian National Congress (INC) serving in the state of Rajasthan.
