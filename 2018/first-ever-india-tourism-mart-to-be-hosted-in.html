First ever ’India Tourism Mart’ to be hosted in

(A) Hyderabad

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Mumbai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To this end, the first ever India Tourism Mart was inaugurated by the union minister for railways, Piyush Goyal, in the presence of union tourism minister, K J Alphons and the tourism minister of Morocco, Mohamed Sajid, in New Delhi on September 17.

Concept note-2: -The first ever Tourism Policy was announced by the Indian Government in 1982 (November). Then in 1988, the National Committee on tourism formulated a comprehensive plan for achieving a sustainable growth in tourism.

Concept note-3: -Karan Singh was appointed the first minister.

Concept note-4: -Kerala is the first state to recognize tourism as an industry. Kerala is one of the states that make a maximum profit through the tourism industry.
