Who was honoured at Cannes Film Festival recently?

(A) ** Smt Late Sridevi

(B) Smt Madhuri Diskidhi

(C) Shri Vivek misra

(D) Shri Nitya Bhaskar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Grand Prix was awarded to two films: Lukas Dhont’s Close and Claire Denis’s Stars at Noon. Claire Denis thanked the jury and the festival. Denis said she had spotted Margaret Qualley in Quentin Tarantino’s film and had found her extraordinary.

Concept note-2: -From Cannes regulars Deepika Padukone and Aishwarya Rai Bachchan to actors like Aditi Rao Hydari, Tamannaah Bhatia, Pooja Hegde and Nawazuddin Siddiqui, from composers like Ricky Kej to folk singer Mame Khan, desi representation was high at Cannes 2022.

Concept note-3: -Cannes 2022: India Named As The ‘Country Of Honour’; Delegation To Be Led By Anurag Thakur. India is the country of honour this year at the Cannes film festival. I&B Minister Anurag Thakur is set to walk the Red Carpet and also participate in the Opening Night festivities of Marche Du Film at the French Riviera.
