(A) Baharampur

(B) Saptagram

(C) Nabadwip

(D) ** Haldia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Varanasi Multi-Modal Terminal or Varanasi Port is an Inland river port situated in the city of Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh. The port is located on the Ganges river.

Concept note-2: -The PM Gati Shakti National Master Plan, launched by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi, aims at providing Multimodal connectivity infrastructure to various SEZs and enhancing logistical efficiency.
