(A) Rs 150.80 crore

(B) Rs 138.80 crore

(C) ** Rs 106.80 crore

(D) Rs 124.80 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A capped fast bowler and an uncapped mystery bowler emerged as the joint highest grossers in the VIVO IPL 2019 Player Auction that concluded in Jaipur. With only 70 spots up for grabs, the one-day event witnessed 60 players being bought including 20 overseas. The eight franchises spent INR 1, 06, 80, 00, 000.
