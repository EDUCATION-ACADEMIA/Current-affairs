Which is the host country for this year’s World Tourism Day (WTD)?

(A) ** Hungary

(B) Qatar

(C) Thailand

(D) Mexico

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The official celebration of world tourism day will be held in Bali, Indonesia this year with the theme of ‘Rethinking Tourism’. This theme seems to be apt as the way and reasons for travel have changed drastically in the past few years for many people.

Concept note-2: -The official World Tourism Day celebration will be held in Bali, Indonesia, on 27 September, highlighting the shift towards tourism being recognized as a crucial pillar of development. Wonderful Indonesia-Witness the 42nd World Tourism Day 2022 in Bali, Indonesia!

Concept note-3: -The theme of this year is “ Rethinking Tourism". The aim is to rethink tourism for development, including through education and jobs, and tourism’s impact on the planet and opportunities to grow more sustainably. This year the official World Tourism Day celebration is being held in Bali, Indonesia.

Concept note-4: -The inaugural report is titled “Rethinking Tourism: From Crisis to Transformation”, reflecting the timely relevance of the 2022 theme as well as the unprecedented crisis that hit the sector in 2020.
