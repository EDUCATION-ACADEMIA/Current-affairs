Ministry of Woman and Child Development organized a Seminar on Technology Partnerships for Steering POSHAN Abhiyaan. What is that seminar called?

(A) TECHSAT

(B) TECH-IGNIZ

(C) ** TECH-THON

(D) TECH-CRAFT

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Poshan Abhiyaan was launched by Hon’ble Prime Minister on 8th March, 2018 in Jhunjhunu district of Rajasthan. The focus of Abhiyaan is to lay emphasis on nutritional status of adolescent girls, pregnant women, lactating mothers and children from 0-6 years age.

Concept note-2: -Poshan Abhiyaan 2022 The main of the scheme is to develop practices that nurture health, fitness and immunity to disease as well as end the menace of malnutrition in the nation.

Concept note-3: -POSHAN Abhiyan 2022: Various activities such as T3 camps (test, treat, talk), IFA distribution, seminars, Ayush for anemia, webinars, quiz being conducted throughout the country.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Gujarat. Gujarat government has launched ‘Poshan Abhiyan’ to eradicate malnutrition in 2018.
