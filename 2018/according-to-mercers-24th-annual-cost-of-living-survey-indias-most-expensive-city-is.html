According to Mercer’s 24th annual “Cost of Living” Survey, India’s most expensive city is

(A) Kolkata

(B) New Delhi

(C) ** Mumbai

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai is the most expensive city in India for expatriates living and working overseas: Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living Survey. Mumbai, June 29, 2022: According to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey, Mumbai (127) is the most costly city in India in terms of both living expenses and accommodation costs.

Concept note-2: -According to Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey, Mumbai is the most expensive city to live in India.

Concept note-3: -Hong Kong tops Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living City Ranking, based on our latest research, followed by Zurich and Geneva, while Ankara closes the ranking at the 227th spot.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai (127) is the most expensive city in India in terms of both living expenses and accommodation costs, followed by New Delhi (155), Chennai (177), Bengaluru (178), and Hyderabad (192), finds Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey. Pune (201) and Kolkata (203) are the least expensive Indian cities in the ranking.

Concept note-5: -This year New York and Singapore climbed up to the number 1 position of the world’s most expensive cities to live in. According to a new index, the Russian war in Ukraine and the pandemic’s aftereffects have caused the cost of living to go up by 8.1 per cent in 2022.
