(B) 250 Billions

(C) 380 Billions

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On a cumulative basis, total investment proposals during 2022 shot up to Rs 23.6 lakh crore as compared with Rs 13.8 lakh core in the previous year and Rs 11.6 lakh crore in 2020.
