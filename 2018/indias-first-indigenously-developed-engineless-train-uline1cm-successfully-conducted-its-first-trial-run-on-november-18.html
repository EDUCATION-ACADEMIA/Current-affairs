India’s first indigenously developed engineless train " _________" successfully conducted its first trial run on November 18.

(A) Train 16

(B) Train 20

(C) ** Train 18

(D) Train 19

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Acknowledging its made-in-India status, the Indian Railways has named the indigenously manufactured Train 18 as Vande Bharat Express. Vande Bharat Express is set to run between Delhi and Varanasi at a maximum speed of 160kmph.

Concept note-2: -11.30 am: The complete booking of the Vande Bharat Express for its first commercial journey on Feb 17 will act as a booster for Indian Railways. Delhi: Visuals of Prime Minister Narendra Modi onboard Vande Bharat Express (Train-18).

Concept note-3: -"Train 18 will now be known as Vande Bharat Express. It’s a train built completely in India by Indian engineers, in a span of 18 months. It’ll ply from Delhi to Varanasi.

Concept note-4: -On 16th April 1853, the first passenger train ran between Bori Bunder (Bombay) and Thane, a distance of 34 km. It was operated by three locomotives, named Sahib, Sultan and Sindh, and had thirteen carriages.
