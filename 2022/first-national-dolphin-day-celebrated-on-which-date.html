First National Dolphin Day celebrated on which date?

(A) October 04

(B) October 09

(C) October 08

(D) ** October 05

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Dolphin Day (April 14th) | Days Of The Year.

Concept note-2: -Sea Shepherd announces the creation of World Dolphin Day on September 12th – the one-year anniversary of the largest single slaughter of cetaceans in recorded history – both in memory of the 1428 dolphins killed in the Faroe Islands on that day, and as a reminder that dolphins around the world need more protection.

Concept note-3: -The Union environment ministry has designated October 5 as the National Dolphin Day, to be celebrated annually starting this year. On 5th October, river dolphin was declared as India’s National Aquatic Animal in 2009.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 5 October. Ganga River Dolphin Day is celebrated on 5 October every year in India. It was on this day in 2009 when the Ganga River Dolphin was formally adopted as the National Aquatic Animal.

Concept note-5: -Union environment minister Bhupender Yadav said generating awareness and community participation is integral for conservation of this dolphins, an indicator species. NEW DELHI: The union environment ministry has designated October 5 as National Dolphin Day, to be observed annually starting this year.
