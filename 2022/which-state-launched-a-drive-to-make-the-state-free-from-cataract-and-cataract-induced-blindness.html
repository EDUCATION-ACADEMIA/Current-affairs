Which state launched a drive to make the state free from cataract and cataract-induced blindness?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) ** Gujarat

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Besides, Gujarat is the only state in the country to provide free hydrophobic intraocular lenses. The Chief Minister further said that Prime Minister Mr. Narendra Modi has decided to create a cataract-free nation by doing surgery of people who are blind due to cataract across the country.

Concept note-2: -National Programme for Control of Blindness (NPCB) launched in 1976. The Trachoma Control Programme started in 1963 was merged under NPCB in 1976. In the beginning, NPCB was a 100% centrally sponsored programme (now from 12th FYP it is 60:40 in all States/UTs and 90:10 in hilly states and all NE States).

Concept note-3: -In India cataract has been reported to be responsible for 50-80% of the bilaterally blind in the country.

Concept note-4: -However, every surgery entails risks and cataract surgery is no exception. Endophthalmitis is the only complication that can cause permanent vision loss. Its incidence is less than 0.04% to 0.2% in individuals at risk.
