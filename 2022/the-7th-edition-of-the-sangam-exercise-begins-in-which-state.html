The 7th edition of the Sangam exercise begins in which state?

(A) Kerala

(B) Odisha

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Goa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The seventh edition of Exercise Sangam started in Goa on December 1. It was first held in 1994 and is considered a key military and diplomatic initiative between India and the US.

Concept note-2: -Exercise Sangam was first conducted in 1994 and is an important military and diplomatic initiative between the two nations, reflective of the trust and friendship between them. It aims to exchange ideas and experiences on various aspects of Maritime Special Operations.

Concept note-3: -The exercise was later upgraded to a company-level exercise, renamed Ex Kazind in 2018 and instituted in 2016. Various missions, ranging from joint planning, joint tactical drills, basics of special arms skills, and HADR to raiding a hostile target, were conducted during the training.

Concept note-4: -The Marine Commandos, abbreviated to MARCOS and officially called the Marine Commando Force (MCF), are the Special Operations Forces unit of the Indian Navy and is responsible for conducting special operations.
