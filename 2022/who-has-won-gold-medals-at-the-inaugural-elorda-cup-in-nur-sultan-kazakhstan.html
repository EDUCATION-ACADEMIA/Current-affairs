Who has won gold medals at the inaugural Elorda Cup in Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan?

(A) Kalaivani Srinivasan, Jamuna Boro

(B) Kalaivani Srinivasan, Gitika

(C) Alfiya Pathan, Jamuna Boro

(D) ** Alfiya Pathan, Gitika

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reigning youth world champions Alfiya Pathan and Gitika produced sensational performances to win gold medals at the inaugural edition of the Elorda Boxing Cup in Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan, today.

Concept note-2: -Young Indian boxers Alfiya Pathan and Gitika helped India end the Elorda Cup 2022 on a high by winning gold medals in their respective weight categories at Nur-Sultan, Kazakhstan, on Monday. Alfiya Pathan defeated the 2016 world champion Lazzat Kungeibayeva of Kazakhstan 5-0 in the women’s +81kg final.

Concept note-3: -Shiva Thapa lost to Uzbekistan’s Ruslan Abdullaev in the final of the men’s 63.5kg. India won 12 medals, including four golds.

Concept note-4: -NAGPUR: City’s Alfiya Khan Pathan became the first-ever Maharashtra woman boxer to win a medal at the ASBC Asian Elite Boxing Championships. On Friday, Alfiya won the gold medal in 81+kg category and is among the four Indian gold medal winners in the women’s section in Amman, Jordan.
