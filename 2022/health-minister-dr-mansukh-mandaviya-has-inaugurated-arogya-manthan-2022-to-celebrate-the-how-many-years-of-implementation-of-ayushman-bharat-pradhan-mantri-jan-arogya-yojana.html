Health Minister Dr. Mansukh Mandaviya has inaugurated Arogya Manthan 2022 to celebrate the how many years of implementation of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri-Jan Arogya Yojana?

(A) 3

(B) ** 4

(C) 5

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mansukh Mandaviya, Union Minister of Health & Family Welfare will inaugurate “Arogya Manthan 2022” on 25th September, 2022 to celebrate four years of implementation of Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri – Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY) and one year of implementation of Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM).

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, in collaboration with MyGov, invites citizens to participate in designing a Logo for PM Ayushman Bharat Health Infrastructure Mission, representing the roots of the ‘Robust and Accessible Healthcare system in India. The last date for submission is 12 January 2023.

Concept note-3: -Visit pmjay.gov.in straight away to download the card if you have already completed the Ayushman Card registration process. Through the login process on pmjay.gov.in, those who have successfully applied for an Ayushman Bharat Card can access their dashboards.

Concept note-4: -India takes a giant leap towards providing accessible and affordable healthcare to the common man with the launch of Ayushman Bharat – Pradhan Mantri Jan AarogyaYojana (AB-PMJAY) by the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi on 23rd September, 2018 at Ranchi, Jharkhand.
