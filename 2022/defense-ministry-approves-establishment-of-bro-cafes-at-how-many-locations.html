Defense Ministry approves establishment of ‘BRO Cafes’ at how many locations?

(A) ** 75

(B) 100

(C) 50

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Border Roads Organisation Cafes: Defence Ministry approves establishment of ‘BRO Cafes’ on 75 border roads in 12 States/UTs - The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Ministry of Defence has approved setting up of wayside amenities under the brand of ‘BRO Cafes’ at 75 locations on different sections of roads with Border Roads Organisation across 12 states and Union territories.

Concept note-3: -Since the inaccessibility and remoteness of these roads preclude adequate commercial infrastructures, BRO, being co existing, is assisting in opening of Cafe at remote tourist places in order to boost tourism, adequate economic activity and generate revenue. The 75 BRO Cafés will be established.

Concept note-4: -Initially, BRO was functional under the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways. But since 2015, it is being managed and is functional under the Ministry of Defence. BRO is operational in 21 Indian states and 1 union territory.
