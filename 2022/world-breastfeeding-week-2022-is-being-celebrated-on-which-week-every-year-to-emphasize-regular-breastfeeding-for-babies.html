(A) August 02-08

(B) August 05-11

(C) August 03-09

Concept note-1: -The idea of celebrating it on a single day was later expanded to a week. Concept note-2: -1-7 AUGUST 2022 This year, the theme for World Breastfeeding Week (WBW 2022) is Step up for Breastfeeding - Educate and Support. Since 2016, WBW has been aligned with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs).
