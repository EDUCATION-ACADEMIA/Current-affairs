Centre has been approved to continue the MPF Scheme. How much amount has been allocated to the scheme for five years?

(A) Rs. 21, 270 cr

(B) Rs. 29, 280 cr

(C) ** Rs. 26, 275 cr

(D) Rs. 23, 285 cr

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Modi government has approved the continuation of a mega police modernisation scheme for five years up to 2025-26 with a financial outlay of Rs 26,275 crore.

Concept note-2: -The umbrella scheme of Modernisation of State Police Forces (MPF) has been approved for the period 2021-22 to 2025-26, the ministry said in a statement on Sunday. The scheme has a total financial outlay of ₹26,275 crore. The Centre will provide ₹4,846 crore for the modernisation of state police forces.

Concept note-3: -Government of India under leadership of Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi approves continuation of umbrella scheme of Modernisation of Police Forces (MPF)

Concept note-4: -Home Division of MHA Police Modernisation Division Modernisation of State Police Forces (MPF) Scheme.
