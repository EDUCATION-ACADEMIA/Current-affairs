Which of the following company has signed definitive transaction agreements to acquire 51 percent shareholding of Badshah Masala in a deal of Rs 587.52 crore?

(A) ** Dabur India

(B) Hamdard India

(C) Nestle India

(D) Baidyanath Group

Concept note-1: -In October 2022, Dabur India announced that it has signed definitive transaction agreements to acquire 51 per cent shareholding of Badshah Masala in a deal worth Rs 587.52 crore.
