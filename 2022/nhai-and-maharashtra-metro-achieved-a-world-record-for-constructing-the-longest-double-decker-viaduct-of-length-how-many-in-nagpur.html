NHAI and Maharashtra Metro achieved a world record for constructing the longest double-decker viaduct of length how many in Nagpur?

(A) 3.34 km

(B) ** 3.14 km

(C) 3.04 km

(D) 3.24 km

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A 3.14 km double-decker viaduct constructed by Maharashtra Metro Rail Corporation Ltd (Maha Metro) in Nagpur has made it to the Guinness Book of World Records.

Concept note-2: -This 3.1 Km long double-decker viaduct was built jointly on Nagpur’s Wadha road by Maha Metro and NHAI. This double-decker viaduct made possible a three-layered transport corridor with the first layer occupied by pre-existing highway, the second layer by a flyover highway, and the third layer by Nagpur metro rail.

Concept note-3: -Nagpur Metro in Maharashtra has entered Guinness Book of World Records for creating the longest double-decker viaduct metro measuring 3.14 km on Wardha Road. Nagpur Metro in Maharashtra has entered Guinness Book of World Records for creating the longest double-decker viaduct metro measuring 3.14 km on Wardha Road.

Concept note-4: -Maha Metro Nagpur has added yet another feather to its cap as the Wardha Road Double Decker Viaduct has been identified by Guinness World Records as the Longest Double Decker in the Metro category across the world.

Concept note-5: -Nagpur Metro has created a Guinness World Record by constructing the longest double-decker viaduct for a metro. The double-decker viaduct on Wardha Road is 3.14 km long and has three stations – Chhatrapati Nagar, Jai Prakash Nagar and Ujjwal Nagar.
