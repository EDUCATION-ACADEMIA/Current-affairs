World Pulses Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) February 08

(B) ** February 10

(C) February 09

(D) February 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Nations General Assembly nominated February 10 as World Pulses Day to raise awareness and highlight the nutritional benefits of pulses and their contribution to sustainable food systems and world hunger.

Concept note-2: -World Pulses Day | 10 February Pulses are the edible seeds of leguminous plants cultivated for both food and feed.

Concept note-3: -Recognizing their value, on 20 December 2013, the UN General Assembly adopted a resolution (A/RES/68/231) proclaiming 2016 as the International Year of Pulses (IYP).

Concept note-4: -World Pulses Day 2022: It is a global event and is celebrated on 10 February to highlight the importance of pulses as a worldwide food. The day recognise a significant role that pulses play in achieving the comprehensive, far-reaching, and people centred set of universal and transformative goals.

Concept note-5: -Celebrating Pulses The Key to a Sustainable and Nutritious Future. Every year on February 10th, World Pulses Day is observed to increase recognition of the significance of pulses, which are dried legume seeds.
