PV Sindhu and Manpreet Singh are India’s flag-bearers in the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Games in July 2022. The 2022 commonwealth games took place in which country?

(A) ** United Kingdom

(B) Chile

(C) Brazil

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Badminton ace PV Sindhu and India men’s hockey captain Manpreet Singh have been named India’s flagbearers for the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Games 2022, which will be held on July 28 at the Alexander Stadium in Birmingham.

Concept note-2: -Sharath Kamal on being the flag bearer of the Indian contingent at the closing ceremony of Commonwealth Games 2022.

Concept note-3: -Former world champion PV Sindhu added one more gold medal to her cabinet by winning the women’s singles title at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham, UK, on Monday (8 August).

Concept note-4: -BIRMINGHAM: Double Olympic medallist badminton ace PV Sindhu and Indian men’s hockey team captain Manpreet Singh were on Wednesday named the country’s flagbearer for the opening ceremony of the Commonwealth Games to be held here on Thursday.
