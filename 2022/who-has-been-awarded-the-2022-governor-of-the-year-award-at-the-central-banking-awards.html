Who has been awarded the 2022 Governor of the year award at the Central Banking Awards?

(A) Sushant Khatri

(B) Jef Marcel

(C) ** Mario Marcel

(D) Joy Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Abhishek Mishra. Answer: Option C Explanation: Mario Marcel, the Governor of the Central Bank of Chile, has won the Governor of the year award at the Central Banking Awards 2022.

Concept note-2: -Q. Mario Marcel, who won the ‘Governor of the year’ award, was the Governor which country’s Central Bank? Notes: The Central Banking team and its editorial advisory panel recently unveiled its ninth edition of Central Banking Awards (2022). Chile’s Mario Marcel has been named for the Governor of the year award.

Concept note-3: -Central Bank of India has been awarded ‘First’ Prizes by Reserve Bank of India in a function held on 24th May, 2016 at Y.B.

Concept note-4: -Mario Marcel received the title of Governor of the Year 2022 Banco Central de Chile is the name of the central bank of Chile. The Central Banking Team and an editorial advisory panel have determined the award by conducting several months of review and interviewing of pitches for the Ninth Annual Awards.

Concept note-5: -Presentation of Central Bank of Chile’s Annual Report by Ms Rosanna Costa, Governor of the Central Bank of Chile, before the Honorable Senate of the Republic, Santiago de Chile, 7 December 2022.
