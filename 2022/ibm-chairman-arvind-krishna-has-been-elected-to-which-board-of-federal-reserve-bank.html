IBM Chairman Arvind Krishna has been elected to which Board of Federal Reserve Bank?

(A) San Francisco

(B) ** New York

(C) Dallas

(D) Chicago

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IBM Chairman and Chief Executive Officer, Arvind Krishna has been elected to the Board of Directors of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York .

Concept note-2: -John C. Williams took office on June 18, 2018, as the 11th president and chief executive officer of the Second District, Federal Reserve Bank of New York.

Concept note-3: -New York Fed names Indian American Sushmita Shukla as first vice president. The Board of Directors of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York has appointed Sushmita Shukla as first vice president and chief operating officer, effective March 2023.

Concept note-4: -Jerome H. Powell first took office as Chair of the Board of Governors of the Federal Reserve System on February 5, 2018, for a four-year term. He was reappointed to the office and sworn in for a second four-year term on May 23, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Arvind Krishna is the Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of IBM. As a business leader and technologist, he has led the building and expansion of new markets for IBM in artificial intelligence, cloud, quantum computing, and blockchain.
