Who is the author of the book titled-A Little Book of India:Celebrating 75 years of Independence?

(A) ** Ruskin Bond

(B) Sudha Murty

(C) Arundhati Roy

(D) Chetan Bhagat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As India completes 75 years of independence, we bring to you a slice of our beloved country in the words of our favourite author, Ruskin Bond . Drawing on his own memories and impressions of this unique land, he pays homage to the country that has been his home for 84 years.

Concept note-2: -Ruskin Bond (born 19 May 1934) is an Indian author . His first novel, The Room on the Roof, was published in 1956, and it received the John Llewellyn Rhys Prize in 1957.
