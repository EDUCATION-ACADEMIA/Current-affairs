PM Modi released Commemorative Coin & Postage Stamp on which birth anniversary 9th Sikh Guru Tegh Bahadur?

(A) 250th

(B) ** 400th

(C) 300th

(D) 350th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mint 400th Birth Anniversary of Sri Guru Tegh Bahadur ji Commemorative Coin (Rs. 400 Coin) By buying this product you can collect up to 12.00 with our loyalty program.

Concept note-2: -Born in 1621 he was the youngest son of Guru Hargobind. This year marks the 400th year of his birth anniversary which will be celebrated on April 21, Thursday.

Concept note-3: -Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day 2022, Guru Tegh Bahadur Shaheedi Diwas 2022: Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day is observed every year on November 24, commemorating Shaheedi Diwas of Guru Tegh Bahadur.

Concept note-4: -Guru Tegh Bahadur is the 9th Guru of the Sikh community. He gave his life to protect human rights, and his death anniversary is celebrated on November 24 as Shaheedi Diwas. Guru Tegh Bahadur was born on April 21, 1621, in Amritsar. Tyaga Mal was his original name.
