What is the theme of National Technology Day 2022?

(A) Science and Technology for a Sustainable Future

(B) Science for Nation Building

(C) ** Integrated Approach in Science and Technology for Sustainable Future

(D) Science for People and People for Science

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Technology Day 2022 theme was “Integrated Approach in Science and technology for sustainable future“. National Technology Day 2023 theme is yet to be released.

Concept note-2: -The theme was launched by Union Minister Jitendra Singh. The theme of National Technology Day 2021 was “Science and Technology for a Sustainable Future”.

Concept note-3: -With multiple technological advancements accomplished on the same day, the Indian government declared May 11 as National Technology Day. It was officially signed by then-Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee.

Concept note-4: -National Technology Day on January 6th recognizes how technology changes the world and looks to the future of technology. Each year, from the wheel to smartphones, the day honors technological achievements that impact our daily lives.

Concept note-5: -First observed on May 11, 1999, National Technology Day was originally celebrated in India. The day was established by then Prime Minister Atal Bihari Vajpayee, in honor of the tenth anniversary of the Pokhran Nuclear Test, and the day was declared by the national Council for Technology Development in India.
