The foundation stone for International Cricket Stadium in Jaipur was laid. It is said to be the third-largest stadium in the world. What is the proposed seating capacity of the stadium?

(A) 95, 000

(B) 80, 000

(C) 1, 00, 000

(D) ** 75, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Chonp (Jaipur). The foundation stone of the world’s third largest cricket stadium was laid on 5th Feb 2022 at Chonp (Jaipur). This stadium will be the third largest stadium in the world and the second largest in India after Motera in Ahmedabad and Melbourne in Australia.

Concept note-2: -The world’s third-largest cricket stadium is going to be built in Jaipur, and Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot and BCCI president Sourav Ganguly on February 5 laid the foundation stone of the project virtually.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Jaipur. The world’s third-largest cricket stadium is going to be built in Jaipur. Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot (As of Feb 2022) and BCCI President Sourav Ganguly (As of Feb 2022) laid the foundation stone of the project virtually in February 2022.

Concept note-4: -The world’s biggest cricket stadium – the Narendra Modi Stadium in the Motera area of Ahmedabad – is a refurbished stadium – earlier known as the Sardar Patel Stadium. It was inaugurated in 2020 and has the capacity to hold 132,000 spectators, making it the biggest cricketing venue ever.
