Who has become the Indian Skipper overtook New Zealand’s Martin Guptill to become the leading run-scorer in men’s T20 Internationals?

(A) Rishab Pant

(B) Hardik Pandya

(C) Dinesh Kartik

(D) ** Rohit Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India skipper Rohit Sharma surpassed Martin Guptill of New Zealand to become the T20I format’s most run-scorer.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Rohit Sharma. Rohit Sharma has become the second Indian batter to complete 10,000 runs in T20 cricket for men. He achieved this historic milestone during the match against Punjab Kings (PBKS) on 13 April 2022 in Pune.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli is the leading run scorer in T20 Internationals, with 4,008 runs.

Concept note-4: -Rohit Sharma, the current captain of Team India, has hit the most sixes in T20I cricket, 182.

Concept note-5: -Virat Kohli (56 Innings) Virat Kohli, also known as Run Machine, is an Indian captain in all formats of cricket and is sitting at the 2nd position for reaching the 2000 runs milestone from his 56 innings. As of Oct 2021, he is the number 1 batter in terms of making the most runs in T20 (International).
