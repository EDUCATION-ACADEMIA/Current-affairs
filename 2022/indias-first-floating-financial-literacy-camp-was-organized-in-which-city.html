India’s first floating financial literacy camp was organized in which city?

(A) Nainital

(B) ** Srinagar

(C) Bhopal

(D) Udaipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi has appreciated IPPB for conducting India’s First Floating Financial Literacy Camp in Dal Lake of Srinagar, Jammu & Kashmir under Niveshak Didi.

Concept note-2: -India Post Payments Bank (IPPB) conducted India’s first floating financial literacy camp in Dal Lake with an initiative called ‘Niveshak Didi’ to promote financial literacy ‘By the Women, For the Women’ in Srinagar.

Concept note-3: -Metropolitan areas like Maharashtra, Delhi, and West Bengal have financial literacy rates of 17%, 32%, and 21%, respectively. States like Bihar, Rajasthan, Jharkhand and Uttar Pradesh where poverty is rampant have low literacy rates.

Concept note-4: -India’s First Floating Financial Literacy Camp was conducted as part of the launch of the initiative called “Niveshak Didi” in Jammu and Kashmir’s Srinagar. It was conducted among local residents around the famous Dal Lake in Srinagar.

Concept note-5: -Literacy camp is a camp to provide banking sevices and products related information and financial advice to the rural or unbanked masses. Spread banking and finance related awareness and advice to the public.
