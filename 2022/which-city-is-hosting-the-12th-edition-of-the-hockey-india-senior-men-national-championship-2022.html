Which city is hosting the 12th edition of the Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2022?

(A) Guwahati

(B) ** Bhopal

(C) Gurugram

(D) Dehradun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Preview: 12th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2022 gets underway in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh from tomorrow - Hockey India.

Concept note-2: -Bhopal (Madhya Pradesh) [India], April 17 (ANI): Hockey Haryana were crowned as the champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Men National Championship 2022 in Bhopal with a thrilling 1-1 (3-1 SO) victory over Hockey Unit of Tamil Nadu in the final of the competition.

Concept note-3: -Bhopal, 17 May 2022: Hockey Association Of Odisha were crowned as the Champions of the 12th Hockey India Senior Women National Championship 2022 in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh with a thrilling 2-0 victory over Hockey Karnataka in the Final of the competition.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Odisha.

Concept note-5: -The senior men’s National hockey championships will be held either in Kovilpatti or Madurai in Tamil Nadu, while the women’s event will be in Telangana, both in the month of April 2023, according to Sekhar J.
