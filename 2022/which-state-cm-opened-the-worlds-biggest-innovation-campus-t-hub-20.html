Which state CM opened the world’s biggest innovation campus T-Hub 2.0?

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Karnataka

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Telangana Chief Minister opens world’s biggest innovation campus T-Hub 2.0.

Concept note-2: -Telangana Chief Minister K Chandrashekar Rao inaugurated T-Hub 2.0, the world’s largest innovation campus in Hyderabad, and said the day is not far when the next big breakthrough in startup innovation will come from the city.

Concept note-3: -Hyderabad-based business incubator organisation, Technology Hub (T-Hub) announced the inauguration of the world’s largest innovation campus for start-ups in Hyderabad. Built in a T-shape, spanning 10 floors and spread over an area of 5,82,689 sq.

Concept note-4: -HYDERABAD: T-Hub today announced the inauguration of the world’s largest innovation campus in Hyderabad. The innovation campus will act as a platform to elevate the stature of India’s innovation ecosystem globally.

Concept note-5: -T-Hub (Technology Hub) is a premier innovation hub and ecosystem enabler based out of Hyderabad, India. T-Hub leads India’s pioneering innovation ecosystem and strives to create impact for startups, corporations and other ecosystem stakeholders. It’s mission is to enable and empower an ecosystem hungry for innovation.
