Aero India 2023 is to be held from 13-17 Feb 2023 in which city?

(A) Surat

(B) Pune

(C) Jaipur

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aero India is a biennial air show and aviation exhibition held in Bengaluru, India at the Yelahanka Air Force Station. It is organised by the Defence Exhibition Organisation, Ministry of Defence .

Concept note-2: -The platform, with theme “Sky is not the limit: Opportunities beyond boundaries", is expected to lay the foundation of a more robust interaction between the industry partners and government with an eye on bolstering the Make in India campaign, a Ministry statement said.

Concept note-3: -Aero India 2023: Entry details Exhibitors will have to submit the details for the badges online on the website at http://www.aeroindia.gov.in. Business visitors can register online on this website.

Concept note-4: -Aero India Ticket Price ADVA ticket – can only visit ADVA (Air Display Viewing area) from 14-17 Feb, 2023. Business Ticket – Can only visit exhibition area on Business days 13-15 Feb, 2023.

Concept note-5: -Ticket prices for Aero India 2023 However, the Business ones is valid for single day with multiple entry. The price ticket for general visitor (for Exhibition and ADVA) is ₹2500 for Indian nationals and $50 for foreign nationals.
