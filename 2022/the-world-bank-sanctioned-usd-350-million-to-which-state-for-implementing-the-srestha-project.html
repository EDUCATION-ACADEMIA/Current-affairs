The World Bank sanctioned USD 350 million to which state for implementing the ‘SRESTHA’ Project?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Bengal

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. The World Bank sanctioned USD 350 billion to which state for implementing the ‘SRESTHA’ Project? Notes: The World Bank sanctioned USD 350 billion to Gujarat for the implementation of Systems Reform Endeavours for Transformed Health Achievement in Gujarat (SRESTHA-G).

Concept note-2: -“The World Bank is pleased to support the Human Development agenda of the state of Gujarat through the SRESTHA-G Program,” said Auguste Tano Kouame, the World Bank’s Country Director for India.

Concept note-3: -World Bank approves 350 million dollars aid for Systems Reform Endeavors for Transformed Health Achievement in Gujarat -SRESTHA-G project. The World Bank has approved 350 million dollars aid for the Systems Reform Endeavors for Transformed Health Achievement in Gujarat -SRESTHA-G project.

Concept note-4: -USD 350 million in aid has been approved by the World Bank for the “Systems Reform Endeavors for Transformed Health Achievement” in Gujarat or the SRESTHA-G project.
