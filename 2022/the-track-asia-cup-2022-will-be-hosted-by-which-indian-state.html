The Track Asia cup 2022 will be hosted by which Indian state?

(A) Karnataka

(B) Telangana

(C) ** Kerala

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala is all set to host the Track Asia Cup-2022 cycling tournament, one of the biggest cycling events, at the LNCPE outdoor velodrome near here from November 25 to 28. Around 200 cyclists from over 25 countries in Asia will take part in the event, which is being conducted outside Delhi for the first time.

Concept note-2: -Pakistan was scheduled to host the tournament after retaining the rights to host the 2022 edition. However, in October 2021, the Asian Cricket Council (ACC) announced that Sri Lanka would host the tournament in 2022, with Pakistan hosting the 2023 edition.

Concept note-3: -Pakistan will host the Asia Cup (T20 Format) from 2nd to 17th September 2023.

Concept note-4: -ICC World Cup 2023 Schedule In fact, the Asia Cup is organized every year, this time according to the Asia Cup 2023 Schedule, the match programs will be held from 2 September 2023 to 17 September 2023. In which there are ODI and T20 cricket matches. In fact, India has won the Asia Cup a total of 7 times.

Concept note-5: -On 28 August 2022, the most exciting match of this competition do be between India & Pakistan & both group are the major group who can win this battle . This opportunity do start on 28 August 2022 at 7.30 pm Indian time do check the Schedule of Asia Cup 2022 on this page.
