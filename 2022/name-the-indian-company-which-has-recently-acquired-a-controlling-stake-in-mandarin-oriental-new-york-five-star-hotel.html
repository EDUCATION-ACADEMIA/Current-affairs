Name the Indian company which has recently acquired a controlling stake in Mandarin Oriental New York five star hotel?

(A) Larsen and Toubro

(B) ITC Limited

(C) Tata Group

(D) ** Reliance Industries

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MUMBAI : Reliance Industries Ltd will pick up a controlling stake in New York’s premium luxury five-star hotel, Mandarin Oriental, for $98.15 million, the company said on Sunday. Reliance Industrial Investments and Holdings Ltd will acquire the 73.37% stake held indirectly by Cayman Island-based Columbus Centre Corp.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Billionaire Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Industries Ltd on Saturday announced acquisition of New York’s premium luxury hotel Mandarin Oriental for $98.15 million.

Concept note-3: -Asia’s richest man is buying the Mandarin Oriental in New York for $98 million. Asia’s biggest billionaire, Mukesh Ambani, has snapped up a controlling stake in the Mandarin Oriental in New York City for just over $98 million.

Concept note-4: -Mandarin Oriental is a member of the Jardine Matheson Group, a conglomerate with strong expertise and heritage in Hong Kong and Asia, which was founded in Canton in 1832 by Scottish traders William Jardine and James Matheson.

Concept note-5: -Reliance Industries Ltd (RIL) has announced the acquisition of a controlling stake of 73.37% in Mandarin Oriental New York, a premium luxury hotel in midtown Manhattan.
