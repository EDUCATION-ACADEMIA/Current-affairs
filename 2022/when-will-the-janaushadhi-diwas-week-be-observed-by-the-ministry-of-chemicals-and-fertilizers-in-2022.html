(B) 07 March to 13 March

(C) 08 March to 14 March

(D) 02 March to 08 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The main event of “Jan Aushadhi Diwas” will be held on 7th March 2022 (Monday) at Vigyan Bhawan, New Delhi. Union Minister of Health & Family Welfare and Chemicals & Fertilizers, Dr.

Concept note-2: -The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 celebrations commenced today. It will be a weeklong celebration starting today ie from 1st March to 7th March 2022. Jan Aushadhi kendras today conducted Jan Aushadhi Sankalp Padyatra at 75 locations, across the country.

Concept note-3: -Q. What is the theme of the Janaushadhi Diwas 2022?
