India ranked at which place in the HURUN Global U40 Self-Made Billionaires 2022 List?

(A) 2nd

(B) 3rd

(C) ** 4th

(D) 1st

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: India ranks 4th In The HURUN Global U40 Self-Made Billionaires 2022 List. Top 10 self-made billionaires under 40 Hurun Rich List 2022: Top 10 self-made billionaires under 40.

Concept note-2: -India jumps on 5th position as 20 companies feature in world’s valuable list: Hurun Global 500 List 2022. India’s rank jumps to 5th position from 9th position with 20 companies featured in the 2022 Hurun Global 500 List. These companies are based in Mumbai, Ahmedabad, Noida, New Delhi, Bengaluru, and Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -India cemented the 3rd spot with 215 billionaires, 38 more than last year. With US$103bn, Mukesh Ambani, 64, of Reliance is the richest person in India.

Concept note-4: -Gautam Adani has surged ahead to the top spot beating Mukesh Ambani with wealth more than doubling (116%) in the last year. He is ahead of the second-ranking Ambani by more than INR 3,00,000 crore.

Concept note-5: -Alexandr Wang is the youngest self-made billionaire as of November 2022, according to Forbes. At the age of 25, Wang is estimated to have a net worth of $1 billion. His San Francisco-based artificial intelligence company, Scale AI was valued at $7.3 billion earlier this year.
