Who has been conferred with the V Shantaram Lifetime achievement award at MIFF 2022?

(A) Sanjay Bisht

(B) Vikram Arora

(C) ** Sanjit Narweka

(D) Ranjeet Tiwari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 17th edition of the International Film Festival of India (MIFF 2022) confers Dr. V. Shantaram Lifetime Achievement Award on Shri Sanjit Narwekar. Shri Narwekar is a noted author and documentary filmmaker and the award recognizes his diverse and inspiring work.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Sanjit Narwekar. 17th edition of the Mumbai International Film Festival of India (MIFF 2022) has conferred Dr. V. Shantaram Lifetime Achievement Award to Sanjit Narwekar.

Concept note-3: -The Federation of Indian Chambers of Commerce & Industry (FICCI), felicitated Rajendra Singh Pawar, Chairman & Founder, NIIT with ‘Lifetime Achievement Award 2022’.

Concept note-4: -V Balakrishnan Wins Lifetime Achievement Award | Pradhana Vizha 2022 | Digital Exclusive - YouTube.

Concept note-5: -Celebrated Spanish film Director Carlos Saura has been honoured with the prestigious Satyajit Ray Lifetime Achievement award at the gala opening ceremony of 53rd edition of the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) in Goa today, 20 November, 2022.
