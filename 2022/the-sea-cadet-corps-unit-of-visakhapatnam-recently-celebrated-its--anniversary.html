The Sea Cadet Corps unit of Visakhapatnam recently celebrated its ____ anniversary?

(A) 25th

(B) ** 50th

(C) 75th

(D) 100th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sea Cadet Corps, Visakhapatnam unit, was commissioned on January 18, 1970, by Admiral A.K.

Concept note-2: -1929 First Sea Cadets The first open Sea Cadet Corps was formed in Christchurch, by the Canterbury Navy League. Units formed in the four main centres and were controlled nationally by the Canterbury branch of the League. The Sea Cadet motto is ‘Ready Aye Ready’.

Concept note-3: -The first Sea Cadet unit was established in 1854 at Whitstable in Kent, created by communities wanting to give young people instruction on a naval theme. Traditionally old seafarers provided training while local businessmen funded the Unit Headquarters.

Concept note-4: -Sea Cadet units are divided into three types: DIVISIONS – NSCC Divisions train primarily in the field of seamanship. SQUADRONS – NSCC Squadrons train primarily in the field of Aviation. BATTALIONS – NSCC Battalions train primarily in the field of Naval Construction.

Concept note-5: -The Sea Cadet Corps is a voluntary youth organisation that gives training to young school-going boys and girls in the age group of 10 to 12 years.
