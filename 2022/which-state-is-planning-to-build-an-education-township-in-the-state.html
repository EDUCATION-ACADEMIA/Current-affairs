Which state is planning to build an education township in the state?

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Haryana

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttar Pradesh government is planning to build an education township in the state. It will provide high-quality education to the youth and equip them with a variety of professional skills in a single place.

Concept note-2: -World Environment Day: Auroville, India’s First Experimental Township Is Showing The Path Of Green Living. On World Environment Day, we present to you Auroville a community in Pondicherry where there ain’t no possessions and people of all nationalities live in harmony.

Concept note-3: -Rajiv Gandhi Education City is a planned education city in Sonipat in the Indian state of Haryana. The city comes under the National Capital Region, Delhi and also under the Municipal Corporation and Urban Agglomeration of Sonipat City.

Concept note-4: -After Kerala, Tamil Nadu is also regarded as the most literate state of the nation. Due to an improved system of Education in Tamil Nadu, maximum people of the state have acquired their literacy level.
