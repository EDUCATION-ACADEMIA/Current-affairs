‘Heal by India’ is an initiative by the Government of India to improve India’s educational institutions with a focus on which sector?

(A) ** Health

(B) Infrastructure

(C) Tourism

(D) Agriculture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government of India is looking to promote ‘Heal by India’ for strengthening the country’s educational institutions in the health sector and enhancing education to churn out quality human resources in health, thus helping them become eligible to work anywhere in the world.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Health and Family Welfare (MoHFW) is launching the “Heal in India” initiative, a pan-India initiative catering to citizens of world.

Concept note-3: -It aims to reduce premature mortality from cancer, cardiovascular diseases, chronic respiratory diseases and diabetes by 25% within 2025. This policy recognises the importance of sustainable development and time-bound quantitative goals.
