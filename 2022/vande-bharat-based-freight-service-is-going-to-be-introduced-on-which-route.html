Vande-Bharat-based freight service is going to be introduced on which route?

(A) Delhi-Katra (Jammu)

(B) Delhi-Varanasi

(C) Gandhinagar-Mumbai

(D) ** Delhi-Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -If Indian Railways uses the Vande Bharat trains for freight transportation, the train will be run on the Delhi-Mumbai route first with a comparatively higher speed to reduce transportation time.

Concept note-2: -The two new Vande Bharat Express trains will operate on the Mumbai-Solapur and Mumbai-Shirdi routes. The new Vande Bharat Express trains will commence from the Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus (CSMT). While one will take the Mumbai-Pune-Solapur route, the other will take the Mumbai-Nashik-Sainagar Shirdi route.

Concept note-3: -Indian Railways after introducing the semi-high-speed train Vande Bharat on the Mumbai-Ahmedabad route is looking to launch a freight version of the train.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai gets its first Vande Bharat on September 30, 2022. The train operates between Gandhinagar Capital and Mumbai Central.
