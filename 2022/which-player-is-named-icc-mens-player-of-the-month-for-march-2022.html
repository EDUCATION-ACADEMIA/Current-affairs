Which player is named ICC Men’s Player of the Month for March 2022?

(A) ** Babar Azam

(B) David Warner

(C) Keegan Petersen

(D) Shreyas Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Virat Kohli scored an unbeaten 53-ball 82* to help India secure an opening victory against Pakistan in their ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 encounter. Virat Kohli’s brilliant form with the bat en-route to the semi-finals of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup earned him the monthly award.

Concept note-2: -It was a double delight for Pakistan skipper Babar Azam as he was picked for both the ‘ODI Cricketer of the Year’ and the ‘Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for the ICC Men’s Cricketer of 2022’ on January 26, 2023.

Concept note-3: -ICC POTM Veteran Sri Lanka star has been voted ICC Men’s Player of the Month for May 2022. Angelo Mathews beat off competition from fellow countryman Asitha Fernando and Bangladesh star Mushfiqur Rahim to win the award.

Concept note-4: -India batter Virat Kohli has been named the ICC Men’s Player of the Month for October 2022 after some outstanding performances in the shortest format of the game.
