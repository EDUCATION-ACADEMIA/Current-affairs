NASSCOM partners with which country agency to attract investment for Indian tech SMEs?

(A) ** Australia

(B) Russia

(C) Brazil

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NASSCOM partners with Australian govt agency to attract investment for Indian tech SMEs.

Concept note-2: -As a part of the shared goal with Government of India, NASSCOM is focussing heavily on creating suitable platforms to explore opportunities in “high potential but under-penetrated markets”.

Concept note-3: -UNICEF is proud to partner with NASSCOM Foundation to develop child rights online. The ongoing focus of the partnership will be on supporting online safety and skilling adolescents by engaging key technology businesses through the Foundation’s active network of companies.

Concept note-4: -Our mission is to Reskill and Upskill India’s workforce to ensure talent is Future Ready in terms of New Age Skills and Jobs and to make India a global hub for Digital Talent. SSC NASSCOM catalyzing the IT-ITeS Skills development ecosystem, enabling India to be the global hub for Digital talent.

Concept note-5: -NASSCOM is the premier trade body and chamber of commerce of the Tech industry in India and comprises over 3000 member companies including both Indian and multinational organisations that have a presence in India.
