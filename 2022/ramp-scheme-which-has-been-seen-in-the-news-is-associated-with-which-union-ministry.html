RAMP scheme, which has been seen in the news, is associated with which Union Ministry?

(A) ** Ministry of MSME

(B) Ministry of Commerce and Industry

(C) Ministry of Education

(D) Ministry of Agriculture

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. RAMP scheme, which was seen in the news, is implemented by which Union Ministry? Notes: MSME Minister Narayan Rane launched the web portal for the Rs 6,062.45-crore Raising and Accelerating MSME Performance (RAMP) for MSMEs.

Concept note-2: -Union Minister Narayan Rane launches web portal for RAMP scheme. Agartala, Jan 9 (KNN) The web portal for the World Bank-assisted Raising and Accelerating MSME Performance (RAMP) scheme of the central government was launched by MSME Minister Narayan Rane on Monday.

Concept note-3: -Aim of RAMP The program’s aims include enhancing center-state cooperation and linkages, enhancing MSMEs’ access to markets and credit, modernizing technology, addressing concerns with late payments, and helping MSMEs become more environmentally friendly.

Concept note-4: -Government has launched a World Bank supported Central Sector Scheme called Raising and Accelerating MSME Performance (RAMP).

Concept note-5: -To support India’s Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSME) sector, the Prime Minister introduced the five-year “Raising and Accelerating MSME Performance” (RAMP) program on June 30, 2022.
