What is the projected GDP Growth rate of India in FY23?

(A) 8.2 %

(B) ** 6. 0 %

(C) 7.5 %

(D) 6.9 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian economy is estimated to grow at 7 percent in 2022-23, as against the expansion of 8.7 percent in the previous fiscal, the National Statistical Office (NSO) said on Friday.

Concept note-2: -India’s GDP had grown at 8.7% in the year ending 31 March, 2022. The country’s nominal GDP growth for FY23 is estimated at 15.4% as against 19.5% in FY22, the Ministry of Statistics & Programme Implementation said in a statement.

Concept note-3: -The economic survey forecast India’s economy to grow at 6.5 per cent in 2023-24, compared to 7 per cent this fiscal and 8.7 per cent in 2021-22. The 6.5 per cent means India’s economic growth next fiscal year will still be the fastest among major economies.

Concept note-4: -The Real GDP growth for 2023-24 is projected at 6.4 percent with Q1 at 7.8%, Q2 at 6.2%, Q3 at 6% & Q4 at 5.8% :RBI Governor Shaktikanta Das.
