The 2028 Summer Olympic and Paralympic Games will be hosted in which country?

(A) New York, United States

(B) ** Los Angeles, United States

(C) Moscow, Russia

(D) Beijing, China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Session of the International Olympic Committee (IOC) meeting in Lima, Peru, elected Paris as host city of the Games of the XXXIII Olympiad 2024 and Los Angeles as the host city of the Games of the XXXIV Olympiad 2028. “Congratulations to Paris 2024 and Los Angeles 2028!
