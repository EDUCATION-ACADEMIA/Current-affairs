Which film has won the “Best Feature Film” at the 68th National Film Awards?

(A) Testimony of Anna

(B) ** Soorarai Pottru

(C) Manah Aru Manuh

(D) Tanhaji

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The winners for the 68th National Awards were announced in a press conference in July. Tamil feature Soorarai Pottru was named the best film of the year. The late Sachidanandan KR, popularly known as Sachy, won the Best Director award for his Malayalam movie Ayyappanum Koshiyum.

Concept note-2: -Suriya’s superhit Tamil film ‘Soorarai Pottru’ also received the Best Feature Film award and the Best Actress award was taken by Aparna Murali. ‘Soorarai Pottru’ was the big winner of this year’s National Awards.

Concept note-3: -68th National Film Awards 2022: S Thaman, Sandhya Raju, Sandeep Raj, Sai Rajesh Neelam and TV Rambabu receive top honours. President of India Droupadi Murmu honoured the winners of the 68th National Film Awards at a ceremony held in New Delhi’s Vigyan Bhavan.

Concept note-4: -68th National Film Awards: Aroh Welankar’s ‘Funral’ wins the best film for social issues. The 68th National Film Awards for the year 2022 was announced in July, and many Marathi films, ‘Me Vasantrao’, ‘Sumi’ and ‘Goshta Eka Paithanichi’ have been honoured with the esteemed award.

Concept note-5: -68th National Film Awards 2022 Winners: Ajay Devgn, Suriya receive Best Actor awards - check out the complete winners’ list | - Times of India.
