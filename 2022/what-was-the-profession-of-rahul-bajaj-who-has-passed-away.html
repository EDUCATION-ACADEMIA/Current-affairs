What was the profession of Rahul Bajaj, who has passed away?

(A) ** Industrialist

(B) Actor

(C) Journalist

(D) Economist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bajaj stepped down from his role in 2005 and his son Rajiv became the Group’s managing director. Bajaj led the split of Bajaj Auto into three units in 2008: Bajaj Auto, Bajaj Finserv and a holding company. He resigned as chairman and non-executive director of Bajaj Finserv to become chairman emeritus in March 2019.

Concept note-2: -Cyrus P. Mistry, industrialist and former chairman of Tata Sons, was killed in a road accident about 150 km from Mumbai on Sunday when the car he was travelling in hit a road divider.

Concept note-3: -Bajaj Group is building on the legacy left by patriarch Rahul Bajaj, who died in February at age 83. The family climbed to No. 10 on India’s 100 richest list with its combined net worth of $14.6 billion.
