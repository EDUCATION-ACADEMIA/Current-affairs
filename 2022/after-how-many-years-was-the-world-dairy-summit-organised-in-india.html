After how many years was the World Dairy Summit organised in India?

(A) 58 years

(B) ** 48 years

(C) 50 years

(D) 40 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Minister Purshottam Rupala, addressing the World Diary Summit 2022, said that it has been organised in India after 48 years. “World Diary Summit has been organized after over 48 years in India.

Concept note-2: -India hosted the World Dairy Summit 2022 (WDS 2022) by International Dairy Federation at Indian Expo Centre and Mart, India Exposition Mart Limited, Greater Noida. The conference was organised from 12th September 2022 to 15th September 2022.

Concept note-3: -World Dairy Summit 2022: Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the International Dairy Federation’s World Dairy Summit (IDF WDS) 2022 at the India Expo Center and Mart in Greater Noida.

Concept note-4: -September 12, 2022 10:00 am - September 15, 2022 6:00 pm at India Expo Centre & Mart. The IDF World Dairy Summit is an annual meeting of the global dairy sector, bringing together approximately 1500 participants from all over the world.

Concept note-5: -The theme for this year is Dairy for Nutrition and Livelihood. The IDF World Dairy Summit will provide a forum to industry experts to share knowledge and ideas on how the sector can contribute to nourish the world with safe and sustainable dairying.
