Which tennis player won ATP Fans’ Favourite Award 2022?

(A) Rohan Bhopanna

(B) Corlos Moya

(C) ** Rafael Nadal

(D) Andrey Rublev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rafael Nadal : The 36-year-old wins the fan-voted ATP Award for the first time, adding to his full set of Awards in the player-voted categories: Newcomer, Most Improved, Comeback and Sportsmanship.

Concept note-2: -Rafael Nadal’s historic 2022 ATP Tour season has ended with the Spaniard treading yet more new ground - the 36-year-old has been voted as Fans’ Favourite for the first time in the ATP Awards.

Concept note-3: -It was the second consecutive year that Alcaraz had been nominated for Most Improved Player in the ATP Awards. His fellow nominees in the category this year were Maxime Cressy, Jack Draper and Holger Rune.

Concept note-4: -Rafael Nadal wins ATP Fans’ Favorite Award for first time, ending Roger Federer’s streak. Federer had been voted Fans’ Favorite singles player the last 19 years in a row, between 2003 and 2021.
