Which Union Minister has recently launched the ‘India Semiconductor Mission’?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Ashwini Vaishnav

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India Aims To Become Key Semiconductor Supplier For World: Union Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: Information Technology Minister Ashwini Vaishnav has launched India Semiconductor Mission to attract large investments for setting up semiconductor wafer fabrication facilities in India.

Concept note-3: -In December 2021, the IT ministry had unveiled the semiconductor fabrication incentive plan as a part of which the central government would make large, upfront investments – 50% fiscal support as per recent modifications – to back the semiconductor wafer fab proposals by various corporate entities.

Concept note-4: -india semiconductor mission: Govt open to investing more in the India Semiconductor Mission : Ashwini Vaishnaw - The Economic Times.

Concept note-5: -Information Technology Minister Ashwini Vaishnaw launched the India Semiconductor Mission on Wednesday.
