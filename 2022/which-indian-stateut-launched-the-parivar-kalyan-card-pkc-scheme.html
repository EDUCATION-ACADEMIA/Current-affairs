Which Indian state/UT launched the Parivar Kalyan Card (PKC) Scheme?

(A) Bihar

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Parivar Kalyan Card scheme is the Uttar Pradesh government’s Family ID plan. It was announced to establish a comprehensive database of the state’s family units and identify beneficiaries for its various schemes.

Concept note-2: -[C]. [D]. Explanation: Parivar Kalyan Card scheme is the Uttar Pradesh government’s Family ID plan.

Concept note-3: -UP Parivar Kalyan Card will provide a unique 12-digit ID number for each family, and the initial collection of data will be done through ration cards.

Concept note-4: -Recently, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath had talked about issuing ‘UP Parivar Kalyan Card’ soon for all the families of the state. Under this, the benefits of various welfare schemes running in the state can be availed. This will also help those families who are deprived of the schemes due to any reason.

Concept note-5: -Now with the family card, the state government will be able to identify families who need an employment source. The aim of this card is to integrate all the other government services through this to make the process simpler.
