Which state has won a coveted UN Award-WSIS prize at Geneva in Switzerland?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Odisha

(D) Nagaland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Meghalaya government key initiative of e-proposal system, part of Meghalaya Enterprise Architect has won a coveted UN Award- World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) prize at Geneva in Switzerland on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -Q. Which state’s ‘e-Proposal system’ has won the ‘Champion Project’ award in UN-WSIS Prizes 2022? Notes: The ‘e-Proposal system’ of Meghalaya’s Planning department has won the prestigious UN Award-World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) Prizes 2022.

Concept note-3: -“Meghalaya was awarded for Best Practice in Advocacy, Communication and Social Mobilization in Tuberculosis control at the National Workshop on ACSM to end TB in India,” he stated.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Meghalaya. Meghalaya’s Planning Department’s key initiative of the e-Proposal System has won UN Award – World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) Prizes 2022.

Concept note-5: -Meghalaya recently won the ‘Best Project’ award at the United Nations World Summit on the Information Society (WSIS) held in Geneva for its paperless governance initiative, called ‘e-Proposal System’.
