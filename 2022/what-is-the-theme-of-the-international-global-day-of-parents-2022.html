What is the theme of the International Global Day of Parents 2022?

(A) Family Awareness

(B) Families and Urbanization

(C) ** Appreciate all parents throughout the world

(D) Honor Your Parents!

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Day of Parents is commemorated on June 1 every year. ‘Appreciate all parents throughout the world’ is the theme for Global Day of Parents, 2022.

Concept note-2: -What is the meaning of Global Day of Parents? Global Day of Parents is a United Nations observance that is celebrated on June 1 each year to honor parents and their commitment to children worldwide.

Concept note-3: -Globally, June 1st is designated as the Global Day of Parents. In the United States, Parents’ Day is celebrated annually on the fourth Sunday of July.

Concept note-4: -The theme for Global Parent Day 2022 is ‘Family Awareness’, awareness internationally. Being conscious of one’s own and one’s family’s emotional and physical well-being might be critical.
