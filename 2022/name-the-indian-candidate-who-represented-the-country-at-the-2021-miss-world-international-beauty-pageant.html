Name the Indian candidate who represented the country at the 2021 Miss World international beauty pageant?

(A) Shree Saini

(B) Suman Rao

(C) Vartika Singh

(D) ** Manasa Varanasi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manasa Varanasi is an Indian model, engineer and beauty pageant title holder who was crowned Femina Miss India 2020. She represented India at the Miss World 2021 pageant in San Juan, Puerto Rico and finished as a Top 13 semifinalist.

Concept note-2: -At the end of the event, Toni-Ann Singh of Jamaica crowned Karolina Bielawska of Poland as Miss World 2021.

Concept note-3: -Miss World 2021: India’s Manasa Varanasi was included in the top 13 contestants’ list. New Delhi: Poland’s Karoline Bielawska has been crowned Miss World 2021 in the pageant held in Puerto Rico where India’s Manasa Varanasi failed to make it to the top 6.

Concept note-4: -At the end of the event, Andrea Meza of Mexico crowned Harnaaz Sandhu of India as Miss Universe 2021. It is India’s first victory in 21 years, and the third victory of the country in the pageant’s history.
