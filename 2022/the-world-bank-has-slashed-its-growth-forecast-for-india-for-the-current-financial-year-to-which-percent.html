The World Bank has slashed its growth forecast for India for the current financial year to which percent?

(A) 5.5

(B) 6.5

(C) 8.5

(D) ** 7.5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Bank cuts India’s GDP growth forecast to 6.5% for 2022-23 | Mint.

Concept note-2: -World Bank revises India’s GDP forecast to 6.9% for FY23 due to robust economic activities. The World Bank has revised India’s 2022-23 GDP growth forecast upward to 6.9 per cent from the earlier estimate of 6.5 per cent, due to robust economic activities according to Dhruv Sharma, Senior Economist at the World Bank.

Concept note-3: -World Bank revises India’s FY 2022-23 GDP growth forecast to 6.9% - BusinessToday.

Concept note-4: -The growth in real GDP during 2022-23 is estimated at 7 per cent as compared to 8.7 per cent in 2021-22, it stated.
