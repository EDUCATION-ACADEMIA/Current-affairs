Who of the following won the 2022 India Open Women’s Singles Title?

(A) PV Sindhu

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) ** Busanan Ongbamrungphan

(D) Supanida Katethong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In India Open Badminton Championship, Korean sensation An Seyoung wins Women’s singles final today at K. D. Jadhav Indoor Stadium in New Delhi. In finals, An Seyoung beat World No. 1 Japanese Akane Yamaguchi 15-21, 21-16, 21-12.

Concept note-2: -Men’s singles player Lakshya Sen and the men’s doubles pair of Chirag Shetty and Satwiksairaj Ranklireddy entered the tournament as the reigning champions, having won their respective events in the 2022 edition.

Concept note-3: -India wins Thomas Cup The Indian team, comprising Kidambi Srikanth, H.S. Prannoy, Lakshya Sen (singles) and Sawtiksairaj Rankireddy-Chirag Shetty in doubles, defeated five-time champion Malaysia and 2016 champion Denmark to win the prestigious title.

Concept note-4: -Denmark’s Viktor Axelsen won his second badminton world title on Sunday, beating Thailand’s Kunlavut Vitidsarn 21-5, 21-16 in the final in Tokyo.
