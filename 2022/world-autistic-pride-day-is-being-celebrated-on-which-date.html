World Autistic Pride Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 17

(B) June 19

(C) June 16

(D) ** June 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Autistic Pride Day is a pride celebration for autistic people held on 18 June each year. Autistic pride recognises the importance of pride for autistic people and its role in bringing about positive changes in the broader society.

Concept note-2: -Autistic Pride Day is celebrated on June 18 every year across the world to raise awareness about Autism Spectrum Disorder (ASD) and to move society away from discrimination and stigma associated with the condition.
