Which Indian economist has been included in the advisory board of the United Nations?

(A) Rohini Pande

(B) Arvind Panagariya

(C) Kaushik Basu

(D) ** Jayati Ghosh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Jayati Ghosh. Indian development economist Jayati Ghosh has been appointed by UN Secretary-General Antonio Guterres to a new high-level advisory board on effective multilateralism. She is a professor at the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian Economist has been chosen to be a part of UN high-level advisory board on socio-economic challenges in post-COVID-19 world? Notes: India’s economist Jayati Ghosh has been chosen to be a part of UN high-level advisory board on socio-economic challenges in post-COVID-19 world.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Indian has been appointed to UN Advisory Board on Effective Multilateralism? Notes: Jayati Ghosh has been appointed by UN Secretary General Antonio Guterres to the Advisory Board on Effective Multilateralism.

Concept note-4: -Manmohan Singh The most famous economist and politician, who served as the Prime Minister of India from 2004 to 2014, is a recipient of the Bharat Ratna. He is also credited as the architect of the 1991 Indian economic reforms.
