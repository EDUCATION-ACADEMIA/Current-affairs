Who has won the women’s singles title at Swiss Open 2022?

(A) ** PV Sindhu

(B) Sania Mirza

(C) Saina Nehwal

(D) Mary Kom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian badminton star PV Sindhu won Swiss Open 2022 women’s singles title with a win over Thailand’s Busanan Ongramrungphan at the St. Jakobshalle arena in Basel on Sunday. Double Olympic medallist PV Sindhu won the final match 21-16, 21-8 in 49 minutes to claim the Super 300 crown.

Concept note-2: -Norway’s Casper Ruud has won the Swiss Open Tennis Tournament 2022 held in Gstaad, Switzerland. He defeated Italy’s Matteo Berrettini in the final by 4-6, 7-6(4), 6-2.
