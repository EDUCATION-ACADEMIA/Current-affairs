Which country has approved the proposal on the ‘Way Finding Application’ to be used at the United Nations Office?

(A) UAE

(B) ** India

(C) USA

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: India’s Union Cabinet has approved the proposal on the agreement between the Government of India and the United Nations on a ‘Way Finding Application’ to be used in the United Nations Office at Geneva (UNOG). The Office is housed at the historic Palais des Nations .

Concept note-2: -Aim: It aims to facilitate navigation in the Palais des Nations premises of United Nations Office at Geneva (UNOG). The application will enable users to find their way from point to point within the 21 floors spread across five buildings of UNOG.

Concept note-3: -India is a charter member of the United Nations and participates in all of its specialised agencies and organizations.

Concept note-4: -United Nations Secretary-General António Guterres announced on 17 February 2021 the appointment of Usha Rao-Monari of India as Under-Secretary-General and Associate Administrator of the United Nations Development Programme (UNDP).
