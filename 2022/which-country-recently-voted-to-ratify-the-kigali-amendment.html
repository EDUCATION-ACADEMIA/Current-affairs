Which country recently voted to ratify the Kigali Amendment?

(A) India

(B) ** USA

(C) UK

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -US ratifies Kigali Amendment, the international climate deal on refrigerants.

Concept note-2: -On Wednesday, October 26, 2022, the President signed the instrument of ratification of the Kigali Amendment to the Montreal Protocol on Substances that Deplete the Ozone Layer. Thank you to Senators Menendez, Risch, Carper, and Kennedy, and Leaders Schumer and McConnell, for their leadership.

Concept note-3: -Important Facts about Kigali Agreement It happened during the 28th Meeting of Parties when the 197 member countries signed the agreement to amend the Montreal Protocol.

Concept note-4: -Article 5 Parties are divided into two groups: Group 1: The majority of Article 5 Parties. Group 2: Bahrain, India, Iran, Iraq, Kuwait, Oman, Pakistan, Qatar, Saudi Arabia, and the United Arab Emirates. Group 2 has a later freeze and phase-down steps compared with Group 1.

Concept note-5: -In strong bipartisan vote, Senate approves first climate treaty in decades. WASHINGTON – U.S. Senator Chris Coons (D-Del.), co-chair of the Climate Solutions Caucus, voted today to ratify the Kigali Amendment to the Montreal Protocol.
