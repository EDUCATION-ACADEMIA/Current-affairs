The Khuvsgul Lake National Park is located in which country?

(A) India

(B) Nigeria

(C) ** Mongolia

(D) Bulgaria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lake Khuvsgul is located in the northwest of Mongolia near the Russian border, at the foot of the eastern Sayan Mountains. It is 1,645 metres (5,397 feet) above sea level, 136 kilometres (85 miles) long and 262 metres (860 feet) deep.

Concept note-2: -Mongolia’s Khuvsgul lake added to UNESCO World Network of Biosphere Reserves. Khuvsul Lake National Park of Mangolia has been added to the World Network of Biosphere Reserve of UNESCO.

Concept note-3: -Ulaanbaatar [Mongolia], June 16 (ANI/Xinhua): Mongolia’s Khuvsgul Lake National Park has been added to the World Network of Biosphere Reserves of UNESCO, the Ministry of Environment and Tourism said Thursday.

Concept note-4: -Mongolia is dotted with hundreds of lakes, the largest being Lake Uvs (covering an area of 3,350 square kilometers), Lake Khovsgol (2.620 km ²) and Lake Khar Us (1.852 km ²).
