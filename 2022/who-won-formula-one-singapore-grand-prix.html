Who won Formula One Singapore Grand Prix?

(A) Pankaj Advani

(B) Michael Schumacher

(C) Michael Kors

(D) ** Sergio Perez

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Red Bull’s Sergio Perez won the Singapore Grand Prix at Marina Bay on Sunday, a rain-delayed Formula 1 race punctuated by safety cars for multiple crashes and breakdowns. Perez beat polesitter Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc to the first corner and dominated the race until the intermediate-shod field switched to slick tyres.

Concept note-2: -Max Verstappen led the Drivers’ Championship by 116 points from Charles Leclerc, second, and teammate Sergio Pérez, third, by 125. Red Bull Racing team led the Constructors’ Championship, leading Ferrari by 139 points and Mercedes by 164 points. Pos.

Concept note-3: -Sergio Perez held off Charles Leclerc for his first-ever win on the streets of Singapore, while Carlos Sainz completed the podium and Max Verstappen finished seventh in an action-packed Singapore Grand Prix. Perez retained his victory despite receiving a five-second post-race penalty for a Safety Car infringement.

Concept note-4: -Max Verstappen formally crowned 2022 Formula 1 world champion: ‘This was better than 2021 title’ | F1 News.

Concept note-5: -Verstappen wins the Abu Dhabi GP with Leclerc second And that’s that: the Dutchman saunters over the finish line for his 15th victory of a frankly ludicrous season.
