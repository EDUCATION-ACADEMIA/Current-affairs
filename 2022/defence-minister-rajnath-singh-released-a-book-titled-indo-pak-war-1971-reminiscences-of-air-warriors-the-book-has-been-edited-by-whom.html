(A) Jagjeet Singh

(B) Shailendra Mohan

(C) Amitava Kumar

(D) ** Both A & B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indo-Pak War 1971: Reminiscences of Air Warriors (Hardcover, Air Marshal Jagjeet Singh PVSM VSM (Retd), Group Captain Shailendra Mohan (Retd)) Safe and Secure Payments. Easy returns. 100% Authentic products.

Concept note-2: -On the occasion, the Raksha Mantri also released a book titled ‘INDO-PAK WAR 1971- Reminiscences of Air Warriors’.
