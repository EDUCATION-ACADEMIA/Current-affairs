Who has been elected as the new President of Iraq?

(A) Ali Allawi

(B) JumaInad

(C) ** Abdul Latif Rashid

(D) Fuad Hussein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The prime minister of Iraq is the head of government of Iraq. On 27 October 2022, Mohammed Shia’ Al Sudani became the incumbent prime minister.

Concept note-2: -Abdul Latif Rashid (Arabic: ; born 10 August 1944), also known as Latif Rashid (Kurdish: ) is an Iraqi Kurdish politician and the ninth president of Iraq, following the 2022 Iraqi presidential election. He was previously the Minister of Water Resources under the government of Nouri al-Maliki.

Concept note-3: -The current Prime Minister of Iraq is Mohammed Shia al-Sudani, who holds most of the executive authority and appointed the Council of Ministers, which acts as a cabinet and/or government.
