‘INS Nirdeshak’ Vessel has been launched by which company?

(A) BHEL

(B) BEML

(C) NHPC

(D) ** GRSE

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Nirdeshak, the second of the four Survey Vessels (Large) (SVL) Project being built by GRSE in collaboration with L&T shipbuilding for Indian Navy was launched on 26 May 22 at Kattupalli, Chennai.

Concept note-2: -Nirdeshak is the second ship in the four survey vessel project under construction by Kolkata-based Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers (GRSE) in association with L&T Shipbuilding. The first class ship ‘Sandhyak’ was launched on 5 December 2021 at Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers (GRSE), Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -Aim is to make India an indigenous ship building hub: RM. Indian Naval Ship (INS) Mormugao (D67), second warship of the P15B class of stealth guided-missile destroyers, was commissioned in the august presence of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh at Naval Dockyard, Mumbai on December 18, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The first of class ship ‘Sandhayak’ was launched on 5 December, 2021. Survey vessels -large (SVL) ships will replace the existing Sandhayak Class survey ships with new generation hydrographic equipment to collect oceanographic data.

Concept note-5: -’Ikshak’ is the third of the four Survey Vessels (Large SVL) Project, being built by GRSE/L&T for the Indian Navy was launched on 26th November 2022 at Kattupalli, Chennai. Ikshak made her first contact with the water of the Bay of Bengal at 1040 hours.
