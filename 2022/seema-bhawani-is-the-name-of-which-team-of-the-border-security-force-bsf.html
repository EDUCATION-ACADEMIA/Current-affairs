‘Seema Bhawani’ is the name of which team of the Border Security Force (BSF)?

(A) Creek Crocodile

(B) All-women Creek Crocodile

(C) Camel Contingent

(D) ** All-women Biker Team

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New event by Seema Bhawani team. ,

Concept note-2: -The Border Security Force’s (BSF) all-women daredevil motorcycle team, ‘Seema Bhawani’, made history at New Delhi’s Kartavya Path in December by vrooming their way into the Limca Book of Records – an annual reference book published in India, which documents world records held by Indians.

Concept note-3: -K.F. Rustamji, IPS was appointed as the first Director General of Border Security Force from 22 July 1965 to 30 September 1972 and the current Director General is Pankaj Singh, IPS, since 1 September 2021.

Concept note-4: -Border Security Force (BSF) Protecting India’s internal security by protecting India’s international borders with Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Myanmar.

Concept note-5: -BSF is the biggest Force after CRPF and also takes part in internal security situations. For example it is present in naxalites belts, north east etc. SSB on the other hand is border guarding force for Nepal and Bhutan Border. The Border is relatively peaceful and the boundary clearly demarcated.
