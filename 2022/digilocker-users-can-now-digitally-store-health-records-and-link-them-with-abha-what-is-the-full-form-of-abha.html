DigiLocker users can now digitally store health records and link them with ABHA. What is the full form of ABHA?

(A) Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission

(B) Ayushman Bharat Health Mission Account

(C) Ayushman Bharat Digital Health Mission Account

(D) ** Ayushman Bharat Health Account

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DigiLocker users can now digitally store health records and link them with Ayushman Bharat Health Account (ABHA)

Concept note-2: -Each citizen can avail a cloud space of up to 1 GB for storing their documents on DigiLocker. DigiLocker is a cloud based document storage system of the Government of India. Using this facility, one can store important documents such as PAN cards, marksheets, insurance policies, certificates, driving license, etc.

Concept note-3: -DigiLocker is a flagship initiative of Ministry of Electronics & IT (MeitY) under Digital India programme. DigiLocker aims at ‘Digital Empowerment’ of citizen by providing access to authentic digital documents to citizen’s digital document wallet.
