What is the theme of National Education Day 2022?

(A) Education of women

(B) ** Changing Course, Transforming Education

(C) Compulsory universal primary education

(D) Free and fair education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, the National Education Day 2022 theme is “Changing Course, Transforming Education.” The theme is set this year to highlight the urgent need to reform the Indian education system as per the current requirements.

Concept note-2: -Building on the global momentum generated by the UN Transforming Education Summit in September 2022, this year’s Day will call for maintaining strong political mobilization around education and chart the way to translate commitments and global initiatives into action.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: National Education Day is celebrated on November 11 every year to commemorate the birth anniversary and contributions of Maulana Abul Kalam Azad, the first education minister of India towards education. The theme of National Education Day 2022 is “Changing Course, Transforming Education.”

Concept note-4: -The fifth International Day of Education will be celebrated on 24 January 2023 under the theme “to invest in people, prioritize education”.

Concept note-5: -The new NEP is based on four pillars which are Access, Equity, Quality, and Accountability. In this new policy, there will be a 5+3+3+4 structure which comprises 12 years of school and 3 years of Anganwadi/ pre-school replacing old 10+2 structure.
