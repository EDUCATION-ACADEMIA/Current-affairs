Which country has topped the Asian Cycling Championships 2022?

(A) America

(B) ** Japan

(C) Australia

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asian Track Cycling Championships: India finish with best-ever nine senior medals.

Concept note-2: -In the just-concluded Asian Track Cycling Championships 2022 at the Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium, New Delhi, the Indian cycling team finished won 23 combined medals (two gold, six silver and 15 bronze), which is the best-ever performance by the country at the championships.

Concept note-3: -Shiva Thapa’s silver also meant India finished the Asian Boxing Championships 2022 with 12 medals, four golds, two silvers and six bronze.

Concept note-4: -Track Asia Cup-2022, one of the premier cycling competitions, will be held at the LNCPE Outdoor Velodrome in Kerala from November 25 to 28. The event will see participation of over 200 cyclists from over 25 Asian countries and will be held outside Delhi for the first time in history.

Concept note-5: -Remco Evenepoel wins men’s elite race at UCI Road World Championships 2022 - results. The Belgian makes history with a first elite world title win in Wollongong, Australia, weeks after winning his first Grand Tours overall title at the Vuelta a Espana.
