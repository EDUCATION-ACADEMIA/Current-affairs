Which airport in India is set to get the status of power positive airport of India in March 2022?

(A) Goa International Airport

(B) ** Cochin International Airport

(C) Kempegowda International Airport

(D) Surat International Airport

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cochin International Airport, world’s 1st airport powered by solar energy, is set to become power positive with the commissioning of its new solar power plant near Payyannur in Kannur district of Kerala.

Concept note-2: -Delhi Airport becomes the first airport in the nation to function solely on hydro and solar energy.

Concept note-3: -CIAL (Cochin International Airport Ltd) is the world’s first airport fully powered by solar energy. The Cochin International Airport has been a torchbearer from its inception on many accounts including the solar power project which prompted many other airports to move the solar way.

Concept note-4: -The Cochin International Airport became the world’s first fully solar-powered airport. It is situated in the city of Kochi, Kerala.
