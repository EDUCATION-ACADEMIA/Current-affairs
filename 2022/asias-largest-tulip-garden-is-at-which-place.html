Asia’s largest Tulip Garden is at which place?

(A) Delhi

(B) Ladakh

(C) ** Srinagar

(D) Chennai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip garden, previously Model Floriculture Center, is a tulip garden in Srinagar, Jammu and Kashmir, India. It is the largest tulip garden in Asia spread over an area of about 30 ha (74 acres).

Concept note-2: -With 20 lakh tulips of 46 varieties in its lap, Asia’s largest Tulip garden overlooking the world famous Dal Lake in Srinagar. It was thrown open to visitors and is spread over an area of about 15 hectares, the garden is situated on the foothills of Zabarwan range.

Concept note-3: -Keukenhof, the Netherlands Also known as the Garden of Europe, Keukenhof is among the largest flower gardens in the world, situated in Lisse, the Netherlands. The garden hsts an incredible collection of tulips. This garden is spread over an area of 79 acres and remains open to tourists from March till May.

Concept note-4: -The largest botanical garden of Asia is Acharya Jagadish Chandra Bose Indian Botanic Garden located at Shibpur, Howrah India.
