Who scored a crucial victory for India against Hungary at the 44th Chess Olympiad?

(A) Koneru Humpy

(B) Harika Dronavalli

(C) Suhani Shah

(D) ** Tania Sachdev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tania Sachdev battled long and hard to rake in a precious point as India A registered a sensational 2.5-1.5 win against Hungary in the fourth-round match of women section at the 44th Chess Olympiad in Mamallapuram, Chennai on Monday.

Concept note-2: -Uzbekistan won the gold medal in the open event, with a total of 19 match points. Their eight wins and three draws made them the only undefeated team in the tournament.

Concept note-3: -India competed in the tournament with three teams in the Men’s Section and three teams in the Women’s section. India ‘B’ team comprising D Gukesh, R Praggnanandhaa, Raunak Sadhwaniand and Adhiban won the bronze medal beating Germany. In women’s section, India ‘A’ team have won the bronze medal.

Concept note-4: -Tania Sachdev shines as Indian women’s team win at 44th Chess Olympiad.

Concept note-5: -D Gukesh and Nihal Sarin won the gold medals on the men’s board 1 and board 2, respectively, while Arjun Erigaisi bagged the silver medal on board 3.
