Who has launched the LiFE Movement, a global initiative?

(A) ** Narendra Modi

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) Ramnath Kovind

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Leaders Applaud India for Focusing on Individual Behaviour Change towards Climate Change. Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi launched the ‘Lifestyle for the Environment (LiFE) Movement’ today via video conferencing.

Concept note-2: -Mr Aniruddha Dasgupta, CEO and President of World Resources Institute also thanked the Prime Minister for a much needed global movement and conversation on how we live, how we consume and how we take care of the planet.

Concept note-3: -First proposed by the Prime Minister at COP 26, Mission LiFE is envisioned as an India-led global mass movement that will nudge individual and collective action to protect and preserve the environment. The Prime Minister and UNSG unveiled the LiFE logo and tagline, and released the Mission Document at the event.

Concept note-4: -An India-led global mass movement to nudge individual and community action to preserve the environment. Over the last two decades, several macro measures have been implemented globally to address environmental degradation and climate change, including policy reforms, economic incentives and regulations.
