Which day has been declared as the International Day of Women and Girls in Science?

(A) February 12

(B) February 09

(C) ** February 11

(D) February 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Royal Academy of Science International Trust (RASIT) celebrates the 7th International Day of Women and Girls in Science Assembly, taking place on 11 February 2022 at UN Headquarters in New York in a virtual format.

Concept note-2: -The International Day of Women and Girls in Science was adopted by the United Nations General Assembly in order to promote full and equal access and participation for women and girls in science. 11 February is an opportunity to celebrate the essential role that women and girls play in science and technology.

Concept note-3: -The International Day of Women and Girls in Science, celebrated on 11 February, is implemented by UNESCO and UN-Women, in collaboration institutions and civil society partners that aim to promote women and girls in science.

Concept note-4: -Use the hashtag #WomenInScience with messages that defy gender stereotypes and spread the word on the need to include more women and girls in STEM fields!
