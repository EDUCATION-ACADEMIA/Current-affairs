Who has been appointed as the Director General of the News Services Division of All India Radio?

(A) Rajiv Kumar

(B) Adille Sumariwalla

(C) ** Dr. Vasudha Gupta

(D) Dixit Joshi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vasudha Gupta named as DG of News Services Division of AIR.

Concept note-2: -Vasudha Gupta has been appointed as the Director General of the News Services Division of All India Radio today. Dr. Vasudha Gupta will assume the charge tomorrow. Principal Director General of News Services Division and Director General of All India Radio N Venudhar Reddy superannuated today.

Concept note-3: -Director General (News) All India Radio Dr. Vasudha Gupta has been given additional charge of Director General of AIR.

Concept note-4: -The News Services Division is headed by a Director General (News) (PB) who is one of the senior most officer of the Indian Information Service.

Concept note-5: -The News Services Division is headed by a Director General (News), who is one of the senior most officers of the Indian Information Service. He is assisted by a team of Additional Directors General (News), Directors (News) and Joint Directors (News), Deputy Directors (News), Asstt.
