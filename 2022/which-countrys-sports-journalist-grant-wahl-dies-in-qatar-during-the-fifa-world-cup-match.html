Which country’s sports journalist Grant Wahl dies in Qatar during the FIFA World Cup match?

(A) UK

(B) USE

(C) ** USA

(D) Fiji

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grant Wahl, an American journalist who helped grow the popularity of soccer in the U.S. and reported on some of the biggest stories in the sport, died Saturday while covering a World Cup match between Argentina and the Netherlands. He was 48.

Concept note-2: -Sports journalist Grant Wahl died from an aortic aneurysm Soccer journalist Grant Wahl died of an aortic aneurysm in Qatar, where he was covering the World Cup, his wife said Wednesday, citing an autopsy.

Concept note-3: -An aortic aneurysm caused the death of Grant Wahl, an American sportswriter who collapsed and died last week while covering a World Cup match in Qatar, his wife said on Wednesday.

Concept note-4: -Grant Wahl, a highly regarded soccer journalist who wrote extensively on the game, died Friday in Qatar, where he was covering the World Cup quarterfinal match between Argentina and the Netherlands.
