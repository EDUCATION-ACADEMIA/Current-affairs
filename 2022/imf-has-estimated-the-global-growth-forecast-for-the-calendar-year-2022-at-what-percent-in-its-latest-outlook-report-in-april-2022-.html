IMF has estimated the global growth forecast for the calendar year 2022 at what percent in its latest outlook report in April 2022? ?

(A) 4.7%

(B) ** 3.6%

(C) 4.1%

(D) 5.3%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Description: Global growth is projected at 6% in 2021, moderating to 4.4% in 2022.

Concept note-2: -“Growth in India is set to decline from 6.8% in 2022 to 6.1% in 2023 before picking up to 6.8% in 2024, with resilient domestic demand despite external headwinds,” said the IMF’s World Economic Outlook update.

Concept note-3: -The World Bank has revised its 2022-23 GDP forecast upward from 6.5 per cent in October to 6.9 per cent.

Concept note-4: -Global growth is forecasted to slow from 3.2 per cent in 2022 to 2.7 per cent in 2023 as per IMF’s World Economic Outlook, October 2022. A slower growth in economic output coupled with increased uncertainty will dampen trade growth.

Concept note-5: -Outlook. Real GDP is estimated to rise in 2022 to 4.9% on the heels of boosted hydrocarbon exports of 10%.
