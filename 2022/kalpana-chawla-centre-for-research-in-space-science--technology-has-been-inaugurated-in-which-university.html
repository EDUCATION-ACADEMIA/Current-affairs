Kalpana Chawla centre for research in space science & technology has been inaugurated in which university?

(A) Allahabad university

(B) Delhi university

(C) ** Chandigarh university

(D) Madras university

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh inaugurated Kalpana Chawla Centre for Research in Space Science & Technology (KCCRSST) at Chandigarh University on January 03, 2022. He also launched a Scholarship Scheme, worth Rs 10 crore, for the wards of Defence Personnel of the three Services.

Concept note-2: -Kalpana Chawla Memorial Planetarium has been setup at Kurukshetra-Pehowa road (near Jyotisar Tirth) by Haryana State Council for Science & Technology, Govt. of Haryana in joint collaboration with National Council for Science Museums, Ministry of Culture, Govt. of India in memory of India’s first woman Astronaut Dr.

Concept note-3: -Kalpana Chawla Government Medical College (KCGMC) is a Medical college located in Karnal, Haryana, India named after Chawla. Kalpana was born in Karnal.

Concept note-4: -Kalpana always dreams of travelling to space. Thus, merely graduating from an engineering college will not do for her. She goes to the United States to get a master’s degree even though her father did not agree.

Concept note-5: -Kalpana Chawla completed her earlier schooling at Tagore Baal Niketan Sr. Sec. School, Karnal. She completed Bachelor of Engineering degree in Aeronautical Engineering at Punjab Engineering College at Chandigarh in 1982.
