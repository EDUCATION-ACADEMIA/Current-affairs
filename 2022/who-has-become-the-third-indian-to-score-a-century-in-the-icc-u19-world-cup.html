Who has become the third Indian to score a century in the ICC U19 World Cup?

(A) Raj Bawa

(B) Shaik Rasheed

(C) ** Yash Dhull

(D) Siddarth Yadav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Shikhar Dhawan and England’s Jack Burnham hold the record for the highest number of centuries (three), and are followed by eleven other players who have scored two centuries.

Concept note-2: -Qasim Akram Smashes Record-Breaking Century in U19 World Cup Qasim brought his hundred on 63 balls in an innings that included 13 boundaries and 6 huge maximums. The previous record for the fastest century was held by India’s Raj Bawa who scored his century off 69 balls.

Concept note-3: -Asked to bat first, Australia rode on centuries from opener Craig Simmons (155) and Shaun Marsh (125) to record a total of 480/6 in 50 overs.

Concept note-4: -India produced a dominant performance against England to triumph in the first-ever ICC U19 Women’s T20 World Cup. A brilliant new-ball performance was followed by a calm run-chase as India cruised to victory over England in the final, sealing their triumph with seven wickets in hand and six overs to spare.

Concept note-5: -Final Highlights: India beat England to be crowned champions | U19 Women’s T20WC.
