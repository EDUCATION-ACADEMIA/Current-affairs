World Thyroid Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** May 25

(B) May 21

(C) May 24

(D) May 23

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -May 25 is celebrated as World Thyroid Day and the goal of the day is to raise awareness about the condition.

Concept note-2: -World Thyroid Day (WTD), 25th May, is dedicated to the global thyroid community, to our patients, physicians, and researchers who are committed to the care of thyroid patients and the treatment of thyroid diseases.

Concept note-3: -However, for Thyroid Awareness Week observed between May 22 and 28, Thyroid Federation international has announced the theme, “It’s not you. It’s your thyroid.” The theme focused on making sure that people understand the most common symptoms of thyroid disorders and take the necessary steps to cure it.

Concept note-4: -WORLD THYROID AWARENESS DAY 2022: THEME The thyroid federation described the theme as ‘It’s not you. It’s your thyroid. ‘The theme focuses on thyroid disorders by taking necessary steps to cure it before causing any complications.

Concept note-5: -Although relatively small, the thyroid gland influences the function of many of the body’s most important organs, including the heart, brain, liver, kidneys, and skin. The blue paisley ribbon icon is the universal symbol of thyroid disease awareness and advocacy.
