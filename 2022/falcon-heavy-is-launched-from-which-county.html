Falcon Heavy is launched from which county?

(A) France

(B) Mexico

(C) ** USA

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Thousands of people came to the coast Saturday to see the Falcon Heavy launch in person. Here in Central Florida, we’ve gotten used to the almost weekly launches of Space X’s Falcon 9 rockets, but Sunday’s launch of the Falcon Heavy was truly spectacular.

Concept note-2: -WASHINGTON - A SpaceX Falcon Heavy lifted off Jan. 15 at 5:56 p.m. Eastern from Launch Complex 39A at Kennedy Space Center, Florida, carrying the U.S. Space Force USSF-67 mission to geostationary Earth orbit.

Concept note-3: -A Falcon 9 rocket launched from Kennedy Space Center in Florida early Thursday with 53 more Starlink satellites for SpaceX’s global internet network.

Concept note-4: -Liftoff of the 229-foot-tall (70-meter) Falcon 9 rocket from pad 40 at Cape Canaveral Space Force Station occurred at 4:32:20 a.m. EST (0932:20 GMT) Thursday.
