Which global group announced the ‘Global Shield’ insurance initiative?

(A) G-20 and V20

(B) WEF

(C) UNEP

(D) ** G-7 and V20

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which global group announced the ‘Global Shield’ insurance initiative? Notes: The ‘Global Shield’ insurance initiative was officially launched by the G7 and the V20 group of 58 climate vulnerable nations at the COP27 climate summit in Egypt.

Concept note-2: -Loss and damage protection V20The Vulnerable Twenty Group and G7Group of 7, seven major industrial countries jointly launch Global Shield against Climate Risks at COP27.

Concept note-3: -The Global Shield against Climate Risks addresses weaknesses in the financial protection structure in climate-vulnerable economies through pre-arranged finance disbursed before or just after disasters happen.

Concept note-4: -The Global Shield gathers together activities in the field of climate risk finance and preparedness under one roof. Under the Global Shield, solutions to provide financial protection will be devised that can be swiftly implemented if climate-related damages occur.

Concept note-5: -It is a social protection and insurance-based finance mechanism for loss and damage outside the UNFCCC process. It will provide pre-arranged financial aid that can be rapidly deployed to respond to disasters like the devastating flood that occurred in Pakistan in August 2022.
