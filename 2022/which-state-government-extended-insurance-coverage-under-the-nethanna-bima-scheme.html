Which state government extended insurance coverage under the ‘Nethanna Bima’ scheme?

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Maharashtra

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Like Rythu Bima scheme for farmers, the State Government launched the Nethanna Bima scheme for the benefit of weavers. For the first time, in the country, Telangana Government has launched such a scheme, which extends insurance coverage to handloom and powerloom weavers in the State.

Concept note-2: -On August 7, 2022, Nethanna Ku Bima will be released. The Telangana Municipal Administration and Urban Development Minister announced this state’s program. This scheme will start on August 7 because that’s National Handloom Day, and it’s for the welfare of handloom weavers.

Concept note-3: -Telangana NethannaBima Scheme The Government of Telangana has announced the Nethanna Ku Bima scheme on 7th August 2022, in order to provide benefits to all the Handloom Weavers.

Concept note-4: -Hyderabad: Handloom and powerloom workers in Telangana came under a Rs five lakh insurance coverage on Sunday when the State government launched the ‘Nethanna Ku Bima,’ a first-of-its-kind scheme in the country.

Concept note-5: -Telangana State Government has announced the extension of insurance coverage for handloom and power loom weavers under the ‘Nethanna Bima’ (Weaver’s Insurance) scheme.
