Which Indian PSU has won the Gold medal at 80th SKOCH Award in the Social Responsibility category?

(A) ** National Mineral Development Corporation

(B) NHPC Limited

(C) ONGC

(D) Steel Authority of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which Indian PSU received a gold prize in the Social Responsibility category at the 80th SKOCH Award? Notes: The National Mineral Development Corporation (NMDC), under Ministry of Steel has won one gold and one silver award at the 80th SKOCH Summit and SKOCH Awards.

Concept note-2: -The Haryana state government won the Skoch Award 2022 for the Gold category in the field of e-governance and innovative IT initiatives.

Concept note-3: -Jaipur: RHB Commissioner Pawan Arora said on Thursday that the Rajasthan Housing Board (RHB) has once again been awarded the ? SKOCH Gold Award- 2022? in the Housing category for innovations and rejuvenation. As many as 1,766 unclaimed bodies were buried after conducting last rites, all with the help of volunteers.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is National Mineral Development Corporation. National Mineral Development Corporation (NMDC) was awarded one gold and one silver award at the 80th SKOCH Summit and SKOCH Awards in April 2022.

Concept note-5: -Mining major NMDC bagged one gold and a silver at the 80th SKOCH Summit and awards held in Delhi recently.
