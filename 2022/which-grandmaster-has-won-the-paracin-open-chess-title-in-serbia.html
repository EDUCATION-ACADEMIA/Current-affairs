Which grandmaster has won the Paracin Open Chess title in Serbia?

(A) Vaishali Rameshbabu

(B) Gukesh D

(C) Pentala Harikrishna

(D) ** R Praggnanandhaa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Grandmaster R Praggnanandhaa wins Paracin Open Chess title in Serbia.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is R Praggnanandhaa. Young Indian Grandmaster R Praggnanandhaa on 16 July 2022 won the Open A section of the 15th International Chess Open Paracin held in the town of Paracin in Serbia. He scored 8 points from nine rounds. Alexandr Predke took the second spot with 7.5 points.

Concept note-3: -Grandmaster R Praggnanandhaa Wins Paracin Open Chess Title | Other Sports News.

Concept note-4: -Answer: R. Praggnanandhaa is an Indian chess Grandmaster. He won the Paracin open A chess tournament 2022 in Serbia. He scored 8 points from nine rounds. The 16-year-old remained unbeaten and finished half a point ahead of the field.

Concept note-5: -R Praggnanandhaa: Meet 17-year-old boy who defeated World Chess champion Magnus Carlsen at FTX crypto cup. Praggnanandhaa had won the World Youth Chess Championship Under-8 title in 2013, earning him the title of FIDE Master at the age of 7 and Under- 10 title in 2015.
