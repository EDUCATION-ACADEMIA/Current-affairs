Name of the participant who received the Best National Costume award at the Mrs World 2022 pageant?

(A) Hardeep Kaur

(B) Harneet Kaur

(C) ** Navdeep Kaur

(D) Pooja Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mrs World 2022: Mrs India Navdeep Kaur wins best National Costume in ‘Kundalini Chakra’ dress.

Concept note-2: -Sargam Koushal, who hails from Jammu wins the Mrs. World 2022 contest after beating contestants from a total of 63 countries. Sargam Koushal has won the Mrs. World crown, after a gap of 21 years ever since an Indian had last won it.

Concept note-3: -Divita Rai, who is representing India at the Miss Universe pageant made heads turn as she appeared in a stunning dress for the national costume round. Her outfit at the costume round symbolised “sone ki chidiya". The outfit was a gold choli, white and gold lehenga.

Concept note-4: -Sini Shetty was crowned Miss World India 2022 at the sparkling finale held at Jio World Convention Center on 3rd July.
