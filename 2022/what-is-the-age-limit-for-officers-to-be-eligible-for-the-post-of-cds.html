What is the age limit for officers to be eligible for the post of CDS?

(A) 70

(B) ** 62

(C) 71

(D) 65

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For Air Force Academy – 20 to 24 years as on 1st January, 2024 i.e. born not earlier than 2nd January, 1999 and not later than 1st January, 2004 (Upper age limit for candidates holding valid and current Commercial Pilot Licence issued by DGCA (India) is relaxable upto 26 yrs.

Concept note-2: -As per the CDS 1 eligibility criteria 2023, applicants wanting to appear for the CDS 1 exam 2023 should be graduates and between the age group of 19 to 25 years.

Concept note-3: -Candidates below 25 years of age must be unmarried. Marriage is not permitted during training. Married candidates above 25 years of age are eligible to apply, but during the training period they will neither be provided married accommodation nor can they live with family out of the premises.

Concept note-4: -CAPF AC Defence Recruitment 2023: Eligibility Criteria An Indian national who has completed graduation from a recognized university and lies in the age group of 20 to 25 years while applying are eligible for the CAPF exam.

Concept note-5: -The CDS is a single exam for the AFA, INA, OTA and IMA. For AFA, INA and IMA the maximum age limit is 24 years of age and for OTA it is 25 years. However there are no age relaxation for OBC , SC, ST candidates as there is no reservation criteria in the Armed forces selection.
