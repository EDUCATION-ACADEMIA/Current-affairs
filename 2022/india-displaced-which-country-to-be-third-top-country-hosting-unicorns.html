India displaced which country to be third top country hosting unicorns?

(A) USA

(B) ** UK

(C) UAE

(D) Uganda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adding 33 “unicorns” in a single year has helped India displace the United Kingdom to be third in the list of countries that are home to such enterprises valued at over $1 billion each.

Concept note-2: -It is reported that India has minted more than half of its 100 unicorns since the start of 2021. It is also noteworthy that the top four unicorns that include Flipkart, Paytm, Byju’s and Oyo are also known as decacorns i.e. companies which have a valuation of $10 billion and above.

Concept note-3: -As of November 2022, the country with the most unicorns, or privately owned companies with a valuation of one billion U.S. dollars or more, was the United States.
