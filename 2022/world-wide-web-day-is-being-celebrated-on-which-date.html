World Wide Web Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** August 01

(B) July 30

(C) August 02

(D) July 31

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Wide Web Day is observed on August 1 each year. The day is marked in honour of the ability of people to browse information freely using the Web. The Web is the tool that billions across the globe use every moment to interact with the global system of interconnected computer systems that is the Internet.

Concept note-2: -On 1st August 1989, is World Wide Web Day, marking the invention by Tim Berners-Lee of an internet-based communication system that changed the world. In 1991, the World Wide Web browser was made available on the public internet.

Concept note-3: -The World Wide Web was conceived on March 12, 1989, by computing legend Tim Berners-Lee.
