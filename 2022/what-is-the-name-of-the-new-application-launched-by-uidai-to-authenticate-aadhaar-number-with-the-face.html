What is the name of the new application launched by UIDAI to authenticate Aadhaar number with the face?

(A) ** Aadhaar Face RD

(B) Digi-Aadhar

(C) Aadhar Authenticate

(D) UIDAI Facetime

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dileep Vishwakarma. The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) recently launched an application, Aadhaar FaceRD Auth App, which will enable the AUAs (Aadhaar Authentication User Agencies) to capture a person’s face for the authentication purpose.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Unique Identification Authority of India (UIDAI) has launched ‘Aadhaar Face RD’ app to enable Aadhaar card holders to authenticate their unique 12-digit identity number using face.

Concept note-3: -First, download Aadhaar Face RD App via the play store (for android users) or Apple Store (for IOS users). Login to the app and allow live location accessibility.

Concept note-4: -To avail of this face authentication feature first, you need to enroll the Aadhaar card in any Aadhaar enrollment center. Aadhaar cardholders will go to the official UIDAI website and enter their 12 digit Aadhaar number and show their face and their Aadhar card will be uploaded.

Concept note-5: -. mAadhaar App is available for both Android and iPhone users in India. In order to install the app follow the steps given below: Visit the Google Play Store for Android and to App Store for iPhone.
