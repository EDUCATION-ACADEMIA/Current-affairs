Which Indian city and the state have been named in TIME magazine’s list of the world’s 50 greatest places of 2022?

(A) Dehradun and Himachal Pradesh

(B) ** Ahmedabad and Kerala

(C) Jaipur and Kerala

(D) Kolkata and Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala and Ahmedabad have been listed by the Time Magazine among 50 extraordinary destinations to explore in its list of “World’s Greatest Places of 2022". Both the places attract a large number of tourists, both from the country as well as abroad.

Concept note-2: -New York City: The prestigious International magazine TIME has placed the Indian state of Kerala and Ahmedabad, a city in the state of Gujarat on its list of “World’s Greatest Places 2022”.

Concept note-3: -Ahmedabad and Kerala have featured on TIME magazine’s list of World’s Greatest Places of 2022. These are two major tourist destinations in India. Seoul, Buahan in Bali, the Great Barrier Reef in Australia have also made it to the list.

Concept note-4: -The Western European cities of Marseille (France), Copenhagen (Denmark), Skellefteå (Sweden) and València (Spain) all receive a nod. Then there’s the Italian region of Calabria, the ancient Greek city of Thessaloniki, the Portuguese region of Alentejo and the archipelago of Madeira.
