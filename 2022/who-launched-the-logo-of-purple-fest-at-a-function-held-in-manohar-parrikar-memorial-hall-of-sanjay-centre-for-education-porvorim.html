(A) ** Pramod Sawant

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Amit Shah

(D) P.S. Sreedharan Pillai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble CM Pramod Sawant along with Subhash Phaldessai, Hon’ble Minister for Social Welfare, Hema Sardesai, playback singer, Guruprasad Pawaskar, the State Commissioner for PwDs and others at the Purple Fest logo launch event.

Concept note-2: -Virendra Kumar said, Goa was earlier known for its New Year celebrations but now with the introduction of Purple Fest, Goa will establish a milestone in Divyangjan empowerment.
