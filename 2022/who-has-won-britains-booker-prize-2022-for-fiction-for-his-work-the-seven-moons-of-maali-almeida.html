Who has won Britain’s Booker Prize 2022 for fiction for his work “The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida”?

(A) Elizabeth Strout

(B) Claire Keegan

(C) NoViolet Bulawayo

(D) ** Shehan Karunatilaka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shehan Karunatilaka wins Booker prize for The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida. The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida by Sri Lankan author Shehan Karunatilaka has won the Booker prize for fiction. The judges praised the “ambition of its scope, and the hilarious audacity of its narrative techniques”.

Concept note-2: -The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida Shehan Karunatilaka’s second novel won the Booker Prize in 2022. It is a searing, mordantly funny satire set amid the murderous mayhem of a Sri Lanka beset by civil war.

Concept note-3: -The winning book. The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida by Shehan Karunatilaka is tonight, Monday October 17, named winner of the Booker Prize 2022.

Concept note-4: -Sri Lankan author Shehan Karunatilaka on 17 October 2022 Monday has become the Booker Prize Winner 2023 for the novel. Karunatilaka has been given this award for the book The Seven Moons of Maali Almeida. .
