Who become the world’s fourth richest person on Forbes Real-Time Billionaires List?

(A) ** Gautam Adani

(B) Jeff Bezos

(C) Mukesh Ambani

(D) Bernard Arnault

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World’s richest people: Billionaire Gautam Adani slips to the 4th spot; net worth declines by $872 million in the last 24 hours - BusinessToday.

Concept note-2: -Billionaire tycoon Gautam Adani has now slipped to the fourth place on the Bloomberg Billionaires List. Billionaire Gautam Adani is, for now, not among the three wealthiest individuals in the world, as he slipped to the fourth spot on the Bloomberg Billionaires Index, a daily ranking of the world’s richest people.

Concept note-3: -Amazon founder Jeff Bezos on Tuesday replaced Gautam Adani becoming the World’s third richest man. Adani Group founder with a total net worth of USD 120 billion ranked fourth place on the Bloomberg Billionaires Index as his net worth fell by USD 872 million in the last 24 hours.

Concept note-4: -America still leads the world, with 735 billionaires worth a collective $4.7 trillion, including Elon Musk, who tops the World’s Billionaires list for the first time.
