Sheikh Mohammed Sabah Al Salem has been appointed as the Prime Minister of Kuwait what is the official currency of Kuwait?

(A) Kuwait Rupee

(B) ** Kuwait Dinar

(C) Kuwait Dollar

(D) Kuwait Pound

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kuwait Currency: Kuwaiti dinar.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Sheikh Sabah Al-Khaled Al-Hamad Al-Sabah - U.S. Embassy in Kuwait.

Concept note-3: -Xi Jinping Meets with Crown Prince of Kuwait Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah. On the afternoon of December 8, 2022 local time, President Xi Jinping met with Crown Prince of Kuwait Sheikh Mishal Al-Ahmad Al-Jaber Al-Sabah in Riyadh.
