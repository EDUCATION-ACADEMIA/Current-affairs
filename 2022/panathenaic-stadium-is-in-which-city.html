Panathenaic stadium is in which city?

(A) London

(B) ** Athens

(C) Paris

(D) Dubai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PANATHENAIC STADIUM ATHENS - Home of the first modern Olympic Games.

Concept note-2: -The Panathenaic stadium is still used. For example, it was the finish place and the opening place for athletics championships. During the Olympic Games in Athens in 2004, it was also the site of archery and the finish of the marathon.

Concept note-3: -The Ancient Stadium of Olympia: The stadium of Ancient Olympia was a holy place for the ancient Greeks, where all the sports activities were held dedicated to the god Zeus. Originally, the stadium was constructed in such a manner that spectators could view the events from the slopes of Mount Cronion.
