Who has been awarded 32nd Bihari Puraskar 2022 for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’?

(A) Kumar Vansi

(B) Raman Prasad Sinha

(C) Acharya Ramlochan Saran

(D) ** Dr Madhav Hada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 32nd Bihari Award for the year 2022 will be given to Dr. Madhav Harda by the Birla Foundation for his literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chol Pahar Sakhi Ri’. K.K. Birla Foundation made this announcement in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Writers Dr Madhav Hada and Madhu Kankariya were awarded the Bihari Puraskar on Wednesday. Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada on Wednesday were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. The Bihari Puraskar is one of the three literary awards instituted by KK Birla Foundation in 1991.

Concept note-3: -Noted Hindi writer Dr Madhav Harda has been selected for the 32nd Bihari Puraskar 2022 for his literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’. This was announced by the K K Birla Foundation on 29 September 2022.

Concept note-4: -Author Madhu Kankariya has been chosen for the 31st Bihari Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’. The award carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation.

Concept note-5: -Literary critic and academic Madhav Hada was awarded the Bihari Puraskar 2022 for his extensive work in literature, media, culture and history. He has also been a member of the General Council of Sahitya Akademi and the Hindi Advisory Board.
