For which language has ICCR signed an MoU with Google for easy search?

(A) ** Sanskrit

(B) Hindi

(C) English

(D) Assamese

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICCR on Thursday signed an MoU with Google to ease searching the Internet in Sanskrit and provided the search engine giant one lakh commonly used sentences in the language with their English and Hindi translations.

Concept note-2: -24 new languages have been added to Google Translate, including Assamese, Bhojpuri, Dogri, Konkani, Maithili, Meiteilon (Manipuri), Mizo and Sanskrit.

Concept note-3: -At Google I/O 2022, the company announced that Google Translate now supports 24 more languages including Indian languages such as Sanskrit, Bhojpuri, Dogri and others. The translation tool from tech giant now supports a total of 133 languages that are used around the globe.

Concept note-4: -Apart from Sanskrit, the other Indian languages in the latest iteration of Google Translate are Assamese, Bhojpuri, Dogri, Konkani, Maithili, Mizo and Meiteilon (Manipuri), taking the total number of Indian languages supported by the service to 19.
