Sandeep Kumar Gupta has been picked to head India’s largest gas utility ____?

(A) SAIL

(B) ONGC

(C) BHEL

(D) ** GAIL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Sandeep Kumar Gupta, Director for Finance at Indian Oil Corporation, has been picked to head India’s largest gas utility GAIL (India) Ltd .

Concept note-2: -Infrastructure GAIL owns the country’s largest pipeline network, the cross-country 2300 km Hazira-Vijaipur-Jagdishpur pipeline with a capacity to handle 33.4 million cubic metre per day at standard conditions gas.

Concept note-3: -appointments. GAIL (India) Limited. Sandeep Kumar Gupta.

Concept note-4: -GAIL (India) Limited on October 3 announced the appointment of Sandeep Kumar Gupta as its new Chairman & Managing Director. Gupta, who will replace Manoj Jain, has a wide experience of over 34 years in the oil and gas industry.
