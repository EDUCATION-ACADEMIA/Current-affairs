What is the theme of National Doctors’ Day 2022?

(A) Family Doctors on the Front

(B) ** Family Doctors on the Front Line

(C) Love Doctors, Respect their duties

(D) Doctors on the Front Line

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for National Doctors’ Day 2022 is “Family Doctors on the Front Line.” Being a doctor brings you many new challenges every day and one such challenge is to keep fighting for the health of your patients. Warm wishes on Doctor’s Day.

Concept note-2: -Dr Roy was born on July 1, 1882, and passed away on the same day in 1962. Also called Doctors’ Day, it has been celebrated across the nation every year since 1991. The theme this year is ‘Family doctors on the front line. ‘

Concept note-3: -In India, it marks the birth and death anniversary of the legendary physician and second Chief Minister of West Bengal, Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy. It was his belief that only a healthy person who is strong in mind and body can ever dream of achieving India’s freedom i.e. Swaraj.

Concept note-4: -India. All across India National Doctors’ Day is celebrated on July 1 in memory of Dr. Bidhan Chandra Roy, physician and the second Chief Minister of West Bengal. He was born on July 1, 1882 and died on the same date in 1962.
