Who has been appointed as the Chief G20 Coordinator of India for the upcoming 2023 G20 Summit?

(A) Rajiv Gauba

(B) ** Harsh V Shringla

(C) Ajay Kumar

(D) Ajay Kumar Bhalla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2023: Chief Coordinator for India’s G20 Presidency in 2023 Harsh Vardhan Shringla and his team had a meeting with Chief Minister Zoramthanga at the latter’s office today. There was a discussion on the ongoing preparation for the B20 meeting to be held in Aizawl on 2nd of March.

Concept note-2: -Harsh Vardhan Shringla (born 1962) is Chief Coordinator for India’s G20 Presidency in 2023.

Concept note-3: -The Indian G20 Presidency’s theme of ‘One Earth, One Family, One Future’ sets the tone for the 2023 Infrastructure Agenda under the 2023 Indian G-20 Presidency.

Concept note-4: -The current chair is held by India. The 2023 and 2024 summits will be hosted by India and Brazil respectively.
