US overtook which country as India’s largest trading partner in the financial year 2022?

(A) Israel

(B) ** China

(C) Iran

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi countered it, saying the US was India’s largest trading partner that year. The US, however, remained India’s largest trading partner in the four months ended 31 July, with shipments worth $46.2 billion, more than China’s $40.4 billion.

Concept note-2: -The US ($57,632.37 million), China ($50,792.83 million), UAE ($36,820.33 million), Saudi Arabia ($23,995 million), Iraq ($18,822.27 million) and Indonesia ($18,816.58 million) were the six countries which recorded higher volumes of trade with India during the first five months of 2022-23.

Concept note-3: -The data showed that China was India’s top trading partner from 2013-14 till 2017-18 and also in 2020-21. Before China, the UAE was the country’s largest trading partner. In 2021-22, the UAE with $72.9 billion, was the third largest trading partner of India.
