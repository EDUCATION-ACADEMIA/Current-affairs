What is the theme of World Head Injury Awareness Day 2022?

(A) ** “More Than My Brain Injury”

(B) ‘Inflicted Brain Injury in Children’

(C) “Change Your Mind.”

(D) “Not Alone.”

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for 2021-2023 campaign for world head injury awareness day is “More Than My Brain Injury".

Concept note-2: -World Head Injury Awareness Day is a public health and awareness campaign that falls on the 20th of March every year. It raises awareness of the number of people that suffer from head injuries and educates the public on how head injuries can be prevented.

Concept note-3: -Brain Injury Association of America (BIAA) has recognized blue as the color for brain injury awareness activities.

Concept note-4: -Wearing a helmet is a must to help reduce the risk of a serious brain injury or skull fracture. However, helmets are not designed to prevent concussions. There is no “concussion-proof” helmet. Using gates at the top and bottom of stairs to prevent serious falls in infants and toddlers.
