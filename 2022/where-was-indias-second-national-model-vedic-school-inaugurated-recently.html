Where was India’s Second National Model Vedic School inaugurated recently?

(A) Dwarka

(B) ** Puri

(C) Badrinath

(D) Guwahati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Union Education Minister Inaugurates India’s Second National Model Vedic School in Puri . Union Education Minister Dharmendra Pradhan inaugurated India’s second Rashtriya Adarsh Veda Vidyalaya (RAVV) in Puri. RAVV has been launched to spread knowledge of the Vedas among people.

Concept note-2: -According to the study conducted by Vediconcepts (April 2022), there is 4500+ gurukul in the whole of India. We have data about 2612 gurukuls and trying hard to map all of the Vedic gurukuls in India.

Concept note-3: -St George’s Anglo- Indian Higher Secondary School was founded in 1715 as the Military (later Madras) Male Orphan Asylum and is one of the oldest schools in the world and the oldest in India.

Concept note-4: -The age group for the Veda school is 8-14 years. It is a full-time, residential, ashram funded program and children from all over India can study here.
