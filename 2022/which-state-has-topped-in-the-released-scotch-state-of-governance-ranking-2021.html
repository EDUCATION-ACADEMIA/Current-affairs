Which state has topped in the released Scotch State of Governance Ranking 2021?

(A) Telangana

(B) Karnataka

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -West Bengal state has topped the list of “SKOCH state of governance report 2021” in the ‘Ease of Doing Business.” The state also topped the table in the education sector. The ‘Star of Governance’ SKOCH award will be conferred on 18th June 2022 in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -The top 5 districts in the District Good Governance Index” are (1) Jammu, (2) Doda, (3) Samba, (4) Pulwama and (5) Srinagar. This index has been prepared by the Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances (DARPG) in collaboration with the Government of Jammu & Kashmir.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh has secured first rank in Group ‘B’ with a score of 4.887 in the overall ranking of Good Governance Index, while Gujarat has secured first rank in Group ‘A’.

Concept note-4: -Haryana has been included in the top achievers category in the fifth edition of ‘State Ease of Doing Business’ released by the Ministry of Commerce and Industry on June 30, 2022. Apart from Haryana, the top achievers category includes Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Karnataka, Punjab, Tamil Nadu and Telangana.

Concept note-5: -Jammu district topped the composite ranking, followed by Doda and Samba districts of the Jammu Division. Pulwama district of the Srinagar Division at the fourth spot and Srinagar district at the fifth.
