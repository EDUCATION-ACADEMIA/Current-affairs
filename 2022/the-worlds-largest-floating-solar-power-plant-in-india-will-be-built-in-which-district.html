The World’s Largest Floating Solar Power Plant in India will be built in which district?

(A) Mandla

(B) Shivpuri

(C) ** Khandwa

(D) Khargone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Khandwa: A floating solar power plant is going to be built in Madhya Pradesh’s Khandwa. Said to be the world’s largest floating solar plant, it will generate 600 Megawatt power by 2022-23. The project is estimated to be worth over ₹3000 crores. “Omkareshwar Dam is built on the Narmada river.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh. The world’s largest floating solar energy project of 600 MW is started in Khandwa district of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Location: Rajasthan, India Bhadla Solar Park is the largest solar farm in the world. The facility, spanning over an area of 14000 acres, is located in the Jodhpur district of Rajasthan. The solar farm has a capacity of 2.25 GW and required an estimated investment of over 1.3 billion dollars.

Concept note-4: -Bhadla Solar Park is the largest solar park in the world as of 2022 and is spread over a total area of 5,700 hectares (14,000 acres) in Bhadla, Phalodi tehsil, Jodhpur district, Rajasthan, India. The park has a total capacity of 2245 MW.
