Hindi film Ayena was screened at which country’s International Mountain Film Festival?

(A) Bhutan

(B) Cuba

(C) ** Nepal

(D) Denmark

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hindi film Ayena, directed and produced by Siddhant Sirin, is being showcased at the 20th edition of Kathmandu International Mountain Film Festival being organized in Kathmandu from 8-12 December 2022.

Concept note-2: -First IFFI The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi, Calcutta and Trivandrum.

Concept note-3: -The 20th Edition of the Kathmandu International Mountain Film Festival kicked off in Kathmandu, Nepal. The 20th Edition of the Kathmandu International Mountain Film Festival kicked off in Kathmandu, Nepal. This year, the festival was scheduled to be held from December 8 to 12, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Founded in 1952, the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) is one of Asia’s most prominent film festivals. Since 2004, it has moved to its permanent venue at Goa, where it is jointly organized by DFF and the State Government of Goa, as per the International Film Festival guidelines and protocols.
