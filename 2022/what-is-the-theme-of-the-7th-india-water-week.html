What is the theme of the 7th India Water Week?

(A) ** Water Security for Sustainable Development and Equity

(B) Sustainable Development and Equity

(C) Water Security and Sustainable Development

(D) Water Conservation for Sustainable Development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for IWW-2022 is “Water Security for Sustainable Development with Equity” focusing on various aspects of water security and management and related challenges for equitable development.

Concept note-2: -THEME FOR IWW 2022: “WATER SECURITY FOR SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT AND EQUITY”

Concept note-3: -World Water Week 2023 is focused on innovation at a time of unprecedented challenges. The theme Seeds of Change: Innovative Solutions for a Water-Wise World invites us to rethink how we manage water.

Concept note-4: -President of India, Droupadi Murmu inaugurated the 7th edition of India Water Week (IWW) today at the India Expo Centre in Greater Noida, Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-5: -The 7 th India Water Week-2022 which was being held at India Expo Centre in Greater Noida,Uttar Pradesh, culminated with the valedictory function today in the august presence of Vice President of India, Shri Jagdeep Dhankhar.
