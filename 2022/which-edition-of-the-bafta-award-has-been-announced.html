Which edition of the BAFTA award has been announced?

(A) 65th edition

(B) 72nd Edition

(C) ** 75th Edition

(D) 52nd Edition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The nominations were announced via a global livestream hosted by actors Hayley Atwell and Toheeb Jimoh on Thursday 19 January at 12-midday (GMT), from the arts charity’s newly redeveloped HQ at 195 Piccadilly, London.

Concept note-2: -The 74th British Academy Film Awards, also known as the BAFTAs, were held on 10 and 11 April 2021 at the Royal Albert Hall in London, honouring the best national and foreign films of 2020 and early 2021.

Concept note-3: -The British Academy Film Awards, more commonly known as the BAFTA Film Awards is an annual award show hosted by the British Academy of Film and Television Arts (BAFTA) to honour the best British and international contributions to film.

Concept note-4: -BAFTA’s annual film awards ceremony, the British Academy Film Awards, has taken place since 1949, while their annual television awards ceremony, the British Academy Television Awards, has taken place since 1955.
