Who has won the BWF World Championships 2022 title in Men’s Singles and Women’s Singles categories?

(A) ** Victor Axelsen and Akane Yamaguchi

(B) Lee Chong Wei and Chen Yufei

(C) Kento Momota and Nozomi Okuhara

(D) Lee ZiiJia and Tai Tzu-Ying

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1) Which country won the BWF world Cup 2022? Denmark won the BWF World Championship, with its player, Viktor Axelsen winning his second BWF World Championships men’s singles title. In women’s single, Akane Yamaguchi from Japan retained her title of World Champion by winning the gold medal in BWF World Cup 2022.

Concept note-2: -Ending what has been a remarkable year on a high note, world champions Zheng Si Wei/Huang Ya Qiong won their 10th title of 2022 in as many finals, beating the two-time defending champions Dechapol Puavaranukroh/Sapsiree Taerattanachai in three (21-19, 18-21, 21-13).

Concept note-3: -BWF World Championships 2022: Imperious Viktor Axelsen Wins Second Badminton World Title.
