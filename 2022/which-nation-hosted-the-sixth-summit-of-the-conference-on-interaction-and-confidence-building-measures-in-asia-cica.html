Which nation hosted the sixth summit of the Conference on Interaction and Confidence Building Measures in Asia (CICA)?

(A) Egypt

(B) Iran

(C) ** Kazakhstan

(D) Iran

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sixth CICA Summit, presided by H.E. Mr. Kassym-Jomart Tokayev, President of the Republic of Kazakhstan, was held in Astana on 12-13 October 2022 with participation of 52 delegations, including 13 heads of Member States, observers and partner organizations, and guests of the Chairmanship.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains Notes: Kazakhstan is set to host Conference on Confidence Building Measures and Interaction in Asia (CICA) Summit in capital Astana. The country has invited Prime Minister Narendra Modi to the CICA Summit.

Concept note-3: -This conference was hailed “one of the most significant post-pandemic foreign policy events in [Kazakhstan] and on the continent.” Attending the summit were heads of state from Azerbaijan, Iran, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Palestine, Russia, Tajikistan, Türkiye, and Uzbekistan, as well as Belarus as an observer state.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Muhammad Shehbaz Sharif participated in the 6th Summit of the Conference on Interaction and Confidence Building Measures in Asia (CICA) held in Astana, Kazakhstan on 13 October 2022.
