Government has approved how much amount of additional Central assistance under the NDRF to five States and one Union Territory?

(A) 1943 crore rupees

(B) 1382 crore rupees

(C) ** 1682 crore rupees

(D) 1250 crore rupees

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Centre has approved over Rs 1,682 crore as additional assistance to five states and a union territory which were hit by floods and landslides last year.

Concept note-2: -The balance 25% in case of general category States and 10% in case of special category States will be contributed by the State Government concerned. - Grants The State 6.

Concept note-3: -Sources: Union Budget 2021-22; PRS. The National Disaster Response Force (NDRF) is a specialised force that is responsible for disaster response and relief. For 2021-22, the budget estimate for the NDRF is Rs 1,209 crore, which is 14% higher than the actual expenditure for 2019-20.

Concept note-4: -At present, National Disaster Response Force consist of 15 battalions from the BSF, CISF, CRPF, ITBP, SSB and Assam Rifles. Each battalion have 18 self-contained specialist search and rescue teams of 45 personnel each including engineers, technicians, electricians, dog squads and medical/paramedics.
