Bangladesh Prime Minister Sheikh Hasina inaugurated the 26th annual DITF on which day?

(A) ** January 01, 2022

(B) December 30, 2021

(C) December 31, 2021

(D) January 02, 2022

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sheikh Hasina took oath as Prime Minister on 6 January 2009. She is still the prime minister of Bangladesh after winning a walkover 5 January 2014 election when Khaleda Zia’s BNP boycotted the general election.

Concept note-2: -She previously served as prime minister from June 1996 to July 2001. She is the longest serving prime minister in the history of Bangladesh, having served for a combined total of over 19 years. As of 27 January 2023, she is the world’s longest-serving female Head of Government in history.

Concept note-3: -As of 4 February 2023, she is the world’s longest-serving female Head of Government in history. DhakaTribune Jan 2018: Salary 115.000 taka plus 100.000 rent of the official residence plus 3.000 daily allowance plus 1.500. 000 discretionary grant plus 2.500. 000 air insurance.

Concept note-4: -Khaleda Zia (Bengali pronunciation: [kʰaled̪a dʒia]; born Khaleda Khanam Putul in 1945) is a Bangladeshi politician who served as the Prime Minister of Bangladesh from March 1991 to March 1996, and again from June 2001 to October 2006.
