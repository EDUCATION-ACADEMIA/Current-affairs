India is to assume chairmanship of Wassenaar arrangement on which date?

(A) 2 February 2023

(B) ** 1 January 2023

(C) 6 January 2023

(D) 5 January 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At the 26th annual plenary of the Wassenaar Arrangement held in Vienna during November 30-December 1, Ireland’s ambassador Eoin O’Leary handed over the chairmanship to India’s ambassador Jaideep Mazumdar, who is the permanent representative to the UN and international organisations in Vienna.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) has announced that India will assume Chairmanship of Wassenaar Arrangement (WA) on 01 January 2023 for a period of one year.

Concept note-3: -India assumed Chairmanship of Wassenaar Arrangement (WA) Plenary starting from January 1, 2023. India will hold the Chairmanship for one year.

Concept note-4: -India joined the Wassenaar Arrangement (WA) in 2017 and became its 42nd member.

Concept note-5: -Agenda in Austria The Wassenaar Arrangement’s secretariat is located in its capital Vienna and India will hold the Chairmanship for one year. Dr Jaishankar will be on an official visit from January 1-3, 2023.
