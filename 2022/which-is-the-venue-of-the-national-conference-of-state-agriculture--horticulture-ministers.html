Which is the venue of the ‘National Conference of State Agriculture & Horticulture Ministers’?

(A) Varanasi

(B) Amritsar

(C) Jaipur

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Conference of State Agriculture & Horticulture Ministers-2022 was held on 14th-15th July, 2022 under the Chairmanship of Shri Narendra Singh Tomar , Hon’ble Minister of Agriculture & Farmers Welfare at Aura Hall, Taj Yeshwantpur, Bengaluru.

Concept note-2: -The agriculture ministry is headed by Minister of Agriculture Narendra Singh Tomar.

Concept note-3: -The Honorable Agricultural Minister of Karnataka state Shri Shivashankar Reddy visited ICAR-Krishi Vigyan Kendra, Hirehalli, Tumakuru on 30th June 2018.
