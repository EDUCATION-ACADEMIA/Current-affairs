Which state/UT has launched the second phase of the Ladli Laxmi scheme (Ladli Laxmi scheme-2.0) to encourage girl children?

(A) Ladakh

(B) Himachal Pradesh

(C) Assam

(D) ** Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On Wednesday, Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan launched the ‘Ladli Laxmi 2.0’ financial assistance scheme to encourage girls to pursue higher education and make them independent.

Concept note-2: -The Ladli Laxmi Yojana 2.0 has already been started by the Government of Madhya Pradesh on 8 May 2022. The Madhya Pradesh Government started the second phase of the yojana to promote the education of daughters. Ladli Laxmi Yojana 2.0 Registration can be done on the official website.

Concept note-3: -Ladli Laxmi Yojana was launched by the Government of Madhya Pradesh in 2007. The objective of this scheme is to bring a change in the negative approach of the society towards the birth of a girl child.

Concept note-4: -Ladli Laxmi Yojana has been implemented in Madhya Pradesh with effect from 01.04. 2007 with the objective of positive thinking towards girl child birth, improvement in sex ratio, improvement in educational status and health status of girls and laying the foundation for their good future.

Concept note-5: -"Ladli Laxmi Yojana” is an initiative of the Madhya Pradesh state government for improving the health and educational status of the girls in the State.
