The World Liver Day is being celebrated every year on which date?

(A) ** April 19

(B) April 17

(C) April 16

(D) April 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World liver day is observed on every 19 April, to spread awareness about liver related disease. The liver is the second largest and the most complex organ in the body, with the exception of the brain. It is a key player in your body’s digestive system.

Concept note-2: -This is why, World Liver Day is observed on April 19 every year - to create awareness about diseases related to the liver.

Concept note-3: -The day is observed to raise awareness about liver diseases across the world. The liver is the largest solid organ of the body that removes toxins from the body. The organ maintains healthy blood sugar levels, regulates blood clotting, and performs several other vital functions in the body.

Concept note-4: -Hepatitis can manifest as acute or chronic illness. This year’s theme for World Hepatitis Day is, “Bringing hepatitis care closer to you."
