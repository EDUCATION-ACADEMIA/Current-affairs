Who has been conferred the title of Honorary General of the Nepali Army by Nepal President Bidya Devi Bhandari in Kathmandu?

(A) General Bipin Rawat

(B) General Dalbir Singh Suhag

(C) ** General Manoj Pande

(D) General Manoj Mukund Naravane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Army Chief Manoj Pande conferred honorary rank of Nepal Army General.

Concept note-2: -The seat of Chief of Army Staff is in Bhadrakali, Kathmandu. The current Chief of Army Staff is General Prabhu Ram Sharma, since 9 September 2021.

Concept note-3: -It was officially renamed to the Nepali Army on 28 May 2008, following the abolition of the 240-year-old Shah dynasty shortly after the Nepali Civil War.
