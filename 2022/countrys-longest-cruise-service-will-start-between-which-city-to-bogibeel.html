Country’s longest cruise service will start between which city to Bogibeel?

(A) ** Varanasi

(B) Jaipur

(C) Jaisalmer

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This cruise will take travellers between Uttar Pradesh’s Varanasi to Assam’s Bogibeel. The cruise will cover a distance of over 4,000 kilometres, Union Minister Sarbananda Sonowal said, writes The Hindu.

Concept note-2: -Beginning of cruise service on River Ganga is a landmark moment. It will herald a new age of tourism in India. Today, the world’s longest river cruise - Ganga Vilas, has embarked on a journey between Kashi and Dibrugarh.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: The MV Ganga Vilas luxury cruise, which was flagged off by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Friday, is already fully booked till March 2024. Ganga Vilas luxury cruise, dubbed the world’s longest river voyage, will take passengers on a 51-day trip from Varanasi to Dibrugarh.

Concept note-4: -Cruising along the Brahmaputra in Assam. Nestled in-between Bangladesh, Bhutan and Myanmar, Assam is a long, narrow state, intersected by the mighty Brahmaputra river.
