How many Indian airports have got a place in ACI World’s ASQ Awards 2021?

(A) ** 6

(B) 3

(C) 4

(D) 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From India, six airports have found a place among the ‘Best Airport by Size and Region’, by the Airports Council International (ACI), in its Airport Service Quality (ASQ) survey for the year 2021.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, from over 370,000 surveys, 111 awards have been won by 88 individual airports around the world. The award categories include Best Airports by Size (passengers per year) and Region, as well as Best Hygiene Measures by Region, of which there are 46 winners.

Concept note-3: -There are 34 operational International Airports in India. Indira Gandhi International Airport is the largest International airport constructed in 5495 acres. It is also the busiest airport in India followed by the Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport.

Concept note-4: -As of January 2022, ACI serves 717 members, operating 1950 airports in 185 countries.
