Who has authored the book ‘Here Yourself’?

(A) Bhabananda Deka

(B) ** Prem Rawat

(C) Bhikhari Thakur

(D) Khwaja Ahmad Abbas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About the Author For more than 50 years, Prem Rawat has spoken to hundreds of millions of people in over 100 countries to spread his message of peace. Born in India, Prem gave his first public address at the age of four, and at thirteen, began speaking around the world.

Concept note-2: -On September 14th, Prem Rawat’s latest book, Hear Yourself: How to Find Peace in a Noisy World, was published by Harper One. The book debuted on the New York Times Best Seller List – quite an extraordinary feat. Indeed, out of 100,000 hardcover print books published each year, fewer than 500 (. 5%) make the list.
