The Geological Survey of India (GSI) has approved to set up the first-ever geological park in the country in which city?

(A) Amravati

(B) Dehradun

(C) ** Jabalpur

(D) Gorakhpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Jabalpur. The Geological Survey of India (GSI) has approved the setting up of India’s first geological park at Lamheta village on the banks of the Narmada river in the Jabalpur district of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Q. Geological Survey of India (GSI) has approved to set up India’s first Geo-Park in which state? Notes: The Geological Survey of India (GSI) has approved to set up India’s first Geo-Park at Lamheta village on the banks of the Narmada River in Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -The Geological Survey of India (GSI) has approved the setting up of the country’s first geo park at Lamheta village on the banks of the Narmada river in Jabalpur district of Madhya Pradesh, local MP Rakesh Singh said on Sunday.

Concept note-4: -The first geological park of India will be built in Lamheta, Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh. The first geological park of India will be built in Lamheta, Jabalpur in Madhya Pradesh. Approval for the park was given by the Geological Survey of India, under the ministry of mining.

Concept note-5: -GSI, headquartered in Kolkata, has six regional offices located in Lucknow, Jaipur, Nagpur, Hyderabad, Shillong and Kolkata and state unit offices in almost all states of the country.
