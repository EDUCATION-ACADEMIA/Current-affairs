On which date Air Force Day is celebrated in India?

(A) ** October 08

(B) October 09

(C) October 10

(D) October 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Air Force Day is celebrated on October 8th every year, to mark the date of establishment of the Bharatiya Vayu Sena. 8th October 2021 was India’s 89th Air Force Day. IAF and Indian Air Force Day both constitute an important part of the UPSC Syllabus.

Concept note-2: -Its primary mission is to secure Indian airspace and to conduct aerial warfare during armed conflicts. It was officially established on 8 October 1932 as an auxiliary air force of the British Empire which honoured India’s aviation service during World War II with the prefix Royal.

Concept note-3: -Back in October 8, 1932, the Indian Air Force was officially established by the erstwhile British regime. The first operational squadron was created in 1933 with six RAF-trained officers and 19 Havai Sepoys (air soldiers).

Concept note-4: -The Indian Air Force was officially established on 8 October 1932.
