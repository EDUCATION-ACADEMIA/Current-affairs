What is the fiscal deficit recorded in India for 2021-22 (in the percentage of GDP)?

6.71 %
B
6.21 %
C
7.01 %
D
7.51 %
Explanation: 

Detailed explanation-1: -Deficits: Revenue deficit is targeted at 5.1% of GDP in 2021-22, which is lower than the revised estimate of 7.5% in 2020-21 (3.3% in 2019-20). Fiscal deficit is targeted at 6.8% of GDP in 2021-22, down from the revised estimate of 9.5% in 2020-21 (4.6% in 2019-20).

Detailed explanation-2: -In the corresponding period last year, the deficit was 46.2 per cent of the Budget Estimates of 2021-22.
