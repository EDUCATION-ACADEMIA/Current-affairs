Who became the first Indian woman to scale five peaks above 8, 000m?

(A) ** Priyanka Mohite

(B) Santosh Yadav

(C) Premlata Agrawal

(D) Bachendri Pal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Priyanka Mohite from Satara, Maharashrta, has summited Mount Kanchenjunga , Mount Everest, Mount Lhotse, Mount Makalu, and Mount Annapurna 1, making her the first Indian woman to climb five peaks above 8,000 metres.

Concept note-2: -Bachendri pal holds the record of being the first Indian women to climb the Mount Everest which is the highest mountain range in the world. Q. Smt. Bachendri Pal was the first Indian woman to climb the Mount Everest.
