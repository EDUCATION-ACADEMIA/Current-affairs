Which film has been awarded the Best International Film Award at the Oscar Awards 2022?

(A) Gold

(B) All my friends hate me

(C) ** Drive my car

(D) The pink cloud

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No Time to Die (No Time to Die) – WINNER!

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is CODA. CODA movie won the award for Best Picture at the 94th Academy Awards. The movie is directed by Director Sian Heder.

Concept note-3: -“CODA” was the first film produced by a streaming service to win Best Picture.

Concept note-4: -02/8RRR. SS Rajamouli’s ‘RRR’ featured on the Academy’s list of productions eligible for Oscar nominations at the 95th Academy Awards.

Concept note-5: -Israel is the foreign country with the most nominations (10) without winning an award, while Portugal has the most submissions (34) without a nomination. In 2020 (92nd), South Korea’s entrant Parasite became the first International Feature Film winner, and the first non-English film overall, to also win Best Picture.
