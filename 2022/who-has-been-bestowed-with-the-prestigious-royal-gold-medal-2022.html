Who has been bestowed with the prestigious Royal Gold Medal 2022?

(A) Anne Lacaton

(B) Diebedo Francis Kere

(C) ** Balkrishna Doshi

(D) Jean-Philippe Vassal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Balkrishna Doshi, has been formally presented, on June 15, with the 2022 Royal Gold Medal for Architecture by RIBA President, Simon Allford.

Concept note-2: -Renowned Indian Architect Balkrishna Doshi received the Royal Gold Medal 2022 by RIBA. Granted by the Royal Institute of British Architects, the recognition «is given to a person or group of people who have had a significant influence on the advancement of architecture».

Concept note-3: -Royal Gold Medal 2023 Given in recognition of a lifetime’s work, the Royal Gold Medal is approved personally by the Monarch and is given to a person or group of people who have had a significant influence either directly or indirectly on the advancement of architecture.

Concept note-4: -The Royal Gold Medal for architecture is awarded annually by the Royal Institute of British Architects on behalf of the British monarch, in recognition of an individual’s or group’s substantial contribution to international architecture.
