Which state Assembly passed Bill to remove Governor as the Chancellor of the state’s universities?

(A) ** Kerala

(B) Goa

(C) Assam

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Governor is ex-officio Chancellor of the State Universities. As per the provisions of the Act of the concerned University, the Governor appoints the Vice Chancellor on the advice/ in consultation with the State Government.

Concept note-2: -The Vice-President of India is the Chancellor of all the Central Universities in India.

Concept note-3: -The Chancellor shall have the power to transfer officer(s) or teacher(s) and assets from one University to another or in the same University. He shall have the power to issue directions to the University in the administrative or academic interest of the University as he may deem fit.

Concept note-4: -Since 6 September 2019, Arif Mohammad Khan, is the governor of Kerala.
