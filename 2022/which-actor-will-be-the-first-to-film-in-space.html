Which actor will be the first to film in space?

(A) ** Tom Cruise

(B) Val Kilmer

(C) Dwayne Johnson

(D) Johnny Depp

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tom Cruise to become first actor to shoot film in space, teams up with The Bourne Identity director Doug Liman.

Concept note-2: -The trailer of the film ‘The Challenge’, which was shot on the International Space Station, has been released, and the frames look more real than ever. Directed by Klim Shipenko, a sequence of the feature film was shot on the Space Station in October 2021.

Concept note-3: -Russia has beat the United States to be the first country to film a movie in space and aboard the International Space Station.

Concept note-4: -Space Station 3D, 47 minutes, 2002, United States. Apogee of Fear, 8 minutes, 2012, United States. A Beautiful Planet, 46 minutes, 2016, United States. The Challenge, in production, 2022, Russia.

Concept note-5: -The following movies have been filmed in space: Return from Orbit, 84 minutes (only partly filmed in space), 1984, Soviet Union. For All Mankind, 80 minutes (using NASA Apollo Program footage), 1989, United States. Space Station 3D, 47 minutes, 2002, United States.
