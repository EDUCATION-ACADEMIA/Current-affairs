Which city is to host the first G20 Sherpa Meeting in India from 04 to 07 December?

(A) ** Udaipur

(B) Varanasi

(C) Srinagar

(D) Thane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 1st Sherpa Meeting of India’s G20 Presidency begins today in the historic city of Udaipur, Rajasthan. The 1st Sherpa Meeting of India’s G20 Presidency commenced today, on 04 December 2022, in Udaipur Rajasthan.

Concept note-2: -The 1st Sherpa Meeting of India’s G20 Presidency, chaired by G20 Sherpa Shri Amitabh Kant, was commencing in the historic city of Udaipur, a city located in Rajasthan State.

Concept note-3: -India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations- the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.

Concept note-4: -The first G20 Sherpa Meeting under India’s Presidency, began in the lake city of Udaipur in Rajasthan on Sunday evening. India’s G20 Sherpa, Amitabh Kant is presiding over the four-day meeting.
