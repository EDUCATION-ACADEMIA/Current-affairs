The Union Government has organised a week-long science exhibition titled ‘Vigyan Sarvatra Pujyate’. The event has been organised at how many locations simultaneously?

(A) 55

(B) 25

(C) ** 75

(D) 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India has organised a week-long science exhibition titled ‘Vigyan Sarvatra Pujyate’ from February 22 to 28, 2022. The Government of India has organised a week-long science exhibition titled ‘Vigyan Sarvatra Pujyate’ from February 22 to 28, 2022, as part of the Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav commemoration.

Concept note-2: -Vigyan Sarvatra Pujyate (translates to universal reverence for science), was organised in 75 cities across the country to mark the National Science Week (22-28 February 2022).

Concept note-3: -Vigyan Sarvatra Pujyate also aims to inspire India’s youth and help them navigate into building a progressive nation; bring to the fore stories of people in science and their achievements; reinforce the commitment of the scientific community towards the economic and social development of the country.
