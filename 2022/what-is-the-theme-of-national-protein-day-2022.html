What is the theme of National Protein Day 2022?

(A) Protein and Health

(B) Action on Protein

(C) ** Food Futurism

(D) Powering with Plant Protein

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the Media. “Right to Protein, ahead of India’s third annual Protein Day celebration on 27th February, declares ‘Food Futurism’ as the theme for 2022 as it takes ahead its mission to drive food and especially protein sufficiency in the country through awareness, advocacy, and action."

Concept note-2: -Every year on February 27th, National Protein Day draws public attention and awareness on the health benefits of protein. It’s also a day to learn more about different sources of plant and animal protein.

Concept note-3: -National Protein Day 2022: Theme The theme is aimed at bringing food scientists, nutrition experts and biologists to discuss food science and its role in protein sufficiency and food security in India.

Concept note-4: -The day urges people to include this crucial macronutrient in their diet. The day also helps people to learn more about different sources of plant and animal protein. Protein Day is celebrating its third year in 2022. This year’s theme of India Protein Day is ‘Food Futurism’.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai, Maharashtra, India: Right To Protein, a national-level public health initiative, has launched India’s first ‘Protein Day’ to raise awareness about the health benefits of protein. Many countries recognize 27th February as the Protein Day and from this year, India will also join this movement.
