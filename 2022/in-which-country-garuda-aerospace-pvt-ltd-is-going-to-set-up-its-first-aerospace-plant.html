In which country Garuda Aerospace Pvt Ltd is going to set up its first aerospace plant?

(A) ** Malaysia

(B) Myanmar

(C) Singapore

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Most Dependable Drone Startup We pride ourselves on the impactful intelligence our drone solutions have to offer. Having only started in 2015, we’ve already made a significant mark on the drone industry.

Concept note-2: -CHENNAI: IT giant Cognizant has signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) with drone startup Garuda Aerospace to power its drones with advanced digital capabilities and bring innovative solutions at scale for enterprises using drone technology.

Concept note-3: -The HiiLSE Garuda Aerospace Plant will be made across 2.42 hectares of land, with an investment of ₹115 crores to provide state-of-the-art technology to public and private sectors alike in the whole region.

Concept note-4: -Founded in 2015 by Agnishwar Jayaprakash, Garuda Aerospace manufactures and sells drones as well as provides drone-as-a-service (DaaS). It designs and builds drones for a wide range of applications, including agriculture, defence, mining, mapping, and warehouse management.
