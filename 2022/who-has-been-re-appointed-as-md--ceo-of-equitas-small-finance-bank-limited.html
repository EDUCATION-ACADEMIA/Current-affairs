Who has been re-appointed as MD & CEO of Equitas Small Finance Bank Limited?

(A) Sanju Panda

(B) Sanju Rai

(C) ** Vasudevan Pathangi Narasimhan

(D) Sanju Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -P N Vasudevan to continue as Equitas Small Finance Bank MD, CEO.

Concept note-2: -Reserve Bank of India has okayed Equitas Small Finance Bank’s proposal to reappoint MD and CEO Vasudevan P N for one year. In a letter to ESFB last month, Vasudevan expressed his desire to quit but did not gave a timeline.

Concept note-3: -Equitas Small Finance Bank on Friday said that PN Vasudevan, in response to suggestions of the board, has decided to stay on as managing director and chief executive of the lender. The founder and CEO had informed the lender’s board in May about his decision to step down to pursue philanthropic activities.

Concept note-4: -Mr. Sanjay Agarwal is promoter and Managing Director & CEO of the Bank and has over 25 years of strategic executive experience. He is a Commerce graduate from Government College, Ajmer and all-India rank holder Chartered Accountant.
