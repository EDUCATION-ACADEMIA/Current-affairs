Buck Moon 2022 be visible on which date?

(A) ** July 13

(B) July 18

(C) July 17

(D) July 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -When to See the Full Moon in July 2022. July’s full Buck Moon will rise on Wednesday, July 13, reaching peak illumination at 2:38 P.M.

Concept note-2: -The supermoon was visible to the naked eye at 5.22 PM IST on June 14. This was the time when the moon was at the closest point in its orbit around Earth.

Concept note-3: -As mentioned above, the next full moon will be Wednesday afternoon, July 13, 2022, at 2:38 p.m. EDT. Since this is less than 10 hours after perigee, this will be a supermoon. The Moon will appear full for about three days, from early Tuesday morning through early Friday morning.

Concept note-4: -Supermoon 2022: Date & Time The supermoon of 2022, also known as ‘buck moon’ is named after antlers that form on a buck’s forehead around this time of the year. The supermoon will be visible at 2:38 pm EDT (12:08 am IST, Thursday) on the night of 13 July.

Concept note-5: -The 13 July 2022 Supermoon was just rising, behind a subtle layer of clouds. Every time the full Supermoon is around, we are ready to image it and share the vision with our community. We did so last July the 13rd, when the largest full Moon of the year rose above Rome, offering as always a stunning experience.
