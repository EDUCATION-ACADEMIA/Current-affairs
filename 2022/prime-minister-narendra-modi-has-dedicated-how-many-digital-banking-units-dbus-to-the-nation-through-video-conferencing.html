Prime Minister Narendra Modi has dedicated how many Digital Banking Units (DBUs) to the nation through video conferencing?

(A) 154

(B) 200

(C) ** 75

(D) 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In yet another measure to deepen financial inclusion, the Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi dedicated 75 Digital Banking Units (DBUs) to the nation via video conferencing.

Concept note-2: -How will digital banking units function in India? On October 17, 2022, 75 digital banking units became operational across 75 districts in India. As of now, there is at least one digital banking unit set up in each of the 29 states and eight union territories (UTs).
