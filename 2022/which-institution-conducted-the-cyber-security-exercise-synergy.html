Which institution conducted the Cyber Security Exercise ‘Synergy’?

(A) Defence Research and Development Organisation

(B) Indian Air Force

(C) Indian Space Research Organisation

(D) ** CERT-In

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dileep Vishwakarma. Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In) under the Ministry of Electronics and IT, in collaboration with the Cyber Security Agency of Singapore, successfully designed and conducted the Cyber Security Exercise “Synergy” for 13 Countries.

Concept note-2: -It is a nodal agency which deals with cybersecurity threats like hacking and phishing. It collects, analyses and disseminates information on cyber incidents, and also issues alert on cybersecurity incidents.

Concept note-3: -Cybersecurity Exercises: These exercises provide stakeholders with effective and practical mechanisms to identify best practices, lessons learned, and areas for improvement in plans and procedures.
