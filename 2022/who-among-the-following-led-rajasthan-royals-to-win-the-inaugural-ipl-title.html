Who among the following led Rajasthan Royals to win the inaugural IPL title?

(A) David Warner

(B) Rahul Dravid

(C) ** Shane Warne

(D) Ross Taylor

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Warne had led the Royals to an improbable title in the first season of the IPL at that venue in 2008. Thank you for celebrating Warnie with us, mipaltan.

Concept note-2: -Shane Warne led the Rajasthan Royals to IPL title in the first edition of the tournament and the franchise paid an emotional tribute to the Aussie legend, who passed away on Friday.

Concept note-3: -Under Warne’s inspirational leadership, RR defeated CSK by 3 wickets in the summit clash of the 2008 IPL to bag the title. Shane Warne was the captain of the Rajasthan Royals team that won the 2008 edition of the Indian Premier League.

Concept note-4: -June 1st, 2008: Shane Warne-led Rajasthan Royals scripted a historic IPL victory after defeating MS Dhoni-led Chennai Super Kings in the inaugural season’s finale.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai Indians have won five titles. Chennai Super Kings have won four titles and Kolkata Knight Riders have won two titles, Sunrisers Hyderabad and Rajasthan Royals, apart from former team Deccan Chargers, are the other teams to have won the tournament title as of June 2022.
