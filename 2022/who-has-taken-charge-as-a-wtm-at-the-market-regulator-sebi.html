Who has taken charge as a WTM at the market regulator Sebi?

(A) ** Ashwani Bhatia

(B) Divya Agrawal

(C) Raunak Singh

(D) Sonam Dixit

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ashwani Bhatia. Ashwani Bhatia has taken charge as a whole-time member (WTM) at the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI). Bhatia was previously the Managing Director of the State Bank of India (SBI).

Concept note-2: -Former banker Ananth Narayan Gopalakrishnan took charge as the fourth whole time member (WTM) in the Securities and Exchange Board of India (Sebi) on Monday, filling the position vacant since November 2021.

Concept note-3: -SEBI | Shri Ashwani Bhatia takes charge as Whole Time Member, SEBI.

Concept note-4: -SEBI is a statutory body and a market regulator, which controls the securities market in India. The basic functions of Sebi is to protect the interests of investors in securities and to promote and regulate the securities market. Sebi is run by its board of members.

Concept note-5: -BSE Ltd announced that the market regulator Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) has approved Sundararaman Ramamurthy as the managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of the exchange.
