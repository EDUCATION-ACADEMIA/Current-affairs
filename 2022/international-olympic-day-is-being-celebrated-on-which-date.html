International Olympic Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) ** June 23

(B) June 24

(C) June 22

(D) June 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Olympic Day is celebrated on June 23 each year and was first observed in 1948. The Olympics is the world’s largest international multi-sport event held every four years.

Concept note-2: -Each year June 23 is a day when athletes and people worldwide congregate and organise various sporting events. Nations across the world have their respective Olympic committees that overlook the celebrations that take place on International Olympic Day.

Concept note-3: -The first Olympic Day was celebrated on 23 June 1948. On this occasion, Sigfrid Edström, IOC President at that time, conveyed a message to the young people of the world. Portugal, Greece, Austria, Canada, Switzerland, Great Britain, Uruguay, Venezuela and Belgium organised an Olympic Day in their respective countries.

Concept note-4: -To mark Olympic Day on 23rd June 2022, people in countries across the globe have been getting active to celebrate sport and peace. The theme for this year is Together, For a Peaceful World, and the International Olympic Committee (IOC) invited everyone to get involved and Move For Peace.
