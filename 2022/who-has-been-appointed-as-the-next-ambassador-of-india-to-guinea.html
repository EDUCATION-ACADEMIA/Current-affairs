Who has been appointed as the next Ambassador of India to Guinea?

(A) ** Avtar Singh

(B) V.M. Kwatra

(C) Rajkumar Ranjan Singh

(D) Shilpak Ambule

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Solution. The Government of India has appointed Avtar Singh as the next Ambassador of India to Guinea. He is presently the Director in the Minister of External Affairs. Avtar Singh is expected to take up the assignment as the Ambassador of India to Guinea shortly.

Concept note-2: -The President approves the appointments of Ambassadors/High Commissioners based on the recommendations of the Prime Minister and the External Affairs Minister and the appointment is also subject to the agre‘ment of the receiving State.

Concept note-3: -Ambassador Santosh Jha assumed the position of Ambassador of India to Belgium, Luxembourg and the European Union on 17 July 2020.

Concept note-4: -Ambassador Pankaj Sharma, a member of the Indian Foreign Service since 1991, took up the position of Ambassador/Permanent Representative of India to the Conference on Disarmament on 12 November, 2018.

Concept note-5: -The Indian Ambassador to France is the chief diplomatic representative of India to France, housed in the Embassy of India, Paris. The current ambassador is Jawed Ashraf. who succeeded Vinay Mohan Kwatra in August, 2020 following Kwatra’s appointment as Indian ambassador to Nepal.
