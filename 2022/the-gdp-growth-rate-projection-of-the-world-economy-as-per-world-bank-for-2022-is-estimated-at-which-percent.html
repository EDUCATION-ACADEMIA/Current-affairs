The GDP growth rate projection of the world economy as per World Bank for 2022 is estimated at which percent?

(A) 5.5%

(B) 2.8%

(C) ** 3.2%

(D) 4.3%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Bank has revised its 2022-23 GDP forecast upward to 6.9 percent from 6.5 percent (in October 2022), considering a strong outturn in India in the second quarter (July-September) of the 2022-23 financial year.

Concept note-2: -The World Bank has revised its 2022-23 GDP forecast upward from 6.5 per cent in October to 6.9 per cent.

Concept note-3: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has also lowered India’s gross domestic product (GDP) growth projection for 2022-23 to 6.8% from the earlier 7.4%, citing the impact of a slowing global economy, stubborn inflation, rising interest rates and the Ukraine war.

Concept note-4: -World Bank forecasts India’s FY23 GDP at 6.9%; warns of global recession. NEW DELHI: The World Bank on Tuesday retained India’s economic growth at 6.9% for financial year 2022-23.

Concept note-5: -The Union Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman tabled the Economic Survey 2022-23 in Parliament today, which projects a baseline GDP growth of 6.5 per cent in real terms in FY24.
