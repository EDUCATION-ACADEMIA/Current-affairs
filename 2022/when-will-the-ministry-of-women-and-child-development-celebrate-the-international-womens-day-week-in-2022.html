When will the Ministry of Women and Child Development celebrate the International Women’s Day week in 2022?

(A) 08 to 16 March

(B) 08 to 15 March

(C) 02 to 08 March

(D) ** 01 to 08 March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Women and Child Development will celebrate the International Women’s Day week from 1st to 8th March 2022 as an ‘Iconic Week’ as a part of ‘Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav’.

Concept note-2: -International Women’s Day 2022-Accelerate Equality: Experience the Impact When you Break the Bias. On the occasion of International Women’s Day 2022, we at the World Bank Group will celebrate women’s achievements and contributions with a series of events and engagements – we look forward to your participation!

Concept note-3: -What is National Women’s Day in India 2022 theme? Although National Women’s Day is widely celebrated in India, there is no specific theme designated for the celebrations. On the other hand, for events like International Women’s Day, the theme for 2022 was “#BreakTheBias."

Concept note-4: -Celebrate International Women’s Week, the week of March 8th, during International Women’s Day. According to the website Gender Champions, the week sprang from the day’s observance because there were so many events that it was difficult to attend them in one day.

Concept note-5: -International Women’s Day 2023: “DigitALL: Innovation and technology for gender equality” The United Nations Observance of IWD recognizes and celebrates the women and girls who are championing the advancement of transformative technology and digital education.
