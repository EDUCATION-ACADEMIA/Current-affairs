As of October 2022, how many beaches in India have been awarded the Blue Flag Certification?

(A) ** 12

(B) 15

(C) 20

(D) 25

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Blue Flag Beaches List in India 2023- Know Top Indian Beaches. Do you want to know the total number of blue flag beaches in India? Till October 2022, there are total 12 blue flag beaches in India. The certification is not only provided to beaches but also to marinas and tourism boats.

Concept note-2: -One of India’s eight blue flag-certified beaches is located in the Honnavar Taluk of the Uttara Kannada District, at Kasarkod Beach. The 5-kilometer-long Kasarkod beach is located in Kerala.

Concept note-3: -This takes the number of beaches certified under the Blue Flag certification to twelve (12).

Concept note-4: -The Minicoy Thundi and Kadmat beaches in Lakshadweep are the latest to be added to India’s tally of 12 Blue Flag beaches.
