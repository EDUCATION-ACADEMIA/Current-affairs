India released its first national report on the state of the climate crisis titled “Assessment of climate change over the Indian region”. Who published this report?

(A) MacMillan India Ltd.

(B) ** Spring Nature

(C) Taxman Publications Limited

(D) Hachette India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India published its first-ever national forecast report on the impact of global warming Assessment of Climate Change over the Indian Region.

Concept note-2: -In 1896, a seminal paper by Swedish scientist Svante Arrhenius first predicted that changes in atmospheric carbon dioxide levels could substantially alter the surface temperature through the greenhouse effect. In 1938, Guy Callendar connected carbon dioxide increases in Earth’s atmosphere to global warming.

Concept note-3: -The first Assessment of Climate Change over the Indian Region has been published by the Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES). Hence 1 statement is correct. It is India’s first-ever national forecast on the impact of global warming on the subcontinent in the coming century.

Concept note-4: -The global average temperature in the last century has gone up by 1.1°C, according to the latest estimates by the IPCC.

Concept note-5: -The National Action Plan on climate change (NAPCC) encompasses eight missions in the specific areas of Solar Energy, Enhanced Energy Efficiency, Sustainable Habitat, Water, Sustaining the Himalayan Eco-system, Strategic Knowledge for Climate Change, Green India, and Sustainable Agriculture.
