Which state has launched India’s first Night Navigation mobile app for ferry services on a river?

(A) Gujarat

(B) West Bengal

(C) ** Assam

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Guwahati: The first night navigation mobile application in rivers in the country was launched for ferries on Brahmaputra river in Assam on Saturday, an official spokesperson said.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Assam. The first-night navigation mobile application in rivers in the country was launched for ferries on the Brahmaputra river in Assam, in Feb 2022.

Concept note-3: -The night navigation mobile application was developed by the transport department in association with K Raju, the principal scientist of IIT Madras and will survey the draft of the rivers, the spokesperson said.
