Which edition of the ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup has started in New Zealand?

(A) ** 12th Edition

(B) 5th edition

(C) 9th Edition

(D) 7th Edition

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2022 ICC Women’s Cricket World Cup was the twelfth edition of the Women’s Cricket World Cup, which was held in New Zealand in March and April 2022.

Concept note-2: -ICC World Cup 2023 host will be the 13th edition of the men’s Cricket World Cup inthe country India and This is the first time that the entire tournament of ICC World Cup 2023 host will be held in India. India already has hosted the World Cup thrice in the past along with other countries in 1987,1996 and 2011.

Concept note-3: -The 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup was the 12th Cricket World Cup, a quadrennial One Day International (ODI) cricket tournament contested by men’s national teams and organised by the International Cricket Council (ICC).

Concept note-4: -ICC Women’s World Cup 2022 began on 4 March 2022 in New Zealand. The 8 teams who are part of it are India, Pakistan, Australia, Bangladesh, New Zealand, South Africa, England, and West Indies. The ICC Women’s World Cup 2022 final is scheduled to be played on 3rd April at Hagley Oval in Christchurch.

Concept note-5: -The event is organised by the sport’s governing body, the International Cricket Council (ICC), with the first edition having been held in England in 2009.
