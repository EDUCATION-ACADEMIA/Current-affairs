Which rule is expected to be introduced in IPL 2023?

(A) Set off Rule

(B) Effect Play Rule

(C) ** Impact Player Rule

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘Impact Player Rule’ allows a team to have four such players named in a squad of 15 before the match and they can be substitute any time during a team’s innings by the 14th over. However, that player has to be an Indian in case the playing XI has all four overseas players.

Concept note-2: -In IPL 2023 season each team will play 14 league matches in total. Among those 14 matches, 7 will be played in their home ground while the remaining 7 will be played in their opponent’s home ground(away).

Concept note-3: -IPL 2023 Retention IPL 2023 auction will be held in December tentatively and the IPL governing council has finalized the retention rules for the franchises. As per the new IPL 2023 auction retention rules, each franchise will be allowed to retain a maximum of three players and two Right to Match or RTM cards.

Concept note-4: -The official broadcaster of the IPL 2023 in India is Star Sports Network. This means that users will be able to watch all of the Indian Premier League 2023 matches on Star Sports Channels. Because Viacom 18 owns the tournament’s digital rights, live streaming of the matches will be available on the Jio TV app.

Concept note-5: -IPL 2023 auction rules There won’t be a Right to Match (RTM) card option in the IPL auction, unlike prior years. Chennai Super Kings – Rs.
