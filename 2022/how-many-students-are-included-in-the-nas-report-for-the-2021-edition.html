How many students are included in the NAS report for the 2021 edition?

(A) 30 lakh students

(B) 15 lakh students

(C) 38 lakh students

(D) ** 34 lakh students

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The latest NAS was conducted in November 2021, covering nearly 34 lakh students from more than 1 lakh schools across states.

Concept note-2: -What are the Key Highlights of the NAS 2021? National Average: The national average percentage of students for class third was 59%, which declined by 10% to 49% in class fifth. It further declined to 41.9% in class eight and then 37.8% in class 10.

Concept note-3: -The NAS 2021 was held on 12.11. 2021 at all India level and covered (a) Government Schools (Central Government and State Government); (b) Government Aided Schools; and (c) Private Unaided Schools.

Concept note-4: -NAS will be conducted in Language, Mathematics & EVS for class 3 & 5; Language, Mathematics, Science & Social Science for class 8 and Language, Mathematics, Science, Social Science and English for class 10.

Concept note-5: -The NAS test will be conducted for mathematics, English, social sciences, science and modern Indian languages.
