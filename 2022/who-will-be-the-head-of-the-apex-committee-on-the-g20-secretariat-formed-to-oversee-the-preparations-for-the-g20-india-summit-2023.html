Who will be the head of the Apex Committee on the G20 Secretariat, formed to oversee the preparations for the G20 India Summit 2023?

(A) G20 Sherpa

(B) Home Minister

(C) External Affairs Minister

(D) ** Prime Minister

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Secretariat will be guided by an Apex Committee headed by Prime Minister, and comprising Finance Minister, Home Minister, External Affairs Minister, and G20 Sherpa (Commerce & Industry, Textiles, Consumer Affairs, Food and Public Distribution Minister), to provide overall guidance to India’s G20 Presidency.

Concept note-2: -India’s G -20 Presidency India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023.

Concept note-3: -“Indonesia will hold the G20 Presidency in DEC 2022 while India will hold the Presidency a year after,” said Retno. Indonesia’s presidency commenced on 1 December 2021, following the transfer of presidency from Italian Prime Minister Mario Draghi to Indonesian president Joko Widodo at the closing of the Rome summit.

Concept note-4: -The G20 Summit 2023 will be held in India this year. It will be held in New Delhi at Pragati Maidan.

Concept note-5: -The Indian G20 Presidency’s theme of ‘One Earth, One Family, One Future’ sets the tone for the 2023 Infrastructure Agenda under the 2023 Indian G-20 Presidency.
