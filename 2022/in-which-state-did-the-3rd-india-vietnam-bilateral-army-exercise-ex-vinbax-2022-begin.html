In which state did the 3rd India-Vietnam Bilateral Army Exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” begin?

(A) Uttarakhand

(B) Gujarat

(C) Rajasthan

(D) ** Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The third edition of Ex VINBAX 2022 was held between 1st August and 20th August, 2022 in Chandimandir.

Concept note-2: -Vietnam-India Bilateral Army Exercise VINBAX 2022 concluded at Chandimandir with an impressive display of Multi Agency Humanitarian Assistance in Disaster Relief (HADR) demonstration and an equipment display showcasing the Make in India and Atmanirbhar Bharat initiatives.

Concept note-3: -Third edition of Vietnam-India bilateral army exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” to begin at Chandimandir in Haryana. The 3rd Edition of Vietnam-India Bilateral Army Exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” will begin today at Chandimandir. It will continue till 20th August.

Concept note-4: -The 3rd Edition of Vietnam India Bilateral Army Exercise “Ex VINBAX 2022” is scheduled to be conducted at Chandimandir from 01 to 20 Aug 2022. The exercise is a sequel to previously conducted bilateral exercise in Vietnam in 2019 and a major milestone in strengthening the bilateral relations between India and Vietnam.

Concept note-5: -Ans : The first protocol for defence collaboration was signed in 1994. VINBAX (Vietnam and India Army Exercise) is the first army exercise in India and Vietnam. India and Vietnam continue unrestricted.
