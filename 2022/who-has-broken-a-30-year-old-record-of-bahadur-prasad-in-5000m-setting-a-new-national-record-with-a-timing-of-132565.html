Who has broken a 30-year-old record of Bahadur Prasad in 5000m, setting a new national record with a timing of 13:25.65?

(A) Serpa Sanjay Thapa

(B) Bahadur Prasad

(C) ** Avinash Sable

(D) Veer Singh Rana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s Avinash Sable broke a 30-year-old record of Bahadur Prasad in 5000m, setting a new national record with a timing of 13:25.65 at the Sound Running Track meet in San Juan Capistrano, USA. The 27-year-old from Maharashtra broke the old record of 13:29.70 set by Bahadur Prasad in 1992.

Concept note-2: -Avinash Sable’s records Avinash Sable’s most recent mark and the standing men’s 3000m steeplechase national record is an 8:11.20 run he recorded at the Commonwealth Games 2022 in Birmingham to win the silver medal.

Concept note-3: -PT Usha has a countrywide document of 45 years in Steeple Chase. Who’s PT Usha? PT Usha is a retired subject and song athlete. She has received several medals for India in the Olympics also.

Concept note-4: -Marathon world record On September 25, 2022, Kenyan Eluid Kipchoge beat his previous marathon world record, running the 26.2 miles (42.2 km) Berlin Marathon in 2 hours, 2 minutes, and 9 seconds.
