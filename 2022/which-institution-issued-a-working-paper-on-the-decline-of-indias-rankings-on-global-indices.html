Which institution issued a working paper on the decline of India’s rankings on global indices?

(A) ** Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister

(B) NITI Aayog

(C) Department of Economic Affairs

(D) Department of Administrative Reforms and Public Grievances

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Economic Advisory Council to the Prime Minister, EAC-PM has issued a working paper that says that the decline in India’s rankings on a number of global opinion-based indices are due to the “serious problems” with the methodology used in these perception-based indices.

Concept note-2: -India’s Rank in Various Index 2022 PDF India is ranked 40th in the index. India was ranked 46th in the Global Innovation Index for 2021, a leap of 6 positions.

Concept note-3: -India is placed in the category of “Flawed Democracy” and its rank deteriorated sharply from 27 in 2014 to 53 in 2020 and then improved a bit to 46 in 2021. The decline in rank has been on account of decline in scores primarily in the categories- Civil Liberties and Political Culture.

Concept note-4: -2022 India’s rank: Out of 180 countries, India is at the least 5 with a grade of 18.9. The country has also performed poorly on various measures such as control of corruption, the rule of law, and government effectiveness.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is the World Bank.
