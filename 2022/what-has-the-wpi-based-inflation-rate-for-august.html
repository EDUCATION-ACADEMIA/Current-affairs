What has the WPI-based inflation rate for August?

(A) 14.41 percent

(B) 13.41 percent

(C) ** 12.41 percent

(D) 11.41 per cent

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The rate of inflation based on WPI Food Index decreased from 9.93% in August, 2022 to 8.08% in September, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Final index for the month of August, 2022 (Base Year: 2011-12=100): For the month of August, 2022 the final Wholesale Price Index and inflation rate for ‘All Commodities’ (Base: 2011-12=100) stood at 153.2 and 12.48% respectively.

Concept note-3: -By current projections, inflation is forecast to average 6.7 per cent in 2022-23 and 5.2 per cent in the first half of 2023-24.

Concept note-4: -The annual rate of inflation based on all India Wholesale Price Index (WPI) falls to 4.95% (Provisional) for the month of December, 2022 (over December, 2021) against 5.85% recorded in November, 2022.

Concept note-5: -The Labor Department on Tuesday reported its consumer-price index rose 8.3% in August from the same month a year ago, down from 8.5% in July and from 9.1% in June, which was the highest inflation rate in four decades. The CPI measures what consumers pay for goods and services.
