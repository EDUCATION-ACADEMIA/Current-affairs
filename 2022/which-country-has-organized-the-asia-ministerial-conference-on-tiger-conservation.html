Which country has organized the ‘Asia Ministerial Conference on Tiger Conservation’?

(A) ** Malaysia

(B) India

(C) UK

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which country has organised the ‘Asia Ministerial Conference on Tiger Conservation’? Notes: Government of Malaysia and Global Tiger Forum (GTF) jointly organised the 4th Asia Ministerial Conference on Tiger Conservation.

Concept note-2: -4th Asia Ministerial Conference on Tiger Conservation was recently organised by the Government of Malaysia and Global Tiger Forum (GTF). The conference is an important event for reviewing progress towards the Global Tiger Recovery Programme and commitments to tiger conservation.

Concept note-3: -The Tiger Range Countries Summit is scheduled to be held at Vladivostok, Russia on 5th September 2022.

Concept note-4: -On 12th-14th April 2016, the 3rd Asia Ministerial Conference on Tiger Conservation was held in New Delhi, India. This was the latest meeting in the Tx2 process to double wild tiger numbers by the year 2022.

Concept note-5: -Ranipur Wildlife Sanctuary will be the 54th tiger reserve in India.
