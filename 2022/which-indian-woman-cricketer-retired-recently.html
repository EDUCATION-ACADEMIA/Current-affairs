Which Indian woman cricketer retired recently?

(A) Smriti Mandhana

(B) Mithali Raj

(C) Shafali Verma

(D) ** Jhulan Goswami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 39-year-old, Jhulan Goswami, played finished with 44 wickets in 12 Tests, 255 wickets in 204 ODIs and 56 wickets in 68 T20Is for India.

Concept note-2: -Mithali Raj announces retirement from international cricket.

Concept note-3: -India’s star batter Suresh Raina retired from all forms of cricket in the year 2022. He left international cricket in 2020. Leg-spinner Rahul Sharma also announced retirement from cricket. He played in limited overs cricket for India and also represented Chennai and Pune in IPL.

Concept note-4: -India women’s cricket team’s ODI and Test captain Mithali Raj, who led the team in the recently concluded Women’s Cricket World Cup 2022 in New Zealand, announced her retirement from all forms of the game on Wednesday, June 08, 2022.
