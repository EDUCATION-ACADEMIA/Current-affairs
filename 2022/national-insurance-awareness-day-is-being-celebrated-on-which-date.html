National Insurance Awareness Day is being celebrated on which date?

(A) June 27

(B) June 30

(C) ** June 28

(D) June 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The National Insurance Awareness Day is observed on 28 June. The main objective of the day is to make people aware about the several benefits of investing in an insurance plan or policy.

Concept note-2: -National Insurance Awareness Day (June 28th) | Days Of The Year.

Concept note-3: -Therefore, the National Insurance Awareness Day is observed annually on June 28 to make people aware of the benefits of investing in an insurance plan. The day also serves as a reminder to people to check if all their insurance payments (or renewals) are up to date.

Concept note-4: -September is National Life Insurance Awareness Month, which provides a great reminder about this important tool, and the impact that life insurance can have on a family or business after the death of a loved one, key person, or owner.

Concept note-5: -Introduction to Insurance Awareness – Meaning. Insurance implies the protection from financial loss. A form of risk management, insurance is mainly used to hedge against the risk of a contingency or uncertain loss.
