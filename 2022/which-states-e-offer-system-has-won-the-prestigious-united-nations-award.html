Which state’s e-offer system has won the prestigious United Nations award?

(A) Manipur

(B) ** Meghalaya

(C) Goa

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Meghalaya.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The ‘e-Proposal system’ of Meghalaya’s Planning department has won the prestigious UN Award-World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) Prizes 2022. The system is a part of the Meghalaya government’s flagship programme, Meghalaya Enterprise Architecture (MeghEA).

Concept note-3: -The Meghalaya government key initiative of e-proposal system, part of Meghalaya Enterprise Architect has won a coveted UN Award- World Summit on the Information Society Forum (WSIS) prize at Geneva in Switzerland on Tuesday.

Concept note-4: -At the United Nations World Summit on the Information Society Forum 2022, an initiative by the Meghalaya government Tuesday received the best project award in the category of “the role of governments in the promotion of ICTs for development". The initiative was named Meghalaya Enterprise Architecture.

Concept note-5: -In a significant achievement and recognition to country’s efforts against hypertension, India has won a UN award for its initiative to control and prevent hypertension under the government’s National Health Mission.
