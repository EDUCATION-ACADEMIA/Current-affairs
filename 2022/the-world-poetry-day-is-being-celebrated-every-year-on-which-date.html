The World Poetry Day is being celebrated every year on which date?

(A) March 17

(B) ** March 21

(C) March 18

(D) March 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Held every year on 21 March, World Poetry Day celebrates one of humanity’s most treasured forms of cultural and linguistic expression and identity.

Concept note-2: -Held every year on 21 March, World Poetry Day celebrates one of humanity’s most treasured forms of cultural and linguistic expression and identity.

Concept note-3: -National Poetry Day is the annual mass celebration on the first Thursday of October that encourages everyone to make, experience and share poetry with family and friends.

Concept note-4: -World Poetry Day is celebrated every year on March 21, celebrating a linguistic expression that people from all cultures can identify with. Poetry can be found in the history of every nation, and brings us together under shared values and common humanity. The most basic of poems have the power to stir dialogue.

Concept note-5: -This year’s National Poetry Day is on Thursday 6 October 2022. The theme for National Poetry Day 2022 is The Environment.
