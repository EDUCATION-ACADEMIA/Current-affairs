Which state passed an ordinance to increase the reservation for the Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes to 17 % and 7 %?

(A) ** Karnataka

(B) Jharkhand

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From 2015 onwards Kerala has implemented a 55% reservation for all posts of its local self governing bodies. On 21 July 2021, Karnataka became the first state in the country to provide one percent reservation for the transgender community in all government services.

Concept note-2: -After the Constitution was promulgated, MHA, in its Resolution of 13-9-50, provided 5 per cent reservation of STs apart from the percentage fixed for SCs already in force. The 1951 Census showed that the percentage of SCS in the total population was 15.05 per cent and that of ST 6.31 per cent.

Concept note-3: -Article 335 “The claims of the members of the Scheduled Castes and the Scheduled Tribes shall be taken into consideration, consistently with the maintenane of efficiency of administration, in the making of appointments to services and posts in connection with • the affairs of the Union or of a State."

Concept note-4: -1.3 Articles 341 and 342 of the Constitution which define as to who would be Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes with respect to any State or Union Territory are quoted below: 341.
