The IN-SPACe has established by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in which Indian city?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) Pune

(C) ** Ahmedabad

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hon’ble Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated IN-SPACe Headquarters at Bopal, Ahmedabad.

Concept note-2: -An independent nodal agency under the Department of Space – the Indian National Space Promotion and Authorization Centre (IN-SPACe) was announced on June 24, 2020. This will enhance the diffusion of space technology and boost the space economy within the country, for a resurgent, Aatmanirbhar Bharat ( ).

Concept note-3: -Indian National Space Promotion and Authorisation Centre (IN-SPACe) IN-SPACe is an autonomous nodal agency, approved by the Union Government and will be governed by the Department of Space. This organisation shall act as a medium between ISRO and the private space sector in India.

Concept note-4: -Organisation. According to Secretary (Space) and chairperson of ISRO, Kailasavadivoo Sivan, IN–SPACe is an autonomous body with its own board-with some members from the private sector and industry -and chairperson.
