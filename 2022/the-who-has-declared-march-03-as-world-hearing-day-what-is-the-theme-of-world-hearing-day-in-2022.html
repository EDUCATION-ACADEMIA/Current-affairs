The WHO has declared March 03 as World Hearing Day. What is the theme of World Hearing Day in 2022?

(A) Hearing Care for All

(B) Check your hearing

(C) Hearing for Life. Don’t let hearing loss limit you

(D) ** To hear for life, listen with care

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Hearing Day 2022 with the theme “To hear for life, listen with care” will focus on the importance and means of hearing loss prevention through safe listening, with the following key messages: It is possible to have good hearing across the life course through ear and hearing care

Concept note-2: -World Hearing Day is celebrated on March 3 every year. This year, the theme focusses on the importance and means of hearing loss prevention.

Concept note-3: -The World Health Organization and all stakeholders are eagerly preparing for the upcoming World Hearing Day 2023. With this year’s theme, Ear and hearing care for all!

Concept note-4: -The posters should focus on the theme of World Hearing Day 2022 “TO HEAR FOR LIFE, LISTEN WITH CARE”.

Concept note-5: -The WHO, on March 3 in 2007, observed World Hearing Day for the first time. In 2016, they decided to declare this day as World Hearing Day. It was known as International Ear Care Day before that.
