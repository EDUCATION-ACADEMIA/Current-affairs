A mobile app ‘Khelo India Uni Games 2021’ has been launched for the 2nd edition of Khelo India University Games. 2nd edition of the Khelo India University Games is being held in which place?

(A) ** Bengaluru, Karnataka

(B) Chennai, Tamil Nadu

(C) Jaipur, Rajasthan

(D) Mumbai, Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -From 24th April 2022 to 3rd May 2022, the Khelo India University Games 2021 was successfully conducted at Jain University, Bengaluru. This is the second edition of these games. How many games were held in this competition? In this competition, a total of 20 games were played.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bengaluru. Vice President of India M. Venkaiah Naidu declared the second Khelo India University Games 2021 open at the Sree Kanteerava Indoor Stadium, Bengaluru on 24 April 2022.

Concept note-3: -The mascot, Veera, is an elephant. The official Khelo India mobile app was launched at the ceremony. KIUG 2021, which is the second edition of the meet, will be hosted by Jain University in Bengaluru from April 24 to May 3. It was postponed last year due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

Concept note-4: -Khelo India University Games 2022: From venues in Bengaluru, to sports to participants and former winners, here’s the lowdown on the event. The Khelo India University Games (KIUG) are all set to return in April. The event which was to be held in 2021 was delayed due to Covid-19 and will finally be held in Bengaluru.
