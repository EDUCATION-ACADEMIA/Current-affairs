For what the International Cricket Council has entered into a partnership with UNICEF?

(A) Strengthen bilateral ties

(B) Promote good health

(C) Strengthen trade ties

(D) ** Promote Transgender equality

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Cricket Council (ICC) and UNICEF have launched a global partnership to empower women and girls and promote inclusion and gender equality through cricket.

Concept note-2: -The ICC is the global governing body for cricket. Representing 108 members, the ICC governs and administrates the game and works with our members to grow the sport. The ICC is also responsible for the staging of all ICC Events.

Concept note-3: -Bira91 and Cricket The partnership, which runs through 2023, confirms Bira91 as the official sponsor of ICC global tournaments including the ICC Cricket World Cup, ICC T20 World Cup, ICC World Test Championship, ICC U19 Cricket World Cup and the ICC Women’s Championship.

Concept note-4: -Mohammad Hafeez, former Pakistani all rounder and captain, will be serving as Pakistan’s International Cricket Council (ICC)’s ambassador at the 2022 T20 World Cup.

Concept note-5: -Greg Barclay has been unanimously re-elected as chairman of the International Cricket Council (ICC) for a second two-year term.
