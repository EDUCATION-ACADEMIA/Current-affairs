Centre has launched the revamped website and mobile app of the Central Government Health Scheme named MyCGHS. In which year was the CHHS scheme launched?

(A) 1991

(B) 1980

(C) 1973

(D) ** 1954

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mansukh Mandaviya , Union Minister for Health and Family Welfare digitally launched the revamped CGHS and mobile app, “MyCGHS”, on 24 Jan 2022. It will greatly benefit the 40 lakh people connected with the CGHS.

Concept note-2: -Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS) is a health scheme for serving / retired Central Government employees and their families. The scheme was started in 1954 in Delhi.

Concept note-3: -Notes: Union Health Minister Mansukh Mandaviya launched the revamped ‘Central Government Health Scheme’ CGHS website and “MyCGHS” mobile application. The website shall benefit over 40 lakh beneficiaries with real-time information.

Concept note-4: -Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. Government of India.

Concept note-5: -CGHS is a special health scheme for the government employees. The medical facilities are provided through Wellness Centres (previously referred to as CGHS Dispensaries) / polyclinics under Allopathic, Ayurveda, Yoga, Unani, Sidha and Homeopathic systems of medicines.
