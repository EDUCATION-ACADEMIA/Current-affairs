Concept note-1: -The Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD) on Tuesday released its budget estimates for the Financial Year 2023-2024, with the estimated expenditure standing at ₹16,023 crore. Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI, Feb 1 (Reuters) - The Indian government on Wednesday unveiled a 45 trillion rupees ($549.14 billion) spending budget for the next fiscal year starting April to boost economic growth, while aiming to lower fiscal deficit before elections due next year.
