Who has been appointed as the chairperson of the CBSE?

(A) V. Krishnaswamy

(B) Manoj Ahuja

(C) Indrajit Mahanty

(D) ** Nidhi Chibber

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The official announcement has been made by the Central Board of Secondary Education that Vineet Joshi, Additional Secretary, Department of Higher Education, will take charge. The position has been allocated to Vineet from February 14, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Ms. Nidhi Chhibber IAS (Chhattisgarh 1994), Additional Secretary, Ministry of Heavy Industries , has been appointed as Chairperson, Central Board of Secondary Education in the rank and pay of Additional Secretary to the Government of India by temporarily upgrading the post.
