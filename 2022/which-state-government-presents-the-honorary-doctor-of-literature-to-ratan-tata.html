Which state Government Presents the Honorary Doctor of Literature to Ratan Tata?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Gujarat

(C) ** Maharashtra

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Industrialist and philanthropist Ratan Tata was presented with Honorary Doctor of Literature by Maharashtra Governor Bhagat Singh Koshyari at Raj Bhavan Mumbai yesterday. The Honorary doctorate was presented to Mr. Ratan at the First Special Convocation of the HSNC University.

Concept note-2: -Litt. to Padma Vibhushan Ratan Tata the first ever Convocation ceremony of HSNC University.

Concept note-3: -In his acceptance speech, Ratan Tata said, “This university has been constituted with a view of creating young people with capability to lead our country in the coming future, with honesty, purpose, and responsibility.

Concept note-4: -By India Today Web Desk: Former Chairman of Tata Sons Ratan Tata was awarded ‘Sewa Ratna’ by the RSS-affiliated Sewa Bharti on Friday for his contribution to the field of philanthropy.

Concept note-5: -7. How can I get honorary doctorate degree in India? The honorary degree may be awarded to a person who has made a significant contribution to the cultural, scientific and / or social development of the state, nation or world. The contribution should last for a few years and be sustainable in nature.
