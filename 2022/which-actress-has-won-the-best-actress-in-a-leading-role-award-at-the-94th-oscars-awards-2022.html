Which actress has won the “Best Actress in a Leading Role” Award at the 94th Oscars Awards 2022?

(A) Nicole Kidman

(B) Scarlett Johansson

(C) Kristen Stewart

(D) ** Jessica Chastain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Will Smith and Jessica Chastain won Best Actor and Best Actress trophies, respectively. The 94th Academy Awards got underway Sunday off-camera, with the first eight awards on the night being handed out at the Dolby Theatre before the start of the broadcast.

Concept note-2: -However, his previous act did steal the award thunder from him. Best actress went to Jessica Chastain, making it her first ever Oscar award. Chastain won for her empathetic portrayal of the televangelist Tammy Faye in ‘The Eyes of Tammy Faye’, a movie she also produced.

Concept note-3: -The winners of the 94th Academy Awards or Oscar awards were announced on 28 March 2022. Will Smith was declared best actor for ‘King Richard. While Jessica Chastain won the best actress Oscar for “The Eyes of Tammy Faye."
