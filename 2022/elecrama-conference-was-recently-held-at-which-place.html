Elecrama conference was recently held at which place?

(A) Gurugram

(B) ** Noida

(C) New Delhi

(D) Faridabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ELECRAMA, the largest stand-alone showcase of the Indian Electrical and Allied Electronics Industry by IEEMA is slated to commence from February 18th to 22nd, 2023 at India Expo Mart, Greater Noida.

Concept note-2: -We are pleased to inform you Indian Electrical & Electronics Manufacturers’ Association (IEEMA) is organizing 15th edition of - ELECRAMA-2023 with the theme “Reimagine Energy for Sustainable Future”, is scheduled to be held from 18th to 22nd February 2023 at India Expo Mart Ltd., Greater NOIDA, Delhi NCR, India.

Concept note-3: -Jitendra K Agarwal – Chairman, ELECRAMA 2023 At IEEMA AGM 2022.

Concept note-4: -Elecrama is the largest conclave of the power sector ecosystem in India. It showcases innovations and technologies in Electrical, industrial electronics and Allied industries. This electric summit brings together a plethora of solutions that powers the planet.
