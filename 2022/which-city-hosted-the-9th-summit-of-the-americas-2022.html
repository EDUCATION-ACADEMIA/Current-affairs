Which city hosted the 9th Summit of the Americas 2022?

(A) Ottawa

(B) New York

(C) ** Los Angeles

(D) Toronto

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The convening of the Ninth Summit of the Americas will take place in June 2022 in Los Angeles, California, our second largest city and one with deep and robust ties throughout our hemisphere.

Concept note-2: -The United States hosted the Ninth Summit of the Americas in Los Angeles on June 6-10, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Ninth Summit of the Americas was an international conference held in Los Angeles, United States, from June 6 to 10, 2022. The theme was “Building a Sustainable, Resilient, and Equitable Future".

Concept note-4: -Hancock, and U.S. National Summit Coordinator Kevin O’Reilly announced that the first Cities Summit of the Americas will take place April 26-28, 2023, in Denver.
