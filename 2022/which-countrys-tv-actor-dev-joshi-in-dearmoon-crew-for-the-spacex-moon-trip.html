Which country’s TV actor Dev Joshi in ‘DearMoon CREW’ for the SpaceX Moon Trip?

(A) ** India

(B) Iran

(C) Iraq

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Baal Veer actor Dev Joshi is the only Indian selected for the dearMoon project. The week-long trip around the moon is led by Japanese billionaire Yusaku Maezawa on the spacecraft Starship developed by Elon Musk’s SpaceX.

Concept note-2: -Indian actor Dev Joshi is among the eight people Japanese billionaire Yusaku Maezawa has picked for a trip around the moon.

Concept note-3: -Baal Veer fame Dev Joshi is one of the eight-member team that has been selected to board the Starship spacecraft to travel around the moon’s orbit! Developed by billionaire Elon Musk, the round trip will be of six days and headed by Japanese billionaire Yusaku Maezawa.

Concept note-4: -K-pop star T.O.P, American DJ Steve Aoki, American YouTuber Tim Dodd, Czech choreographer Yemi A.D., Irish photographer Rhiannon Adam, British photographer Karim Iliya, American filmmaker Brendan Hall will also join him in the first civilian moon mission led by Japanese billionaire Yusaku Maezawa.

Concept note-5: -Japanese billionaire invites ‘Baalveer’ actor Dev Joshi to join SpaceX moon mission. The phrase “To the moon and back” could prove true for actor Dev Joshi, who is known for his work on the television show “Baalveer". Joshi is the youngest among the 7 people who will fly to the moon in 2023 in a SpaceX flight.
