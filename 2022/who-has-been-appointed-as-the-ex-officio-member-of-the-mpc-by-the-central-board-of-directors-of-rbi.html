Who has been appointed as the ex-officio member of the MPC by the Central Board of Directors of RBI?

(A) Mridul Saggar

(B) Ashima Goyal

(C) Shashanka Bhide

(D) ** Rajiv Ranjan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The composition of the current monetary policy committee is as follows: Governor of the Reserve Bank of India – Chairperson, ex officio - Shaktikanta Das. Deputy Governor of the Bank in charge of monetary policy – Michael Debrata Patra. Executive director of the Bank in charge of monetary policy – Rajiv Ranjan.

Concept note-2: -The central board of the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) approved the appointment of executive director Rajiv Ranjan as an ex-officio member of the Monetary Policy Committee (MPC).

Concept note-3: -RBI Governor is the ex-officio chairperson of the Central Board of Directors of the RBI.

Concept note-4: -The Governor of the Reserve Bank of India is the ex-officio chairperson of the RBI. Q. Who among the following is the Chairperson of the 13th Finance Commission?

Concept note-5: -“The Board approved the nomination of Dr. Rajiv Ranjan, Executive Director as an ex-officio member of the Monetary Policy Committee,” RBI said after the board met. Ranjan was an advisor in-charge in the monetary policy department, before he was elevated as an executive director.
