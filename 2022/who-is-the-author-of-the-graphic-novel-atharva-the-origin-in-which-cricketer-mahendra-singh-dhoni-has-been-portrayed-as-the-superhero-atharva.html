Who is the author of the graphic novel Atharva-The Origin, in which Cricketer Mahendra Singh Dhoni has been portrayed as the superhero Atharva?

(A) Shamik Dasgupta

(B) ** Ramesh Thamilmani

(C) Sarnath Banerjee

(D) Ashok Banker

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atharv, The Origin is a graphic novel with the story by Ramesh Thamilmani and illustrations by Virzu Studios, and the entire thing was quite unique to my taste. And yes, the face of the protagonist is M.S. Dhoni, and this idea is brilliant (to see from the marketing point of view.)

Concept note-2: -What is Atharva: The Origin? The novel is touted as India’s first three-dimensional graphic novel with a realistic visual interpretation of about 150 scenes. Former Indian cricket captain MS Dhoni as a mythological superhero on the cover of the novel.

Concept note-3: -The co-collaborators on the project Virzu Studios and MIDAS Deals Pvt ltd also released an official trailer on their YouTube Channel on 24 February 2022. Atharva is a story of a warrior leader portrayed by Captain Cool MS Dhoni.

Concept note-4: -The comic is developed in vertical reading format which makes it convenient for scrolling and reading on Pratilipi Comics’ app. MS Dhoni commented, “It is great to see all the hard work come to life in ‘Atharva; The Origin’. Pratilipi Comics has captured the essence of our story well in its comic adaptation.

Concept note-5: -MS Dhoni’s Graphic Novel Atharva.
