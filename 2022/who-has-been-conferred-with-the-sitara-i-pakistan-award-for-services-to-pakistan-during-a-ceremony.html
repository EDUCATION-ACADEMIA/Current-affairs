Who has been conferred with the Sitara-i-Pakistan Award for Services to Pakistan during a ceremony?

(A) Brian Lara

(B) ** Daren Sammy

(C) Jason Holder

(D) Dwayne Bravo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ans. (b) Sol. Former West Indies captain, Daren Sammy has been conferred with the Sitara-i-Pakistan Award for Services to Pakistan during a ceremony. The 38-year-old all-rounder was being recognized for his role in helping bring international cricket back to Pakistan.

Concept note-2: -Story highlights. Pakistan’s Babar Azam was one among three cricketers who recently received a civil award on the occasion of the country’s Independence Day for their exploits in the realm of sports.
