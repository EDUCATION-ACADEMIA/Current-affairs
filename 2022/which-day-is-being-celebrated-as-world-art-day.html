Which day is being celebrated as World Art Day?

(A) April 14

(B) April 12

(C) ** April 15

(D) April 13

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The IAA/AIAP and UNESCO founded World Art Day in 2012. They chose the date of April 15th as it coincided with Leonardo da Vinci’s birthday. Known as one of the most famous artists in history, Leonardo da Vinci has become a symbol of peace, freedom of expression, and tolerance and brotherhood.

Concept note-2: -By India Today Web Desk: World Art Day is celebrated every year on April 15, to promote the development, diffusion, and enjoyment of art. The day was adopted by the International Association of Art, a partner of UNESCO, to create awareness of creative activity worldwide.

Concept note-3: -World Art Day 2022 Theme 2022 marks the 75th year of India’s Independence and the 150th birth anniversary of Abanindranath Tagore. Therefore, the IAA decided that the theme of International Art Day 2022 would be ‘Bande Bharat’.

Concept note-4: -World Art Day is observed every year on 15 April globally. This day is being celebrated all over the world to emphasize the importance of art that nurtures creativity, innovation and cultural diversity for all peoples across the globe.

Concept note-5: -History of World Art Day World Art Day was declared on April 15 at the General Assembly of the International Association of Art. The first celebration was in 2012. The date was chosen to honor the birthday of the famous Leonardo da Vinci.
