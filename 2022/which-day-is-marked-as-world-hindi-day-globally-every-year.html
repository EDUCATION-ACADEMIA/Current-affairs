Which day is marked as World Hindi Day globally every year?

(A) January 09

(B) ** January 10

(C) January 06

(D) January 08

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Hindi Day (Vishwa Hindi Diwas) is a day celebrated on January 10th every year to promote the use of the Hindi language around the world.

Concept note-2: -World Hindi Day is celebrated on January 10th every year, whereas, Hindi Diwas is celebrated on September 14th every year to mark the adoption of Hindi written in the Devanagari script as the official language of India.

Concept note-3: -World Hindi Day 2023: World Hindi Day, also known as Vishwa Hindi Diwas, is observed on January 10 every year. The day aims to create awareness as well as promote the Hindi language across the world.

Concept note-4: -The date of January 10 was chosen as Hindi was spoken for the first time in the United Nations General Assembly on January 10, 1949.

Concept note-5: -Hindi Day (Hindi: , romanized: hindī divas) is celebrated in India to commemorate the date 14 September 1949 on which a compromise was reached-during the drafting of the Constitution of India-on the languages that were to have official status in the Republic of India.
