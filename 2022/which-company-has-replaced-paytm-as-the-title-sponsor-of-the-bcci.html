Which company has replaced Paytm as the title sponsor of the BCCI?

(A) Puma

(B) Dream11

(C) ** MasterCard

(D) Vivo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"The BCCI welcomes Mastercard as the Title Sponsor for home cricket matches for the 2022-23 season. Along with the international home series, the BCCI’s domestic tournaments are crucial as they are a stepping stone towards making India a strong international side."

Concept note-2: -MUMBAI: The Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) has signed Kewal Kiran Clothing Ltd (KKCL), maker of Killer jeans, as the official sponsor of the Indian cricket team, replacing gaming and esports company MPL, which has been looking at relinquishing its existing contract.

Concept note-3: -By India Today Web Desk: Team India’s existing title sponsor, Byju’s, is set to continue on with the Men in Blue until March 2023.

Concept note-4: -Mastercard replaces Paytm as BCCI title sponsor for 2022-23 season.

Concept note-5: -Apart from the BCCI jersey deal, Byju’s is also an official sponsor of International Cricket Council (ICC) events. In line with its global expansion plans, the company became the official sponsor of the FIFA World Cup 2022.
