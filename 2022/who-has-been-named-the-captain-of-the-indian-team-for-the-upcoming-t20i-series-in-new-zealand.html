Who has been named the Captain of the Indian team for the upcoming T20I series in New Zealand?

(A) Rishabh Pant

(B) Shikhar Dhawan

(C) Rohit Sharma

(D) ** Hardik Pandya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ADELAIDE: All-rounder Hardik Pandya was on Monday named India’s captain for the upcoming three-match T20I series in New Zealand as BCCI announced three different leaders for four upcoming assignments across formats.

Concept note-2: -Hardik Pandya will lead India’s T20I squad in its first home series of 2023 against Sri Lanka, the BCCI announced on Tuesday.

Concept note-3: -India’s T20I squad against New Zealand: Hardik Pandya (Captain), Suryakumar Yadav (vice-captain), Ishan Kishan (wk), Ruturaj Gaikwad, Shubman Gill, Deepak Hooda, Rahul Tripathi, Jitesh Sharma (wk), Washington Sundar, Kuldeep Yadav, Yuzvendra Chahal, Arshdeep Singh, Umran Malik, Shivam Mavi, Prithvi Shaw, Mukesh Kumar.
