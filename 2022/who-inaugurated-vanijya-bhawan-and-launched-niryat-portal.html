Who inaugurated ‘Vanijya Bhawan’ and launched NIRYAT Portal?

(A) Piyush Goyal

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) ** Narendra Modi

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister, Shri Narendra Modi inaugurated ‘Vanijya Bhawan’ and launched the NIRYAT portal in Delhi today. Union Ministers Shri Piyush Goyal , Shri Som Prakash and Smt Anupriya Patel were among those present on the occasion.

Concept note-2: -Vanijya Bhawan, the new premises of Ministry of Commerce and Industry will be unveiled by Prime Minister Tomorrow. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will inaugurate the new premises of the Ministry of Commerce and Industry Vanijya Bhawan on Thursday at 10:30 AM.

Concept note-3: -During the Programme, Prime Minister will also launch a new portal - NIRYAT (National Import-Export Record for Yearly Analysis of Trade) - which is developed as a one stop platform for stakeholders to get all necessary information related to India’s foreign trade.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi, June 22 (KNN) The NIRYAT (National Import-Export Record for Yearly Analysis of Trade) portal will be launched by Prime Minister Modi on June 23.
