Who was awarded Bihari Puraskar of KK Birla Foundation for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’?

(A) Biri Singh

(B) Birju Pathak

(C) Raju Srivastava

(D) ** Madhav Hada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. Kankariya was awarded the Puraskar for her 2018 novel ‘Hum Yahan The’ while Hada was awarded for his 2015 literary criticism book ‘Pachrang Chola Pahar Sakhi Ri’.

Concept note-2: -Writers Dr Madhav Hada and Madhu Kankariya were awarded the Bihari Puraskar on Wednesday. Noted writers Madhu Kankariya and Dr Madhav Hada on Wednesday were awarded the 31st and 32nd Bihari Puraskar, respectively. The Bihari Puraskar is one of the three literary awards instituted by KK Birla Foundation in 1991.

Concept note-3: -Bihari Puraskar It carries a cash prize of ₹2.5 lakh, a plaque and a citation. The first Bihari Puraskar was in 1991, to the famous poet Jaisingh Neeraj for his poetry compilation ‘Dhani Ka Aadmi’.

Concept note-4: -Madhu Kankariya won 2021 Bihari Puraskar for her book ‘Hum Yahan The’.

Concept note-5: -The Saraswati Samman is the most prestigious award of the KK Birla Foundation. 2. The Samman is given annually to an outstanding literary work published in Hindi only during the last 10 years.
