Who is the winner of Genesis Prize 2022?

(A) Steven Spielberg

(B) Rabbi Lord

(C) ** Albert Bourla

(D) Natan Sharansky

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On January 19, 2022, The Genesis Prize Foundation announced Dr. Albert Bourla, Chairman and CEO of Pfizer, as the 2022 Genesis Prize Laureate. Dr. Bourla was recognized for his leadership, determination, and willingness to assume great risks in delivering a safe and effective COVID-19 vaccine in record time.

Concept note-2: -The prize was established under the administration of the Genesis Prize Foundation, in partnership with the Israeli Prime Minister’s Office and the Jewish Agency for Israel. It has been referred to as the “Jewish Nobel Prize", causing some commentators to question the need for a “Jewish” Nobel Prize.

Concept note-3: -The Genesis Prize is located in Tel Aviv, Tel Aviv, Israel .
