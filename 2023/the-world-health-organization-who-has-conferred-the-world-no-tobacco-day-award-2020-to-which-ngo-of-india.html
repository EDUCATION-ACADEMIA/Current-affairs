The World Health Organization (WHO) has conferred the World No Tobacco Day Award 2020 to which NGO of India?

(A) GOONJ

(B) KATHA

(C) ** SEEDS

(D) KUTUMB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The award will be presented to the State Tobacco Control Cell of the Health Department in New Delhi in honour of World No Tobacco Day. The coveted award, which honoured the State Government’s achievements in reducing tobacco usage, was given by the National Tobacco Control Program in Jharkhand.

Concept note-2: -Recognising its efforts in controlling tobacco consumption, the World Health Organization (WHO) has selected Jharkhand for the World No Tobacco Day (WNTD) Award-2022, an official said on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -Saint Lucia’s Ministry of Health and Wellness received the award for its role in adoption in June 2020 of the Public Health (Smoking Control) Regulations, Statutory Instrument, 2020, No. 81. The regulation establishes a smoking ban in enclosed public and working places and on public transport.

Concept note-4: -Every year, WHO recognizes individuals or organizations in each of the six WHO Regions for their accomplishments in the area of tobacco control. This recognition takes the form of WHO Director-General Special Recognition Award and World No Tobacco Day Awards.

Concept note-5: -WHO Award to Jharkhand for Tobacco Control.
