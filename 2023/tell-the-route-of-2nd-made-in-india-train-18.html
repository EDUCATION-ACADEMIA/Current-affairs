Tell the route of 2nd ‘Made in India’ Train 18?

(A) ** New Delhi-Katra

(B) New Delhi-Varanasi

(C) New Delhi-Mumbai

(D) New Delhi-Srinagar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The second Vande Bharat Express is running from Delhi to Katra (Jammu and Kashmir), making the journey to Vaishno Devi faster than before. It was inaugurated by Amit Shah, Minister of Home Affairs on October 3, 2019, and started running commercially on October 5, 2019.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off the inaugural run on 15 February 2019, with the commercial run starting on 17 February 2019. It runs on the Delhi–Varanasi route, via Kanpur and Prayagraj, connecting Varanasi to the capital city and reducing travel time along the route by 15%.

Concept note-3: -The very first route is connecting the capital city of Delhi to the holy city of Varanasi (Uttar Pradesh), making stops at Thyagraj and Kanpur. This train runs five days a week, except Monday and Thursday. The second Vande Bharat Express is running from Delhi to Katra (Jammu and Kashmir).
