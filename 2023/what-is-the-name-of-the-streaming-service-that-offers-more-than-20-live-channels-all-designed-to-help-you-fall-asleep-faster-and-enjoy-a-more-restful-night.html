What is the name of the streaming service that offers more than 20 live channels, all designed to help you fall asleep faster and enjoy a more restful night?

(A) Sleepflix

(B) Napflix

(C) ** Restflix

(D) Relaxflix

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Restflix provides a constant stream of helpful channels to encourage rest and relaxation. Aside from insomnia, Restflix may also help you overcome tinnitus, nighttime anxiety, and everyday stress you experience.

Concept note-2: -All Night, a comedy series starring Jenn McAllister, Jake Short, and Tetona Jackson is available to stream now. Watch it on Hulu, Prime Video or Apple TV on your Roku device.

Concept note-3: -Sleep Easy naturally recreates the calming sound of moving air to block out disturbing background noises and create a relaxed, comforting environment so you can fall asleep and stay asleep. The secret of Sleep Easy as seen on TV is the soothing sound of white noise.

Concept note-4: -Our revenue model is subscription based. Restflix allows users to subscribe on an annual basis after a 7 day free trial.

Concept note-5: -Plenty of content to soothe you to sleep. Restflix has something for everyone, and the videos are all of equal audio and video quality-and all of them are relaxing. We were able to find what we personally preferred (we liked the electronic instrumentals over the nature sounds) and put it to use every night.
