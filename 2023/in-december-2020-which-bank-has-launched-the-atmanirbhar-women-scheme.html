In December, 2020 which bank has launched the Atmanirbhar Women Scheme

(A) Bank of India

(B) ** Bank of Baroda

(C) Union bank of India

(D) Central Bank of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is BOB. Bank of Baroda (BoB) has launched the Atmanirbhar Women Scheme as part of its Baroda Gold Loan.

Concept note-2: -Women can get loans at 0.50% concession, under this scheme. ii.In addition to this, the bank also offers agri-gold loan at 0.25% concession and retail loan at 0.50% concession, under this scheme. BoB was founded on 20 July 1908, by Maharaja Sayajirao Gaekwad III of Baroda.

Concept note-3: -SBI tie-up with Ministry of Culture for Development of Atmanirbhar Bharat Centre for Design.

Concept note-4: -This scheme aims to target the self-reliant woman in India. Under the scheme, the bank is offering loans at 0.50 per cent concession for women. The bank under the gold loan scheme is offering agri-gold loan at 0.25 per cent concession and for a retail loan at 0.50 per cent concession.
