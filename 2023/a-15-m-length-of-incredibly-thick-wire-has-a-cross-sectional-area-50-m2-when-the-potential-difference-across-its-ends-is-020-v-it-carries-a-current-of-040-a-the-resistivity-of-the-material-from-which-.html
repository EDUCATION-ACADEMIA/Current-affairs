(A) 6.0 m

(B) ** 1.7 m

(C) 1.1 m

(D) 9.4 m

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, the resistivity of the material is equal to 3. 14×10−7−m.

Concept note-2: -Answer: Resistance of wire = 25 Ohm.
