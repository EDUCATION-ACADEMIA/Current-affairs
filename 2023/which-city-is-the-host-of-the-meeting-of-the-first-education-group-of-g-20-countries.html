Which city is the host of the Meeting of the First Education Group of G-20 countries?

(A) Kochi

(B) ** Chennai

(C) Bengaluru

(D) Pune

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The G 20 education working group began its deliberations at Chennai this morning. The meeting was inaugurated by Union Minister of State Dr L. Murugan.

Concept note-2: -The first G20 Summit was held in 2008 in Washington DC (USA). It set the scene for the most dramatic reform of global finance in over 60 years. At the follow-up summit in 2009 in London (UK), the G20 agreed to blacklist states that refused to cooperate on efforts to tackle tax evasion and avoidance.

Concept note-3: -The G20 Summit 2023 will be held in India this year. It will be held in New Delhi at Pragati Maidan. This is like international financial Stability, environment changes, greenhouse gas emissions and for sustainable development.
