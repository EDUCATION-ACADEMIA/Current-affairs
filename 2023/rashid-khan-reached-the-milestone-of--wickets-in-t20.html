Rashid Khan reached the milestone of ____ wickets in T20?

(A) 200

(B) 300

(C) ** 400

(D) 100

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rashid achieved the feat in his 289th T20 match. Only three other bowlers have crossed the 400-wicket mark in T20 cricket. Dwayne Bravo, the first to reach the feat in 364 matches, also became the first bowler to cross 500 T20 wickets.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Rashid Khan. Afghanistan’s leg-spinner Rashid Khan picked up his 400th wicket in competitive T20 cricket during the team’s T20 World Cup match against New Zealand on 7 Nov’21. He achieved the feat in his 289th T20 match.

Concept note-3: -Rashid Khan achieved a major milestone of 500 T20 wickets at the age of only 24 years. The rockstar of the T20 format also became the fastest to grab 400 wickets in the T20 format.

Concept note-4: -Rashid Khan Took 100 Wickets (In 65 T20 innings) with Strike Rate of 14.68 and Average of 14.89. R Khan’s delivered 1468 balls (with 0 Maiden over) and took 2 wickets 18 times 3 wickets 12 times, 5 wickets 1 times, 8 wickets 0 times, 10 wickets 0 times.
