Who has become the fastest batsman to score 11, 000 runs in ODIs?

(A) ** Virat Kohli

(B) Rohit Sharma

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) KL Rahul

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In Short. By Rajarshi Gupta: Virat Kohli broke Sachin Tendulkar’s record to become the fastest cricketer to reach 11, 000 runs in one-day internationals.

Concept note-2: -1. Virat Kohli (India) – 222 innings. Sachin’s record of 11000 ODI runs in 276 matches was widely regarded as the best.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi: Pakistan captain added another feather to his cap and breaks Virat Kohli’s record as the batter scored 11, 000 International runs in 251 innings Overtaking Virat Kohli who Scored in 261 Innings.

Concept note-4: -Virat Kohli holds the record for scoring the fastest century in ODI cricket by an Indian player. Kohli took 52 balls to reach his hundred against Australia in Jaipur in 2018.

Concept note-5: -Guwahati, October 2 In the match after scoring 19 runs, Virat became the fastest to reach 11, 000 T20 runs in just 354 matches. Kohli slammed a stunning six over the long-off in the Wayne Parnell’s over to reach this milestone.
