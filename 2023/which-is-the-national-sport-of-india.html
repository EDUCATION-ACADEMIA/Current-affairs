Which is the national sport of India?

(A) Cricket

(B) ** Hockey

(C) Kabaddi

(D) Football

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -No official sport has been designated as India’s national sport by the Indian government. Hockey is believed to be the national game of India due to its massive success. However, India does not officially have a national game, and hockey and Kabaddi are often assumed to be the national sports of India.

Concept note-2: -What is the national game of India? There is no national game in India. Hockey is often perceived as the national sport due to its historical significance, but that isn’t the case. The Government of India recognizes all sports equally and doesn’t want to encourage one sport over another.

Concept note-3: -The national game of India: What is it? The Indian hockey team is the most successful side in the history of the Olympics, having won eight gold, one silver and three bronze medals. The golden years, from 1928 to 1956, saw them clinch six successive gold medals.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Hockey. Hockey is one of the most popular sports in India. The national team’s early success in Olympic Games made Hockey’s status as the national sport of India, whereas it is not the official national sport of India.

Concept note-5: -Hockey is referred to as the national game of India. It consists of two teams with eleven members in each team. India is the first non-European team to be a part of the international hockey federation. In the year 1928, the team had won its very first Olympic gold medal.
