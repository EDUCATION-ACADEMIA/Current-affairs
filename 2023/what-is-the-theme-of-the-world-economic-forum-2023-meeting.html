What is the theme of the World Economic Forum 2023 meeting?

(A) ** Cooperation in a Fragmented World

(B) Planet People Partnership

(C) Inclusive and sustainable growth

(D) Promotion of Regional Trade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The World Economic Forum’s Annual Meeting 2023, held in the Swiss town of Davos, ended Friday – a conference that started in a world possibly fundamentally altered, but whose processes and outcomes remained pretty much business as usual. The theme this year was ‘Cooperation in a Fragmented World’.

Concept note-2: -World Economic Forum: ‘Cooperation in a fragmented world’ the focus of Davos 2023. The World Economic Forum (WEF) aims to tackle global issues through collaboration and sustainable solutions. The theme for this year’s event is ‘cooperation in a fragmented world” in wake of COVID-19.

Concept note-3: -January 21, 2023 Against the backdrop of a ground war in Europe, the threat of recession, and evolving globalization, the World Economic Forum’s annual meeting in the Swiss mountain town of Davos came to a close on Friday.

Concept note-4: -What is the theme of this year’s World Economic Forum (WEF) Davos summit? The theme for this year’s summit is ‘Cooperation in a Fragmented World’.
