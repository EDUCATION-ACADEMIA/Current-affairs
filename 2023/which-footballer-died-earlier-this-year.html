Which footballer died earlier this year.

(A) Sergio Ramos

(B) ** David Astori

(C) Lionel Messi

(D) Harri Kane

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A club statement said “Fiorentina are profoundly shaken and forced to announce that their captain Davide Astori has died.” His former Cagliari teammate and coach Diego López and former Fiorentina teammate Carlos Sánchez collapsed to the ground after hearing the news of his death.

Concept note-2: -The 31-year-old Fiorentina captain was found dead in a hotel room in Udine in March 2018, before his team was due to play a league match. According to the autopsy report, Astori died from tachyarrhythmia, an abnormal acceleration of the heartbeat, during his sleep.

Concept note-3: -Italian player Piermario Morosini dies after suffering cardiac arrest during Serie B game.
