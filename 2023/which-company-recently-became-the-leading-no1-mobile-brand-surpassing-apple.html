Which Company recently became the leading No.1 Mobile brand surpassing Apple?

(A) Samsung

(B) Asus

(C) Oppo

(D) ** Xiaomi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple is the number one smartphone brand in India. It has an increasing market share and a wider presence every year. Apple’s iPhone 12, iPhone SE 2022, and iPhone 13 are well received and are one of the best-selling smartphones in India.

Concept note-2: -Samsung was named the world’s largest smartphone brand in 2022. Apple and Samsung took the first and second spots in the fourth quarter of 2022. Xiaomi, Vivo and OPPO took third, fourth, and fifth place, respectively.

Concept note-3: -Xiaomi still number one for 2022 Samsung was a close second with a 19% market share. Vivo, Oppo, and Realme completed the top five brands with 17%, 15% and 14% market shares respectively. India’s smartphone shipments in 2022 fell to 151.6 million units, which was a 6% decline compared with 2021.
