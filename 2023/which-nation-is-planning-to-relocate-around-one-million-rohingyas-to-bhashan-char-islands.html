Which nation is planning to relocate around one million Rohingyas to Bhashan Char Islands?

(A) Myanmar

(B) ** Bangladesh

(C) India

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To reduce the pressure of Rohingyas in Cox’s Bazar, the government of Bangladesh has started the process of relocating some of the Rohingyas to Bhasan Char Island located in Noakhali district, Bangladesh.

Concept note-2: -Today, 900, 000 Rohingya refugees reside in the Bangladesh Cox’s Bazar District receiving humanitarian aid. Although Bangladesh is the largest Rohingya refugee-hosting country, the country has no refugee laws, which has left the Rohingya without any rights or freedom of movement.

Concept note-3: -In addition, 306 Rohingyas who tried to go to Malaysia illegally by sea were rescued from the sea and taken to Bhashan Char.

Concept note-4: -The government of Bangladesh plans to move 100, 000 Rohingya refugees to the island of Bhasan Char.

Concept note-5: -One year since Bangladesh began relocating thousands of Rohingya refugees from Cox’s Bazar to Bhasan Char – a remote and cyclone-prone island in the Bay of Bengal – it’s time to take stock.
