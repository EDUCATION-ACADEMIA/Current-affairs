Who is the overall champions of 2019 Khelo India Youth Games

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) New Delhi

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution: The correct answer is option 1) i.e. Pune. The second edition of Khelo India Youth Games was held in Pune. Maharashtra topped the Medal tally with 228 medals, whereas Haryana and Delhi claimed the second and third spot.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra, with a haul of 56 gold, 55 silver and 50 bronze medals emerged as the overall champions, while Haryana (40 golds) and hosts Madhya Pradesh with 39 golds were second and third respectively.

Concept note-3: -In the second edition of Khelo India Youth Games (2nd edition), Maharashtra topped with 228 medals (85 gold, 62 silver and 81 bronze). Haryana was second with total 178 medals (62 gold, 56 silver and 60 bronze) while Delhi was third with total 136 medals (48 gold, 37 silver and 51 bronze) for a total of 136 medals.

Concept note-4: -Hockey Haryana’s women’s team won the Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Women’s Under 18 Qualifiers after defeating Madhya Pradesh 2-0 in the final at Bhubaneswar yesterday, December 30.
