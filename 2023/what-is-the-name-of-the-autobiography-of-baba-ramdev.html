What is the name of the autobiography of Baba Ramdev?

(A) ** My Life, My Mission

(B) My Life, My Lesson

(C) My Lesson, My Life

(D) Autobiography of a Yogi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"My Life, My Mission” is an autobiography of yoga guru Swami Ramdev, who wrote this book with senior journalist Uday Mahurkar.

Concept note-2: -The book, My Life, My Mission, co-authored with senior journalist Uday Mahurkar, also addresses the major controversies, turning points and achievements of the yoga exponent’s life.

Concept note-3: -The total net worth of Baba Ramdev is estimated to be $190 Million, which in Indian currency is approximately equal to 1400 Crore Indian Rupee (i.e., One thousand Four Hundred Crore INR). Baba Ramdev Income and Net Worth come from his Yoga, FMCG Business, and Royalties. He is none other than Yoga Guru Baba Ramdev.

Concept note-4: -Ramdev was born on 25 December in a Hindu family in 1965 to Ram Niwas Yadav and Gulabo Devi at Hazaribag Ali Saiyad Pur village of Mahendragarh district, Alipur, Haryana Both of his parents were farmers. He studied Indian scripture, Yoga and Sanskrit in various Gurukuls schools.
