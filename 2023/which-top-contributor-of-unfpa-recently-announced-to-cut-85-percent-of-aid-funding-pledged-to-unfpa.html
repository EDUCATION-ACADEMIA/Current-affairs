Which top contributor of UNFPA recently announced to cut 85 percent of aid funding pledged to UNFPA?

(A) USA

(B) Russia

(C) ** UK

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As a result of the economic impact of coronavirus pandemic, United Kingdom imposed 85% aid cut to UNFPA.

Concept note-2: -Governments provide contributions toward UNFPA core and non-core resources on a voluntary basis, since UNFPA does not assess a required contribution from governments.

Concept note-3: -UNFPA supports countries in using population data for policies and program-mes to reduce poverty and to ensure that every pregnancy is wanted, every birth is safe, every young person is free of HIV/AIDS, and every girl and woman is treated with dignity and respect.

Concept note-4: -Dr. Natalia Kanem is the Executive Director of the United Nations Population Fund (UNFPA). UNFPA is the United Nations sexual and reproductive health and rights agency.
