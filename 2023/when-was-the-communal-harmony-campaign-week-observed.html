When was the Communal Harmony Campaign Week observed?

(A) 29th-30th November

(B) 26th-30th November

(C) 20th-28th November

(D) ** 19th-25th November

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The last working day of the week is observed as the Flag Day of the Foundation. This year also the Foundation will observe Communal Harmony Campaign Week from 19th to 25th November 2022 and the Flag Day will be observed on 25th November 2022 (Friday).

Concept note-2: -Every year 25th November is observed as ‘ Flag Day’ of the National Foundation for Communal Harmony (NFCH) . This fund is used by NFCH to provide financial assistance to children rendered orphan or destitute in communal, caste, ethnic or terrorist violence for their education and rehabilitation under ‘Project Assist’.

Concept note-3: -The National Foundation for Communal Harmony (NFCH) observes Communal Harmony Campaign and Fund Raising Week every year from 19th to 25th November and the last working day of this week is observed as the Flag Day. 19 November 2022-9:00am to 25 November 2022-6:00pm.

Concept note-4: -Mary’s English High School observed Communal Harmony Week (12th to 19th, Nov., 2022), with the aim of promoting fraternity among the people, maintaining peace and communal harmony and thereby propagating national integration. The Communal Harmony week started with “Message on Communal Harmony”.

Concept note-5: -1 Communal harmony means that people of different religions, castes, creeds, sex and different background live together in the society with love and peace amongst them. Communal harmony strives to create goodwill and harmony among various communities.
