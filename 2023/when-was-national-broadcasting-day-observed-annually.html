When was National Broadcasting Day observed annually?

(A) July 13

(B) July 11

(C) ** July 23

(D) July 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 1927. National Broadcasting Day is observed on July 23 every year. The Indian Broadcasting Company (IBC) started organizing radio broadcasting from Bombay station on this day in 1927.

Concept note-2: -National Broadcasting Day History It was on July 23, 1927, that organised broadcasting kick-started in India. It was on July 23 that All India Radio began its journey as a private company called Indian Broadcasting Company ltd (IBC).

Concept note-3: -On the 8th of June, 1936, the Indian State Broadcasting Service became All India Radio. Since 1927, Radio has been an important part of people’s life in India.

Concept note-4: -National days on Sun Jul 23rd, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s National Gorgeous Grandma Day, National Parents’ Day, National Vanilla Ice Cream Day, Peanut Butter and Chocolate Day, International Yada, Yada, Yada Day… and much more!

Concept note-5: -23 July in Indian history is celebrated as the birth anniversary of the following personalities: Bal Gangadhar Tilak (23 July 1856 – 1 August 1920) was a scholar, mathematician, philosopher, and radical nationalist who helped lay the foundation of India’s independence.
