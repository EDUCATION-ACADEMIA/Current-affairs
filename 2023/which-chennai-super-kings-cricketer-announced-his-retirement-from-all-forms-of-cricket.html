Which Chennai Super Kings cricketer announced his retirement from all forms of cricket?

(A) MS Dhoni

(B) ** Shane Watson

(C) Dwayne Bravo

(D) Faf du Plessis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shane Watson retires: “It all started as a dream as a young kid saying to my Mum, as I watched a Test match, that I want to play for Australia. Now as I officially announce my retirement from all cricket I feel crazily lucky to live my dream, ” Watson said in a video posted on his Twitter handle.

Concept note-2: -Watson played for Australia in the 2016 World Twenty20 in March 2016 in India. Partway through the tournament, on 24 March, 14 years to the day since his international debut for Australia, Watson announced that he would retire from international cricket at the end of the tournament.

Concept note-3: -Bravo was part of the CSK’s all four IPL winning campaigns. “Congratulations to Dwayne Bravo on a tremendous career in the IPL.

Concept note-4: -Hashim Amla, who had already left international cricket in 2019, officially announced his retirement from all forms of cricket.

Concept note-5: -IPL 2022: Shane Watson joins Delhi Capitals as assistant coach.
