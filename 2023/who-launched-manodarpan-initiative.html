Who launched MANODARPAN initiative?

(A) PM Narendra Modi

(B) ** Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank

(C) Piyush Goyal

(D) Smriti Irani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MANODARPAN – an initiative by Ministry of Education (MOE), Government of India (GOI) aims to provide psychosocial support for mental health and well-being of students during the COVID outbreak and beyond.

Concept note-2: -HRD Minister Dr. Ramesh Pokhriyal Nishank launches VidyaDaan 2.0 program for inviting e-learning contributions from individuals & organizations across the country.

Concept note-3: -Dharmendra Pradhan is the Cabinet Minister for Education and Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in the Government of India.
