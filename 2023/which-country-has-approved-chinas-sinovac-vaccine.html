Concept note-1: -CoronaVac, a COVID-19 vaccine designed by Sinovac Biotech in China, passed all clinical trials and was licensed by the Indonesian Food and Drug Control Agency (BPOM bpom ri). The efficacy of CoronaVac was 65.3%, above the WHO threshold of 50%.

Concept note-2: -In the interim, WHO recommends the use of the Sinovac-CoronaVac (COVID-19) vaccine in pregnant women when the benefits of vaccination to the pregnant woman outweigh the potential risks.

Concept note-3: -The 10 EU countries that do not currently recognise Sinovac or Sinopharm are Bulgaria, Czech Republic, France, Germany, Iceland, Italy, Lithuania, Luxembourg, Malta, and Romania. Poland recognises Sinovac, but not Sinopharm.
