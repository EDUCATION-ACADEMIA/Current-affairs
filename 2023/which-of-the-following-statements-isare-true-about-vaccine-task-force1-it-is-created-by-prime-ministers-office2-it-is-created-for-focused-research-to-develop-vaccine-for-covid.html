Which of the following statement(s) is/are True about Vaccine Task Force:1) It is created by Prime Minister’s Office.2) It is created for focused research to develop vaccine for COVID.

(A) Only 1

(B) Only 2

(C) ** Both 1 & 2

(D) Neither of them

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The pathogens, given in a vaccine are unable to cause disease but are sufficient to generate the primary immune response. So, the correct answer is ‘Develops resistance against the attack of a disease.

Concept note-2: -Vaccination is a simple, safe, and effective way of protecting you against harmful diseases, before you come into contact with them. It uses your body’s natural defenses to build resistance to specific infections and makes your immune system stronger.

Concept note-3: -Vaccines contain dead or weakened microbes of a particular disease. When a vaccine is introduced into a healthy body, the body fights and kills them by producing suitable antibodies. These antibodies remain in the body and protects it from the microbe when it invades the body again.

Concept note-4: -Definition of Terms Vaccine: A preparation that is used to stimulate the body’s immune response against diseases.
