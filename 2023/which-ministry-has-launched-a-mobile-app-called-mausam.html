Which Ministry has launched a mobile app called ‘Mausam’?

(A) Environment Ministry

(B) ** Earth science Ministry

(C) Home Ministry

(D) Health Ministray

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MAUSAM is a Mobile App of the India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), Government of India to provide seamless and user-friendly access to weather products available on https://mausam.imd.gov.in/.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Earth Sciences launches Mobile App “Mausam”
