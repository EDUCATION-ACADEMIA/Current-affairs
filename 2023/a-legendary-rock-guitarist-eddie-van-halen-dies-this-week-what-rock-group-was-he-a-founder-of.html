A Legendary Rock Guitarist, Eddie Van Halen dies this week, what rock group was he a founder of?

(A) The Rolling Stones

(B) Panic At the Disco

(C) Foo Fighters

(D) ** Van Halen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Van Halen, American heavy metal band distinguished by the innovative electric-guitar playing of Eddie Van Halen.

Concept note-2: -Eddie Van Halen died of cancer at 65 in October 2020, after struggling with the disease on and off for two decades.

Concept note-3: -Surprisingly, considering how Van Halen was never one to use sparseness or restraint, Van Halen’s number one influence appears to have been British six-string master Eric Clapton. “Clapton was it.

Concept note-4: -Alex and Eddie wanted to rechristen the quartet Rat Salad-a nod to the Black Sabbath track of the same title. Roth, however, lobbied for Van Halen because of its inherent power. The name, it turned out, perfectly matched their music.
