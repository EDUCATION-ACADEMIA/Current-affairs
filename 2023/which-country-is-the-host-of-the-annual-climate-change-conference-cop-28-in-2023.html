Which country is the host of the annual climate change conference (COP 28) in 2023?

(A) USA

(B) ** UAE

(C) India

(D) Bangladesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 28th session of the Conference of the Parties (COP 28) to the UNFCCC will convene from 30 November to 12 December 2023. It will take place in the United Arab Emirates.

Concept note-2: -COP28 UAE will take place at Expo City Dubai from November 30-December 12, 2023.

Concept note-3: -The UAE will host the 28th Conference of the Parties to the UN Framework Convention on Climate Change (COP28) in November 2023 at Expo City Dubai to unite the world towards agreement on bold, practical and ambitious solutions to the most pressing global challenge of our time.

Concept note-4: -The latest edition of COP was held in November 2022 in Sharm El Sheikh, Egypt, resulting in many key decisions to limit some of the most destructive impacts of climate change.

Concept note-5: -UN Climate Change Conference-United Arab Emirates Nov/Dec 2023 | UNFCCC.
