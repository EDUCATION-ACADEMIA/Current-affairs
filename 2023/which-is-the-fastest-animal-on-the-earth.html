Which is the fastest animal on the Earth?

(A) Leopard

(B) Tiger

(C) ** Cheetah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What’s faster than a cheetah?-no animal on earth can run faster. But a peregrine falcon can swoop faster than a cheetah can run. And the falcon can’t compare to an airplane, a rocket, or the speed of light.

Concept note-2: -Whereas the cheetah is the fastest sprinter, the pronghorn, also known as the American antelope, is the fastest long-distance runner of the animal kingdom. It is capable of maintaining a speed of nearly 35 miles per hour over several miles and is even faster over shorter distances.

Concept note-3: -Fastest land animal: Cheetah Cheetahs’ long, flexible spines allow them to cover huge distances with each stride, and they vary their strides per second as they speed up, taking more strides per second as they run faster. At top speed, cheetahs can go 23 feet in a single stride and can complete four strides per second.

Concept note-4: -While Cheetahs can run anywhere from 50 to 75mph in short bursts, Tigers can only run from 30 up to 40mph(again, in short bursts) so in short, a Cheetah is faster than a Tiger.
