The RBI has proposed an upper age limit of ____ years for CEOs and whole-time directors of banks?

(A) 65 years

(B) 60 years

(C) ** 70 years

(D) 68 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With regard to upper age limit for MD & CEO and WTDs in the private sector banks, the RBI said that no person can continue in such positions beyond the age of 70. The maximum age limit for chairman and non-executive directors has been fixed at 75 years.

Concept note-2: -The government has raised the maximum tenure of the managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of public sector banks (PSBs) to 10 years from five years, while retaining the superannuation age at 60.
