IRCTC has launched RuPay card with which bank now?

(A) ICICI

(B) HDFC

(C) ** SBI

(D) HSBC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -First Credit Card Live on RuPay Contactless( National Common Mobility Card). Daily purchases now made easy with IRCTC SBI Platinum Card. Simply wave your card at a secure reader and your transaction is done.

Concept note-2: -The IRCTC Rupay card launched in collaboration with the State Bank of India is a travel credit card which is specially curated for the regular travellers by railways.

Concept note-3: -SBI IOCL Co-Branded RuPay Debit Card is a Contactless Debit Card. The card can be issued to the all the existing / new SBI customers. The card has been specially designed to earn additional Reward Points at all IOCL retail outlets. The card comes with an EMV chip.

Concept note-4: -Railway Ticket Booking Register free of cost. Log in with your user name and password. Follow instructions on the website to make your reservations. Make payments through your SBI Card at the payment gateways that accept a Visa Card.

Concept note-5: -SBI IRCTC Credit Card, launched in association with IRCTC offers multiple travel benefits to the users at a minimal annual fee. It offers value back benefits on booking tickets through IRCTC, domestic lounge access, and waiver on railway ticket bookings. Additionally, you get basic rewards on other retail purchases.
