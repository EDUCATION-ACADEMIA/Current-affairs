After what grade in school do Eritrean boys have to join the military?

(A) 12th Grade

(B) 9th Grade

(C) ** 11th Grade

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A large proportion of the adult population of Eritrea is currently engaged in national service, which is mandatory for men and women between the ages of 18 and 50. While the service period is supposed to be 18 months, this is frequently extended indefinitely.

Concept note-2: -For 2023, Eritrea is ranked 113 of 145 out of the countries considered for the annual GFP review. The nation holds a PwrIndx* score of 2.2956 (a score of 0.0000 is considered ‘perfect’). This entry last reviewed on 01/05/2023.

Concept note-3: -Education in Eritrea is officially compulsory for children aged between 7 and 14, and the government is striving to make this a reality despite sectarian conflict. The system follows 5 stages, beginning at kindergarten level, and for some perhaps ending with university degrees.
