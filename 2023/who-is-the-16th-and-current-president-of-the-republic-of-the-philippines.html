Who is the 16th and current president of the Republic of the Philippines?

(A) Joseph Estrada

(B) Gloria Macapagal-Arroyo

(C) Benigno Simeon Aquino

(D) ** Rodrigo R. Duterte

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rodrigo Roa Duterte KGCR (English: /dəˈtɜːrteɪ/, Tagalog: [ɾɔˈdɾɪgɔ ˈɾɔwa dʊˈtɛɾtɛ] ( listen); born March 28, 1945), also known as Digong, Rody, and by the initials DU30 and PRRD, is a Filipino lawyer and politician who served as the 16th president of the Philippines from 2016 to 2022.

Concept note-2: -President Rodrigo Roa Duterte, 16th President of the Republic.

Concept note-3: -Rodrigo Duterte became the 16th President of the Philippines on June 30, 2016, succeeding Benigno Aquino III. He was the first president from Mindanao, the first president to have worked in all three branches of government, and the oldest to be elected.

Concept note-4: -The current President, Rodrigo Roa Duterte, is the sixth President of the Fifth Republic and the 16th President of the Philippines.
