What is the name of the Indian Air Force’s largest military exercise conducted from April 8 to 22, 2018?

(A) Akash Shakti study

(B) ** Gagan Shakti Study

(C) Vyom Shakti Practice

(D) Friendship Power Study

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’EX-Gagan Shakti 2018’, the biggest ever war combat game exercise on the Pakistan and China border was conducted by IAF to showcase its air dominance over the entire extended area of the Indian Ocean Region (IOR). now again the game is on and i wish history will be repeated again..

Concept note-2: -Notably, the Indo-US military exercise ‘Yudh Abhyas 2022’ commenced in Uttarakhand’s Auli on November 19.

Concept note-3: -Exercise GARUDA SHAKTI is the eighth edition of the series of bilateral exercises under this banner. The exercise which commenced on 21 November 2022 aims at enhancing understanding, cooperation and interoperability between the Special Forces of both armies.

Concept note-4: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas, conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, tactics, techniques and procedures between the Armies of the two nations.

Concept note-5: -India and Japan conducted a joint military exercise titled ‘Dharma Guardian 2022’ at Maratha Light Infantry Regimental Centre in Belgaum, Karnataka. It was held from 27th February to 10th March 2022. It covers platoon-level joint training on operations in the jungle and semi-urban and urban terrain.
