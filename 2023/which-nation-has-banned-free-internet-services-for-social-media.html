Which nation has banned free internet services for social media?

(A) India

(B) Nepal

(C) ** Bangladesh

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which country has banned free internet services for accessing social media? Notes: The Regulatory body of Bangladesh, the Bangladesh Telecom Regulatory Commission (BTRC) has directed telecom companies to stop free internet services to its subscribers for accessing social media.

Concept note-2: -In 2015, the Government blocked social media sites for 22 days following protests the verdicts of International Crimes Tribunal. The government also monitors bloggers in the country. A leaked report showed that blogger killed in Bangladesh overlapped with those under surveillance of the state.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Bangladesh. Bangladesh has banned free internet for social media in July 2020.
