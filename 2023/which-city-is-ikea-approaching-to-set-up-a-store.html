Which city is IKEA approaching to set up a store.

(A) Bangalore

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Delhi

(D) Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IKEA will open its third store in Mumbai at R CITY Mall on July 28th 2022. Mumbai, India, 20th July 2022: IKEA India (part of the Ingka Group), the world’s leading Swedish home furnishings retailer will be opening doors to newest store in Mumbai, located at R CITY, Ghatkopar (W) on July 28 2022.

Concept note-2: -IKEA set to launch its second city store in Mumbai in R CITY Mall. Mumbai, June 29th, 2022: Continuing its omnichannel expansion in India, IKEA, the world’s leading Swedish home furnishings retailer, today announced the location of its second small-format city store in India.

Concept note-3: -IKEA Store-Navi Mumbai is located in Turbhe on Thane-Belapur highway. Store is accessible through public transport.

Concept note-4: -The store will be situated in Borivali and will be spread over 3.5 lakh sqft, making it the largest space to be occupied by a single furniture house anywhere in the country.
