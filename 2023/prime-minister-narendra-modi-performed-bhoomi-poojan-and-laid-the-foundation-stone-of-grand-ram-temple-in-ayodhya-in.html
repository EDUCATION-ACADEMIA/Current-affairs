Prime Minister Narendra Modi performed Bhoomi Poojan and laid the foundation stone of Grand Ram Temple in Ayodhya in

(A) ** 5 AUG

(B) 3AUG

(C) 7AUG

(D) 9AUG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ram Mandir in Ayodhya will be India’s national temple, Uttar Pradesh Chief Minister Yogi Adityanath stated on Wednesday as he laid the foundation stone of the garba griha (sanctum sanctorum) of the temple with a traditional Hindu ‘ shila pujan’.

Concept note-2: -Construction. The Shri Ram Janmabhoomi Teerth Kshetra trust began the first phase of construction of the Ram Temple in March 2020. However, the COVID-19 pandemic lockdown in India followed by the 2020 China–India skirmishes caused a temporary suspension of the construction.

Concept note-3: -PM Narendra Modi along with Yogi Adityanath performs Bhoomi Pujan rituals for the construction of the Ram Mandir, in Ram Janmabhoomi premises in Ayodhya.
