What holiday did the Chinese Government cancel due to the Coronavirus?

(A) ** Chinese New Year

(B) Qingming Festival

(C) Dragon Boat Festival

(D) National Day of the People’s Republic of China

(E) Golden Week

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SOURCE: Titoma Blog. There’ll be a China factory shutdown in 2023, but; it’s the same shutdown that happens every year. The Chinese New Year (CNY) holiday 2023 on the calendar lasts only 7 days, but the disruption it causes in manufacturing can be up to 40 days.

Concept note-2: -Is Lunar New Year’s Day a Public Holiday? Lunar New Year’s Day is not a public holiday. It falls on Sunday, January 22, 2023 and most businesses follow regular Sunday opening hours in Philippines.

Concept note-3: -While various Asian nations also celebrate their own Lunar New Year festivals, the Chinese New Year is in fact a public holiday in several nations around the world, not just in the People’s Republic.

Concept note-4: -Public Life. Lunar New Year is not considered a holiday in India. Schools, government and private institutions are open during these times.
