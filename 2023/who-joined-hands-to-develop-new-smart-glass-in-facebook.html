Who joined hands to develop new smart glass in Facebook?

(A) ** Ray-Ban Ray Ban

(B) Fastrack

(C) Prada

(D) Oakley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ray-Ban Stories smart glasses are the first product to come out of our multi-year partnership with EssilorLuxottica. Together, we’re committed to delivering innovative technology and fashion-forward style-while helping people better connect with friends and family.

Concept note-2: -Ray-Ban Stories are smartglasses created as a collaboration between Meta Platforms and EssilorLuxottica. The product includes two cameras, open-ear speakers, a microphone, and touchpad, all built into the frame.

Concept note-3: -Facebook in partnership with Ray-Ban has launched its first-generation smart glasses.

Concept note-4: -Meta recently acquired the Netherlands-based 3D smart glass maker Luxexcel to bolster its vision for the metaverse in 2023. With Luxexcel’s technology, Meta can now integrate the essential elements for an augmented reality experience into a prescription lens, such as holographic film and projectors.

Concept note-5: -Besides its own brands like Ray-Ban or Oakley, EssilorLuxottica has the eyewear licenses to Giorgio Armani, Dolce & Gabbana, Chanel, Prada, Ralph Laurent, Versace, Burberry and Tory Burch; and controls retail chains like Sunglass Hut and LensCrafters.
