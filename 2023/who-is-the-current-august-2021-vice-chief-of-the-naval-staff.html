Who is the current (August, 2021) Vice Chief of the Naval Staff?

(A) Vice Admiral Satish Namdeo

(B) ** Vice Admiral SN Ghormade

(C) Vice Admiral Karambir Singh

(D) Vice Admiral G. Ashok Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Vice Chief is the deputy of the Chief of the Naval Staff and is usually the second highest ranking officer of the Indian Navy. The current VCNS is Vice Admiral Satish Namdeo Ghormade who took over from Vice Admiral G. Ashok Kumar on 31 July 2021.

Concept note-2: -The office is held by a Three Star Officer in the rank of Vice Admiral. The current DCNS is Vice Admiral Sanjay Mahindru who took over Vice Admiral Ravneet Singh on 1 April 2022.
