“Transforming Food Systems for Rural Prosperity report” has been released by which organization?

(A) UNESCO

(B) FAO

(C) ** IFAD

(D) NABARD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IFAD’s operations are primarily financed by member state contributions, loan repayments, investment income and special contributions from non-member states. Other sources of financing include sovereign borrowing and, more recently being explored, concessional loans and borrowing in international capital markets.

Concept note-2: -1. NABARD is a Development Bank with a mandate for providing and regulating credit for the development of agriculture, small-scale industries, cottage and village industries, handicrafts and other allied economic activities in rural areas to promote prosperity of rural areas.

Concept note-3: -Established in 1977, the International Fund for Agricultural Development (IFAD) works to enable poor rural people to improve their food and nutrition security, increase their incomes and strengthen their resilience. IFAD headquarters is located in Rome, Italy.

Concept note-4: -At present, IFAD has 172 members Countries.
