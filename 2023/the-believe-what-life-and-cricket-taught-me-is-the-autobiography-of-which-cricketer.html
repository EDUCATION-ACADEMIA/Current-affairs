The “Believe-What life and cricket taught me” is the autobiography of which cricketer?

(A) ** Suresh Raina

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) MS Dhoni

(D) Gautam Gambhir

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Cricketer Suresh Raina has come up with his autobiography ‘Believe-What Life Taught me’. The cricketer has shared a glimpse of his cricket career which included success, failure, injuries, setbacks, and how he came out on top of it, in the book.

Concept note-2: -About the Author Suresh Raina is one of India’s most successful international cricketers. An aggressive left-handed middle-order batsman, a useful off-spin bowler and one of the best fielders in world cricket, Raina was part of the World Cup-winning team in 2011.

Concept note-3: -Believe: What Life and Cricket Taught Me.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sachin Tendulkar. Playing It My Way is the autobiography of former Indian cricketer Sachin Tendulkar. It was launched on 5 November 2014.
