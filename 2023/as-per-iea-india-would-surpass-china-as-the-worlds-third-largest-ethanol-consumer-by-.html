As per IEA, India would surpass China as the world’s third largest ethanol consumer by ____

(A) 2023

(B) 2025

(C) ** 2026

(D) 2030

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India is on track to surpass China as the world’s third-largest ethanol consumer by 2026, with the objective of becoming carbon neutral by 2070. India also introduced its 20 per cent ethanol blending target with gasoline from 2030 to 2025.

Concept note-2: -“India is on track to surpass China as the world’s third-largest ethanol consumer by 2026. In January 2021, India brought forward its 20 per cent ethanol blending target with gasoline from 2030 to 2025, and is aiming to start selling 20 per cent blends in 2023, ” the agency said in a report.

Concept note-3: -United States. The United States produces and consumes more ethanol fuel than any other country in the world.

Concept note-4: -Achieving this target early made the government advance by five years the target of 20 per cent ethanol blending to 2025, the Cooperation Minister said.
