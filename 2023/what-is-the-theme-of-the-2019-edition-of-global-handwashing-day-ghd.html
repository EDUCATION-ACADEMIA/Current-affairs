What is the theme of the 2019 edition of Global Handwashing Day (GHD)?

(A) ** Clean Hands for All

(B) Make handwashing a habit

(C) Our Hands, Our Future

(D) Clean Hands-a recipe for health

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year’s Global Handwashing Day theme is ‘Clean hands for all’. Inequalities in access to handwashing facilities with soap, discriminatory promotional programmes and low levels of compliance can put people and societies at risk from disease transmission, low educational attainment and productivity.

Concept note-2: -The theme for Global Handwashing Day 2019 focuses on leaving no one behind in our handwashing advocacy. Our tagline, ‘clean hands for all’, reminds us to ensure handwashing initiatives are equitable and inclusive.

Concept note-3: -Global Handwashing Day 2022: this year’s theme is Unite for Universal Hand Hygiene.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 Global Handwashing Day theme is “Unite for Universal Hand Hygiene.”
