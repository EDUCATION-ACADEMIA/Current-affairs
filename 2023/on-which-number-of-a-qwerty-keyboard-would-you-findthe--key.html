On which number of a QWERTY keyboard would you findthe * key?

(A) 5

(B) 7

(C) ** 8

(D) 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The home keys are where you place your fingers when you are learning to type. The home keys include F, D, S, and A on the left of the keybard, and J, K, L, and; (semicolon) on the right of the keyboard. Home Row key position (ASDF JKL; ).

Concept note-2: -The standard computer keyboards typically contain 101 keys for inputting character sets including alphabets, numbers, symbols, or functions.

Concept note-3: -A numeric keypad, number pad, numpad, or ten key, is the palm-sized, usually-17-key section of a standard computer keyboard, usually on the far right.
