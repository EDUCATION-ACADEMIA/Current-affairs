Sakshi Malik and these 2 women wrestler won silver in Asian Wrestling Championship?

(A) ** Vinesh Phogat & Divya Kakran

(B) Divya Kakran & Geeta Phogat

(C) Babita Kumari & Vinesh Phogat

(D) Babita Kumari & Geeta Phogat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Asian Wrestling Championships: Sakshi settles for silver; Vinesh, Anshu and Gursharanpreet bag bronze | More sports News-Times of India.

Concept note-2: -Vinesh Phogat becomes first Indian woman to win two World Championship medals. Vinesh Phogat with coach Jitender with the bronze medal.

Concept note-3: -She was then only the fourth Indian woman to win an Olympic medal and was India’s first medal of that edition of the Games. After 12 days of hopelessness at the Olympics, the wrestler’s bronze medal somewhat lifted the spirits in the Indian camp.

Concept note-4: -U-23 World Wrestling Championships 2022: Ankush Panghal wins silver medal.

Concept note-5: -Sakshi Malik, competing in the women’s 62kg category, pinned down former junior Asian champion Irina Kuznetsova of Kazakhstan in the final to clinch the gold.
