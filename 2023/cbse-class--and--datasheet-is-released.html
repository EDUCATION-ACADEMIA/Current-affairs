(A) 9

(B) ** 10

(C) 11

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The date sheet has not been released yet and we will have to wait for the CBSE 12th Date Sheet 2023 Release Date and as per news it is released on 29th December 2022. The CBSE has released the CBSE 12th Board exam date sheet 2023 for the academic year 2023 on 29 December 2022.

Concept note-2: -The Central Board of Secondary Education will release the CBSE Class 12 Date Sheet 2023 on the official website of CBSE in December 2022.

Concept note-3: -The students are advised to keep checking the official website of the board-cbse.gov.in-for the latest update on CBSE Board Exam Date Sheet 2023. If we refer to the past trend, the Board usually released the CBSE Board Exam Datesheet at least 75 to 90 days before the start of the examinations.
