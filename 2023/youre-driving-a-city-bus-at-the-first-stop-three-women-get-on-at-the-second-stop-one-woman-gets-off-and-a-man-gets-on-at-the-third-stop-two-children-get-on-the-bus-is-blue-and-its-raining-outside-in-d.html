You’re driving a city bus. At the first stop, three women get on. At the second stop, one woman gets off and a man gets on. At the third stop, two children get on. The bus is blue and it’s raining outside in December. What color is the bus driver’s hair?

(A) The colour of driver s hair is black

(B) ** The colour of driver s hair MY HAIR’S COLOUR

(C) The colour of driver s hair is BROWN

(D) The colour of driver s hair is NOT KNOWN

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Answer: An apple a day keeps the Doctor away!

Concept note-2: -Conversation. The driver gave seven apples to Sarah so that the girl would consume one a day, if he returns and find any amount of apple(s) it proves that she was busy with the apples but caring a doctor. The doctor has to be kept away from Sarah and an apple a day does just that.

Concept note-3: -YOU ARE THE BUS DRIVER. So the color of the bus driver’s eyes is the color of your eyes! the color of my eyes are black, as i’m the bus driver.
