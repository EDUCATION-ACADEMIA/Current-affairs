As per the ‘Traffic Index-2018’, which city was ranked first with a congestion level of 65%?

(A) ** Mumbai

(B) New Delhi

(C) Hyderabad

(D) Pune

(E) Bangaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bengaluru tops the chart of India’s most traffic-congested cities, followed by Pune (ranked 6), New Delhi (34) and Mumbai (47). TomTom Traffic Index ranks cities around the world by their average travel time and provides free access to city-by-city information.

Concept note-2: -Check out Top 10 most congested cities in the world in 2021. Most congested city in the world 2021: Mumbai has been ranked as the fifth most congested city in the world 2021, as per TomTom’s 2021 Traffic Index released on February 9, 2022.

Concept note-3: -As per the TomTom Traffic Index Ranking 2021, Mumbai has been ranked at 5th, Bengaluru at 10th in terms of the most congested cities in the world in 2021. Delhi and Pune ranked 11th and 21st among 404 cities across 58 countries, according to TomTom Traffic Index’s global top 25 lists.
