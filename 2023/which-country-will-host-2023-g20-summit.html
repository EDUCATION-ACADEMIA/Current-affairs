Which country will host 2023 G20 Summit?

(A) USA

(B) Nepal

(C) China

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2023 G20 Delhi summit (Hindi: 2023 G20 ) is the upcoming eighteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Pragati Maidan, New Delhi in 2023.

Concept note-2: -This year the Host of G20 Summit 2023 schedule will be India and this will be held in the country India. The G20 Summit 2023 Schedule is from 9 to 10 September 2023. This will be held in Delhi. The motto for the G20 Summit 2023 schedule is One Earth, One Family, One Future.

Concept note-3: -The Indian G20 Presidency’s theme of ‘One Earth, One Family, One Future’ sets the tone for the 2023 Infrastructure Agenda under the 2023 Indian G-20 Presidency.

Concept note-4: -The 2024 G20 Brazil summit is the upcoming nineteenth meeting of Group of Twenty (G20), a summit scheduled to take place in Brazil in 2024.

Concept note-5: -G20 Members The Group of Twenty (G20) comprises 19 countries (Argentina, Australia, Brazil, Canada, China, France, Germany, India, Indonesia, Italy, Japan, Republic of Korea, Mexico, Russia, Saudi Arabia, South Africa, Türkiye, United Kingdom and United States) and the European Union.
