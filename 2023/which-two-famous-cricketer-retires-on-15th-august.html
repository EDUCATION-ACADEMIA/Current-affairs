Which two famous Cricketer Retires on 15th August.

(A) Virat kohli

(B) ** MS. Dhoni

(C) Sachin Tendulkar

(D) ** Suresh kumar Raina

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former Captain of the Indian Cricket team, M. S. Dhoni along with Suresh Raina announced his retirement on 15th August 2020.

Concept note-2: -I would like to announce my retirement from all formats of cricket, ” Raina tweeted while thanking the BCCI, Uttar Pradesh Cricket Association and CSK. Since an active India or domestic player can’t take part in overseas leagues, Raina needed to take this step for him to explore T20 leagues around the world.

Concept note-3: -There are various reasons floating around Raina’s pull out that season – the death of his uncle, covid-19 positive cases in the CSK camp; few reports also said the southpaw was unhappy with not being given a room with a balcony!
