The death anniversary of Dr Babasaheb Ambedkar, which falls on December 6, is dubbed as ____ On this day each year, millions of people throng to Dr Ambedkar’s Samadhi in Mumbai, which is called Chaitya bhoomi to pay respect to the father of Indian Constitution.

(A) Mahima Divas

(B) ** Mahaparinirvana Divas.

(C) Mahan Divas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Babasaheb Ambedkar’s death anniversary, which is on December 6th, is commemorated by Mahaparinirvan Diwas. People gather to pay respects to him, and many flocks to the Chaitya Bhoomi in Dadar while yelling Jai Bhim. What is Mahaparinirvan Diwas?

Concept note-2: -Mahaparinirvan Din is observed annually on December 6 to mark the death anniversary of Dr. Bhim Rao Ambedkar, the chief architect of the Constitution of India. Babasaheb Ambedkar, who breathed his last on December 6, 1956, was born in Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Bhimrao Ambedkar Jayanti Mahaparinirvan Diwas 2022: The nation paid tributes to Dr. B R Ambedkar on December 6 (Tuesday) on his 66th death anniversary. He is known as the architect of India’s Constitution. Ambedkar, whose struggles give hope to millions of people, died on December 6, 1956.
