Which state commemorates the Shaheedi Diwas (Martyr’s Day) on March 23 as the ‘Youth Empowerment Day’?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Odisha

(C) Gujarat

(D) ** Punjab

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Martyr’s Day or Shaheed Diwas 2023: The government of Punjab has declared a public holiday on March 23, which is the martyrdom day of three freedom fighters, namely Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev Thapar, and Shivaram Rajguru .

Concept note-2: -They are named in honour of those who are recognised as martyrs for the nation. Recently Bihar government has announced to celebrate 15 February as Martyr’s Day in the memory of 34 freedom fighters killed by the police in Munger in 1932.

Concept note-3: -What is the theme of Shaheed Diwas 2022? The Theme of Shaheed Diwas 2022 is “Tribute to Revolutionaries.” This day is celebrated in honor of all the freedom fighters who fought for the independence of the nation.

Concept note-4: -Martyrs’ Day is commemorated on January 30 to mark the death anniversary of Mahatma Gandhi. Martyrs’ Day or Shaheed Divas is also commemorated on March 23 to remember the sacrifice of three extraordinary revolutionaries of India – Bhagat Singh, Shivaram Rajguru, Sukhdev Thapar.

Concept note-5: -In memory of Gandhi’s sacrifice and to honour his legacy, the Indian government declared January 30 as Martyrs’ Day or Shaheed Diwas. The day is observed as a day of non-violence and peace and is marked by various ceremonies and events across the country.
