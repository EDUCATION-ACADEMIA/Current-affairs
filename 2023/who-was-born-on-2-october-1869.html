Who was born on 2-October 1869?

(A) Dr Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar

(B) Pandit Jawaharlal Nehru

(C) ** Mohandas Karamchand Gandhi

(D) Sarojini Naidu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mahatma Gandhi was born on 2 October, 1869 in a trading family in Porbandar, a small town in Kathiawar, Gujarat. The day is now celebrated as Gandhi Jayanti .

Concept note-2: -Did you know October 2nd is a historic day for us Indians as two greats of our nation were born that day? Happy birthday to Mahatma Gandhi and Lal Bahadur Shastri!

Concept note-3: -Mahatma Gandhi was born on 2 October 1869.

Concept note-4: -2nd October 1869: Mahatma Gandhi, the Father of the Nation, is born.

Concept note-5: -Gandhi Jayanti is an event celebrated in India to mark the birthday of Mahatma Gandhi. It is celebrated annually on 2 October, and is one of the three national holidays of India.
