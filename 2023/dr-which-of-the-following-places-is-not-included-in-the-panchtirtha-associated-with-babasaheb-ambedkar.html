Dr. Which of the following places is not included in the Panchtirtha associated with Babasaheb Ambedkar?

(A) Nagpur (Maharashtra)

(B) London (England)

(C) Mumbai (Maharashtra)

(D) ** Paris (France)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Explanation: A party called Dalit Shoshit Samaj Sangharsh Samiti was formed by Kanshi Ram, not by Dr. Ambedkar.

Concept note-2: -Babasaheb Ambedkar Birth place, located at Mhow in Madhya Pradesh, India. This was birthplace of Babasaheb Ambedkar, who was born on 14 April 1891 in Mhow. where the local government built this grand memorial.

Concept note-3: -Ambedkar. There are two most selling books of Ambedkar namely the “Problem of the rupee” and “The Annihilation of caste”. These two books are based on the economy of India and the social structure of India respectively.
