What is the tallest building in Africa?

(A) Tanzania Ports Authority HQ

(B) Accra’s Villagio Vista

(C) Johannesburg’s Carlton Centre

(D) ** The Leonardo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Since 2021, the tallest skyscraper is Iconic Tower in New Administrative Capital, Egypt, which is 393.8 m (1, 292 ft) tall. The tallest skyscraper currently under construction in Africa is the F Tower in Abidjan, Côte d’Ivoire, which will reach 400 m (1, 300 ft) in height-work by PFO Africa and BESIX started in 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Leonardo tower, located in Sandton, Johannesburg, has become the tallest building in Africa. It measures 234 metres high and is divided into 55 floors.

Concept note-3: -The Leonardo Sandton, South Africa Comprising 55 floors and reaching a height of 234m (768ft), it is 11m (36ft) taller than the Carlton Centre.
