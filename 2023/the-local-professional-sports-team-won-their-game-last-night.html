The local, professional sports team won their game last night.

(A) ** The Sports Section

(B) The Entertainment Section

(C) The Business Section

(D) The Local Section

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Statistically, Glasgow Rangers are the most successful sports team in the world with a total of 55 league titles.

Concept note-2: -The impact of their efforts, however, continues to be felt today. The Cincinnati Base Ball Club, also known as the Cincinnati Red Stockings, fielded the first known openly professional team in 1869 and played its first game against an opposing club on May 4.

Concept note-3: -The professional sports industry encompasses mainstream and niche sports from across the world. While some of the big players in the industry include football, basketball, baseball, and soccer, there is also a huge appeal for sports such as cricket and hockey.
