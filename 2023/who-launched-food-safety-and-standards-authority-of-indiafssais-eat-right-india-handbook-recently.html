Who launched Food Safety and Standards Authority of India(FSSAI)’s ‘Eat Right India’ handbook recently?

(A) Smriti Irani

(B) ** Harsh Vardhan

(C) Piyush Goyal

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. On the occasion of World Food Day, Union Minister for Health and Family Welfare Dr Harsh Vardhan launched Food Safety Mitra Scheme, Eat Right Jacket and Eat Right Jhola.

Concept note-2: -The Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI) is pleased to present to you ‘The Eat Right India Handbook’.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Option 1, i.e Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI). Eat Right Movement was launched by the Food Safety and Standards Authority of India (FSSAI).
