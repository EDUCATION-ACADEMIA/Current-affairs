Over the past decade (or ten years), how many people or refugees tried to flee Eritrea?

(A) ** 400, 000

(B) 40, 000

(C) 4, 000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In recent years, 400, 000 Eritreans, almost 10% of the population, have fled the country.

Concept note-2: -29, 792 people from Eritrea fled in 2021 and applied for asylum in other countries, according to UNHCR data. This corresponds to approximately 0.823% of all residents. The most common destination countries have been the United Kingdom, Sudan and Uganda.

Concept note-3: -In the past decade, the global refugee crisis has more than doubled in scope. In 2022, the UNHCR announced that we had surpassed the 100 million mark for total displacement, meaning that over 1.2% of the global population have been forced to leave their homes. Among these people are over 32.5 million refugees.
