Last week a passenger plane crashed killing all 71 people on board. The Saratov Airlines jet vanished minutes after take-off and crashed about 80km into the flight. Where did this take Place?

(A) China

(B) ** Russia

(C) Australia

(D) Ukraine

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In May 2022, all 22 people onboard a plane operated by Nepali carrier Tara Air died when it crashed, and in March 2018, 51 people died when a US-Bangla Airlines plane crashed near Kathmandu.

Concept note-2: -Worldwide air traffic-number of fatalities 2006-2021. Air travel fatalities have been recorded in each of the last 16 years, with a total of 176 deaths in 2021 due to air crashes.

Concept note-3: -The Tenerife Airport Disaster (1977) The deadliest accident in aviation history, where around 583 passengers on board were killed. The incident took place after the plane captain, mistakenly believing that he had been cleared for takeoff, went ahead.
