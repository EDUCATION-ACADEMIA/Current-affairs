According to a nationwide survey conducted by the Indian Police Foundation (IPF), which states scored the lowest among all states?

(A) ** Bihar and Uttar Pradesh

(B) Punjab and Delhi

(C) Karnataka and Assam

(D) Kerala and Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to a nationwide survey conducted by Indian Police Foundation (IPF), police in Bihar and Uttar Pradesh scored the lowest among all States when it came to perception of “sensitivity”.

Concept note-2: -Andhra Pradesh Police has topped the ‘IPF Smart Policing’ Index 2021, among 29 states and Union Territories. Key points: Delhi Police has been ranked at seventh spot, among Indian states and union territories (UT).

Concept note-3: -Aska Police Station of Odisha Awarded as the Best Police Station in India-Key Points. The award was given during the DGsP/IGsP Conference 2022 which began in the National Agriculture Science Complex in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pradesh is the best state in India for law and order situations. According to the IPF Smart Police Index 2021, the states with the finest policing are Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Assam, Kerala, and Sikkim. The lowest five states are Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, Chhattisgarh, Jharkhand, and Punjab.
