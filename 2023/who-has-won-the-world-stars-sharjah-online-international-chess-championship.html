Who has won the World Stars Sharjah Online International Chess Championship?

(A) ** Shakhriyar Mamedyarov

(B) Pentala Harikrishna

(C) Vladimir Kramnik

(D) Magnus Carlsen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The talented Uzbek GM Nodiberk Abdusattorov (pictured right) won the title of the 5th Sharjah Masters International Chess Championship 2022 after scoring 7 points out of 9 games.

Concept note-2: -The tournament was conceived and organised by English player Howard Staunton, and marked the first time that the best chess players in Europe would meet in a single event. Adolf Anderssen of Germany won the sixteen-player tournament, earning him the status of the best player in the world.

Concept note-3: -The oldest modern chess tournament is the Ries Divan knockout, organized in London in 1849 and won by Buckle. The oldest international tournament is the London International, won by Anderssen in 1851.

Concept note-4: -1851 London: The first international chess tournament was held in London as part of the Great Exhibition of 1851. Sixteen invited players competed in an elimination event, won by Adolf Anderssen of Germany.
