Who is the only Indian to feature on Forbes 2020 list of the World’s highest paid celebrities?

(A) Shahid Kapoor

(B) ** Akshay Kumar

(C) Salman Khan

(D) Amitabh Bachchan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Akshay Kumar is the only Bollywood star to feature in top 10 of Forbes’ highest-paid actors list for the year 2020. Akshay Kumar features in Forbes’ list of highest-paid actors.

Concept note-2: -Some time back, Akshay also featured in Forbes’ list of Asia-Pacific’s Most Influential Celebrities on Social Media. The others actors on the list were Amitabh Bachchan, Shah Rukh Khan, Alia Bhatt, Ranveer Singh, Katrina Kaif, Hrithik Roshan, Shahid Kapoor, Anushka Sharma and Madhuri Dixit.

Concept note-3: -Bollywood actor Akshay Kumar tops the richest celebrities chart while Dharma Productions owner and Brahmāstra producer Karan Johar also features among celebrities raking in the moolah.

Concept note-4: -New Delhi: Akshay Kumar made it to another Forbes list this year and nobody’s surprised. The 52-year-old actor ranks at #6 on Forbes’ 2020 list of 10 Highest Paid Male Actors with his earnings estimated at $48.5 million-he is the only Indian on the list.

Concept note-5: -Akshay Kumar only Bollywood star among Forbes’ highest-paid actors of 2020 with Rs 362 cr earning, Dwayne Johnson tops list.
