Which town in Bataan became the capital of the Philippines during the Spanish era?

(A) Dinalupihan

(B) Hermosa

(C) Balanga

(D) ** Abucay

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Balanga is the provincial capital and the only city in Bataan.

Concept note-2: -Bataan is a province at the Central Luzon region of Luzon. A peninsula surrounded by Manila Bay to the east and the West Philippine Sea (or South China Sea) to the west, it is known in history as the last stronghold of the Americans in the Philippines when the Japanese invaded it during World War II.

Concept note-3: -Bataan, then known as Vatan, was part of the vast Capampangan Empire that included provinces known as Pampanga, Nueva Ecija, Tarlac and some portions of Bulacan, Zambales and Pangasinan.

Concept note-4: -Abucay is an agricultural town where rice and fish are the main product.
