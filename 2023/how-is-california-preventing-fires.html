How is California preventing fires?

(A) Using more sprinklers

(B) No more camping

(C) ** Cutting electricity and gas

(D) No more bonfires

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ecological Forest Restoration (ecological thinning combined with controlled burns) can significantly reduce the risk of megafires in fire-adapted conifer forests.

Concept note-2: -California uses controlled burns as a technique to mitigate wildfires. The California fire agency purposefully sets fires in order to minimize the threat of destruction from accidental fires.

Concept note-3: -In 2020, Newsom signed an agreement with the U.S. Forest Service, in which USFS and California each promised to perform 500, 000 acres of fire prevention and forest management work in the state every year by 2025.
