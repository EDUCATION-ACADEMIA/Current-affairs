Dr. Which statement is false regarding Babasaheb Ambedkar?

(A) With his inspiration Finance Commission was established.

(B) RBI was established with his ideas.

(C) ** He founded the “Independent Labor Party” in 1940.

(D) His role in Damodar Ghati scheme was important.

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Founding conference It proved to be the foundation conference of the Independent Labour Party and MP Keir Hardie was elected as its first chairman.

Concept note-2: -Bhimrao Ramji Ambedkar is known as the father of the Indian Constitution. He was the then Law Minister who introduced the final draft of the Constitution in the Constituent Assembly. He took a very prominent part in the deliberations of the Assembly.

Concept note-3: -The Independent Labour Party (ILP) was a political Organisation formed under the leadership of B. R. Ambedkar on 15 August 1936. It opposed the brahmanical and capitalist structures in India, supported the Indian working class and sought to dismantle the caste system.
