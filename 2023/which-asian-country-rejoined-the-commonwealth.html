Which Asian country rejoined the Commonwealth?

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** The Maldives

(C) Malaysia

(D) Thailand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which country recently joined the Commonwealth of Nations despite not having historic ties with Britain? Notes: Gabon and Togo in West Africa have recently joined the Commonwealth of Nations, an intergovernmental grouping mostly consisting of former British colonies.

Concept note-2: -The five current South Asian members of the Commonwealth (India, Pakistan, Bangladesh, Sri Lanka, and the Maldives) with their individual diversities, differences, persisting problems and potential illustrate many of the perplexities inherent in the contemporary Commonwealth.

Concept note-3: -Dileep Vishwakarma. Notes: Maldives officially re-joined the Commonwealth on February 1, 2020 and became the 54th member of the Commonwealth Union.
