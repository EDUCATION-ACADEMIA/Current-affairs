Who among the following won two gold medals in the ISSF Presidents Cup?

(A) ** Manu Bhekar

(B) Rahi Sarnobat

(C) Saurabh Chaudhary

(D) Abhishek Verma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manu Bhaker is an Indian Olympian who competes at airgun shooting. She represented India at the 2018 ISSF World Cup and won two gold medals.

Concept note-2: -India’s Manu Bhaker bagged two gold medals.

Concept note-3: -At the 2021 edition of the ISSF President’s Cup, India won five medals-two golds, two silvers and one bronze. It is an international body which governs International shooting. It was founded in 1907 as International Shooting Union and later renamed as ISSF in 1998.

Concept note-4: -The ISSF President’s Cup 2022 will take place in Cairo from November 28th till December 4th. The top 12 ranked athletes in each individual Olympic event according to the 2022 World Ranking will be invited to take part there. Their accommodation and travel expenses will be covered by the ISSF.
