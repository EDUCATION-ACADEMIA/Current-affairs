The navies of which two nations jointly launched ‘CARAT’ naval exercise on November 4?

(A) India, Sri Lanka

(B) ** India, Bangladesh

(C) Bangladesh, US

(D) US, Vietnam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -US military personnel and Bangladesh Navy (BN) conducted the 27th annual Cooperation Afloat Readiness and Training (CARAT) maritime exercise in the Bay of Bengal.

Concept note-2: -21st EDITION OF INDIA FRANCE BILATERAL NAVAL EXERCISE ‘VARUNA’ – 2023. The 21st Edition of the Bilateral Naval Exercise between India and France – Exercise Varuna commenced on the Western Seaboard today, 16 Jan 23.

Concept note-3: -The 2023 edition of CARAT/MAREX Sri Lanka is the fifth such bilateral exercise between Sri Lanka and the United States designed to promote regional security cooperation and strengthen maritime partnerships.

Concept note-4: -Bongo Sagar is the naval exercise between Bangladesh and India.

Concept note-5: -The 19th (and most recent) edition of the Indian and French Navy bilateral exercise ‘VARUNA-2021’ was held in the Arabian Sea. The United Arab Emirates (UAE) took part in the Varuna maritime exercise for the first time. Since 1993, the Indian and French navies have conducted bilateral maritime exercises.
