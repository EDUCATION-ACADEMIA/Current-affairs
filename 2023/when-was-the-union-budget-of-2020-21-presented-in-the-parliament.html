When was the union budget of 2020-21 presented in the parliament?

(A) Feb 29, 2020

(B) Mar 18, 2020

(C) ** Feb 1, 2020

(D) April 1, 2020

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2020 Union Budget of India (ISO: 2020 kē liē Bhārat kā Kēndrīya Bajaṭ) was presented by the Finance Minister, Nirmala Sitharaman on 1 February 2020, as her second budget.

Concept note-2: -On 1 February 2021, Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman presented the first paperless budget.

Concept note-3: -The first Union Budget of the third decade of 21st century was presented by the Minister for Finance & Corporate Affairs, Ms Nirmala Sitharaman in the Parliament on February 1st, 2020.

Concept note-4: -The pre-budget Economic Survey 2023 will be tabled by Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman in Parliament on January 31. The Survey highlights the state of the economy in the current financial year 2022-23.

Concept note-5: -At 11 am on February 1, the Union Finance Minister, Nirmala Sitharaman, will present her straight fifth Budget.
