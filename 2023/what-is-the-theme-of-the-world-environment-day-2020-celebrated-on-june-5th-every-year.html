What is the theme of the World Environment Day 2020 celebrated on June 5th every year?

(A) Beat Plastic Pollution

(B) ** Biodiversity

(C) Connecting People to Nature

(D) Green Economy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for World Environment Day 2020 is, ‘Time for Nature, ’ with a focus on its role in providing the essential infrastructure that supports life on Earth and human development.

Concept note-2: -"Biodiversity” is the theme declared by United Nations for World Environment Day, 2020. The 2020 World Environment Day theme focused on biodiversity – a concern both urgent and existential.

Concept note-3: -World Environment Day 2022 is hosted by Sweden. “Only One Earth” is the campaign slogan, with the focus on “Living Sustainably in Harmony with Nature”.

Concept note-4: -The theme of this year’s World Environment Day is Ecosystem Restoration. Pakistan will act as global host of the day. World Environment Day 2021 will see the launch of the UN Decade on Ecosystem Restoration.

Concept note-5: -2020: Biodiversity and Time for Nature The theme for World Environment Day 2020 is “Biodiversity” with the slogan “Time for Nature”-a call to action to combat the accelerating species loss and degradation of the natural world.
