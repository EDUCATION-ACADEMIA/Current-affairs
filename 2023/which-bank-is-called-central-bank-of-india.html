WHICH BANK IS CALLED CENTRAL BANK OF INDIA?

(A) PUNJAB NATIONAL BANK

(B) ICICI BANK

(C) ** RBI

(D) SBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India is the central bank of the country.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India is the central bank of the country.

Concept note-3: -Reserve Bank of India (RBI) is India’s central bank. It controls the monetary policy concerning the national currency, the Indian rupee. The basic functions of the RBI are the issuance of currency, to sustain monetary stability in India, to operate the currency, and maintain the country’s credit system.

Concept note-4: -A central bank, reserve bank, or monetary authority is an institution that manages the currency and monetary policy of a country or monetary union, and oversees their commercial banking system.

Concept note-5: -RBI (Reserve Bank of India) is the Central Bank of the country.
