Asia Cup is a popular tournament of which sports?

(A) 1.Football

(B) ** 2.Cricket

(C) 3.Hockey

(D) 4.Badminton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Asian Cricket Council Asia Cup is a men’s One Day International and Twenty20 International cricket tournament. It was established in 1983 when the Asian Cricket Council was founded as a measure to promote goodwill between Asian countries.

Concept note-2: -Asian Cup, in full AFC Asian Cup, Asian football (soccer) competition that takes place every four years and is that continent’s premier football tournament. The Asian Cup is governed by the Asian Football Confederation (AFC) and was first held in 1956, with South Korea winning the inaugural title.

Concept note-3: -The 2022 Asia Cup (also known as DP World Asia Cup for sponsorship reasons) was the 15th edition of the Asia Cup cricket tournament, with the matches played as Twenty20 Internationals (T20Is) during August and September 2022 in the United Arab Emirates. Sri lanka won the tournament after beating Pakistan in the Final.

Concept note-4: -The winner of the Asia Cup 2022 was Sri Lanka in the tournament held in UAE. This tournament is held biennially (every two years). After defeating Pakistan by 23 runs in the final match, Sri Lanka was added to the prestigious Asia Cup Winners List.

Concept note-5: -Asia Cup 2023 Schedule is from 2nd September 2023 to 17th September 2023. Which is the host team of Asia Cup 2023? The host team of Asia Cup 2023 is Pakistan.
