In the text, which country borders or is a neighboring country to Eritrea?

(A) ** Sudan

(B) Egypt

(C) Libya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is bordered by Ethiopia in the south, Sudan in the west, and Djibouti in the southeast.

Concept note-2: -The land boundary between Eritrea and Sudan extends for 686 kilometers from the Red Sea to the tripoint with Ethiopia, mainly following a course laid out by Italian and British colonial surveyors.

Concept note-3: -Bordered by Sudan, Ethiopia and Djibouti, it occupies a strategically important area in the Horn of Africa.

Concept note-4: -↔ Kilometers: 1063.33 km. / Miles: 660.72 miles. / Nautical Miles: 573.77 NM. ✈ Estimated flight time: 1.17 hours.

Concept note-5: -Eritrea is located in Eastern Africa in the Horn of Africa, to the North of Ethiopia and bordering the Red Sea in Northeast. It has a mixed Afro-Asiatic population that is divided by religion and language. The former Italian and British colony gained formal independence from Ethiopia, its last colonizer, in 1993.
