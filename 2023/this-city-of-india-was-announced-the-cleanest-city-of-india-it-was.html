This city of India was announced the cleanest city of India, it was?

(A) ** Indore

(B) Jaipur

(C) Bangalore

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indore was named the cleanest city in the country for the sixth time in a row, drawing praise from Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan.

Concept note-2: -Indore city of Madhya Pradesh has won the title of the cleanest city in India for the sixth consecutive year, thanks to the daily processing of 1, 900 tons of municipal trash, which generates millions of rupees in revenue and supplies fuel for its buses.

Concept note-3: -Indore was named as the first cleanest city in India by the Minister of Housing and Urban Affair. It has been on top of the list for two consecutive years – 2021 and 2022.

Concept note-4: -1. Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Thanks to planned waste segregation, conversion and disposal, Indore is the cleanest city in India 2022.
