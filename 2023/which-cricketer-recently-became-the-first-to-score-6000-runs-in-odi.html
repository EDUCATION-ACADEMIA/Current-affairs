Which cricketer recently became the first to score 6000 runs in ODI?

(A) Smriti Madhana

(B) ** Mithali Raj

(C) Harmanpreet Kaur

(D) Isha Bhojwani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In February 2017, she became the second player to score 5, 500 runs in ODIs. Mithali is the first player to captain most matches for India in ODI and T20I. In July 2017, she became the first player to score 6, 000 runs in WODIs.

Concept note-2: -Mithali Raj is the first Indian woman cricketer to score runs in International T-20 women’s cricket 2000. Mithali Raj created history in the recently concluded Women’s ODI World Cup tournament by becoming the leading run-getter in women’s ODI. By then she had more than 6, 000 runs in ODIs.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Mithali Raj. Former Indian Women’s Cricket team captain Mithali Raj became the first female cricketer in history to score 7, 000 ODI runs. She achieved this landmark in her 213th ODI on 15 March 2021.

Concept note-4: -Leading female ODI cricket run-scorers 2023 As of September 2022, Mithali Raj tops the list of all-time run scorers in women’s ODIs, having amassed 7, 805 in her career between 1999 and 2022.

Concept note-5: -Sachin Tendulkar The master blaster, Sachin Tendulkar took 120 innings to score 6000 test runs.
