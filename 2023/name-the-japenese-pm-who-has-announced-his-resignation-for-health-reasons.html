name the japenese pm who has announced his resignation for health reasons

(A) ** PM Shinzo Abe

(B) Yoshihide Suga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Confirmed by the National Diet, Abe became Japan’s youngest post-war prime minister and the first born after World War II. Abe resigned as prime minister after one year due to ulcerative colitis and his party’s recent election losses.

Concept note-2: -Shinzo Abe, (born September 21, 1954, Tokyo, Japan-died July 8, 2022, Nara, Japan), Japanese politician, who twice was prime minister of Japan (2006–07 and 2012–20).
