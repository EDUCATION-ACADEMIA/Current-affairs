When is World Malala Day celebrated every year?

(A) July 4

(B) July 8

(C) ** July 12

(D) July 16

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year on July 12th, the world celebrates the birthday of Malala Yousafzai. At age 17, Malala was the youngest recipient ever of the Nobel Peace Prize. The day also honors the rights of children and women. Malala Yousafzai was born in Mingora, Pakistan, on July 12th, 1997.

Concept note-2: -July 12 is observed as International Malala Day to salute the Nobel Laureate Malala Yousafzai, who works for women’s right to education across the globe.

Concept note-3: -The United Nations declared July 12 as Malala Day to honour Pakistani activist Malala Yousafzai, who became the international symbol of the fight for girls’ education.

Concept note-4: -Malala Yousafzai spoke to the U.N. Youth Assembly on her 16th birthday on July 12, 2013.

Concept note-5: -14 Nov The Fight Goes On: The International Malala Day One month after the 15-year-old Pakistani education activist, Malala Yousafzai was shot in the head by the Taliban, the United Nations called upon the international community to observe November 10 as the International Malala Day.
