Which is the smallest emirate of UAE?

(A) Dubai

(B) Sharjah

(C) Abu Dhabi

(D) ** Ajman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ajman is the smallest of the seven Emirates, covering 259 square kilometres, about 0.3% of the UAE’s main landmass.

Concept note-2: -The Emirate of Ajman (Arabic: ; pronounced [ʔɪmaːra(t) ʕaʤmaːn]) (Gulf Arabic: emāratʿymān) is one of the seven emirates of the United Arab Emirates.

Concept note-3: -It is the fifth-largest city in UAE after Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah and Al Ain. Located along the Persian Gulf, it is engulfed by the larger emirate of Sharjah in territory.

Concept note-4: -Ajman is the smallest of the seven emirates and is centrally located on the western coast of the United Arab Emirates. The Emirate lie on the Gulf coast where its beach extends for 16 Km. whereas the total area of Ajman is 259 square Km. The area of the Emirate of Ajman is about 460 Sq.
