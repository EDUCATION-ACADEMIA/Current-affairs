Which Indian International Airport introduced India’s first ever fully contactless airport car parking?

(A) ** GMR International Airport, Hyderabad

(B) Indira Gandhi International Airport, New Delhi

(C) Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel International Airport, Ahmedabad

(D) Kempegowda International Airport, Bengaluru

(E) Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose International Airport, Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first fully contact-less car parking at GMR’s Hyderabad International Airport. Imagine how good it would be if you zip through the parking entry or exit gates at airport without having to worry about keeping change or payment receipts handy.

Concept note-2: -Customers use their phones to locate a car park, book space and pay. Digital wallets such as Google Wallet, Apple Pay, etc., provide the convenience of ticketless parking.

Concept note-3: -The group was founded in 1978 by Grandhi Mallikarjuna Rao (G M Rao) and comprises several companies including GMR Infrastructure, GMR Energy, GMR Airports, GMR Enterprises.

Concept note-4: -GMR Hyderabad International Airport Limited (GHIAL) is a company promoted as a joint venture comprising the GMR Group (63%) in partnership with Airports Authority of India (AAI) (13%), Government of Telangana (13%) and Malaysia Airports Holdings Berhad (11%).

Concept note-5: -Quality parking service is assured from car park operator and efficient integrated parking automation system is implemented for the quick entry and quick exit from the parking points.
