How many days it took India to achieve one crore COVID-19 vaccination mark?

(A) 100 days

(B) 50 days

(C) 45 days

(D) ** 34 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CDC has shortened its recommended interval for receiving a booster shot from six months to five for people who received the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine.

Concept note-2: -Your appointment should last for around 30 to 45 minutes. You’ll be asked some questions about your medical history. It’s important to tell the staff giving you the vaccination if you have ever had a severe allergic reaction or you are pregnant.

Concept note-3: -However, it typically takes some weeks for the body to produce immunity against COVID-19 after vaccination.

Concept note-4: -The vaccine received DCGI approval for Phase I & II Human Clinical Trials in July, 2020.
