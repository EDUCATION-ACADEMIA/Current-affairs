The Chief Financial officer, M D Ranganath has recently resigned. He was a CFO of which company?

(A) . Tech Mahindra

(B) . Wipro

(C) . TCS

(D) ** . Infosys

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Synopsis. The Board at its meeting held today has accepted resignation of M. D. Ranganath as the CFO, Infosys said. Infosys said its chief financial officer M D Ranganath has resigned.

Concept note-2: -Infosys – Nilanjan Roy | Management Profiles.
