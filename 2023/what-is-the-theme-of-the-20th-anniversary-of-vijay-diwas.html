What is the theme of the 20th anniversary of Vijay Diwas

(A) Peace & Humanity

(B) Salute valour of the armed forced

(C) Pride, Honour & Inspire

(D) ** Remember, Rejoice and Renew

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 20th anniversary of Operation Vijay is being celebrated across the nation with the theme of “Remember – Rejoice – Renew” by remembering the martyrs and by revisiting their sacrifices to instill pride and respect, rejoicing by celebrating the victory and renewing the resolve to safeguard the honour of the tricolour.

Concept note-2: -This day commemorates the success of ‘’Operation Vijay” which was launched to take back the Indian territories captured by the Pakistani intruders in the Kargil-Drass sector in 1999. On Kargil Vijay Diwas, let’s remember the courage and determination of our armed forces, who steadfastly protected our nation in 1999.

Concept note-3: -Vijay Diwas 2022: The day commemorates the sacrifices made by the defence forces in the decisive war that saw India emerge victorious. India celebrates Vijay Diwas on December 16 to mark its victory over Pakistan in the 1971 war. The war led to the liberation of East Pakistan and the creation of Bangladesh.

Concept note-4: -Vijay Diwas is celebrated on December 16 every year to honour the victory of Indian armed forces over Pakistan in the 1971 war. On the day, India pays homage to all the soldiers who defended the nation.

Concept note-5: -Vijay Diwas is being celebrated across the nation on December 16 to commemorate India’s victory against Pakistan in the 1971 war. December 16 as a day forms a very significant part of modern Indian history. It is a reminder of India’s emerging as a celebrated country in the 1971 war against Pakistan.
