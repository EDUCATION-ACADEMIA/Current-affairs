Who is a lawyer among the elected members of Rajya Sabha?

(A) ** Abhay Bharadwaj

(B) Ramila Bara

(C) Narahari Amin

(D) ** Shaktisinh Gohil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shaktisinh Harishchandrasinhji Gohil (born 4 April 1960) is an Indian politician. He is a Member of Parliament and a member of the Indian National Congress. He is party in-charge for state affairs of Delhi and Haryana and national spokesperson of All India Congress Committee.

Concept note-2: -The only other BJP members to serve four terms as a Rajya Sabha member are Arun Jaitley, Pramod Mahajan, Venkaiah Naidu, and Ravi Shankar Prasad. Among the sitting members, Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi has the longest total tenure length of 6791 days.

Concept note-3: -Membership is limited to 250 members, and the present Rajya Sabha has 245 members. 233 members are elected by the Vidhan Sabha members and 12 are nominated by the President for their contributions to art, literature, science, and social services.
