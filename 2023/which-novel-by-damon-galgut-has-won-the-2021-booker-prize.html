Which novel by DAMON GALGUT has won the 2021 Booker Prize?

(A) The Good doctor

(B) At night all blood is black

(C) Small circle of beings the imposter Arctic summer the quarry.

(D) ** The promise

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DAMON GALGUT is the author of nine novels. He won the Booker Prize 2021 for The Promise, having been shortlisted for the prize twice before (The Good Doctor and In A Strange Room). He lives and works in Cape Town.

Concept note-2: -Actor Ken Nwosu reads an extract from At Night All Blood is Black, winner of the 2021 International Booker Prize, in a performance created for Edinburgh International Book Festival in partnership with the Royal Shakespeare Company, and directed by Blanche McIntyre.

Concept note-3: -Geetanjali Shree has become the first Indian writer to win the International Booker Prize. Her novel Tomb of Sand, a family saga set in the shadow of the partition of India, follows an 80-year-old woman after the death of her husband. It was the first Hindi-language book to be shortlisted for the £50, 000 prize.

Concept note-4: -Author Geetanjali Shree’s translated Hindi novel, Tomb of Sand, became the first Indian language book to win the International Booker Prize Friday. The 2018 novel titled ‘Ret Samadhi’ was translated by Daisy Rockwell and published as ‘Tomb of Sand’ in 2021.

Concept note-5: -Damon Galgut wins 2021 Booker Prize for ‘The Promise’ Salome is absolutely tangible, a figure I know from everyday life. But in fact I chose not to give her character much weight or substance in the book, but rather to outline her through the perceptions of the white characters around her.
