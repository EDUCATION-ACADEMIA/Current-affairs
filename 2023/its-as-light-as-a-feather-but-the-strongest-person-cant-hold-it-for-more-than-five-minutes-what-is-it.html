It’s as light as a feather, but the strongest person can’t hold it for more than five minutes. What is it?

(A) Cotton

(B) Snow

(C) ** Air

(D) Water

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We have the answer right down below! The answer to the riddle is Breath.

Concept note-2: -Answer-Snowflakes. I’m lighter than a feather yet the strongest person can’t hold me for more than six minutes.

Concept note-3: -To be ‘as light as a feather’ means to be very light in weight. Example of use: “Didn’t you pack anything? Your luggage is as light as a feather.”

Concept note-4: -What’s as big as a Christmas tree but is lighter than a feather? Its shadow.
