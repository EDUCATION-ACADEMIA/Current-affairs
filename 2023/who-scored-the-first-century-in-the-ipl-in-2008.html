Who scored the first century in the IPL in 2008?

(A) Suresh Raina

(B) Virat Kohli

(C) ** Brendon McCullum

(D) Michael Hussey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first century in the IPL was scored in the first match on 18 April 2008 at M. Chinnaswamy Stadium, Bangalore by Brendon McCullum for Kolkata Knight Riders against Royal Challengers Bangalore.

Concept note-2: -Why not share? Teenager Manish Pandey whacked 114 not out to steer Bangalore Royal Challengers to a 12-run win over Deccan Chargers and into the Indian Premier League semifinals on Thursday.

Concept note-3: -Kiwi hard hitter Brendon McCullum is the first batter to score a century in IPL history. His 158* ( 10 fours and 13 sixes )for Kolkata Knight Riders in the inaugural match against Royal Challengers Bangalore held the record for the highest individual score for a few years to come.

Concept note-4: -Royal Challengers Bangalore vs Kolkata Knight Riders, 1st match, Indian Premier League 2008-Cricbuzz.

Concept note-5: -Brendon McCullum scored the first ever century in the history of the IPL.
