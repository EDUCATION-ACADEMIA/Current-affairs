Burj Khalifa, the tallest structure in the world measures more than ____

(A) ** 2716 feet

(B) 2616 feet

(C) 2516 feet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -At over 828 metres (2, 716.5 feet) and more than 160 stories, Burj Khalifa holds the following records: Tallest building in the world. Tallest free-standing structure in the world. Highest number of stories in the world.

Concept note-2: -At 2, 722 feet from base to tip, the Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world. When construction finished in 2010, it claimed the record from Taipei 101, a mixed-use building in Taipei, China, that stands at 1, 667 feet tall.

Concept note-3: -This prompted the Burj Khalifa to open a new deck called “At the Top Sky.” At 555 meters, it is currently the highest observation deck in the world. The very top of the Burj Khalifa is the 163rd floor; however, this is not accessible to the public.
