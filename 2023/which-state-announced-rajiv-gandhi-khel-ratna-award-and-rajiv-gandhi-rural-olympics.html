Which state announced ‘Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award’ and ‘Rajiv Gandhi Rural Olympics’?

(A) Chhattisgarh

(B) ** Rajasthan

(C) Punjab

(D) Manipur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which state announced ‘Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award’ and ‘Rajiv Gandhi Rural Olympics’? Notes: Rajasthan Chief Minister Ashok Gehlot announced that the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award will be introduced in the state for sportspersons.

Concept note-2: -Rajasthan: Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award and Rural Olympics.

Concept note-3: -Rajasthan CM introduced Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award in the State for Sportspersons. Rajasthan Chief Minister(CM) Ashok Gehlot has announced that the state government is set to introduce the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award to the State’s sportspersons.

Concept note-4: -The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, previously called the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award in Sports and Games, is India’s highest athletic honour. The Ministry of Youth Affairs and Sports grants it annually to honour and reward outstanding athletic achievement.

Concept note-5: -Table tennis legend Achanta Sharath Kamal will receive the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award, the country’s highest sporting honour, from President Droupadi Murmu on November 30 at the Rashtrapati Bhavan in New Delhi.
