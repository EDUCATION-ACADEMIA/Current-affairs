What is the full form of TOEFL

(A) Test on English as a Foreign Language

(B) Training of English as a Foreign Language

(C) Test of English Fluency Levels

(D) ** Test of English as a Foreign Language

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is the Full form of TOEFL? The full form of TOEFL is the Test of English as a Foreign Language. TOEFL is a standard test to assess the potential of non-native English speakers who plan to study in English-speaking universities to understand English.
