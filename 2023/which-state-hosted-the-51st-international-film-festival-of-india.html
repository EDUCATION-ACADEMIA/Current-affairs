Which state hosted the 51st International Film Festival of India?

(A) Karnataka

(B) ** Goa

(C) Maharashtra

(D) Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 51st International Film Festival of India was held from 16 to 24 January 2021 in Goa.

Concept note-2: -51st International Film Festival of India (IFFI) has announced a line-up of programs that will be showcased on the Over-the-top (OTT) platform. The festival will be held in Goa from 16th to 24th January 2021. Bangladesh will be this edition’s Country in Focus.

Concept note-3: -53rd Edition of International Film Festival of India hosted in Goa – November 20 – 28, 2022.

Concept note-4: -The 51st edition of International Film Festival of India has announced its International Jury with eminent filmmakers from across the world. The Jury will comprise Pablo Cesar (Argentina) as Chairman, Prasanna Vithanage (Sri Lanka), Abu Bakr Shawky (Austria), Priyadarshan (India) and Ms. Rubaiyat Hossain (Bangladesh).

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Bangladesh. Bangladesh was the country in focus at the 51st International Film Festival of India held in Goa in January 2021.
