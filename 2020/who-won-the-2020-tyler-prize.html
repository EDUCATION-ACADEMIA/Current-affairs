Who won the 2020 Tyler Prize?

(A) Jagdish Bhagwati

(B) Rajendra K. Pachauri

(C) Arvind Subramanian

(D) ** Pavan Sukhdev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Renowned Indian environmental economist and UN Environment Programme (UNEP) Goodwill Ambassador Pavan Sukhdev has won the 2020 Tyler Prize. It is regarded as the “Nobel Prize for the Environment". He was awarded for his groundbreaking “green economy” work.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Tyler Prize for Environmental Achievement has been won by Andy Haines, professor of environmental change and public health at the London School of Hygiene & Tropical Medicine (London, UK).

Concept note-3: -’Nobel for environment’: India’s Pavan Sukhdev wins Tyler Prize.

Concept note-4: -The Tyler Prize Haines will receive a US$200, 000 prize and join the ranks of previous Tyler Prize Laureates such as Michael E. Mann, E.O. Wilson, and Jane Goodall.

Concept note-5: -Q. Which Indian has been recently awarded the Tyler Prize for Environmental Achievement? Notes: Renowned environmental economist and UN Environment Programme (UNEP) Goodwill Ambassador Pavan Sukhdev has been awarded the prestigious Tyler Prize for Environmental Achievement, for this year.
