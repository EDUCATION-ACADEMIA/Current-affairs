Makar Sankranti is celebrated on which date?

(A) 12th January

(B) 13th January

(C) ** 14th January

(D) 15th January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This is the day when Sun begins its movement from Dakshinayana (South) to Uttarayana (North) hemisphere. On this auspicious day, Sun travels from Sagittarius zodiac to Capricorn sun sign. As per Drik Panchang, Makar Sankranti is being celebrated today, on January 15, 2023 this year.

Concept note-2: -Makar Sankranti is a unique festival which happens on the 14/15 January each year. It is the only day which is celebrated according to the solar calendar and marks the time when the Sun moves into Makara ( Capricorn) constellation/zodiac or towards the North direction which is also known as Uttarayana.

Concept note-3: -This sequence will continue till 2030. After this Sankranti will be celebrated on 15th January for 3 years and on 14th January for 1st year. From 2080, Sankranti will be celebrated only on 15 January.

Concept note-4: -Every year the Makar sankranti date and time was on 14 January but due to some reason the makar sankranti puja time 2023 was shifted to 15 January. The Makar sankranti puja time is 8:21 Pm.
