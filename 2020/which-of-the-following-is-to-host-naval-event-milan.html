Which of the following is to host naval event MILAN?

(A) ** Visakhapatnam

(B) Kochi

(C) Chennai

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise Milan-2022 is all set to be organized in two phases from February 25 in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh. Milan will be conducted over a duration of nine days in two phases with the harbour phase scheduled from February 25 and the Sea Phase from March 1 to March 4.

Concept note-2: -INDIAN NAVY’S MULTI-NATIONAL EXERCISE MILAN-2022 TO COMMENCE 25 FEBRUARY 2022. The latest edition of Indian Navy’s multilateral exercise MILAN 2022 is scheduled to commence from 25 Feb 22 in the ‘City of Destiny’, Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-3: -Milan (Hindi: Milan, lit. “Meeting", “Unification", or “Confluence") is a multilateral naval exercise hosted by the Indian Navy.

Concept note-4: -India has invited a total of 46 friendly foreign countries to participate in the multinational naval exercise Milan in Visakhapatnam, scheduled from February 25, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 46. India has invited a total of 46 nations to participate in the multinational naval exercise ‘Milan’ in Visakhapatnam, scheduled from February 25, 2022. The theme of this edition of exercise Milan is camaraderie, cohesion and collaboration.
