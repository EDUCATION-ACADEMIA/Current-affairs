Which state’s tourism won UNWTO’s Accessible Destination Award?

(A) Rajasthan

(B) Madhya Pradesh

(C) West Bengal

(D) ** Kerala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kerala Tourism earns a special mention by UNWTO for its Barrier-Free Tourism Project | Times of India Travel.

Concept note-2: -Kerala is the first state to recognize tourism as an industry. Kerala is one of the states that make a maximum profit through the tourism industry.

Concept note-3: -Kerala has won the prestigious Responsible Tourism Global Award for its STREET Project By The Responsible Tourism Mission. Known for its backwaters and popularly known as ‘God’s Own Country, ’ Kerala has been a famous tourist destination in India.

Concept note-4: -The Kerala Tourism has bagged the prestigious Responsible Tourism Global award at the World Travel Mart held in London. State Tourism Minister PA Mohammed Riyas received the award on behalf of the department at London.
