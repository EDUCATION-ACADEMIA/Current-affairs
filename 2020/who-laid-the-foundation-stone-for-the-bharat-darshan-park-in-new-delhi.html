Who laid the foundation stone for the ‘Bharat Darshan Park’ in New Delhi?

(A) R. K. Singh

(B) ** Hardeep Singh Puri

(C) Durga Shanker Mishra

(D) Piyush Goyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Undertaken by the South Delhi Municipal Corporation (SDMC), the project was constructed over a period of 22 months in an area spanning across 8.5 acres using 350 metric tonnes of waste material - old automobile parts, iron rods and electricity poles.

Concept note-2: -The park, which spans 8.5 acres in west Delhi’s Punjabi Bagh, has been created at the cost of Rs 14 crore. The 22 replicas have been constructed with 350 tonnes of scrap material, for example, old vehicles, fans, iron rods, nuts and bolts that were unused/left at the SDMC stores or other places.
