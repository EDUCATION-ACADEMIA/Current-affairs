Who recently became New Zealand’s top run scorer in test cricket?

(A) Kane Williamson

(B) ** Ross Taylor

(C) Tom Latham

(D) Colin de Grandhomme

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Former middle order batsman Ross Taylor holds the top place on the list of most test runs for New Zealand. Starting in 2007, Taylor made 7633 runs in 112 test matches at a decent average of 44.66.

Concept note-2: -The number three will be the most technically sound batsman in this batting lineup and the New Zealand cricket captain, Kane Williamson. His calm nature in captaincy and classy batting in all formats of the game made him one of the greatest in this era.

Concept note-3: -The leading run-scorer of all time in cricket test matches is India’s Sachin Tendulkar. Nicknamed “The Little Master", Tendulkar scored 15, 921 in his test match career spanning from 1989 to 2013.
