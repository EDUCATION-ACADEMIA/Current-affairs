Nitin Gadkari inaugurated Mahatma Gandhi bridge in which state?

(A) Haryana

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) ** Bihar

(D) Rajasthan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The length of Mahatma Gandhi Setu is 5, 750m (18, 860) making it the third-longest bridge in India. In May 1982, the bridge was inaugurated by the then Prime minister Ms. Indira Gandhi, from Hajipur, Bihar.

Concept note-2: -The bridge is located near Patna, capital of the state of Bihar in Eastern India.
