What is Mirabai Chanu’s rank in the Olympics qualifiers ranking list?

(A) 5th

(B) 6th

(C) 7th

(D) ** 8th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mirabai Chanu is second in her category in the absolute ranking list announced by the International Weightlifting Federation.

Concept note-2: -India’s ace weightlifter was rewarded for winning a silver medal at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics. Indian weightlifter Mirabai Chanu was inducted as an Additional Superintendent of Police (sports) by the Manipur Police in Imphal on Saturday.

Concept note-3: -The Indian weightlifter once thought of quitting after a dismal showing at Rio 2016 but the Tokyo Olympics gave Mirabai Chanu’s career a fresh lift. Ever since winning a silver at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics, Mirabai Chanu has become one of India’s most loved sporting heroes.

Concept note-4: -Commonwealth Games 2022 weightlifting: Mirabai Chanu wins India’s first gold medal.
