Which company is going to partner with various banks for Co-Branded credit cards?

(A) PhonePe

(B) Amazon

(C) Reliance

(D) ** Paytm

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tata Neu and HDFC Bank on Wednesday announced a partnership to launch co-branded credit cards. The card will be launched in two variants: Tata Neu Plus HDFC Bank Credit Card and Tata Neu Infinity HDFC Bank Credit Card.

Concept note-2: -Samsung India, Axis Bank launch co-branded Credit Card.

Concept note-3: -Samsung, India’s largest consumer electronics company, and Axis Bank, India’s third largest private sector bank, have partnered to launch an exclusive co- branded credit card, powered by Visa.

Concept note-4: -Tata Neu HDFC Bank Credit Card Details: HDFC Bank and Tata Neu have partnered to launch the ‘Tata Neu HDFC Bank Credit Card’. It will be available in two variants and allow customers to earn rewards on all spends, both online and in-store in the form of NeuCoins.

Concept note-5: -SBI Cards and Payment Services on Thursday announced that it has partnered with Punjab & Sind Bank to launch co-brand credit cards for the bank’s customers. The company said that the collaboration also marks the introduction of ‘credit cards’ as a new product segment under the bank’s portfolio.
