How many MoU have recently been signed between India and Brazil?

(A) 4

(B) 9

(C) ** 15

(D) 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: India and Brazil on Saturday signed 15 agreements to cement cooperation in areas ranging from energy and trade and investment to cyber security and information technology after talks between visiting Brazilian President Jair Messias Bolsonaro and Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-2: -India and Panama have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to enhance cooperation in the training of diplomats.

Concept note-3: -The two nations cooperate not only bilaterally but also at plurilateral fora such as BRICS, IBSA, G-20 and in multilateral bodies such as the UN and WTO, among others. India-Brazil relations were elevated to a Strategic Partnership in 2006, thereby heralding a new phase in bilateral ties.

Concept note-4: -The relations are based on a common global vision, shared democratic values and a commitment to foster inclusive economic growth for the welfare of the people of both countries. Brazil was the first Latin American nation to establish diplomatic relations with India in 1948.

Concept note-5: -The bilateral strategic partnership, which has opened a new phase for India-Brazil relations in 2006, is based on a common global vision, shared democratic values, and a commitment to foster economic growth with social inclusion for the welfare of the people of both countries.
