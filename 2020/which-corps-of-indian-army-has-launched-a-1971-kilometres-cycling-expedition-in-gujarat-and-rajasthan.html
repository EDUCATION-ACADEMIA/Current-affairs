Which Corps of Indian Army has launched a 1971 kilometres cycling expedition in Gujarat and Rajasthan?

(A) ** Konark Corps

(B) Nagrota Corps

(C) Kharga Corps

(D) Rising Star Corps

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Konark Corps flags-off 1971 Kms cyclothon to mark 50 years of 1971 Indo-Pak war .

Concept note-2: -The Correct Answer is Field Marshall SHFJ Manekshaw. During the Indo-Pakistani War of 1971, Field Marshal Sam Manekshaw was the Head of the Indian Armyâ€™s Army Staff and the first Indian Army officer to be promoted to the rank of field marshal. Hence, Option 2 is correct.

Concept note-3: -Corps: A command generally consists of two or more corps. Indian Army has 13 Corps each one commanded by a General Officer Commander (GOC), known as the Corps Commander, who holds the rank of Lieutenant General. Each corps is composed of 3 or 4 Divisions.
