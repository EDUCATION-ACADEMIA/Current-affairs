ICICI Lambord Signed Definitive Agreements to Merge their Insurance Businesses with which company?

(A) Bajaj Alliance

(B) LIC

(C) ** Bharti AXA

(D) Birla Sun lIfe

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The combined entity shall have a market share of   8.6%* on pro-forma basis. Through this proposed transaction, ICICI Lombard General Insurance shall be able to augment its distribution strength with erstwhile Bharti AXA General Insurance’s existing distribution partnerships.

Concept note-2: -The scheme of arrangement between Bharti AXA and ICICI Lombard came into effect on September 8, 2021.

Concept note-3: -ICICI Lombard’s combined ratio - the sum of incurred losses and expenses divided by the premium earned - was at about 101% before the merger and has worsened to 104% after the Bharti takeover. The combined ratio is a measure of an insurance company’s profitability.
