(A) President

(B) Prime Minister

Concept note-1: -Construction machinery means machines or devices drawn as trailers which are designed and used for construction, tree trimming and waste maintenance projects, which derive no revenue from the transportation of persons or property, whose use of the highway is.

Concept note-2: -1. Excavators. Excavators are considered a standard of the industry. They are large, heavy construction machines that are designed to move earth.

Concept note-3: -For building dams, heavy Machines are required which as Bulldozer with Blade and Ripper, Bulldozer with Hydraulic Scraper, Crusher Loaders Dumpers Sheepsfoot Roller, etc.
