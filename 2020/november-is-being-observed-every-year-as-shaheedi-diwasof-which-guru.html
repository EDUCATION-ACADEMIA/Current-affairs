November is being observed every year as Shaheedi Diwasof which Guru?

(A) Guru Angad

(B) Guru Hargobind

(C) ** Guru Tegh Bahadur

(D) Guru Amar Das

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year, November 28 is celebrated as Martyrdom Day of Guru Tegh Bahadur, the ninth Guru of Sikhs of Sikh religion. The day is celebrated as Shaheedi Divas of Guru Tegh Bahadur across the country.

Concept note-2: -Guru Tegh Bahadur’s martyrdom day Every year on November 24th, Shaheed Diwas, or Shaheedi Diwas, is observed to commemorate Guru Tegh Bahadur’s martyrdom.

Concept note-3: -Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day 2022, Guru Tegh Bahadur Shaheedi Diwas 2022: Guru Tegh Bahadur Martyrdom Day is observed every year on November 24, commemorating Shaheedi Diwas of Guru Tegh Bahadur.

Concept note-4: -Guru Tegh Bahadur is the 9th Guru of the Sikh community. He gave his life to protect human rights, and his death anniversary is celebrated on November 24 as Shaheedi Diwas. Guru Tegh Bahadur was born on April 21, 1621, in Amritsar.

Concept note-5: -Every year November 24 is observed as the day of the Martyrdom of Guru Tegh Bahadur. On this day in 1675, he was publicly Executed by the Mughal Emperor Aurangzeb in Delhi for refusing to accept Aurangzeb’s authority.
