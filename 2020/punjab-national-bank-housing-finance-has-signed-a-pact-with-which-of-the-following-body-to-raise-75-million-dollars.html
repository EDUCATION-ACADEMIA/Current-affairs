Punjab National Bank Housing Finance has signed a pact with which of the following body to raise 75 million dollars?

(A) ** Japan International Cooperation Agency

(B) The Asian Development Bank

(C) The Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank

(D) Industrial and Commercial Bank of China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PNB Housing Finance Ltd, promoted by Punjab National Bank, will raise $75 million from International Finance Corporation (IFC).

Concept note-2: -As on 30 September 2022, Punjab National Bank held 32.57% in the company. The housing finance company’s consolidated net profit rose 11.7% to Rs 262.63 crore on 6.1% rise in total income to Rs 1, 683.43 crore in Q2 FY23 over Q2 FY22.

Concept note-3: -Kousgi has over 25 years of extensive experience in mortgage and retail lending businesses New Delhi: PNB Housing Finance has appointed Girish Kousgi as its Managing Director and Chief Executive Officer, effective October 21.
