What is the theme of the united nations Africa industrialization day (AIW2020)?

(A) Positioning African Industry

(B) Financing Industrialization in Africa:Challenges

(C) ** Inclusive and sustainable industrialization in the AfCFTA and COVID-19 era

(D) None of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“Industrializing Africa: Renewed Commitment Towards an Inclusive and Sustainable Industrialization and Economic Diversification”

Concept note-2: -Africa Industrialization Day is celebrated on November 20 every year. It’s a period when governments and organizations in several African countries consider how to accelerate the continent’s industrialization.

Concept note-3: -It was found that foreign direct investment (FDI), total natural resource (TNR), trade openness (TO), and financial development (FD), were significant determinants of industrialization whereas human capital development and inflation were observed as insignificant determinants of industrialization.

Concept note-4: -The African Union Summit on Industrialization and Economic Diversification aims to highlight Africa’s renewed determination and commitment to Industrialization as one of the central pillars in attaining the continent’s economic growth and development goals as articulated in Agenda 2063 and Agenda 2030.
