How many military reserve troops have been called by Australian PM Morrison to combat raging bush fire?

(A) 1000

(B) 2000

(C) ** 3000

(D) 4000

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -routine fuel reduction burns, including roadside burns, done in accordance with state or territory law requirements. routine maintenance of fence lines, access roads or tracks. routine maintenance of existing fire breaks, fire infrastructure, services and utilities.

Concept note-2: -The Department of Parks and Wildlife (Parks and Wildlife) have a responsibility for managing fires within their jurisdiction. Parks and Wildlife are also responsible for bushfire mitigation on lands within their control.

Concept note-3: -The Australian navy has evacuated around 1, 000 tourists and residents who were trapped in the fire-ravaged town of Mallacoota on the Victoria coast.

Concept note-4: -Australia was well-organised and well-resourced to respond to the recent bushfires and many parts of the country were unaffected. We continue to be a world-leading and safe tourism destination, whether in unaffected regions or those that will recover from these bushfires in the months and years ahead.
