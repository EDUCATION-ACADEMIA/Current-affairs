Which of the following won the first-ever Khelo India Ice Hockey Championship?

(A) ** Ladakh Scouts

(B) ITBP

(C) BSF

(D) CISF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Khelo India Ice Hockey Championship 2020 trophy men’s section was lifted by Ladakh Scout Regimental Centre (LSRC) Red team. They beat ITBP by 3-2 goals at NDS New Ice Hockey Rink on 9 Feb 2020.

Concept note-2: -Hockey Haryana’s women’s team won the Khelo India Youth Games 2022 Women’s Under 18 Qualifiers after defeating Madhya Pradesh 2-0 in the final at Bhubaneswar yesterday, December 30.

Concept note-3: -Indo Tibetan Border Police (ITBP) won the 10th National Ice Hockey Championship trophy in Gulmarg in January 2021.

Concept note-4: -Birth of Hockey in India The first hockey club in India was established in Calcutta in 1885-86, and Bombay and Punjab soon followed suit. The Indian hockey team won its first Olympic gold medal without conceding a single goal in its Olympic debut at the 1928 Amsterdam Games.

Concept note-5: -Beighton Cup is a field hockey tournament organized by Hockey Bengal (formerly the Bengal Hockey Association). Instituted in 1895, it is one of the oldest field hockey tournaments in the world and is held every year in Kolkata.
