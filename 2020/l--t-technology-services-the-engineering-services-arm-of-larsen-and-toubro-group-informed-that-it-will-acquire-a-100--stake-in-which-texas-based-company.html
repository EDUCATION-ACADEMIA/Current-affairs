L & T Technology Services, the engineering services arm of Larsen and Toubro Group, informed that it will acquire a 100 % stake in which Texas-based company?

(A) PCL Construction

(B) ** Orchestra Technology

(C) Bechtel Group Inc

(D) Turner Construction

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Orchestra Technology. L&T Technology Services (LTTS), the engineering services arm of Larsen and Toubro Group, informed that it will acquire a 100 % stake in Texas-based Orchestra Technology(Tech). It is an all-cash deal of USD 25 million (around INR 185 crores).

Concept note-2: -L&T Technology Services Limited (LTTS) is a publicly listed subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Limited providing Engineering and R&D (ER&D) services. LTTS offers consultancy, design, development and testing services across the product and process development life cycle.

Concept note-3: -L&T Technology Services Limited (LTTS) is a listed subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Limited focused on Engineering and R&D (ER&D) services. We offer consultancy, design, development and testing services across the product and process development life cycle.

Concept note-4: -L&T Technology Services Limited (LTTS) is a listed subsidiary of Larsen & Toubro Limited. The Company is a leading global pure-play Engineering Research & Development (ER&D) services company.
