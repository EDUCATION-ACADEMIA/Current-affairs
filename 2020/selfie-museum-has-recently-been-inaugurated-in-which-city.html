Selfie museum has recently been inaugurated in which city?

(A) New York

(B) ** Dubai

(C) Sydney

(D) Brussels

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This Hyderabad Selfie Museum has more than 20 fascinating sets for your selfies.

Concept note-2: -The Original Selfie Museum opened its first location in 2019 in Denver and its second location the following year in Seattle. In 2021, locations opened in Miami, Atlanta and Texas. The studio will be selling tickets for timed entry. During that time, you can go in and take photos with your phone or a DSLR camera.

Concept note-3: -We are the original authors of the “SELFIE MUSEUM” concept nationwide and called our first locations after the names of the cities where we originally opened: Denver Selfie Museum (2019), Seattle Selfie Museum (2020), Miami Selfie Museum (2020), Atlanta Selfie Museum (2021), Texas Selfie Museum (2021).

Concept note-4: -A “selfie museum” or “Instagram museum” is a type of art gallery or installation designed to provide a setting for visitors to pose in photographs to be posted on social media sites such as Instagram.
