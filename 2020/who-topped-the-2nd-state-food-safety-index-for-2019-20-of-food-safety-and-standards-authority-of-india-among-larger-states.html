Who topped the 2nd State Food Safety Index for 2019-20 of Food Safety and Standards Authority of India among larger states?

(A) ** Gujarat

(B) Rajasthan

(C) Haryana

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Gujarat has topped among larger states in terms of food safety index, while Chandigarh tops among UTs.

Concept note-2: -Tamil Nadu topped the State Food Safety Index followed by Gujarat and Maharashtra. Among Smaller States, Goa stood first, followed by Manipur and Sikkim. Among UTs, Jammu and Kashmir, Delhi and Chandigarh secured first, second and third ranks.

Concept note-3: -Among the Large States: Tamil Naduis the top-ranking state, followed by Gujarat and Maharashtra. ii. Among the Small States: Goa is the top-ranking state followed by Manipur and Sikkim. iii.

Concept note-4: -Overall: Tamil Nadu topped the State Food Safety Index followed by Gujarat and Maharashtra.
