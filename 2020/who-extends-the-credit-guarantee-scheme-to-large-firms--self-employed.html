Who Extends the Credit Guarantee Scheme to Large Firms & Self Employed?

(A) State Govrernment

(B) RBI

(C) ** Central Government

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises and Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI), established a Trust named Credit Guarantee Fund Trust for Micro and Small Enterprises (CGTMSE) to implement the Credit Guarantee Fund Scheme for Micro and Small Enterprises.

Concept note-2: -The extent of guarantee cover is 85% for micro enterprises for credit up to 5 lakh. The extent of guarantee cover is 50% of the sanctioned amount of the credit facility for credit from 10 lakh to 100 lakh per MSE borrower for retail trade activity.
