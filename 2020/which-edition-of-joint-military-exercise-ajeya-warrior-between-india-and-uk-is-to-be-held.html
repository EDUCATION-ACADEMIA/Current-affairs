Which edition of joint military exercise “Ajeya Warrior” between India and UK is to be held?

(A) 3rd

(B) 4th

(C) ** 5th

(D) 6th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Ajeya Warrior 2021 is the latest edition that is taking place in October 2021 at Chaubatia, Uttarakhand.

Concept note-2: -Ajeya Warrior Exercise Aims and Objectives Train the army personnel of both nations for counter-terrorism and insurgency operations. To advance strategic ties between India and the United Kingdom. To acquire operation experience and expertise from the bilateral military training campaign.

Concept note-3: -The Royal Air Force of the United Kingdom and the Indian Air Force conduct a joint air force exercise known as Exercise Indradhanush. The exercise aims to improve mutual operational understanding between the two air forces through close interaction. The exercise began in the year 2006 and has since had five editions.

Concept note-4: -The 6th Edition of India–UK Joint Company Level Military Training EXERCISE AJEYA WARRIOR has commenced at Chaubatia, Uttarakhand.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Mongolia.
