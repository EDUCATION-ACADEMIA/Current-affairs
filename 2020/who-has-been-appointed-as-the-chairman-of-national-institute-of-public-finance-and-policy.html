Who has been appointed as the Chairman of National Institute of Public Finance and Policy?

(A) ** Urjit Patel

(B) Amitabh Kant

(C) Raghuram Rajan

(D) Rajiv Gauba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Urjit Patel, Chairman, NIPFP .

Concept note-2: -The National Institute of Public Finance and Policy (NIPFP) is a centre for research in public economics and policies. Founded in 1976, the institute undertakes research, policy advocacy and capacity building in areas related to public economics.

Concept note-3: -The average National Institute of Public Finance & Policy salary ranges from approximately ₹5.4 Lakhs per year for a Research Associate to ₹6.2 Lakhs per year for a Research Fellow.

Concept note-4: -Public finance relates to the income and expenditure of the government.

Concept note-5: -NIPFP invites applications in the prescribed format from qualified and experienced candidates for one regular post of Senior Library and Information Officer [in the Level 12, Cell 1, Rs. 78, 800] to be filled initially for a period of two years on contractual basis through direct recruitment.
