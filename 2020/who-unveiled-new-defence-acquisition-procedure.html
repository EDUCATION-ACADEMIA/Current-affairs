Who unveiled new Defence Acquisition Procedure?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) ** Rajnath Singh

(C) Nitin Gadkari

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DAC is the highest decision-making body in the Defence Ministry for deciding on new policies and capital acquisitions for the three services – Army, Navy and Air Force, and the Indian Coast Guard. The Defence Acquisition Council is headed by the Defence Minister.

Concept note-2: -What is DAP 2020? It enables the notification of a List of Weapons or Platforms that will be banned for import. It focuses on Foreign Direct Investment (FDI) in defence manufacturing and indigenization of the manufacturing prices.

Concept note-3: -The Correct Answer is Defence Minister. The Defence Acquisition Council is the highest decision-making body in the Defence Ministry. It decides on new policies and capital acquisitions for the three services (Army, Navy, and Air Force) and the Indian Coast Guard. The Council is headed by the Defence Minister.

Concept note-4: -What is the role of the Defence Acquisition Council? Pursuant to the recommendations made by the group of ministers on reforming the national security system in February 2001, a separate, dedicated structure for defence procurement was set up.
