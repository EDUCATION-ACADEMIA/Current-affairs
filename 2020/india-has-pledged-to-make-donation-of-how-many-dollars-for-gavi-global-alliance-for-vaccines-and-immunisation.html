India has pledged to make donation of how many dollars for GAVI-Global Alliance for Vaccines and Immunisation?

(A) USD 10 million

(B) USD 25 million

(C) USD 20 million

(D) ** USD 15 million

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Three-quarters (76%) of Gavi’s funding comes from direct donations provided by donor governments and private organizations and individuals. The top three government donors were the United Kingdom, the U.S., and Norway, while the largest private donor (and largest donor overall) was the Gates Foundation.

Concept note-2: -Background: The Global Alliance for Vaccines and Immunisation (GAVI) was created in 1999 to enable even the poorest countries to provide vaccines to all children. We aimed to assess the effect of GAVI on combined diphtheria, tetanus, and pertussis vaccine (DTP3) coverage.

Concept note-3: -About our Alliance As part of its mission to save lives and protect people’s health by increasing equitable and sustainable use of vaccines, Gavi has helped vaccinate more than 981 million children in the world’s poorest countries, preventing more than 16.2 million future deaths.

Concept note-4: -Narendra Modi serving as the 14th and current prime minister of India since 2014.
