Who is to assume charge as new Northern Command chief?

(A) Satish Dua

(B) ** YK Joshi

(C) DeependraHooda

(D) Nathu Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Lieutenant General Upendra Dwivedi.

Concept note-2: -Its present commander is Lieutenant General Upendra Dwivedi.

Concept note-3: -Lieutenant General Upendra Dwivedi AVSM is the present General Officer-Commanding-in-Chief (GOC-in-C) Northern Command of the Indian Army. He assumed the post upon the superannuation of Lieutenant General Yogesh Kumar Joshi. Previously he served as Deputy Chief of Army Staff and GOC of IX Corps of Indian Army.

Concept note-4: -"On the LAC, our response to Chinese attempts to unilaterally change the status quo was a swift, undaunted and synergised action by the Indian Armed Forces, ” General officer Commanding in Chief of Army’s Northern Command Lt General Upendra Dwivedi said at an investiture ceremony at Badami Bagh Cantonment area here.

Concept note-5: -The current VCOAS is Lt. General B. S. Raju who assumed the office on 1 May 2022.
