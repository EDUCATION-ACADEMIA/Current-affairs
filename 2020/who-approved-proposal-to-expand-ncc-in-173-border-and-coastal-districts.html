(A) Narendra modi

(B) Smriti Irani

(C) ** Rajnath Singh

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Defence Minister Rajnath Singh has approved the proposal to expand the National Cadet Corps (NCC) in 173 border and coastal districts from where one lakh new cadets will be inducted, an official statement said on Sunday.

Concept note-2: -The Bill received the assent of the Governor General on 16 Apr 1948, and the National Cadet Corps came into being by an Act of the Parliament Act No. XXXI of 1948 designated ‘The National Cadet Corps Act 1948’. This Act with 13 clauses, prescribed the formation of the National Cadet Corps in India.
