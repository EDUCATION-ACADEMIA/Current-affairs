Athletics Coach Purushotham Rai died a day before receiving which award?

(A) Arjuna Award

(B) Padmashri

(C) ** Dronacharya Award

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Veteran athletics coach Purushotham Rai died due to a cardiac arrest on Friday, tragically a day before he was to be conferred with the prestigious Dronacharya Award . He was 79.

Concept note-2: -Dronacharya Award 2022 Winners: President Draupadi Murmu honored Raj Singh, Jeevanjot Singh Teja Mohammad Ali Qamar, Suma Siddharth Shirur, Sujit Mann, Dinesh Jawahar Lad, Bimal Prafulla Ghosh with the Dronacharya Award at the National Sports Awards 2022.

Concept note-3: -In India, the Dronacharya Award is given to coaches as recognition for their contribution to a particular sport or for their role in an athlete’s rise to the top. It is also given to motivate coaches to dedicate themselves to raising the performance of their athletes to international standards.

Concept note-4: -Raghunandan Gokhale (1986) Raghunandan Gokhale received the Dronacharya award for Chess at the young age of 32.
