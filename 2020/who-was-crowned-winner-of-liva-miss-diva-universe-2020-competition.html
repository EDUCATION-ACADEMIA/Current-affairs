Who was crowned winner of LIVA Miss Diva Universe 2020 competition?

(A) Anukreethy Vas

(B) ** Adline Castelino

(C) Suman Rao

(D) Vartika Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Adline Castelino from Mangalore was crowned LIVA Miss Diva Universe 2020. Aavriti Choudhary from Jabalpur clinched LIVA Miss Diva Supranational 2020 title and Neha Jaiswal from Pune was crowned LIVA Miss Diva 2020 - Runner – Up during the grand finale.

Concept note-2: -Karnataka’s Divita Rai was crowned as the LIVA Miss Diva Universe 2022 in a glittering ceremony in Mumbai on August 29, 2022. The model will now represent India at Miss Universe, the global pageant.

Concept note-3: -Miss Diva 2020 was the 8th edition of the Miss Diva beauty pageant held on February 22, 2020. A total of 20 contestants were shortlisted from various auditions across the country. Vartika Singh crowned Adline Castelino as her successor and she represented India at Miss Universe 2020.

Concept note-4: -Divita Rai, 23, is from Karnataka. She won the title of Miss Diva Universe 2022 on August 28, 2022 and was crowned by Harnaaz Sandhu, Miss Universe 2021. Rai was the second runner-up at Miss Diva Universe in 2021.

Concept note-5: -Karnataka’s Divita Rai has been crowned LIVA Miss Diva Universe 2022. She will be representing India in the Miss Universe 2022 pageant. Miss Universe 2021 Harnaaz Sandu was present at the event to crown the new LIVA Miss Diva 2022. Pragnya Ayyagari of Telangana was declared LIVA Miss Diva Supranational 2022.
