What is the name of the mobile app launched by Harsh Vardhan, Union Minister of Earth Sciences in the event of14th Foundation day of the Ministry of Earth Sciences(MoES)?

(A) DHARTI

(B) ** MAUSAM

(C) WEATHER

(D) UMANG

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harsh Vardhan has launched a new mobile application called “Meghdoot” in New Delhi, developed by experts from the India Meteorological Department (IMD) and Indian Institute of Tropical Meteorology(IITM) and the Indian Council of Agricultural Research(ICAR).

Concept note-2: -Recently, the Ministry of Earth Sciences has launched the Deep Ocean Mission (DOM). DOM is a mission mode project to support the Blue Economy Initiatives of the Government of India.

Concept note-3: -MAUSAM is a Mobile App of the India Meteorological Department (IMD), Ministry of Earth Sciences (MoES), Government of India to provide seamless and user-friendly access to weather products available on https://mausam.imd.gov.in/.

Concept note-4: -India Meteorological Department was established in 1875. It is the National Meteorological Service of the country and the principal government agency in all matters relating to meteorology and allied subjects.
