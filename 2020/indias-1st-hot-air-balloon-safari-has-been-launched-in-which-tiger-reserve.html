India’s 1st hot air balloon safari has been launched in which tiger reserve?

(A) Mudumalai Tiger Reserve

(B) Anamalai Tiger Reserve

(C) ** Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve

(D) Mukundara Hills Tiger Reserve

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s First Hot Air Balloon Safari Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve, Madhya Pradesh is the first in India to launch The Hot Air Balloon Safari for tourists.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve. In Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve the first hot air balloon wildlife safari launched in India in December 2020.

Concept note-3: -Madhya Pradesh state government is planning to introduce the hot air balloon service in its other tiger reserves as well including Kanha, Panna and Pench tiger reserves. The Balloon Safari will be operated by SkyWaltz, which is India’s first fully licensed and government approved commercial hot Air Balloon operator.

Concept note-4: -India’s first hot air balloon wildlife safari in a tiger reserve was launched recently in Madhya Pradesh’s famous Bandhavgarh Tiger Reserve. It was launched by Madhya Pradesh Forest Minister Vijay Shah.
