Which state’s Hornbill Festival to be celebrated virtually?

(A) Meghalaya

(B) ** Nagaland

(C) Goa

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hornbill Festival is an annual festival of Nagaland. The vibrant festival takes place mainly in the first week of December every year. The Hornbill Festival will start on 1st December and continue until 10th December 2022. The main venue of the Hornbill Festiva 2022 is Kohima, the capital of Nagaland.

Concept note-2: -The Festival is celebrated in Nagaland to encourage inter-tribal interaction and to preserve, protect and revive the uniqueness and richness of the Naga heritage. It is organized by State Tourism and Art & Culture Departments and also supported by Union Government.

Concept note-3: -Novaturient Managements presented Hornbill Fashion Night under the theme ‘Lieyie, fall/ winter 2022’ at The Heritage, Kohima on Tuesday. The word “Lieyie” is derived from the Angami Dialect meaning harvesting accolades. It is an expression used in garnering acknowledgement of merit.
