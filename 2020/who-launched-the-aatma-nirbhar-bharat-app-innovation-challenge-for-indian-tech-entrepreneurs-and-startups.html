Who launched the ‘Aatma Nirbhar Bharat App Innovation Challenge’ for Indian tech entrepreneurs and startups?

(A) ** Narendra Modi

(B) Nitin Jairam Gadkari

(C) Raj Nath Singh

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Days after the government banned 59 Chinese apps, the Ministry of Electronics & Information Technology (MeitY) in partnership with Atal Innovation Mission - Niti Aayog has launched Digital India Atma Nirbhar Bharat App Innovation Challenge for Indian tech entrepreneurs and start-ups.

Concept note-2: -In the 1990s, Prime Minister P. V. Narasimha Rao redefined and adapted the meaning of self-reliance for the country compared with the Nehru era.

Concept note-3: -This massive market holds a plethora of opportunities for Indian innovators and tech gurus. Under the vision of Aatmanirbhar Bharat, it is the aim of Government to make Indians one of the major “uploaders” of apps, apart from “downloaders".

Concept note-4: -Finance Minister further announces Government Reforms and Enablers across Seven Sectors under Aatmanirbhar Bharat Abhiyaan. The government took several bold reforms such as Supply Chain Reforms for Agriculture, Rational Tax Systems, Simple & Clear Laws, Capable Human Resource and Strong Financial System.

Concept note-5: -They were then presented to the public through a live Webcast and with the help of public voting at the MyGov platform, 9 Apps were selected as the best of the best. In the ‘Business’ category, ‘Zoho’ was declared the winner.
