The Mediterranean Sea is connected to Atlantic Ocean through which strait?

(A) Bering Strait

(B) ** Strait of Gibraltar

(C) Strait of Hormuz

(D) Malacca strait

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -11, 2020) — The Strait of Gibraltar connects the Atlantic Ocean with the Mediterranean Sea and separates Spain on the European continent from Morocco on the African continent.

Concept note-2: -1] The Strait of Gibraltaris a narrow strait that connects the Atlantic Ocean to the Mediterranean Sea.

Concept note-3: -The Strait of Gibraltar connects the Mediterranean Sea with the Atlantic Ocean and separates southernmost Spain from northernmost Africa. The channel is 58 km long and narrows to 13 km in width between Point Marroquí (Spain) and Point Cires (Morocco).

Concept note-4: -To the north the Bering Sea connects with the Arctic Ocean through the Bering Strait, at the narrowest point of which the two continents are about 53 miles (85 kilometres) apart. The boundary between the United States and Russia passes through the sea and the strait.
