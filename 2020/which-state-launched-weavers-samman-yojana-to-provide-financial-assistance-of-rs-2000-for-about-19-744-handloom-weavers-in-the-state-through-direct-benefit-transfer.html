Which State launched “Weavers Samman Yojana” to provide financial assistance of Rs 2000 for about 19, 744 handloom weavers in the state, through Direct Benefit Transfer?

(A) Madhya Pradesh

(B) Jharkhand

(C) ** Karnataka

(D) Uttar Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What is Karnataka Nekar Samman Yojana 2023? The Nekar Samman Scheme was launched on 6th July 2023 by the Karnataka Chief Minister BS Yeddyurappa for the weaver’s community. In the pandemic of COVID-19, to help the handloom weavers, the CM announced this scheme to support them by providing one-time financial aid.

Concept note-2: -Benefits Of Nekar Samman Yojana 19, 744 handloom weavers in the state will get annual financial assistance of two thousand rupees through Direct Benefit Transfer. The State Government has allocated 10.96 crore rupees under the programme.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Jeevan Jyoti Bima Yojana (PMJJBY), Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana (PMSBY), and Converged Mahatma Gandhi Bunkar Bima Yojana (MGBBY), Handloom Weavers Comprehensive Welfare Scheme (HWCWS) offers life, accidental, and disability insurance coverage.

Concept note-4: -Nekar Samman Scheme. A financial assistance has been increased from Rs. 2, 000/- to Rs. 5, 000/- for the financial year 2022-23 assistance will be provided for all working Handloom & allied weavers annually through Aadhar based DBT payment mode to each Handloom weaver beneficiary.
