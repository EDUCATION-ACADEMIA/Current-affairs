Who delivered its 7th consecutive speech on 74th Independence Day?

(A) Amit Shah

(B) ** Narendra Modi

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) Smriti Irani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Independence Day Speech: PM Modi delivered his seventh consecutive address from Red Fort . New Delhi: Prime Minister Narendra Modi today addressed the nation from the majestic Red Fort on the occasion of India’s 74th Independence Day.

Concept note-2: -The Independence day celebrations start with the Prime Minister of India hoisting the national flag on the Red Fort in Delhi. This is followed by a 21-gun salute and the singing of the national anthem. As a part of the celebrations, the PM delivers an annual speech which is called ‘Address to the Nation’.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi to address the Nation after unfurling the National Flag at Red Fort in Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Compliments to my dear countrymen on the momentous occasion of completion of 75 years of independence. Many congratulations to all!
