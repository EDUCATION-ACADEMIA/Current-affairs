The Central Board of Direct Taxes signed a MOU with which organisation for the data exchange between the two organisations?

(A) NASSCOM

(B) PFRDA

(C) IRDAI

(D) ** SEBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A formal Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) was signed today between the Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) and Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs (CBIC), Ministry Of Finance for data exchange between the two organizations.

Concept note-2: -CBIC is a statutory body established under the Central Boards of Revenue Act, 1963. CBIC was formed in 1964 when the Central Board of Revenue was split into the Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) and the Central Board of Excise and Customs, which was renamed the Central Board of Indirect Taxes and Customs in 2018.
