What is the name of the first mobile app of National Anti-Doping Agency?

(A) HEALTHY INDIA

(B) ** NADA INDIA

(C) INDIA ANDOP

(D) INDIA NADA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 30 June, 2020 the first mobile app of National Anti-Doping Agency (NADA), “NADA INDIA” was released by Sports Minister Kiren Rijiju. The initiative is an important step towards dope free sports, providing updates & information about the banned substances and their use to aware the athletes & Coaches.

Concept note-2: -NADA developing App to assist athletes verify medicines with prohibited substances.

Concept note-3: -The World Anti-Doping Agency (WADA) was established on 10 November 1999. Its mission is to promote and coordinate the fight against doping in sport internationally.
