Which edition of Odisha Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) International Trade Fair 2020 recently started in Bhubaneshwar?

(A) 6th

(B) 7th

(C) ** 8th

(D) 9th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bhubaneswar, Jan 6 (KNN) The 8th Odisha Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises (MSME) International Trade Fair themed ‘Focus MSME 2020’ will begin at the Exhibition Ground in Odisha capital from January 8.

Concept note-2: -The objectives of the policy are to: Facilitate scaling up of women SHG enterprises. Enable growth of Odisha’s focus sectors. Provide easier access to domestic and international markets. Make concerted efforts to encourage MSMEs to adopt and practice green initiatives, sustainability and waste management.

Concept note-3: -Odisha Assembly of Small and Medium Enterprises (OASME) was established on 12th August, 1985 with the concept of providing common platfrom for Cottage, Handicraft, Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises of the State of Odisha.

Concept note-4: -At present, Odisha is having 4.5 lakh registered MSMEs providing 16.9 lakh employment.

Concept note-5: -Bhubaneswar, Jan 16 (KNN) The Odisha Minister for Industries, MSME and Power Pratap Keshari Deb on Sunday said that startups are the only gateway through which state can make self-made entrepreneurs and can become the Industrial hub of the country in next five to seven years.
