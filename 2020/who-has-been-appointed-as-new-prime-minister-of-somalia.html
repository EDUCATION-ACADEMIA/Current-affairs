(A) ** Mohamed Hussein Roble

(B) Farmaajo

(C) Abdirahman Abdi Mohamed.

(D) Mohamed Hassan Adam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In June 2022, Hamza Abdi Barre succeeded the 20th Mohamed Hussein Roble as the 21st Prime Minister.

Concept note-2: -Hassan Sheikh Mohamud was declared president in a peaceful transition of power after incumbent President Mohamed conceded defeat and congratulated the victor.

Concept note-3: -The Council of Ministers of the Federal Government of Somalia consists of Ministers appointed by the Prime Minister. The Prime Minister may dismiss members of the council, and new appointees must again be approved by the Parliament. The Council meets weekly on Thursdays in Mogadishu.
