The Prime Minister Narendra Modi has released a special postal stamp to mark the centenary celebrations of which University?

(A) Indian Institute of Technology Madras

(B) ** Aligarh Muslim University

(C) Amity University

(D) Banaras Hindu University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The First Stamp of Independent India was issued on 21 November 1947. It depicts the Indian Flag with the patriotsâ€™ slogan, Jai Hind (Long Live India), on the top right hand corner. It was valued at three and one-half annas.

Concept note-2: -Indian Postal System was developed under the governor-generalship of Lord Dalhousie. He served as the Governor General of India from 1848 to 1856. The postal stamps were used in 1854 for the first time.

Concept note-3: -The first postal stamp in India was introduced on 1 July 1852 in the Scinde district. In 1854 the introduction of uniform postage rates led to the development of the first postage stamps valid for use throughout India.

Concept note-4: -A postage stamp is evidence of pre-paying a fee for postal services. Usually a small paper rectangle that is attached to an envelope, the postage stamp signifies that the person sending the letter or package may have either fully, or perhaps partly, pre-paid for delivery.
