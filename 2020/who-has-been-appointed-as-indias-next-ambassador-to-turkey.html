Who has been appointed as India’s next Ambassador to Turkey?

(A) ** Sanjay Kumar Panda

(B) Saurabh Kumar

(C) Sanjeev Kumar Singla

(D) Birender Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ambassador of India to Türkiye Virander Paul joined the Indian Foreign Service in 1991.

Concept note-2: -The President approves the appointments of Ambassadors/High Commissioners based on the recommendations of the Prime Minister and the External Affairs Minister and the appointment is also subject to the agre‘ment of the receiving State.

Concept note-3: -Sanjay Panda, 56 years, is an Indian diplomat, a member of the Indian Foreign Service (IFS). He was the Indian High Commissioner to Seychelles till November 2016 before returning to India and taking over as Joint Secretary (Indian Ocean Region) in the Ministry of External Affairs, New Delhi.
