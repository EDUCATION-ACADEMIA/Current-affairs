Andhra Pradesh’s first Disha police station was recently inaugurated in which city?

(A) Nellore

(B) Guntur

(C) Kakinada

(D) ** Rajamahendravaram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Minister Y.S. Jagan Mohan Reddy at the inauguration of the first Disha police station in Rajamahendravaram on Saturday. Chief Minister Y.S.

Concept note-2: -Jaganmohan Reddy launched the first Disha Police Station in the State in Rajamahendravaram on Saturday. Disha Police Stations are model stations aimed at rendering speedy justice for female victims of Sexual assault (rape), harassment and Protection of Children from Sexual Offences (POCSO) Act, 2012.

Concept note-3: -Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister Y S Jagan Mohan Reddy on Saturday inaugurated the first ‘Disha Police Station’ in Rajamahendravaram city in East Godavari district to exclusively deal with crime against women and children.

Concept note-4: -Disha app, developed by Andhra Pradesh government to provide quick response of women’s safety issues, was launched in February 2020 and has been downloaded more than 1.30 crore times.

Concept note-5: -Chief minister YS Jaganmohan Reddy inaugurates Disha Police station in Rajahmundry on Saturday.
