AAI signed a MoU with which PSU to collaborate and support the emerging global opportunities in the airport business, including those in the Asia Pacific region?

(A) BHEL

(B) GAIL

(C) ONGC

(D) ** BEL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AAI signs MoU with Sweden to facilitate smart and sustainable aviation technology collaboration.

Concept note-2: -The Airports Authority of India, or AAI, is a public sector enterprise under the ownership of the Ministry of Civil Aviation, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The Airports Authority of India was established in 1995 under the Ministry of Civil Aviation by the Airports Authority of India Act, 1994. As a result, it is a statutory entity. The AAI is responsible for the development, enhancement, maintenance, and management of India’s civil aviation infrastructure.

Concept note-4: -Airports Authority of India (AAI) was constituted by an Act of Parliament and came into being on 1st April 1995 by merging erstwhile National Airports Authority and International Airports Authority of India.

Concept note-5: -(2) It shall be the duty of the Authority to provide air traffic service and air transport service at any airport and civil enclaves. establishment of private airports by rendering such technical, financial or other assistance which the Central Government may consider necessary for such purpose”.
