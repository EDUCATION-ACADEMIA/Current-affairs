The government of India has approved how much fund for the implementation of JJM in the Maharashtra during 2020-21?

(A) ** Rs.1, 828.92 crore

(B) Rs.1, 328.92 crore

(C) Rs.1, 228.92 crore

(D) Rs.1, 728.92 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The total outlay for Jal Jeevan Mission is ₹ 3.60 lakh crore. The state-wise allocation of funds for 2019-20, 2020-21 and 2021-22 is placed at Annexure. This Information was given by the Minister of State for Jal Shakti, Shri Prahlad Singh Patel in a written reply in Lok Sabha today.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 90:10.

Concept note-3: -The coverage of Har Ghar, Nal Se Jal under Jal Jeevan Mission (JJM) will increase by 3.8 crore households in 2022-23. Rs 60, 000 crore will be allocated towards this mission for this year. Implementation of the Ken-Betwa Link Project, at an estimated cost of Rs 44, 605 crore will be taken up.

Concept note-4: -The budget under Jal Jeevan Mission for 2022-23 has been enhanced to Rs. 60, 000 Crore by the Central government, thereby making it evident, the importance of ‘Har Ghar Jal’ programme.
