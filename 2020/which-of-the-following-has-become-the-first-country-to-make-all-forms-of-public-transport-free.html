Which of the following has become the first country to make all forms of public transport free?

(A) Monaco

(B) Liechtenstein

(C) ** Luxembourg

(D) Andorra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Luxembourg is first country in world to make public transport free | Euronews.

Concept note-2: -Luxembourg was the first country to offer free public transport (trams, trains, and buses).

Concept note-3: -The first form of public transport was multiple people riding animals. Animal-drawn ferries are thought to be the earliest form of public transit. The wheel was invented in 3, 500 BC but it wasn’t until 1, 600 BC that it was used for a chariot. This is when the idea of longer distance travel was possible by road.

Concept note-4: -29, 2020, it became the first nation in the world to make all public transit entirely free at point of use. With the exception of ( not especially popular) first-class tickets, no one has paid a cent to ride a bus, tram or train within Luxembourg’s borders ever since.
