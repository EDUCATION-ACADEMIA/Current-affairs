Who is the author of The book “Delhi Riots 2020:The Untold Story”?

(A) Chetan Bhagat

(B) ** Monika Arora

(C) Sahshi Tharoor

(D) Mahadevi Verma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi Riots 2020 is authored by Monika Arora, Sonali Chitalkar, and Prerna Malhotra. Arora is a Delhi-based lawyer practicing in the Supreme Court of India. She is a standing counsel for the Government of India, in the Delhi High Court. She is former DUSU President from ABVP, during 1992–1993.

Concept note-2: -Delhi riots broke out on 23 February 2020, leaving 53 dead and nearly 600 injured. Police say 755 FIRs were lodged and 1, 818 people arrested.

Concept note-3: -The Jyoti Nagar police station had filed a charge sheet against the accused under various provisions of the Indian Penal Code (IPC), including those related to rioting, armed with a deadly weapon, unlawful assembly, and mischief by fire or explosive substance with an intent to destroy houses, etc.
