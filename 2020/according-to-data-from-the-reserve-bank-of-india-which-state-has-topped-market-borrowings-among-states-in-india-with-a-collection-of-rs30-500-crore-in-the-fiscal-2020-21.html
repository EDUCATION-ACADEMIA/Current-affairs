According to data from the Reserve Bank of India, which State has topped market borrowings among States in India with a collection of Rs.30, 500 crore in the fiscal 2020-21?

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Haryana

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Answer. According to the Reserve Bank of India, Tamil Nadu led the country in market borrowings during 2020-21.

Concept note-2: -Tamil Nadu had an outstanding liability of about ₹4.73 lakh crore at the end of March 2022 on funds raised through the issue of bonds. About 32.4%, or about ₹1.53 lakh crore, of the outstanding amounts are coming up for repayment by 2026-27.

Concept note-3: -States can’t borrow beyond the annual limits set by the central government under Article 293(3) of the Constitution. But states do not need prior consent from the Centre to guarantee the loans and advances, and bonds issued by its entities.
