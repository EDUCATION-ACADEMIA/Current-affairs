BRO (Border Roads Organization) opens Rohtang Pass of Himachal Pradesh. It connects which of the following valley with the Lahaul and Spiti Valleys of Himachal Pradesh?

(A) Barot Valley

(B) Parvati Valley

(C) ** Kullu Valley

(D) Kangra Valley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It connects the Kullu Valley with the Lahaul and Spiti Valleys of Himachal Pradesh, India.

Concept note-2: -Kunzum Pass (Tibetan: Kunzum La, elev. 4, 551 m or 14, 931 ft), is a high mountain pass in the eastern Kunzum Range of the Himalayas. It connects Lahaul valley and Spiti valley. It is on the route from Gramphoo in Lahaul to Kaza the subdivisional headquarters of Spiti.

Concept note-3: -Parang la connects the Spiti valley to parts of Ladakh. On the Spitian side of this pass, flows the river Parilungbi and the track runs on its side, climbing up the pass towards Ladakh.

Concept note-4: -Rohtang Pass (el. 13, 051 ft (3, 978 m) is a high mountain pass 51 km from Manali that connects the Kullu Valley with the Lahul and Spiti valleys of Himachal Pradesh, Republic of India. The pass is open only from May to November.

Concept note-5: -Many valleys are situated in the Himachal range. Among them Kashmir valley (in jammu and Kashmir), Kangra and Kullu valleys (in Himachal Pradesh) are some of the examples.
