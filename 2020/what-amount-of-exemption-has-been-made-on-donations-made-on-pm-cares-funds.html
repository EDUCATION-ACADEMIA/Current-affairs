What amount of exemption has been made on donations made on PM Cares Funds?

(A) ** 100%

(B) 10%

(C) 50%

(D) 75%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Donation to PMCARES Fund is eligible for 100% tax exemption u/s 80G.

Concept note-2: -All the donations made to the PM CARES Fund will be eligible for 100% tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, 1961. It should be noted that no upper limit will be applicable for tax deduction u/s 80G with respect to the donations made towards this Fund.

Concept note-3: -Yes, the fund is exempted from paying Income Tax as per Section 10(23)(c) of Income Tax Act, 1961.

Concept note-4: -Open Print receipt menu on PMCARES website (https://www.pmcares.gov.in/) Click on NEFT/RTGS/IMPS (Draft, Cheque, Cash) Logo on Print Receipt page: Fill all the required details in the form: Enter UTR/ DD/ Cheque/ Bank Reference Number*.

Concept note-5: -Tax deduction – PM Cares fund 80G details You will be delighted to know that the amount that you donate towards the PM Cares fund can be claimed for tax deduction. This tax deduction will fall under the section 80G, of the Income Tax of 1961. The Government has paved the way for a 100% tax deduction under this scheme.
