Ajeya Warrior 2020, a joint military exercise was recently concluded between India and which country?

(A) USA

(B) Malaysia

(C) ** UK

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -How was the Ajeya Exercise carried out? The exercise will comprise soldiers each from the Indian and United Kingdom Army who would be sharing their experiences gained during the conduct of various counter-insurgency and counter-terrorist operations in the past.

Concept note-2: -LONDON: Indian and British armed forces on Thursday began their joint ‘Ajeya Warrior’ exercises in the Salisbury Plain of central southern England to train troops in counter insurgency and counter terrorist operations.

Concept note-3: -India, Kazakhstan hold joint military exercise in Meghalaya | North East India News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-4: -Exercise Ajeya Warrior is a joint exercise conducted by the armed forces of India and the United Kingdom. The fifth edition of India-UK joint military exercise ‘Ajeya Warrior’ was conducted at Salisbury Plains, the United Kingdom from 13th-26th February 2020.

Concept note-5: -The 6th Edition of India–UK Joint Company Level Military Training EXERCISE AJEYA WARRIOR has commenced at Chaubatia, Uttarakhand.
