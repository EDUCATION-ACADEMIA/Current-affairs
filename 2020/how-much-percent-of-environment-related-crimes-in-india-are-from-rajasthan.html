How much percent of Environment Related Crimes in India are from Rajasthan?

(A) 20%

(B) 30%

(C) ** 40%

(D) 50%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A total of 40.59 per cent of the crimes related to environment in the country were committed in Rajasthan during 2014-16, according to a report by the Comptroller and Auditor General (CAG) .
