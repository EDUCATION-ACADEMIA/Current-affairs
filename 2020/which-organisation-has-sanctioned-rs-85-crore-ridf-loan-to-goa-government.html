Which organisation has sanctioned Rs 85 crore RIDF loan to Goa government?

(A) ** NABARD

(B) SEBI

(C) SIDBI

(D) MANREGA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NABARD has sanctioned loans worth INR 85.04 crore under Rural Infrastructure Development Fund (RIDF) to the Government of Goa for various Social Infrastructure Projects in the State.

Concept note-2: -3.54 The RIDF was set up by the Government in 1995-96 for financing ongoing rural Infrastructure projects. The Fund is maintained by the National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development (NABARD).

Concept note-3: -Objectives of RIDF NABARD also gives financial level assistance to State Governments from RIDF for more than 30 activities. Some commercial banks also help in funding to a certain period of time.

Concept note-4: -RIDF Tranche I was placed with NABARD during 1995-96 with an allocation of Rs. 2, 000 crore. The annual corpus has now reached to Rs. 40, 001.98 crores under RIDF XXVIII (2022-23).
