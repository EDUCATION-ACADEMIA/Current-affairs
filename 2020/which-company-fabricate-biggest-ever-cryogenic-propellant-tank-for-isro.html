Which company fabricate biggest ever cryogenic propellant tank for ISRO?

(A) Madras Radiators and Pressings Ltd

(B) ** Hindustan Aeronautics Limited

(C) Belmaks Ltd

(D) Larsen and Toubro

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HAL has delivered critical structures, tankages, satellite structures for the PSLV, GSLV-Mk IIand GSLV-Mk III launch vehicles .

Concept note-2: -State-run aerospace behemoth Hindustan Aeronautics Ltd (HAL) on Thursday said the “heaviest” Semi-Cryogenic propellant tank (SC120- LOX) ever fabricated by it has been delivered to Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Concept note-3: -The heaviest semi cryogenic propellant tank (SC120 LOX) ever fabricated by Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) has been delivered to the Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO).

Concept note-4: -The Hindustan Aeronautics Limited (HAL) has delivered the biggest cryogenic propellant tank (C32 LH2) ever formed by the company to the Indian Space Research Organisation (Isro).

Concept note-5: -ISRO’s Cryogenic Upper Stage Project (CUSP) envisaged the design and development of the indigenous Cryogenic Upper Stage to replace the stage procured from Russia and used in GSLV flights. The main engine and two smaller steering engines of CUS together develop a nominal thrust of 73.55 kN in vacuum.
