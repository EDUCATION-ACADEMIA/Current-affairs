Keshubhai Patel passed away, he was the CM of which state?

(A) Goa

(B) ** Gujarat

(C) Odisha

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Patel resigned as the chief minister on 2 October 2001 due to poor health.

Concept note-2: -Balwantrai Mehta (19 February 1899 –19 September 1965) was an Indian politician who served as the second Chief Minister of Gujarat state, India.

Concept note-3: -The state of Gujarat was created on 1 May 1960, composed of the Gujarati-speaking districts of Bombay State following the Mahagujarat Movement. Jivraj Narayan Mehta of the INC was the inaugural chief minister.

Concept note-4: -Shri Bhupendra Rajnikant Patel was sworn-in as Gujarat’s 17th Chief Minister on December 12, 2022. Shri Bhupendrabhai Patel began his political journey as a member in Memnagar municipality and got elected as MLA from Ghatlodia constituency (Ahmedabad) in 2017 Gujarat assembly elections.
