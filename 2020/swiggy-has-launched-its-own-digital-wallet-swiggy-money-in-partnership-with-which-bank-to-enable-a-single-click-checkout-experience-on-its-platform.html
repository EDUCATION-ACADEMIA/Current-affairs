Swiggy has launched its own digital wallet, ‘Swiggy Money’ in partnership with which Bank to enable a ‘single-click checkout experience’ on its platform?

(A) Axis Bank

(B) ** ICICI Bank

(C) DCB Bank

(D) Federal Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We had also introduced UPI-led instant one-click payment for millions of Swiggy’s customers, ” informed Bijith Bhaskar, Head- Digital Channels & Partnership, ICICI Bank.

Concept note-2: -’Swiggy Money’ is powered by ICICI Bank’s ‘insta wallet service’, which is built on a cloud platform with API integration.

Concept note-3: -Press the Virtual Chat Assistant icon present at the bottom right side of the screen. Choose the option “I can’t find my voucher.” Enter the registered mobile number used to purchase the Swiggy Money Voucher Code. From the listed brands, select ‘Swiggy Money’

Concept note-4: -“Swiggy Money Codes” shall mean digital codes issued by ICICI which can be redeemed by its holder on prepared food and beverages, genie and instamart orders ordered on the Platform through the Digital Wallet.

Concept note-5: -You can activate Swiggy Money by going to your account from the Swiggy app and then tap the Swiggy Money option. You’ll then see an Activate Swiggy Money button that you should press after agreeing to the terms and conditions of the service.
