The new helicopter service has been started in which state under UDAN scheme and is to be operated by Pawan Hans Limited?

(A) ** Uttarakhand

(B) Haryana

(C) Bihar

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pawan Hans Ltd. commenced its helicopter services on six routes in the state of Assam on 8th February, 2023. These new helicopter services launched under the RCS UDAN scheme will provide connectivity on the “Dibrugarh-Jorhat-Tezpur-Guwahati-Tezpur-Jorhat-Dibrugarh” network.

Concept note-2: -1. ONGC:- Pawan Hans has been providing Helicopter Transport services for offshore operation of ONGC for carrying its men and vital supplies round the clock to drilling rigs situated in Bombay off-shore platforms.

Concept note-3: -Pawan Hans Limited is a premier Central Public Sector Enterprise and India’s largest Helicopter Company under the administrative control of Ministry of Civil Aviation, Government of India.

Concept note-4: -It helps them reach offshore sites and inaccessible areas of the north-eastern states and interior parts of Jammu and Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, and Uttarakhand.

Concept note-5: -The national helicopter company of India was incorporated in 1985. Pawan Hans was incorporated with the primary objective of providing helicopter support services to the oil sector for its off-shore exploration operations, services in remote and hilly areas and charter services for promotion of tourism.
