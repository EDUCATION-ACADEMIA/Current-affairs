Which organization has successfully conducted final trial of Nag anti-tank guided missile?

(A) ** DRDO

(B) ISRO

(C) NASA

(D) USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Minister Rajnath Singh congratulated the DRDO and the Indian Army for the successful trial of NAG missile .

Concept note-2: -Understanding ATGM Indigenously-developed Laser-Guided Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM) employs a tandem High Explosive Anti-Tank (HEAT) warhead to defeat Explosive Reactive Armour (ERA) protected armoured vehicles.

Concept note-3: -India has successfully flight-tested Helina, an Anti-Tank Guided Missile (ATGM), in Pokhran. According to the Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO), it is one of the most advanced Anti-Tank Weapons in the world.

Concept note-4: -The Nag missile is a Indian third-generation, all-weather, fire-and-forget, lock-on after launch, top-attack, anti-tank guided missile with an operational range of 500 metres to 7000 metres km with the potential of being developed in future versions to 20, 000 metres.
