At which place the union minister for tribal affairs has inaugurated Two Tribes India showrooms?

(A) ** Kolkata and Gwalior

(B) Kolkata and Bhopal

(C) Kolkata and Patna

(D) Kolkata and Madras

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Munda inaugurated the 144th, 145th, 146th and 147th Tribes India showrooms in Patna and Chennai virtually in a grand opening ceremony.

Concept note-2: -Shri Arjun Munda, Union Minister of Tribal Affairs, noted that, “I congratulate Smt. Nirmala Sitharaman for declaring a comprehensive Budget that focuses on tribal affairs and related concerns.

Concept note-3: -UPSC Mains. Notes: In the states of Chattisgarh, Jharkhand, Madhya Pradesh and Odisha, the Governor is responsible for the appointment of the Tribal Welfare minister.

Concept note-4: -Hon’ble Union Minister of Tribal Affairs Shri Arjun Munda Ji Visit - 25 April 2022. Hon’ble Union Minister of Tribal Affairs Shri Arjun Munda Ji visited ICAR-NRC on Mithun, Nagaland on 25.04. 2022.
