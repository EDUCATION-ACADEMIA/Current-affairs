Who won Portuguese Grand Prix 2020?

(A) Valterri Bottas

(B) ** Lewis Hamilton

(C) Max Versteppen

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hamilton secures record-breaking 92nd win with victory in Portugal. Lewis Hamilton won Sunday’s Portuguese Grand Prix to take the 92nd victory of his career and move one clear of Michael Schumacher in the list of all-time F1 victories.

Concept note-2: -Hamilton secures record-breaking 92nd win with victory in Portugal. Lewis Hamilton won Sunday’s Portuguese Grand Prix to take the 92nd victory of his career and move one clear of Michael Schumacher in the list of all-time F1 victories.

Concept note-3: -The 66-lap race was won by Mercedes driver Lewis Hamilton from second.

Concept note-4: -Max Verstappen won his first World Championship (and the first for a Dutch driver), driving for Red Bull Racing-Honda. Lewis Hamilton, the defending champion, finished runner-up, driving for Mercedes. Mercedes won their eighth consecutive Constructors’ Championship.

Concept note-5: -Verstappen pulled away to win the title on the last lap, beating Hamilton, Sainz, Tsunoda and Gasly. Norris battled back to seventh, ahead of Alonso, Ocon and Leclerc.
