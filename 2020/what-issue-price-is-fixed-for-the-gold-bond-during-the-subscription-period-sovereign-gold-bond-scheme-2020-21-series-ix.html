What issue price is fixed for the gold bond during the subscription period Sovereign Gold Bond Scheme 2020-21 (Series IX)?

(A) 4500 per gram

(B) 6500 per gram

(C) 5500 per gram

(D) ** 5000 per gram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Reserve Bank of India (RBI) has fixed the issue price at Rs 4, 786 per gram. Under this scheme, the RBI issues the bonds on behalf of the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -For such investors, the issue price of Gold Bond will be ₹5, 359/- (Rupees Five thousand three hundred and fifty nine only) per gram of gold.

Concept note-3: -For such investors the issue price of Gold Bond will be Rs 4, 612 (Rupees Four thousand Six hundred twelve only) per gram of gold.
