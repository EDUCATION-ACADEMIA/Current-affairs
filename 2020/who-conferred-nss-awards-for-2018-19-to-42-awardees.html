Who conferred NSS Awards for 2018-19 to 42 Awardees?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Amit Shah

(C) ** Ram Nath Kovind

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The President of India Shri Ram Nath Kovindvirtually conferred the National Service Scheme (NSS) Awards for the year 2018-19 on 24th September, 2020 from Rashtrapati Bhawan, New Delhi today.

Concept note-2: -Rashmi J Anchan, studying in III BCom, a talented girl Who has secured 92.62% in her SSLC board Exams and 96.01 % in PU board exam.

Concept note-3: -Nature of NSS Awards 3, 00, 000/- (for NSS Programme Development) with a Trophy to the University/ +2 Council. A Certificate and a Silver Medal to the Programme Coordinator. Second Award: Rs. 2, 00, 000/- (for NSS Programme Development) with a Trophy to the University/ +2 Council.

Concept note-4: -Objective of NSS Awards: The objective of NSS Awards is to recognise and reward outstanding contributions towards voluntary community service made by the Universities/ Senior Secondary (+2) Councils, NSS Units/ Programme Officers and NSS Volunteers, with a view to further promote NSS.
