Who is the head of Government Forms Panel Under RoDTEP Scheme for Exporters?

(A) G Parande

(B) Gautam Roy

(C) ** G K Pillai

(D) Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of India under the Ministry of finance, Department of Revenue has set up a three-member committee headed by Secretary (retired)G.K. Pillai for determination of ceiling rates under Remission of Duties and Taxes on Exported Products (RoDTEP) scheme.

Concept note-2: -Shri G.K. Pillai, Chairman, RODTEP Committee.

Concept note-3: -Under RoDTEP, various central and state duties, taxes, and levies imposed on input products, among others, will be refunded to exporters. The three-member committee is chaired by former secretary G K Pillai.

Concept note-4: -The scheme is being implemented from 1st January 2021 and the rebate is issued as a transferable electronic scrip by the Central Board of Indirect Taxes & Customs (CBIC) in an end to end IT environment.

Concept note-5: -Question: Are any governing regulatory bodies there for the RoDTEP? RoDTEP scheme is notified by the Department of Commerce. The Scheme shall be fully administered by the Department of Revenue. A separate RODTEP Policy Committee (RPC) chaired by DGFT is constituted to address residual issues related to the Scheme.
