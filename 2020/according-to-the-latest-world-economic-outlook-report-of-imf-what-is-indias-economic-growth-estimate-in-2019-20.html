According to the latest World Economic Outlook Report of IMF, what is India’s economic growth estimate in 2019-20?

(A) 4.2%

(B) ** 4.8%

(C) 5.5%

(D) 6.1%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The global economy is projected to grow at 3.5 percent in 2019 and 3.6 percent in 2020, 0.2 and 0.1 percentage point below last October’s projections.

Concept note-2: -Full Report and Executive Summary. Global growth is forecast at 3.0 percent for 2019, its lowest level since 2008–09 and a 0.3 percentage point downgrade from the April 2019 World Economic Outlook.

Concept note-3: -Growth is expected to moderate reflecting the less favorable outlook and tighter financial conditions. Real GDP is projected to grow at 6.8 percent and 6.1 percent in FY2022/23 and FY2023/24 respectively.

Concept note-4: -IMF cuts India’s FY 2022-23 growth forecast to 6.8%
