(A) RBI

(B) ** SBI

(C) HDFC

(D) PNB

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai, 24 August, 2022: BOB Financial Solutions Limited (BFSL), a wholly owned subsidiary of Bank of Baroda (BoB) and the Indian Army have launched a co-branded RuPay credit card for the Indian Army personnel, in partnership with National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI).

Concept note-2: -Bank of Baroda subsidiary launches co-branded RuPay contactless credit card for Indian Army | Mint.

Concept note-3: -Paytm Payments Bank Limited (PPBL) in collaboration with the National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI) has introduced RuPay Credit Card on UPI.

Concept note-4: -Samsung India, Axis Bank launch co-branded Credit Card.
