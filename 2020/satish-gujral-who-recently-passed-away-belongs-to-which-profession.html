Satish Gujral, who recently passed away, belongs to which profession?

(A) Actor

(B) ** Architect

(C) Director

(D) Artist

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Padma Vibhushan award winning artist, architect, sculptor and writer Satish Gujral passed away. He was awarded with India’s second-highest civilian honour i.e. the Padma Vibhushan in 1999. Satish Gujral designed the Belgium Embassy in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Gujral was also an architect and his design of the Belgium Embassy in New Delhi was selected by the international forum of architects as one of the finest buildings built in the 20th century.

Concept note-3: -"The noted painter and architect passed away peacefully at 10.30 last night in his home. He died of old age, ” the artist’s nephew and Rajya Sabha MP Naresh Gujral told news agency PTI. The last rites will be held on Friday at 2 pm at the Lodhi Crematorium, he added.

Concept note-4: -Satish Gujral was a Padma Vibhushan awardee. New Delhi: Eminent artist Satish Gujral, known for his versatility across different mediums, died in Delhi last night at the age of 94 after a prolonged illness.
