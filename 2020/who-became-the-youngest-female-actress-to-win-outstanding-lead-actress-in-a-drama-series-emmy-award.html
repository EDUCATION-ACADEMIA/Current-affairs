Who became the youngest female actress to win ‘Outstanding lead actress in a Drama series’ Emmy award?

(A) Billi Eilish

(B) ** Zendaya

(C) Dua Lipa

(D) Selena Gomez

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Zendaya previously made history at the 2020 Emmys when she became the youngest woman - and only the second Black woman (after Viola Davis) - to win Outstanding Lead Actress in a Drama at the age of 24.

Concept note-2: -Zendaya Becomes Youngest Two-Time Emmy Winner in History With ‘Euphoria’ Lead Actress Win. Zendaya made history - once again - by winning the Emmy award for lead actress in a drama series for her performance in “Euphoria.” She became the youngest lead actress in a drama winner with her first win for “Euphoria” in 2020.

Concept note-3: -While Eve Arden was the first winner in the female acting category, Loretta Young was the first actress to win for a lead performance in a drama series. By 1966, the acting awards were split into drama and comedy categories, undergoing several name changes until settling with the current title.
