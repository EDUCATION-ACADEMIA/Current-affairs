Which medal did India win at the Badminton Asia Team Championships?

(A) Gold

(B) Silver

(C) ** Bronze

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MANILA (Philippines): Double Olympic medallist P V Sindhu ended her Badminton Asia Championships campaign with a bronze medal after going down fighting to Japan’s Akane Yamaguchi in three games on Saturday.

Concept note-2: -Lee Zii Jia defeats Jonatan Christie in close-fought match Brilliant badminton from both, this is on a knife’s edge. And Lee closes it out, he’s 2022 Asia champion winning 21-17, 23-21.

Concept note-3: -Sindhu became the first Indian to win Badminton World Championships gold by defeating Nozomi Okuhara. Nozomi Okuhara is from Japan. It was the BWF World Championships of 2019.

Concept note-4: -First Indian to Win Olympic Medal in Badminton Saina Nehwal has acquired 24 international titles containing 11 tiles in superseries. She is also the first Indian to reach the finals of the BWF World Championship, achieving the title of the only Indian to win the BWF World Championship.
