Who has been appointed as the new Ambassador for World Wide Fund (WWF) India’s Environment Education program?

(A) ** Viswanathan Anand

(B) Sourav Ganguly

(C) Sachin Tendulkar

(D) Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Five-time world chess champion Viswanathan Anand has joined WWF(World Wide Fund) India as its ambassador for the environmental education programme. WWF India is celebrating its 50 years of conservation in India.

Concept note-2: -Who has been named the new Ambassador for World Wide Fund (WWF) India’s Environment Education programme?
