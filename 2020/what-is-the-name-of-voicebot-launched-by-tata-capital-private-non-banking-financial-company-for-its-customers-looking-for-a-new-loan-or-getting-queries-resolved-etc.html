What is the name of voicebot launched by Tata Capital, Private Non-Banking Financial Company, for its customers looking for a new loan or getting queries resolved etc?

(A) ** TIA

(B) KIA

(C) VIA

(D) BIA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -TIA is designed to voice-assist the personal loan customer journey, check loan eligibility and aid the customer through the loan approval process in minutes.

Concept note-2: -The Company is registered with the Reserve Bank of India as a Systemically Important Non Deposit Accepting Non Banking Financial Company (NBFC) and offers fund and fee-based financial services to its customers, under the Tata Capital brand.

Concept note-3: -The essence of the Tata Capital brand is encapsulated in its promise - “We only do what’s right for you".

Concept note-4: -Tata Capital Financial Services Limited (our “Company” / TCFSL) was incorporated at Mumbai, Maharashtra on November 19, 2010, as a public limited company, under the provisions of the Companies Act, 1956 bearing CIN U67100MH2010PLC210201, under the name “Tata Capital Financial Services Limited”.

Concept note-5: -Tata Capital Limited ("TCL"), the flagship financial services company of the Tata Group, is a subsidiary of Tata Sons Private Limited and is registered with the Reserve Bank of India as a Systemically Important Non-Deposit Accepting Core Investment Company ("CIC").
