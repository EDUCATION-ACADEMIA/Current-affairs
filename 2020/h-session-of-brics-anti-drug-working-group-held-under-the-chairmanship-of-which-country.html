h session of BRICS anti-drug working group held under the chairmanship of which country?

(A) India

(B) China

(C) ** Russia

(D) South Africa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The most recent BRICS leaders’ summit took place virtually on 23 June 2022 hosted by China.

Concept note-2: -The headquarter of BRICS bank is located in Shanghai, China. BRICS is an association of five emerging economies that are Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa. The first formal summit was held in Yekaterinburg. Emerging nations with quickly expanding economies are referred to as BRICS.

Concept note-3: -The BRICS countries, namely Brazil, Russia, India, China, and South Africa, are considered the five foremost emerging economies in the world.

Concept note-4: -Concerning a BRICS expansion, Lavrov stated that Algeria, Argentina, and Iran had all applied, while it is already known that Saudi Arabia, Türkiye, Egypt and Afghanistan are interested, along with Indonesia, which is expected to make a formal application to join at the upcoming G20 summit in Bali.
