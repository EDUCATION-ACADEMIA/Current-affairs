When will National Digital Health Mission be rolled out across India?

(A) February 2021

(B) March 2021

(C) April 2021

(D) ** January 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Digital Health Ecosystem: An open platform for the National Digital Health Ecosystem will be rolled out. It will consist of digital registries of health providers and health facilities, unique health identity and universal access to health facilities.

Concept note-2: -The prime minister of India launched the National Digital Health Mission on 27th September 2021. He announced the pilot project on 15th August 2021. This mission is now known as Pradhan Mantri Digital Health Mission. This historical initiative was taken to develop the digital healthcare infrastructure of our country.

Concept note-3: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched the National Digital Health Mission on 15th August 2020. The mission aims to create an integrated healthcare system linking practitioners with patients digitally by giving them access to real-time health records.

Concept note-4: -The Union Cabinet, chaired by Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi has approved the national roll-out of Central Sector Scheme, Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM) of Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India, with a budget of Rs. 1, 600 crore for five years.
