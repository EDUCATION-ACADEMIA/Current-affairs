World Kidney Day is observed on which date?

(A) ** March 12

(B) March 13

(C) March 11

(D) March 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Kidney Day started in 2006 and has not stopped growing ever since. Every year, the campaign highlights a particular theme.

Concept note-2: -On 10 March 2022, World Kidney Day celebrates Kidney Health for All – Bridge the gap to better kidney care, this year’s theme.

Concept note-3: -This year, it is being observed on March 10. World Kidney day is celebrated on the second Thursday of March every year. This year, it is being observed on March 10.

Concept note-4: -The World Kidney Day Joint Steering Committee has declared 2022 to be the year of “Kidney Health for All”. Specifically, it calls on all of us to work to bridge the knowledge gaps to better kidney care.
