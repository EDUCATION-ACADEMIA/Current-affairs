Who launched ‘AIM-iCREST’, in partnership with Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and Wadhwani Foundation?

(A) ** AIM

(B) NITI Aayog

(C) iCREST

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is option 4 i.e. Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation and Wadhwani Foundation. NITI Aayog’s Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), has launched AIM iCREST – an Incubator Capabilities enhancement program for a Robust Ecosystem.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog Launches’Poshan Gyan’, a Digital Repository on Nutrition Information. NITI Aayog, in partnership with Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation and Centre for Social and Behaviour Change, Ashoka University, today launched Poshan Gyan, a national digital repository on health and nutrition.

Concept note-3: -The Atal Innovation Mission (AIM) of the NITI Aayog has launched an Incubator Capabilities Enhancement Programme by the name of AIM iCREST. The initiative is supported by the Wadhwani Foundation and the Bill & Melinda Gates Foundation.

Concept note-4: -Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), NITI Aayog is Government of India’s flagship initiative to promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in the country and was setup in 2016.

Concept note-5: -ABOUT AIM iCRESt Atal Innovation Mission (AIM), over the last few years, has created a network of incubators that energize the innovation value chain in India. Within this ecosystem, AIM is conscious of the team related challenges faced by incubators as they strive to produce viable, successful businesses.
