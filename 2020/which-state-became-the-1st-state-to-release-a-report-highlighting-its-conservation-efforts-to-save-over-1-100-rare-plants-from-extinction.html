Which state became the 1st state to release a report highlighting its conservation efforts to save over 1, 100 rare plants from extinction?

(A) Uttar Pradesh

(B) Madhya pradesh

(C) ** Uttarakhand

(D) Himachal Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttarakhand is the very first province to issue a one-of-a-kind report outlining its conservation programs to rescue over 1, 100 endangered floras from extinction.
