Which of the following won its second I-League title?

(A) Aizawl FC

(B) Gokulam

(C) ** Mohun Bagan

(D) Neroca

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is the 3rd tier of Indian football, behind Indian Super League and I-League. It operates as a system of promotion and relegation with I-League. To avoid confusion with I-League being the second tier, the league was renamed to I-League 2 by AIFF in 2022.

Concept note-2: -Since the inception of the I-League, a total of seven clubs have been crowned champions. Dempo have won the most titles in league history, being crowned champions three times.

Concept note-3: -Since its inception in the 1996–97 season, the champions of the NFL were considered as the national champions. However, after the 2006–07 season of the NFL, the league was rebranded as the I-League. Since 2007–08 season, the I-League champions were considered the national champions until 2016–17 season.

Concept note-4: -However, on 16 December, the league committee decided that 20 teams will play in I-League 2, consisting of 12 state league champions along with 6 ISL reserve sides and 2 qualification tournament winners.
