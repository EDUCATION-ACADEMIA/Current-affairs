Which edition of ‘All India Tiger Estimation-2018, report was released by Union Minister of Environment, Forest and Climate Change, Prakash Javadekar?

(A) ** 4th

(B) 3rd

(C) 5th

(D) 2nd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the occasion of International Tiger Day, Prime Minister Narendra Modi released the results of the fourth cycle of All India Tiger Estimation - 2018, at Lok Kalyan Marg in New Delhi today. The count of tigers in India has risen to 2967, in 2018, according to this census.

Concept note-2: -NTCA in collaboration with the State Forest Departments, Conservation NGO’s and coordinated by the Wildlife Institute of India (WII), has conducted a National assessment for the “Status of Tigers, Co-predators, Prey and their Habitat” every four years since then.

Concept note-3: -Palamu forest is located in Jharkhand and is also a tiger reserve. The world’s first tiger census was carried out here in 1932. The tiger population has greatly reduced now, apart from tigers it is home to elephants, leopards, wild dogs, gaurs, etc. So the correct answer is (D) Palamu forest.
