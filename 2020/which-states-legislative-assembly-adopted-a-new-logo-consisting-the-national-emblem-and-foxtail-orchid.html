Which state’s legislative assembly adopted a new logo consisting the national emblem and foxtail orchid?

(A) Assam

(B) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(C) Nagaland

(D) Tripura

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Foxtail orchid (Rhynchostylis retusa), of the Vanda genus, is the state flower of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Notes: Arunachal Pradesh has adopted a new logo for its Legislative Assembly (APLA) recently. The new logo reflects the distinct identity and culture of the state.

Concept note-3: -The mithun bison and hornbill are the official state animals and birds of Arunachal Pradesh and mountains and sunrise allude to the name of the state which translates into “the land of dawn-lit mountains".

Concept note-4: -The blue colour denotes the autonomy of state Legislative Assembly. The national emblem and state flower represents federal system of Indian constitution and the state of Arunachal Pradesh respectively.
