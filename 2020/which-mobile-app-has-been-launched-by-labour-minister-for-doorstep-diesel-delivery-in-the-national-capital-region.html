Which mobile app has been launched by Labour Minister for doorstep diesel delivery in the national capital region?

(A) Yatri

(B) Sadak

(C) ** Humsafar

(D) Madad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Nishit Goel, Founder director, Humsafar India. “Humsafar started its services in the NCR region, including Delhi, Haryana, Punjab and parts of Rajasthan. Since then, it has made approximately 300 partners across states. Of these, 92 partners are actively using the Fuel Humsafar App and these numbers continue to grow.

Concept note-2: -Notes: The Labour Minister Santosh Gangwar recently launched the mobile application named ‘Humsafar’. The app is launched with an objective of delivering fuel at the door-steps of Housing societies, hotels and hospitals in the National capital region.

Concept note-3: -Q: Is it safe to use FuelCall app? All transactions on FuelCall are encrypted at every stage and completely secure.

Concept note-4: -Santosh Gangwar (Source: Verified Twitter account of Santosh Gangwar). Housing societies, hotels, and hospitals in the national capital region will soon be able to get diesel delivered at their doorsteps, with the launch of mobile application ‘Humsafar’ by Labour Minister Santosh Gangwar.
