How many Jyotirling pilgrim centres will be connected by IRCTC’s latest Kashi Mahakal Express?

(A) 2

(B) ** 3

(C) 4

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Kashi Mahakal Express derives its name from the fact that it will connect the three Jyotirlinga pilgrim centres – Varanasi, Ujjain and Omkareshwar – Omkareshwar (near Indore), Mahakaleshwar (Ujjain) and Kashi Vishwanath (Varanasi) in its journey.

Concept note-2: -The train fares will be dynamic in nature and IRCTC will have the exclusive authority to decide the fare. The minimum ticket fare of Kashi Mahakal Express will be Rs 1, 629 for one side journey.

Concept note-3: -Tour Option (A): Mahakal Darshan (Ujjain – Omkareshwar) The package cost without train fare for Single Occupancy is Rs 12, 320 per passenger (group size 2-3), Rs 11, 630 (group size 4-5), Rs 11, 250 (group size 6-10).

Concept note-4: -Train 20414 MAHAKAL EXPRESS runs from Indore Junction BG(INDB) to Varanasi Junction(BSB) on Wednesday, Friday. Train departs from Indore Junction BG(INDB) at 10:15 and reaches Varanasi Junction(BSB) at 05:00.
