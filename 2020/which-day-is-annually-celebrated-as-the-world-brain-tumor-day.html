Which day is annually celebrated as the World Brain Tumor Day?

(A) June 6

(B) ** June 8

(C) June 7

(D) June 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Every year 8th of June is celebrated as World Brain Tumor Day. This initiative was initially taken by German Brain Tumor Association and is now celebrated worldwide to raise awareness and educate people about the brain tumor.

Concept note-2: -June 8 is World Brain Tumour Day each year. Watch BrainTumourFdn for a special awareness-raising social media activity. The “Deutsche Hirntumorhilfe e.V.” (German Brain Tumor Association) started this international commemoration day in the year 2000 as a tribute to all brain tumour patients and their families.

Concept note-3: -World Brain Tumor Day 2022 will be observed on 8 June and the theme is “Together We Are Stronger.” World Brain Tumor Day 2022 will be observed on Wednesday, 8 June this year. It’s an annual event to increase awareness and educate people about the causes, symptoms, and treatment of brain tumors.

Concept note-4: -World Brain tumour Day 2022 observed every year on 8th June.

Concept note-5: -The World Brain Tumour Day is observed on June 8 every year to spread awareness about brain tumours and educate people about them. The day also commemorates all the people who have been affected by brain cancer and support people who are fighting it.
