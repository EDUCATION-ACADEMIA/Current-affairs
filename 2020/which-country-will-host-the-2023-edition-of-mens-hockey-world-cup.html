Which country will host the 2023 edition of Men’s Hockey World Cup?

(A) China

(B) ** India

(C) Japan

(D) Austria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2023 Men’s FIH Hockey World Cup: Players involved, schedule and how to watch the live action. For the second consecutive edition, the Men’s FIH Hockey World Cup will take place in India, with 16 teams vying for glory in the cities of Bhubaneswar and Rourkela between 13 and 29 January.

Concept note-2: -The host country for the 2023 Men’s Hockey World Cup was announced in November 2019, and India was confirmed to host the tournament in January 2023.

Concept note-3: -Krishan Bahadur Pathak and PR Sreejesh are the goalkeepers. Manpreet Singh, Hardik Singh, Nilakanta Sharma, Shamsher Singh, Vivek Sagar Prasad, and Akashdeep Singh are all midfielders. Mandeep Singh, Lalit Kumar Upadhyay, Abhishek, and Sukhjeet Singh are the forwards.
