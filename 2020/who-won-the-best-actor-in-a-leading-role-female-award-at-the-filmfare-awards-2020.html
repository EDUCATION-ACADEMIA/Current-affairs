Who won the Best Actor in A Leading Role (Female) Award at the Filmfare Awards 2020?

(A) Bhumi Pednekar

(B) Taapsee Pannu

(C) Deepika Padukone

(D) ** Alia Bhatt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 65th Filmfare Awards were held on 15 Feb 2020 at Indira Gandhi Athletic Stadium, Guwahati, Assam. Gully Boy, directed by Zoya Akhtar, was awarded the Best Film. Ranveer Singh received Best Actor in a Leading Role for Gully Boy. Alia Bhatt was awarded Best Actress in a Leading Role for Gully Boy.

Concept note-2: -Best Actor In A Leading Role (Female) - Alia Bhatt (Gully Boy) Alia Bhatt brings back her effusive charm with the portrayal of Safina in Gully Boy.

Concept note-3: -Riddhi Sen won the best actor award at 65th National Film Awards. The award for Best Actress was given to Sridevi for her acting in Mom.
