International Animation Day was observed on which date?

(A) 25 October

(B) 26 October

(C) 27 October

(D) ** 28 October

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Animated Film Association established International Animation Day in 2002. They chose October 28th to commemorate Theatre Optique, the first public performance of animation.

Concept note-2: -INTERNATIONAL ANIMATION DAY HISTORY They chose October 28th to commemorate Theatre Optique, the first public performance of animation. Charles-Emile Reynaud invented the moving picture system used in the animated performance.

Concept note-3: -ASIFA created the International Animation Day (IAD) in 2002, honoring the birth of animation, recognized as the first public performance of projected moving images: Emile Reynaudâ€™s Theatre Optique in Paris, on the 28th October 1892. Every day we witness the importance and beauty of animation in a variety of media.

Concept note-4: -International Animation Day is celebrated on 28 October every year. It was in this year when the International Animated Film Association (ASIFA) declared International Animation Day (IAD) as a worldwide occasion to commend the speciality of animation.

Concept note-5: -During International Animation Day cultural institutions are also invited to join in by screening animated films, organizing workshops, exhibiting artwork and stills, providing technical demonstrations, and organizing other events helping to promote the art of animation.
