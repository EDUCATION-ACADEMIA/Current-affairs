Which Union Ministry launched 100-day ‘Catch the Rain’ campaign?

(A) Environment Ministry

(B) Health Ministry

(C) WCD Ministry

(D) ** Jal Shakti Ministry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Jal Shakti Abhiyan is the flagship water conservation campaign under the Ministry of Jal Shakti. Under the water conservation campaign, the government launched another programme with a theme, ‘catch the rain, where it falls, when it falls’.

Concept note-2: -Ministry of Jal Shakti launched “Catch the Rain” campaign with the tag line “Catch the rain, where it falls, when it falls” to nudge the states and all stakeholders to create Rain Water Harvesting Structures (RWHS) suitable to the climatic conditions and sub-soil strata, with people’s active participation.

Concept note-3: -President Ram Nath Kovind launched the ‘Jal Shakti Abhiyan: Catch the Rain campaign, 2022’ while presenting the 3rd National Water Awards.

Concept note-4: -It will be implemented from March 22 to November 30 - the pre-monsoon and monsoon period in the country.

Concept note-5: -The Correct Answer is Jal Shakthi Abhiyan.
