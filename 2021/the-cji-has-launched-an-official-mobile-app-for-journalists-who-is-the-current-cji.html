The CJI has launched an official mobile App for Journalists. Who is the current CJI?

(A) ** NV Ramana

(B) Anand Raj

(C) Sunil Singh

(D) Sudip Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chief Justice of India (CJI) D Y Chandrachud on Wednesday launched ‘Supreme Court Mobile App 2.0’, an updated version of the top court’s mobile application for Android users.

Concept note-2: -The new app, Supreme Court Mobile App 2.0, will digitize the judicial system and allow all law officers and government officials to track court cases and watch court proceedings. Users will also be able to track court verdicts and the pendency of cases, thus highlighting the transparency the new app provides.

Concept note-3: -The Chief Justice of India and the Judges of the Supreme Court are appointed by the President under clause (2) of Article 124 of the Constitution.

Concept note-4: -Justice Dhananjaya Yashwant Chandrachud was sworn in as the 50th Chief Justice of India on November 9, 2022. He will serve as the CJI for two years.
