Vishwavir Ahuja has been re-appointed as the MD & CEO of which bank by RBI till June 2022?

(A) UCO Bank

(B) IndusInd Bank

(C) ** RBL Bank

(D) Federal Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The announcement comes nearly six months after RBL Bank’s long-term MD & CEO Vishwavir Ahuja had stepped down. Following Ahuja’s exit in December 2021, the lender had appointed Rajeev Ahuja at the helm for an interim period – which was scheduled to expire on June 24.

Concept note-2: -Subramaniakumar, whose appointment came nearly six months after RBL Bank’s long-term MD and CEO Vishwavir Ahuja had stepped down In December 2021, has been appointed to helm RBL for three years from the date of taking charge.

Concept note-3: -Subramaniakumar is a veteran banker with over 40 years of experience.
