Who has grabbed the 24th World title when he triumphed at the IBSF 6-Red Snooker World Cup?

(A) P Suresh

(B) ** Pankaj Advani

(C) Aditi Roy

(D) Neha Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He has grabbed his 24th World title when he triumphed at the IBSF 6-Red Snooker World Cup with a victory over Pakistan’s Babar Masih in the final. Hence, Option 3 is correct. Advani, who won his 11th Asian title, started the final with a comfortable 42-13 win in the opening frame.

Concept note-2: -Pankaj Advani Wins His 24th World Title with Triumph at IBSF 6-Red Snooker World Cup.

Concept note-3: -Pankaj conquers Qatar 6Reds World Cup 2021 Indian player Pankaj Advani has conquered back-to-back two major International championships.

Concept note-4: -Kok Leong makes history with World Snooker Championships 2022 win. National snooker player Lim Kok Leong has created history after emerging as the first Malaysian player to win the IBSF World Championship in Antalya, Turkey, yesterday.

Concept note-5: -Stephen Hendry and Ronnie O’Sullivan hold the joint record of seven world titles each in the modern era. Both Ray Reardon and Steve Davis have won six titles; John Higgins and Mark Selby four; John Spencer and Mark Williams three; and Alex Higgins two.
