The annual military exercise KAZIND-21 is being conducted between the Indian Army and which Army?

(A) ** Kazakhstan

(B) Kyrgyzstan

(C) Kenya

(D) Kuwait

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Exercise KazInd 2022 is the 6th edition of the Indo-Kazakhstan Joint exercise and is aimed at building trust and understanding of each other in the field of national security.

Concept note-2: -KazInd 2022: India and Kazakhstan carry out joint military drill in Meghalaya. To strengthen positive military relations between India and Kazakhstan, the nations conducted their 6th edition of Army Joint Exercise KazInd 2022 in Meghalaya’s Umroi.

Concept note-3: -Vajra Prahar’ is a Special Forces joint training exercise conducted alternately in India and the US to share the best practices and experiences in areas such as joint mission planning and operational tactics as also to improve interoperability between the Special Forces of both nations.

Concept note-4: -Bongo Sagar is the naval exercise between Bangladesh and India.
