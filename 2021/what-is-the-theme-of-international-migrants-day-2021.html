What is the theme of International Migrants Day 2021?

(A) Migration with Dignity

(B) ** Harnessing the Potential of Human Mobility.

(C) Safe Migration in a World on the Move

(D) The issue of migration is a vast problem

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Global Compact for Safe, Orderly and Regular Migration (GCM) offers the opportunity and guidance to actualize human mobility and seize the opportunities it presents.

Concept note-2: -Integrating migrants into primary health care.

Concept note-3: -While there is no formal legal definition of an international migrant, most experts agree that an international migrant is someone who changes his or her country of usual residence, irrespective of the reason for migration or legal status.

Concept note-4: -18 December - International Migrants Day The International Organisation for Migration (IOM) is calling on the international community to come together and remember the migrants and refugees who have lost their lives or have disappeared while reaching a safe harbor.

Concept note-5: -International migration means when people move in or out of their native place and cross the state boundaries within different countries for a certain period. The country they chose to live in is termed as the host country. -Option A is the movement of people within the country.
