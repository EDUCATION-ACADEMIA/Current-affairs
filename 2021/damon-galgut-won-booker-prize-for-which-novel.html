Damon Galgut won Booker Prize for which novel?

(A) ** The Promise

(B) In a Strange Room

(C) The Good Doctor

(D) None of above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022 International Booker Prize awards ceremony in pictures Author Geetanjali Shree and translator Daisy Rockwell with the novel Tomb of Sand, winner of the International Booker Prize 2022. Geetanjali Shree, winner of the International Booker Prize 2022 for her novel Tomb of Sand.

Concept note-2: -Damon Galgut (born 12 November 1963) is a South African novelist and playwright. He was awarded the 2021 Booker Prize for his novel The Promise, having previously been shortlisted for the award in 2003 and 2010.

Concept note-3: -Damon Galgut has won the Booker prize for his portrait of a white South African family navigating the end of apartheid. The judges praised The Promise as “a spectacular demonstration of how the novel can make us see and think afresh”, and compared it to the work of William Faulkner and Virginia Woolf.
