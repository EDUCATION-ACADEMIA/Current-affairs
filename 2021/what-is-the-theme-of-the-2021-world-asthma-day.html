What is the theme of the 2021 World Asthma Day?

(A) Never too early, never too late

(B) Enough Asthma Deaths

(C) ** Uncovering Asthma Misconceptions

(D) STOP for asthma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year’s World Asthma Day theme is “Uncovering Asthma Misconceptions”. The theme provides a call to action to address common widely held myths and misconceptions concerning asthma that prevent persons with asthma from enjoying optimal benefit from the major advances in the management of this condition.

Concept note-2: -GINA has chosen ‘Closing Gaps in Asthma Care’ as the theme for the 2022 World Asthma Day. There are a number of gaps in asthma care which require intervention in order to reduce preventable suffering as well as the costs incurred by treating uncontrolled asthma.

Concept note-3: -World Asthma Day is an annual event organized by the Global Initiative for Asthma (GINA) to improve asthma awareness and care around the world. World Asthma Day is held on the first Tuesday in May. The theme of 2021’s event was “Uncovering Asthma Misconceptions, ” and for 2022, “Closing Gaps in Asthma Care."

Concept note-4: -In 2023, the theme of World Asthma Day is ‘Uncovering Asthma Misconceptions’. The idea behind this is to provide a call to action to address common myths and misconceptions about asthma. These misconceptions are often detrimental and don’t help to raise asthma awareness.

Concept note-5: -While there is no cure for asthma, there are a variety of treatments depending on the severity of the disease and a person’s triggers. Asthma Awareness is represented by the color gray.
