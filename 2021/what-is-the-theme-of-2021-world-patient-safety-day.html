What is the theme of 2021 World Patient Safety Day?

(A) ** Safe maternal and newborn care

(B) Everyone’s Saftey; Global Health

(C) Safe health workers, Safe patients

(D) Patient Safety:A Global Health Priority

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -For World Patient Safety Day, 17 September 2021, WHO urges all stakeholders to “Act now for safe and respectful childbirth!” with the theme “Safe maternal and newborn care”.

Concept note-2: -Every year, World Patient Safety Day is observed under a specific theme. This year, the theme of World Patient Safety Day 2022 is ‘Medication Safety’ along with the slogan ‘Medication Without Harm’ with a call to action to ‘Know, Check & Ask’.

Concept note-3: -The theme for World Patient Safety Day 2022 is the ongoing campaign of the WHO on Medication Without Harm, which is focused on reducing avoidable medication-related harm across the globe.

Concept note-4: -It is in this context that ‘Medication safety’ has been selected as the theme for World Patient Safety Day 2022, with the slogan ‘Medication without harm’.

Concept note-5: -17 September is World Patient Safety Day The Day brings together patients, families, caregivers, communities, health workers, health care leaders and policy-makers to show their commitment to patient safety.
