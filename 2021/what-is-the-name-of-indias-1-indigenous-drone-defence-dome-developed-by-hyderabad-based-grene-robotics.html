What is the name of India’s 1 indigenous drone defence dome developed by Hyderabad-based Grene Robotics?

(A) Akashdome

(B) Nagpash

(C) Indrarakshak

(D) ** Indrajaal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It has real time awareness of the situation. It has an integrated all current weapons suite and infrastructure.

Concept note-2: -’Indrajaal’ is India’s 1st indigenous drone defence dome developed by Hyderabad-based Grene Robotics. ‘Indrajaal’ is capable of autonomously protecting an area of 1000-2000 sq km against aerial threats.

Concept note-3: -’Varuna’ country’s first human-carrying drone, developed by the Indian startup Sagar Defence Engineering, will soon be inducted into Indian Navy.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Indrajaal.

Concept note-5: -Drone Dome is a counter unmanned air system (C-UAS) anti-aircraft system developed by Israel-based miltech Rafael Advanced Defense Systems. The system was first displayed in 2016, and joins similar protection systems developed by Rafael such as the Iron Dome.
