The International Conference and Exhibition on Digital technologies “Future Tech 2021” has been organised by which body?

(A) FICCI

(B) NASSCOM

(C) ** Confederation of Indian Industry (CII)

(D) NPCI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Rajeev Chandrasekhar, Minister of State in the Ministry of Electronics & IT, Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship, virtually attended the inaugural session of the International Conference and Exhibition on Digital technologies - “Future Tech 2021” organized by the CII.

Concept note-2: -The Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) has organised an International Conference and Exhibition on Digital technologies, “Future Tech 2021- a Journey of digital transformation to technology adoption and acceleration” from October 19 to 27, 2021.

Concept note-3: -India is now prepping for cutting-edge technologies including 5G, AI, blockchain, augmented reality & virtual reality, machine learning & deep learning, robots, natural language processing, etc.
