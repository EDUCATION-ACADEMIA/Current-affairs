Which event has been launched by PM Modi to mark 75 years of India’s independence?

(A) ** Azadi ka Amrut Mahotsav

(B) Azadi Mahotsava

(C) Amrut Mahotsava

(D) Amrut ki Azadi Mahotsav

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav or 75th Anniversary of Indian Independence was an event, in which the 75th Anniversary of the Independence of India was celebrated in India and abroad. It was 76th Independence Day of India.

Concept note-2: -Under the leadership of Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi Azadi Ka Amrit Mahotsav is an initiative of the Government of India to celebrate and commemorate 75 years of independence and the glorious history of India’s people, culture and achievements.

Concept note-3: -Independence Day 2022 Theme This year, the theme of the 75th Independence Day would be ‘Nation First, Always First.

Concept note-4: -This year, the theme of the Independence day 2022 is ‘Nation first, always first’.

Concept note-5: -Vidya Amrit Mahotsav’ is an initiative launched by Ministry of India, GoI in association with DoSEL and NCERT to promote and recognise teaching and learning innovations led by teachers, 2. 6. NCERT.
