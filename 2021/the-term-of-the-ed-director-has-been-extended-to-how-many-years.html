The term of the ED Director has been extended to how many years?

(A) ** 5 years

(B) 7 years

(C) 6 years

(D) 4 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government last year brought an ordinance mandating that the tenure of ED and CBI directors can be extended by up to three years after the mandated term of two years.

Concept note-2: -This is the third extension given to the Indian Revenue Services officer. The Union Government on Thursday gave a fresh one-year extension in service to the director of the Enforcement Directorate (ED) Sanjay Kumar Mishra, according to an official order. This is the third extension given to the officer.
