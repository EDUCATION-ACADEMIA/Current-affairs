KVIC has unveiled which e-commerce portal to promote khadi and village industry products?

(A) eKVproducts

(B) ekhadi

(C) evillagekhadi

(D) ** eKhadiIndia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) has unveiled an e-commerce portal - eKhadiIndia.com. Over 50, 000 products, ranging from apparel to home decor, will be available on its newly launched platform.

Concept note-2: -The KVIC is charged with the planning, promotion, organisation and implementation of programs for the development of Khadi and other village industries in the rural areas in coordination with other agencies engaged in rural development wherever necessary.

Concept note-3: -Context: Khadi and Village Industries Commission (KVIC) unveils Khadi India’s official e-Commerce site eKhadiIndia.com.

Concept note-4: -Khadi & Village Industries Commission (KVIC), under the Ministry of MSME, has launched two new products- Khadi babywear and handmade paper ‘Use and Throw’ Slippers on 15.07. 2021.

Concept note-5: -Gandhiji conceived Khadi Programme as a key to solve the economic problems of the country. According to him “ Its’one great merit is that it solves, as nothing else cab, the economic problem of Indian and removes starvation” (collected works of Mahatma Gandhi, Volume 23:45859).
