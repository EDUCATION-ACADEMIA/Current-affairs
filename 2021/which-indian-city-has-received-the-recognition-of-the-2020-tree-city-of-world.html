Which Indian city has received the recognition of the 2020 Tree City of World?

(A) Bengaluru

(B) Chennai

(C) Kochi

(D) ** Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Why Did Hyderabad & Mumbai Get the Tag? Hyderabad was recognized as a ‘Tree City of the World’ for the second consecutive year for planting 3.5 crore trees in 500 volunteer hours under the state government’s ‘Haritha Haram’ programme and Urban Forest Parks initiative.

Concept note-2: -Only Indian city other than Mumbai to be on the list. Hyderabad is among the two cities in India, along with Mumbai, to be listed as ‘Tree Cities of the World-2021’ by Arbor Day Foundation and FAO of UN. Hyderabad finds a place on the list for the second year in a row.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Mumbai. Mumbai has been recognized as the “2021 Tree City of the World” by the Food and Agriculture Organisation of the United Nations (UN) jointly with Arbor Day Foundation.

Concept note-4: -Q. Which Indian city has been recognised as one of the ‘Tree Cities of the World’ in 2022? Notes: For the second consecutive year, Hyderabad has been recognised as one of the ‘Tree Cities of the World’. It is a tag presented by Arbor Day Foundation and Food and Agriculture Organization of United Nations.

Concept note-5: -United Nations recognise Hyderabad as one of the ‘Tree Cities of the World’
