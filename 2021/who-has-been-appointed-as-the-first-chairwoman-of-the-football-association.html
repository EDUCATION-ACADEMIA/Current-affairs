Who has been appointed as the first chairwoman of the Football Association?

(A) Olivia Davidson

(B) ** Debbie Hewitt

(C) Daniel Hewitt

(D) Margret Thacher

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Here is some greater information about the person who is appointed as the first female chairperson in FA history. England’s Football Association (FA) made history after appointing Debbie Hewitt as the first female chair in its 157-year history.

Concept note-2: -England’s Football Association named corporate executive and former RAC chief, Debbie Hewitt as its first woman chair, ending months of speculation over Greg Clarke’s successor following his exit over inappropriate remarks.

Concept note-3: -Executive Director: Mark Bullingham, Chief Executive Officer He previously held the position of CEO EMEA for Fuse Sports & Entertainment, responsible for running one of the fastest growing businesses in the sector with a client portfolio including ten of the top 30 global brands.

Concept note-4: -Yvonne Harrison. Yvonne became Women in Football CEO in January 2022. Equality is a huge passion for Yvonne, from grassroots participation to the boardroom, and with over 20 years in the sports industry, she has experience across the board, in sport, health and business.
