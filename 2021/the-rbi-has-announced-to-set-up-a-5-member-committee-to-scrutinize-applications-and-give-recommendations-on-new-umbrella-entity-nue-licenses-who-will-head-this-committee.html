(A) Vikram Dhanda

(B) ** P. Vasudevan

(C) Debojit Barua

(D) Mohd. Anwar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The NUE, like the NPCI is supposed to be responsible for setting up, managing and operating new retail digital payment systems. It will also manage clearing and settlement systems and undertake initatives to strengthen the digital payments ecosystem in the country.

Concept note-2: -The Reserve Bank of India was set up on the basis of the recommendations of the Hilton Young Commission. The Reserve Bank of India Act, 1934 (II of 1934) provides the statutory basis of the functioning of the Bank, which commenced operations on April 1, 1935.
