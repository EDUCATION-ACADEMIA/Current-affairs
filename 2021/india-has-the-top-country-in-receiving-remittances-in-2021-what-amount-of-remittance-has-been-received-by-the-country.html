India has the top country in receiving remittances in 2021. What amount of remittance has been received by the country?

(A) ** USD 87 billion

(B) USD 90 billion

(C) USD 97 billion

(D) USD 81 billion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India had received $89.4 billion in remittances in 2021, according to the World Bank, making it the top recipient globally last year.

Concept note-2: -Notably in 2021, India had received $89.4 billion in remittances, according to the World Bank report, which then made India the top recipient globally.

Concept note-3: -The US has surpassed the UAE as the top source of remittances to India, with its share rising to 23.4 percent in 2021 from 22.9 percent in 2017.

Concept note-4: -In 2021, India was the country with the largest inflow of remittances worldwide. That year, Indians abroad sent the equivalent of 89.4 billion U.S. dollars in remittances. Mexico followed, with remittances amounting to a total of 54.1 billion U.S. dollars.

Concept note-5: -India is expected to receive a whopping $100 billion in remittances in 2022, according to the World Bank’s latest ‘Migration and Development Brief’. It noted that remittances to India, the world’s biggest recipient, stood at $89.4 billion last year and will grow at 12 per cent in 2022.
