National Summit on Agro & Food Processing started on which date?

(A) December 12

(B) December 11

(C) December 15

(D) ** December 14

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -To emphasize on such strategies and to deliver message to farmers in far flung areas of the country Government of Gujarat is organizing a National Summit on Agro and Food Processing from 14th December to 16th December 2021 at Anand, Gujarat with focus on Natural Farming.

Concept note-2: -UPSC Mains. Q. Which Union Territory of India recently hosted the first ever Food Processing Summit? Notes: The Union Territory of Ladakh recently hosted the first ever Food Processing Summit to guide the entrepreneurs and attract investments.

Concept note-3: -The first ever National Agriculture Policy was announced in July, 2000. The Policy seeks to actualise the vast untapped potential of Indian agriculture and aims at achieving a growth rate in excess of 4 per cent per annum in the agriculture sector.

Concept note-4: -Ladakh is hosting the 1st ever Food Processing Summit to attract investors to the region.
