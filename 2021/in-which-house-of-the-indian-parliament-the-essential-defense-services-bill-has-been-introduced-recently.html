(A) Rajya Sabha

(B) Rajasthan Court

(C) ** Lok Sabha

(D) Planning Commission

Concept note-1: -2022. Earlier, the Constitution (Scheduled Castes and Scheduled Tribes) Order (Second Amendment) Bill, 2022 was passed unanimously by the Rajya Sabha during the ongoing Winter Session of the Parliament on 14.12. 2022 wrt state of Uttar Pradesh.
