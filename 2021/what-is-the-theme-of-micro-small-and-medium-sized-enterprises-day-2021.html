(B) Msme Day 2021:The Digital Dimension

(C) ** Msme 2021:Key to an Inclusive and Sustainable Recovery

(D) Covid-19:The Great Lockdown and Its Impact On Small Business

Concept note-1: -Resilience and Rebuilding: MSMEs for sustainable development at the forefront of building back better and stronger from the impacts of the COVID-19 pandemic, climate crisis and conflicts.
