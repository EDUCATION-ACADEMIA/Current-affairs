Which state government has released the third instalment of the ‘Jaganna Vidya Deevena’ scheme?

(A) Odisha

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Andhra Pradesh

(D) Telangana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jagananna Vidya Deevena is a program launched by the Government of Andhra Pradesh to encourage students hailing from poor economic backgrounds to pursue higher education by reimbursing full fee which includes the tuition, special and examination fees.

Concept note-2: -AP Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme The scholarship scheme was launched by the Andhra Pradesh Government, mainly the chief minister of Andhra Pradesh that is Mr. Jagan Mohan Reddy.

Concept note-3: -Jagananna Vidya Deevena Installment Date 2023. This is the first time in the history of Andhra Pradesh, under Jagananna Vidya Deevena Scheme 2023 (JVD Scholarship), the quarterly fees (four installments) will be deposited in the bank accounts of mothers of selected candidates in the same financial year.

Concept note-4: -Second Installment Under Jagananna Vasathi Deevena The state government of Andhra Pradesh has released the second installment of the Jagananna Vasathi Deevena Scheme 2023. Jagan Mohan Reddy has transferred Rs. 1, 048.94 crores under 10, 89, 302 beneficiaries.
