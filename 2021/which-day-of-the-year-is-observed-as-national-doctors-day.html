Which day of the year is observed as National Doctors’ Day?

(A) Fourth Wednesday of June

(B) 30 June

(C) First Thursday of July

(D) ** 01 July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Doctors are saviours; hence, many consider them as next to God for their noble work. And to acknowledge their contribution to the society, July 1 is being celebrated as Doctor’s Day in India. The first Doctor’s Day was celebrated by the Indian Medical Association (IMA) on July 1, 1991.

Concept note-2: -1st July 2022 is commemorated as National Doctors Day in India to remember Dr BC Roy, a renowned physician and second Chief Minister of West Bengal. The day marks his birth anniversary as well as death Anniversary.

Concept note-3: -The National Doctor’s Day was first observed in India in 1991. July 1, the birth anniversary of Dr Bidhan Chandra Roy, a physician, academician, philanthropist, freedom fighter and politician, is observed as the National Doctor’s Day in India. On this day, contributions of doctors to mankind are acknowledged.

Concept note-4: -The special day is observed on July 1 in India. The first time this day was established was in 1991 and ever since annually, National Doctor’s Day is celebrated across the country.
