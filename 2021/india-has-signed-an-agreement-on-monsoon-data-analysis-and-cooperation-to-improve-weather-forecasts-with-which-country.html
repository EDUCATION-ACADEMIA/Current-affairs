India has signed an agreement on monsoon data analysis and cooperation to improve weather forecasts with which country?

(A) Maldives

(B) Germany

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) ** USA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and USA signed an agreement on Monsoon Data Analysis and cooperation in order to improve weather forecasts.

Concept note-2: -The India Meteorological Department (IMD) is the principal department responsible for meteorological observations and weather forecasting under Ministry of Earth Sciences, Government of India .

Concept note-3: -Noted Scientist Vasant Gowariker is known as the Father of the Indian Monsoon Model. He had made valuable contributions in the fields of space research, weather forecasting and the popularisation of science.

Concept note-4: -Forecasting Indian monsoon rainfall has a long history and dates back to 1886. The first official seasonal monsoon forecast was issued by Sir Henry Blanford in 1886 which was based entirely on Himalayan snowfall.
