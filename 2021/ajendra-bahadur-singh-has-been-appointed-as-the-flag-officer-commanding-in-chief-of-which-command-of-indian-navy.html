Ajendra Bahadur Singh has been appointed as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief of which command of Indian Navy?

(A) ** Eastern Naval Command

(B) Western Naval Command

(C) Andaman and Nicobar Command

(D) Southern Naval Command

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice Admiral Ajendra Bahadur Singh, PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC is a flag officer of the Indian Navy. He is currently the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief (FOC-in-C), Western Naval Command since 29 November 2021 succeeding Vice Admiral R.

Concept note-2: -The Command is commanded by a Three Star Flag Officer of the rank of Vice Admiral with the title Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief Western Command (FOC-in-C). Vice Admiral Ajendra Bahadur Singh is the current FOC-in-C WNC, who took over on 30 November 2021.

Concept note-3: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta, PVSM, AVSM, YSM, VSM Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta assumed charge as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Naval Command (ENC) on 01 December 2021.

Concept note-4: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta is the current FOC-in-C ENC, who took over on 30 November 2021.

Concept note-5: -The current Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS) and only full admiral in the Indian Armed Forces is Admiral R. Hari Kumar.
