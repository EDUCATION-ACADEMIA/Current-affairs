Who has announced the World’s Largest 2-Wheeler Charging Network?

(A) Uber

(B) ** Ola

(C) GM

(D) Rapido

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ola Electric on Thursday revealed its plans to set up the world’s largest electric two-wheeler charging network. SoftBank-backed Ola Electric plans to provide charging solutions to all its electric two-wheeler customers.

Concept note-2: -The largest EV charging station companies in the world are EVBox (Netherlands), ChargePoint, Inc (USA), ABB (Switzerland), Teld New Energy (China), Star Charge (China)

Concept note-3: -Gurugram: India’s largest electric vehicle (EV) charging station with a capacity of 121 charging points for 4 wheelers, has been opened here in Sector 86.

Concept note-4: -The station, opened on Thursday, has 75 AC, 25 DC and 21 Hybrid charging points with a capacity of charging 1, 000 cars in a single day.

Concept note-5: -The world’s highest charging station for electric vehicles was inaugurated at Kaza in Spiti today! The world’s highest charging station for electric vehicles was inaugurated at Kaza in Spiti today. Mahender Pratap Singh, SDM, inaugurated it. It was set up by goEgo company.
