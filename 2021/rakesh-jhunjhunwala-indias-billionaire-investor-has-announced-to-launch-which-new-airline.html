Rakesh Jhunjhunwala, India’s billionaire investor, has announced to launch which new airline?

(A) SpiceJet

(B) Akasha

(C) ** Akash Air

(D) Firoj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Akasa Air : An Air Operator Certificate (AOC) allows a new airline to carry out commercial air transport operations.

Concept note-2: -Akasa Air, India’s newest airline, Sunday operated its first flight from Mumbai to Ahmedabad.

Concept note-3: -Rakesh Jhunjhunwala, Billionaire Investor Who Launched Akasa Air, Passes Away At 62.

Concept note-4: -Akasa Air will be adding its 11th network point on December 25th, when it begins connecting the northern city of Lucknow (LKO) with both Bengaluru and Mumbai. Flights will operate in a W pattern from its Bengaluru base. Four days later, it will also begin flights from Bengaluru to Kochi (COK).

Concept note-5: -Akasa Air (India) A new Indian ultra low cost carrier backed by a billionaire investor and former Jet Airways executives.
