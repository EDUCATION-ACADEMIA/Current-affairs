Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated the Sardardham Bhavan in which city?

(A) Hyderabad

(B) ** Ahmedabad

(C) Lucknow

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Prime Minister and other ministers of his cabinet get salary under Article 75 of the Indian Constitution. Prime Minister Narendra Modi gets a salary of more than one lakh 60 thousand rupees every month.

Concept note-2: -Located in Ahmedabad, the largest city in the western state, the Sardardham Bhavan has state-of-the-art facilities for students, with modern amenities. The Kanya Chhatralaya, meanwhile, will house 2000 girls, irrespective of their economic criteria.

Concept note-3: -Inauguration. Prime Minister Narendra Modi arrived at the inauguration site in the C-130 Hercules and later touch and go exercise was also performed on the expressway by the Indian Air Force (IAF).

Concept note-4: -The project is developed by the Vishwa Patidar Samaj. About the project: The state-of-the-art Sardardham Bhavan provides separate hostel facilities to girls and boys from rural areas who aspire for better career prospects.
