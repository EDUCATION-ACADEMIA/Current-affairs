Who is the author of the book titled “The Terrible, Horrible, Very Bad Good News”?

(A) Sarita Mittal

(B) ** Meghna Pant

(C) Rashmi Desai

(D) Riya Gupta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Meghna Pant is an award-winning author, journalist, feminist and speaker. commercial and critical acclaim. Award, Frank O’Connor International Award and the Commonwealth Short Story Prize.

Concept note-2: -Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day | Book by Judith Viorst, Ray Cruz | Official Publisher Page | Simon & Schuster.

Concept note-3: -”’ In 1972, Judith Viorst wrote Alexander and the Terrible, Horrible, No Good, Very Bad Day thinking it would cheer up her son Alexander. “He didn’t think it was at all amusing, ” she recalls. By 1972, Viorst had already written a couple of children’s books.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the book put simply is that everyone has bad days, and it leaves you with the hopeful message that every bad day comes to an end, eventually. Because the author includes relatable topics, the reader can better appreciate the theme of the book.
