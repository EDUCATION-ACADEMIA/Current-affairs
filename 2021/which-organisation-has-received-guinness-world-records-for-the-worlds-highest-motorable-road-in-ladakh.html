Which organisation has received Guinness World Records for the world’s highest motorable road in Ladakh?

(A) DRO

(B) BOD

(C) ** BRO

(D) BRC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Border Roads Organisation has received the Guinness World Record for constructing & blacktopping the world highest motorable road passing through the 19, 024 feet 0.73 inches (5798.251m) high Umlingla Pass in the Union Territory of Ladakh.

Concept note-2: -The highest motorable road at Umlingla Pass is located at an altitude higher than Siachen Glacier. The Border Roads Organisation has constructed the road at an altitude of 19, 024 feet at Umling La Pass in Ladakh.

Concept note-3: -Director General of Border Roads Lt Gen Rajeev Chaudhry today received the Guinness World Records certificate for the achievement of Border Roads Organisation of constructing and black topping the world’s highest motorable road.

Concept note-4: -A virtual ceremony was organized in which the official adjudicator of the Guinness World Records based in the UK, Rishi Nath, acknowledged the remarkable achievement of BRO for constructing the highest altitude road in the world.

Concept note-5: -What is the record for? BRO received the Guinness World Records certificate for the 52-kilometre long Chisumle to Demchok tarmac road.
