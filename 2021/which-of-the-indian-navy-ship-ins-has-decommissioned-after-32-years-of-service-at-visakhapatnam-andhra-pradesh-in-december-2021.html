Which of the Indian Navy Ship (INS) has decommissioned after 32 years of service at Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh in December 2021?

(A) INS Vikrant

(B) ** INS Khukri

(C) INS Vijaydurg

(D) INS Surat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INS Khukri, the first of the indigenously built Missile Corvettes, was decommissioned after 32 years of glorious service. The Sun has finally set on INS Khukri (Pennant number 49), the first of the indigenously built missile corvettes, in Visakhapatnam on December 23 evening.

Concept note-2: -INS Ajay was decommissioned on 19 September 2022 after rendering 32 years of glorious service to the nation.

Concept note-3: -INS Ajay was decommissioned after rendering 32 years of service to the nation. The decommissioning of INS Ajay was conducted at Naval Dockyard, Mumbai in Traditional manner.

Concept note-4: -Indian Navy decommissions warships INS Nirbhik and INS Nirghat. The warships were decommissioned at a solemn ceremony at the Naval Dockyard yesterday, a Defence spokesperson said today.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is INS Nishank. The Indian Navy will bid farewell to its two ships INS Nishank and INS Akshay, at Naval Dockyard in Mumbai on 3 June 2022. Both the ships will be decommissioned at Naval Dockyard after 32 years of being at the forefront.
