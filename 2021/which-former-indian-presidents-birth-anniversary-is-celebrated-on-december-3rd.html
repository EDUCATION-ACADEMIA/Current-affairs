Which former Indian President’s birth anniversary is celebrated on December 3rd?

(A) V. V. Giri

(B) ** Rajendra Prasad

(C) K. R. Narayanan

(D) Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The recording of the lecture, is broadcast across the entire network of All lndia Radio on 3rd December every year to mark the birth anniversary of Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of India.

Concept note-2: -International Day of Persons with Disabilities (IDPD) is a UN day that is celebrated every year on 3 December.

Concept note-3: -Indian Council of Agricultural Research (ICAR) commemorates the birth anniversary of Dr. Rajendra Prasad, the first President of Republic of India, as Agriculture Education Day on 3 December every year.

Concept note-4: -When India became a republic in 1950, Prasad was elected as its first president by the Constituent Assembly. As president, Prasad established a tradition for non - partisanship and independence for the office-bearer and retired from Congress party politics.

Concept note-5: -Rajendra Prasad was awarded the Bharat Ratna, the nation’s highest civilian award in 1962. Rajendra Prasad was a scholar who has written 8 books during his lifetime.
