Miguel Mario Diaz-Canel has been appointed as the “First Secretary of the Communist Party” of which country?

(A) Puerto Rico

(B) Haiti

(C) Mexico

(D) ** Cuba

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Miguel Mario Díaz-Canel y Bermúdez (Spanish: [mi. ˈɣel ˈdi. as kaˈnel]; born 20 April 1960) is a politician and engineer who is the third first secretary of the Communist Party of Cuba . Díaz-Canel succeeds the brothers Fidel and Raúl Castro, making him the first non-Castro leader of Cuba since the revolution.

Concept note-2: -The President is the second-highest office in Cuba and the highest state office. Miguel Díaz-Canel became President of the Council of State on 19 April 2018, taking over from Raúl Castro, and has been President of Cuba since 10 October 2019.
