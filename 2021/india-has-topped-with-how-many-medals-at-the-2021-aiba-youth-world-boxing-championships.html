India has topped with how many medals at the 2021 AIBA Youth World Boxing Championships?

(A) ** 11

(B) 7

(C) 5

(D) 10

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India topped the medal table with eight gold and three bronze medals.

Concept note-2: -Sachin’s medal was India’s eighth gold and 11th medal overall after the women’s golden show in Poland. India’s Sachin won the men’s 56kg gold medal at the 2021 AIBA Youth World Boxing Championships on Friday to take India’s total medal haul at the event to 11.

Concept note-3: -Polish city Kielce staged the Youth World Boxing Championships in April 2021. More than 400 boxers aged 17, 18 and 19 from in excess of 50 countries traveled to Poland for the competition at the Hala Legionow Sports Complex.

Concept note-4: -Manipur’s young boxer Sanamacha Thokchom Chanu, who won gold medal at the 2021 Youth World Championships, also put up an impressive show to secure the title in the 70kg by beating Madhya Pradesh’s Shruti Yadav 3-2 in the final.
