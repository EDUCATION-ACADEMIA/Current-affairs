Who among the following personality was SpaceX’s first flight surgeon?

(A) Rakesh Menon

(B) ** Anil Menon

(C) Raja Jon Vurputoor Chari

(D) Sunita Williams

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Menon is a lieutenant colonel in the US air force and was SpaceX’s first flight surgeon. He served Nasa as the crew flight surgeon for various expeditions on the International Space Station (ISS).

Concept note-2: -Menon was SpaceX’s first flight surgeon, helping to launch the company’s first humans to space during NASA’s SpaceX Demo-2 mission.

Concept note-3: -To that end, NASA assigns a special doctor called a ‘Flight Surgeon’ to each crew once they are assigned to a mission. Flight surgeons oversee the health care and medical training when they are preparing for their mission and also take care of any medical issues that arise before, during, or after spaceflight.
