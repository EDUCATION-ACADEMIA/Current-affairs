The Indian Navy has received the 10th anti-submarine warfare aircraft P-8I. With which country had India signed the deal to procure the same?

(A) ** United States

(B) France

(C) Russia

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Navy received the 10th anti-submarine warfare aircraft P-8I from the US-based aerospace company Boeing. The Defence Ministry had signed a contract for eight P-8I aircraft in 2009.

Concept note-2: -Kolkata: Indian Navy has launched its first Anti-Submarine Warfare Shallow Water Craft (ASWSWC) on Tuesday at Kattupalli in Tamil Nadu which was built by the Garden Reach Shipbuilders and Engineers (GRSE) in Kolkata.

Concept note-3: -Q. Which firm supplied P-8I long range maritime patrol aircraft to Indian Navy? Notes: The Indian Navy received its 11th P-8I long range maritime patrol aircraft from Boeing. The Navy has signed a contract of 12 P-8Is from Boeing in two batches.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Navy has received the 12th anti-submarine warfare aircraft P-8I from the US-based aerospace company Boeing. This is the fourth of four additional aircraft, for which the contract was signed in 2016. The Ministry of Defence had signed a contract for eight P-8I aircraft in 2009.
