Who won the Men’s Single Badminton Title at Thailand Open?

(A) Prakash Padukone

(B) ** Viktor Axelsen

(C) Pullela Gopichand

(D) Jwala Gutta

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The champion of this WTA 250 event will be rewarded with 280 points as well as $34, 228 in prize money. The runner-up will also get a nice share, as they will be rewarded with 180 points and $20, 226.

Concept note-2: -The crowning achievement of Gopichand’s career came in March 2001 with his All England badminton championship win. He defeated China’s Chen Hong 15–12 and 15–6 in the final match, which lasted just 44 minutes.
