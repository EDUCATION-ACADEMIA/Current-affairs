Which nation has announced the boycott of the 2022 Winter Olympics in Beijing?

(A) Japan

(B) India

(C) ** US

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"The government has decided that we will not attend the Winter Olympics in China.” A U.S. boycott to protest against China’s human rights record has been joined by Australia, Britain, Canada and Japan.

Concept note-2: -India has announced a diplomatic boycott of the Beijing Winter Olympics. What does this mean?

Concept note-3: -The US, UK and Canada declared a diplomatic boycott, along with India, Australia, Lithuania, Kosovo, Belgium, Denmark and Estonia.

Concept note-4: -Last December, the Biden administration announced a diplomatic boycott of the Beijing 2022 Winter Olympic and Paralympic Games.
