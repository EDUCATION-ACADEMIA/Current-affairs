What is the theme of the 2021 World Bamboo Day?

(A) Keep Bamboo Strong

(B) Bamboo For Sustainable Development Goals (SDG) of United Nations

(C) ** PlantBamboo:It Is Time To Plant Bamboo

(D) Bamboo Now

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Bamboo Day 2021 theme was “#PlantBamboo - It’s time to plant bamboo.” The theme was developed after realizing that the awareness around the bamboo plant is spreading to more and more people through the efforts of the WBO. However, bamboo plantations are not growing at the rate they should.

Concept note-2: -This year the theme of World Bamboo Day 2022 is- Bamboo for Green Life and Sustainable Development.

Concept note-3: -World Bamboo Day is observed on September 18 in order to raise awareness about the conservation of this extremely useful plant. Conceptualised by the World Bamboo Organisation (WBO), this day also promotes the bamboo industry by highlighting its concerns.

Concept note-4: -Kamesh Salam founded World Bamboo Day. Salam served as president of the World Bamboo Organization from 2007 to 2010. In 2009, he organized the 8th World Bamboo Congress in Bangkok. During this meeting, he and delegates from over 100 countries declared September 18th as World Bamboo Day.

Concept note-5: -World Bamboo Organization Headquarters: Antwerp, Belgium. World Bamboo Organization Founded: 2005.
