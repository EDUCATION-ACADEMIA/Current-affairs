Who has won the gold medal in men’s individual compound archery in Archery World Cup?

(A) Sonu Malik

(B) ** Abhishek Verma

(C) Vinesh Phogat

(D) Sumit Roy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A one-point deficit had been turned into a three-point lead by India at 174-171. End 4: India now just needed a steady end to the match and they managed a 58 to France’s 59 to clinch the gold medal. “The feeling is good, ” said Verma after the final.

Concept note-2: -The Uttarakhand archer’s dream run finally ended in the final by none other than Dutch world number one Mike Schloesser (141-149). This was his first individual medal at the international stage.

Concept note-3: -This was Abhishek Verma’s third Archery World Cup gold medal this season. Abhishek Verma had won the men’s compound team events in Turkey and South Korea early in the year.

Concept note-4: -Kim Woojin stormed to 2022 Hyundai Archery World Cup Final victory with an irrepressible set of performances in Tlaxcala, Mexico.

Concept note-5: -Parth Salunke, in fact, was part of all three of India’s medals in recurve archery events. Parth teamed up with Mrinal Chauhan and Akash to win the men’s recurve team gold medal.
