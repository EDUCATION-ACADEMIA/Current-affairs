Kerala became which number state to passed the resolution against farm law?

(A) ** 5th

(B) 4th

(C) 6th

(D) 7th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Allied sectors. The farm plan-based approach will cover agriculture as well as allied sectors such as animal husbandry, dairy, and fisheries. The idea is to develop at least 25 farm units in every panchayat, according to the State plan for 2022-23, the first year of the 14th Five Year Plan.

Concept note-2: -Six state governments (Kerala, Punjab, Chhattisgarh, Rajasthan, Delhi and West Bengal) passed resolutions against the farms acts, and three states (Punjab, Chhattisgarh and Rajasthan) have tabled counter-legislation in their respective state assemblies.

Concept note-3: -President Trump signed the Farm Bill into law on December 20, 2018, and the U.S. Department of Agriculture (USDA) promptly began implementing key programs.
