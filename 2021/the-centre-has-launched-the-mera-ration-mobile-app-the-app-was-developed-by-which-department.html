The centre has launched the Mera Ration Mobile App. The app was developed by which department?

(A) IT Ministry

(B) DRDO

(C) NITI

(D) ** National Informatics Center

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mera Ration has been launched by the Ministry of Consumer Affairs, Food And Public Distribution and the App is a part of the Government’s One Nation One Ration Card (ONORC) system.

Concept note-2: -Mera Ration App 2022 Mera ration app is launched by the Indian govt on Friday, 12th of March 2021. To furnish services of “One Nation One Ration Card”, NIC (Nation Informatics Center) has designed and developed a mobile application named “Mera Ration App”.

Concept note-3: -Mera Ration App: The Food and Public Distribution department in association with NIC has launched a mobile app known as Mera ration App for ONORC beneficiaries.

Concept note-4: -In June, 1997, the Government of India launched the Targeted Public Distribution System (TPDS) with focus on the poor.

Concept note-5: -Go to the Gujarat Government official website. If you are not registered on this portal, make sure to register to download your application form. If you have already registered, simply fill in all login details and enter the portal. Select the “Revenue” button.
