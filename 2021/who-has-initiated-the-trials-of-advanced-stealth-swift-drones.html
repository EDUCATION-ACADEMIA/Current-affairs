Who has initiated the trials of advanced stealth SWiFT drones?

(A) ** DRDO

(B) DESTO

(C) DARPA

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to DRDO, the SWiFT UAV is intended to develop and demonstrate technologies for controlling the flying wing configuration and flight characteristics at high-subsonic speed. The ground trials of SWiFT UAV began in June 2021, picture and videos of it carrying out taxi trials surfaced on 29 October 2021.

Concept note-2: -The DRDO has developed the multi-copter with payload capacity of 5 kg to 25 kg and is working on increasing the capacity up to 30 kg, he said. The multi-copter can carry out autonomous missions with waypoint navigation up to 5 km radius.
