The team India archers settled for how many medals at the 2021 World Archery Championships?

(A) ** 3

(B) 5

(C) 6

(D) 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is 3. India archers won three silver medals at the 2021 World Archery Championships, held in Yankton, South Dakota, United States. The three silver medals were won in the Women’s Compound Individual, Women’s Compound Team, and Compound Mixed Team events.

Concept note-2: -Key Highlights: India won highest ever 15 medals atYouth World Archery Championship in Wroclaw (Poland) held from 9th to 15th August, 2021. 15 medals (8 Gold, 2 Silver and 5 Bronze medals) which is India’s best ever performance in the Youth World Archery Championship till date.

Concept note-3: -While the recurve contingent drew blank, Indian compound archers won three silver medals - in the women’s team, the mixed team and women’s individual events. Jyothi Vennam was part of all medal-winning feats.

Concept note-4: -Four of the five medals – one gold, one silver and three bronze - were won by the compound archers while the recurve contingent clinched just one bronze.

Concept note-5: -The Indians managed just one 10 in the second end as the French duo reduced the deficit to one-point. The third was a tie, while in the deciding fourth end, Abhishek and Jyothi held their nerves to edge out their opponents by two points to annexe the title.
