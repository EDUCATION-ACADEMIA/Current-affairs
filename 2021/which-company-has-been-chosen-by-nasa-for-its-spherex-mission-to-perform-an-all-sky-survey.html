Which company has been chosen by NASA for its SPHEREx Mission to perform an all-sky survey?

(A) Blue Origin

(B) ** SpaceX

(C) Boeing

(D) National Aeronautics and Space Administration

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -About the mission The Spectro-Photometer for the History of the Universe and Ices Explorer (SPHEREx) is a planned two-year mission that will survey the sky in optical as well as near-infrared light which, though not visible to the human eye, serves as a powerful tool for answering cosmic questions.

Concept note-2: -NASA has awarded a contract modification to SpaceX to further develop its Starship human landing system to meet agency requirements for long-term human exploration of the Moon under Artemis.

Concept note-3: -SpaceX now handles about two-thirds of NASA’s launches, including many research payloads, with flights as cheap as $62 million, roughly two-thirds the price of a rocket from United Launch Alliance, a competitor.

Concept note-4: -NASA on Wednesday announced it has awarded five more astronaut missions to Elon Musk’s SpaceX, with a contract worth an additional $1.4 billion to the company. The extra flights fall under NASA’s Commercial Crew program, which delivers astronauts and cargo to and from the International Space Station.
