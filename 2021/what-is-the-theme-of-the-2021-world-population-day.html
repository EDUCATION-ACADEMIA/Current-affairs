What is the theme of the 2021 World Population Day?

(A) Family Planning is a Human Right

(B) How to safeguard the health and rights of women and girls now

(C) ** The impact of the Covid-19 pandemic on fertility

(D) Empowering People, Developing Nations

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year’s World Population Day theme is A world of 8 billion: Towards a resilient future for all - Harnessing opportunities and ensuring rights and choices for all.

Concept note-2: -This 2022 year, World population day 2022 theme is “A world of 8 billion: Towards a resilient future for all - harnessing opportunities and ensuring rights and choices for all".

Concept note-3: -World Population Day is celebrated on July 11 every year across the world to raise awareness about the rising population. The event was established by the Governing Council of the . United Nations Development Programme in 1989. It seeks to focus on the urgency of population growth and its threats.

Concept note-4: -By resolution 45/216 of December 1990, the United Nations General Assembly decided to continue observing World Population Day to enhance awareness of population issues, including their relations to the environment and development. The Day was first marked on 11 July 1990 in more than 90 countries.

Concept note-5: -Global population projected to exceed 8 billion in 2022; half live in just seven countries. The world’s population will cross 8 billion in November, according to recently released projections from the United Nations. And more than half of all people live in just seven countries.
