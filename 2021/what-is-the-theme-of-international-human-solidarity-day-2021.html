What is the theme of International Human Solidarity Day 2021?

(A) ** Cultural equality, social justice

(B) Social development

(C) Eradicate poverty

(D) Promote human & social development

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The fund was set up in February 2003 as a trust of the United Nations Development Programme. The theme of International Human Solidarity remains unchanged every year. It aims to promote cultural equality and social justice for bringing human and social development, specifically in developing countries.

Concept note-2: -International Human Solidarity Day: Celebration International Human Solidarity Day 2022 theme is to promote solidarity among all and work together towards eliminating hunger on a global scale.

Concept note-3: -The theme is centered around promoting cultural equality, social justice that is directed towards human and social development (specifically in developing countries).

Concept note-4: -International Human Solidarity Day 2022: Theme It aims to advance social justice and cultural equality in order to advance human and social development, particularly in developing nations.

Concept note-5: -On December 20, 2002, the UN General Assembly introduced a World Solidarity Fund to help counter global poverty. This got included in the United Nation Development Programme (UNDP) trust fund in February 2003. To commemorate the above day, the UN declared December 20 as International Human Solidarity Day.
