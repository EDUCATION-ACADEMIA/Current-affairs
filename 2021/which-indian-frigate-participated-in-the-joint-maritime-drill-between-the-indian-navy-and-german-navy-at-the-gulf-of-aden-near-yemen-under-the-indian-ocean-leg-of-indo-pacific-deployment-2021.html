Which Indian frigate participated in the joint maritime drill between the Indian Navy and German Navy at the Gulf of Aden near Yemen under the Indian Ocean leg of Indo-Pacific Deployment 2021?

(A) ** Trikand

(B) Teg

(C) Talwar

(D) Trisul

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key Facts. The joint exercise included helicopter landings as well as search & seizure operations. Frigate ‘Trikand’ from Indian Navy exercised with German frigate called ‘Bayern’.

Concept note-2: -Last year in August, Navies of India and Germany carried out a joint exercise, which included helicopter landings and search and seizure operations, in Gulf of Aden near Yemen. The Indian Navy’s frigate Trikand exercised with German frigate Bayern in the Gulf of Aden.

Concept note-3: -Q. Indian Navy has undertaken a joint exercise with which country, in Gulf of Aden near Yemen? Notes: Navies of India and Germany carried out a joint exercise, in Gulf of Aden near Yemen.

Concept note-4: -Theatre Level Operational Readiness Exercise (TROPEX) is an inter-service military exercise involving the participation of the Indian Army, Air Force, Navy and the Coast Guard.

Concept note-5: -The participating Indian Naval Ships Shivalik and Kadmatt are the latest indigenously designed and built Guided Missile Stealth Frigate and Anti-Submarine Corvette respectively.
