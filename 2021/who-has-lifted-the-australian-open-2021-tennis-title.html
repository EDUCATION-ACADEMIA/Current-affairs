Who has lifted the Australian Open 2021 Tennis title?

(A) Daniil Medvedev

(B) ** Novak Djokovic

(C) Federico Gaio

(D) Aslan Karatsev

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Two-time defending champion Novak Djokovic defeated Daniil Medvedev in the final, 7–5, 6–2, 6–2 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2021 Australian Open. It was his record-extending ninth Australian Open title and his 18th major title overall.

Concept note-2: -Top seed Novak Djokovic defeated No. 4 Daniil Medvedev in straight sets to win his 18th Grand Slam singles championship and his ninth overall (and third consecutive) Australian Open, in a game that lasted an hour and 53 minutes.

Concept note-3: -Rafael Nadal defeated Daniil Medvedev in the final, 2–6, 6–7, 6–4, 6–4, 7–5 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 Australian Open.

Concept note-4: -Naomi Osaka defeated Jennifer Brady in the final, 6–4, 6–3 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2021 Australian Open.

Concept note-5: -As of 2023, Serbian tennis legend Novak Djokovic had won the most Australian Open men’s singles titles, with a total of 10. Meanwhile, Roger Federer, who retired in 2022, won six Australian Opens during his career.
