Who has been appointed as the new President of INTERPOL?

(A) Garba Baba UMAR

(B) Juan Carlos HERNANDEZ

(C) Michael A. HUGHES

(D) ** Ahmed Nasser AL-RAISI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Interpol said Mr. Al-Raisi was elected following three rounds of voting and received 68.9% of the votes cast in the final round. “It’s an honor to have been elected to serve as the next president of Interpol, ” the global police agency quoted Mr. Al-Raisi as saying.

Concept note-2: -NEW DELHI: Interpol president Ahmed Naser Al-Raisi on Tuesday reiterated his commitment to drive for a modern Interpol, to enhance transparency and to support collaboration between police organisations of member countries.

Concept note-3: -INTERPOL President Ahmed Naser Al-Raisi said, “As the world’s largest policing organization, it is the job of INTERPOL to make sure that all countries are supported. “Partnership and information sharing helps us to better tackle and prevent crimes.

Concept note-4: -Last year, during the General Assembly’s 89th ordinary session, INTERPOL announced the admission of the Federated States of Micronesia (“Micronesia”) as the 195th member of INTERPOL.

Concept note-5: -Jurgen Stock, the secretary general of Interpol, said the General Secretariat provides a range of expertise and services to member countries.
