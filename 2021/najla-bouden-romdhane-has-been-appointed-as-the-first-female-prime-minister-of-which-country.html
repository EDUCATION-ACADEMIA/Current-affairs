Najla Bouden Romdhane has been appointed as the first female Prime Minister of which country?

(A) Algeria

(B) ** Tunisia

(C) Turkey

(D) Syria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Najla Bouden (Arabic: ), also known as Najla Bouden Romdhane (Arabic: ; born 29 June 1958), is a Tunisian geologist and university professor who is serving as the Prime Minister of Tunisia.

Concept note-2: -44 people have held the office since 1759. The current prime minister is Najla Bouden since October 2021, as Tunisia’s first female prime minister.
