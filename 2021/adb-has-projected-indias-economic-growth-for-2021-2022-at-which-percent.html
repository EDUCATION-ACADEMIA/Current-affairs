ADB has projected India’s economic growth for 2021-2022 at which percent?

(A) 10.0%

(B) ** 9.7%

(C) 5.9%

(D) 8.9%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ADB’s 7 per cent growth projection for fiscal 2022-23 (April 2022 to March 2023), unchanged from its September forecast, compares to 8.7 per cent GDP growth in 2021-22.

Concept note-2: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has also lowered India’s gross domestic product (GDP) growth projection for 2022-23 to 6.8% from the earlier 7.4%, citing the impact of a slowing global economy, stubborn inflation, rising interest rates and the Ukraine war.

Concept note-3: -The growth forecast for 2023-34 is unchanged at 7.2 per cent, “sustained by positive effects from structural reform and from public investment catalysing private investment".

Concept note-4: -Real GDP growth is estimated at 7.0 per cent in 2022-23, according to the first advance estimate of the National Statistical Office (NSO).
