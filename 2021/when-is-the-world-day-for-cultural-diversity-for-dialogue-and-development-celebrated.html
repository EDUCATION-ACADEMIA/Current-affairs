When is the World Day for Cultural Diversity for Dialogue and Development celebrated?

(A) May 18

(B) May 20

(C) May 19

(D) ** May 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Day for Cultural Diversity for Dialogue and Development, also known as Diversity Day, is celebrated on May 21 every year. It is a day set aside to appreciate the cultural diversities between different countries, states, and people in the World.

Concept note-2: -Held every year on 21 May, the World Day for Cultural Diversity for Dialogue and Development celebrates not only the richness of the world’s cultures, but also the essential role of intercultural dialogue for achieving peace and sustainable development.

Concept note-3: -National days on Sun May 21st, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s World Meditation Day, International Tea Day, I Need A Patch For That Day, National Memo Day, Rapture Party Day… and much more!

Concept note-4: -In 2001, the UN Educational, Scientific and Cultural Organization (UNESCO) adopted the Universal Declaration on Cultural Diversity and in December 2002, the UN General Assembly, in its resolution 57/249, declared 21 May to be the World Day for Cultural Diversity for Dialogue and Development.
