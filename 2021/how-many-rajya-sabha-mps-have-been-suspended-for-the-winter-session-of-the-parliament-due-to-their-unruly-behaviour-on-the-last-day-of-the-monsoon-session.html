How many Rajya Sabha MPs have been suspended for the Winter session of the Parliament due to their unruly behaviour on the last day of the Monsoon session?

(A) 15

(B) 14

(C) 13

(D) ** 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rajya Sabha should consist of not more than 250 members - 238 members representing the States and Union Territories, and 12 members nominated by the President.

Concept note-2: -12 members can be nominated by the President. One-third of the members of the Rajya Sabha retire every two years.

Concept note-3: -The main reason for this retirement rule is to preserve the status of Rajya Sabha as a permanent house.

Concept note-4: -Members sit for staggered terms lasting six years, with about a third of the 238 designates up for election every two years, in even-numbered years.
