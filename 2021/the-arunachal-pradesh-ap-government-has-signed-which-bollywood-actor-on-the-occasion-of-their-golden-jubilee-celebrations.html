The Arunachal Pradesh (AP) Government has signed which Bollywood actor on the occasion of their golden jubilee celebrations?

(A) Aamir Khan

(B) Akshay Kumar

(C) ** Sanjay Dutt

(D) Salman Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Arunachal Pradesh. Actor Sanjay Dutt has been appointed as the brand ambassador for Arunachal Pradesh. The announcement was made on the occasion of the golden jubilee celebrations, marking the 50th year of the naming of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Sanjay Dutt is the brand ambassador of 50 Years of Arunachal Pradesh celebrations. By Anindita Mukherjee: Northeastern state, Arunachal Pradesh, came into being as a Union Territory (UT) on January 20, 1972.

Concept note-3: -Which state has signed Bollywood actor Sanjay Dutt as the brand ambassador for its Golden jubilee celebrations?

Concept note-4: -Star Sanjay Dutt has been roped in by the Arunachal Pradesh government to be the brand ambassador for the state.

Concept note-5: -New Delhi: Bollywood actor Sanjay Dutt, recently roped in as the brand ambassador for Arunachal Pradesh for its golden jubilee celebrations, took to Twitter on Thursday to share the breath-taking & maginificent beauty of the hilly state.
