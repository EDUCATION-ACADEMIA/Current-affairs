What is the estimated GDP growth rate of India as per S & P Global Ratings for the financial year 2021-22?

(A) 8.5%

(B) ** 9.8%

(C) 10.4%

(D) 11%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -S&P Lowers India GDP Growth Forecast by 30 bps to 7% For 2022-23. S&P Global Ratings cut India’s economic growth forecast for the current fiscal year ending March to 7 per cent as against 7.3 per cent projected earlier.

Concept note-2: -The growth in real GDP during 2022-23 is estimated at 7 per cent as compared to 8.7 per cent in 2021-22, it stated.

Concept note-3: -India’s output will expand 7 per cent in fiscal year 2022-2023 and 6 per cent in next fiscal year, ” S&P Global Ratings Asia-Pacific chief economist Louis Kuijs said. The Indian economy grew 8.5 per cent in 2021.

Concept note-4: -Full Year GDP Growth in India averaged 6.12 percent from 2006 until 2023, reaching an all time high of 8.70 percent in 2022 and a record low of -6.60 percent in 2021. This page includes a chart with historical data for India Full Year GDP Growth.

Concept note-5: -The growth in real GDP during 2022-23 is estimated at 7.0% as compared to 8.7% in 2021-22, ” the ministry said.
