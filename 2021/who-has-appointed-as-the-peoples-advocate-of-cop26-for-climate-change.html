Who has appointed as the People’s Advocate of COP26 for Climate Change?

(A) ** David Attenborough

(B) Greta Thunberg

(C) Leonardo DiCaprio

(D) Priyanka Chopra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Who has been named the COP 26 People’s Advocate? Notes: Conservationist, Broadcaster and historian Sir David Attenborough has been named as the People’s Advocate for the COP 26 of UN climate change summit to be held under the U.K. Presidency, in partnership with Italy. The summit is to be held this year in Glasgow.

Concept note-2: -Sir David Attenborough becomes COP26 People’s Advocate for the UK’s Presidency of the UN climate change summit in Glasgow this November. This appointment marks the pivotal moment of 6 months to go to COP26 – the most important climate meeting since the signing of the Paris Agreement in 2015.

Concept note-3: -Sir David Attenborough, COP26 People’s Advocate.

Concept note-4: -COP26 President Alok Sharma’s Remarks at the COP27 Closing Plenary - UN Climate Change Conference (COP26) at the SEC – Glasgow 2021.

Concept note-5: -Malala Yousafzai, Vanessa Nakate & Greta Thunberg Speak Out at COP26.
