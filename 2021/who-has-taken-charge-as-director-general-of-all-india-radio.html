Who has taken charge as Director General of All India Radio?

(A) Ravi Vijaykumar Malimath

(B) Vivek Ram Chaudhari

(C) Pushkar Singh Dhami

(D) ** N Venudhar Reddy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vasudha Gupta has been given additional charge of Director General of AIR. A 1989-batch Indian Information Service Officer, Dr.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is N Venudhar Reddy.

Concept note-3: -Vasudha Gupta, an officer from the 1989 batch, has worked for the Ministry of Information and Broadcasting for 32 years in various positions.

Concept note-4: -Lionel Fielden was appointed the first Controller of Broadcasting in August 1935. In the following month Akashvani Mysore, a private radio station was set up. On June 8, 1936, the Indian State Broadcasting Service became All India Radio. The Central News Organisation (CNO) came into existence in August, 1937.
