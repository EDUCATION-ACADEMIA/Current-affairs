Which player has won the 2021 WTA Finals Tennis title?

(A) Simona Halep

(B) Karolina Pliskova

(C) ** Garbine Muguruza

(D) Victoria Azarenka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Champion’s Reel: How Garbine Muguruza won the 2021 WTA Finals Guadalajara. Sixth seed Garbiñe Muguruza claimed her 10th career title, third of 2021 and third in Mexico at the Akron WTA Finals Guadalajara.

Concept note-2: -Garbiñe Muguruza’s 10th career WTA singles title proved to be a huge one: her first Akron WTA Finals championship. The No. 6 seed from Spain defeated No. 8 seed Anett Kontaveit of Estonia 6-3, 7-5 to capture her third and biggest title of the year Wednesday night in front of a excited crowd in Guadalajara.

Concept note-3: -Ashleigh Barty, Aryna Sabalenka and Barbora Krejčíková were announced as the first three qualifiers on September 20. Ashleigh Barty began 2021 by defeating Garbiñe Muguruza in the final of the Yarra Valley Classic, the Australian’s first tour event in 11 months.
