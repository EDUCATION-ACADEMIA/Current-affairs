Which country has developed the supercomputer named ‘Simorgh’?

(A) Japan

(B) ** Iran

(C) China

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All About Simorgh Supercomputer. Iran, in 2021, launched a domestically made supercomputer, Simorgh. It is the strongest supercomputer made by Iran to date. Developed by the engineers at Tehran’s Amirkabir University of Technology, Simorgh will be used for complex AI and ML-based processes data analysis.

Concept note-2: -The Simorgh supercomputer was created by Iranian scientists at the Amirkabir University of Technology in Tehran.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Simorgh. Iran has unveiled a supercomputer – its strongest to date – developed domestically by Tehran’s Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT). The supercomputer is named Simorgh after a mythical Persian bird.

Concept note-4: -The supercomputer has been developed indigenously by Tehran’s Amirkabir University of Technology (AUT). It has been named after a mythical Phoenix-like bird ‘Simurgh’.
