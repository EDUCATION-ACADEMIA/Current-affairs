How many Women Military Soldiers have been inducted into the Indian Army?

(A) ** 83 Women

(B) 56 Women

(C) 100 Women

(D) 120 Women

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women soldiers are also being inducted through the Agnipath entry scheme. The first batch of over 100 women Agniveers will join their training centre in Bengaluru in March 2023.

Concept note-2: -There are 9, 118 women currently serving the army, navy and air force.

Concept note-3: -Including Priya Jhingan and her, the first batch of women officers of the Indian Army was commissioned in March 1993.
