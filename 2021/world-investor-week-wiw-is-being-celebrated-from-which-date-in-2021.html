World Investor Week (WIW) is being celebrated from which date in 2021?

(A) ** October 4 to 10, 2021

(B) October 5 to 11, 2021

(C) October 6 to 12, 2021

(D) October 7 to 13, 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During October 4-10, 2021, the Securities and Exchange Commission, along with several other agencies, organizations and supporters, worked together to promote World Investor Week 2021 and its goals in the United States.

Concept note-2: -This year, WIW – 2022 is being celebrated in India from October 10 – 16, 2022. Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) is the national coordinator for celebrating WIW-2022 in India. Investors are invited to participate in various investor awareness activities like Quiz contest, Investor Awareness Programs, etc.

Concept note-3: -The key messages of the IOSCO WIW campaign in 2021 will be based on two themes: 1) sustainable finance and 2) frauds and scams prevention. The IOSCO WIW campaign continues to gain support among a growing number of jurisdictions, stakeholders and major international organizations.

Concept note-4: -World Investor Week is a global campaign promoted by the International Organization of Securities Commissions (IOSCO) to raise awareness about the importance of investor education and protection. For more information, visit our Office of Investor Education and Advocacy’s World Investor Week 2022 page.
