India’s first International Maritime Services is set to be established in which place?

(A) Nashik

(B) ** GIFT City

(C) Dhanbad

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Establishing a maritime cluster is a much-needed step towards advancing India’s credentials and economic viability in this sector. India’s first international maritime cluster will be located in GIFT City which will help in realising the full potential of India’s marine prowess.

Concept note-2: -India’s first commercial maritime cluster THE GUJARAT MARITIME CLUSTER IS A MODERN FOCAL POINT FOR THE GLOBAL MARITIME INDUSTRY.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat Maritime Board (GMB) will set up the country’s first international maritime services cluster at GIFT City. The maritime cluster will be developed as a dedicated ecosystem comprising ports, shipping, logistics services providers and government regulators, all present in the same geographic vicinity - GIFT City.

Concept note-4: -It is conceived as a dedicated ecosystem of Ports, Maritime Shipping and Logistics services providers.
