Which state has retained its top spot in the National Logistics Index-LEADS 2021?

(A) Assam

(B) Odisha

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rankings of LEADS Index 2021: Gujarat is ranked first and best-performing state among the 21 regions in the 2021 national transport index chart for ‘well-developed infrastructure and responsive government services. ‘

Concept note-2: -Notes: Union Commerce and industry Ministry released the Logistics Ease Across Different States (LEADS) Report 2021. Gujarat retained its top slot for the third consecutive year followed by Haryana and Punjab while Uttar Pradesh jumped seven slots from its previous 13th position.

Concept note-3: -Gujarat again ranked 1st in the LEADS 2021 Index among 21 states for its ‘proactive policies, well-developed infrastructure and services driven by a responsive government’. Gujarat retained its top position in the 3rd edition of the national logistics index similar to the previous two rankings in 2018 and 2019.

Concept note-4: -National logistics index 2021: Gujarat, Haryana and Punjab best performing states.
