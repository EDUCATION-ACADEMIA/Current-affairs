Who has been appointed as the chairperson of the Public Enterprises Selection Board?

(A) ** Mallika Srinivasan

(B) Naina Lal Kidwai

(C) Vinita Bali

(D) Lakshmi Venu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Former finance secretary Rajiv Kumar has been recently appointed as the chairperson of the Public Enterprises Selection Board (PESB), for a period of three years. He replaces Kapil Dev Tripathi who has recently named the Secretary to President Ram Nath Kovind.

Concept note-2: -Mallika Srinivasan, Chairman and Managing Director of Tractors and Farm Equipment (TAFE) Limited has been appointed as the chairperson of the Public Enterprises Selection Board (PESB), an order issued by the personnel ministry on Thursday said.

Concept note-3: -The Public Enterprises Selection Board [P.E.S.B] is a high powered body constituted by Government of India Resolution dated 3.3. 1987 which was subsequently amended from time-to-time, the latest being on 11.11. 2008.
