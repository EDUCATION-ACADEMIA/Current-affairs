Name the women mountaineer who has created a record of the world’s fastest ascent of Everest?

(A) Moni Mulepati

(B) ** Tsang Yin-hung

(C) Gerlinde Kaltenbrunner

(D) Oh Eun-sun

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -What helped her scale Mount Everest so fast? Tsang Yin-hung recounted that she had barely encountered any other climbers on her way to the highest camp at South Col and after that, she only ran into people who were making their descent, which did not impact her speeding scaling the mountain.

Concept note-2: -Hong Kong mountaineer, Tsang Yin-hung, recorded “world’s fastest ascent of Everest by a woman” in just 26 hours.

Concept note-3: -Fastest climber of Mount Everest with oxygen Pemba dorje shrepa was born on Beding Gaurisankar, Rolwaling Valley, Dolakha, Nepal. He is the fastest climber of Mount Everest on 21 May 2004. He reached Everest summit in 8 hours 10 minutes with oxygen.

Concept note-4: -Bachendri Pal, (born May 24, 1954, Nakuri, India), Indian mountaineer who in 1984 became the first Indian woman to reach the summit of Mount Everest.

Concept note-5: -Baljeet Kaur Indian Mountaineer break the record by completing the true summit of the eighth-highest peak in the World Mount Manaslu 8163M without Oxygen.
