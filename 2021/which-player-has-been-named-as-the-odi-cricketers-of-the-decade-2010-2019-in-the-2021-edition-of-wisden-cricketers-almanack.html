Which player has been named as the ODI cricketers of the decade 2010-2019, in the 2021 edition of Wisden Cricketers’ Almanack?

(A) M.S. Dhoni

(B) Steve Smith

(C) ** Virat Kohli

(D) Joe Root

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Virat Kohli. The Indian Cricketer, Virat Kohli has been named Wisden Almanack’s ODI player of the 2010s. In a ten-year period, he hit over 11, 000 runs, averaging over 60 and scoring 42 centuries.

Concept note-2: -Virat Kohli has been named Wisden Almanack’s ODI player of the 2010s, while England all-rounder Ben Stokes was declared the ‘Cricketer of the Year’ for a second successive time.

Concept note-3: -Virat Kohli got the first place in the list of the 50 best-performing cricketers of the last decade released by Wisden. Steve Smith got second place in the list of the 50 best-performing cricketers of the last decade released by Wisden.

Concept note-4: -India skipper Virat Kohli has been named Wisden Almanack’s ODI cricketer of the 2010s.
