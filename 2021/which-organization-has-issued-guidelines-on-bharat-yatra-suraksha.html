Which organization has issued guidelines on Bharat Yatra Suraksha?

(A) ESIC

(B) PFRDA

(C) ** IRDAI

(D) RBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India (Irdai) has come out with guidelines for a standard domestic travel insurance product, to be known as “Bharat Yatra Suraksha”.

Concept note-2: -International travel insurance This policy is designed in keeping with what customers – travelling internationally – would want.

Concept note-3: -There are two main types of travel insurance: trip protection plans and travel medical plans.

Concept note-4: -Types of Travel Insurance Travel insurance policies can broadly be categorized as Domestic Travel Insurance Policy and International Travel Insurance Policy. Most of the time, insurance companies offer a travel insurance policy for a single trip while booking the trip.
