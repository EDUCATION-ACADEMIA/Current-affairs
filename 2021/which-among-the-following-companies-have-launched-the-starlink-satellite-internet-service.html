Which among the following companies have launched the Starlink satellite internet service?

(A) Amazon

(B) Virgin Galactic

(C) Bharti Airtel

(D) ** SpaceX

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elon Musk’s Starlink satellite-based internet now in all seven continents.

Concept note-2: -Starlink is a satellite internet constellation operated by SpaceX, providing satellite Internet access coverage to 47 countries. It also aims for global mobile phone service after 2023. SpaceX started launching Starlink satellites in 2019.

Concept note-3: -Elon Musk’s SpaceX has built a global internet service with thousands of satellites in space. Starlink, operated by SpaceX, was launched in October 2020. The company said on Monday the service has more than one million active subscribers worldwide. This was up from 145, 000 users at the start of 2022.

Concept note-4: -In the years since, SpaceX has deployed thousands of Starlink satellites into the constellation across dozens of successful launches, the most recent of which took place on Oct. 27 and delivered another 53 satellites into low-Earth orbit.

Concept note-5: -SpaceX’s Starlink project started in 2014, with the first satellites launched in May 2019. Who founded Starlink? Starlink was started by SpaceX founders Elon Musk, Kimbal Musk, and Gwynne Shotwell.
