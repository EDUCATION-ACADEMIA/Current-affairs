(B) ** Reliance

(C) HAL

(D) DRDO

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -Billionaire Mukesh Ambani’s Reliance Industries Ltd has sourced, The world’s first consignment of ‘carbon-neutral oil’ from the US as it looks to become a net zero-carbon company by 2035.
