The police department of which state has launched the Mobile app named ‘Call Your Cop’?

(A) ** Nagaland

(B) Ladakh

(C) Gujarat

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The Nagaland Police recently launched ‘Call your Cop’ mobile App, which will enable all citizens of the state and those in distress to get in touch with the police, in a single click.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Call Your Cop. Nagaland DGP T. John Longkumer officially launched the ‘Call your Cop’ mobile App on 29 November 2021 at Police Headquarters in Kohima.

Concept note-3: -It will enable all citizens in the state, including those in distress, to get in touch with the police directly, as police will be available easily, with just one click away. This initiative is also in line with the total modernization of police department across the country.

Concept note-4: -Ltd said that ‘Call Your Cop’ mobile app is simple and robust, which will have the contact numbers of all Nagaland Police officials. It features directory, alerts, tourist tips, SOS and emergency numbers, nearest police station etc.
