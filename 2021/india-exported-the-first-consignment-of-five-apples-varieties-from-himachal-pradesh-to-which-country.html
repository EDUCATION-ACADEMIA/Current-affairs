India exported the first consignment of five apples varieties from Himachal Pradesh to which country?

(A) United Kingdom

(B) Netherlands

(C) ** Bahrain

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -APEDA exports five varieties of apples to Bahrain - Read facts for UPSC current affairs! - GKToday.

Concept note-2: -The apples are sourced from the farmers of Himachal Pradesh and exported by APEDA registered DM Enterprises.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Apples. UK has exported apples from England to India, for the first time in 50 years, as a sign of the UK-India Enhanced Trade Partnership in action.

Concept note-4: -Notes: The United Kingdom has exported apples to India for the first time in over 50 years. This is seen as a sign of the UK-India Enhanced Trade Partnership. The Enhanced Trade Partnership was agreed by British Prime Minister Boris Johnson and Prime Minister Narendra Modi during their virtual summit in May.
