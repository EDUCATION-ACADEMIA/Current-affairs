Prime Minister Narendra Modi has announced the Swarna Jayanti Scholarship for which country’s youths?

(A) ** Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) Bhutan

(D) Tajikistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He has announced the ‘Swarna Jayanti’ scholarships for the youth of Bangladesh to mark half a century ling ties between the two countries.

Concept note-2: -Govt. of India had instituted a scheme “The Swarnajayanti Fellowships” to commemorate India’s fiftieth year of Independence.

Concept note-3: -PM Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) 2023 Rewards A total of 5, 500 students are awarded annually under PMSS 2023 scheme. The students will get benefits of INR 2, 500 per month for the boys and INR 3000 per month for the girls. Auunally INR36, 000 to each girl and 30, 000 to each boy.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister Scholarship For 12th: This Scholarship Scheme is launched to encourage higher, technical, and professional education for the wards of widows and Ex-servicemen of the Armed Forces after 12th Passes Students. The Government of India is pleased to announce the Modi Scholarship 2023 for 12th.
