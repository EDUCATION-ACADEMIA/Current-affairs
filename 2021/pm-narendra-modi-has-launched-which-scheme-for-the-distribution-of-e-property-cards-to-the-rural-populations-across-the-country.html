PM Narendra Modi has launched which scheme for the distribution of e-property cards to the rural populations across the country?

(A) Samridhi

(B) Adarsh Gram

(C) Kaushal

(D) ** Svamitva

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Svamitva Scheme Framework (2021-2025)

Concept note-2: -To bring financial stability to the citizens in rural India by enabling them to use their property as a financial asset for taking loans and other financial benefits. Creation of accurate land records for rural planning.

Concept note-3: -Property cards or ownership certificates provide information about the ownership of a property and history of holdings of land.

Concept note-4: -Benefits of Swamvita Yojana Proof of ownership of property. Facilitates buying and selling of property without any disputes. Beneficiaries can avail loans from banks using their properties as an asset. Accurate land records will be created in every village that will facilitate better rural planning.

Concept note-5: -In 2021-22, a budget provision of Rs 200 crores has been made for a SVAMITVA scheme targeting 2.30 lakh villages. The pilot phase of SVAMITVA had been approved with a budget outlay of Rs 79.65 crore.
