What is the theme of World Arthritis Day 2021?

(A) Don’t Late, Connect Today:Time2Work

(B) Don’t Be late, Connect Today:Time2Work

(C) ** Don’t Delay, Connect Today:Time2Work

(D) None

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On this day, we strive to raise awareness and share information and support with RMD patients and their caregivers. This year’s theme, “Don’t Delay, Connect Today, ” initiated by EULAR, aims to highlight the importance of early diagnosis of RMDs.

Concept note-2: -World Arthritis Day 2022 Theme The theme “It’s in your hands, take action” aims to encourage people with arthritis, their caregivers, families, and the general public to avail every opportunity to take action to improve their lifestyle.

Concept note-3: -The theme for the World Arthritis Day 2022 is ‘It’s in your hand, take action.

Concept note-4: -World Arthritis Day, referred to as WAD, is a global awareness day held every year on 12 October.

Concept note-5: -The theme for World Arthritis day 2022 is “It’s in your hands, take action". It hopes to seek attention of the masses for this cause to make this useful and helpful for the ones deprived of the support.
