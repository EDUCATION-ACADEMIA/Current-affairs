Who has been appointed as the additional director of at Britannia Industries board?

(A) Shaktikanta Das

(B) Raghuram Rajan

(C) ** Urjit Patel

(D) Viral Acharya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Dr. Urjit Patel .

Concept note-2: -Britannia Industries Limited on Friday announced the appointment of Varun Berry as Executive Vice-Chairman and Managing Director of Britannia Industries Limited with immediate effect. The FMCG major also appointed Rajneet Kohli as Executive Director and Chief Executive Officer.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Urjit Patel. Former Reserve Bank of India (RBI) Governor Urjit Patel has resigned from the post of non-executive and independent Director of Britannia Industries Limited. His appointment was for a period of five years, which would have ended in 2026.

Concept note-4: -He is currently serving as the Vice President of the Asian Infrastructure Investment Bank (AIIB), a multinational development investment bank based out of Beijing. India is the largest lender of the AIIB.
