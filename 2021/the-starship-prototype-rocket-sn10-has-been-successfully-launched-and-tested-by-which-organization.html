The Starship prototype rocket SN10 has been successfully launched and tested by which organization?

(A) European Space Agency

(B) Boeing

(C) ** SpaceX

(D) NASA

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SpaceX Starship SN10 launch: How to watch rocket prototype fly today.

Concept note-2: -The upcoming test flight will lift off from Starbase. Ship 24 will travel around Earth once and then splash down in the Pacific Ocean off the Hawaiian island of Kauai.

Concept note-3: -Starship prototypes SN12, SN13, and SN14 were scrapped before completion; SN15 was selected to fly instead. SN15 had better avionics, structure, and engines. On 5 May 2021, SN15 launched, completed the same maneuvers as older prototypes, and landed softly after six minutes.

SpaceX was formed by entrepreneur Elon Musk in the hopes of revolutionizing the aerospace industry and making affordable spaceflight a reality.
