Which organisation has successfully flight-tested the New Generation Akash Missile (Akash-NG)?

(A) ISRO

(B) BSF

(C) ** DRDO

(D) BEL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DRDO conducts successful flight test of Akash-NG.

Concept note-2: -The Defence Research and Development Organisation (DRDO) has successfully flight-tested the latest Akash surface-to-air missile system.

Concept note-3: -2nd Test : Defence Research and Development Organisation and Indian Air Force successfully conducted the 2nd test of the missile on 21 July 2021 from Launching Complex - III at the Integrated Test Range near Chandipur, Odisha at about 12:45 PM against an electronic target.

Concept note-4: -New generation ballistic missile ‘Agni P’ successfully test-fired by DRDO | Defence Research and Development Organisation - DRDO, Ministry of Defence, Government of India.

Concept note-5: -On April 27, 2022, akash prime, the advance version of akash missile, was successfully test-fired at pokhran field firing range in the border district of jaisalmer.
