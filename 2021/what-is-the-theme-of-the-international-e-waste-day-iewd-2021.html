What is the theme of the International E-Waste Day (IEWD) 2021?

(A) ** Consumer is the key to Circular Economy!

(B) Understand Your E-Waste

(C) Education

(D) Time of Recycle

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International E-Waste Day is held on October 14 every year since 2018 as an opportunity to reflect on the impacts of e-waste. This year’s theme is ‘Recycle it all, no matter how small’.

Concept note-2: -2022 Theme: Recycle it all, no matter how small! In 2022, the focus of International E-Waste Day will be on small items of e-waste, under the slogan ‘Recycle it all, no matter how small!

Concept note-3: -“Recycle it all, no matter how small!” The theme of the International E-waste Day 2022 highlights the importance of recycling even the smallest of e-waste.

Concept note-4: -Electronic waste or e-waste in China refers to electronic products that are no longer usable and are therefore dumped or recycled. China is the world’s largest importer and producer of electronic waste with over 70% of all global e-waste ending up in the world’s largest dumpsites.
