Which day is observed as the National Protein Day in India?

(A) 25 February

(B) ** 27 February

(C) 28 February

(D) 26 February

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Protein Day is an annual awareness day observed on February 27th, that aims to rally towards the mission of protein awareness and sufficiency in India.

Concept note-2: -National Protein Day, on February 27, focuses on the health benefits of one of the body’s most basic building blocks.

Concept note-3: -February 27 is observed as National Protein Day. ‘Food Futurism’ is the theme for the year 2022, declared by Right to Protein, a national-level public health initiative. The first National Protein Day was observed in 2020.

Concept note-4: -National Protein Day 2022: Theme The theme is aimed at bringing food scientists, nutrition experts and biologists to discuss food science and its role in protein sufficiency and food security in India.

Concept note-5: -In India, February 27th is celebrated as the National Protein Day, to create awareness about protein deficiency and encourage people to include of this macronutrient in their diet. The day urges people to include this crucial macronutrient in their diet.
