Ahmed Nasir Al Raisi of UAE has been appointed as the new chief of which police organization?

(A) CBI

(B) CARFT

(C) ** Interpol

(D) CAPF

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INTERPOL’s current President is Major General Ahmed Naser Al-Raisi, of the United Arab Emirates. Mr Al-Raisi was elected at the 89th General Assembly in Istanbul in November 2021 and will serve as President until 2025.

Concept note-2: -Leadership of Interpol Ahmed Nasser Al Raisi of the United Arab Emirates (UAE) is the president of Interpol.

Concept note-3: -Ahmed Naser Al-Raisi (also romanised as Ahmed Nasser Al-Raisi) is an Emirati military general officer. He currently serves as the 30th president of Interpol and the inspector general of the United Arab Emirates’ interior ministry.

Concept note-4: -Union Home Minister, Shri Amit Shah, Interpol President Mr Ahmed Naser Al Rais, Interpol Secretary General, Mr Jurgen Stock, and CBI Director, Shri Subodh Kumar Jaiswal were those present on the occasion.
