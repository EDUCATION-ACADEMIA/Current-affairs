Which city has topped the 2021 Mercer’s Cost of Living Survey?

(A) New York

(B) Dubai

(C) ** Ashgabat

(D) HongKong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mercer’s 2021 Key India Findings Mumbai (78) occupied its position in the top spot as the most expensive in Indian cities surveyed, followed by New Delhi (117) and Chennai (158), while Bengaluru (170) and Kolkata (181) are the least expensive Indian cities in ranking.

Concept note-2: -Tel Aviv is the world’s most expensive cities. It was followed by Paris in second place and Singapore in third place. In 2020, Paris was the most expensive city according to the index. In 2021, Zurich and Hong Kong were at fourth and fifth places respectively.

Concept note-3: -Hong Kong tops Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living City Ranking, based on our latest research, followed by Zurich and Geneva, while Ankara closes the ranking at the 227th spot.
