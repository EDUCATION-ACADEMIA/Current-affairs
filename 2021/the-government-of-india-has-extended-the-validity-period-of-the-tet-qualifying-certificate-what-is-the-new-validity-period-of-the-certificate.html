The Government of India has extended the validity period of the TET qualifying certificate. What is the new validity period of the certificate?

(A) ** Lifetime

(B) 15 Years

(C) 20 Years

(D) 10 Years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -TET Certificate Validity Extended to Lifetime from 7 Years: Education Minister. CTET & TET Certificate Lifetime Validity 2022: Ministry of Education has finalised the notice for the extension of the validity of the TET Certificate to lifetime. Earlier, the Certificate of CTET was valid for up to 7 years.

Concept note-2: -The guidelines dated February 11, 2011 of the National Council for Teacher Education (NCTE) laid down that TET would be conducted by the state governments and the validity of the TET certificates was seven years from the date of passing TET.

Concept note-3: -UPTET certificate Validity has been extended from 5 Years to a lifetime.

Concept note-4: -The CTET qualified certificate for appointment will be valid for seven years from the date of its result declaration in all categories.

Concept note-5: -The government has approved some amendments made by the Committee of Ministers recently regarding TET. In the past, the validity period for TET was seven years. It has now been extended for a lifetime.
