The National Research Centre on Yak is located in which state?

(A) Shimla

(B) Dehradun

(C) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(D) Ladakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICAR-National Research Centre on Yak is a premier research organization exclusively engaged in research and development of yak (Poephagus grunniens L.) in India. The institute was established in 1989 by the Indian Council of Agricultural Research at Dirang, West Kameng district of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -National Research Centres - 15.

Concept note-3: -There is a total of 15 National research centres in India, which are listed below: ICAR-National Research Center for Banana Located at Tiruchirappalli.

Concept note-4: -The current director-in-charge of NBRC is Prof. Pravat Kumar Mandal.

Concept note-5: -Indian Council for Agricultural Research – Overview. ICAR is an autonomous organisation under the Department of Agricultural Research and Education (DARE), Ministry of Agriculture and Farmers Welfare, Government of India. It was established on 16 July 1929.
