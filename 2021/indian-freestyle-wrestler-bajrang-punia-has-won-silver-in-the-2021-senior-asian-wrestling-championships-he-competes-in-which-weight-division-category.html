Indian freestyle wrestler Bajrang Punia has won Silver in the 2021 Senior Asian Wrestling Championships. He competes in which weight division category?

(A) 95 kg

(B) ** 65 kg

(C) 70 kg

(D) 80 kg

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -He competes in the 65 kg weight class, and this year he won the bronze medal at the Tokyo Olympics.

Concept note-2: -Indian wrestler Bajrang Punia won a bronze medal in the men’s 65kg on the final day of the World Wrestling Championships 2022 in Belgrade, Serbia on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -The celebrated Indian wrestler had earlier won a bronze at in 2013, silver in 2018 and bronze in 2019.

Concept note-4: -Bajrang Punia won a bronze medal and became the only Indian to win four medals at the World Wrestling Championships. The wrestler captured bronze in the men’s 65 kg category.

Concept note-5: -Bajrang Punia (born 26 February 1994) is an Indian freestyle wrestler, who competes in the 65-kg weight category. At the 2020 Tokyo Olympics, Punia won a bronze medal by defeating Kazakhstan’s Daulet Niyazbekov with a wide 8-0 margin.
