Who has been appointed as chairman of Capacity Building Commission/Mission Karmayogi?

(A) Praveen Pardeshi

(B) ** Adil Zainulbhai

(C) Taraknath Banerjee

(D) Shivam Dubey

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Praveen Pardeshi With 29 years of experience in the Indian Administrative Services (IAS), Mr. Pardeshi has served in various senior executive roles.

Concept note-2: -National Programme for Civil Services Capacity Building (NPCSCB) - Mission Karmayogi aims to create a competent civil service rooted in Indian ethos, with a shared understanding of India’s priorities, working in harmonisation for effective and efficient public service delivery.

Concept note-3: -iGOT Karmayogi is an online learning platform being developed as an integral part of the Digital India stack for capacity building of all government employees. It will provide ‘anytime-anywhere-any device’ learning to train around 2.0 crores users which was so far unachievable through traditional measures.

Concept note-4: -Hemang Jani - Secretary - Capacity Building Commission | LinkedIn.
