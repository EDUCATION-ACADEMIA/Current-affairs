North India’s first Dog Park is set to be established in which city?

(A) New Delhi

(B) Lucknow

(C) Rohtak

(D) ** Chandigarh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Chandigarh. North India’s 1st Dog park to come up in Chandigarh’s Sector 42. The Finance and Contract Committee (F&CC) of the MC has approved the creation of the park within the Mango Park in Sector 42. It will cost ₹49-lakh and project will come up on 1.5-acre land.

Concept note-2: -By Ram Kinkar Singh: North Delhi Municipal Corporation (NDMC)has inaugurated Delhi’s first dog park in Karol Bagh Zone at Old Rajinder Nagar.

Concept note-3: -Yari Road Pet Park is the first active pet park in Maharashtra. Pets now get to play in a safe, secure and clean environment in a space dedicated for them courtesy of the BMC.

Concept note-4: -LUCKNOW: Good news for the pooch lovers, the Lucknow Development Authority is going to develop the state’s first dog park in CG city, which will have all the necessary facilities for the pet dogs. On Monday, LDA issued a tender for its development.

Concept note-5: -It has been equipped with amenities like dog training and exercise equipment, two lawns, an amphitheatre, loo cafe, splash pool, separate enclosures for big and small dogs, and so forth. As of now, there are only two dog parks in India–one in Mumbai and another in Hyderabad.
