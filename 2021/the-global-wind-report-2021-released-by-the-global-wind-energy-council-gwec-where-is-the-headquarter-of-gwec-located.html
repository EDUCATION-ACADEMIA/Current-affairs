The ‘Global Wind Report 2021, released by the Global Wind Energy Council (GWEC). Where is the headquarter of GWEC located?

(A) London, United Kingdom

(B) Paris, France

(C) ** Brussels, Belgium

(D) Vienna, Austria

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s headquarters are located in Belgium.

Concept note-2: -Where is Global Wind Energy Council ‘s headquarters? Global Wind Energy Council is located in Brussels, Brussels Hoofdstedelijk Gewest, Belgium .

Concept note-3: -The 1, 500MW Muppandal wind farm is the biggest onshore wind farm in India. It consists of a group of wind farms in the Kanyakumari district of the Indian state of Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-4: -UPSC Mains Q. Which organisation released the ‘Global Wind Report 2022’? Notes: Global Wind Energy Council released its flagship Global Wind Report 2022. The report says that 93.6 gigawatts of capacity was installed last year, a little less than the 95.3 GW installed in 2020.

Concept note-5: -Tamil Nadu is a leader in Wind Power in India. The largest capacity wind turbine of 4.2 MW is installed in Tamil Nadu state as of October 2022. In Muppandal windfarm, the total capacity is 1500 MW with nearly 3000 wind turbines, the largest wind power plant in India.
