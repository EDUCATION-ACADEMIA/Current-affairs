The digital platform ‘e-Source, ‘ is being developed by which institution to tackle the problem of e-waste?

(A) IIT Kanpur

(B) IIT Delhi

(C) IIT Hyderabad

(D) ** IIT Madras

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Institution of Technology Madras (IIT-M) is developing an online platform named e-Source. This platform aims to tackle e-waste by linking various participants in the formal and informal sectors.

Concept note-2: -Called e-Source, this initiative is being spearheaded by Indo-German Centre for Sustainability (IGCS). The IGCS team believes that the problem of e-waste could be resolved by connecting different buyers and sellers of Used and Waste electronic equipment and components without compromising their interests.

Concept note-3: -IIT Madras is in process of developing an online platform called e-Source that will be used to tackle electronic wastes (e-waste). What is the need of e-Source initiative?

Concept note-4: -About the ‘e-Source’ platform: The initiative is being led by the Indo-German Centre for Sustainability (IGCS), a centre set up at IIT Madras in 2010 at the initiative of the German and Indian governments.
