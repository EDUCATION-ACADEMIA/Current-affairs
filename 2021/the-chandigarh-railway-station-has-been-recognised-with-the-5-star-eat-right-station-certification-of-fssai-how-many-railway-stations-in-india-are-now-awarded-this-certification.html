The Chandigarh Railway Station has been recognised with the 5-star ‘Eat Right Station’ certification of FSSAI. How many Railway stations in India are now awarded this certification?

(A) ** 5

(B) 3

(C) 4

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The certification is part of the ‘Eat Right India’ movement- a large-scale effort by FSSAI to transform the country’s food system to ensure safe, healthy and sustainable food for all Indians. Chandigarh Railway Station becomes the fifth station in India to get this recognition.

Concept note-2: -The other railway stations with this certification include Anand Vihar Terminal Railway Station; (Delhi), Chhatrapati Shivaji Terminus; (Mumbai), Mumbai Central Railway Station; (Mumbai), Vadodara Railway Station, Chandigarh Railway Station.

Concept note-3: -The validity of this certificate is two years i.e., till 19 July 2024.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is the Habibganj railway station. Habibganj railway station is a railway station in Bhopal City and part of the West Central Railway.
