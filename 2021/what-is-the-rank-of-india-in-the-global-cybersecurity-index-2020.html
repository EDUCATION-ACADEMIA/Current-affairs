What is the rank of India in the Global Cybersecurity Index 2020?

(A) ** 10

(B) 37

(C) 52

(D) 29

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India was ranked 10th in the Global Cybersecurity Index [GCI] for 2020 by scoring 97.5 points out of 100.

Concept note-2: -In 2020, the United States ranked first in the Global Cybersecurity Index (GCI) with a score of 100 index points. Among the countries with the highest commitment to cyber security, the United Kingdom and Saudi Arabia shared second place with a GCI score of 99.54 for each.

Concept note-3: -1. India now ranks second only to the US in most security threats on Cloud, followed by Australia, Canada and Brazil. 2. India saw a 64% increase in publicly reported cyber incidents.

Concept note-4: -Global Cybersecurity Outlook 2022: Context Recently, World Economic Forum has released a first every report on cybersecurity and presented findings on how to shift from cybersecurity to cyber resilience.
