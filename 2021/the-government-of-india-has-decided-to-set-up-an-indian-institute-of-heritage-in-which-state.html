The Government of India has decided to set up an Indian Institute of Heritage in which state?

(A) Odisha

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Haryana

(D) Gujarat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government has decided to set up the Indian Institute of Heritage in Uttar Pradesh’s Noida which will provide higher education and research on country’s heritage and conservation, the government said on Thursday.

Concept note-2: -Kishan Reddy in Rajya Sabha today. Government have decided to set up ‘Indian Institute of Heritage’ (IIH) as a Deemed to be University as per UGC (Institutions deemed to be Universities) Regulations, 2019 at Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar, Uttar Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -The Union Government has decided to set up the ‘Indian Institute of Heritage’ at Noida, Gautam Buddha Nagar. This will impact higher education and research in the field related to rich Indian heritage and its conservation leading to Masters and Ph.

Concept note-4: -Heritage of India is divided into two types: Natural and Cultural. This division is applicable to all the countries. Natural heritage includes forests, rivers, and animals; Cultural heritage includes temples and paintings.

Concept note-5: -The Archaeological Survey of India (ASI) is an Indian government agency that is responsible for archaeological research and the conservation and preservation of cultural historical monuments in the country.
