What is the theme of World Standards Day 2021?

(A) Video standards create a global stage

(B) Protecting the planet with standards

(C) International Standards and the Fourth Industrial Revolution

(D) ** Standards for sustainable development goals-shared vision for a better world

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) represent a shared vision for peace and prosperity, for people and planet. Every SDG is a call for action, but we can only get there if we work together.

Concept note-2: -The theme for World Standards Day 2022 is ‘Shared Vision for a Better World. The theme aims at increasing the understanding of how standardization is significant in achieving the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals.

Concept note-3: -2022 Theme: Shared Vision for a Better World.

Concept note-4: -On 14 October 2022, CEN and CENELEC join the international standardization community in celebrating World Standards Day. Under the motto “Shared visions for a better world”, the event is an opportunity to showcase the many ways in which international standards can contribute to building a sustainable future.

Concept note-5: -World Standards Day is marked every year on October 14 to raise awareness about the importance of using standardised measurements, technologies, and industries. This day, also known as International Standards Day, strives to educate consumers, policymakers, and businesses about the value of standardisation.
