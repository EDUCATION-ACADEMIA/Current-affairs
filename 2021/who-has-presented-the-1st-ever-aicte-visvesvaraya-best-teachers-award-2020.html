Who has presented the 1st ever “AICTE Visvesvaraya Best Teachers Award 2020”?

(A) Nirmala Sitaraman

(B) Nitin Gadkari

(C) Harsh Vardhan

(D) ** Ramesh Pokhriyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mr Ramesh Pokhriyal ‘Nishank’ Union Education Minister presented the award to Dr Malathy R along with 11 other teachers of AICTE approved institutes.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Visvesvaraya Best Teachers Award.

Concept note-3: -Surekha Jagannath is a science teacher who has brought in many innovations into her school. She has created over a hundred innovations and experiments in science teaching which promote personalized learning, development of curiosity and creativity in students.

Concept note-4: -Visvesvaraya Teachers Award is to celebrate the unique contribution of some of the finest teachers at the institutes and to honour those teachers who through their commitment and industry have not only improved the quality of school education but also enriched the lives of their students.

Concept note-5: -The awards were conferred by President Ram Nath Kovind on the occasion of Teacher’s Day on September 5, 2020. 47 teachers have been selected by the government for ‘National Awards to Teachers 2020.
