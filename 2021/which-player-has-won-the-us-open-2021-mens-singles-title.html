Which player has won the US Open 2021 men’s singles title?

(A) Novak Djokovic

(B) Alexander Zverev

(C) ** Daniil Medvedev

(D) Roger Federer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Daniil Medvedev defeated Novak Djokovic in the final, 6–4, 6–4, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2021 US Open.

Concept note-2: -Carlos Alcaraz defeated Casper Ruud in the final, 6–4, 2–6, 7–6, 6–3 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2022 US Open.

Concept note-3: -Daniil Medvedev won the men’s singles title. Emma Raducanu won the women’s singles title, becoming the first qualifier, male or female, to reach a major final and win a major title. Dominic Thiem and Naomi Osaka were the men’s and women’s singles defending champions.

Concept note-4: -Emma Raducanu defeated Leylah Fernandez in the final, 6–4, 6–3 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2021 US Open.
