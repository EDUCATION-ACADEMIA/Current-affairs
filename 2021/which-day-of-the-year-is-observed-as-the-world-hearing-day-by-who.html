Which day of the year is observed as the World Hearing Day by WHO?

(A) March 2

(B) First Tuesday of March

(C) First Wednesday of March

(D) ** March 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Hearing Day is held on 3 March each year to raise awareness on how to prevent deafness and hearing loss and promote ear and hearing care across the world.

Concept note-2: -World Hearing Day 2022: History In 2007, WHO observed World Hearing Day for the first time. The day was earlier known as International Ear Care Day. After 2016, WHO changed its name to World Hearing Day. WHO selects a theme every year and develops educational materials.

Concept note-3: -It was a global call for action to address hearing loss and ear diseases across the life course. [2] The WRH was developed to provide guidance for WHO Member States to integrate ear and hearing care into their national health plan per the World Health Assembly resolution WHA70. 13, adopted in 2017.
