The Indian Army has laid the foundation of the national flag in J-K’s Gulmarg. What is the height of that flag-pole?

(A) 180 foot-high

(B) ** 100 foot-high

(C) 150 foot-high

(D) 90 foot-high

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The tallest national flag was hoisted in Jammu and Kashmir by the Indian Army on Tuesday, just five days ahead of the 75th Independence Day. The 100-metre-high flag was hoisted at Gulmarg, a famous tourist destination of the valley.

Concept note-2: -Army Installs 100-ft-high Tricolor In Jammu & Kashmir’s Kishtwar, Dedicates It To Residents. The Army has installed a 100-ft high national high in Jammu & Kashmir which is aimed at instilling pride among the residents of the union territory. Indian Flag on a Pole.

Concept note-3: -The project is undertaken by civil administration. The 108 feet monumental National Flag is the one of the tallest flag ever installed in entire North Kashmir’s Kupwara region.

Concept note-4: -The tallest flag pole for flying India’s national flag is in Belagavi, Karnataka - 361 ft. The 2nd tallest is in Attari-Wagha border - 360 ft. The 3rd tallest is in Ranchi, Jharkhand - 293 ft.

Concept note-5: -The second tallest flag can be witnessed in Kolhapur which was inaugurated by CM Devendra Fadnavis. This Indian flag is 303 foot flag which is located just opposite the SP office of Kolhapur and was inaugurated on 1st of May in 2017.
