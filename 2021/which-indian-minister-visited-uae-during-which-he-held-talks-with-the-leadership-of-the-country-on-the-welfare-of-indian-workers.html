Which Indian minister visited UAE during which he held talks with the leadership of the country on the welfare of Indian workers?

(A) Shripad Yesso Naik

(B) Pralhad Joshi

(C) Mukhtar Abbas Naqvi

(D) ** V Muraleedharan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -External Affairs Minister Dr. S. Jaishankar paid a visit to UAE in December 2022 while UAE Foreign Minister H.H. Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan visited India in November 2022.

Concept note-2: -Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed bin Sultan Al Nahyan (Arabic: ; born 30 April 1972) is the Minister of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation of the United Arab Emirates.

Concept note-3: -Indian expatriate community is the largest ethnic community in UAE constituting roughly 30 per cent of the country’s population. The number of resident Indian Nationals is estimated to be 3.5 million in 2021 as per UAE records. Approx.

Concept note-4: -"India and the UAE are both pursuing dynamic trade and investment policies. India hopes to see its exports touch US$1 trillion in the near to medium term. Our growing bilateral trade will play an integral role in the UAE’s efforts to double the size of its economy by 2030.

Concept note-5: -They are both in I2U2 Group. Indians also make up the largest ethnic group in the UAE, making up roughly 38% of UAE’s total residents. India is UAE’s largest exports destination & trading partner with bilateral trade turnover of US$ 68.4 billion during the calendar year 2021.
