Currently, government permission is mandatory only for investments coming from which of the following two countries?

(A) Afganistan and Pakistan

(B) Nepal and Pakistan

(C) Bangladesh and Afganistan

(D) ** Bangladesh and Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Further, a citizen of Pakistan or an entity incorporated in Pakistan can invest, only under the Government route, in sectors/activities other than defence, space, atomic energy, and sectors/activities prohibited for foreign investment. 2.

Concept note-2: -Foreign Direct Investments (FDI) can be made under two routes-Automatic Route and Government Route.

Concept note-3: -Gambling and Betting. Lottery business (including government/ private lottery, online lotteries etc) Activities /sectors not open to private sector investment (eg, atomic energy /railways) Retails trading (expect single-brand product retailing)

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Mauritius.
