The Reliance Jio has collaborated with which of the following technology giant to provide 5G?

(A) Microsoft

(B) Apple

(C) ** Google Cloud

(D) Amazon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Xiaomi India, a Chinese technology giant has announced its collaboration with Reliance Jio to offer 5G experience to their customers.

Concept note-2: -Jio supports SA and NSA 5GC deployments, which gives the highest return on investment for telecom operators and avoids all wasteful integration efforts. The 5G Core network follows a set of fluid principles, which have been further enhanced to create a Hyper scale architecture for Jio’s 5G Core Network.

Concept note-3: -Motorola on Wednesday announced that it has signed an agreement with Reliance Jio to ensure that its 5G smartphones in India support Jio’s advanced standalone 5G technology.

Concept note-4: -Reliance Jio’s 5G services are now available across 184 cities. Reliance Jio on January 24 announced the launch of its ‘True 5G’ services across 50 cities. Calling it the “largest-ever” launch of its services, the company added that Jio’s 5G services are now available across 184 cities.
