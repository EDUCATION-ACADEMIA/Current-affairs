(A) Spain

(B) France

(C) ** Italy

(D) India

Concept note-1: -File: PMC has beautified the Bund Garden road stretch ahead of the G20 meetings in the city.

Concept note-2: -G20 Presidency: Foreign Ministers of 20 countries to grace meet in Delhi in March. The Foreign Ministers of the Group of Twenty (G20) countries are set to meet in New Delhi in March this year, sources said on Thursday. India assumed the Presidency of the G20 starting in December 2022.

Concept note-3: -Since 1999, the meetings or summits of the G20 are organized a G20 Leaders’ Summit annually. The last G20 Summit was held in November 2022 in Indonesia.
