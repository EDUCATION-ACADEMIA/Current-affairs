The BH series number plate be introduced on which date?

(A) September 30th

(B) September 20th

(C) ** September 15th

(D) September 25th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Now, with the introduction of the BH series number plate, the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has make the re-registration of a vehicle in a new state easy & hassle-free. The new rule was introduced on 28th August and registration for the platter will commence on September 15th.

Concept note-2: -The registration for the BH series number plate started from 15th September 2021 and the first vehicle with BH series number plate was issued from Maharashtra on October 27th 2021.

Concept note-3: -This rule has been implemented in many states and the rule was launched on 28th August 2022, then BH registration procedure for the vehicle was started on 15th September 2021. It is mandatory for all the citizens who get transferred to another state they must have the BH series number plate.

Concept note-4: -NIC dept has launched software for BH series registration whose testing is currently under process in states like Uttarakhand and others. BH series registration begins for vehicles in Chandigarh.

Concept note-5: -Bharat Number Plate – Tax Structure If your vehicle has a BH series license plate, the road tax you have to pay is for two years or for multiple of 2 years. You will be able to pay the road tax for the BH series license plate online and it can be applied only for 14 years.
