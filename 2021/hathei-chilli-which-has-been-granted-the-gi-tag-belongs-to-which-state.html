Hathei chilli, which has been granted the GI tag, belongs to which state?

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Kerala

(C) ** Manipur

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Manipur’s exceptional Tamenglong mandarin orange and the state’s Hathei chilli, well known for its energetic red tone and flavour, has been awarded the Geographical Indication (GI) tag.

Concept note-2: -Hathei chilli, which is found in Manipur’s Ukrul district and is known for its unique flavour, and Tamenglong mandarin orange have been granted the Geographical Indication (GI) tag. Confirming the news in a Facebook post, Manipur Chief Minister N Biren Singh wrote, “What a great start to the day for Manipur!

Concept note-3: -New Delhi: Manipur’s Hathei chilli, known for its vibrant red colour and flavour, and the fruit Tamenglong orange have been granted the Geographical Indication (GI) tag.

Concept note-4: -Three handloom products of Manipur have got GI tag so far- Saphee Lanphee, Wangkhei Phee and Moirang Phee under Sub-section (1) of Section 13 of Geographical Indications of Goods (Registration and Protection) Act, 1999.

Concept note-5: -From the Kani Shawl of Jammu & Kashmir to the Kodaikanal Malai Poondu of Tamil Nadu, India has more than 300 Geographical Indications, out of which Manipur has four agricultural produce/products in its pocket.
