Who has announced the autobiography ‘Sach Kahun Toh’?

(A) ** Neena Gupta

(B) Anurag Thakur

(C) NS Tomar

(D) Amit Shah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Neena Gupta’s autobiography Sach Kahun Toh gave a deep insight into the roller coaster life of the actor, who is enjoying the second innings of her career.

Concept note-2: -Many, many thoughts on Neena Gupta’s autobiography ‘Sach Kahun Toh’ published by Penguin India. I sat up till 3 AM reading the book, and finished it in one sitting - I could almost hear her voice read out every word. She’s led an eventful life, but her candidness is not devoid of self-awareness.

Concept note-3: -In Sach Kahun Toh, actor Neena Gupta chronicles her extraordinary personal and professional journey-from her childhood days in Delhi’s Karol Bagh, through her time at the National School of Drama, to moving to Bombay in the 1980s and dealing with the struggles to find work.

Concept note-4: -Masaba Gupta (born 2 November 1989) is an Indian fashion designer and an actress with her own label called House of Masaba.
