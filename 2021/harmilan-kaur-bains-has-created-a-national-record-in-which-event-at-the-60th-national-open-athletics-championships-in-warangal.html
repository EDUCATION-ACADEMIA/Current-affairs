Harmilan Kaur Bains has created a national record in which event at the 60th National Open Athletics Championships in Warangal?

(A) 800m

(B) 1000m

(C) 100m

(D) ** 1500m

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Punjab’s Harmilan Kaur Bains completed a double in women’s middle-distance, winning the 800m to add to her 1500m gold in the 60th National Open Athletics Championships in Warangal.

Concept note-2: -Breaking the national record of 4:06.03 set by Sunita Rani in 1500m title at the 2002 Asian Games. She completed the race in 4:05.39 seconds on 16 September 2021 at the 60th National Open Athletics Championships in Warangal.

Concept note-3: -Hima Das (born 9 January 2000), nicknamed Dhing Express, is an Indian sprinter from the state of Assam. She holds the current Indian national record in 400 meters with a timing of 50.79 s that she clocked at the 2018 Asian Games in Jakarta, Indonesia.

Concept note-4: -Jyothi Yarraji (319) of Railways, winning the Women’s 100m Hurdles, during the 61st National Open Athletics Championship 2022, at the Sree Kanteerva Stadium, in Bengaluru on October 17, 2022. | Photo Credit: K. MURALI KUMAR.

Concept note-5: -Harmilan Bains became the queen of middle-distance running in India as she set a new national record in the 1500m event at the 60th National Open Athletics Championships in Warangal.
