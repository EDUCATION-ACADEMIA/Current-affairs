Asia’s longest High-Speed Track, NATRAX has been inaugurated in which city of India?

(A) ** Indore

(B) Hyderabad

(C) Pune

(D) Agra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Minister of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Shri Prakash Javadekar today inaugurated NATRAX- the High Speed Track(HST) in Indore which is the longest such track in Asia.

Concept note-2: -Minister of Heavy Industries and Public Enterprises Prakash Javdekar on Tuesday inaugurated Asia’s longest high-speed test track located in Pithampur, Indore. The National Automotive Test Tracks (NATRAX) facility measures 11.3 km in length and will facilitate automotive and component testing for automobile companies.

Concept note-3: -The longest track in Asia The Natrax is the longest such testing track in India and the fifth-longest in the world. It is 11.3 km in length and 16 metres in width.

Concept note-4: -The High-speed track is one of the largest in World i. e 11.3 km with 4 lane and is used to carry out development and homologation tests for all kinds of vehicles.

Concept note-5: -Today, inaugurated the longest high-speed track in Asia at Indore."
