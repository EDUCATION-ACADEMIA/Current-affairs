Who has launched its new Taj Mahal inspired Engineering Hub in NCR?

(A) Snapdeal

(B) ** Microsoft

(C) Amazon

(D) Flipkart

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Microsoft’s new engineering hub in Noida is inspired by the Taj Mahal.

Concept note-2: -“The expansion of the India Development Center in Noida is our continued effort to empower every person and every organization on this planet to achieve more.

Concept note-3: -The Taj Mahal is a mausoleum complex that houses the tombs of Mumtaz Mahal (“Chosen One of the Palace”) and her husband, the Mughal emperor Shah Jahān (reigned 1628–58).

Concept note-4: -Microsoft today announced the launch of its new India Development Center (IDC) facility at the National Capital Region. The facility will serve as a premier hub for driving cutting-edge engineering and innovation, Microsoft said. IDC NCR is Microsoft’s third Development Center in India after Bengaluru and Hyderabad.

Concept note-5: -Microsoft India Development Center expands its footprint in Noida. The newest worksite is spread across 150, 000 square feet and built to drive digital innovation through expanded teams in Cloud & AI, Experiences and Devices Group, Microsoft Digital Teams, and the new Gaming division have a growing presence in India.
