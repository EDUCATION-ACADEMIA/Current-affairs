Who shall head the National Steering Committee (NSC) constituted for implementation of the NIPUN Bharat Mission?

(A) ** Dharmendra Pradhan

(B) Ramesh Pokhriyal

(C) Mansukh Mandaviya

(D) Anurag Thakur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Dharmendra Pradhan. A National Steering Committee (NSC) for implementation of the NIPUN Bharat Mission has been constituted on 25th October 2021. It has been constituted under the chairpersonship of the Union Education Minister, Dharmendra Pradhan.
