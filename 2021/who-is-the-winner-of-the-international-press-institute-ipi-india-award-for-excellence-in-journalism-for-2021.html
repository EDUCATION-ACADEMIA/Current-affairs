Who is the winner of the International Press Institute (IPI) India Award for Excellence in Journalism for 2021?

(A) Sreenivasan Jain and Mariyam Alavi

(B) Lakshmi Subramanian and Bhanu Praksh Chandra

(C) Mariyam Alavi

(D) ** Both A and B

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -IPI-India awards go to The Print, Saurabh Shukla for excellence in journalism.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: The International Press Institute (IPI) India Award for Excellence in Journalism-2021 has been awarded jointly to Sreenivasan Jain and Mariyam Alavi of NDTV, and Lakshmi Subramanian and Bhanu Praksh Chandra of The Week.

Concept note-3: -International Press Institute (IPI) is a global organisation dedicated to the promotion and protection of press freedom and the improvement of journalism practices.

Concept note-4: -The award has been given by Berkshire Media, USA (specialists in Brand and Market Research), experts who are internationally-recognised for their brand research and analysis. NDTV has also won “Asia’s Most Trusted Media Company” for 2022. The award honours NDTV for its innovation, reputation and content.
