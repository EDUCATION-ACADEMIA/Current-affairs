India’s first-ever geothermal power project will be implemented by state-owned Oil and Natural Gas Corporation in which state?

(A) Himachal Pradesh

(B) West Bengal

(C) Jammu and Kashmir

(D) ** Ladakh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ladakh is all set to get India’s firstgeothermal power project soon to tap the potential of natural geysers dottingthe Puga area, 170 km east of Leh and in Changthang plains.

Concept note-2: -Published: Friday 31 August 2001. india’s first geothermal minor hydel power plant tapping energy from natural hot water springs would be soon installed in Tatapani village of Chattisgarh.

Concept note-3: -In India, geothermal plants are located in Manikaran in Himachal Pradesh and Puga Valley in Ladakh.

Concept note-4: -Prince Piero Ginori Conti invents the first geothermal power plant at the Larderello dry steam field in Tuscany, Italy.

Concept note-5: -State-owned Oil and Natural Gas Corporation (ONGC) to implement India’s maiden geothermal field development project in Ladakh.
