Who has become the first Chinese woman astronaut to walk in space?

(A) Chyou Daiyu

(B) Baozhai Chu Hua

(C) ** Wang Yaping

(D) Ah Lam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Astronaut Wang Yaping has become the first Chinese woman to walk in space. She is a part of a six-month mission to the country’s space station.

Concept note-2: -Wang Yaping on Monday became the first Chinese woman astronaut to walk in space as she moved out of the under-construction space station and took part in extravehicular activities for over six hours along with her male colleague Zhai Zhigang, official media here reported.

Concept note-3: -Zhai Zhigang, (born October 10, 1966, Longjiang, Qiqihar, Heilongjiang, China), Chinese astronaut who performed China’s first spacewalk. Zhai was the child of an illiterate mother who peddled sunflower seeds to pay for her children’s education.

Concept note-4: -Two Chinese astronauts took four-hour spacewalk outside the country’s growing Tiangong station this weekend to work on its newest laboratory module, according to state media reports.
