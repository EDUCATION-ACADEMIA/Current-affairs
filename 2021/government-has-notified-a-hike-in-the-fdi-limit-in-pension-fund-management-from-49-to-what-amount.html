Government has notified a hike in the FDI limit in Pension fund management from 49% to what amount?

(A) 65%

(B) 75%

(C) ** 74%

(D) 67%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The government has issued a notification to raise the FDI limit in pension funds from 49 percent to 74 percent.

Concept note-2: -Effects of the FDI Limit The foreign direct investment (FDI) limit has also been raised from 49 per cent to 74 per cent as a result of amendments to the Pension Fund Regulatory and Development Act.

Concept note-3: -Such investment would be subject to the following conditions: (i) It would be made under the Government approval route. (ii) The 49% limit will subsume FDI and FII/FPI investment.

Concept note-4: -Air transport services Automatic up to 49% (up to 100% and automatic for NRls), Government route beyond 49%.

Concept note-5: -Previous amendment (2015) However, after the initial days of this amendment, the Indian Insurance Companies Rules 2015 prescribed that FDI investment up to 26% under automatic route was allowed.
