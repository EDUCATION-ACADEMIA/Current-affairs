Which hospital has become the first hospital in India to house a fire station inside the hospital premises?

(A) RML Hospital

(B) ** AIIMS Delhi

(C) ESIC Delhi

(D) FIIMS

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The infrastructure of the fire station shall be provided by AIIMS, the manpower shall be managed by DFS.

Concept note-2: -AIIMS Delhi becomes 1st hospital in India to have fire station on its campus.

Concept note-3: -All India Institute of Medical Sciences, New Delhi, also known as AIIMS Delhi, is a public medical research university and hospital in New Delhi, India.

Concept note-4: -The first AIIMS was established in 1956 under the All India Institute of Medical Sciences Act, 1956. Originally proposed by the then Prime Minister of India Jawaharlal Nehru for establishment in Calcutta, it was established in New Delhi following the refusal of Chief Minister of West Bengal Bidhan Chandra Roy.

Concept note-5: -All India Institute of Medical Sciences.
