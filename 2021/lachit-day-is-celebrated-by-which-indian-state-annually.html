Lachit Day is celebrated by which Indian state annually?

(A) Haryana

(B) ** Assam

(C) Goa

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On November 24 each year, Lachit Divas (Lachit Day) is celebrated state-wide in Assam to commemorate the heroism of Lachit Borphukan and the victory of the Assamese army at the Battle of Saraighat.

Concept note-2: -This is the first time the legendary Ahom Army General Lachit Barphukan and his achievements are celebrated outside his home state.

Concept note-3: -Lachit Borphukan’s birthday, November 24, honors the Assamese army’s victory at the Battle of Saraighat. On November 23, a three-day celebration for the illustrious Assamese general got underway in New Delhi.

Concept note-4: -Lachit Divas is a regional holiday observed in Assam on 24 November every year in remembrance of the brave Ahom army general Lachit Borphukan. Lachit Borphukan was born on 24 November 1622 in Charaideo and is known for his military intelligence in the Battle of Saraighat.

Concept note-5: -Lachit Divas (Lachit Day) is a regional public holiday in the Indian state of Assam, observed on November 24th each year.
