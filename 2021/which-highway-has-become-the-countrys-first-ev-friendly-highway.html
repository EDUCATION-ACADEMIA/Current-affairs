Which highway has become the country’s first EV-friendly highway?

(A) Delhi-Meerut

(B) ** Delhi-Chandigarh

(C) Delhi-Roorkee

(D) Panipat-Ambala

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Delhi-Chandigarh Highway has become the first e-vehicle friendly highway in the country. It now has a network of Solar-based Electric Vehicle Charging stations (SEVCs) set up by Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) under the FAME-1 scheme of the Ministry of Heavy Industries.

Concept note-2: -Delhi-Jaipur Expressway to turn India’s 1st ‘Electric highway’, trial run of e-vehicles begin. New Delhi: Under the Ease of Doing Business initiative, the National Highway for Electric Vehicles (NHEV) initiated a one-month tech trial run for e-vehicles on the Delhi-Jaipur expressway on Friday.

Concept note-3: -An electric road, eroad, or electric road system (ERS) is a road which supplies electric power to vehicles travelling on it. Common implementations are overhead power lines above the road and ground-level power supply through conductive rails or inductive coils embedded in the road.
