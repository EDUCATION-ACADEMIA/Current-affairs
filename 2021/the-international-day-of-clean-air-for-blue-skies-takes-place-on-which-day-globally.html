The International Day of Clean Air for blue skies takes place on which day globally?

(A) September 06

(B) ** September 07

(C) September 05

(D) September 03

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The third International Day of Clean Air for blue skies will be held on 7 September 2022, under the theme of ‘The Air We Share’.

Concept note-2: -International Day of Clean Air for blue skies: History During its 74th session, the United Nations General Assembly adopted a resolution to hold an International Day of Clean Air for Blue Skies on December 19, 2019.

Concept note-3: -On 7 September 2022, the third International Day of Clean Air for blue skies will be observed under the theme of The Air we share.
