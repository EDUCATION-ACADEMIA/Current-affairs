BJP leader Sudhanshu Mittal"s book on the Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (RSS) has now been translated into which languages?

(A) Arabian

(B) Persian

(C) ** Chinese

(D) English

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The resulting book, We, or Our Nationhood Defined, was published in Golwalkar’s name and regarded as a systematic treatment of RSS ideology; the claim that it was an abridged translation was only made by Golwalkar in a 1963 speech.

Concept note-2: -The Rashtriya Swayamsevak Sangh (abbr. RSS ; Rāṣṭrīya Svayaṃsevak Saṅgh, Hindi pronunciation: [raːʂˈʈriːj(ə) swəjəmˈseːʋək səŋɡʱ], lit. ‘National Volunteer Organisation’) is a Hindu nationalist paramilitary volunteer organisation in India.

Concept note-3: -Mittal is a member and official spokesperson of BJP. He was a confidant of Pramod Mahajan, a senior BJP leader who died in 2006. Although described as a top contender for the party’s Chandni Chowk parliament seat, Mittal was not selected to contest it in the 2014 general elections.
