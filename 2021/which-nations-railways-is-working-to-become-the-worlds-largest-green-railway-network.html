Which nation’s railways is working to become the world’s largest green railway network?

(A) UK

(B) ** India

(C) Russia

(D) Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Railways has set a target to become world’s largest ‘Green Railways’ with Zero Carbon Emission by 2030. In 2022-23, a total of 1, 973 route km (2, 647 TKM) has been electrified.

Concept note-2: -The Chennai Central railway station comes under the South Central Railway (SCR) zone and which is going to become the world’s largest green railway network.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is the USA. America’s rail network is the world’s largest rail network. Its total length is 2, 50, 000 kilometers.

Concept note-4: -The Udhna Junction railway station is the first not only in India but across the world to gain the coveted place in the India Book of Records as the green railway station, which is working in the direction of climate action.
