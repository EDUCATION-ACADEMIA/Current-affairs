The 130th edition of the Durand Cup is scheduled to be held in which Indian city?

(A) Kohima

(B) Shimla

(C) Guwahati

(D) ** Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -130th EDITION OF DURAND CUP 2021 SCHEDULED TO BE HELD AT KOLKATA FROM 05 SEP TO 03 OCT 21. Durand Cup, the world’s third oldest and Asia’s oldest football tournament is poised to make a comeback after a year’s hiatus due to the Covid-19 pandemic.

Concept note-2: -131st Durand Football tournament : It was hosted by three states, Assam, Manipur and West Bengal. Assam and Manipur hosted the tournament for the first time. This year 20-top teams of the country, including all 11 teams from the Indian Super League (ISL) participated as compared to 16 teams that participated last year.

Concept note-3: -Venues. A total of 47 matches were played across 3 cities: Kolkata, Guwahati and Imphal, with 27 matches played in Kolkata, including the knock-out fixtures, and 10 matches each played in Guwahati and Imphal.

Concept note-4: -In a clash of former Indian Super League champions, Bengaluru FC edged Mumbai City FC 2-1 to claim their first Durand Cup title. Siva Sakthi (10th minute) and Alan Costa (61st) scored for the winners in the final of the 131st edition of Asia’s oldest football tournament at Salt Lake Stadium, Kolkata.
