Which state has become the first to start ‘Air Health Service’ in rural areas?

(A) Bihar

(B) Kerala

(C) Telangana

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik launched Mukhyamantri Vayu Swasthya Seva (air health services). With this scheme, it became one of the first states to provide air ambulance facilities for the poor in the rural interior areas.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Karnataka. Karnataka Chief Minister B.S. Yediyurappa unveiled the country’s first integrated air ambulance service.

Concept note-3: -Dileep Vishwakarma. Notes: Odisha Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik launched Mukhyamantri Vayu Swasthya Seva (air health services).
