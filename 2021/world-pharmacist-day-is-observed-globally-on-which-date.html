World Pharmacist Day is observed globally on which date?

(A) September 22

(B) ** September 25

(C) September 26

(D) September 21

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Pharmacy Day started in 2009 in Istanbul during the FIP Council. The aim of this exercise is to promote and advocate for the role of pharmacists in delivering safe effective healthcare. September 25th was the date on which FIP was found and hence was chosen as World Pharmacy Day.

Concept note-2: -“Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world” was the theme of World Pharmacists Day on 25 September 2022.

Concept note-3: -Notably, September 25 was chosen as the date to observe World Pharmacists Day as the International Pharmaceutical Federation was established on this day in 1912. World Pharmacists Day 2022 Theme: According to the FIP, this year’s theme is ‘Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world’.

Concept note-4: -M. R. Doreswamy, an Intercollegiate Competition Pharmagala 2022, as part of World Pharmacist Day, has been organised from FPS, PESU at HN Campus. The function is scheduled from 9 a.m. to 5 p.m. The event shall provide an opportunity for students to display their pharma knowledge and bring pride in them.

Concept note-5: -World Pharmacists Day 2021: History 25 September is chosen as World Pharmacist Day because on this date FIP came into existence in 1912. The main aim of this day is to encourage the role and activities of the pharmacist in improving health a in every part of the world.
