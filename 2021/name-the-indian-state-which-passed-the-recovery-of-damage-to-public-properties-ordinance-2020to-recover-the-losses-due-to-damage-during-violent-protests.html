Name the Indian state which passed the ‘’Recovery of Damage to Public Properties Ordinance 2020’to recover the losses due to damage during violent protests.?

(A) Telangana

(B) ** Uttar Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Madhya Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Uttar Pradesh Recovery of Damages to Public and Private Property Ordinance, 2020. Empowers state government to set up tribunals to decide claims for damage to property.

Concept note-2: -Key Points. Notably, this act was approved by the Governor of Madhya Pradesh on January 3, 2022.

Concept note-3: -Section 425:- Mischief It is sufficient if he intends to cause, or knows that he is likely to cause, wrongful loss or damage to any person by injuring any property, whether it belongs to that person or not.

Concept note-4: -The Act aims at “recovery of damage to public or private property during hartal, bandhs, riots, protests etc through those accused through claim tribunals with no judicial review by any other court.
