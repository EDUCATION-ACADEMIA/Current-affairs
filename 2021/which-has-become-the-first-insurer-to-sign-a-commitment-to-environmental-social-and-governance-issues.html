Which has become the first insurer to sign a commitment to environmental, social and governance issues?

(A) Bharti AXA Life Insurance

(B) HDFC Standard Life Insurance

(C) Bajaj Allianz Life Insurance

(D) ** ICICI Prudential Life Insurance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ICICI Prudential Life Insurance has become the first Indian insurance company to sign the UN-supported Principles for Responsible Investment (UNPRI), demonstrating its commitment towards Environment, Social, and Governance (ESG) issues.

Concept note-2: -ICICI Prudential Life Insurance became the first Indian insurance company to sign United Nations-supported Principles for Responsible Investment (UNPRI), to demonstrate commitment towards the environmental, social and governance (ESG) issues.

Concept note-3: -1710 Charles Povey formed the Sun, the oldest insurance company in existence which still conducts business in its own name. It is the forerunner of the Royal & Sun Alliance Group.

Concept note-4: -Oriental Life Insurance Company started by Europeans in Calcutta was the first life insurance company on Indian Soil.

Concept note-5: -Amicable Society for a Perpetual Assurance Office, established in 1706, was the first life insurance company in the world.
