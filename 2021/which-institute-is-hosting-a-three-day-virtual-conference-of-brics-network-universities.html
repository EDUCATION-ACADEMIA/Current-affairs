Which Institute is hosting a three-day virtual “Conference of BRICS Network Universities”?

(A) ** IIT Bombay

(B) IIT Madras

(C) IIT Delhi

(D) IIT Kharagpur

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is IIT Bombay.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi led India’s participation at the 14thBRICS Summit, convened under the Chairship of President Xi Jinping of China on 23-24June 2022, in a virtual format.

Concept note-3: -IIT Bombay hosted a three day virtual conference of BRICS Network universities during June 16-18, 2021. This conference is part of the engagement that India hosted under the education stream as chair of the 13th BRICS Summit in 2021.

Concept note-4: -BRICS Network University is a union of higher education institutions of the five BRICS member countries. IIT-Bombay is the lead institution of India for the BRICS Network University.

Concept note-5: -15th BRICS summit to be held in August in South Africa’s Durban. Mint.
