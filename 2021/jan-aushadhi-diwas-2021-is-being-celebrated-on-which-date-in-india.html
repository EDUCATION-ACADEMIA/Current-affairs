Jan Aushadhi Diwas 2021 is being celebrated on which date in India?

(A) 3rd-9th March

(B) 4th-10th March

(C) ** 1st-7th March

(D) 2nd-8th March

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Theme of 4th Janaushadhi Diwas: “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi” Pharmaceuticals & Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), under the aegis of Department of Pharmaceuticals is going to celebrate their 4th Jan Aushadhi Diwas by organizing weeklong celebrations at different locations across the country covering all States/UTs.

Concept note-2: -The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 celebrations commenced today. It will be a weeklong celebration starting today ie from 1st March to 7th March 2022. Jan Aushadhi kendras today conducted Jan Aushadhi Sankalp Padyatra at 75 locations, across the country.

Concept note-3: -In news– The 4th Janaushadhi Diwas 2022 was observed from 1st March to 7th March.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi”. Pharmaceuticals and Medical Devices Bureau of India (PMBI), under the aegis of Department of Pharmaceuticals is celebrating their 4th Janaushadhi Diwas. It will generate awareness about the usages of generic medicines.

Concept note-5: -The theme of 4th Janaushadhi Diwas is “Jan Aushadhi-Jan Upyogi”. PM Bharatiya Jan Aushadhi Pariyojana ensures easy reach of affordable medicine to the people in every district.
