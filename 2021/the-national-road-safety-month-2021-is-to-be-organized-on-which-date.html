The National Road Safety Month 2021 is to be organized on which date?

(A) January 1 to Jan 31

(B) January 15 to Feb 14

(C) ** January 18 to Feb 17

(D) January 10 to Feb 9

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With Road Safety being a very important healthcare issue, NSS IIT Roorkee will be celebrating 33rd National Road Safety Month 2021 from January 22 to February 21, as decided by the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -This year, we are celebrating 32nd Road Safety Week and its theme is “safe yourself to save your family”.

Concept note-3: -Every year, road crashes result in the loss of lakhs of lives and serious injuries to crores of people. The Ministry of Road Transport & Highways is observing the Road Safety Week from January 11th to 17th, 2023, under “Swachhata Pakhwada”, to propagate the cause of safer roads for all.

Concept note-4: -The period from January 5 to January 28 is being celebrated as National Road Safety Month. During this campaign to make citizens aware of road safety, different types of events are being organized by the Safety Engineering Department of BSL.

Concept note-5: -National Road Safety Day has been observed on 4th March since 1972. The Minister of Road Transport and Highways is Nitin Gadkari.
