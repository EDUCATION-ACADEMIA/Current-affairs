India will be observing the 75th year of Independence in which year?

(A) 2021

(B) ** 2022

(C) 2024

(D) 2023

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India will complete the 75 years of Independence on 15th August 2022 but India will celebrate the 76th Independence day.

Concept note-2: -India is celebrating its 76th Independence Day on August 15, 2022, and marking its 75 years of freedom.

Concept note-3: -It was celebrated on August 15 2022. The 75 years of Independence was celebrated with the theme of Azadi ka Amrit Mahotsav to pay tribute and honor the leaders of the freedom struggle who sacrificed their lives for the welfare of the nation.

Concept note-4: -This means that India celebrated its first year of independence on August 15, 1948, 10 years on August 15, 1957, 20 years in 1967, and 70 years in 2017. Accordingly, India celebrates 75 years of independence from British rule in 2022.
