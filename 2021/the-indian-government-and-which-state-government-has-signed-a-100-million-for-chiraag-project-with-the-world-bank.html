(A) ** Chhattisgarh

(B) Assam

(C) Kerala

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Bank Approves US$100 Million for Barbados’ Green and Resilient Recovery. Bridgetown, Barbados, January 11, 2023 - The World Bank approved yesterday US$100 million in financing to support Barbados’ low carbon economic development and resilience to climate change.

Concept note-2: -In 2021, it started a USD 500 million project called “Strengthening Teaching-Learning and Results for States Program (STARS)”. This programme is being implemented across 6 Indian states to improve quality and governance of school education.
