Which player has won the F1 Dutch Grand Prix 2021?

(A) Sebastian Vettel

(B) Lewis Hamilton

(C) ** Max Verstappen

(D) Valtteri Bottas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dutch Grand Prix: Max Verstappen wins home race to extend title lead. Max Verstappen’s serene march to a second title continued with a comfortable victory in an eventful Dutch Grand Prix.

Concept note-2: -It was the thirteenth round of the 2021 Formula One World Championship and the first Dutch Grand Prix to take place since 1985. The race was won by Max Verstappen – prior to him, no Dutch driver had won their home race.

Concept note-3: -F1 Dutch Grand Prix: Verstappen wins to extend championship lead – as it happened | Formula One | The Guardian.

Concept note-4: -Sao Paulo Grand Prix 2021 Last year’s epic victory by Hamilton in Brazil will go down in F1 history, with Sky Sports F1’s Damon Hill describing the seven-time champion’s performance as “one of the great drives” that he had ever seen.

Concept note-5: -Lewis Hamilton (103) The most successful driver in the history of F1 is Lewis Hamilton. The man from Stevenage has won 103 Grands Prix throughout his career and is still looking to add to that total in the coming seasons with Mercedes. No other driver has reached three figures in terms of race wins.
