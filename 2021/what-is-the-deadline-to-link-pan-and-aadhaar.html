What is the deadline to link PAN and Aadhaar?

(A) ** March 31, 2022

(B) January 31, 2022

(C) October 31, 2021

(D) December 31, 2021

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PAN Aadhaar Link Last Date, Status Check, Penalty: The Income Tax department has informed that it is mandatory to link permanent account numbers (PAN) with Aadhaar. The last date to link Pan with Aadhaar is March 31, 2023. After March 31, PAN holders will be not able to use the 10-digit unique alphanumeric number.

Concept note-2: -The last date to link the PAN with Aadhaar was earlier extended from 31st March 2022 to 31st March 2023.

Concept note-3: -Last date 31/03/2023, ‚Äù the income tax department said. You will not be able to use your PAN card if it becomes inoperative. The deadline to link PAN with Aadhaar has been extended several times in the past. The latest deadline for PAN-Aadhaar linking is March 31, 2023.

Concept note-4: -Last date to link PAN with Aadhaar is extended till March 31, 2023. People are requested to link their PAN-Aadhaar by deadline or the PAN number will become inoperative.

Concept note-5: -If the PAN Card and the Aadhaar Card are not linked, you may be subject to a penalty of up to Rs. 10, 000 according to the Income Tax Act. In addition, if the two cards are not linked by March 31, 2023, your PAN Card may become inoperable.
