Bhasha Sangam mobile app has been launched by which minister?

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Rajnath Singh

(C) Amit Shah

(D) ** Subhas Sarkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Link for Download Bhasha Sangam Mobile App launched by Hon’ble Education Minister under Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat Programme - ,

Concept note-2: -Bhasha Sangam App is a mobile application developed by the Ministry of Education in collaboration with MyGov India. The App has been developed by a start-up Multibhashi Solutions Pvt.

Concept note-3: -Education Ministry has launched a mobile app called Bhasha Sangam in order to help people acquire basic conversation ability in languages other than one’s mother tongue to at least 75 lakh people. The App has more than 100 sentences of common usage, both in text and audio format, in 22 Indian languages.

Concept note-4: -The Ministry of Education has launched a campaign titled ‘Bhasha Certificate Selfie’. The aim of this campaign is to promote the Bhasha Sangam mobile app, launched by the Ministry under the aegis of Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat to encourage cultural diversity and promote multilingualism.

Concept note-5: -Q. Which Union Ministry launched the ‘Bhasha Certificate Selfie’ Campaign? Notes: Union Ministry of Education recently launched a campaign ‘Bhasha Certificate Selfie’ to encourage cultural diversity and promote Multilingualism.
