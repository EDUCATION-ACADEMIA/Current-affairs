Which bank has topped the Forbes Worlds Best Banks 2021 in India?

(A) ** DBS Bank

(B) HDFC Bank

(C) YES Bank

(D) SBI

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. Which bank was named in the first position in India in the Forbes’ World’s Best Banks 2021 list? Notes: DBS has been named by Forbes in their list of World’s Best Banks 2021, based on global customer survey. DBS was ranked in the first position out of 30 domestic and international banks in India.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai, April 11, 2019: HDFC Bank was ranked No 1 in India by customers in the ‘World’s Best Banks’ survey by Forbes magazine. In the first edition of this survey, Forbes partnered with market research firm Statista to measure the best banks in 23 countries.

Concept note-3: -Annual Global Bank Ranking (2021), the State Bank of India was at 57th rank with $638.49 billion in assets, according to S&P Global Market Intelligence.

Concept note-4: -SBI is the 43rd largest bank in the world and ranked 221st in the Fortune Global 500 list of the world’s biggest corporations of 2020, being the only Indian bank on the list. SBI RECOGNISED AS ICONIC BRAND OF INDIA 2021 BY THE ECONOMIC TIMES.

Concept note-5: -Other Indian companies in the top 100 rankings were ICICI Bank at 65, HDFC Bank at 77, and HCL Technologies at 90, according to the Forbes ranking. State Bank of India (SBI) was placed at 119 and Larsen & Toubro at 127. Infosys was ranked at 588 and Tata Group at 746.
