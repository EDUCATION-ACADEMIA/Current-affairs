The exercise “Konkan Shakti 2021” is a maiden Tri-Service exercise of Indian Armed Forces with which country?

(A) ** United Kingdom

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) Australia

(D) Israel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The maiden India – UK Joint Tri-Service exercise Ex KONKAN SHAKTI is scheduled in India between 21 - 27 October 2021. The maritime component of the exercise, to be conducted off the west coast of India, is scheduled to be held in two phases.

Concept note-2: -The Armed Forces of India and the United Kingdom (UK) are undertaking the sea phase of the maiden Tri-Service exercise ‘Konkan Shakti 2021’ off the Konkan coast in the Arabian Sea from October 24 to 27, 2021. The harbour phase of the seven-day exercise was held in Mumbai from October 21 to 23, 2021.

Concept note-3: -The armed forces of India and the United Kingdom have concluded their first bilateral tri-service exercise, ‘Konkan Shakti-21’.

Concept note-4: -Initial Planning Conference for Exercise Konkan 23 between Indian Navy and Royal Navy was held on 01 December 2022 at Mumbai .

Concept note-5: -Konkan Shakti 2021 is the first bilateral tri-service exercise between the armed forces of India and the UK. It culminated in the Arabian Sea on 27 October 21.
