Which film won the Best Motion Picture-Drama award at the Golden Globe Awards 2021?

(A) Promising Young Woman

(B) Mank

(C) The Trial of the Chicago 7

(D) ** Nomadland

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Universal’s The Fabelmans (DP Janusz Kaminiski) took Best Motion Picture Drama and Searchlight’s The Banshees of Inisherin (DP Ben Davis BSC) won for Musical or Comedy at the 80th annual Golden Globe Awards.

Concept note-2: -The 78th Golden Globe Awards kicked off Sunday night after being delayed for nearly two months by the coronavirus pandemic. “Nomadland” won the award for best drama film and “Borat Subsequent Moviefilm” won best comedy film.

Concept note-3: -India bagged its first-ever Golden Globe award on January 11, Wednesday for the song “Natu Natu, ” from SS Rajamouli’s “RRR". The movie RRR was also nominated for the category ‘best picture-non English’.

Concept note-4: -Golden Globes 2021 Winners: Chadwick Boseman bestowed with Best Actor; The Crown and Schitt’s Creek win big. While Chadwick Boseman posthumously won Best Actor for his final role in Ma Rainey’s Black Bottom, The Crown, Schitt’s Creek, Borat Subsequent Moviefilm and Soul were triumphant too at the Golden Globes 2021.
