The International Aero India 2017 was held in which city

(A) Kochi

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) Chennai

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aero India is a biennial air show and aviation exhibition held in Bengaluru, India at the Yelahanka Air Force Station. It is organised by the Defence Exhibition Organisation, Ministry of Defence. The Aero India is the largest air show in Asia.

Concept note-2: -The show would be held at the Air Force Station, Yelahanka in Bengaluru.”

Concept note-3: -According to Prime Minister’s Office (PMO), the theme of Aero India 2023 is “The Runway to a Billion Opportunities".

Concept note-4: -Ahead of Asia’s biggest airshow, a full-swing rehearsal was done in Bengaluru on February 11. ‘Aero India 2023’ will be held at Air Force Station in Yelahanka in Bengaluru from February 13-17. The biennial air show and aviation exhibition will be inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Concept note-5: -Aero India 2023 will witness participation by more than 80 countries. Ministers of about 30 countries and 65 CEOs of global and Indian OEMs are likely to participate in Aero India 2023.
