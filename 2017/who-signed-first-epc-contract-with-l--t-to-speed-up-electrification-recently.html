Who signed first EPC contract with L & T to speed up electrification recently?

(A) ** Indian railways

(B) Indian Airlines

(C) Port Trust of India

(D) National Institute of Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Railways decided in 1957 to adopt 25 kV AC system of electrification as a standard with SNCF as their consultant in the initial stages. The first section to be electrified on 25 kV AC system was Raj Kharswan-Dongoposi of South Eastern Railway in the year 1960.

Concept note-2: -The foundation of the Taj Mahal, laid in 1898, is 40 feet deep. The hotel was built at a cost of more than Rs. 4 crore. It was the first building in Bombay to be lit by electricity, the first to have telephones in every room, electric elevators and an ice-making machine!

Concept note-3: -Lord Dalhousie is known as the Father of Indian Railways.

Concept note-4: -West Central Railway has become the first zone on Indian Railways whose entire route is lectrified. Electrification has been done for 3012 kms.
