Who became the First Indian to Solo finish Race across America?

(A) ** Srinivas Gokulnath

(B) Harish Raghavendra

(C) Ashok Selvan

(D) Narayana Moorty

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Srinivas Gokulnath and Amit Samarath become the first Indians to complete the Race Across America.

Concept note-2: -Mahajan brothers : Winners of team of 2 in 2015. First ever RAAM FINISHERS from India. RAAM 2015-Dr Mahajan brothers, India, stood First in Two person team, 18-49 year age group.

Concept note-3: -The Great Himalayan Ultra race organised by Pune-based Inspire India has most of the terrain above 3, 000 metres. The lowest point of the race is 2, 600 m in Kargil, while Fotu La at 4, 100 m is the highest point. It is considered to be one of the toughest ultra-cycling races in India and the highest in the world.

Concept note-4: -RAAM is seen as a pinnacle of athletic achievement not only in cycling circles but the greater sporting community as well. Racers must traverse 3000 miles across 12 states and climb over 170, 000 vertical feet. Team racers have a maximum of nine days and most finish in about seven and a half days.
