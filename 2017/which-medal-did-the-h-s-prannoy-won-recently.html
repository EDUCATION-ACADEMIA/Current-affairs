Which medal did the H S Prannoy won recently?

(A) Badminton World Federation (BWF)

(B) BWF Super series Finals

(C) Badminton Asia Championship

(D) ** US Open Grand Prix Gold Badminton title

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HS Prannoy finally wins Arjuna Award, ‘told you so’ moment at National Sports Awards-Sportstar.

Concept note-2: -He got further success in the 2021 BWF World Championships, held in December, where he upset World no. 9 Ng Ka Long of Hong Kong in the 1st round and World no. 10 Rasmus Gemke in the pre-quarterfinals. He lost to the eventual world champion Loh Kean Yew of Singapore in the quarterfinals.

Concept note-3: -Indian badminton player HS Prannoy got married to long-time partner Swetha Gomes on Wednesday. The 30-year-old HS Prannoy, who last week climbed to the top of the BWF World Tour 2022 rankings, tied the knot in a private ceremony in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala.

Concept note-4: -The All England win catapulted Padukone to the number one world badminton ranking, making him the first Indian to achieve that status.

Concept note-5: -Coach Siyadatullah recalls watching Prannoy splayed after a slide on the forecourt. “As coaches we have to seem cool, can’t show our panic which I felt when he fell.
