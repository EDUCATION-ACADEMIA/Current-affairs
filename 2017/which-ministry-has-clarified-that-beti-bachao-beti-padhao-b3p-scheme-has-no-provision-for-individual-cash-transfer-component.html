Which ministry has clarified that Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (B3P) Scheme has no provision for individual cash transfer component.

(A) ** Ministry of Women & Child Development

(B) Ministry of Human Resource Management

(C) Ministry of Home Affairs

(D) Ministry of Science and Technology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It has come to the notice of Ministry of Women & Child Development, Government of India that certain unauthorised sites/organisations/NGOs/individuals are distributing illegal forms in the name of cash incentive under Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) Scheme.

Concept note-2: -It is a tri-ministerial effort of Ministries of Women and Child Development, Health & Family Welfare and Human Resource Development.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution Ministry of Rural Development is NOT associated with the initiative of Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Scheme. Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao is a Government of India initiative aimed at raising awareness and increasing the quality of child welfare programs in India.

Concept note-4: -Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana Scheme (SSY) It offers parents the option to open a savings account per girl child (under the age of 10 years) and allows parents to make deposits in the account for a period of 15 years.

Concept note-5: -Support. The Govt of India has formed a National Executive Committee to promote Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) across the country. The committee is organizing several programs to promote “Save Girl Child” and “to Educate Girl Child” since January 2015. Dr. Rajendra Phadke is the National Convener of BBBP Abhiyan.
