Which of the following scheme approved by the Union Government to make 6 crore rural households digitally literate by March 2019

(A) Suraksha Bima Yojana

(B) Sukanya Samriddhi Yojana

(C) ** Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan

(D) MUDRA Bank Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Gamin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan ( PMGDISHA), is the scheme to make six crore people in rural areas, across the States/UTs, Digitally literate, reaching to around 40% of rural households by covering one member from every eligible household by 31st March 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Scheme would empower the citizens in rural areas by training them to operate computer or digital access devices (like tablets, smart phones etc.), send and receive e-mails, browse Internet, access Government services, search for information, undertake digital payment etc.

Concept note-3: -The correct option is 1 i.e., 2017. Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan was launched in 2017. Prime Minister Narendra Modi launched Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan (PMGDISHA) at Gandhinagar in Gujarat.

Concept note-4: -PMGDISHA scheme or Pradhan Mantri Gramin Digital Saksharta Abhiyan is a government-initiated scheme which aims to give Digital education to around 2.22 crore villagers. This scheme is registered under the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.

Concept note-5: -Outcomes of PMGDISHA A person trained under this program would be able to operate computers, send/receive emails, undertake cashless transactions, search for information, access Government Services etc. and hence use the ability in the process of nation-building and contributing to the nationâ€™s economic growth.
