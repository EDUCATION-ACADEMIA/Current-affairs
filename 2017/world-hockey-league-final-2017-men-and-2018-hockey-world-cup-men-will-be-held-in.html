EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2018 Men’s Hockey World Cup was the 14th edition of the Hockey World Cup, the quadrennial world championship for men’s national field hockey teams organized by the FIH. It was held from 28 November to 16 December 2018, at the Kalinga Stadium in Bhubaneswar, India.

Concept note-2: -Odisha’s Bhubaneswar and Rourkela will host the FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup 2023 from January 13 to 29.

Concept note-3: -Which state will host the 2023 Men’s Hockey World Cup? Ans-Hockey world cup 2023 Men’s Hockey World Cup will be played from January 13 to 29, 2023 in Odisha.

Concept note-4: -The FIH received the following final three bids for the Men’s 2022 World Cup. In November 2019, India was confirmed to host the tournament in January 2023. For the preferred time window 1–17 July 2022: Belgium.
