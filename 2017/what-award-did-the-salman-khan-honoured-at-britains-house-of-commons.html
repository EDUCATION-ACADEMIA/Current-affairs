What award did the Salman Khan honoured at Britain’s House of Commons?

(A) ** Global Diversity Award

(B) Oscar Awards

(C) National Awards

(D) Cannes Festival Best Actor Award

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bollywood star Salman Khan received a Global Diversity Award at Britain’s House of Commons. Salman was at the House of Commons on Friday, and received the award from Keith Vaz, British Parliament’s longest-serving Asian MP.

Concept note-2: -Presenting the award, Keith Vaz, the longest serving Indian-origin MP in Parliament, said, “Salman Khan is a role model and a hero to millions of people worldwide.

Concept note-3: -In 1999, Khan won the Filmfare Award for Best Supporting Actor for his extended guest appearance in Kuch Kuch Hota Hai (1998). In 2011, he won the Screen Award for Best Actor for his performance in Dabangg and in 2012, he won the Best Actor Popular Choice for his performances in Ek Tha Tiger and Dabangg 2.

Concept note-4: -However, a lesser-known fact is that Salman has won not one but two National Film Awards. He marked his debut as a producer with the children film Chillar Party that won the National Film Award for Best Children’s Film. A few years later, he produced Bajrangi Bhaijaan besides starring in it.

Concept note-5: -Since 2012, Khan is in a relationship with Romanian actress Iulia Vântur.
