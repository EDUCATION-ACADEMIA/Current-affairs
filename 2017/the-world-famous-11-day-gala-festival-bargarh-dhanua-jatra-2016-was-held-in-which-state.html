The World famous 11-day gala festival Bargarh Dhanua Jatra 2016 was held in which state

(A) Bihar

(B) Punjab

(C) Andhra Pradesh

(D) ** Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sambalpur (Odisha), Jan 6 (PTI) The 11-day-long Baragrh Dhanu Jatra, which is believed to be the largest open-air theatre in the world, concluded on Friday.

Concept note-2: -The ‘Dhanu Yatra’ festival, the largest open-air theatre festival began after a gap of two years, in Bargarh at the Odisha. The vibrant Dhanu Yatra is associated with the culture of Odisha. The Festival is being held from December 27 to Jan 6, 2023.

Concept note-3: -Dhanu Jatra or Dhanu Yatra is an annual drama-based open air theatrical performance celebrated in Bargarh, Odisha. Spread across a 8 km radius area around the Bargarh municipality, it is world’s largest open air theater, one that finds a mention in the Guinness Book of World Records.

Concept note-4: -Dhanu Jatra Festival | Date, Time & Venue This biggest open-air theatre festival is held in the Bargarh district in Odisha. Dhanu Yatra 2022-2023 will be organised from December 28, 2022, to January 6, 2023.

Concept note-5: -The famous 11-day long Dhanu Jatra, considered as the largest open-air theatre in the world is celebrated in Odisha. Spread over an 8 km radius area around the municipality of Bargarh, it is the largest open-air theatre in the world, one that is listed in the Guinness Book of World Records.
