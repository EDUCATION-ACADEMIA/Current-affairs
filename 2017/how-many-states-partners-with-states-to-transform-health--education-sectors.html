How many states partners with states to transform Health & Education sectors?

(A) ** 6

(B) 5

(C) 4

(D) 3

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Read on to know all about it. By India Today Web Desk: NITI Aayog has recently launched ‘Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital’ (SATH), an initiative through which it will partner with three states and turn them into role models for other states by helping them improve their education and health sectors.

Concept note-2: -NITI Aayog has selected Uttar Pradesh, Assam, and Karnataka to improve healthcare delivery and key outcomes in these States.

Concept note-3: -Health education in India is a state government liability with the national health policy laying down the necessary health policy in India. The central council of health and welfare plans the various health care projects and health department reform policies.

Concept note-4: -Importance of health education They are qualified and certified to talk about these issues. Apart from this, they also undergo training related to health and hygiene to educate people. Health education is very important to enhance the overall health status of various communities and people.

Concept note-5: -Sustainable Action for Transforming Human Capital (SATH) focusses on two main sectors-Education and Health and to build three ‘Role Model’ States.
