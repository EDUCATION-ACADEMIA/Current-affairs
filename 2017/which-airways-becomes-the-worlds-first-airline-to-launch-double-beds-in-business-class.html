Which Airways becomes the World’s First Airline to launch Double beds in Business Class?

(A) ** Qatar Airways

(B) Singapore Airlines

(C) Etihad Airways

(D) ANA All Nippon Airways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Qatar Airways has launched the feature on a daily Boeing 777 service from London Heathrow.

Concept note-2: -The first airline to offer a full business class service, different seating from economy and first class and a different class of fare, was Qantas, which launched the first true business class in 1979.

Concept note-3: -Qatar Airways is the national airline of the State of Qatar. Based in Doha, the Airline’s trendsetting on-board product focuses on: comfort, fine cuisine, the latest in-flight audio & video entertainment, award-winning service and a modern aircraft fleet averaging around 5 years of age.

Concept note-4: -Qatar Airways-The World’s Best Airline It flies to all continents in the world (except Antarctica). Qatar Airlines has a hub and spoke model, with most of its flights channeling through Hamad International Airport.

Concept note-5: -Qatar Airways has an oryx, the national animal of the State of Qatar, as its logo.
