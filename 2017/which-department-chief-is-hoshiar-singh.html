Which department chief is Hoshiar Singh?

(A) Department of Telecommunications

(B) Department of Income Tax

(C) Department of Health

(D) ** Department of Personnel and Training (DoPT)

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hoshiar Singh Dahiya was born in Sisana village, Sonipat district, Haryana to Choudhary Hira Singh in a Jat family. After his schooling and one yearâ€™s study at the Jat College, Rohtak, he joined the Army. He was married to Dhano Devi, who is still alive as of December 2021.

Concept note-2: -Brigadier Hoshiar Singh OBE, Croix de Guerre, IOM, VSM was the commander of the Indian 62 Brigade stationed at Sela Pass. Brigadier Singh was killed in action, along with a few Indian troops during the 1962 Sino-Indian War. Brigadier Hoshiar Singh was commended for his bravery in the war.

Concept note-3: -On 15 December 1971, during India-Pakistan War Major Hoshiar Singh was commanding a company of 3 GRENADIERS and was ordered to capture the enemy locality of Jarpal. During the assault, his company came under intense shelling. Undeterred, he led the charge and captured the objective after a fierce hand-to-hand fight.
