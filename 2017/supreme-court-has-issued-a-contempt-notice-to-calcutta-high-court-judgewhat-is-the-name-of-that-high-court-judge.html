Supreme Court has issued a contempt notice to Calcutta High Court Judge.What is the name of that High Court Judge

(A) J.S. Khehar

(B) Dipak Misra

(C) Jasti Chelameswar

(D) ** C.S. Karnan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: For the first time, the Supreme Court today issued contempt notice to a sitting high court judge, Justice CS Karnan for levelling allegations against former judges of the Supreme Court and sitting judges of Madras High Court.

Concept note-2: -The current Chief Justice of the court is Justice Prakash Shrivastava.

Concept note-3: -High courts are headed by a chief justice. The chief justices rank fourteenth (within their respective states) and seventeenth (outside their respective states) on the Indian order of precedence.

Concept note-4: -According to the central notification, the nine new judges who will join are Biswaroop Chowdhury, Partha Sarathi Sen, Prasenjit Biswas, Uday Kumar, Ajay Kumar Gupta, Supratim Bhattacharya, Partha Sarathi Chatterjee, Apurba Sinha Ray and Md Shabbar Rashidi.
