Which program was inaugurated by the Goa chief minister Manohar Parrikar recently?

(A) ** Man of Developed India program

(B) Inauguration of app

(C) Inauguration of Hospital

(D) Election campaign Meeting

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Pramod Sawant (born 24 April 1973) is an Indian politician and the 11th and current Chief Minister of Goa. Sawant represents the Sanquelim constituency in the Goa Legislative Assembly and is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party.

Concept note-2: -On Wednesday, the Union Cabinet gave ex-post facto approval for naming Greenfield International Airport Mopa in Goa as ‘Manohar International Airport–Mopa, Goa’, as a mark of tribute to Late Manohar Parrikar, former Defence Minister and four-time Goa Chief Minister.

Concept note-3: -What does the term MOPA mean? The term MOPA is an abbreviation for Master Oscillator Power Amplifier. The master oscillator produces the beam while the optical amplifier is used for boosting the output power.

Concept note-4: -Dabolim Airport is located in the village of Dabolim in Goa. It is the only airport in the state and operates as a civil enclave in a military airbase named INS Hansa. TOTAL PASSENGER MOVEMENTS: Goa Airport handled around 84.6 Lakhs passengers during the financial year 2018-19.
