Which day is observed on May 21st across the Indian Country?

(A) Independence Day

(B) Labours Day

(C) ** Anti - terrorism Day

(D) Telangana Formation Day

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This day commemorates the death anniversary of one of the former Prime Ministers of India, Late Shri Rajiv Gandhi (20 August 1944 – 21 May 1991).

Concept note-2: -National days on Sun May 21st, 2023. Explore worldwide events, festivals, funny, weird, and national days on this day! It’s World Meditation Day, International Tea Day, I Need A Patch For That Day, National Memo Day, Rapture Party Day… and much more!

Concept note-3: -Labour Day (1st May) Labour Day is one of the most important days in May and it is celebrated across the world. Antarrashtriya Shramik Diwas or Kamgar Din is the name given to Labour Day in India.

Concept note-4: -The Anti-Terrorism Day came into being after on May 21, 1991, India’s former Prime Minister Rajiv Gandhi was assassinated during a campaign in Tamil Nadu. Rajiv Gandhi became the victim of a suicide bombing, which also resulted in around 14 other people being killed.

Concept note-5: -Q. Which special day is celebrated on May 2, and is also celebrated twice a year during autumn and spring? Notes: International Astronomy Day is celebrated twice a year, during autumn and spring.
