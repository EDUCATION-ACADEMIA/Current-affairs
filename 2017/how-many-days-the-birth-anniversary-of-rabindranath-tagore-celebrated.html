How many days the birth Anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore celebrated?

(A) 2 days

(B) 3 days

(C) 7 days

(D) ** 5 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 161st birth anniversary of Rabindranath Tagore is being observed on May 7, 2022. Gurudev, a name given to him by Mahatma Gandhi, became the first Indian and the first non-European to receive the Nobel Prize in Literature in 1913 for his work, Gitanjali.

Concept note-2: -To celebrate the 161st birth anniversary of one of the most loved and influential poets of all time, Rabindranath Tagore Jayanti will be observed on May 9 this year.

Concept note-3: -Rabindranath Tagore Jayanti is celebrated on the 7th of May as a cultural event throughout the nation in the remembrance of Rabindranath Tagoreâ€™s birth anniversary.

Concept note-4: -Prevalent among the people from the Bengali community, this public holiday falls on the 25th day of Baisakh as per the Hindu calendar i.e. on or near 7 May on the Gregorian calendar. In 2023, the festival will be observed on 9th May (Tuesday).
