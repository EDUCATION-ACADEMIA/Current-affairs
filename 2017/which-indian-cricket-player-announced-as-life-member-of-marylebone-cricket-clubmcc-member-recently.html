Which Indian Cricket player announced as life member of Marylebone Cricket Club(MCC) member recently?

(A) ** VVS Laxman

(B) Srinath

(C) Ashwin

(D) Parthiv Patel

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -VVS Laxman, the former batsman, has been awarded the honorary life membership of Marylebone Cricket Club. With this, he joined fellow Indian icons Sachin Tendulkar, Rahul Dravid and Sourav Ganguly on the prestigious list of MCC Honorary Life Members.

Concept note-2: -Anjum Chopra, a former Indian captain, has become the first Indian woman cricketer to get the prestigious honorary life membership of the Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC).

Concept note-3: -The MCC awards Honorary Life Membership of the Club to the finest sportsperson to have played the game. A total of 18 legendary cricketers, namely Hashim Amla, Ian Bishop, and Jacques Kallis, were included in the list. “MCC awards Honorary Life Membership of the Club to some of the world’s finest cricketers.

Concept note-4: -And that number came after a record year of female sign ups, with 134 names being added. The waiting list for full membership currently comprises around 11, 000 candidates, including 5, 500 associate members. Women make up approximately eight per cent of that total.
