Whom to share his thoughts in ’Mann Ki Baat’ program?

(A) ** Shri Narendra Modi

(B) Shri Vijay Sharma

(C) Shri Naresh Iyer

(D) Shri Arun Jaitley

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi to share his thoughts in ‘Mann Ki Baat’ programme on 26th February. Prime Minister Narendra Modi will share his thoughts with the people of the country and abroad in the ‘Mann Ki Baat’ programme on All India Radio at 11 AM on the 26th of this month. It will be the 98th episode of the monthly radio programme.

Concept note-3: -Mann Ki Baat (Hindi: , transl. Inner Thoughts) is an Indian radio programme hosted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in which he addresses the people of the nation on All India Radio, DD National and DD News. Since the first show on 3 October 2014, there have been 97 episodes.
