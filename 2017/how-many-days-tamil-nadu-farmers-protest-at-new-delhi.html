How many days Tamil nadu farmers protest at New Delhi?

(A) ** 31 days

(B) 28 days

(C) 16 days

(D) 17 days

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After two months of protests, farmer unions-mainly from Punjab and neighbouring Haryana-began a movement named Dili Chalo ( transl. Let’s go to Delhi), in which tens of thousands of union members marched towards the nation’s capital.

Concept note-2: -The 2021 Farmers’ Republic Day protest was a protest on 26 January 2021 at Delhi. Part of the 2020–2021 Indian farmers’ protest, it turned violent when a group of protestors deviated from the parade to storm the Red Fort.

Concept note-3: -As thousands of farmers sought to reach Jantar Mantar in the heart of the capital on Monday (August 22), Delhi Police said several border entry points including Tikri, Singhu, and Ghazipur were jammed and advised commuters to stay away.

Concept note-4: -NHAI suffered ₹2, 731.32 crore loss due to farmers’ protest, Parliament told | Latest News India-Hindustan Times.
