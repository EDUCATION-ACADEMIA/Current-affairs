Who has been appointed as the brand ambassador of OnePlus India

(A) Virat Kohli

(B) Priyanka Chopra

(C) ** Amitabh Bachchan

(D) Deepika Padukone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The oneplus India brand ambassadors are Shahid and Mira Rajput Kapoor for their Marketing & Advertising News, Smart TV Category, and ET BrandEquity.

Concept note-2: -Sharing his thoughts on the association, the renowned Bollywood actor Shahid Kapoor said, “As passionate enthusiasts of technology and innovation, Mira and I are thrilled to be partnering with OnePlus.

Concept note-3: -Bangalore, 18 June 2021: OnePlus, the global technology brand today welcomed renowned cricketer Jasprit Bumrah as Brand Ambassador for its Wearables category. The partnership with Bumrah reiterates the brand philosophy of ‘Never Settle’ and the company’s pursuit towards perfection.

Concept note-4: -Some recently appointed brand ambassadors: Randeep Hooda of Ramagya Mart ropes. Pooja Hegde of Ptron ropes. Tiger Shroff of GreatWhite ropes, Esports Premier League 2021. Ravindra Jadeja of ASICS.
