Which country is 100th rank in FIFA rating?

(A) Japan

(B) China

(C) ** India

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The FIFA Men’s World Ranking is a ranking system for men’s national teams in association football, led by Brazil as of 2022.

Concept note-2: -If we talk about the world ranking under FIFA Football Ranking 2022, then Brazil is at the top and after Brazil, Belgium, Argentina, France, England, Spain, Italy, Netherlands, Portugal and Denmark are making their own rankings.

Concept note-3: -INDIAN FOOTBALL TEAM FIFA RANKING They were 104 in the 2021 FIFA Rankings but dipped two places in March 2022. The Blue Tigers again jumped to 104 in June but lost two places to find themselves at 106.

Concept note-4: -India is in the 106th spot on the FIFA Ranking 2022 list. Indian men’s football team has a score of 1192 points. On the other hand, the Indian women’s football team is in 61st place with 1405.79 points. India has never qualified for FIFA World Cup.
