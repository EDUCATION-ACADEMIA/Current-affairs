How much billions the softbank invested on Paytm recently?

(A) ** 1.4 billion

(B) 3.5 billion

(C) 7.9 billion

(D) 6.3 billion

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SoftBank’s investment in Paytm was $1.6 billion.

Concept note-2: -While SoftBank has invested $1.6 billion into the digital payments firm over the years, among its biggest investments in India, at Paytm’s current share price, the 17.5% stake SoftBank held in the company before Thursday’s share sale is worth only about $900 million.

Concept note-3: -SoftBank had made a total investment of $1.6 billion in Paytm and taken out around $220-250 million in the company’s IPO last November, the report said.

Concept note-4: -According to the company’s December 2022 shareholding, Alibaba had a 6.26 per cent stake in Paytm and another affiliate firm Ant Financial held a 24.86 per cent stake.

Concept note-5: -SYDNEY: SoftBank Group Corp aims to sell shares worth up to $215 million in Indian e-payments giant Paytm’s parent One 97 Communications Ltd through a block deal, according to a term sheet reviewed by Reuters.
