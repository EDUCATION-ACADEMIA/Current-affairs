Who inaugurated theTelangana Solar Plant recently?

(A) Shri Narendra Modi

(B) ** Shri Arun Jaitley

(C) Shri Suresh Prabhu

(D) Shri Dinesh Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi inaugurates India’s largest floating solar projects-The Hindu BusinessLine.

Concept note-2: -The solar power plant Telangana I at Palwai village near Gadwal in the Mahbubnagar district of Telangana is a 12 megawatt (MWDC) photovoltaic power station, commissioned in June 2016.

Concept note-3: -Union Territory Administrator, Banwari Lal Purohit has inaugurated northern India’s largest floating solar power project of 2000kWp worth Rs 11.70 crore at waterworks, Sector 39, Chandigarh. The inauguration was held in the presence of MP Kirron Kher.

Concept note-4: -India’s largest floating solar power project in Telangana India’s largest floating Solar Power Project is now fully operational. NTPC declared Commercial Operation of the final part capacity of 20 MW out of 100 MW Ramagundam Floating Solar PV Project at Ramagundam, Telangana with effect from 1 July.

Concept note-5: -Telangana has 5 large solar plants with a total capacity of 178MW. Along with this, soon this state will have a 100 MW capacity of solar power plant, which will be the largest floating solar power plant in India. Apart from this 15 MW solar power plant is also in process.
