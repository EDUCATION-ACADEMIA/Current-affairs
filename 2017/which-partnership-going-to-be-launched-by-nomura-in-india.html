Which partnership going to be launched by Nomura in india

(A) ** Fintech partnership

(B) Health care Partnership

(C) Wealth management Partnership

(D) Financial Advisory Partnership

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ‘Voyager-Nomura FinTech Partnership’ programme in India was launched earlier this year with an aim to build innovative solutions for CMIB that can be deployed across the firm and financial services industry, a company release said.

Concept note-2: -Nomura Capital (India) Private Limited ("NCIPL") was incorporated on 4 August 2009, to conduct the following business activities: Loans to corporates including origination, syndication and securitization, Structured financing (including promoter and acquisition financing) Asset Backed Securitization.

Concept note-3: -Nomura Holdings, Inc. (, Nomura hōrudingusu kabushiki gaisha) is a Japanese financial holding company and a principal member of the Nomura Group.

Concept note-4: -Essentially, big banks can outsource to FinTech companies that are dedicated to a particular solution. FinTech companies can provide capabilities that can be integrated into current processes or customer experiences for an immediate improvement that does not require upkeep by the bank.
