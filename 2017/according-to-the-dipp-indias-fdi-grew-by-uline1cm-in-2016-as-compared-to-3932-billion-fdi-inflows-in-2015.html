According to the DIPP, India’s FDI grew by _________ in 2016 as compared to $39.32 billion FDI inflows in 2015.

(A) 12 %

(B) 9 %

(C) 23 %

(D) ** 18 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Computer Software and Hardware becomes the top recipient sector of FDI Equity inflow with a share of around 25%

Concept note-2: -New Delhi: India improved its ranking by one notch to 9th position as one of the highest recipients of foreign direct investment (FDI) in 2016, at a time global FDI flows fell, the UN trade and development agency said in its latest World Investment Report 2017.

Concept note-3: -India’s foreign direct investment (FDI) inflow increased from USD 45.15 billion in 2014-2015 to USD 84.84 billion in 2021-22, said Minister of State for Commerce and Industry, Som Parkash said in reply to a parliament question on Wednesday.

Concept note-4: -Services sectors have attracted the highest Foreign Direct Investment flows into India during 2014-2015.
