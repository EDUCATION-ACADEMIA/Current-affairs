Which network partner with Facebook to provide more Express Wifi?

(A) ** Bharati Airtel

(B) Vodafone

(C) Idea

(D) Jio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -“With Express Wi-Fi, Cisco and Facebook will extend access to the internet in underserved areas where connectivity can offer more opportunities for development and growth.”

Concept note-2: -Express Wi-Fi is a carrier-provided connectivity service bringing fast and affordable internet to neighborhoods around the world.

Concept note-3: -India. Service offered in partnership with internet service providers AirJaldi, LMES, NetVision, Tikona and Shaildhar. in Rajasthan, Uttrakhand, Gujarat, Mizoram and Meghalaya.

Concept note-4: -Explanation : Facebook launched its “Express Wi-Fi” in India to help more people get online. It means a Free Basics platform, ties up with entrepreneurs to help them set up public WiFi hotspots and helping them provide internet to a lot of citizens in poor or no connectivity areas.
