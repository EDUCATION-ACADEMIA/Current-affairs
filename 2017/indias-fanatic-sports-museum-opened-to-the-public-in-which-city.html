India’s Fanatic Sports Museum opened to the public in which city

(A) Mumbai

(B) Chennai

(C) New Delhi

(D) ** Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Fanattic Sports Museum (FSM) is a sports museum dedicated to various types of sports and is located at Ecospace Business Park in Action Area-II of New Town, Kolkata, India.

Concept note-2: -Where: Ecospace, New Town, West Bengal. (40min by road from Central Kolkata; the nearest metro stop is Salt Lake City.) Entry: Tickets ₹100. Discounts available for students of all ages, with valid ID (₹50).

Concept note-3: -It is this sports legacy that may have played its invisible hand in bringing about India’s first sports museum in Kolkata. Named Fanatic Sports Museum (FSM), this is a first of its kind set up located in Newtown region of Kolkata.
