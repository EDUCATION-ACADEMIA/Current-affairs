Which state government has announced an Idea2POC (Proof of Concept) fund of Rupee.10 crore for women entrepreneurs.

(A) ** Karnataka

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Odisha

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This scheme is launched by the Karnataka government to provide an early stage funding to ideas or concepts to establish the proof of concept of the women technocrats.

Concept note-2: -Stree Shakti Yojana The Stree Shakti package is a unique government scheme for women that supports entrepreneurship by providing certain concessions. This woman loan scheme is eligible for women who have the majority of ownership in the business.

Concept note-3: -Mudra Yojana Scheme Mudra Yojana is a scheme that might benefit women seeking to start or expand their small business. Though this is not a scheme designed exclusively for women, it is highly beneficial. Women entrepreneurs can apply for a loan ranging between ₹50000 to ₹10 lakh.

Concept note-4: -State Bank of Mysore SBM has two schemes for women entrepreneurs, the Annapurna Scheme and the Stree Shakti package.

Concept note-5: -NITI Aayog launched the Women Entrepreneurship Platform (WEP) initiative to provide a platform to women entrepreneurs across the country. Under this initiative, the Small Industries Development Bank of India (SIDBI) has collaborated with NITI Aayog to provide financial assistance to women entrepreneurs.
