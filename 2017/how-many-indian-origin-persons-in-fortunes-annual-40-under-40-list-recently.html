How many Indian-Origin Persons in Fortune’s Annual ‘40 Under 40’ List recently?

(A) ** 5

(B) 6

(C) 9

(D) 8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Among the list, 2 Indian-origin entrepreneurs Kanav Kariya and Ankit Gupta have featured in the 2022 edition of Fortune’s 40 under 40 list that also includes celebrated artist Rihana and world’s most-followed TikToker Khaby Lame.

Concept note-2: -Vineeta Singh, Sugar Cosmetics; Pratik Gauri, 5ire; Pranav Goel, Porter; Rashi Agarwal, Zypp; Ankit Tandon, OYO join the elite list of BW Businessworld’s 40 Under 40, 2022.

Concept note-3: -The Economic Times 40 under Forty is the definitive listing of corporate India’s future leaders.

Concept note-4: -To be eligible for consideration in the Top 40 Under 40 Class of 2023, nominees must be over the age of 18 and UNDER the age of 40 on November 1, 2023. Candidates who turn 40 between the nomination deadline and November 1 are NOT eligible.
