Which village bags the smart village award?

(A) ** Govardhan Village, Thane District

(B) Hallur, Dharwad district

(C) Hampi, Bellary district

(D) Dharapuram, Tiruppur District

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Let’s get to know Dhanora, India’s first Smart Village | Times of India Travel.

Concept note-2: -ACF adopted ‘Mangi Village’ Becomes Smart Village at District Level. Setting an example of self-reliance, good infrastructure and scenic surroundings, Ambuja Cement Foundation’s (ACF) adopted core village-Mangi in Chandrapur, Maharashtra-was awarded the R.R Aba Patil Smart Village Award at the district level.

Concept note-3: -The Govardhan Eco Village, set up by the International Society for Krishna Consciousness (ISKCON) in Wada taluka of Palghar district, Maharashtra has won the Green Platinum Award.

Concept note-4: -Smart Village refers to a concept developed in rural area that provides solutions to problems occurred and improves the quality of life. The main problems faced by rural areas are cover poverty, low level of education, and limited access to technology.
