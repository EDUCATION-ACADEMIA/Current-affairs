Who won the French Open to Clinch 4th Super Series Title of 2017?

(A) ** Kidambi Srikanth

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) Prakash Padukone

(D) Chetan Anand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kidambi Srikanth lifts French Open, wins fourth Super Series title of 2017 | Sports News, The Indian Express.

Concept note-2: -The winner of 2017 French Open badminton men’s singles title is Kidambi Srikanth. Kidambi Srikanth of India beats Kenta Nishimoto of Japan in the final to win the 2017 french open badminton men’s singles title.

Concept note-3: -K. Srikanth is the first Indian to win the Australian Open Badminton superseries title in men’s singles. On 25 June 2017, Kidambi Srikanth won the 2017 Australian Open Men’s Singles title by defeated Olympic champion Chen Long. Kidambi Srikanth also won the Indonesia Open Super Series Premier.
