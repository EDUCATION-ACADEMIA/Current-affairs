Which union Minister launched the skill for Life, Save a Life initiative to promote skill development in the health sector?

(A) ** Shri J P Nadda

(B) Shri Rajnath Singh

(C) Shri Arun Jaitley

(D) Shri Kannan Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -J P Nadda, Hon’ble Minister for Health & Family Welfare “We would like to convey our heartfelt thanks to the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and Ministry of Human Resource and Development, for partnering with us on this initiative.

Concept note-2: -Profile of Hon’ble Minister Dharmendra Pradhan is the Cabinet Minister for Education and Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in the Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The Skill Mission launched by the Prime Minister on 15 July 2015, has gathered tremendous steam under the guidance of Shri Dharmendra Pradhan, Minister for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship and Shri Rajeev Chandrasekhar, Minister of State for Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.

Concept note-4: -Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana. Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana is the name of the skill development program of the Government of India.
