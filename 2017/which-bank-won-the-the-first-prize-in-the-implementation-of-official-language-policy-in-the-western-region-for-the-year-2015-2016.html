Which bank won the the first prize in the implementation of Official Language Policy in the western region for the year 2015-2016

(A) Indian Bank

(B) SBI

(C) ** Central Bank

(D) Canara Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Central Bank of India has been awarded First Prize for excellent implementation of Official Language Policy of Government of India in Mumbai Metro area for the year 2015 2016.

Concept note-2: -Subject: Rajbhasha Kirti Puraskar Yojna for In–house Hindi journals published by the Ministries/ Departments of the Central Government / Public Sector Undertakings / Autonomous Bodies/Headquarters of the Government Sector Banks and Financial Institutions in the year 2016-17 (financial year).

Concept note-3: -The Rajbhasha Gaurav Puraskar is given for writing a book or excellent article by any citizen of India in the Hindi language. This award is given to encourage the writing of Hindi language books related to technical / science.

Concept note-4: -The Rajbhasha Kirti Puraskar is the highest award given by the Government of India in the field of official language implementation.

Concept note-5: -However, article 343(1) of the Indian constitution specifically mentions that, “The official language of the Union shall be Hindi in Devanagari script.
