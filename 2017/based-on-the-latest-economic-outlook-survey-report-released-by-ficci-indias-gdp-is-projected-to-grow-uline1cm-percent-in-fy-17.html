Based on the latest Economic Outlook Survey report released by FICCI, India’s GDP is projected to grow _________ percent in FY 17

(A) 7.3

(B) 5.9

(C) 8.1

(D) ** 6.8

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s GDP to grow 7.4% in 2022-23: FICCI survery | 5paisa Blog.

Concept note-2: -The latest round of FICCI’s Economic Outlook Survey puts forth an annual median GDP growth forecast for 2022-23 at 7.0 percent-with a minimum and maximum growth estimate of 6.5 percent and 7.3 percent respectively.

Concept note-3: -The International Monetary Fund (IMF) has also lowered India’s gross domestic product (GDP) growth projection for 2022-23 to 6.8% from the earlier 7.4%, citing the impact of a slowing global economy, stubborn inflation, rising interest rates and the Ukraine war.

Concept note-4: -The Economic Survey 2022-23 estimated India’s GDP growth at 6.5 per cent, which is lower than the projected 7 per cent growth for the ongoing financial year.
