(B) Raj

(C) Suresh Kumar

(D) Naresh Iyer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ashok Kumar is an alumnus of University of Delhi where he studied B.Sc.

Concept note-2: -Mr. Vikram K Doraiswami joined the Indian Foreign Service in 1992. Prior to joining the Indian Government, he worked for one year as a journalist.

Concept note-3: -Asoke Kumar Mukerji (born December 1955) is a former Indian diplomat and writer, who teaches diplomacy at the Diplo Foundation. He was Permanent Representative of India to the United Nations from April 2013 to December 2015.

Concept note-4: -An Ambassador is the chief diplomat in an embassy. A High Commissioner is the chief diplomat in a high commission.
