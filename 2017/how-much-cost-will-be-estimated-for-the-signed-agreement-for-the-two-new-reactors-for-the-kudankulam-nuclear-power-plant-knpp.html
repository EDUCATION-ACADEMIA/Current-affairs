How much cost will be estimated for the signed agreement for the two new reactors for the Kudankulam Nuclear Power Plant (KNPP)?

(A) 20, 000 crores

(B) ** 50, 000 crores

(C) 60, 000 crores

(D) 35, 000 crores

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advanced nuclear reactors are estimated to cost $5, 366 for every kilowatt of capacity. That means a large 1-gigawatt reactor would cost around $5.4 billion to build, excluding financing costs. By contrast, a new wind farm costs just $1, 980 per kilowatt.

Concept note-2: -Companies that are planning new nuclear units are currently indicating that the total costs (including escalation and financing costs) will be in the range of $5, 500/kW to $8, 100/kW or between $6 billion and $9 billion for each 1, 100 MW plant.
