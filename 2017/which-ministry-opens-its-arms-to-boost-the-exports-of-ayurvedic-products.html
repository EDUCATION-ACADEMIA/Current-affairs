Which ministry opens its arms to boost the exports of Ayurvedic products?

(A) Ministry of Telecommunication

(B) Ministry of Health and Affairs

(C) ** Ministry of Ayush

(D) Ministry of External Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Ayush has taken following steps towards promoting export of Ayurvedic medicines: Ministry has signed 25 Country to Country MoUs for Cooperation in field of Traditional Medicine and Homoeopathy with foreign nations.

Concept note-2: -Under Central Sector Scheme for promotion of International Cooperation, (IC Scheme), the Ministry of AYUSH undertakes various steps to promote and publicize AYUSH systems of medicine including Ayurveda across the globe.

Concept note-3: -It was then renamed as the Department of Ayurveda, Yoga, and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homoeopathy (Ayush) in November 2003 with focused attention towards education and research in Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha, and Homoeopathy.

Concept note-4: -Union Ayush Minister Sarbananda Sonowal on Monday launched a dedicated storefront for Ayurveda products on Amazon.in at a virtual event.
