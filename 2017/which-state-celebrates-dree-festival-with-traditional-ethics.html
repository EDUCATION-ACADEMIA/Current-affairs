Which state celebrates Dree festival with traditional ethics?

(A) Kerala

(B) ** Arunachal Pradesh

(C) Uttar Pradesh

(D) West Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Arunachal Pradesh. Dree is celebrated in order to bring a bountiful harvest season in the state of Arunachal Pradesh. It is widely celebrated by The Apatanis, who inhabit a tranquil pine-clad valley called Ziro at the core of Lower Subansiri District of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -THE DREE FESTIVAL is an agricultural festival celebrated by the Apatani tribes of Arunachal Pradesh. Every year, it is observed on 5th July with great fervour and zest by the Apatanis. The impressive celebration takes place at Ziro in Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -This festival is celebrated on 5th of July and you can witness this auspicious festival in Ziro district of Arunachal Pradesh. Dree is celebrated in order to bring a bountiful harvest season, hence, Apatani offers prayers to four Gods they follow-Tamu, Harniang, Metii, and Danyi.

Concept note-4: -The correct answer is Arunachal Pradesh. “Dree Festival” is a famous crop harvest festival, observed in Arunachal Pradesh.
