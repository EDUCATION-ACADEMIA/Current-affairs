Which medical institute won the RS Five crore for maintaining high standards of sanitation and hygiene under the Government’s Kayakalp awards.

(A) NEIGRIHMS

(B) PGIMER

(C) NITRD

(D) ** AIIMS Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Delhi won first prize under Central Government Hospitals category of Kayakalp awards. AIIMS was rewarded with ₹ 5 crore, for maintaining high standards of sanitation and hygiene.

Concept note-2: -Bhubaneswar: Adding another feather to its cap, the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), Bhubaneswar, has once again earned recognition in the country for ensuring cleanliness and providing a safe environment to the patients.

Concept note-3: -The scheme is intended to encourage and incentivize Public Health Facilities (PHFs) in the country to demonstrate their commitment for cleanliness, hygiene and infection control practices.

Concept note-4: -NEW DELHI: The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) on Friday bagged the first prize from the Centre for maintaining cleanliness within the institute premises and showing dedication towards the mission of the ‘Swachh Bharat Abhiyan’.
