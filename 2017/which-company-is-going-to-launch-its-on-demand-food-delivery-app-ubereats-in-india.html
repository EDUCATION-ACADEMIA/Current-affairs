Which company is going to launch its on-demand food delivery app UberEATS in India.

(A) Ola

(B) Flipkart

(C) Epay

(D) ** Uber

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uber has launched UberEATS in India, with Mumbai the first city to go live with the service.

Concept note-2: -STORY OUTLINE. Zomato has acquired the Indian operations of Uber Eats, the food delivery biz run by Uber, for around $350 million.

Concept note-3: -1. Zomato. Zomato is, without a doubt, one of the best online food delivery apps in India and has almost become synonymous with the same. It provides customers with detailed review of restaurants along with the current menu and food delivery options available.

Concept note-4: -Uber Eats is an online food ordering and delivery platform launched by Uber in 2014. Meals are delivered by couriers using cars, scooters, bikes, or on foot. It is operational in over 6, 000 cities across 45 countries as of 2021.

Concept note-5: -Zomato acquired Uber Eats for an all-stock acquisition deal.
