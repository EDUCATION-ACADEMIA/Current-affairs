Who won the 29th the National Snooker Championship title

(A) Aditya Mehta

(B) Geet Sethi

(C) Anuja Thakur

(D) ** Pankaj Advani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aditya Mehta, representing PSPB (Petroleum Sports Promotion Board), stunned multiple-time World Champion Pankaj Advani (also representing PSPB) to win the title in the National Snooker Championships held in Pune.

Concept note-2: -Aditya Mehta defeated multiple-time world champion Pankaj Advani 6-2 to win the National Snooker Championship.

Concept note-3: -Pankaj Arjan Advani (born 24 July 1985) is an Indian billiards and former professional snooker player. He is a 25-time International Billiards and Snooker Federation (IBSF) world champion.

Concept note-4: -Champion: David Causier David Causier is once again on top of the world of English Billiards as he defeated outgoing champion Peter Gilchrist 1776-1092 in the final to win the 2022 Grand Cuvee World Billiards Championship in Singapore.

Concept note-5: -Pankaj Arjan Advani had won the 34th National Snooker Championship, he is a 23-time world champion of the International Billiards and Snooker Federation. Pankaj has also received several government accolades from India, including the Padma Bhushan in 2018.
