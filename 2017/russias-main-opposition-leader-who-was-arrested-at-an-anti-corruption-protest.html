Russia’s main opposition leader, who was arrested at an anti-corruption protest?

(A) Dmitry Medvedev

(B) ** Alexei Navalny

(C) Vladimir Putin

(D) Alexander Rutskoy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Navalny was a Russian Opposition Coordination Council member. He is the leader of the Russia of the Future party and founder of the Anti-Corruption Foundation (FBK). He is recognised by Amnesty International as a prisoner of conscience, and was awarded the Sakharov Prize for his work on human rights.

Concept note-2: -The largest protests and main opposition events include rallies to support the old NTV staff (2001), mass protests against Mikhail Zurabov’s reforms (2005), Dissenters’ March (2005–2008), Russian Marches, “I am free!
