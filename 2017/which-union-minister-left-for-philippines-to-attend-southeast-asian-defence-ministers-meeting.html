Which union minister Left for Philippines to Attend Southeast Asian Defence Ministers’ Meeting?

(A) Shri Arun jaitley

(B) Shri Vijay Prakash

(C) Shri Suresh Menon

(D) ** Smt Nirmala Sitharaman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The ADMM-Plus is a platform for ASEAN and its eight Dialogue Partners Australia, China, India, Japan, New Zealand, Republic of Korea, Russia and the United States (collectively referred to as the “Plus Countries”), to strengthen security and defence cooperation for peace, stability, and development in the region.

Concept note-2: -Raksha Mantri participates in 9th ASEAN Defence Ministers’ Meeting plus in Cambodia; Calls for urgent & resolute global efforts to counter transnational & cross-border terrorism.

Concept note-3: -India became the dialogue partner of ASEAN in 1992 and the inaugural ADMM-Plus was convened in Hanoi, Vietnam on October 12, 2010.

Concept note-4: -The Inaugural ADMM-Plus was convened in Ha Noi, Viet Nam, on 12 October 2010.
