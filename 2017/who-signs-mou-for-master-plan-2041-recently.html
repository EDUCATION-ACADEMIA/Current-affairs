Who signs MoU for Master Plan 2041 recently?

(A) ** Delhi Development Authority (DDA)

(B) National Institute of Technology

(C) Anna university

(D) Kerala University

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Centre has informed the Supreme Court that the Master Plan for Delhi (MPD)-2041 will be placed before the Authority, the Delhi Development Authority’s (DDA) highest decision-making body, in the first week of December 2022.

Concept note-2: -It was prepared with the assistance of Ford Foundation team. It was promulgated on 1-9-1962. It was formulated as per the provisionof Delhi Development Act, 1957. It aimed at the integrated development of Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The Draft Master Plan for Delhi-2041 (MPD-2041) has been divided into 2 volumes and 9 sections, which contain 22 chapters. The Vision for MPD-2041 is to “Foster a Sustainable, Liveable and Vibrant Delhi by 2041". Don’t miss out on ET Prime stories!

Concept note-4: -To be Delhi’s fourth master plan, the Master Plan 2041 offers a development perspective for the national capital for the next 20 years-from 2021 to 2041. However, till the time the vision document for the next two decades is not implemented, the Delhi Master Plan of 2021 will be in force.
