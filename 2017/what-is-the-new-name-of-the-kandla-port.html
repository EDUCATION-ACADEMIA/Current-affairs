What is the new name of the Kandla Port?

(A) ** Deendayal Port

(B) Chennai Port

(C) Kochi Port

(D) Kamaraj Port

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kandla Port was renamed as Deendayal Port in 2017 under the Indian Ports Act, 1908.

Concept note-2: -The Union Ministry of Shipping has renamed Kandla Port Trust as Deendayal Port Trust, by exercising powers conferred on it under Indian Ports Act, 1908. The name was changed as part of yearlong centenary celebrations of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay, who had stood for uplift of poor and weaker sections of society.
