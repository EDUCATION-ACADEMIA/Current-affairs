Who inaugurated the Bicentenary Celebration of Paika Rebellion recently?

(A) Shri Rajesh Sharma

(B) Shri Suresh raj

(C) Shri Vivek Raj

(D) ** Shri Pranab Mukherjee

Concept note-1: -The rebellion took place in Odisha in 1817 under the leadership of Bakshi Jagabandhu. Commander of the forces of king Khordha, Bakhshi is regarded as one of the earliest freedom fighters of India. It was the extortion and oppression of the Paikas in Odisha by the East India Company that led to the rebellion.
