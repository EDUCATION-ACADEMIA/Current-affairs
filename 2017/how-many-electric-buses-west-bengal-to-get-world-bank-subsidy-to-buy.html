(A) ** 130

(B) 125

(C) 190

(D) 185

Concept note-1: -The World Bank has agreed to provide a subsidy of Rs seven lakh per bus, Adhikari said during the question hour. While 100 buses would ply in Kolkata, the remaining 30 have been earmarked for industrial towns of Durgapur and Asansol.

Concept note-2: -As per reports, Out of the total 1, 200 e-buses, 400 will be functional by January 2023. This initiative is being taken by the state government to tackle the problem of air pollution in the city.
