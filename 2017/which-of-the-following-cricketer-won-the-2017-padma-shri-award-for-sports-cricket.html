Which of the following Cricketer won the 2017 Padma Shri Award for Sports-Cricket

(A) Yuvraj Singh

(B) Ravichandran Ashwin

(C) Ravindra Jadeja

(D) ** Virat Kohli

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Virat Kohli. Virat Kohli received the Padma Shri award in 2017. Virat Kohli is an Indian cricket player. Kohli captained India Under-19 cricket team to victory at the 2008 Under-19 World Cup.

Concept note-2: -Virat Kohli (2017) He won the Padma Shri award in 2017.

Concept note-3: -New Delhi, Jan 25: India’s captain Virat Kohli and seven other sports persons were on Wednesday (January 25) confered the prestigious Padma Shri award, country’s 4th highest civilian honour.

Concept note-4: -Padma Shri 2022 winners in sports: Neeraj Chopra. Sumit Antil. Pramod Bhagat. Avani Lekhara.
