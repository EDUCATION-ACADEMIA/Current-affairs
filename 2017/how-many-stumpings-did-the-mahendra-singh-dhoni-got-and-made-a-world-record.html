How many Stumpings did the Mahendra Singh Dhoni got and made a world Record?

(A) ** 100

(B) 90

(C) 250

(D) 300

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -195: MS Dhoni has made 195 stumpings in international cricket as player. Also he remains to be the only keeper to have 100-plus stumpings in ODIs alone.

Concept note-2: -Former India captain Mahendra Singh Dhoni is widely regarded as one of the greatest wicketkeepers in the game. Dhoni has 444 dismissals to his name in 350 ODIs, which is the third-highest for a wicketkeeper in the format. 123 of those include stumpings–the highest by any keeper so far.

Concept note-3: -Winning All 3 ICC Trophies During his tenure as captain, MS Dhoni won all 3 ICC Trophies-World T20 in 2007, One Day World Cup in 2011, and Champions Trophy in 2013 –which no other Indian captain in the history of cricket has achieved before.

Concept note-4: -Dhoni holds the world record for most stumpings (123) in ODI.

Concept note-5: -Stumping by Dhoni to stump out West Indies batsman Keemo Paul in 0.08 seconds is the fastest stumping. 4.
