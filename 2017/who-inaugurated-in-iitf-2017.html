Who inaugurated in IITF 2017?

(A) Shri Vignesh raj

(B) Shri Suresh Sharma

(C) ** Shri Hunar Haat

(D) Shri Kailash Rajan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By India Today Web Desk: The President of India, Ram Nath Kovind, inaugurated the 37th India International Trade Fair (IITF)-2017, yesterday (November 14, 2017) at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -On November 14, Union Minister of Commerce and Industry, Piyush Goyal inaugurated the trade fair which provides an excellent venue for showcasing Indian goods as part of the “Vocal for Local” campaign and revitalising the Indian economy.

Concept note-3: -The International Table Tennis Federation (ITTF), founded in 1926, is the world-governing body of the sport of table tennis with 226 member Associations in the world. The ITTF supervises the staging of World Championships annually, and several other world title events.

Concept note-4: -The India Trade Promotion Organization conducts the India International Trade Fair, one of the biggest trade shows in India. It is a yearly event that begins on November 14 and lasts 14 days at Pragati Maidan in Delhi.

Concept note-5: -The partner states of IITF 2022 are Bihar, Jharkhand, and Maharashtra, whereas the focus states are Uttar Pradesh and Kerala. ii. IITF 2022 featured exhibitors from 29 States/Union Territories (UTs), with Leh (Ladakh) participating for the first time this year.
