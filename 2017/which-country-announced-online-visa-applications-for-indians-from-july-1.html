Which country announced online visa applications for Indians from July 1?

(A) United States of America

(B) Russia

(C) China

(D) ** Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Netherlands You can now apply for The Netherlands’ visa across six cities in India for select visa categories. As of July 1, 2021, fully vaccinated travellers are allowed entry. Fully vaccinated persons may travel for any purpose. Vaccine Declaration for Covid-19 to be presented with visa application.

Concept note-2: -The India tourist e-Visa or e-tourist visa was introduced by the government of India as an official document that allows travelers who do not have Indian citizenship to enter upon arrival in India.

Concept note-3: -E-visa. E-visa service has now been restored and you can apply for certain categories of visas online. Check that you are eligible before you apply and apply in good time. Visit the Government of India’s e-Visa website for details.
