Prime Minister Narendra Modi has inaugurated the 104th Indian Science Congress in which city

(A) Chennai

(B) Kochi

(C) Ranchi

(D) ** Tirupati

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It was inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in Mumbai University. Studies and papers on Ancient Indian Vedas were presented in this Congress. Accomplishments of Ancient Indian Science in the fields of medicine, mathematics, surgery etc.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Tirupati. The 104th Indian Science Congress was held at Tirupati. The 104th Indian Science Congress (ISC) has been inaugurated by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on January 3, 2017, at the Sri Venkateswara University in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -The 107 Indian Science Congress 2022 was held at the University of Agricultural Sciences, GKVK Campus, Bangalore, Karnataka in January 2022.

Concept note-4: -108th Indian Science Congress will be held from January 3-7, 2023 at R.T.M. Nagpur University, Nagpur, Maharashtra, India.

Concept note-5: -Nobel Laureate Dr. Ada Yonath appealed all to take this glorious tradition ahead. The 108th Indian Science Congress organised at Rashtrasant Tukdoji Maharaj Nagpur University concluded today.
