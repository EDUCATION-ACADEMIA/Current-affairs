Which Ministry has has released the new format for recording and reporting road accident data.

(A) ** Ministry of Road Transport and Highways

(B) Ministry of Finance

(C) Ministry of science and Technology

(D) Ministry of Human Resource Management

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Crash Data | Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of Road Transport and Highways has published the annual report ‘Road accidents in India-2021’.

Concept note-3: -Road Safety | Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India.

Concept note-4: -The Integrated Road Accident Database (iRAD) Project is an initiative of the Ministry of Road Transport and Highways (MoRTH), Government of India and is funded by World Bank, with objective to improve road safety in the country.

Concept note-5: -It is observed that the rate of deaths per thousand vehicles in 2021 has increased from 0.45 in 2020 to 0.53 in 2021. 4, 03, 116 road accidents caused 1, 55, 622 deaths and injuries to 3, 71, 884 persons during 2021.
