Which month China’s Tianzhou-1 going to be launched?

(A) May

(B) June

(C) ** April

(D) August

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tiangong, (Chinese: “Heavenly Palace”) any of a series of three Chinese space stations, the first of which was launched on September 29, 2011.

Concept note-2: -On 31 October 2013, CMSA announced the new names for the whole space station program: The precursor space labs would be called Tiangong (Chinese: ; pinyin: Tiān Gōng; lit. ‘Sky Palace’), code TG. Tiangong-1 and Tiangong-2 were launched respectively in 2011 and 2016.

Concept note-3: -China sends second Shiyan-10 test satellite sent into orbit with its final launch of 2022. HELSINKI-China has conducted its 64th and final launch of the year, sending the Shiyan-10 (02) into orbit from Xichang spaceport.

Concept note-4: -On 15 October 2003, the Long March 2F rocket successfully launched the Shenzhou 5 spacecraft, carrying China’s first astronaut into space.
