Which company launched the floating dock for Indian Navy?

(A) Tech Mahindra

(B) ** Larsen & Toubro

(C) Ashok Leyland

(D) Ford Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The launch of Floating Dock (FDN-2), Yard 55000, designed in-house and built by L&T Shipyard, Kattupalli is a milestone in India’s quest for self-reliance in shipbuilding.

Concept note-2: -India’s first floating terminal for liquefied natural gas (LNG) at Jaigarh in Maharashtra is expected to be operational in the second half of 2022. Besides, another floating terminal at Jafrabad in Gujarat is also likely to begin operations this calendar year.

Concept note-3: -Indian Naval Ship (INS) Mormugao (D67), second warship of the P15B class of stealth guided-missile destroyers, was commissioned in the august presence of Raksha Mantri Shri Rajnath Singh at Naval Dockyard, Mumbai on December 18, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Reliance Naval and Engineering Limited (R-NAVAL)-Pipavav Shipyard.
