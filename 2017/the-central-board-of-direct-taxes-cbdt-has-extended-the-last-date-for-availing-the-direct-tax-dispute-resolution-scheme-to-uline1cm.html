The Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) has extended the last date for availing the Direct Tax Dispute Resolution Scheme to _________

(A) March 31, 2017

(B) December 31, 2016

(C) ** January 31, 2017

(D) April 01, 2017

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Last date of payment under Vivad se Vishwas extended Last date of payment of amount under Vivad se Vishwas (with additional amount) has been notified as 31st October, 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Dispute Resolution Committee (the Committee)5 has been constituted under the Scheme which would be responsible for adjudicating these income tax related disputes. The taxpayers who have return income of up to Rs 50 lakhs and having dispute not exceeding Rs 10 lakhs can access the Scheme.

Concept note-3: -As per Section 6 of The Direct Tax Vivad se Vishwas Act, 2020, the DA will not carry out any proceedings in respect of an offence, or impose or levy any penalty, or charge any interest in respect of the tax arrears.
