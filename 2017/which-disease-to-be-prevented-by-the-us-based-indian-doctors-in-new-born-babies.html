Which disease to be prevented by the US based Indian Doctors in new born babies?

(A) Breathing problems

(B) Hypoglycemia

(C) ** Sepsis

(D) Macrosomia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Common health problems in babies include colds, coughs, fevers, and vomiting. Babies also commonly have skin problems, like diaper rash or cradle cap. Many of these problems are not serious.

Concept note-2: -The programme provides vaccination against eight life-threatening diseases (diphtheria, whooping cough, Haemophilus influenzae type B (Hib) causing pneumonia and meningitis, tetanus, polio, tuberculosis, measles and hepatitis B) in the entire country.
