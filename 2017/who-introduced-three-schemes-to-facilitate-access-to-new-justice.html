Who introduced three schemes to facilitate access to new justice?

(A) Mr. Suresh Prabhu

(B) ** Mr.Ravi Shankar Prasad

(C) Mr.Arun Jaitley

(D) Mr.Rajnath singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -INITIATIVES TOWARDS ACCESS TO JUSTICE PROJECTS Incorporation of Legal literacy into National Literacy Mission Authority (NLMA). Activities started in States-62 Districts of Uttar Pradesh and 31 Districts of Rajasthan. Establishing Helpdesks for Juveniles in Observation Homes in Maharashtra. and Jharkhand.

Concept note-2: -The fountain source of law in India is the Constitution which, in turn, gives due recognition to statutes, case law and customary law consistent with its dispensations. Statutes are enacted by Parliament, State Legislatures and Union Territory Legislatures.

Concept note-3: -People are provided with fundamental rights by the constitution, which is enforced by law, and if it is violated or denied by any state authority, can be sued in court. This shows that all citizens of India have equal access to the court.

Concept note-4: -Justice can be attained in the society only by correct interpretation and implementation of Laws. Justice means to give each and every person what they deserve. Justice and fairness are closely associated terms which are sometimes used in place of each other.
