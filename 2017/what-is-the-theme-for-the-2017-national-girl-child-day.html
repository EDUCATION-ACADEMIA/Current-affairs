What is the theme for the 2017 National Girl Child Day

(A) Girl child day is safety and Development of girl child

(B) ** Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao.

(C) Girls’ Progress = Goals’ Progress: What Counts for Girls

(D) No Girl Left Behind: Unlocking Data to Power the Girls’ Rights Movement

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -> Theme for national girl child day of 2017 was “Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao (BBBP)”. >

Concept note-2: -The 2016 theme was “Girls’ Progress = Goals’ Progress: What Counts for Girls", the 2017 theme was “EmPOWER Girls: Before, during and after crises", and the theme for 2018 was “With Her: A Skilled Girl Force".

Concept note-3: -However, the slogan for National Girl Child Day 2021 is “Digital Generation, Our Generation". In the year 2019, the theme was ‘Empowering Girls for a Brighter Tomorrow’ and ‘My voice, Our Common Future’ was the theme in the year 2020. National Girl Child Day 2023: How it is celebrated?

Concept note-4: -All girls deserve better-the world deserves better. This year’s theme puts the international community on notice. It calls upon us to do better collectively. Because if anniversaries are normally associated with celebration, this year is a wake-up call to refocus attention on girls and their solutions.

Concept note-5: -International Day of the Girl Child theme for 2012: Ending child marriage.
