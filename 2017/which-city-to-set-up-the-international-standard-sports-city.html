Which city to set up the International Standard Sports City?

(A) ** Odisha

(B) Chennai

(C) Mumbai

(D) Kolkata

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Ahmedabad. A new sports complex will be built in Naranpura, Ahmedabad, which will be recognised as “the sports city of India.” It will be able to host any international sporting event, together with the Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel Sports Enclave and Narendra Modi Stadium in Motera.

Concept note-2: -Abdul Rahim Al Zarooni is the Founder and Chairman of Dubai Sports City and the Al Zarooni Group of Companies.

Concept note-3: -Located on Mohammad Bin Zayed Road the residential aspect of the project consists of mid-rise apartment buildings, townhouses and villas. Sports City contains three distinct residential districts: Canal Residence, Victory Heights and Gallery Villas.

Concept note-4: -areas or supplemented existing facilities to create a themed sport zone. Some have branded these. areas as “sports cities” to give them visibility and coherence.
