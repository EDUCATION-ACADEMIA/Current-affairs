Which game will be featured at 2022 Asian Games?

(A) ** Video Games

(B) Volley ball

(C) Badminton

(D) Cycling

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On February 4, Ningbo “New Year Cup” Futsal Football Tournament 2023 came to an end in Ningbo Public Fitness Instruction Centre Football Pitch. There are 40 sports including swimming, archery, athletics, badminton, baseball and softball, basketball, boxing, etc., and 61 disciplines at Asian Games Hangzhou 2022.

Concept note-2: -On 12 September 2019, baseball, softball, karate, and sport climbing (which were optional events at the then-upcoming 2020 Summer Olympics) were added to the programme, expanding it to 61 disciplines in 40 sports.

Concept note-3: -Mobile Legends: Bang Bang from Moonton, Garena Free Fire (including Garena Free Fire MAX) and Roblox rounded out the top five most installed mobile games in Southeast Asia for the month.

Concept note-4: -The Hangzhou 2022 Asian Games are scheduled to take place from September 23 to October 8, having originally been due to take place in 2022 before being postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic. Weightlifting competition during Hangzhou 2022 is due to be held at the Xiaoshan Sports Center Gymnasium.
