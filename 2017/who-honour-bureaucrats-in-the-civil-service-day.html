Who honour bureaucrats in the civil service day

(A) ** Prime Minister Narendra Modi

(B) Mr.Suresh Prabhu

(C) Mr Arun Jaitley

(D) Mr Naveen Patnaik

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Civil servants are the actual makers of Indian law and policy. They work on behalf of the elected government and cannot publicly show their disinterest or disapproval for it. It is mandatory for them to form certain rules and policies according to the government’s views and interests.

Concept note-2: -For his efforts, Charles Cornwallis is known as the ‘Father of Civil Service in India’.

Concept note-3: -Civil Service Day theme 2022 is “Vision India-Bringing Citizens and Government Closer.” This theme indicates that a dedicated day such as National Civil Service Day brings the bureaucracy and citizens closer together by highlighting the efforts of the civil servants.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Cabinet Secretary. The Cabinet Secretary is also the ex-officio Chairman of the Civil Services Board.
