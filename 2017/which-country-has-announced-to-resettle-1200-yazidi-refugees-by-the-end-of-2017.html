Which country has announced to resettle 1200 Yazidi refugees by the end of 2017

(A) Indonesia

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) Iraq

(D) ** Canada

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -News release. Over the past few years, the Government of Canada has kept its commitment to help Yazidi refugees and other survivors of Daesh start new lives in this country. Over 1, 400 have settled in Canada since 2017, escaping unimaginable horrors at the hands of Daesh.

Concept note-2: -The 2021 Census revealed Toowoomba is now home to the largest group of Yazidi people in Australia. Services are under major strain trying to supply interpreters and case workers to almost 3, 000 people.

Concept note-3: -30, 000-40, 000 Yazidis fled to Syria, 100, 000 Yazidis took refuge in Kurdish controlled Zakho, Iraq. In Syria, the UNHCR provided material and transportation to Yazidi refugees and local Syrian communities cooked them meals.

Concept note-4: -Those who survived experienced sexual slavery, torture, family separation and forced displacement. Canada welcomed more than 1400 Yazidi refugees, mostly women and girls, as Government Sponsored Refugees (GARs).

Concept note-5: -Sinjar was liberated a year later, but some 3, 000 Yazidis are still missing, and over 200, 000 remain displaced.
