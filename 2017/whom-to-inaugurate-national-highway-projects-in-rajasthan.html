Whom to inaugurate National Highway projects in Rajasthan?

(A) ** Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi

(B) President Shri Ram Nath Kovind

(C) Shri Nithish Kumar

(D) Shri Naresh Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This project is managed by the National Highways Authority of India (NHAI) under the Ministry of Road, Transport and Highways. The NHDP represents 49, 260 km of roads and highways work and construction in order to boost economic development of the country.

Concept note-2: -The longest national highway of Rajasthan that connects the state to the Kandla port of Gujarat is NH-15.

Concept note-3: -There is a total of 20 national highways with a total stretch of 6373 kms in Rajasthan including NH-3, NH-8, NH-11, NH-12, NH-14, NH-15, etc.

Concept note-4: -NH-8 length is around 688 kms in the Rajasthan state. Other national highways in the state are as follows: NH 11, NH 3, NH 11B, NH 11A, NH 14, NH 12, NH 65, NH 15, NH 79, NH 76, NH 89, NH 79A, NH 112, NH 90, NH 113, NH 116, and NH 114.
