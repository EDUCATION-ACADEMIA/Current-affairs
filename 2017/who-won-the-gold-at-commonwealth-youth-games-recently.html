Who won the Gold at Commonwealth Youth Games recently?

(A) ** Sachin Siwach

(B) Mary Kom

(C) Shiva Tapa

(D) Manoj Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -AP Wrestler Vinesh Phogat celebrates after winning gold medal on Day 9 of the competition at the 2022 Commonwealth Games. India ended its spectacular run at the Commonwealth Games with a total of 61 medals placing it at the fourth spot in the overall medals tally.

Concept note-2: -45-year old Lawn Bowls player Sunil Bahadur was the oldest player in the contingent. India’s first medal of the Games was won by Sanket Sargar with a silver in weightlifting. Saikhom Mirabai Chanu won the first gold medal for the country, also in weightlifting.

Concept note-3: -Commonwealth Games 2022, Day 11 Highlights: India Finishes 4th With 22 Gold Medals. India won four gold medals on the final day of Commonwealth Games 2022 through PV Sindhu, Lakshya Sen, Achanta Sharath Kamal and the men’s doubles badminton pair. India finished on 61 medals, including 22 gold, 16 silver and 23 bronze.
