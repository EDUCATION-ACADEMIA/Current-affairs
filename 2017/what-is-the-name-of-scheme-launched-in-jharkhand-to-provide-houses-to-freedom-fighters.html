What is the name of scheme launched in Jharkhand to provide houses to freedom fighters?

(A) Jan Dhan Yojana

(B) Atal Pension Scheme

(C) ** Shaheed Gram Vikas Yojana

(D) Pradhan mantri Mudra Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government has started Meethi Kranti Yojana (Sweet Revolution Scheme) after the success of the blue revolution. Scheme is applicable to all 27 districts of the state to quadruple the farmer‘s income by 2022. Jharkhand farmers will now get farm loan on zero percent interest rate.

Concept note-2: -Now under this scheme, the girl child will be given Rs 2500 for enrollment in class 8, Rs 2500 for enrollment in class 9, Rs 5000 for enrollment in class 10, Rs 5000 for enrollment in class 11 and Rs 5000 for enrollment in class 12. .

Concept note-3: -The state government of Jharkhand launched the ‘Shaheed Gram Vikas Yojana’ on 18 September 2017 to develop villages of freedom fighters.

Concept note-4: -Jharkhand, staring at a drought-like situation, has set an ambitious target of linking 25 lakh farmers with the Kisan Credit Card (KCC) scheme by March 2023.
