Which country has successfully tested a new version of a DF-5C missile with 10 nuclear warheads

(A) USA

(B) Russia

(C) Japan

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -DF-5C. China has begun testing a new variant of a DF-5 missile, which has a MIRV with 10 nuclear warheads. It is called the DF-5C.

Concept note-2: -Dongfeng 5 (CSS-4) The DF-5 (CSS-4) is an intercontinental ballistic missile (ICBM), designed to carry a 3 megaton (Mt) nuclear warhead to distance up to 12, 000 km.

Concept note-3: -Though 2020 reports had the number at almost 300, there are no official reports saying how many China nukes there are in 2022. Some estimates and rumors put current numbers at anywhere between 350 and 750, however.

Concept note-4: -India conducted its first nuclear explosive test in 1974 and possesses nuclear weapons, full nuclear fuel cycle capabilities, and a variety of nuclear delivery systems.
