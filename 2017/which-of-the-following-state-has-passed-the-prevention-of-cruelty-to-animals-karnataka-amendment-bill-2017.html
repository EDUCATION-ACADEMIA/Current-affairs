Which of the following state has passed the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Karnataka Amendment) Bill, 2017

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) Punjab

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) ** Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prevention of Cruelty to Animals (Tamil Nadu Amendment) Act, 2017 (Act No. 1 of 2017). An Act to amend the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act, 1960 so as to preserve the cultural heritage of the State of Tamil Nadu and to ensure the survival and wellbeing of the native breeds of bulls.

Concept note-2: -The objects of this Act are to prevent cruelty to animals, promote the welfare of animals by requiring a person in charge of an animal to provide care for the animal, and to treat the animal in a humane manner, and to ensure the welfare of the animal.

Concept note-3: -[26th December, 1960.] An Act to prevent the infliction of unnecessary pain or suffering on animals and for that purpose to amend the law relating to the prevention of cruelty to animals.

Concept note-4: -Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Amendment (Animal Sentience) Bill 2022. Long Title: An Act to amend the Prevention of Cruelty to Animals Act 1979 to recognise the sentience of animals and their intrinsic value, to define cruelty to an animal by reference to different forms of suffering, and for other purposes.
