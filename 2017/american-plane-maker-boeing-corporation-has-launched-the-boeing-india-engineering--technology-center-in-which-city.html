American plane maker Boeing Corporation has launched the Boeing India Engineering & Technology Center in which city

(A) New Delhi

(B) Mumbai

(C) Ahmedabad

(D) ** Bengaluru

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Elevation of the upcoming Boeing engineering and technology campus in Bengaluru, Karnataka that will conduct core engineering and research for Boeing’s iconic products as well as future platforms. The campus will be one of the largest for Boeing, outside of the US.

Concept note-2: -Boeing India Engineering & Technology Center (BIETC) In our new Engineering and Technology Center in Bengaluru, you can work on cutting-edge projects that will drive the future of aerospace.

Concept note-3: -Over 300 Indian suppliers are manufacturing critical systems and components, including aerostructures, wire harness, composites, forgings, avionics mission systems and ground support equipment, for some of Boeing’s most advanced commercial and defence aircraft.

Concept note-4: -Boeing India currently helps its parent source supplies worth $1 billion per year from India of which 66% is actual manufacturing and the remaining is software and services.
