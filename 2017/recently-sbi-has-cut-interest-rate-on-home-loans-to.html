Recently, SBI has cut interest rate on home loans to

(A) ** 8.3 %

(B) 5.2 %

(C) 6.3 %

(D) 7.4 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI is offering a concession of up to 0.25 percent on home loans, with rates starting at 8.4 percent. It has also waived off the processing fee till January 2023.

Concept note-2: -The rates remain at 9.60% for scores of 650 to 699, 9.90% for scores of 550 to 649, and 9.50% for scores of NTC/NO CIBIL/-1. Additionally, SBI is offering borrowers with a CIBIL Score of 750 or higher a 5 basis point discount on card rates for MaxGain & Realty loans (apart from CRE Loans).

Concept note-3: -From April till December 2022 in fiscal FY23, RBI has hiked the repo rate by 225 basis points taking it to the highest level since August 2018 at 6.25%.

Concept note-4: -State Bank of India, HDFC cut home loan rates to 8.4% for new applicants | Business Standard News.
