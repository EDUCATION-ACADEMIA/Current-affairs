Which party sweeps the by poll elections in Malappuram Lok Sabha constituency?

(A) AIADMK

(B) Aam Adami

(C) ** Indian Union Muslim League (IUML)

(D) BJP

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Malappuram Lok Sabha Constituency went for bypoll on 6 April 2021 along with the 2021 Kerala Legislative Assembly Election. On May 2, the results were announced along with the results of the Legislative Assembly Elections. Former Rajya Sabha MP Abdussamad Samadani won the election by a margin of 1, 14, 615 votes.

Concept note-2: -There are 16 Legislative Assembly Constituencies in district. These are included in 3 Lok Sabha constituencies.

Concept note-3: -As of the 2021 assembly elections, the current MLA is Najeeb Kanthapuram of IUML.

Concept note-4: -In addition to the 29 MPs representing the state in the upper and lower houses, there are 4 MPs from Kerala who have either been nominated or elected from other states . Totally there are 33 MPs working for Kerala and representing Kerala in both houses in Parliament.
