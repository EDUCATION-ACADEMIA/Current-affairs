Which team got defeated by the Mumbai Indians in the IPL finals recently?

(A) Sunrisers Hyderabad

(B) Chennai Super Kings

(C) ** Rising Pune Supergiant

(D) Kolkata Knight Riders

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai Indians defeated Chennai Super Kings by 1 run to lift the IPL trophy for the record fourth time. Mumbai Indians became the only team to have four IPL trophies, and the most successful team ever.

Concept note-2: -Gujarat Titans won IPL 2022 by 7 wickets. Gujarat Titans and Rajasthan Royals were in the finals, and the finals were held on 29th May 2022, in Ahmedabad. Under the Captaincy of Hardik Pandya, Gujarat Titans have created history in their debut season.

Concept note-3: -And for the first time in their history, Mumbai Indians finished last on the points table after losing 10 out of their 14 matches.

Concept note-4: -Delhi Capitals On Top With Most Losses in IPL Delhi Capitals is topping the undesirable list of teams with the most losses in the history of IPL. In 202 IPL games of 13 IPL editions, they have lost 118 matches out of 224. The Delhi-based team has a win percentage of 46.94.

Concept note-5: -Mumbai Indians team is the most successful IPL team, having won the tournament five times and having finished runners-up on one occasion.
