Which firms tops Brand finance 2017 list?

(A) Cognizant

(B) ** Tata Group

(C) Virtusa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With a brand value of more than $245 billion, BrandZ’s top 100 most valuable global brands ranking for 2017 saw Google retain its top spot, Apple trails right behind with $234 billion.
