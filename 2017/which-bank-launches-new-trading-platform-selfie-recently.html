Which bank launches new trading platform Selfie recently?

(A) Dena Bank

(B) ** Federal bank

(C) Bank of India

(D) Canara Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Geojit BNP Paribas on Wednesday launched a new trading platform branded as Selfie, which is as a one-stop-shop for investment decisions, and is available for all asset classes, except commodities. Selfie is available on the web as well as tablets, specifically for Geojit’s clients.

Concept note-2: -Federal Bank – Geojit. Fed-e-Trade is a feature packed 3 in 1 account. An unbeatable combination of Savings account and Demat account with Federal bank and Trading account with Geojit. Selfie is a next generation trading platform which combines several new web technologies to meet your emerging needs.

Concept note-3: -Mr. C. J. George, a financial services industry entrepreneur, is the founder and Managing Director of Geojit Financial Services Ltd.

Concept note-4: -The wide range of products and services on offer includes: Equities, Derivatives, Currency Futures, Custody Accounts, Mutual Funds, Life Insurance & General Insurance, e-Insurance, IPOs, Portfolio Management Services, Property Services, Margin Trading and Loans against Shares.

Concept note-5: -Key in the order details like Exchange, Symbol, Series, Price condition, Quantity and Price etc. in the order panel, click on submit option. After verifying the details, click on “confirm” to send the order to exchange. Note: Trading can also be done via the phone by calling at 1800 103 5503/1800 425 5501.
