Who elevates T V Narendran as global CEO, MD?

(A) ** Tata Steel

(B) Ashok Leyland

(C) L & T Infotech

(D) Mahindra & Mahindra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Narendran has been appointed Managing Director and CEO of Tata Steel. Tata Steel has elevated T V Narendran as Chief Executive Officer and Managing Director globally, the company said on Tuesday.

Concept note-2: -Mr. T V Narendran is the CEO & Managing Director of Tata Steel Limited.

Concept note-3: -T V Narendran is the CEO & Managing Director of Tata Steel Limited. As the CEO & MD, he has overseen the organic and inorganic growth of Tata Steel over the last few years. He has over 34 years of experience in the Mining and Metals industry.

Concept note-4: -T. V. Narendran, CEO & Managing Director of Tata Steel Limited was the President of the Confederation of Indian Industry (CII) for the year 2021-22. In May 2022, He was succeeded by Sanjiv Bajaj as the President of CII. On 31 October 2017, he was appointed as the global CEO and MD of Tata Steel.
