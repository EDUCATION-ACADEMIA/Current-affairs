India’s first international exchange India INX at the International Financial Service Centre (IFSC) at GIFT inaugurated in which city

(A) Kochi

(B) New Delhi

(C) Chandigarh

(D) ** Gandhinagar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PM Modi inaugurates India’s first bullion exchange at GIFT city, Gandhinagar-The Hindu.

Concept note-2: -PM Modi to visit India’s first International Financial Services Centre in Gujarat. New Delhi [India], July 27 (ANI): Prime Minister Narendra Modi will visit India’s maiden International Financial Services Centre (IFSC) in Gujarat International Finance Tec-City (GIFT City) on Friday.

Concept note-3: -It is located at the International Financial Services Centre, GIFT City in Gujarat. Ministry of Finance, Government of India. It was inaugurated by Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi on 9 January 2017.

Concept note-4: -Gujarat: PM Modi launches India’s first International Bullion Exchange in Gandhinagar. Prime Minister Narendra Modi today launched India’s first International Bullion Exchange (IIBE) at Gujarat International Finance Tech (GIFT) City in Gandhinagar today.

Concept note-5: -The International Financial Services Centres Authority (IFSCA) has been established on April 27, 2020 under the International Financial Services Centres Authority Act, 2019. It is headquartered at GIFT City, Gandhinagar in Gujarat.
