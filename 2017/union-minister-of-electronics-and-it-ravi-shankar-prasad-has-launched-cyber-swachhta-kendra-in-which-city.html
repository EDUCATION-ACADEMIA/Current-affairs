Union Minister of Electronics and IT Ravi Shankar Prasad has launched Cyber Swachhta Kendra in which city

(A) Banglore

(B) Ahmedabad

(C) Chennai

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Information technology Act, 2000 (“IT Act”) is the primary frame work for cyber security and provides the whole enquiry process for governing cyber-crimes. The IT Act contains provisions for the protection of electronics records.

Concept note-2: -By India Today Web Desk: M-Kavach app is an initiative by the government of India along with the Cyber Swachhta Kendra to add security to the smartphone devices that create a multi-factor authentication application system.

Concept note-3: -In a joint effort with Cyber Swachhta Kendra under the Indian Computer Emergency Response Team (CERT-In), Ministry of Electronics and IT, MicroWorld has built a Bot Removal Tool that assists clients in removal of botnet infection and other malware from their computers.
