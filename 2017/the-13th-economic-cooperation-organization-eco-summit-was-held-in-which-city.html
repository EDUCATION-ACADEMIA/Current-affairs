The 13th Economic Cooperation Organization (ECO) Summit was held in which city

(A) New Delhi

(B) Jakarta

(C) Kabul

(D) ** Islamabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -13th ECO Summit was held in Islamabad.

Concept note-2: -The Secretariat: Located in Tehran. Ambassador Hüseyin Avni Bıçaklı currently serves as one of the Deputy Secretary Generals. Relations of Türkiye with the Organization: Türkiye is one of the three founding members of the ECO along with Iran and Pakistan.

Concept note-3: -Ecosummit Berlin 14-15 June 2023 Ecosummit accelerates smart green startups in energy, mobility and cities to save our climate, decarbonise our economy and electrify our planet. Our events are startup markets enabling fundraising, lead and co-investing, corporate venturing, open innovation and M&A.

Concept note-4: -Since ECO enlargement in 1992, 15 Summit meetings have been held. The last meeting, 15th ECO Summit, was held on November 28, 2021 in Ashgabat, Turkmanistan.

Concept note-5: -In 1985, RCD was renamed to the Economic Cooperation Organization (ECO).
