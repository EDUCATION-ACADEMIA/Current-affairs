Which award did Lionel Messi got recently?

(A) ** European Golden Shoe Award

(B) National Award

(C) Lebanese Cinema Movie Guide Awards

(D) Canadian Screen Awards

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lionel Messi won Golden Ball, the best player award, at the 2022 Fifa World Cup in Qatar Sunday night (December 18), as he led Argentina to their third triumph on the global stage.

Concept note-2: -Messi makes Golden Ball history. Lionel Messi has won the adidas Golden Ball for Qatar 2022, with Kylian Mbappe and Luka Modric claiming the Silver and Bronze Balls.

Concept note-3: -Argentina and Benfica midfielder Enzo Fernandez has been named the best youngster at the 2022 World Cup. WHAT HAPPENED? The 21-year-old midfielder has won the Best Young Player of the Tournament award at World Cup 2022.

Concept note-4: -France’s Kylian Mbappe won the Golden Boot award at Qatar 2022 courtesy his hat-trick in the final.

Concept note-5: -Lionel Messi, who is known as the world’s best player, won the Iffhs award 2022. Including this award now Messi has a total number of five times record of IFFHS playmaker. In the second number Spain’ Xavi won the total number of award four times.
