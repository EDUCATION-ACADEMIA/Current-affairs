Which date is celebrated as World Environment Day across the world?

(A) May 3

(B) ** June 5

(C) April 7

(D) July 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Environment Day (WED) is celebrated annually on 5 June and encourages awareness and action for the protection of the environment. It is supported by many non-governmental organizations, businesses, government entities, and represents the primary United Nations outreach day supporting the environment.

Concept note-2: -This day acts as a reminder to people to be more considerate and aware of the Earth’s existing conditions, in terms of the environment. On World Environment Day, people take action to spread awareness about the environment and also motivate others to do the same, in hopes of creating a better future.

Concept note-3: -At the Stockholm Conference, the idea of World Environment Day was formalized, with the first one being celebrated in 1973. In early June 2022, the high-level Stockholm+50 international meeting will be held in Sweden, a few days before World Environment Day.

Concept note-4: -World Environment Day 2023 to be hosted by Côte d’Ivoire with a focus on solutions to plastic pollution. Nairobi/Abidjan, 2 February 2023 – Côte d’Ivoire will host World Environment Day 2023 on 5 June with the theme of solutions to plastic pollution, UN Environment Programme (UNEP) and Côte d’Ivoire announced today.

Concept note-5: -World Environment Day. First held on 5 June 1973, World Environment Day has grown into a global platform for raising environmental awareness and spurring environmental action.
