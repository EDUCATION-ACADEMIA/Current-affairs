Which country is invited as the Guest country for the upcoming 2017 St. Petersburg International Economic Forum

(A) USA

(B) Russia

(C) China

(D) ** India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In response, SPIEF forums have adjusted their focus to highlight leading politicians and business executives from various countries such as the People’s Republic of China, India, Turkey, Iran, Egypt, United Arab Emirates, Serbia, Belarus, Armenia, Kazakhstan, Cuba, Nicaragua and the Central African Republic.

Concept note-2: -In January 2022 ST. PETERSBURG was the number 2 in total exports and the number 3 in total imports in Russia. Exports In 2021, Russia’s St. Petersburg exported $29.9B, making it the 2nd largest exporter out of the 85 exporters in Russia.

Concept note-3: -Petersburg International Economic Forum is set to take place on 15–18 June 2022. As in previous years, the business programme will focus on the global and Russian economies, social issues, and technological development.
