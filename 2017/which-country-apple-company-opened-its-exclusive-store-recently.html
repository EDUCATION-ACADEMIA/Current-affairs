Which country Apple company opened its exclusive store recently?

(A) New York

(B) Denmark

(C) ** Singapore

(D) California

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apple is already hiring for Apple Stores in Mumbai and Delhi, the two locations where the flagship retail stores will open first. Upping its game in India, Apple is expected to launch two retail stores in the country, in Mumbai and then New Delhi, around April this year, it is learnt.

Concept note-2: -The first Apple Store in India could open by March 2023, according to the latest report by Financial Times. Apple has started hiring for its retail store positions in India as it readies to open the first flagship store sometime in Q1 2023. Apple set to open physical stores in India in 2023, starts hiring.

Concept note-3: -Additionally, India does not allow company-branded retail stores unless the same conditions are met. That particular requirement was mostly intended to target Apple. The plan worked, with Apple asking its suppliers and iPhone assemblers to establish manufacturing plants within India.
