Who partnered with the Paytm recently?

(A) ** National Payments Corporation of India (NPCI)

(B) Indian Railways

(C) Indian Airlines

(D) Bharat Petroleum Limited

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -With this partnership, Paytm and Jana Small Finance Bank aims at further driving the digital payments revolution in India.

Concept note-2: -Paytm Partners with Jana Small Finance Bank for Deployment of Card Devices, Aims to Further Drive Digitisation Among Merchants.

Concept note-3: -We have today launched ‘Paytm Payment Protect’, a group insurance plan in association with HDFC ERGO General Insurance, a leading general insurance company of India, to insure transactions made through UPI across all apps and wallets.

Concept note-4: -Paytm’s pre-IPO investors like Warren Buffet, SoftBank, Elevation Capital, Alibaba are long-term investors.

Concept note-5: -Paytm Postpaid is a Buy Now, Pay Later facility offered by Paytm’s NBFC partners (Aditya Birla Finance Limited and Fullerton India Credit Company Limited). This facility is available in the form of line of credit (small on-demand loans) that can be used to make online/offline purchases on 1crore + websites & merchants.
