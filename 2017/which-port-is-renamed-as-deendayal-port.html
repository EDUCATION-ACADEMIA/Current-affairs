Which port is renamed as Deendayal Port?

(A) Kochi Port

(B) Chennai Port

(C) ** Kandla port

(D) Port Blair port

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kandla, now officially Deendayal Port Authority, is a seaport and town in Kutch district of Gujarat state in Western India, near the city of Gandhidham. Located on the Gulf of Kutch, it is one of Indiaâ€™s major ports on the west coast.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. Kandla Port in India has been renamed as Deendayal Port. It is situated in the Kutch district of Gujarat. It has been named after Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Gujarat. The Kandla port (Gujrat): One of the top 12 major ports in the country, had been rechristened as Deendayal Port in the name of Pandit Deendayal Upadhyaya.

Concept note-4: -While inaugurating various projects at Kandla Port in May this year, Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi had given the suggestion for renaming of Kandla Port after Pandit Deendayal Upadhyay, who stood for the uplift of the poor and weaker sections of society.

Concept note-5: -Three major ports namely Deendayal Port, Visakhapatnam Port and Kamarajar Port are having a depth of 18m or more.
