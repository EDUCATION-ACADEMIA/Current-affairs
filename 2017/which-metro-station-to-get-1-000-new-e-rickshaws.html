Which metro station to get 1, 000 new e-rickshaws?

(A) Kolkata Metro Station

(B) Chennai Metro Station

(C) ** Gurugram Metro Station

(D) Bengaluru Metro Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mini Metro E Rickshaw is an electric three-wheeler passenger carrier that comes in a single chassis with a cabin variant with a fully built body and a range of advanced technologies and features that makes it one of the best at its price point, which starts at just ₹1.10 lakhs.

Concept note-2: -Passenger Electric Rickshaw, Max Speed: 35 km/h.

Concept note-3: -Less speed – E-rickshaws are usually slow in speed and it runs around 30-35 kmph and thus, it is unable to meet the speed of other vehicles. It is not a preferred mode of transportation during an emergency period.
