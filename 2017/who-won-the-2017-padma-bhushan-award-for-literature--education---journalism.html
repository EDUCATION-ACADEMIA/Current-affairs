Who won the 2017 Padma Bhushan Award for Literature & Education - Journalism

(A) Ratna Sundar Maharaj

(B) ** Cho Ramaswamy (Posthumous)

(C) Balbir Dutt

(D) Bhawana Somaaya

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Cho Ramaswamy. Cho Ramaswamy was awarded the 2017 Padma Bhushan Award in the category of Literature and Education-Journalism.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sharad Pawar. In 2017, Sharad Pawar was honoured with the Padma Vibhushan 2017.

Concept note-3: -Sanjeev Kapoor was awarded Padma Shri 2017 for his culinary contributions.

Concept note-4: -In the field of literature and education, Shri Radheyshyam Khemka (Posthumous) will be honored with the Padma Vibhushan and Ms. Pratibha Ray, Swami Sachidanand Shri Vashishth Tripathi will be honored with Padma Bhushan. The list of Padma Shri awardees includes Pro.

Concept note-5: -The 2017 Padma Bhushan Award in the field of Art-Music was awarded to Vishwa Mohan Bhatt. Bhatt is a Grammy-winning Hindustani classical music instrumentalist who plays the Mohan Veena.
