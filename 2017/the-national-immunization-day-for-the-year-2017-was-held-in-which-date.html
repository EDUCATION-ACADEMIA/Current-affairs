The National Immunization Day for the year 2017 was held in which date

(A) 18 April

(B) 31 December

(C) 9 October

(D) ** 29 January

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Background note on Immunization :-Expanded Programme on Immunization was launched in 1978. It was renamed as Universal Immunization Programme in 1985 when its reach was expanded beyond urban areas.

Concept note-2: -National Vaccination Day. The Government of India observes 16 March as National Vaccination Day to acknowledge and appreciate the hard work of frontline health care workers to ensure the vaccination of every child.
