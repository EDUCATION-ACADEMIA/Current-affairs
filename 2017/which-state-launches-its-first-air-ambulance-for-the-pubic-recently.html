Which state launches its first air ambulance for the pubic recently?

(A) ** Assam

(B) Bihar

(C) Punjab

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Karnataka. Karnataka Chief Minister B.S. Yediyurappa unveiled the country’s first integrated air ambulance service. ICATT, a team comprising emergency medical service experts, and Kyathi, an aviation technology firm, have come together to start integrated air-ambulance service.

Concept note-2: -ICATT-KYATHI launches India’s first integrated “AIR AMBULANCE” services. It is South India’s first fixed wing air ambulance, to be stationed at Bangalore equipped with ISOLATION POD for safe transport of COVID-19 patients.

Concept note-4: -History. Dr. AP Ranga Rao is credited with first conceptualizing 108 ambulance services in India, having spent 10 years working with the National Health Service in Britain learning about emergency systems.
