Which state government has inaugurated the Campaign against Drug Addiction

(A) Assam

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) ** Himachal Pradesh

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -More than 60% of all illicit drugs seized in India are from Punjab. According to a study, most addicts are between the age group of 15 and 35 and many are unemployed.

Concept note-2: -Vimukthi is an anti-narcotics campaign launched by the Government of Kerala to highlight the seriousness of the situation and to create awareness among the people, especially the youth.

Concept note-3: -World Drug Campaign UNODC leads the global campaign to raise awareness about the major challenge that illicit drugs represent to society as a whole, and especially to the young.

Concept note-4: -BCCI president Sourav Ganguly is brand ambassador of the campaign.
