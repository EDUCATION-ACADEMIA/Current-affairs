Who was named “Actor of Year” in GQ Men Of The Year Awards 2017?

(A) Irrfan Khan

(B) Ranveer Singh

(C) ** Rajkummar Rao

(D) Aamir Khan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Apart from Mr. Khan, it was Hasan Minhaj who had the audience in splits with his sense of humour, as he accepted GQ’s International Man of the Year Award.

Concept note-2: -Our GQ Men of the Year bash, hosted by editor-in-chief Jim Nelson, has it all. GQ cover stars (Gal Gadot, Robert Pattinson), movie stars (Armie Hammer), music legends (John Legend), TV gods (Kyle MacLachlan), and more folks who managed to win 2017 (Taika Waititi, Ali Wong, Timothée Chalamet).

Concept note-3: -Houston Texans defensive end J.J. Watt was named the 2017 Walter Payton NFL Man of the Year. Considered the league’s most prestigious honor, the Walter Payton NFL Man of the Year Award recognizes a player for his excellence on and off the field.

Concept note-4: -Meet GQ Men Of The Year 2022 winners: Ranveer Singh, Allu Arjun, Deepika Padukone and other awardees of this year’s MOTY. Kartik Aaryan achieved what many others could not this year: get Hindi film audiences back into cinemas post the pandemic.
