Who is sworn in as Governor of Tamil Nadu?

(A) ** Banwarilal Purohit

(B) Surijith Singh

(C) Banarala Nithya

(D) Sukumar Suresh Rajan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Honorable Thiru R.N. Ravi, Governor of Tamil Nadu is the Constitutional Head of the state. Hon’ble Chief Minister of Tamil Nadu Thiru M.K.Stalin is the Head of the elected Government and heads the Council of Ministers.

Concept note-2: -Ravindra Narayana Ravi (born 3 April 1952) is an Indian politician and former bureaucrat serving as the current and 15th Governor of Tamil Nadu. Ravi served as the 18th Governor of Nagaland from 1 August 2019 to 9 September 2021 and Governor of Meghalaya from 18 December 2019 to 26 January 2020.

Concept note-3: -They exist in the state appointed by the president of India for a term of five years and they are not local to the state that they are appointed to govern. The factors based on which the president evaluates the candidates is not mentioned in the constitution.

Concept note-4: -Shri Banwarilal Purohit is a senior political personality having shaped public affairs in a big way for last four decades. Shri Purohit has been three time Lok Sabha member from Nagpur and Managing Editor of ‘The Hitavada’, Central India’s oldest and English daily for last one hundred years.
