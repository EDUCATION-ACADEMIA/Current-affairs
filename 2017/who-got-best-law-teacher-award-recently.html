Who got Best Law Teacher Award recently?

(A) ** N R Madhava Menon

(B) Suresh rajan

(C) Naresh Iyer

(D) Vivek Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madhava Menon Best Law Teacher Award, SILF-MILAT Institutional Excellence Award, Justice A.B.

Concept note-2: -BENGALURU: Umesh TP, a teacher at Government Lower Primary School, Amruthapura, and G Ponsankari, a teacher at Kendriya Vidyalaya, Tumakuru, were two teachers from Karnataka to be honoured at the National Award to Teachers 2022, on the occasion for Teachers’ Day.

Concept note-3: -These two teachers of Bihar who were awarded the National Teacher Award-2022 include Nishi Kumari from Mahadev Higher Secondary School Khushrupur, Patna and Saurav Suman from Lalit Narayan Laxmi Narayan Project Girls’ High School Triveniganj, Supaul.

Concept note-4: -Bharat Shiksha Puraskar is highest national recognition in the field of education. The purpose of this award is to celebrate the achievements of finest institutions and teachers of our country who have not only improved the quality of education but also enriched the lives of students.

Concept note-5: -National Award for Teachers (India)
