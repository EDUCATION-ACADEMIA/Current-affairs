Which Government approved revised policy of scheme to improve Girl child Ratio?

(A) ** Maharashtra

(B) Gujarat

(C) Kerala

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Beti Bachao Beti Padhao is a central government scheme for girl child schemes which is applicable throughout the country. The key objective of this scheme is to save the girl child from social ills such as gender-biased abortions and advance the education of girl children all over the country.

Concept note-2: -Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao Prime Minister Narendra Modi unveiled the Beti Bachao Beti Padhao (BBBP) Scheme in Panipat, Haryana, on January 22, 2015. The primary aim of this program is to protect girls from social problems such as gender-based abortions and to promote girl-child education throughout the world.

Concept note-3: -ICPS or the Integrated Child Protection Scheme is a Centrally Sponsored Scheme of the Ministry of Women and Child Development, Government of India, implemented in partnership with the State Governments and Civil Society Organizations (CSO).

Concept note-4: -Balika Samriddhi Yojana is another central government scheme to support girls in financially vulnerable sections of society.
