State bank of India has approved the merger of its operations with _________ of its associate banks.

(A) 4

(B) ** 5

(C) 6

(D) 7

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This brought into SBI the 50 largest banks in the world. These associate banks included State Bank of Jaipur, Bikaner, Mysore, Travancore, Hyderabad, and Patiala. The sixth bank was the Bharatiya Mahila Bank. Although the banks were merged, the shares of this associate were termed as individual entities.

The merger will create a bigger SBI that will account for nearly a quarter of all outstanding loans in the country.
