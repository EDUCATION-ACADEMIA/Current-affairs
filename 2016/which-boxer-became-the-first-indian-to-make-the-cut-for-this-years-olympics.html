Which boxer became the first Indian to make the cut for this year’s Olympics

(A) ** Shiva Thapa

(B) Vijender Singh

(C) Akhil Kumar

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian boxing at the Olympics Babu Lall, in men’s bantamweight (54kg), registered India’s first Olympic victory in boxing as he beat Allan Monteiro of Pakistan in the Round of 32.

Concept note-2: -India’s boxing medals at the Olympics – Trailblazer Vijender, supermom Mary and heartthrob Lovlina. India have won three Olympic medals in boxing, all bronze. Vijender Singh won at Beijing 2008, Mary Kom at London 2012 and Lovlina Borgohain at Tokyo 2020.
