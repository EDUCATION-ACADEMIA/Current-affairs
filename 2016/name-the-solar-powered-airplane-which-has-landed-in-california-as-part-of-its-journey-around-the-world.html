Name the solar powered airplane which has landed in California as part of its journey around the world

(A) Solar System 3

(B) Solar Power 1

(C) Solar Eclipse 4

(D) ** Solar Impulse 2

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After 14 months of travel and 550 hours in the air, the plane had accomplished what many had deemed impossible: traveling 25, 000 miles around the world-over four continents, two oceans and three seas-without a drop of liquid fuel. The sun’s vibrant rays supplied the craft’s only power.

Concept note-2: -The plane, which has a wingspan wider than a Boeing 747 and carries more than 17, 000 solar cells on its wings, began the circumnavigation in March 2015 in Abu Dhabi.

Concept note-3: -By India Today Web Desk: Solar Impulse 2, an airplane powered solely by energy from the sun has created history by successfully completing its journey around-the-world, when it landed in Abu Dhabi on Tuesday, July 26.
