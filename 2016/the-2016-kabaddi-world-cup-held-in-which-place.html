The 2016 Kabaddi World Cup held in which place

(A) Jalalabad

(B) Nabha

(C) Ludhiana

(D) ** Ahmedabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2016 World Kabaddi Cup was the sixth edition of the circle style World Kabaddi Cup, held from 4 November to 17 November 2016 with the Opening Ceremony on 3 November 2016 at the Nehru stadium Roopnagar. The tournament took place in Punjab, India.

Concept note-2: -The 2016 Kabaddi World Cup was the third edition of the standard-style Kabaddi World Cup. It was organised by the International Kabaddi Federation and contested from 7 to 22 October 2016 in Ahmedabad, India. Twelve countries competed in the tournament. India won their 3rd World Cup by defeating Iran 38–29 in the final.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is India. The International Kabaddi Federation organized Kabaddi World Cup 2016 in Ahmedabad, India.

Concept note-4: -The Kabaddi World Cup 2025 will be the first edition after the 2020 edition that could not be held due to Covid-19. The previous three editions of the Kabaddi World Cup were all held in India with the hosts emerging as the winner of the three editions.
