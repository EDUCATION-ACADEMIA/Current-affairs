Which of the following E-commerce company has introduced "No Cost EMI " option to make online shopping affordable for high-end purchases on 1 June 2016

(A) Jabong

(B) ** Flipkart

(C) HomeShop18

(D) Dealsandyou

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Now, Flipkart introduces the ‘No Cost EMI’ option-The Economic Times.

Concept note-2: -No cost EMI is available on credit cards from all major banks (Axis, ICICI, HDFC, Citi Bank, SBI, IndusInd, Yes Bank, Kotak, RBL, HSBC, Standard Chartered) and Bajaj Finserv EMI cards.

Concept note-3: -The No Cost EMI facility helps you to pay your monthly EMIs without any additional cost. This means, when repaying your purchase cost, you only have to pay the actual cost of the product without any interest.

Concept note-4: -With Flipkart’s Zero Cost EMI on mobiles, you pay just the price of the phone; the interest that you normally pay to your bank is given as an instant discount during purchase.

Concept note-5: -No Cost EMI is a great credit facility to pay for large purchases on Amazon. You can easily repay the loan amount in 3, 6, 9 or 12 months without any interest or processing fees. No Cost EMI is an offer in which you pay your EMI provider only the product price, which is evenly divided over your repayment period.
