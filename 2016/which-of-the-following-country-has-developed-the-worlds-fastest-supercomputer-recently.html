Which of the following country has developed the world’s fastest supercomputer recently

(A) Japan

(B) ** China

(C) US

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Japan has developed the worlds most powerful supercomputer, Fugaku .

Concept note-2: -1. Fugaku (Japan) Japan’s Fugaku, which was jointly developed by RIKEN and Fujitsu, is now the world’s fastest supercomputer.

Concept note-3: -Frontier, the new number 1, is built by Hewlett Packard Enterprise (HPE) and housed at the Oak Ridge National Laboratory (ORNL) in Tennessee, USA.

Concept note-4: -Soon after starting operations in 2022, Frontier took the title of the world’s fastest computer, as judged by publicly available data. It broke the so-called exascale barrier-meaning it’s able to complete more than 1 quintillion calculations in one second.

Concept note-5: -One of the fastest supercomputer in India is PARAM Ananta which was launched on May 30, 2022, at IIT, Gandhinagar. This supercomputer was developed under the NSM by C-DAC and IIT Gandhinagar. It is at 102 positions among the world’s top 500 supercomputers, with a peak performance of 3.3 Petaflops.
