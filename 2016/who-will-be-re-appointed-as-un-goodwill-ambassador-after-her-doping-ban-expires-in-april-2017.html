Who will be re-appointed as UN Goodwill ambassador after her doping ban expires in April 2017

(A) Serena Williams

(B) Venus Williams

(C) Steffi Graf

(D) ** Maria Sharapova

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Maria Sharapova to be reinstated as UN goodwill ambassador | Global development | The Guardian.

Concept note-2: -Priyanka Chopra Jonas (Goodwill Ambassador) on the SDG Moment 2022 | UN Web TV.

Concept note-3: -Millie Bobby Brown (November 2018) Vanessa Nakate (September 2022)
