Which company got the solar energy project for Sri Lanka

(A) Vivint Solar

(B) Trina Solar, LTD

(C) Suzlon Energy

(D) ** Gamesa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Thermal Power Corporation (NTPC) of India on Friday signed an agreement with Ceylon Electricity Board (CEB) to jointly set up a solar power plant in Sampur, in Sri Lanka’s eastern Trincomalee district, a decade after a joint coal power project deal was signed and subsequently scrapped.

Concept note-2: -Tata Power Solar Systems This solar power company remains an eminent leader in manufacturing one of the Best Solar Panels in India The company boasts a stronghold on residential rooftop solar units and has shipped about 1.4 GW of solar modules worldwide over the past 20 years.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh. The world’s largest floating solar energy project of 600 MW is started in Khandwa district of Madhya Pradesh. Will begin power generation by the year 2022-23.

Concept note-4: -The Laugfs Solar Power Station is a 20-megawatt photovoltaic power station built 10 km (6.2 mi) north of Hambantota, in the Hambantota District of Sri Lanka. It is the single largest solar power station in the country.
