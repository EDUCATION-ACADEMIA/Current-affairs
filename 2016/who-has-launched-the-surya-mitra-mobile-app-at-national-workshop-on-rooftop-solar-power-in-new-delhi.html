Who has launched the Surya Mitra mobile App at National Workshop on Rooftop Solar Power in New Delhi

(A) Narendra Modi

(B) Arun Jaitley

(C) ** Piyush Goyal

(D) Rajanath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The app was launched by Union Power Minister Piyush Goyal at National Workshop on Rooftop Solar Power held in New Delhi.

Concept note-2: -Launched in 2015, the Suryamitra training programme is envisioned to train 50000 ‘Suryamitras’ in the next five years.

Concept note-3: -The Delhi Solar Policy 2016 was formulated with the target of achieving 1995 MW rooftop solar installation by 2025. The policy outlined a combination of regulations, mandates, incentives, and tax breaks for the growth of rooftop solar power in the capital.

Concept note-4: -Delhi’s new solar policy targets 6, 000 MW of installed solar capacity by 2025 to increase the share of solar energy in Delhi’s annual electricity demand from 9 per cent to 25 per cent in three years, the highest in India, said Deputy Chief Minister Manish Sisodia.

Concept note-5: -The Government of India (GoI) has set a target of, 100 GW (100000 MW) of solar energy generation in India by the year 2022, of which 40 GW (40000 MW) is from rooftops.
