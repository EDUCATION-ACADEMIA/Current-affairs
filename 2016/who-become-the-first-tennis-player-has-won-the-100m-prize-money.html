Who become the first Tennis player has won the $100m prize money

(A) Roger Federer

(B) Rafael Nadal

(C) Pete Sampras

(D) ** Novak Djokovic

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1 tennis player, Novak Djokovic, has become the first player earn $100 million in prize money in his career, following a fourth round win at the French Open this week.

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic is the career leader in prize money earnings on the ATP tennis World Tour.

Concept note-3: -The tennis player has accumulated a total of USD 125, 050, 235 from the ATP tour since 2001, winning over a total of 89 titles. Nadal ranks third on the list of players who have won the most prize money on tour, behind Novak Djokovic and Roger Federer.

Concept note-4: -Federer has been playing tennis professionally since 1998 and has won 20 Grand Slams and more than 100 titles. In his 24 years playing, the Swiss legend has collected $131 million in prize money, third in ATP Tour history behind Djokovic’s $159 million and Nadal’s $132 million.
