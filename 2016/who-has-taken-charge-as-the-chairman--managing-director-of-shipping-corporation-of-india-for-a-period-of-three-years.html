Who has taken charge as the chairman & managing director of Shipping Corporation of India for a period of three years.

(A) Barun Mitra

(B) B. B. Sinha

(C) H.K. Joshi

(D) ** Anoop Kumar Sharma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shipping Corporation of India on Monday said Binesh Kumar Tyagi has taken over as its Chairman and Managing Director (CMD).

Concept note-2: -Captain Binesh Kumar Tyagi as the new chairman and managing director of Shipping Corporation of India Ltd (SCI).

Concept note-3: -Capt. Binesh Kumar Tyagi has taken over as Chairman and Managing Director of The Shipping Corporation of India Ltd. with effect from 03.09.2022.

Concept note-4: -Captain Tyagi graduated from Meerut University and joined SCI in 1990 as a trainee nautical officer and sailed on board various ships in different ranks including as master.
