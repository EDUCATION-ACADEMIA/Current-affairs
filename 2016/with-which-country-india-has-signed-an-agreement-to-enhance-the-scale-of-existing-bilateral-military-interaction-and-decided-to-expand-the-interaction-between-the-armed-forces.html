With which country India has signed an agreement to enhance the scale of existing bilateral military interaction and decided to expand the interaction between the armed forces

(A) US

(B) Russia

(C) Dubai

(D) ** Singapore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and Cyprus have signed a number of agreements in various areas including on Defence and Military Cooperation between the two countries.

Concept note-2: -The 123 Agreement signed between the United States of America and the Republic of India is known as the U.S.–India Civil Nuclear Agreement or Indo-US nuclear deal. The framework for this agreement was a July 18, 2005, joint statement by then Indian Prime Minister Manmohan Singh and then U.S. President George W.

Concept note-3: -Ex Dharma Guardian – 2022 is a joint military exercise between India and Japan, is being conducted at the Foreign Training Node in Belagavi.

Concept note-4: -Yudh Abhyas is called as one of the largest joint running military training and defence corporation endeavours between India and the USA. Yudh Abhyas 2021 is the 17th edition of this bilateral joint military exercise between India and the USA. The exercise is hosted alternately by the two countries since 2004.
