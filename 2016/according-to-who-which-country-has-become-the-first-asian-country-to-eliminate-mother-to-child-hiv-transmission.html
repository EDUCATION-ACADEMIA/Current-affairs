According to WHO which country has become the first Asian country to eliminate mother-to-child HIV transmission

(A) Cuba

(B) ** Thailand

(C) Zimbabwe

(D) Swaziland

Concept note-1: -SEAR/PR/1627. New Delhi/Bangkok : Thailand today received validation from WHO for having eliminated mother-to-child transmission of HIV and syphilis, becoming the first country in Asia and the Pacific region and also the first with a large HIV epidemic to ensure an AIDS-free generation.
