Who has unveiled the official slogan for Olympic and Paralympic Games

(A) Richard Peterkin

(B) ** Thomas Bach

(C) Kirsty Coventry

(D) Paul Tergat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -"Games Wide Open. This is the shared slogan for both the Olympic Games and Paralympic Games, unveiled by Paris 2024 on the occasion of the 2 years to go anniversary. Above all, since the beginning of this adventure, it is the ambition that drives us and inspires each of our actions.

Concept note-2: -The symbol also reflects the Paralympic Motto, “Spirit in Motion, ” representing the strong will of every Paralympian. The Paralympic Symbol also emphasises the fact that Paralympic athletes are constantly inspiring and exciting the world with their performances: always moving forward and never giving up.

Concept note-3: -International Olympic Committee President Thomas Bach has officially revealed the slogan for the 2024 Paris Games. For those who prefer English, the official slogan is “Games Wide Open.” For French-speakers, the slogan is “Ouvrons Grand les Jeux.”

Concept note-4: -Rio 2016 Organising Committee was revealed the official slogan for the first Olympic and Paralympic Games in Latin America A New World. Along with the torch, the medals, and the mascots, the slogan plays a major part in creating the unique identity of each Olympic and Paralympic Games.

Concept note-5: -On the afternoon of September 17, “Together for a Shared Future” was revealed as the official motto of the Olympic and Paralympic Winter Games Beijing 2022 at the motto launch ceremony held in the Capital Museum.
