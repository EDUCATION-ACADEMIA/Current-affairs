Who won the Calcutta Football League (CFL) for the seventh consecutive time

(A) Mohun Bagan

(B) Mohammedan Sporting

(C) Army XI

(D) ** East Bengal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -East Bengal had won their record 38th and seventh consecutive CFL title.

Concept note-2: -East Bengal have won the National Football League thrice: 2000–01, 2002–03 and 2003–04, and became runners-up 7 times, the most of any Indian clubs. Among other trophies, East Bengal have won the Calcutta Football League 39 times, IFA Shield 29 times, Federation Cup 8 times and the Durand Cup 16 times.

Concept note-3: -Emami East Bengal FC will open their campaign in CFL Super Six against Khidderpore at Naihati on September 25. As per the fixture released by IFA, East Bengal FC’s match against Khidderpore and Aryan is confirmed, while, matches against Mohammedan SC & Bhowanipur dates have been proposed.

Concept note-4: -The ATK Mohun Bagan vs East Bengal ISL 2022-23 match will be telecast live on the Star Sports 1, Star Sports 1 HD, and Star Sports 3 TV channels in India. The match start at 7:30 PM IST. ISL broadcast in Hindi commentary will be on the Star Sports 1 Hindi and Star Sports 1 HD Hindi TV channels.
