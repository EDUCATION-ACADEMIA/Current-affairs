What is the name of Former Chairman and CEO of Intel who passed away on 21 March 2016

(A) Atoru Iwata

(B) ** Andrew Grove

(C) Androse

(D) Jose Rios

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Andrew Stephen Grove (born András István Gróf; 2 September 1936 – 21 March 2016) was a Hungarian-American businessman and engineer who served as the third CEO of Intel Corporation. He escaped from Communist-controlled Hungary at the age of 20 and moved to the United States, where he finished his education.

Concept note-2: -Brian Matthew Krzanich (born May 9, 1960) is an American engineer and Krzanich joined Intel as an engineer in 1982 and served as chief operating officer (COO) before being promoted to CEO in May 2013. As CEO, Krzanich was credited for diversifying Intel’s product offerings and workforce.

Concept note-3: -The former CEO of Intel Corp. is continuing to line up new assignments. Bob Swan, who joined venture firm Andreessen Horowitz an operating partner over the summer, has now also joined the board of Nike Inc. the company announced on Wednesday. Swan left Intel in January 2021.

Concept note-4: -Brian Krzanich, Intel chief operating officer and senior vice president, was named CEO of the company, succeeding Paul Otellini.

Concept note-5: -Patrick (Pat) Gelsinger is Chief Executive Officer of Intel Corporation and serves on its board of directors. On Feb. 15, 2021, Mr. Gelsinger returned to Intel, the company where he had spent the first 30 years of his career.
