Who became the ambassador of AIBA for women’s world championship

(A) Yvonne Caples

(B) ** Mary Kom

(C) Usha Nagisetty

(D) Aruna Mishra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sarah Ourahmoune, who is also an Ambassador of the Olympic Games Paris 2024, won a bronze medal at the 2016 AIBA Women’s World Boxing Championships, as well as a silver medal at the Olympic Games Rio 2016.

Concept note-2: -Mangte Chungneijang Mary Kom OLY (born 24 November 1982) is an Indian amateur boxer, politician, and former Member of Parliament, Rajya Sabha.

Concept note-3: -The International Boxing Association will now be called IBA in its abbreviated form instead of AIBA. The Congressional approval of constitutional reforms came after IBA unanimously approved the recommendations made in a report by independent Governance Reform Group (GRG), led by Professor Ulrich Haas.

Concept note-4: -The International Boxing Association (AIBA) is a non-profit international organization that succeeded the former Fédération Internationale de Boxe Amateur in 1946.

Concept note-5: -The International Boxing Association (IBA), previously known as the Association Internationale de Boxe Amateur (AIBA), is an independent sport organization that sanctions amateur (Olympic-style) boxing matches and awards world and subordinate championships.
