Who has won the French Open Mixed Doubles title 2016

(A) Ekaterina Makarova, Bruno Soares

(B) Sania Mirza, Ivan Dodig

(C) ** Martina Hingis, Leander Paes

(D) Andrea Hlavackova, Max Mirnyi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Martina Hingis and Leander Paes defeated Sania Mirza and Ivan Dodig in the final, 4–6, 6–4, [10–8] to win the mixed doubles tennis title at the 2016 French Open.

Concept note-2: -Novak Djokovic defeated Andy Murray in the final, 3–6, 6–1, 6–2, 6–4 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 French Open.

Concept note-3: -Garbiñe Muguruza defeated the defending champion Serena Williams in the final, 7–5, 6–4 to win the women’s singles tennis title at the 2016 French Open.

Concept note-4: -Margaret Court and Ken Fletcher won an all-time record 6 consecutive majors as a team; the only team to complete the Grand Slam. Owen Davidson is the most recent player to complete the Grand Slam.

Concept note-5: -Ena Shibahara and Wesley Koolhof defeated Ulrikke Eikeri and Joran Vliegen in the final, 7–6, 6–2 to win the mixed doubles tennis title at the 2022 French Open. According to The New York Times, Koolhof and Shibahara had never met before competing in this tournament.
