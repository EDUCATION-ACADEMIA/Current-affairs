Who became the New Member (Electrical) of the Railway Board

(A) ** Ashwani Kumar Kapoor

(B) V.K. Gupta

(C) Mohd. Jamshed

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shri Ashwani Kumar Kapoor has taken our as the New Member Electrical, Railway Board and ex-officio Secretary to the Government of India.

Concept note-2: -Shri Anil Kumar Lahoti has taken over the charge of new Chairman & Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Railway Board (Ministry of Railways). The appointments committee of the Cabinet approved the appointment of Shri Anil Kumar Lahoti as Chairman & CEO of Railway Board.

Concept note-3: -Anil Kumar Lahoti takes charge as Chairman and CEO, Railway Board.

Concept note-4: -Member (Electrical) is better known by the acronym ML. In each of the zones the organisation is headed by a Chief Electrical Engineer . The CEE reports to the General Manager of the Railway. The office of the Member (Electrical) of the Railway Board guides the CEE on technical matters and policy.

Concept note-5: -Members of Staff means all employees, contractors and volunteers.
