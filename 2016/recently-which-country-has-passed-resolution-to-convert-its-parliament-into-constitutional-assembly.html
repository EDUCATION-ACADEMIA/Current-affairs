Recently, which country has passed resolution to convert its Parliament into Constitutional Assembly

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Bhutan

(C) Pakistan

(D) ** Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Assembly met for the first time in New Delhi on 9 December 1946, and its last session was held on 24 January 1950.

Concept note-2: -On 25 May 1849, the constituent assembly approved the new constitution, and on 5 June 1849 it was signed by King Frederick VII. For this reason, it is also known as the June constitution.

Concept note-3: -Sir Benegal Narsing Rau CIE (26 February 1887 – 30 November 1953) was an Indian civil servant, jurist, diplomat and statesman known for his key role in drafting the Constitution of India. He was the Constitutional Advisor to Constituent Assembly.

Concept note-4: -Dr Sachidanand Sinha was the first president of the Constituent Assembly. Later, Dr Rajendra Prasad was elected its president.
