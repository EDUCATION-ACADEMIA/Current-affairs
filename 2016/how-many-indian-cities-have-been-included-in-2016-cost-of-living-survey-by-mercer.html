How many Indian cities have been included in 2016 Cost of Living Survey by Mercer

(A) 3

(B) 4

(C) ** 5

(D) 6

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The survey includes over 400+ cities throughout the world; this year’s ranking includes 227 cities across five continents and measures the comparative cost of more than 200 items in each location, including housing, transportation, food, clothing, household goods, and entertainment.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai (127) is the most expensive city in India in terms of both living expenses and accommodation costs, followed by New Delhi (155), Chennai (177), Bengaluru (178), and Hyderabad (192), finds Mercer’s 2022 Cost of Living survey.

Concept note-3: -Mercer’s Quality of Living Survey offers objective information about the standards of living for employees sent to work abroad. This survey provides key insights for employers, employees, governments and city authorities looking to improve their attractiveness to entice foreign talent, businesses and investments.
