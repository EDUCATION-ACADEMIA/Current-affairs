Where has the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) set up a Electropreneur Park

(A) ** University of Delhi South Campus

(B) University of Delhi North Campus

(C) Indian Institute of Technology- Delhi

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY) has approved project for setting up of an Electropreneur Park for development of Product and IP creation in the ESDM sector at a total estimated cost of Rs.

Concept note-2: -Centre for Materials for Electronics Technology (C-MET), established in 1990 is an Autonomous Scientific Society under Ministry of Electroics and Information Technology (MeitY), Govt. of India dedicated to R&D on Electronic Materials. It has three laboratories located in Pune, Hyderabad and Thrissur.

Concept note-3: -Atal Incubation Centers AIM is a flagship initiative to promote the culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in India. It is an umbrella structure that has been mandated to promote innovation and revolutionize the entrepreneurial ecosystem in the country.

Concept note-4: -An incubator firm helps grow a startup from an early-stage idea to a company that can stand on its own. Services provided by incubators include office space, administrative functions, education and mentorship, access to investors and capital, and idea generation.
