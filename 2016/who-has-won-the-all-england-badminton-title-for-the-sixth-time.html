Who has won the All England Badminton title for the sixth time

(A) ** Lin Dan

(B) Lee Chong Wei

(C) Chen Long

(D) Tian Houwei

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lin Dan is a Chinese former professional badminton player. He is a two-time Olympic champion, five-time World champion, as well as a six-time All England champion. Lee Chong Wei is a Malaysian former badminton player.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Prakash Padukone. On March 23, 1980, Prakash Padukone became the first Indian to win the All England Badminton Championships. Padukone clashed with Indonesia’s Liem Swie King in the summit clash at the Wembley Stadium.

Concept note-3: -Olympic champion Viktor Axelsen has overpowered India’s Lakshya Sen to win the All England Open men’s singles title 21-10, 21-15 in 53 minutes. Axelsen didn’t drop a single game on his way to victory here in Birmingham and in his fourth consecutive final he dominated the unseeded Sen.

Concept note-4: -Lakshya Sen loses to Viktor Axelsen in All England Open final-The Hindu.
