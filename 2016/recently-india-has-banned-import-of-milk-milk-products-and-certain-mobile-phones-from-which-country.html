Recently India has banned import of milk, milk products and certain mobile phones from which country

(A) Russia

(B) ** China

(C) Netherlands

(D) France

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Commerce Minister Nirmala Sitharaman said in Lok Sabha that India banned import of milk and milk products from China as their quality was unacceptable.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Milk and milk products. Milk and milk products have been recently banned from China on import into India. Although India does not import milk, milk products from China, it has imposed the ban as a preventive measure.

Concept note-3: -electronic items, specific mobile phones, milk and milk products and some steel products on April 25, 2016. Import of such items from China has been harming India’s small and medium enterprises. Quality concerns have also been cited as one of the reasons for the ban.

Concept note-4: -Food items containing certain food colorings and additives deemed harmful to human health by the National Health and Family Planning Commission (NHFPC) are also barred. In addition, China restricts or prohibits the importation of certain commodities related to the processing trade.

Concept note-5: -The imports from China to India have increased by 7.94% over the last six years. The country’s trade with China exceeded $100 billion in 2021. The year before, in 2020, India imported goods worth $58.71 billion from China, one of the leading exporters to the country.
