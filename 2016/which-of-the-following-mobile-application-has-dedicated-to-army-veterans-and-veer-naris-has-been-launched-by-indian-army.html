Which of the following mobile application has dedicated to Army Veterans and Veer Naris has been launched by Indian Army

(A) Veterans Save App

(B) Veterans Safe App

(C) ** Veterans Outreach App

(D) Veterans Special App

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This App has been developed exclusively for serving soldiers of the Indian Army by the technical team of Army Soldiers (Adjutant Generals Branch (MP-8)) for communication of their service and pay-related information to them on their mobile phones.

Concept note-2: -The Indian Army on Thursday launched a new messaging application called ASIGMA for in-house communication. ASIGMA, which stands for Army Secure IndiGeneous Messaging Application, has been developed entirely in-house by a team of officers of the Corps of Signals, an official statement said.

Concept note-3: -Taking yet another step in leveraging technology, the Indian Army with a motto of “Taking Care of Our Own, No Matter What” launched a single window facility named the “Veerangana Sewa Kendra” (VSK) for welfare and grievances redressal of veer naris.

Concept note-4: -The Indian Army has launched a contemporary messaging application named ASIGMA (Army Secure IndiGeneous Messaging Application) .

Concept note-5: -Developed by the Indian Army, the original ARMAAN app is used for information decimation, messaging, military engineering services related complaints, army rest house related services, and many others. It also has a feature that allows army members to send messages to the office of the Chief of Army Staff (COAS).
