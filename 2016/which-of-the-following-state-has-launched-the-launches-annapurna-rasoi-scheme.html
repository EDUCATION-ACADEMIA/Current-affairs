Which of the following state has launched the launches Annapurna Rasoi Scheme

(A) Tamil Nadu

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-2: -Annapurna Rasoi Yojana was an ambitious meal scheme launched by the state Government of Rajasthan to provide subsidized meal in the state.

Concept note-3: -IAS Exam Latest Updates. Annapurna Scheme was launched in the year 2000 with effect from 1st April by the Ministry of Rural Development. It is a Central Sector Scheme. It is one of the sub-scheme of the National Assistance Program.

Concept note-4: -On 1st April, 2000 a new Scheme known as Annapurna Scheme was launched. This scheme aimed at providing food security to meet the requirement of those senior citizens who, though eligible, have remained uncovered under the NOAPS.

Concept note-5: -Under this scheme, 10 Kgs of food grains is distributed per month free of cost to destitute above the age of 65 years with no or meager subsistence.
