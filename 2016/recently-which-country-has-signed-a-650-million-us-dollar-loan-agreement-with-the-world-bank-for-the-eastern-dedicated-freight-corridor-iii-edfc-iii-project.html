Recently, which country has signed a 650-million US dollar loan agreement with the World Bank for the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor-III (EDFC-III) Project

(A) ** India

(B) Bangladesh

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Myanmar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Government of India and World Bank Sign $650 Million Agreement for the Eastern Dedicated Freight Corridor Project.

Concept note-2: -New Delhi, Dec 26 (KNN) Comprising Western and Eastern Corridors, the mega infrastructure project called the Dedicated Freight Corridor (DFC) has completed a total route length of 1610 Km out of total 2843 Km till October, 2022.

Concept note-3: -There are six freight corridors in India. Two corridor passages, i.e., Western and Eastern DFC, are under construction and partially operated. However, the East Coast, East-West, and North-South DFC have been approved for preparing a Detailed Project Report.

Concept note-4: -Western Corridor Last Updated On:19/09/2022 Junction Stations between the existing railway system and the Western DFC have been provided at Vasai Road, Kosad/Gothangam, Makarpura (Vadodara), Amli Road (Sabarmati), Palanpur, Marwar Jn., Phulera, Rewari and Pirthala Road.
