Which state government on 29 April 2016 launched Adi Praman, a web-based application for tribal caste validity certificate?

(A) Odisha Government

(B) Jharkhand Government

(C) Assam Government

(D) ** Maharashtra Government

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In order to obtain caste validity certificate, applicant shall go to the respective Thasil / Revenue / Block Office OR the respective office of “department of welfare for backward classes”as applicable.

Concept note-2: -It usually lasts the rest of your life. However, in some areas, the validity period is limited to a set number of years, such as three. For those who obtain it, the validity of the caste certificate is stated on the certificate itself.

Concept note-3: -A caste validity certificate is a proof that you hold an authentic caste certificate and that you aren’t pretending to belong to one of the backward categories to take advantage of the government benefits.
