Recently, which state has increasing motherhood leave period for women government employees from six to nine months

(A) Andhra Pradesh

(B) ** Tamil Nadu

(C) Kerala

(D) Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Tamil Nadu Chief Minister Jayalalithaa had announced in September that maternity leave for women government employees will be increased from the present six months to nine, fulfilling her partyâ€™s electoral promise.

Concept note-2: -The Government made amendments to the Maternity Benefit Act of 1961 by extending the maternity leave duration from the former 12 weeks to 26 weeks.

Concept note-3: -Originally, the maternity leave in India was just three months long, but after the 2017 Amendment, it was increased to six months. In India, all women working in factories and establishments with at least 10 workers have access to about 6 months of paid maternity leave.

Concept note-4: -CHENNAI: The State government on Friday approved 270 days of maternity leave for women government employees and teachers to take care of their newborns delivered through surrogacy.
