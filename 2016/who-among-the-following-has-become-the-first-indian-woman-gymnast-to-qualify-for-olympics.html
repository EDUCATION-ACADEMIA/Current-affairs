Who among the following has become the first Indian woman gymnast to qualify for Olympics

(A) Geetha Kumar

(B) Saina Nehwal

(C) Meera Nandan

(D) ** Dipa Karmakar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dipa Karmakar changed the face of gymnastics in India. She was the first Indian female gymnast to represent the country at the Olympics and the first Indian gymnast to do so in 52 years.

Concept note-2: -At Rio 2016, Dipa Karmakar became the first woman gymnast to represent the country at an Olympic Games, and inspired many with her huge-hearted performances that left her a whisker away from stepping onto the podium.

Concept note-3: -Karmakar herself, who is currently at her home in Agartala, and her coach Bisheshwar Nandi did not respond to calls.

Concept note-4: -Dipa Karmakar became the first Indian woman gymnast to win an international medal at the 2014 Commonwealth Games in Glasgow.
