Which country is introducing law with provision to impeach Supreme Court judges on ground of misconduct or incapacity

(A) India

(B) ** Bangladesh

(C) China

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Bangladesh is introducing a law with provision to impeach Supreme Court judges if allegation of misconduct or incapacity is proved.

Concept note-2: -1. A Judge of the Supreme Court shall not be removed from his office except by an order of the Parliament. 2. Supreme Court Judges can be removed from the office on grounds of proved misbehaviour or incapacity.

Concept note-3: -Article 217 in The Constitution Of India 1949.

Concept note-4: -Ramaswami J was the first judge against whom impeachment proceedings were initiated. In 1993, the motion was brought up in Lok Sabha but failed to secure the required two-thirds majority. Soumitra Sen J of the Calcutta High Court resigned in 2011 after the Rajya Sabha passed an impeachment motion against him.

Concept note-5: -Ramaswami. Veeraswami Ramaswami was a judge of the Supreme Court of India and the first judge against whom removal proceedings were initiated in independent India.
