Yash Chopra has been honoured with special statue by which country

(A) France

(B) ** Switzerland

(C) Hong Kong

(D) Mali

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Director Yash Chopra, who shot most of his romantic dramas in the lush mountains of Switzerland, has been honoured with a special statue by the government.

Concept note-2: -In May 2016, a bronze statue of Chopra was installed by the Switzerland government as a tribute. The statue weights about 250 kg and was inaugurated by wife Pamela and actor Rani Mukerjee (his daughter-in-law). The statue is located near the Congress Centre in Interlaken, a popular tourist spot.

Concept note-3: -Many of his films featured Switzerland as a backdrop, and all credit goes to him for enhancing the popularity of the Alpine nation among Indian tourists. Switzerland honour Indian filmmaker Lt. Yash Chopra with a statue for his spectacular contribution towards Swiss tourism.

Concept note-4: -For his contributions to film, the Government of India honoured him with the Dadasaheb Phalke Award in 2001, and the Padma Bhushan in 2005. In 2006, British Academy of Film and Television Arts presented him with a lifetime membership, making him the first Indian to receive the honour.

Concept note-5: -The government of Interlaken awarded him the honorary title of “Ambassador of Interlaken” in 2011, and Jungfrau Railways named a train after him – an honour shared only with the railway’s founder, Adolf Guyer. In addition, the five-star Victoria Jungfrau Grand Hotel & Spa in Interlaken named a suite after Yash Chopra.
