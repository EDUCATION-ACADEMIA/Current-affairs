The International Olympic Day was celebrated on which date

(A) ** June 23

(B) June 24

(C) June 25

(D) June 26

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -International Olympic Day is celebrated on June 23 each year and was first observed in 1948. The Olympics is the worldâ€™s largest international multi-sport event held every four years. Thousands of athletes and sportspersons from all across the world take part in various games and sports.

Concept note-2: -Each year June 23 is a day when athletes and people worldwide congregate and organise various sporting events. Nations across the world have their respective Olympic committees that overlook the celebrations that take place on International Olympic Day.

Concept note-3: -Olympic Day is a celebration of sport, health, and being together. It invites everyone around the globe to be active and move together with purpose on 23 June every year.

Concept note-4: -Olympic Day was introduced in 1948 to commemorate the birth of the modern Olympic Games on 23 June 1894 at the Sorbonne in Paris. The goal was to promote participation in sport across the globe regardless of age, gender or athletic ability.
