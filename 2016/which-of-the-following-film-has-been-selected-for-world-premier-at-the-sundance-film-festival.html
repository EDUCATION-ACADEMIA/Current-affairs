Which of the following film has been selected for world premier at the Sundance Film Festival

(A) Bridget Jones’s Baby

(B) The Perfect Match

(C) La La Land

(D) ** The Big Sick

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Writing with Fire . “Writing with Fire” has won the audience award in the World Cinema Documentary category at the Sundance Film Festival 2021.

Concept note-2: -The Sundance Film Festival is the ultimate gathering of original storytellers and audiences seeking new voices and fresh perspectives. Our annual program includes dramatic and documentary features, short films, and episodic content.

Concept note-3: -In 1991, the festival was officially renamed the Sundance Film Festival, after Redford’s character the Sundance Kid from the film Butch Cassidy and the Sundance Kid.

Concept note-4: -2023 Sundance Film Festival Announces Lineup of 99 Feature Films. Top L–R: Bravo, Burkina!, Girl, Polite Society, Mami Wata. Center L–R: Going Varsity in Mariachi, The Accidental Getaway Driver, Deep Rising, Cassandro.

Concept note-5: -The Sundance Film Festival is returning to Park City, Utah, with a slate of more than 120 independent films making their debut to an in-person crowd for the first time in two years. The festival attracts filmmakers and actors from around the globe hoping to become the next “Little Miss Sunshine” or “Get Out."
