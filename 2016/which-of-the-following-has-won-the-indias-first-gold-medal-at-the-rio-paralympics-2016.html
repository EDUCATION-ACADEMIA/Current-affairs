Which of the following has won the India’s first gold medal at the Rio Paralympics 2016

(A) ** Mariyappan Thangavelu

(B) Sathish Kumar

(C) Rama Chandran

(D) Arun Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Varun Singh Bhati won India’s first gold medal at the Rio Paralympic Games. Q. Who won the gold medal in the 2016 Rio Paralympics in the Men’s High Jump T42 event.

Concept note-2: -Mariyappan Thangavelu is the first gold medallist for India in 2016 Paralympics.

Concept note-3: -Deepa Malik, the first Indian woman to win a Paralympics medal. The 49-year-old from Bhaiswal, Haryana, created history during the Rio Paralympic Games in 2016, clinching the silver medal in the F53 shot put event.

Concept note-4: -Deepa Malik is the first Indian woman to win a medal at the Paralympics. She won the silver medal in the shot put in 2016 Paralympic Games. She was previously honored with the Arjuna award in 2012, at the age of 42 years. She has also been conferred the prestigious Padma Shri award in 2017.

Concept note-5: -At the Rio Paralympics in 2016, Mariyappan Thangavelu from Salem, Tamil Nadu, won the gold medal in the Men’s High Jump T42 event, with a leap of 1.89 m. He suffers from a permanent disability in his right leg. Q.
