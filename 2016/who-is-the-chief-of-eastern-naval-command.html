Who is the chief of Eastern Naval Command

(A) Admiral RK Dhowan

(B) ** Vice Admiral Harish Bisht

(C) Vice Admiral Girish Luthra

(D) Vice Admiral Sunil

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Vice Admiral Biswajit Dasgupta assumed charge as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Eastern Naval Command (ENC) on 01 December 2021.

Concept note-2: -Vice Admiral MA Hampiholi assumed command of the Southern Naval Command as the Flag Officer Commanding-in-Chief, Southern Naval Command on 30 November 2021. He was awarded the Nao Sena Medal in 2011, the Ati Vishisht Seva Medal in 2019 and the Param Vishisht Seva Medal in 2023.

Concept note-3: -The current Chief of the Naval Staff (CNS) and only full admiral in the Indian Armed Forces is Admiral R. Hari Kumar.
