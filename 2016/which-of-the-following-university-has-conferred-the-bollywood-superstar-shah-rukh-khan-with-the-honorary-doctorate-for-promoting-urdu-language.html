Which of the following university has conferred the Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan with the honorary doctorate for promoting Urdu Language

(A) Osmania University

(B) Mohammad Ali Jauhar University

(C) ** Maulana Azad National Urdu University

(D) Jamia Millia Islamia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HYDERABAD: Bollywood superstar Shah Rukh Khan has been conferred with honorary doctorate by the city-based Maulana Azad National Urdu University today. President Pranab Mukherjee was as the chief guest at the sixth convocation of the university where nearly 48, 000 students were awarded degrees.

Concept note-2: -Shah Rukh Khan received an honorary doctorate degree from University of Edinburgh in in October 2015 for his contribution to film and philanthropy. Shah Rukh Khan said that he was ‘extremely grateful and extremely humbled’ for this honor bestowed upon him.

Concept note-3: -Mr Khan said having his humanitarian endeavours recognised for the first time in Australia was a great honour.

Concept note-4: -Shah Rukh Khan who is the first foreign actor to be conferred the Malaysian title of Datuk-akin to a British knighthood-in 2008, received his first honorary doctorate in Arts and Culture from the university of Bedfordshire, UK in July 10, 2009.

Concept note-5: -2. Akshay Kumar. Akshay Kumar has an Honorary Doctorate of Law by the University of Windsor in Ontario, Canada for his outstanding work in the film industry and contribution to social work.
