Which railways has launched the missed call service for local train

(A) ** Western Railway

(B) North Eastern Railway

(C) South central Railways

(D) Eastern Railway

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Kolkata Suburban Railway is a suburban rail system serving the Kolkata metropolitan area and its surroundings in India. It is the largest suburban railway network in the country with the highest number of stations. It is also the 7th largest suburban rail system in the world.

Concept note-2: -Bengal Nagpur Railway was formed in 1887.

Concept note-3: -Suburban trains are passenger trains that cover short distances of up to 150 km. These trains help in facilitating movement of passengers within cities and suburbs.

Concept note-4: -The system is known as the “destination alert wake-up alarm.” To utilize this service, you must call the 139-railway helpline number. In order to avail of this service, dial seven and two digits after selecting the destination alert option. Your ten-digit PNR number will then need to be entered.
