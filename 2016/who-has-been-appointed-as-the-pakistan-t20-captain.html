Who has been appointed as the Pakistan T20 Captain

(A) ** Sarfraz Ahmed

(B) Shahid Afridi

(C) Umar Akmal

(D) Waqar Younis

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current Captain is Babar Azam He was appointed in November 2020. But an injury to him made his tenure to be postponed. Mohammad Rizwan lead the Pakistan test team as 33rd captain when his team played the Boxing day test against New Zealand in Dec 2020.

Concept note-2: -Why not share? Pakistan captain Babar Azam has defended Pakistan’s approach with the bat following their loss to England in the final of the ICC Men’s T20 World Cup 2022 at the MCG.

Concept note-3: -Babar Azam is the current captain of Pakistan T20 cricket team.

Concept note-4: -Babar Azam continues to claim records as an individual and skipper as he now holds the record for winning the most number of T20Is as a Pakistan skipper.
