Fish production in Himachal Pradesh increased by what percent

(A) ** 9 %

(B) 19 %

(C) 16 %

(D) 7 %

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As per the data provided in ‘Handbook on Fisheries Statistics’ published by Department of Fisheries, the Fish production in India increased by over 75% between 2009-10 and 2019-20. While Inland fish production increased by over three times during this period, Marine fish production is up by around 30%.

Concept note-2: -India is the second largest fish producing country in the world accounting for 7.56% of global production and contributing about 1.24% to the country’s Gross Value Added (GVA) and over 7.28% to the agricultural GVA.

Concept note-3: -The production and farming of fish in Andhra Pradesh are the highest in India. Moreover, the state produces 34.50 lakh tonnes of fish per year.
