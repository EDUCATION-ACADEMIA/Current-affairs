Who has been appointed as the Managing Director of 2019 World Cup

(A) Gulam Bodi

(B) ** Steve Elworthy

(C) Naumanullah

(D) James Marshall

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -ECB’s Steve Elworthy Appointed Managing Director Of ICC World Cup 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Greatest Game is the story of England’s last-gasp victory over New Zealand in the iconic Super Over to win a dramatic 2019 World Cup final in arguably the greatest game of cricket ever played.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is United Kingdom. United Kingdom hosted the ODI Cricket World Cup (ICC World Cup) 2019. The 2019 ICC Cricket World Cup was the 12th Cricket World Cup.
