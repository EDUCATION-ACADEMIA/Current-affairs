State-run RITES Ltd has supplied 60 modern passenger coaches to which country railways

(A) Nepal

(B) Myanmar

(C) ** Bangladesh

(D) Sri Lanka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Some list as many as forty, using the term broadly to cover numerous domestic rites, while others give just twelve. The medieval handbooks enumerate sixteen.

Concept note-2: -An average coach length will be around 25 meters, hence in 650 meters 24 coaches and an engine can be accommodated comfortably. Hence passenger trains can have maximum 24 coaches. It varies from 12 to 24 coaches based on the route and train type.

Concept note-3: -Coaches. This train runs with two AC first-class coach, five AC 2 tier coaches, three AC 3 tier coaches, ten sleeper coaches, two general coaches along with two EOG coaches. Thus, having a total of 24 LHB coach. It is the only train in India to be running with 24 LHB coach.

Concept note-4: -Coaches for railway passengers are produced at Kapurthala. It is a city in Punjab state of India. rail Coach Factory was established in 1986. It is a coach manufacturing unit of Indian Railways.
