Who has taken charge as Chief Executive for the Nuclear Fuel Complex (NFC) on 8 June 2016

(A) Parthasarathy

(B) ** Kalyanakrishnan

(C) Rama Krishanan

(D) Venkatramanan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hyderabad: Dr Komal Kapoor has taken over as chief executive of Nuclear Fuel Complex (NFC) on Saturday following the retirement of Dr Dinesh Srivastava on superannuation. Dr Kapoor will also be the Chairman of NFC Board. Earlier he was holding the position of Additional Chief Executive, NFC.

Concept note-2: -Saratchandran Nagarajan-Deputy Chief Executive-Nuclear Fuel Complex (NFC) | LinkedIn.

Concept note-3: -Nuclear Fuel Complex, Hyderabad is an industrial unit of the Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India. NFC is the only organization in India which caters to the fuel requirements of nuclear power reactors.

Concept note-4: -Nuclear Fuel Complex-Kota Rawatbhatta, Rajasthan, (India).
