The Haryana day _________ was observed as Haryana Golden Jubilee (Swarna Jyanti) across the state.

(A) ** 51st

(B) 47th

(C) 82nd

(D) 63rd

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 51st Haryana Day was observed on Nov 1st, 2016 as the Swarna Jayanti-Haryana Golden Jubilee.

Concept note-2: -The day is celebrated by people of Haryana and is a regional holiday in the state. It is always observed on 1 November to commemorate the formation of the state of Haryana which happened in the year 1966.

Concept note-3: -Haryana Day is celebrated on 1 November every year all over the state of Haryana. The state of Haryana was carved out of Punjab and formally declared a separate state on 1 November 1966.
