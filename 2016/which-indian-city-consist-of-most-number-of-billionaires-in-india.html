Which Indian city Consist of most number Of billionaires In India

(A) Delhi

(B) ** Mumbai

(C) Chennai

(D) Banglore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of September 2022, there were around 283 people with a net worth of more than ten billion Indian rupees living in the Indian city of Mumbai. It was followed by Delhi and Bengaluru with185 and 89 entries to the wealth list of the country respectively.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai is the financial capital of India and is also home to the maximum number of wealth creators of the country with over 280 of the richest Indians residing in the city that is also known as the ‘City of Dreams’.

Concept note-3: -In 2021, Mumbai was home to the highest number of millionaires, followed by India’s capital New Delhi, and the IT capital-Bengaluru.
