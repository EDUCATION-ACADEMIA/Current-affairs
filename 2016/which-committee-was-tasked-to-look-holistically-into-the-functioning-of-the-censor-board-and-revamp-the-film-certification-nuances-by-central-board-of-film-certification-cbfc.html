Which committee was tasked to look holistically into the functioning of the censor board and revamp the film certification nuances by Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC)

(A) Ramanujam committee

(B) T.K. Vishwanathan committee

(C) G N Bajpai Committee

(D) ** Shyam Benegal committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Mudgal committee had deliberated on ways to restructure the CBFC two years ago.

Concept note-2: -Central Board of Film Certification (CBFC) is a statutory body under Ministry of Information and Broadcasting, regulating the public exhibition of films under the provisions of the Cinematograph Act 1952.

Concept note-3: -In 1952, it was reconstituted as the Central Board of Film Censors. In 1983, the name was changed to Central Board of Film Certification.
