National Security Advisors of India and Japan held bilateral consultations in which of the following city

(A) Osaka

(B) Mumbai

(C) Sapporo

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The inaugural India-Japan Space Dialogue was held in Delhi on 8 March 2019 for enhancing bilateral cooperation in outer space and information exchange on the respective space policies. Both sides also held consultations on civil nuclear cooperation in March and September 2018. Dr.

Concept note-2: -36. S&T Cooperation: India-Japan S&T cooperation was formalized through an Inter-Governmental Agreement signed in 1985 and bilateral S&T cooperation began in 1993 with the establishment of India-Japan Science Council (IJSC).

Concept note-3: -In 1934, the “Indo-Japanese Trade Agreement of 1934” was signed in Delhi on 5 January 1934 and went effective on 12 July 1934.

Concept note-4: -Japan and India signed a peace treaty and established diplomatic relations on 28th April, 1952.

Concept note-5: -Japan maintains diplomatic relations with every United Nations member state except for North Korea, in addition to UN observer states Holy See, as well as Kosovo, Cook Islands and Niue.
