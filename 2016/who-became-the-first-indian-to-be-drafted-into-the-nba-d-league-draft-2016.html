Who became the first Indian to be drafted into the NBA D-League Draft 2016

(A) Gurdit Singh

(B) ** Palpreet Singh

(C) Ajay Singh

(D) Ram Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -While Satnam Singh, the only Indian to be drafted into the NBA, and Amjyot Singh Gill went on to play for their respective G-League teams, Palpreet Singh Brar was one of the unpicked players after being drafted in 2016.

Concept note-2: -Satnam Singh, the first Indian-born player drafted by an N.B.A. team, never realized his dream of playing in the league. Now, he is living a new dream: slam-dunking other people.

Concept note-3: -Satnam Singh Bhamara (Punjabi: ; born 10 December 1995) is an Indian professional wrestler and former basketball player, currently signed to the American professional wrestling promotion All Elite Wrestling (AEW).

Concept note-4: -Although he never appeared in an NBA regular-season game for the Mavs, he established himself as a potential trailblazer for other Indian players. After making the transition to wrestling, Singh signed with AEW in 2021 and made his on-screen debut in April.
