Who has been appointed as the India’s head coach for Zimbabwe tour

(A) Brad Hodge

(B) ** Sanjay Bangar

(C) Jacques Kallis

(D) Paddy Upton

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India legend and current head of the National Cricket Academy (NCA) VVS Laxman will assume the role of the head coach for the tour of Zimbabwe.

Concept note-2: -VVS Laxman, the former India batter and current National Cricket Academy (NCA) chief, will be head coach on the week-long tour of Zimbabwe.

Concept note-3: -However, on 10 June 2021, Sri Lanka Cricket confirmed the tour itinerary with only three T20Is. On 8 June 2021, Rahul Dravid was named as the head coach of India’s team for the tour.

Concept note-4: -After a disappointing loss to England to end their T20 World Cup 2022 campaign, India’s head coach Rahul Dravid accepts that England were too good, but refuses to label India’s performance a ‘debacle’.
