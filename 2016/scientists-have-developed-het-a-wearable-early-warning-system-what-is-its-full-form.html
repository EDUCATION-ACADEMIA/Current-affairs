Scientists have developed HET a wearable early warning system. What is its full form

(A) ** Health and Environmental Tracker

(B) Health and Environment Tracker

(C) Healthy Ecosystem Tracker

(D) Healthy Ecology Tracker

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Early warning system – The set of capacities needed to generate and disseminate timely and meaningful warning information to enable individuals, communities and organizations threatened by a hazard to prepare and to act appropriately and in sufficient time to reduce the possibility of harm or loss.

Concept note-2: -Early warning system is an adaptive measure for climate change, using integrated communication systems to help communities prepare for hazardous climate-related events. A successful EWS saves lives and jobs, land and infrastructures and supports long-term sustainability.

Concept note-3: -Space technologies for early warning. While there are three types of space technologies including satellite telecommunications, Earth observation and GNSS, at this time satellite telecommunications and Earth observation are being used more frequently in several types of early warning systems.
