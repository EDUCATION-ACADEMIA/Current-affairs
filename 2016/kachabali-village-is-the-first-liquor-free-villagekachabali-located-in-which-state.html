Kachabali village is the first liquor free village.Kachabali located in which state

(A) Assam

(B) Uttarakhand

(C) ** Rajasthan

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It’s all happening in Kachabali village in Rajsamand district of Rajasthan!

Concept note-2: -How a Democratic Process Led by a Woman Sarpanch Created Rajasthan’s First Alcohol-Free Village. Kachabali village in Rajsamand district, located about 330 km from Jaipur, has become the first in Rajasthan to vote in support of closing down the lone liquor shop in the village.

Concept note-3: -Lakshadweep. Lakshadweep, since 2021 no longer prohibits consumption of alcohol. Earlier it was the only union territory that banned the sale and consumption of alcohol.
