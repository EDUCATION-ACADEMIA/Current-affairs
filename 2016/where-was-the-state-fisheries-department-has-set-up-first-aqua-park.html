Where was the State Fisheries Department has set up first aqua park

(A) ** Pookode Lake

(B) Karalad Lake

(C) Vembanad

(D) Vellayani Lake

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s first Aqua Park in Arunachal Pradesh The Government of India has given its approval for the establishment of India’s first-ever Aqua Park in Arunachal Pradesh at Tarin (Ziro) in the lower Subansiri District. The ambitious project is sanctioned under the CSS Component of Pradhan Mantri Matsya Sampada Yojana.

Concept note-2: -Itanagar: A fish museum, the first of its kind in the Northeast, would soon be built in Arunachal Pradesh, Fisheries Minister Tage Taki said here. The museum would be a part of the Integrated Aqua Park (IAP), sanctioned by the Union Ministry of Fisheries, he said on Sunday.

Concept note-3: -Taraporewala Aquarium or Taraporevala Aquarium is India’s oldest aquarium and one of Mumbai’s main attractions.

Concept note-4: -Noun. aquapark (plural aquaparks) A water park.
