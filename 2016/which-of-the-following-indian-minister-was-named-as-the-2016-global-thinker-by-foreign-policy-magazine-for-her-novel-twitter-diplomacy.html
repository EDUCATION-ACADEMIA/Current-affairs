Which of the following Indian Minister was named as the 2016 Global Thinker’ by Foreign Policy magazine for her ’novel Twitter diplomacy’.

(A) Sushri Uma Bharati

(B) Narendra Modi

(C) Maneka Sanjay Gandhi

(D) ** Sushma Swaraj

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The current Minister of External Affairs is Subrahmanyam Jaishankar, succeeding Sushma Swaraj of the Bharatiya Janata Party on 30 May 2019.

Concept note-2: -The Ministry of External Affairs is the Indian government’s agency responsible for the foreign relations of India. The Minister of External Affairs holds cabinet rank as a member of the Council of Ministers.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) balances and maintains foreign policy making in India. It looks after all the foreign affairs and is in charge of foreign policy making in India, policy execution, and the day-to-day management of international relations.
