Which bank has enabled online shopping portal Flipkart in its mobile banking application in order to ensure a seamless shopping experience for its customers.

(A) Axis Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) IDBI Bank

(D) ** Kotak Mahindra Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, your shopping experience can be easy, hassle-free, and also beneficial with this Flipkart Axis bank credit card. To be eligible to apply for this card, you should be a salaried employee with a monthly income of a minimum of Rs.

Concept note-2: -Digital Banking Products Bank of Baroda provides various digital products like ATM, debit card, mobile banking, internet banking, etc. to make your banking experience smooth and save time & money. These products help customer in banking without actually walking into the branch.

Concept note-3: -On the payment page, under payment options, select Debit Card EMI. Once you do that, you have the option to select the EMI tenure of your choice. After authorising using OTP or your PIN number, you can confirm the EMI payment plan of your choice.

Concept note-4: -State Bank of India (SBI) offers an EMI facility for its customers using SBI debit cards in order to purchase consumer durables from merchant stores by swiping their cards at POS. They can also avail of this facility while buying online via e-commerce portals such as Amazon & Flipkart through SBI debit cards.
