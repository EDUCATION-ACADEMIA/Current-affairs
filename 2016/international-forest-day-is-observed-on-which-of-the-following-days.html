International Forest Day is observed on which of the following days

(A) March 23

(B) March 24

(C) ** March 21

(D) March 22

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United Nations General Assembly proclaimed 21 March the International Day of Forests in 2012 to celebrate and raise awareness of the importance of all types of forests.

Concept note-2: -Event: International Day of Forests 2022 | SDG Knowledge Hub | IISD. The UN General Assembly proclaimed 21 March as the International Day of Forests in 2012. The Day celebrates and raises awareness of the importance of all types of forests.

Concept note-3: -2022 Theme The theme for 2022 is “Forests and sustainable production and consumption”.

Concept note-4: -The theme for each International Day of Forests is chosen by the Collaborative Partnership on Forests. The theme for 2023 is “Forests and health."

Concept note-5: -The International Day of Forests was established on the 21st day of March, by resolution of the United Nations General Assembly on November 28, 2013.
