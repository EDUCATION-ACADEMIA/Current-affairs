(A) ** Competent, Adaptive, Partner

(B) Capable, Adaptive, Partners

(C) Capable, Adaptive, Security

(D) Competent, Partner, Security

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The exercise is taking place in and around the Hawaiian Islands and the southern California naval exercise areas from 30 June until 4 August.

Concept note-2: -Theme for 2022: The theme of RIMPAC 2022 is ‘capable, adaptive, partners.
