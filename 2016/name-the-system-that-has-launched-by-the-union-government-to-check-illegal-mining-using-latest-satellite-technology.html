Name the system, that has launched by the Union Government to check illegal mining using latest satellite technology.

(A) ** Mining Surveillance System

(B) Mining Super System

(C) Mining Service System

(D) Mining System Surveillance

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -MSS is a satellite-based monitoring system, which aims to check illegal mining activity through automatic remote-sensing detection technology.

Concept note-2: -The illegal mining of precious metals refers to prospecting and extraction of metals with high economic value (mainly gold, silver, platinum, palladium and iridium), 2 undertaken without appropriate land rights, exploration and mining licenses or mineral transportation and other permits.

Concept note-3: -Justice MB Shah Commission was set up to investigate illegal mining in different states.

Concept note-4: -(A) “Illegal mining” means any reconnaissance or prospecting or mining operation undertaken by any person or a company in any area without holding a mineral concession as required under sub-section (1) of section 4.

Concept note-5: -To examine the causes or factors influencing the activities of illegal mining in Ghana, the research used five influential factors which are in line with literature: High Unemployment, high dependency rate, Poverty, Mining Mismanagement (Laws), and High Illiteracy.
