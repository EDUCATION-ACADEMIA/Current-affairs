Who has been appointed a member of the International Coordinating Committee of National Human Rights Institutions Bureau in Geneva

(A) T.S.Thakur

(B) P.Sathasivam

(C) Rajendra Mal Lodha

(D) ** H.L. Dattu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Global Alliance of National Human Rights Institution (GANHRI) was formerly known as the International Coordinating Committee (ICC) of National Human Rights Institutions. It was established in the year 1993.

Concept note-2: -The Chairperson and members of the NHRC are appointed by the President of India, on the recommendation of a committee consisting of: The Prime Minister (Chairperson) The Home Minister. The Leader of the Opposition in the Lok Sabha (Lower House)

Concept note-3: -The Human Rights Committee is composed of 18 independent experts, who are persons of high moral character and recognized competence in the field of human rights.
