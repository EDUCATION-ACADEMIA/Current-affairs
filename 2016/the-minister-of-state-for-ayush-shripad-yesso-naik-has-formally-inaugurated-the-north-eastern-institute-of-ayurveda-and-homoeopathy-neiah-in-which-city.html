The Minister of State for AYUSH Shripad Yesso Naik has formally inaugurated the North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda and Homoeopathy (NEIAH) in which city

(A) Guwahati

(B) Dimapur

(C) Agartala

(D) ** Shillong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Shripad Yesso Naik, Hon’ble Minister of State (IC), Ministry of AYUSH, Govt. of India, formally inaugurated the institute on 22nd December, 2016 in the august presence of Smt. R. Warjiri, Hon’ble Minister of Health and Family Welfare, Govt.

Concept note-2: -The North Eastern Institute of Ayurveda & Homoeopathy (NEIAH), Shillong, Meghalaya is an autonomous institute newly established under the Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -Introduction. AYUSH is an acronym for Ayurveda, Yoga and Naturopathy, Unani, Siddha and Homeopathy and are the six Indian systems of medicine prevalent and practiced in India and some of the neighboring Asian countries with very few exceptions in some of the developed countries.

Concept note-4: -(Dr.) S. P. Bhattacharjee, Director, NEIAH accompanied him while inaugurating the 1st Foundation Day Celebration of NEIAH.

Concept note-5: -During our internship, we get a stipend of 23, 000 rupees for our service as an internee.
