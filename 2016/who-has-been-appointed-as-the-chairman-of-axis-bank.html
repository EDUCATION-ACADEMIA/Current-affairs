Who has been appointed as the chairman of Axis Bank

(A) ** Sanjiv Misra

(B) V. R. Kaundinya

(C) Shikha Sharma

(D) Prasad Menon

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Amitabh Chaudhry is the Managing Director & Chief Executive Officer (MD & CEO) of Axis Bank Limited.

Concept note-2: -RBI approves Rakesh Makhija’s reappointment as Axis Bank’s Non-Executive Chairman.

Concept note-3: -Rakesh Makhija, the independent director was appointed as the non-executive (part-time) chairman of the axis bank, for a period of 3 years, with effect from July 18, 2019, up to July 17, 2022.

Concept note-4: -Rajiv joined Axis AMC in 2009 and was the founding Managing Director & CEO of Axis Asset Management Co. Ltd. He was appointed as the President (Retail Banking) of Axis Bank in May 2013, to spearhead the business with his sharp acumen which led to the achievement of several key milestones.
