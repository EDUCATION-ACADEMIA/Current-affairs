What is the theme for the 2016 World AIDS Day

(A) ** Hands up for #HIVprevention

(B) On the fast track to end AIDS

(C) Close the gap

(D) Zero Discrimination

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of this year’s campaign in the Eastern Mediterranean Region is “Dignity above all”. The Constitution of the World Health Organization enshrines the right of every human being to have the highest attainable standard of health as a fundamental entitlement.

Concept note-2: -November 27, 2015 / 64(46); 1281 World AIDS Day, observed on December 1, draws attention to the current status of the human immunodeficiency virus/acquired immunodeficiency syndrome (HIV/AIDS) epidemic worldwide. This year’s theme is World AIDS 2015: The Time to Act is Now.

Concept note-3: -Each World AIDS Day focuses on a specific theme, which this year will be Equalize. UNAIDS is urging each of us to address the inequalities which are holding back progress in ending AIDS.

Concept note-4: -World AIDS Day 2014 is an opportunity to harness the power of social change to put people first and close the access gap.

Concept note-5: -Equalize-World AIDS Day 2022 The “Equalize” slogan is a call to action. It is a prompt for all of us to work for the proven practical actions needed to address inequalities and help end AIDS.
