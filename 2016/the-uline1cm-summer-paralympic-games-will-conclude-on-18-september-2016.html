The _________ Summer Paralympic Games will conclude on 18 September 2016.

(A) ** 15th

(B) 23rd

(C) 16th

(D) 13th

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 15th Paralympic Summer Games will be held in Rio, Brazil, in 2016 just days after the completion of the Summer Olympic Games. They are scheduled to be held from 7 to 18 September 2016. There will be a maximum of 22 sports on the program, and 526 medal events.

Concept note-2: -it has been India’s best ever performance in the history of the summer Paralympic games with a total of 4 medals won ( 2 Gold, 1 Silver and 1 Bronze ) with Devendra Jhajharia breaking the World Record to win a gold medal at the Paralympics.

Concept note-3: -SPORTS. Rio 2016 featured 22 sports, two more than London 2012, with canoe and triathlon making their Paralympic debut.

Concept note-4: -Devendra Jhajharia was India’s flag-bearer at the 2016 Summer paralympic Games.
