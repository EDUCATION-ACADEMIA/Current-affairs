Who has won the ninth edition of the Indian Premier League (IPL) on 29 May 2016

(A) Mumbai Indians

(B) Royal Challengers Bangalore

(C) Gujarat Lions

(D) ** Sunrisers Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sunrisers Hyderabad won the match by 8 runs and secured the 2016 Indian Premier League trophy, which happens to be their maiden title in the history of the tournament.
