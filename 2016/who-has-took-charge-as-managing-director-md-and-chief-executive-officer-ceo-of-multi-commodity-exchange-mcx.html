Who has took charge as managing director (MD) and chief executive officer (CEO) of Multi Commodity Exchange (MCX)

(A) Parveen Kumar Singhal

(B) Sandeep Kumar Sarawgi

(C) Krishnan Vishwanathan

(D) ** Mrugank Paranjape

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PS Reddy has taken charge as the Managing Director (MD) and Chief Executive Officer (CEO) of Multi Commodity Exchange (MCX) of India on Friday.

Concept note-2: -Representative Image. Multi Commodity Exchange of India (MCX) Tuesday announced the appointment of P S Reddy as the managing director and CEO of the company. The appointment is for a period of five years subject to the regulatory approvals, MCX said in a BSE filing.
