Which of the following Indira Gandhi International Airport terminal has achieved Platinum rating from Indian Green Building Council (IGBC)

(A) Terminal 1

(B) Terminal 2

(C) ** Terminal 3

(D) Terminal 4

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Terminal 3 also achieved “Platinum Rating” from Indian Green Building Council (IGBC) on Green Existing Building standard.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai’s Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (CSMIA), on Monday, announced that it has entirely switched to green sources for its energy consumption needs, making it one of India’s 100 per cent sustainable airports.

Concept note-3: -The recently revamped Terminal 2 boasts of high-end facilities and offers amazing restaurants, premium lounges as well as telecommunication, baggage wrap, and porter services. This terminal at IGI Airport can handle up to 12 million passengers per year.

Concept note-4: -Named after Indira Gandhi, the former Prime Minister of India, it is the busiest airport in India in terms of daily flight traffic and second busiest in term of passenger traffic in India after Mumbai’s Chhatrapati Shivaji International Airport.

Concept note-5: -Airport Terminal 3, New Delhi-India.
