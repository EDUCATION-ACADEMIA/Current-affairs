G-20 meeting (2016) Finance ministers and Central Bank Governors meeting held in which city

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Chengdu

(C) Ankara

(D) Beijing

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The participants held extensive discussions on four key agenda items-breaking a new path for growth, more effective and efficient global economic and financial governance, robust international trade and investment, and inclusive and interconnected development.

Concept note-2: -The First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting, under the Indian Presidency, was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka, during December 13-14, 2022, under the co-chairship of Secretary, Economic Affairs, Shri Ajay Seth and Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India, Dr. Michael Patra.

Concept note-3: -The G20 Finance Track, led by Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20 countries, focuses on economic and financial issues. It provides an effective forum for global economic discourse and policy coordination.

Concept note-4: -India holds the Presidency of the G20 from December 1, 2022 to November 30, 2023. The 43 Heads of Delegations-the largest ever in G20-will be participating in the final New Delhi Summit in September next year.

Concept note-5: -Pune, 9 January 2023 Around 200 delegates from 38 countries will be visiting Pune next week to attend this conference. After landing at the Lohgaon Airport the delegates will travel to the conference venue on Senapati Bapat Road.
