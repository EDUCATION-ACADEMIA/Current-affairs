Who has appointed as the new Director of Tamil Nadu Cricket Association (TNCA) Academy

(A) K.S.Viswanathan

(B) V. P. Narasimhan

(C) R. R. Kalidas Vandayar

(D) ** D. Vasu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CHENNAI: Dr P Ashok Sigamani, son of Tamil Nadu Minister for Higher Education K Ponmudy, was on Saturday elected as president of the Tamil Nadu Cricket Association (TNCA).

Concept note-2: -The Tamil Nadu Cricket Association.

Concept note-3: -The stadium was formerly known as Madras Cricket Club Ground or Chepauk Stadium and was established in the year 1916 making it the oldest Cricket Stadium in India which is in continuous use. Commonly known as Chepauk, its first match was played on 10 February 1934.
