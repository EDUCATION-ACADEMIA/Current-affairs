Which actor was felicitated at the first edition of BRICS Film Festival that recently commenced in New Delhi

(A) Rajinikanth

(B) ** Rishi Kapoor

(C) Amitabh Bachchan

(D) Jackie Chan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Veteran actor, film producer and director Randhir Kapoor and actor Manoj Bajpayee and also were felicitated at the closing ceremony.

Concept note-2: -KOLKATA: ‘Aparajito’, the Anik Dutta film that is inspired by the story behind the making of Satyajit Ray’s ‘Pather Panchali’, has won the special jury award at the 2022 BRICS Film Festival.

Concept note-3: -The 1st BRIC Summit was held in Yekaterinburg, Russia, on 16 June 2009.

Concept note-4: -Hosted by China Film Administration and undertaken by the Shanghai International Film and TV Festival Center, the 2022 BRICS Film Festival was held several days ago. This year, the festival is composed of three programs, that is film competition, themed forums and film screenings.
