Which of the following book is the best-selling book of 2016

(A) Strengths Finder

(B) First 100 Words

(C) Born Again on the Mountain

(D) ** Harry Potter

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -That’s according to Amazon’s roundup of the best-selling books of 2016. Harry Potter and the Cursed Child (a post-Battle for Hogwarts play written by J.K. Rowling, Jack Thorne, and John Tiffany) topped the list of the 20 best-sellers across all categories.

Concept note-2: -According to Guinness World Records as of 1995, the Bible is the best-selling book of all time with an estimated 5 billion copies sold and distributed.

Concept note-3: -The best-selling book of all time is the Christian Bible.

Concept note-4: -The Harry Potter series is far and away the highest-selling series of novels ever. Written by British author J.K. Rowling, the series has sold at least 500 million copies, 150 million more than the next-highest selling series.
