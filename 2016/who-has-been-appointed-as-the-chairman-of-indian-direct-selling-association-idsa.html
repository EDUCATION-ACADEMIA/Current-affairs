Who has been appointed as the Chairman of Indian Direct Selling Association (IDSA)

(A) P. Sanker

(B) Rajat Banerji

(C) ** Jitendra Jagota

(D) Rini Sanyal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajat Banerji is Vice President-Corporate Affairs at Amway India Enterprises, has been an integral part of the direct selling industry for over two-decades.

Concept note-2: -Member companies of IDSA include companies such as 4Life, Altos, Amway, Avon, Blulife, DXN, Enagic, Glaze, Herbalife, IMC, Jeunesse, K-Link, Modicare, Oriflame, PM International, Tianshi, Tupperware and Unicity. The members comprise of both Indian and international companies.

Concept note-3: -Incorporated in the year 1996, the Indian Direct Selling Association (IDSA) is an autonomous, self-regulatory body for the direct selling industry in India. The Association acts as an interface between the industry and policy-making bodies of the Government facilitating the cause of Direct Selling Industry in India.

Concept note-4: -In 1962, Maxwell Finland, MD, a premier investigator, spearheaded this gathering.
