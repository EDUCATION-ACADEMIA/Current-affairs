Who is the author of ’Prince of Tides’

(A) Nelson Algren

(B) ** Pat Conroy

(C) Sam Greenlee

(D) Scott Heim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pat Conroy (1945–2016) was the author of The Boo, The Water Is Wide, The Great Santini, The Lords of Discipline, The Prince of Tides, Beach Music, The Pat Conroy Cookbook: Recipes of My Life, My Losing Season, South of Broad, My Reading Life, and The Death of Santini.

Concept note-2: -Pat Conroy (1945–2016) was the author of The Boo, The Water Is Wide, The Great Santini, The Lords of Discipline, The Prince of Tides, Beach Music, The Pat Conroy Cookbook: Recipes of My Life, My Losing Season, South of Broad, My Reading Life, and The Death of Santini.

Concept note-3: -Set in New York City and the low-country of South Carolina, THE PRINCE OF TIDES opens when Tom, a high school football coach whose marriage and career are crumbling, flies from South Carolina to New York after learning of his twin sister’s suicide attempt.

Concept note-4: -It also helps that the narration was full of self-deprecating and sarcastic humor from the book’s narrator, Tom Wingo.
