ICHR is planning to document stories and legends relating to villages and towns across India into an encyclopedia.What does stands for ICHR

(A) ** Indian Council of Historical Research

(B) Information Council of History Research

(C) Institute of Common History Resource

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Home : Indian Council of Historical Research-Govt of India. ICHR…

Concept note-2: -The Indian Council of Historical Research (ICHR) is a captive body of the Ministry of Education, Government of India established by an Administrative Order. The body has provided financial assistance to historians and scholars through fellowships, grants, and symposia.

Concept note-3: -Professor Raghuvendra Tanwar (DOB 20.02. 1955) has an outstanding academic record with two Gold Medals at the Post Graduate level for standing ‘First Class First’ M.A. History and for securing the highest percentage of marks in the Faculty of Social Sciences (Kurukshetra University – 1977).

Concept note-4: -Become a member of ICHR by paying a nominal registration fee. Account payee cheques to be made to ‘The Member Secretary, ICHR, NEW DELHI’.
