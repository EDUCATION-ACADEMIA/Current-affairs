How Many Beaches are Located in India among top 10 beaches in asia

(A) 0

(B) 1

(C) ** 3

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has three of the top 10 beaches in Asia favoured by travellers, a survey by travel website TripAdvisor said.

Concept note-2: -These eight beaches include Shivrajpur in Gujarat, Puri Golden Beach in Odisha, Kasarkod and Padubidri in Karnataka, Ghoghla in Diu, Rushikonda in Andhra Pradesh, Kappad in Kerala, and Radhanagar in Andaman and Nicobar Islands.

Concept note-3: -Radhanagar Beach, Havelock Island So, it’s needless to say that it is the best beach in India. Many people across the world come over to marvel at this lovely beach. But if you want some time alone go for a stroll along the shores, you’ll find much-needed tranquility around.

Concept note-4: -Palolem Beach in South Goa Palolem Beach | #25 of 51 Famous Beaches in India. Explore a slice of heaven at the Palolem Beach, which is also called Paradise Beach. The serene waters, white sand, quaint wooden shacks and swaying palm trees make it one of the top beach places in India.
