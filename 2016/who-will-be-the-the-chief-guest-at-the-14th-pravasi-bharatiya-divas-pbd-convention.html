Who will be the the Chief Guest at the 14th Pravasi Bharatiya Divas (PBD) Convention

(A) Cristina Fernandez de Kirchner

(B) Dilma Rousseff

(C) Justin Trudeau

(D) ** Antonio Costa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 09 January 2023, the PBD Convention will be inaugurated by Shri Narendra Modi, Hon’ble Prime Minister of India and will feature addresses by the Chief Guest, H.E. Dr Mohamed Irfaan Ali, the President of Cooperative Republic of Guyana, and the Special Guest of Honour, H.E.

Concept note-2: -Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi met President of the Cooperative Republic of Guyana, H.E. Dr. Mohamed Irfaan Ali, on the sidelines of 17th Pravasi Bhartiya Divas (PBD) in Indore today. President Irfaan Ali is on an official visit to India from 8-14 January 2023 and is the Chief Guest at the 17th PBD.

Concept note-3: -The Special Guest of Honour is Suriname President Chandrikapersad Santokhi and the Chief Guest is Dr. Mohamed Irfaan Ali, President of Guyana. An award called The Pravasi Bharatiya Samman Award is given out as part of the programme.

Concept note-4: -Mohamed Irfaan Ali is the chief guest for the event that will be held this year. These are the details that one should know about the upcoming event.

Concept note-5: -The theme of this PBD Convention is ‘Diaspora: Reliable partners for India’s progress in Amrit Kaal’.
