Dharmendra Pradhan, Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) for Petroleum and Natural Gas launched the _________ campaign to promote use of natural gas

(A) ** #Gas4India

(B) *GasIndia

(C) #GasBharat

(D) #Gas1India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI: Petroleum Minister Dharmendra Pradhan on Tuesday launched the ‘Gas4India’ campaign that is aimed at promoting the use of natural gas in the country and said the country “will move towards a gas-based economy".

Concept note-2: -Petroleum Minister launches SATAT initiative to promote Compressed Bio-Gas as an alternative, green transport fuel.

Concept note-3: -Shri Hardeep S Puri, Minister of Petroleum and Natural Gas, Minister of Housing and Urban Affairs.

Concept note-4: -Dharmendra Pradhan (pronounced [dʰɔɾmendɾɔ pɾɔdʰān]; born 26 June 1969) is an Indian politician serving as the Minister of Education and Minister of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship in the Government of India.

Concept note-5: -The Ministry has been allocated Rs 8, 940 crore for 2022-23, a 1% increase over the revised estimates of 2021-22. Note: SPR = Strategic Petroleum Reserves.
