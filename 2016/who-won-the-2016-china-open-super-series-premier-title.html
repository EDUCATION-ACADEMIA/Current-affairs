Who won the 2016 China Open Super Series Premier title

(A) Carolina Marin

(B) Sun Yu

(C) Saina Nehwal

(D) ** P.V. Sindhu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s ace shuttler PV Sindhu won the 2016 China Open Super Series title by defeating China’s Sun Yu (World No. 9). It was her maiden Super Series Premier title and first post-2016 Rio Olympics title.

Concept note-2: -The season-ending World Tour Finals, scheduled for Guangzhou from Dec. 14-18, will feature the top eight players and pairs in each category with a prize pool of $1.5 million, the biggest the event has ever offered, the BWF said.

Concept note-3: -The finals will feature the biggest prize pool ever seen at a BWF event: $1.5m (€1.5m). However, despite the impending return of international badminton events, this season’s China Open in Changzhou and Fuzhou China Open will no longer take place. The tournaments would have been held in December.
