Prime Minister Narendra Modi’s radio address ’Mann Ki Baat’ translated into the native tongue and incorporating answers to questions invited from the people of which country

(A) ** Bangladesh

(B) Nepal

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Myanmar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mann Ki Baat (Hindi: , transl. Inner Thoughts) is an Indian radio programme hosted by Prime Minister Narendra Modi in which he addresses the people of the nation on All India Radio, DD National and DD News. Since the first show on 3 October 2014, there have been 97 episodes.

Concept note-2: -The current Prime Minister of India is Mr Narendra Modi.

Concept note-3: -People can also give a missed call on 1922 and follow the link received in SMS to directly give their suggestions to the Prime Minister.

Concept note-4: -Shri Narendra Modi was sworn-in as India’s Prime Minister on 30th May 2019, marking the start of his second term in office.
