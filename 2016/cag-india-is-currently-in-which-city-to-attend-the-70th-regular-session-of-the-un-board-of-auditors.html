CAG India is currently in which city to attend the 70th Regular Session of the UN Board of Auditors

(A) Paris

(B) London

(C) ** New York

(D) Dubai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CAG of India, Sh. Girish Chandra Murmu (centre) along with the Heads of delegation from Niger, Philippines, Brazil, Egypt, Qatar and Kenya (left to right) at the 14th KSC SC Meeting held in Cairo, Egypt from 12th to 14th September, 2022.

Concept note-2: -Explanation: The function of the Public Accounts Committee is to examine the annual audit reports of the Comptroller and Auditor General of India (CAG), which are laid before the Parliament by the President.
