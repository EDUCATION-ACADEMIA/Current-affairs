The country’s first industrial park, exclusively for women entrepreneurs, was launched in which state

(A) Mizoram

(B) Manipur

(C) Assam

(D) ** Uttarakhand

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Uttarakhand will be the first state in the country to set up an industrial park for women entrepreneurs.

Concept note-2: -Q. India’s first ‘Women-owned Industrial Park’ is located in which city? Notes: India’s first ‘Women-owned Industrial Park’ has begun operations with 25 Green Projects in Hyderabad. The ‘FLO Industrial Park’ is promoted by FICCI Ladies Organization (FLO) in partnership with the Telangana government.

Concept note-3: -India’s first 100% women-owned FLO industrial park was inaugurated in Hyderabad. The 50-acre FLO Industrial Park was established with an investment of Rs 250 crores. It is the flagship project and the first of its kind at a national level.

Concept note-4: -The FICCI Ladies Organization industrial park is the country’s first of its sort This park was built on 50 acres of land in Sultanpur, which is near Patancheru. It was made with a Rs 250 crore investment. Female entrepreneurs are already expressing a strong desire to launch and operate their businesses at this park.

Concept note-5: -The history of courageous female entrepreneursin India started quite early with Kalpana Saroj. Known as India’s first female entrepreneur and the original “Slumdog Millionaire”, Kalpana Saroj bought the distressed assets of Kamani Tubes Company and steered the company back to profitability like a boss back in 2001.
