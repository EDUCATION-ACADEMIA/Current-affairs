Which of the following Indian actress won the Lifetime Achievement Award at Dubai film fest 2016

(A) Hema Malini

(B) Jaya Bachchan

(C) Madhuri Dixit

(D) ** Rekha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Rekha. Indian actress Rekha will be honored by the Dubai International Film Festival (DIFF) with a Lifetime Achievement Award. The festival commenced on 7 December 2016 and will conclude on 14 December 2016. She started her career in 1966 as a child actress in the Telugu film Rangula Ratnam.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answe is Rekha. Rekha was honoured with Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2016 Dubai International film Festival (DIFF).
