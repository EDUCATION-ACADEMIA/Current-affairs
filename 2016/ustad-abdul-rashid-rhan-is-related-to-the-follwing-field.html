Ustad Abdul Rashid Rhan is Related to the Follwing Field

(A) Music Director

(B) Singer

(C) ** Classical Singer

(D) Western Singer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Ustad Abdul Rashid Khan (19 August 1908 – 18 February 2016) was an Indian vocalist of Hindustani music. Apart from khayal, he performed dhrupad, dhamar and thumri. As of 2020, he is the oldest person to have been conferred a Padma award.

Concept note-2: -Ustad Rashid Khan Sahab is a world renowned veteran Indian Classical Vocalist. He belongs to the Rampur Sahaswan Gharana, and is the great grandson of Gharana founder Ustad Inayat Hussain.

Concept note-3: -Ustad as a title in Hindustani classical music Ustad (abbreviated as Ust. or Ut. or Ud. and from Persian ) is an honorific title for an expert person in Indian classical singing and instrumental playing, used for a Muslim musician. It is used in Hindustani classical music to recognize master performers.

Concept note-4: -Ustad Rashid Khan is a skilled player of the swarmandal, and the distinctive drone he produces on the instrument makes an ideal foundation for his powerful vocal performances.
