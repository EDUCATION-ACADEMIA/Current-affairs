What is the theme for the World Science Day for Peace and Development 2016

(A) Quality Science Education: Ensuring a sustainable future for all

(B) Science for a Sustainable Future

(C) Scientific Issues for Development of the World

(D) ** Celebrating Science Centres and Science Museums

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Basic Sciences for Sustainable Development.

Concept note-2: -About the theme: Basic Sciences for Sustainable Development World Science Day is contributing to the Year in 2022 by celebrating this theme. ‘We need more basic science to achieve The 2030 Agenda and its 17 Sustainable Development Goals’, the United Nations General Assembly noted in December 2021.

Concept note-3: -The theme for WSDPD 2015 is Science for a Sustainable Future. It will focus on celebrating the launch of UNESCO Science Report.

Concept note-4: -Union Minister Dr Jitendra Singh unveils the theme for National Science Day 2023, titled Global Science for Global Wellbeing.

Concept note-5: -The theme for NSD of the year 2022 is ‘Integrated Approach in S&T for Sustainable Future’. On 28 February 2009, five institutions in India were presented the ‘National Award for Science Communication’ by the Indian Department of Science and Technology (IDST).
