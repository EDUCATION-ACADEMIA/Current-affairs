India’s second eco-friendly airport comes up in which of the following city

(A) Visakhapatnam

(B) ** Vadodara

(C) Surat

(D) Kochi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -By Press Trust of India: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday inaugurated the new integrated airport terminal in Vadodara, which is now the country’s second green airport after Kochi, and said such projects will encourage people to take up environment-friendly constructions.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai’s Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (CSMIA), the first in India to launch hybrid technology, has announced that it has entirely switched to green sources for its energy this year.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: Prime Minister Narendra Modi on Saturday inaugurated Arunachal Pradesh’s first greenfield airport, Donyi Polo Airport, in Itanagar’s Hollongi, in a move that will connectivity and tourism in India’s northeast region.

Concept note-4: -Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi has opened a new greenfield international airport at Mopa in the state of Goa.

Concept note-5: -Pasighat Airport (IATA: IXT, ICAO: VEPG) is a regional airport serving Pasighat, Arunachal Pradesh, India.
