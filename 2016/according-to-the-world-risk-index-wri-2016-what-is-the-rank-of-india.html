According to the World Risk Index (WRI) 2016, what is the rank of India

(A) ** 77

(B) 121

(C) 56

(D) 145

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India has been ranked 77th on the World risk index, read on to know more about the World Risk Report 2016. By India Today Web Desk: India has been ranked 77th on the World risk index.

Concept note-2: -The World Risk Report 2016 analyzes the role that infrastructure plays in shaping a country’s disaster risk. The World Risk Index, an integral part of the report, ranks 171 countries according to their risk of becoming a victim of a disaster as a result of natural hazards such as floods, cyclones, or earthquakes.

Concept note-3: -Global disaster risk index 2022, by select country Philippines is the country with the highest natural disaster risk. According to a 2022 study, it was given the disaster risk index (WRI) of 46.82. India followed, with a disaster risk index of 42.31.

Concept note-4: -The WorldRiskIndex calculates disaster risk for 193 countries and thus 99 percent of the world’s population; the Philippines, India and Indonesia have the highest risk, followed by Colombia and Mexico. Germany ranks 101 in the global midfield-and is no longer in the lowest of the five risk classes as in the past.
