Which Indian short film has been invited for special screening at the 42nd Seattle International Film Festival (SIFF)

(A) Even the Walls

(B) Me and Earl and the Dying Girl

(C) ** Beti

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Beti’ is a 4-minute documentary film on girl child education from India that has been invited for special screening at the 42nd Seattle International Film Festival (SIFF) in Washington DC.

Concept note-2: -A non-fiction short film on girl child education “Beti” has been invited for a special screening at the 42nd edition of Seattle International Film Festival (SIFF) 2016, Washington DC.

Concept note-3: -The 53rd International Film Festival of India was an event held from 20-28 November 2022 with Alma & Oskar by Dieter Berner as the Opening feature film, and Perfect Number by Krzysztof Zanussi as the Closing feature film.

Concept note-4: -Directed by Costa Rican director Valentina Maurel, Spanish film ‘I Have Electric Dreams’ won The Golden Peacock which includes a cash award of Rs 40 lakh.
