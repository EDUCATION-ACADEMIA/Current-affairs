Exercise Desert Eagle-II concluded in which country

(A) India

(B) ** Abu Dhabi

(C) Dubai

(D) Ajman

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This was a 10days air combat exercise that started on May 22 in Al-Dhafra Air Base, Abu Dhabi, between IAF and the UAE.

Concept note-2: -Joint Military Exercises with the UAE: With the UAE, India has In-UAE BILAT (bilateral naval exercise) as well as Desert Eagle-II (bilateral air force exercise).

Concept note-3: -In October 2021, the Indian Air Force-Egyptian Air Force held the first-ever Joint Tactical Air Exercise ‘Desert Warrior’.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is France. Indian Air Force and French Air and Space Force conducted a bilateral Air exercise, Ex Desert Knight-21.
