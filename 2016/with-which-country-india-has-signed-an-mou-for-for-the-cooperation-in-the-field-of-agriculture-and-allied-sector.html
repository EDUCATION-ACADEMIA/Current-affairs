With which country India has signed an MoU for for the cooperation in the field of agriculture and allied sector

(A) Qatar

(B) Oman

(C) China

(D) ** Taiwan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India and Fiji have signed an MoU for cooperation in the field of agriculture and allied sectors.

Concept note-2: -India and Panama have signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) to enhance cooperation in the training of diplomats.

Concept note-3: -India and France have signed an MoU with a scope for cooperation in health research. Science and Technology Ministry said, the MoU was signed on Tuesday between Council of Scientific and Industrial Research, CSIR and Institut Pasteur, France.

Concept note-4: -The Comptroller & Auditor General of India and the Comptroller General of the Republic of Chile signed a memorandum of understanding (MoU) to strengthen institutional and professional capacities and learn from each other’s best practices.

Concept note-5: -Mahendra Reddy signed a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for cooperation in the field of Agriculture and allied sectors between India and Fiji in a virtual meeting held today. On this occasion, Shri Tomar said that India believes in the spirit of ‘Vasudhaiv Kutumbakam’.
