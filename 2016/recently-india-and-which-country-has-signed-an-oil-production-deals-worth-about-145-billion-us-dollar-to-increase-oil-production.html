Recently, India and which country has signed an oil production deals worth about 1.45 billion US dollar to increase oil production.

(A) Dubai

(B) ** Venezuela

(C) Saudi Arabia

(D) US

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Rajasthan is the largest producer of petroleum in India followed by Gujarat and Assam. Rajasthan has a share of about 23.29% in crude oil production in the country. In India, oil is found in Assam, Gujarat, Mumbai High and in the river basins of Godavari and Krishna.

Concept note-2: -Assam is the highest natural gas-producing state with a production of 8.3 MMSMCD.

Concept note-3: -Answer: India’s top three petroleum-producing states are Rajasthan, Gujarat, and Assam. Approximately 23.29 per cent of the nation’s crude oil is produced in Rajasthan. Oil can be discovered in India throughout Assam, Gujarat, Mumbai High, and the Godavari or Krishna river basins.
