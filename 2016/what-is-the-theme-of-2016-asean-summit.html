What is the theme of 2016 ASEAN Summit

(A) ** Turning Vision into Reality for a Dynamic ASEAN Community

(B) Our People, Our Community, Our Vision.

(C) Moving Forward in Unity to a Peaceful and Prosperous Community

(D) ASEAN: One Community, One Destiny

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -We, the Heads of State/Government of ASEAN Member States, gathered for the 28th and 29th ASEAN Summits, held under the Chairmanship of the Lao People’s Democratic Republic (Lao PDR) with the theme “Turning Vision into Reality for a Dynamic ASEAN Community” in Vientiane on 6-7 September 2016.

Concept note-2: -The ASEAN 2016 theme is “Turning vision into reality for a dynamic ASEAN community” and the website is www.asean2016.gov.la.

Concept note-3: -The 28th and 29th ASEAN Summits were held in September 2016 in Vientiane, Laos.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the Kingdom of Cambodia’s Chairmanship of ASEAN for 2022 is “ASEAN A.C.T.: Addressing Challenges Together”.
