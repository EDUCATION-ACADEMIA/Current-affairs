Which team has won Irani Cup Cricket title

(A) Karnataka

(B) Mumbai

(C) Delhi

(D) ** Rest of India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Key Points related to the Rest of India winning the Irani Cup.

Concept note-2: -Irani Cup 2022: Hanuma Vihari-led Rest of India hammer defending champions Saurashtra by 8 wickets to win title | Cricket News | Zee News.

Concept note-3: -After Mumbai, Karnataka have won the most Ranji Trophies (8) followed by Delhi (7) and Baroda (5).

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Saurashtra. Saurashtra team won the Ranji Trophy title for the first time in 70 years.

Concept note-5: -The tournament was conceived during the 1959–60 season to mark the completion of 25 years of the Ranji Trophy championship and was named after the late BCCI president Zal R. Irani, who was associated with the BCCI from its inception in 1928, till his death in 1970.
