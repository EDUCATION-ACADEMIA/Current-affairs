An independent panel of British scientists and experts has formally given nod to introduce so-called three-parent baby fertility treatments or _________ Procedures

(A) Microbial Gene Therapy

(B) ** Mitochondrial Gene Therapy

(C) Microdystrophin Gene Therapy

(D) Microrna Gene Therapy

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -three-parent baby, human offspring produced from the genetic material of one man and two women through the use of assisted reproductive technologies, specifically mitochondrial replacement therapy (MRT) and three-person in vitro fertilization (IVF).

Concept note-2: -Doctors have received permission to create the UK’s first “three-person” babies for two women at risk of passing inheritable diseases to their children. The two cases involve women who have mitochondrial diseases, which are passed down by the mother and can prove fatal.

Concept note-3: -When defective mitochondria of the woman’s egg were replaced with mitochondria from a donor who did not carry the mutation, the resulting child carried DNA from three people: the female nuclear DNA donor, the male nuclear DNA or sperm donor, and the female mitochondria donor.

Concept note-4: -LONDON: Britain today created history by becoming the first country to legalise children conceived with DNA from three parents after lawmakers voted in favour of the controversial procedure that sparked a fierce ethical debate with senior Church figures.
