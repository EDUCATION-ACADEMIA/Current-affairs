Which of the following Indian -American was named as the Future Leader of Audiology

(A) P.S. Ayyaswamy

(B) Mahzarin Banaji

(C) Homi K. Bhabha

(D) ** Vinaya Manchaiah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Ratna, often addressed as the “father of Indian speech and hearing field”. He was one of the past directors of AYJNIHH, Mumbai and AIISH, Mysore. Dr Ratna has been a multifaceted leader and has been considered as“ teacher of teachers “ in the field of Audiology and Speech Language Patholgy in India.

Concept note-2: -While all of these pioneers substantially contributed to the development of the profession, one individual-Raymond Carhart-clearly stands out as the undisputed worldwide “Father of Audiology.” Raymond Thomas Carhart was born on March 28, 1912, in Mexico City.

Concept note-3: -History of the audiogram The doctor turned a knob that altered sound frequencies, and patients raised their hand when they had difficulty hearing. The first audiometer in the U.S. was developed in 1919 by Cordia C. Bunch.

Concept note-4: -Audiologists are hearing health professionals who identify, diagnose and manage individuals with peripheral or central hearing loss, tinnitus, vestibular and balance disorders and other communication disorders across the lifespan.
