Who won the 2016 US Open Men’s Doubles title.

(A) ** Pablo Carreno Busta and Guillermo Garcia-Lopez

(B) Jamie Murray and Bruno

(C) Herbert and Mahut

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Stan Wawrinka defeated the defending champion Novak Djokovic in the final, 6–7, 6–4, 7–5, 6–3 to win the men’s singles tennis title at the 2016 US Open. It was his third Grand Slam title; on all three occasions he defeated the world No. 1 player in the final.

Concept note-2: -Rafael Nadal defeated Kevin Anderson in the final, 6–3, 6–3, 6–4. It was his third US Open title and 16th major title overall. It was also his first hard court title since January 2014.
