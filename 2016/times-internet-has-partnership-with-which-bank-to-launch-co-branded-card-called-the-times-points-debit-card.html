Times Internet has partnership with which bank to launch co-branded card called the Times Points Debit Card.

(A) ICICI Bank

(B) Axis Bank

(C) IDBI Bank

(D) ** HDFC Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Times Points Debit Card-Earn Times Point on Every Spends | HDFC Bank.

Concept note-2: -Venu Nair, Customer Care Associate, MD and CEO, Shoppers Stop said, “Our co-branded cards with HDFC Bank will bring unmatched benefits to our ‘Citizen First’ members. They will be able to avail discounts and offers provided by the bank and redeem points at our stores.”

Concept note-3: -The Times Points Debit Card combines the synergies of HDFC Bank, one of the largest issuer of credit cards in the country offering a bouquet of cards catering to different segments of customers, and Times Points, an online loyalty product with over 18 million users.

Concept note-4: -Bhavesh Gupta, chief executive, Paytm Lending, said the company is delighted to partner with HDFC Bank, and Visa, to launch a comprehensive suite of credit cards across customer segments, with special focus on millennials, business owners and merchants.
