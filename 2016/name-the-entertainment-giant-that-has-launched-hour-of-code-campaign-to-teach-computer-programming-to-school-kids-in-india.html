Name the entertainment giant that has launched Hour of Code campaign to teach computer programming to school kids in India.

(A) Warner Bros.

(B) Red Giant Entertainment

(C) 20th Century Fox

(D) ** Disney

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -During Computer Science Education Week from December 9 to December 15, 2013, Code.org launched the “Hour of Code Challenge” on its website to teach computer science to school students, enticing them to complete short programming tutorials.

Concept note-2: -The Hour of Code is a one-hour introduction to computer science, using fun tutorials to show that anybody can learn the basics. This grassroots campaign is supported by over 400 partners and 200, 000 educators worldwide. Read our How-to guide to plan an event for your class.

Concept note-3: -Over the past years since its inception in 2013, the Hour of Code has reached tens of millions of students in 180+ countries. This grassroots campaign is supported by over 400 partners and 200, 000 educators worldwide.

Concept note-4: -CodeMonkey is an AWARD-WINNING online platform that teaches kids real coding languages like CoffeeScript and Python. Children and teenagers learn block-based and text-based coding through an engaging game-like environment. Millions of CodeMonkey’s students are now excited about coding!
