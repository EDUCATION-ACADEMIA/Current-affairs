Who among the following author has been received (Knight in the French Legion Of Honour) French civilian honour

(A) Bella Forrest

(B) James Patterson

(C) ** JK Rowling

(D) Jojo Moyes

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Mumbai, 13 November 2022 Mr Emmanuel Lenain, Ambassador of France to India, conferred the Chevalier de la Legion d’Honneur (Knight of the Legion of Honour) on Mr Sumeet Anand, Founder, IndSight Growth Partners, and President of the Indo-French Chamber of Commerce & Industry, in Mumbai.

Concept note-2: -Martin E. Copenhafer (Friday, 1 April 2022) (US), was awarded the Légion d’Honneur at 101 years old for his service in The United States Navy during the D-Day invasion of Normandy, France, on June 6, 1944, by President Emmanuel Macron.

Concept note-3: -Founded in 1802 by Napoléon Bonaparte, the National Order of the Legion of Honour is the highest French distinction for military and civil accomplishments. The National Order of Merit consist of three ranks: Chevalier (Knight), Officier (Officer) and Commandeur (Commander).
