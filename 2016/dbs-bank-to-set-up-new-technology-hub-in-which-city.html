DBS Bank to set up new technology hub in which city

(A) Mumbai

(B) Chennai

(C) ** Hyderabad

(D) New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The lender has appointed Mohit Kapoor as the head of its Technology Development Centre in Hyderabad.

Concept note-2: -Corporate Banking Address: DBS Bank India Limited, 19th Floor, Express Towers, Nariman Point, Mumbai – 400021.

Concept note-3: -It employs more than 30, 000 employees from more than 40 different countries. The first offshore technology development centre for the bank was built in Hyderabad in 2016 as DBS Tech India, formerly known as DBS Asia Hub 2.

Concept note-4: -(Your legal guide on estate planning, inheritance, will and more.) Lakshmi Vilas Bank (LVB) was merged with DBS Bank in November 2020, following the amalgamation with DBIL due to which the IFSC & MICR codes of all branches of erstwhile LVB have changed.
