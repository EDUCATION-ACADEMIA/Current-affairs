The World Theatre Day is observed on

(A) March 26

(B) ** March 27

(C) March 28

(D) March 31

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -So, to dedicate a day to theatres, March 27 is observed as ‘World Theatre Day’ across the globe. Theatre is one of the most popular forms of art that depicts culture, a tradition that exists around the world. This day preserves dramas and their essential role in the theatrical arts.

Concept note-2: -WORLD THEATRE DAY 2022: SIGNIFICANCE The significance of this day is to raise awareness regarding the importance of theatre and to know the importance of theatre around the world. The day also provides opportunities for theatrical communities to put forward their work on a large scale.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Theatre and a Culture of Peace. World Theatre Day is celebrated on 27 March every year. The theme for World Theatre Day 2022 is “Theatre and a Culture of Peace.” World Theatre Day was first celebrated by the International Theatre Institute (ITI) in the year 1961, on 27 March.

Concept note-4: -World Theatre Day was initiated in 1961 by the International Theatre Institute ITI. It is celebrated annually on the 27th March by ITI Centres and the international theatre community. Various national and international theatre events are organized to mark this occasion.

Concept note-5: -World Theatre Day 2022 Online Celebration Since 1962 World Theatre Day has been celebrated by ITI Centres, ITI Cooperating Members, theatre professionals, theatre organizations, theatre universities and theatre lovers all over the world on the 27th of March.
