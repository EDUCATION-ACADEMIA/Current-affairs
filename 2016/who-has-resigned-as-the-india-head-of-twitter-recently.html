Who has resigned as the India Head of Twitter recently

(A) Raheel Khursheed

(B) Rupert Murdoch

(C) Evan Williams

(D) ** Rishi Jaitly

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Parag Agrawal became the CEO of Twitter in November following the resignation of Twitter co-founder Jack Dorsey. Agrawal has been working with Twitter for almost a decade and was the chief technology officer before stepping into the CEO role.

Concept note-2: -Elon Musk’s acquisition of Twitter was completed on October 27, 2022. Post his acquisition, Musk immediately fired four of Twitter’s top executives, namely, Parag Agrawal(CEO), Ned Segal(CFO), Sean Edgett(General Counsel) and Vijaya Gadde(Policy Head).

Concept note-3: -Elon Musk announced he will be stepping down as Twitter CEO. “He’s great, ” came the response from Musk who has been scouting options for a new Twitter CEO after he announced that he would be stepping down from the position following a poll that decided the verdict.
