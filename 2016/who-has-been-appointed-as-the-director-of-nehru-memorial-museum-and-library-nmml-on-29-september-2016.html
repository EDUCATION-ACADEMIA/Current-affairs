Who has been appointed as the Director of Nehru Memorial Museum and Library (NMML) on 29 September 2016

(A) H M Cairae

(B) Bhaskar Mushahary

(C) Hemanga Kishore Sharma

(D) ** Shakti Sinha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Another member Pratap Bhanu Mehta had resigned in 2016 over the appointment of Shakti Sinha as NMML director.

Concept note-2: -The Nehru Memorial Museum & Library (NMML) is a museum and library in New Delhi, India, which aims to preserve and reconstruct the history of the Indian independence movement.

Concept note-3: -Pradhanmantri Sangrahalaya The Pradhan Mantri Sangrahalaya is a tribute to every Prime Minister of India since Independence, and a narrative record of how each one has contributed to the development of our nation over the last 75 years.

Concept note-4: -Former principal secretary to the Prime Minister, Nripendra Misra, has been appointed chairperson of the executive council of the Nehru Memorial Museum and Library (NMML). According to a government order, the Executive Council of the NMML has been reconstituted and it will be now chaired by Misra.

Concept note-5: -The Teen Murti Bhavan (Teen Murti House; formerly known as Flagstaff House) was built by British as the residence New Delhi of the Prime Minister of India, Jawaharlal Nehru. However, He stayed there for 16 years until his death on 27 May 1964.
