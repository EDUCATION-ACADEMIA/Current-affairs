Government has proposed major changes in how BBC is run and will be regulated by which external organization

(A) UN

(B) WHO

(C) Oxform

(D) ** Ofcom

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2.1 Ofcom became the first independent external regulator of the BBC under the current BBC Charter and Agreement, which began on 1 January 2017 and ends on 31 December 2027. The Charter and Agreement set out the different roles and duties of the BBC and Ofcom, including the principal functions of the BBC Board.
