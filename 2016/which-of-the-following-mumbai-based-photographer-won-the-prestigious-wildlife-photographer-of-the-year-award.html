Which of the following Mumbai-based photographer won the prestigious Wildlife Photographer of the Year Award

(A) Rathika Ramasamy

(B) Shekar Dattari

(C) Sudhir Shivaram

(D) ** Nayan Khanolkar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Aishwarya Sridhar became the first Indian to win the Wildlife Photographer of the Year award.

Concept note-2: -The Natural History Museum has announced the Wildlife Photographer of the Year winners for 2022. American photographer Karine Aigner won the grand title of the prestigious award for his photo, titled The Big Buzz, of a bunch of buzzing cactus bees spinning over hot sand in Texas.

Concept note-3: -TWC India. A stunning camera-trap snapshot by Sascha Fonseca, which captures a majestic snow leopard basking in the golden sunset hues against the breathtaking backdrop of Ladakh’s peaks, has won the Wildlife Photographer of the Year People’s Choice Award.

Concept note-4: -Meet Rathika Ramasamy, India’s First Woman Wildlife Photographer.

Concept note-5: -Sudhir Shivaram Sudhir Shivaram grew up in Karnataka, is one of India’s most respected and renowned wildlife photographers.
