Which of the following Committee has suggested a five-point agenda to bring at least 80 percent of farmers into the institutional credit net in Maharashtra

(A) Ramanujam committee

(B) Arvind Mayaram Committee

(C) T.K. Vishwanathan committee

(D) ** Kishore Tiwari committee

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Vaidyanathan Task Force was set in 2004 to revamp rural cooperative credit institutions and favoured revival of cooperative institutions offering short and long term credit facilities.

Concept note-2: -In order to achieve higher growth in productivity in agriculture, the NCF recommends: Substantial increase in public investment in agriculture related infrastructure particularly in irrigation, drainage, land development, water conservation, research development and road connectivity etc.

Concept note-3: -Sivaraman Committee: At the insistence of the Indian government, the Reserve Bank of India (RBI) established a committee to review the Institutional Credit Arrangement for Agriculture and Rural Development (CRAFICARD).

Concept note-4: -Sivaraman, also known as Sivaraman committee recommended the establishment of NABARD.
