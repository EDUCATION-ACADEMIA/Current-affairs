According to the Skytrax, which airline is the world’s best airline for 2016

(A) Singapore Airlines

(B) Cathay Pacific

(C) Qatar Airways

(D) ** Emirates

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Emirates has won about 20 Skytrax awards since 2001 and has also been ranked as the best airline in the world for 2016 at the Farnborough Airshow, 2016.

Concept note-2: -Farnborough, England (CNN)-The world’s best airline for 2016 is Emirates, according to a survey of millions of airline passengers conducted by Skytrax. The international airline, based in Dubai, won the top honors at Tuesday’s Skytrax World Airline Awards ceremony at the Farnborough International Airshow in England.

Concept note-3: -1 airline of 2022: Qatar Airways. Qatar Airways ranked at the top of Skytrax’s list.

Concept note-4: -World’s best airline: Dethroning Emirates, Qatar Airways was voted the airline of the year. Keep clicking through the gallery to find out which airlines won in other categories. World’s best cabin staff: For the fourth consecutive year, Garuda Indonesia won the world’s best cabin staff award.

Concept note-5: -Qatar Airways were named Airline of the Year in the 2015 Skytrax World Airline Awards at Paris Air Show. This is the third time in 5 years that the Doha based carrier has triumphed in the top award category.
