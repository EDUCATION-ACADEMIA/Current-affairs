The eighth BRICS summit 2016 was concluded in which city

(A) Durban

(B) ** Goa

(C) Ufa

(D) Fortaleza

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On October 15-16, in Goa, India, President Michel Temer will take part in the 8th Summit of the BRICS countries – the association formed by Brazil, Russia, India, China and South Africa.

Concept note-2: -The theme of India’s BRICS Chairmanship is ‘Building Responsive, Inclusive and Collective Solutions.

Concept note-3: -The 3rd Urbanisation Forum with the theme ‘Urban Transition in BRICS’ is being organised by the Ministry of Urban Development, Government of India and Ministry of External Affairs and hosted by Government of Andhra Pradesh in Visakhapatnam from14-16 September 2016.

Concept note-4: -It was hosted by Russia, under the theme Global Stability, Shared Security and Innovative Growth. India will be taking over the Chairship of the BRICS, which would be the third BRICS Presidency for India since its inception after 2012 and 2016.
