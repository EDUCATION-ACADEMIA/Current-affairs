Which of the following states government has planned to enact State Sports Council Act recently

(A) Maharashtra

(B) ** Haryana

(C) Gujarat

(D) Tamil Nadu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -All India Sports Council Of The Deaf is a non-profit organisation, established in 1965 that works primarily in the domain of Sports.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Kerala. The All India Sports Council of the Deaf has got approval from the International Committee of Sports for the Deaf (ICSD) to host the first World Deaf T20 Cricket Championship in Kerala. It will be held from January 10 to 20, 2023.

Concept note-3: -It was only in 1960 that the central government took up the task of sports promotion with earnestness, and an All India Council of Sports was set up with the late Maharaja Yadavindra Singh of Patiala as the first chairman.

Concept note-4: -Honorary General Secretary, AISCD, G Suresh Kumar said the first World Deaf T20 cricket championship will be held in Thiruvananthapuram, Kerala from January 10 to January 20, 2023.
