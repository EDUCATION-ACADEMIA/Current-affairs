Which of the following Indian city will host the men’s Hockey Junior World Cup 2016

(A) New Delhi

(B) ** Lucknow

(C) Kolkata

(D) Ranchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 2016 Men’s Hockey Junior World Cup was the 11th edition of the Hockey Junior World Cup for men, an international field hockey tournament. It was held in Lucknow, India from 8–18 December 2016. A total of sixteen teams competed for the title.

Concept note-2: -The FIH Men’s Hockey World Cup 2023 will be held in Bhubaneswar and Rourkela, two cities in India’s Odisha state, from January 13 to 29.

Concept note-3: -2023 Men’s FIH Hockey World Cup: Players involved, schedule and how to watch the live action. For the second consecutive edition, the Men’s FIH Hockey World Cup will take place in India, with 16 teams vying for glory in the cities of Bhubaneswar and Rourkela between 13 and 29 January.
