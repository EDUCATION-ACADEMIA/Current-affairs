Which Union Minister has inaugurated the world’s first White Tiger Safari on April 3, 2016

(A) ** Prakash Javdekar

(B) Jagat Prakash Nadda

(C) Smriti Zubin Irani

(D) Rajnath Singh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Madhya Pradesh Chief Minister Shivraj Singh Chouhan on Sunday inaugurated the world’s maiden ‘White Tiger Safari’ at Mukundpur in Satna district in the State’s Vindhya region where the feline was first discovered more than 100 years ago.

Concept note-2: -But now, the wildlife tourism in the state has reached a new high with the introduction of world’s first White Tiger Safari in Mukundpur Forest of Rewa.

Concept note-3: -The first white tiger safari was inaugurated in Madhya pradesh. White tigers were first found in the madhya pradesh in the year 1951 by Maharaja Martand Singh on whose name the safari is named.

Concept note-4: -White Tiger Safari | District Rewa, Government of Madhya Pradesh | India.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is Madhya Pradesh. The world’s first ‘White Tiger Safari’ has recently opened for the public at Mukundpur in Satna district, Madhya Pradesh. The first of its kind safari has cost Rs. 50 crore and is spread over an area of 25 hectares.
