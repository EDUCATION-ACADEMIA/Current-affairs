India has signed the new Air Services Agreement (ASA) with which country by Union Government

(A) Germany

(B) Denmark

(C) ** Laos

(D) Saudi Arabia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Context: India has signed a bilateral air service agreement with 116 countries including neighbouring Bangladesh, Afghanistan, and Pakistan along with the US, UK, UAE etc.

Concept note-2: -The 116 countries with which India has signed a pact for bilateral ASA, range from those in Asia and the Middle East to those in Europe and South America.

Concept note-3: -The agreement aims to allow an unlimited number of flights from India to Greece whereas Greece is allowed to make unlimited flights to 6 metro cities in India.

Concept note-4: -An air transport agreement (also sometimes called an air service agreement or ATA or ASA) is a bilateral agreement to allow international commercial air transport services between signatories.
