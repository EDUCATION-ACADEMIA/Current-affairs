Name the online tool, that can predict whether citizens will vote for Donald Trump or Hilary Clinton in the upcoming US Presidential elections has been developed.

(A) VoteEsy

(B) SelectPre

(C) QuickVote

(D) ** TweetCast

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Most States have a “winner-take-all” system that awards all electors to the Presidential candidate who wins the State’s popular vote.

Concept note-2: -They can choose who will form the government and take major decisions. They can choose the party whose policies will guide the government and law making.

Concept note-3: -By midday of election day, 6, 567, 859, constituting to 30% of registered voters had cast their ballots. An hour to closure of polling centers at 5 pm, the electoral commission reported a voter turnout of 12, 065, 803 equating to 56.17% of registered voters.

Concept note-4: -A candidate must receive 270 of the 538 electoral votes to become President or Vice President. If a candidate for President fails to receive 270 votes, the House itself will choose the President from among the three individuals who received the most electoral votes.
