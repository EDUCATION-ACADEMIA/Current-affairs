Where was the12th South Asian Games held

(A) Sri lanka

(B) ** India

(C) Nepal

(D) Bhutan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 1994 Asian Games (Japanese: 1994, Senkyūhyakukyūjūyon-nen Ajia kyōgi taikai), also known as the XII Asiad and the 12th Asian Games (Japanese: 12, romanized: Daijūni-kai Ajia kyōgi taikai), were held from October 2 to 16, 1994, in Hiroshima, Japan.

Concept note-2: -13th South Asian Games concluded in Nepal on Tuesday evening; Thousands of sports lovers witnessed gala closing ceremony held at historic Dasaratha Rangshala in Kathmandu.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The 12th South Asian Games or the 2016 South Asian Games were conducted in Shillong and Guwahati from 5-16 February 2016. India won a total of 308 medals, including 188 gold.
