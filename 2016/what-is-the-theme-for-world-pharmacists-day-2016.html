What is the theme for World Pharmacists Day 2016

(A) Safety first with medicines, ask your pharmacist

(B) ** Pharmacists: Caring for you

(C) Pharmacists - simplifying your medicines use, no matter how complex

(D) None of these

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The event was organized on 26th September, 2016 during 6.00 to 8.30 pm. The event included felicitation of the Pharmacists, Skit, Debate competition and Quiz competition. Pharmacists are an integral part of the health care society. “Pharmacists: Caring for you” is the theme of this year’s World Pharmacists Day.

Concept note-2: -In 2015, “Pharmacist(s): your partner(s) in health” was the theme of world pharmacist day.

Concept note-3: -“Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world” was the theme of World Pharmacists Day on 25 September 2022. The theme highlighted pharmacy’s positive impact on health around the world and aimed to further strengthen solidarity among the profession.

Concept note-4: -World Pharmacists Day 2022 Theme: According to the FIP, this year’s theme is ‘Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world’. The theme was chosen with an aim to “showcase pharmacy’s positive impact on health around the world and to further strengthen solidarity among the profession”, the federation said.

Concept note-5: -WORLD PHARMACY DAY 2022: THEME This year’s theme, “Pharmacy united in action for a healthier world, ” aims to promote unity and highlight the sound effects of pharmacy on health.
