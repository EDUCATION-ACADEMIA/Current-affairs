Which country has ban Pokemon Go over ’security concerns’ recently

(A) ** Iran

(B) Iraq

(C) Brazil

(D) Afghanistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Iran has become the first country to ban the Pokémon Go mobile game, citing security concerns about the game’s use of location-based virtual reality technology.

Concept note-2: -After the renewal of a 2001 fatwah (religious doctrine) banning Pokemon from the nation, Saudi Arabia has banned Pokemon GO play due to the game’s depiction of evolution and polytheism.

Concept note-3: -The short answer is NO. There is currently no ban on Pokemon Go in India.

Concept note-4: -China had banned Pokemon Go in the past, citing safety concerns. The official alleged that players would cause accidents by concentrating on their phones. It also alleged that access to maps might also expose other users to safety risks.

Concept note-5: -Pokemon Go has been popular in Hong Kong but is banned on the mainland.
