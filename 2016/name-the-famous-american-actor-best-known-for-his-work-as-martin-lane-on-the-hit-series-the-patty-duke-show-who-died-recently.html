Name the famous American actor best known for his work as Martin Lane on the hit series The Patty Duke Show who died recently

(A) Jonathan Brandis

(B) Chris Farley

(C) Louis de Funes

(D) ** William Schallert

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Schallert began his film career in the late ‘40s, but it was his portrayal of Martin Lane, the father and uncle to the identical cousins on The Patty Duke Show (1963-66), that became his signature role.

Concept note-2: -Yeah, we’re well aware that William Schallert doesn’t have much in common with classic rock. But if you’re of a certain age, you invited him into your home each week in his role as Patty Duke’s father on the ‘60s sitcom The Patty Duke Show. The 93-year-old actor died on Sunday, May 8, 2016, in Pacific Palisades, CA.

Concept note-3: -He is known for his roles on Richard Diamond, Private Detective (1957–1959), Death Valley Days (1955–1962), and The Patty Duke Show (1963–1966).

Concept note-4: -Teenager Patty Lane and her worldly identical cousin Cathy navigate family and high school life. Teenager Patty Lane and her worldly identical cousin Cathy navigate family and high school life.

Concept note-5: -Patty’s body double went on to be a soap star. When you see the back of one cousin’s head in a conversation, it is likely Rita Walker (née McLaughlin). From 1970 to 1981, the actress would get to show her face-and skills-as Carol Deming on As the World Turns.
