Name the football club that on 2 May 2016 was crowned champions of English Premier League

(A) ** Jamie Vardy

(B) Kasper Schmeichel

(C) Danny Drinkwater

(D) Danny Simpson

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In what was described as one of the greatest sporting stories of all time, Leicester were confirmed as champions of the 2015–16 Premier League season on 2 May 2016, finishing top of England’s highest league for the first time in the club’s history.

Concept note-2: -(Premier League was earlier sponsored by Barclays Bank, thus the reason for its previous official name Barclays Premier League.)

Concept note-3: -Chelsea won their fifth Premier League title, and sixth English title, with two matches to spare following a 1–0 away win over West Bromwich Albion on 12 May.

Concept note-4: -Premier League best finish: Winners in 2004/5, 2005/06, 2009/10, 2011/12 & 2016/17.

Concept note-5: -Since the League began in 1992, there have been seven different winners: Manchester United, Arsenal, Chelsea, Manchester City, Blackburn Rovers, Leicester City and Liverpool.
