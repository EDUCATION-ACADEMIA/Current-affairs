Andhra Pradesh Chief Minister has inaugurated the interim Secretariat complex in which city

(A) Vijayawada

(B) Visakhapatnam

(C) ** Amaravati

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -As of October 2021, 15, 004 Village/Ward Secretariats had been established with 2, 54, 832 volunteers to served around 3.2 crore people, including home delivery of pensions and monthly provisions.

Concept note-2: -Tanguturi Prakasam Pantulu (also known as Andhra Kesari – “The Lion of Andhra") became the first Chief Minister of the new state.

Concept note-3: -As per the A.P. Reorganisation Act, 2014, the capital has been notified by the Government of Andhra Pradesh in 2015, says Minister of State for Home Affairs Nityanand Rai. The Union Ministry of Home Affairs has made it clear that as on date, Amaravati is the capital of Andhra Pradesh.

Concept note-4: -Andhra Pradesh Secretariat is the administrative office of the employees of the state of Andhra Pradesh, located in Velagapudi.
