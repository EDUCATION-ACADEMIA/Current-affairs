State Bank MobiCash Mobile Wallet was launched. State Bank of India (SBI) MobiCash was collaborated in with which telecom operator

(A) ** Bharat Sanchar Nigam Ltd

(B) Airtel

(C) Aircel

(D) Vodafone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -SBI in collaboration with BSNL has launched its new app – ‘mobicash’ – which is featured with three-way access to transfer fund from your mobile phone.

Concept note-2: -SBI launched mobile banking service ‘State Bank Freedom’ in 2009 and has around 54 lakh users as of now with about 10, 000 users adding per day. Following the success of its first product in this space, the bank has now launched ‘Mobicash Easy’.

Concept note-3: -The State Bank MobiCash Mobile Wallet application is menu driven and user friendly. The Mobile Wallet application can be downloaded from the link received via SMS at the time of registration, if GPRS connectivity is available on the handset. It may also be downloaded from the M/s.

Concept note-4: -1.3 “Mobile Wallet” shall mean a mobile based prepaid wallet registered with the Bank (State Bank MobiCash and State Bank MobiCash Easy).

Concept note-5: -Oxigen wallet is the first-ever e-wallet in India, launched in July 2004. In 2006, the media firm Times Group, in association with YES Bank, introduced Wallet365.com. But, the use of digital wallets picked up pace after the launch of Mobikwik in 2009.
