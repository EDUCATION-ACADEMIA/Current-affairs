Which of the following solar company has the first indian company to trade on New York Stock Exchange (NYSE)

(A) Elecssol

(B) Vikram Solar

(C) Tata Power Solar

(D) ** Azure Power

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is ReNew Power Pvt Ltd.

Concept note-2: -Infosys was the first Indian company to be listed on the NASDAQ, an American Stock Exchange.

Concept note-3: -Infosys Technologies is the first Indian company to be added to the NASDAQ-100 index and is the only Indian company to be part of any of the major global indices.
