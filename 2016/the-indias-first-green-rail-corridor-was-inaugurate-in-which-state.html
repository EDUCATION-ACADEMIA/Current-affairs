The India’s first Green Rail Corridor was inaugurate in which state

(A) ** Tamil Nadu

(B) Uttar Pradesh

(C) Karnataka

(D) Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Tamil Nadu. Indian Railways launched the country’s first green rail corridor between Rameswaram-Manamadurai on 24 July 2016 in Tamil Nadu.

Concept note-2: -3.
