Whose birthday celebrated as the National Milk Day in India

(A) Norman Borlaug

(B) MS Swaminathan

(C) ** Dr.Verghese Kurien

(D) DS Rajesh Varma

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Verghese Kurien ’s birthday is commemorated on November 26th with the National Milk Day. He is credited with bringing India’s “white revolution.” The white revolution was started by Dr. Verghese Kurien, who also made India’s largest self-sustaining industry the dairy farm.

Concept note-2: -The day was started by the Indian Diary Association (IDA). The story lies in the year 1970 when the National Diary Development Board (NDDB) began a rural development program called Operation Flood. This program was considered one of the largest and aimed to develop a milk grid throughout the country.

Concept note-3: -Department of Animal Husbandry is celebrating National Milk Day on 26th November 2022. National Gopal Ratna Awards 2022 are conferred as part of the celebrations. Animal Quarantine Certification Services are also to be inaugurated.

Concept note-4: -Kurien was given the title ‘The Milkman of India’ because he created milk powder using buffalo milk when the world was still using milk. For his contribution to the dairy industry in India, he was awarded the Ramon Magsaysay Award, Padma Shri, Padma Bhushan, and Padma Vibhushan.
