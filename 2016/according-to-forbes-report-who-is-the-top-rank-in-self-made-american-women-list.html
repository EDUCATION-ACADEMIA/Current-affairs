According to Forbes report Who is the top rank in self-made American women list

(A) ** Diane Hendricks

(B) Neerja Sethi

(C) Meg Whitman

(D) Marissa Mayer

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to Forbes, Jayshree V Ullal at number 15 is one of America’s richest self-made women with her current net worth of around $1.9 billion.

Concept note-2: -Forbes India has released the India Rich List for this year. While Adani Group’s chairman Gautam Adani has topped Forbes India’s 100 richest list in 2022, Jindal Group Chairperson, Savitri Jindal was listed as the richest woman in India in the Forbes Billionaire’s list 2022.

Concept note-3: -Diane Hendricks was the wealthiest female self-made billionaire in the United States as of June 2022. The founder of ABC Supply was worth eight billion U.S. dollars. The second richest self-made woman in the US was Judy Faulkner, reaching a net worth of 6.7 billion U.S. dollars that year.

Concept note-4: -Francoise Bettencourt Meyers, cosmetics giant L’Oréal’s founder’s granddaughter, was listed as the richest woman in the world this year – with a net worth of $74.8 billion, according to the report.
