According to the National Sample Survey Office survey, Which state is the least rank in cleanest state in India

(A) ** Jharkhand

(B) Assam

(C) Tamil Nadu

(D) Bihar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Sikkim. Sikkim has been declared as the cleanest state while Jharkhand comes last in the list. The results of the survey carried out last year by the National Sample Survey Office (NSSO) was released by Union Rural Development Minister Narendra Singh Tomar.

Concept note-2: -Maharashtra Is India’s Third Cleanest State, Madhya Pradesh And Chhattisgarh Bag First Two Spots. Chhattisgarh secured the 2nd position, Maharashtra took the 3rd spot.

Concept note-3: -1. Indore, Madhya Pradesh. Thanks to planned waste segregation, conversion and disposal, Indore is the cleanest city in India 2022.

Concept note-4: -Madhya Pradesh has secured the first position, followed by Chhattisgarh and Maharashtra in the category of best performing states in ‘Swachh Survekshan Awards 2022’.
