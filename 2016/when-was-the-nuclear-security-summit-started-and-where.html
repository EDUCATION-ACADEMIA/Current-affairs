When was the Nuclear Security Summit started and where

(A) 2012, Seoul

(B) ** 2010, Washington

(C) 2010, Seoul

(D) 2008, The Hague

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first Nuclear Security Summit was held in Washington, D.C. from April 12-13, 2010. Forty-seven national delegations as well as the heads of the United Nations, the International Atomic Energy Agency (IAEA ) and the European Union attended.

Concept note-2: -The first Nuclear Security Summit was held in 2010 at Washington DC.

Concept note-3: -The Hague in the Netherlands provided the setting on March 24th and 25th for the third Nuclear Security Summit (NSS).

Concept note-4: -India’s endorsement of this initiative demonstrates New Delhi’s support for the IAEA’s recommended “ fundamental principles” of nuclear security and its commitment to “meet the intent” of the IAEA’s nuclear security recommendations.
