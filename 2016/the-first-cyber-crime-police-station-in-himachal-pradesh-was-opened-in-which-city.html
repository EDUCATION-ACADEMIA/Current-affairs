The first Cyber Crime Police Station in Himachal Pradesh was opened in which city

(A) Dharamsala

(B) Palampur

(C) Sundernagar

(D) ** Shimla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A centralised cyber station has already been sanctioned in Shimla, which handles cases from across the state.

Concept note-2: -India’s first police station for cybercrimes inaugurated at Bengaluru in Karnataka. India’s first police station for cybercrimes inaugurated in the year 2001. The station for cybercrimes will register cybercrime cases under the information technology act, 2000, with jurisdiction all over the state.

Concept note-3: -In 1992, the first cyber crime appeared wherein the first polymorphic virus was released. In India, one of the very first cases of cyber crime was that of Yahoo v. Akash Arora. This case occurredin 1999.

Concept note-4: -1988. The first major cyber attack on the internet came courtesy of Cornell grad student Robert Morris. The “Morris Worm” struck in the year before the World Wide Web debuted, back when the internet was primarily the domain of academic researchers.
