Which of the following company has became India’s first firm to get debt recast under RBI’s new Scheme for Sustainable Structuring of Stressed Assets (S4A)

(A) Reliance Construction Company

(B) ** Hindustan Construction Company

(C) Birla Construction Company

(D) ATI Construction Company

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -HCC was the first company to receive RBI nod for debt reconstruction under the S4A scheme.

Concept note-2: -Restructuring is an act in which a lender, for economic or legal reasons relating to the borrower’s financial difficulty, grants concessions to the borrower.

Concept note-3: -Stressed assets are a sum of Non-performing assets, restructured loans, and written-off assets. During the period of 90 days before becoming non-performing assets, these are called ‘stressed assets.

Concept note-4: -The evergreening of loans is a well-known exercise, in which banks revive a loan on the verge of default by granting further loans to the same firm.
