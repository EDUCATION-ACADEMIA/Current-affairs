Who was honoured with the ’Audrey Hepburn Humanitarian Award’ for her work as UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador

(A) Mia Farrow

(B) Mahmoud Kabil

(C) Zahra Al-Harazi

(D) ** Katy Perry

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The United States Fund for UNICEF presented Katy with the Audrey Hepburn Humanitarian Award at the annual UNICEF Snowflake Ball to honour her extraordinary commitment to children. Katy joined other UNICEF Goodwill Ambassadors and global influencers in contributing to the “World Version” of John Lennon’s Imagine.

Concept note-2: -UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador Priyanka Chopra is one of India’s most popular actresses with global following.

Concept note-3: -LUCKNOW/UTTAR PRADESH, India, 9 November 2022-UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador Priyanka Chopra Jonas travelled to Uttar Pradesh in northern India this week to visit UNICEF programmes helping to improve children’s education, and access to health, nutrition, and protection.

Concept note-4: -UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador since 2005 Amitabh Bachchan is one of India’s most prolific actors, bursting onto the Bollywood silver screen in the 1970s and going on to star in more than 190 films.

Concept note-5: -UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador from 1988 to 1993 Internationally renowned actress Audrey Hepburn was a tireless advocate for children’s rights. Soon after becoming a UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador in 1988, she went on a mission to Ethiopia, where years of drought and civil strife had caused terrible famine.
