What is the amount allocated for the Pradhan Mantri Kaushal Vikas Yojana

(A) 18000 crore

(B) 6000 crore

(C) ** 12000 crore

(D) 15000 crore

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The PMKVY scheme will be implemented by the ministry of skill development. Of the total Rs 3, 517.31 crore, the majority-Rs 2, 278.37 crore-has been allocated to the Skill India program.

Concept note-2: -The average PMKVY monthly salary ranges from approximately ₹ 11, 927 per month for Trainer to ₹ 80, 000 per month for Chemical Engineer. The average PMKVY salary ranges from approximately ₹ 1, 20, 000 per year for New Graduate to ₹ 15, 00, 000 per year for Trainer.

Concept note-3: -In PMKVY scheme, student gets a reward of Rs. 5, 000 – 10, 000 based on the course selected. This reward amount can be used to pay the course fee to the Training Partner.

Concept note-4: -Applicants should be between 15 and 45 years of age.

Concept note-5: -Key Components of the Scheme Duration of the training varies according tothe job role, however, majority of courses range between 200-600 hrs (2 – 6 months).
