What is theme of World Heart Day 2016

(A) Creating heart-healthy environments

(B) Take the road to a healthy heart

(C) ** Light Your Heart, Empower Your Life.

(D) One World, One Heart, One Home

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The campaign theme for World Heart Day 2016 is: Power Your Life. Your heart is the body’s engine room and powers your whole body – that’s why it is so important to look after it.

Concept note-2: -USE HEART FOR EVERY HEART Use Heart means to think differently.

Concept note-3: -The 2023 Summit will be a platform for high-level discussions on the prevention, treatment and management of cardiovascular disease worldwide, centred around the universal theme of cardiovascular health for everyone.

Concept note-4: -Created by the World Heart Federation, World Heart Day informs people around the globe that CVD, including heart disease and stroke, is the world’s leading cause of death claiming 18.6 million lives each year, and highlights the actions that individuals can take to prevent and control CVD.

Concept note-5: -The first World Heart Day took place back in 2000. Since then, in 2012, leaders from around the globe committed to the reduction of worldwide mortality from non-communicable diseases by 25 percent by 2025. Did you know that almost half of the NCD deaths happen because of cardiovascular disease?
