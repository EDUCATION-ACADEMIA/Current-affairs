Who has been appointed as the Executive Director (ED) of state-owned Indian Overseas Bank

(A) Annie George Mathew

(B) Nirmal Chand

(C) ** R Subramania Kumar

(D) Chinnaiah

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -S Srimathy. Ms. S Srimathy, has assumed charge as Executive Director of Indian Overseas Bank on 10.03. 2021 from Canara Bank where she was Chief General Manager.

Concept note-2: -Ajay Kumar Srivastava has been elevated as Managing Director and CEO of Indian Overseas Bank with effect from January 1, 2023 from his current posting as executive director.
