Which state became the first state in the country to have a cyber-police station in each district to take on cyber criminals.

(A) Karnataka

(B) New Delhi

(C) Bihar

(D) ** Maharashtra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Detailed Solution. Maharashtra state is developing 42 state-of-the-art cybercrime labs into cyber police stations.

Concept note-2: -Maharastra is the first state in the country to have a cyber police station in all districts.

Concept note-3: -In 1992, the first cyber crime appeared wherein the first polymorphic virus was released. In India, one of the very first cases of cyber crime was that of Yahoo v. Akash Arora. This case occurredin 1999.

Concept note-4: -Karnataka was the first to establish a dedicated police station to handle digital crime 15 years ago. Other states, including Uttar Pradesh and Maharashtra, have stepped up police training, including seeking out experts from industry.
