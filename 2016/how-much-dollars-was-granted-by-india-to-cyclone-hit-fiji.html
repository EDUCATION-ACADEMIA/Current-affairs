(B) Two million Dollars

(C) Four milion dollars

(D) Five million Dollars

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Irene: January 18-19, 2023. The cyclone did not make direct landfall. On January 20th, 2023 at 12:00 am it had the shortest distance at about 679 km southeast of Suva in Central.

Concept note-2: -As one of the strongest storms ever recorded in the Southern Hemisphere, tropical cyclone (TC) Winston and its 185-mph winds caused widespread devastation across the island nation of Fiji when it made landfall February 19-20.

Concept note-3: -The accumulated rainfall from all three tropical cyclones caused extensive flooding across all four divisions and left urgent needs in shelter, health, WASH, and livelihoods; specifically, access to clean water and the risk of leptospirosis, typhoid, dengue, and diarrhoea (LTDD).
