What is the name of App that is launched by Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) for measuring real time mobile Internet speed that consumers get

(A) Speedtest

(B) ** Myspeed

(C) Speednet

(D) Mobilspeed

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This application allows you to measure your data speed experience and sends the results to TRAI. The application captures and sends coverage, data speed and other network information along with device and location of the tests.

Concept note-2: -Telecom Regulatory Authority of India (TRAI) has launched new mobile apps such as TRAI MyCall App, TRAI MySpeed App, Do Not Disturb (DND 2.0) & new updated TRAI website. The TRAI MyCall is an Android application for Crowd sourced Voice Call Quality Monitoring.

Concept note-3: -| Telecom Regulatory Authority of India. |

Concept note-4: -Telecom Regulatory Authority of India(TRAI) Act, 1997.

Concept note-5: -Telecom Regulatory Authority of India was established on 20 February 1997 by an Act of Parliament to regulate telecom services and tariffs in India.
