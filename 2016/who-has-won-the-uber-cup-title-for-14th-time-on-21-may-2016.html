Who has won the Uber Cup title for 14th time on 21 May 2016

(A) South Korea

(B) ** China

(C) Germany

(D) Hong Kong

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Denmark won the Thomas Cup of 2016. Denmark defeated Indonesia in the final round of Thomas Cup. China was the host country. Denmark became the first European nation to win the Thomas Cup.

Concept note-2: -The Uber Cup final featured the top two teams, China and Japan. Backed by wins from the world’s top two singles players, China came out on top by a 3–1 score. It was their 13th title overall.

Concept note-3: -Uber Cup, trophy representing the women’s world championship in the sport of badminton. The cup was contributed by Mrs. H.S. Uber, former English champion, in 1956 for a series of women’s international team competitions to be held every three years.

Concept note-4: -Thomas Cup, trophy signifying world supremacy in the sport of badminton. The cup was donated in 1939 by Sir George Thomas for a series of men’s international team competitions to be managed by the International Badminton Federation (IBF), of which Thomas was then president.
