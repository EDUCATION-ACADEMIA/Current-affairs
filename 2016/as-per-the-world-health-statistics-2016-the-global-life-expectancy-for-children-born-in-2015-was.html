As per the World Health Statistics 2016, the global life expectancy for children born in 2015 was

(A) 51.4 years

(B) 61.4 years

(C) ** 71.4 years

(D) 81.4 years

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The latest World Health Statistics 2016: Monitoring Health for the SDGs, finds that global life expectancy for children born in 2015 was 71.4 years (73.8 years for females and 69.1 years for males), but an individual childâ€™s outlook depends on where he or she is born.

Concept note-2: -Results-In 2015, the overall expectation of life at birth was 78.7 years, decreasing by 0.2 year from 2014. From 2014 to 2015, life expectancy at birth decreased by 0.2 year for both males (76.5 to 76.3) and females (81.3 to 81.1).

Concept note-3: -For the same period, the life expectancy for females is 70 years and 66.9 years for males.

Concept note-4: -In 2015-20, life expectancy at birth averaged 78.3 years for men and 83.4 years for women.
