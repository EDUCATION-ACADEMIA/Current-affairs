Which team has won the 2016 Asian Nations cup chess Tournament

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) England

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Team is the Winner of the Asian Nations Cup 2016! – Chessdom.

Concept note-2: -However, India B edged ahead due to better tie-breaks and won the Gold.

Concept note-3: -The FIDE World Senior Chess Championship takes place November 15-26, 2022, in Assisi, Italy. The format is an 11-round Swiss. The time control is 90 minutes for the first 40 moves, followed by 15 minutes for the rest of the game, plus a 30-second increment starting on move one.

Concept note-4: -The Asian Chess Championship is a chess tournament open to all players from Asian chess federations (FIDE zones from 3.1 to 3.7). It’s held with the Swiss system and consists in two divisions, Open and Women’s, the latter of which is reserved to female players.

Concept note-5: -Format-Championship is designed to make participants compete not only against each other but also against the clock.-Back-to-back games for 2 hours; Participants will be continuously paired with best match opponents game-after-game for 2 hours, each game will have a maximum time limit of 10 minutes.
