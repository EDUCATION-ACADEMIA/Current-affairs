Under National Rurban Mission, how many Smart villages can be developed over the next 3 years across the country

(A) 400

(B) 250

(C) 150

(D) ** 300

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -A Rurban cluster, would be a cluster of geographically contiguous villages with a population of about 25, 000 to 50, 000 in plain and coastal areas and with a population of 5000 to 15000 in desert, hilly or tribal areas.

Concept note-2: -Shyama Prasad Mukherjee Rurban Mission which is wide known as National Rurban Mission is Initiated on September 2015 by Ministry of Rural Development, India.

Concept note-3: -Parson in 1949 exposed the idea of “rurbanisation” in his book – Essays in Sociological Theory. According to him, Rurban communities are the rural socio-geographic spaces where styles of life and the standard of living have changed so much that they resemble those in urban localities (Parsons, 1949, p. 435).
