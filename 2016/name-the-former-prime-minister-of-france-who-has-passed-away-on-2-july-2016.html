Name the Former Prime Minister of France, who has passed away on 2 July 2016

(A) Pierre Mauroy

(B) Raymond Barre

(C) Maurice Couve de Murville

(D) ** Michel Rocard

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -François Marie Adrien Maurice Mitterrand (26 October 1916 – 8 January 1996) was President of France, serving in that position from 1981 to 1995, the longest time in office in the history of France.

Concept note-2: -The head of the government of France has been called the prime minister of France (French: Premier ministre) since 1959, when Michel Debré became the first officeholder appointed under the Fifth Republic.

Concept note-3: -Fifth Republic records The only person to serve as prime minister more than once under the Fifth Republic was Jacques Chirac (1974–1976 and 1986–1988).

Concept note-4: -André Pierre Gabriel Amédée Tardieu (French: [ɑ̃dʁe taʁdjø]; 22 September 1876 – 15 September 1945) was three times Prime Minister of France (3 November 1929 – 17 February 1930; 2 March – 4 December 1930; 20 February – 10 May 1932) and a dominant figure of French political life in 1929–1932.
