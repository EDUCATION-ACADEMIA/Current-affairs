Who won the Men’s Doubles Title at 2016 Wimbledon Championships

(A) J. Benneteau and E. Roger-Vasselin

(B) ** Pierre-Hugues Herbert and Nicolas Mahut

(C) T. Huey and M. Mirnyi

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jean-Julien Rojer and Horia Tecău were the defending champions, but lost in the first round to Jonathan Marray and Adil Shamasdin. Pierre-Hugues Herbert and Nicolas Mahut won the title, defeating Julien Benneteau and Édouard Roger-Vasselin in the final, 6–4, 7–6, 6–3.

Concept note-2: -Henri Kontinen and Heather Watson defeated Robert Farah and Anna-Lena Grönefeld in the final, 7–6, 6–4 to win the mixed doubles tennis title at the 2016 Wimbledon Championships.

Concept note-3: -Andy Murray defeated Milos Raonic in the final, 6–4, 7–6, 7–6, to win the gentlemen’s singles tennis title at the 2016 Wimbledon Championships. It was his second Wimbledon title and third major title overall. Novak Djokovic was the two-time defending champion, but lost in the third round to Sam Querrey.

Concept note-4: -Matthew Ebden and Max Purcell defeated the defending champions Nikola Mektić and Mate Pavić in the final, 7–6, 6–7, 4–6, 6–4, 7–6 to win the gentlemen’s doubles tennis title at the 2022 Wimbledon Championships.

Concept note-5: -Defending champion Serena Williams defeated Angelique Kerber in the final, 7–5, 6–3 to win the ladies’ singles tennis title at the 2016 Wimbledon Championships. It was her seventh Wimbledon singles title and 22nd major singles title overall, equaling Steffi Graf’s Open Era record.
