Which of the following Women’s hockey team won the first Asian Champions Trophy 2016

(A) Malaysia

(B) Bangladesh

(C) ** India

(D) China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India won the Women’s Hockey Asian Champions Trophy 2016 which was held in Singapore.

Concept note-2: -India women’s hockey team won the Asian Champions Trophy after beating China 2-1 in the final. India registered a win over China in the 4th Asian Champions Trophy hockey tournament in Singapore. The Women’s Asian Champions Trophy 2016 took place in Singapore from 29 October to 5 November 2016.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is India. The 2016 Asian Hockey Champions Trophy tournament held in Kuantan, Malaysia. India stormed into the final of the fourth Asian Champions Trophy Hockey tournament, beating South Korea.

Concept note-4: -The tournament has been won by three different teams. South Korea are the defending champions and have to most titles with three. Japan has two titles and India has won the tournament once. The tournament was expanded to six teams in 2021.
