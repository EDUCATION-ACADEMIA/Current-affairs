(A) Free voice calls

(B) Zero roaming charges

(C) Complementary Jio-Apps bouquet

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -JioSecurity – security app. JioTV – TV Channels streaming service. JioVoice – VoLTE phone simulator. MyJio – manage Jio account and digital services associated with it.

Concept note-2: -The company commercially launched its services on 5 September 2016. Within the first month, Jio announced that it had successfully acquired 16 million subscribers. This is the fastest ramp-up by any mobile network operator anywhere in the world.
