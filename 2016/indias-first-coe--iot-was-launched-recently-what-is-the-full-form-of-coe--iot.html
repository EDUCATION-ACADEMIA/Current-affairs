India’s first CoE- IoT was launched recently. What is the full form of CoE- IoT

(A) ** Centre of Excellence on Internet of Things

(B) Control of Excellence on Internet of Things

(C) Center of Excellence on Internet of Themes

(D) Centre of Experience on Internet of Things

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -CENTER OF EXCELLENCE. FOR. IOT & AI IN INDIA. The Center of Excellence is the largest deep tech innovation ecosystem in India comprising of startups, innovators, enterprises and the government.

Concept note-2: -The Center of Excellence, Gandhinagar is the largest deep tech innovation ecosystem in India comprising of manufacturing enterprises, startups, government, research and academia. The core objective of CoE is to accelerate industry 4.0 adoption to solve the real world manufacturing challenges.

Concept note-3: -today launched India’s first Centre of Excellence-Internet of Things (CoE-IoT) at the NASSCOM Startups Warehouse in Bengaluru.
