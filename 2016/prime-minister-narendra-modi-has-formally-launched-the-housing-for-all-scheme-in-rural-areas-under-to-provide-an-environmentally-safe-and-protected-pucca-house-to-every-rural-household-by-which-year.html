Prime Minister Narendra Modi has formally launched the Housing for All scheme in rural areas under to provide an environmentally safe and protected pucca house to every rural household by which year

(A) 2019

(B) 2020

(C) ** 2022

(D) 2025

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) is a government initiative that works towards providing ‘Housing for All by 2024’. PMAY is divided into two components-PMAY Gramin or PMAY rural and PMAY urban. In this article, we will know everything about PMAY Gramin or PMAY rural including knowing about the PMAY rural scheme.

Concept note-2: -PMAY-G – A door to Dignity PMAY-G aims to provide a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless families and families living in kutcha and dilapidated house in rural areas by 2024.

Concept note-3: -Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana (PMAY) was launched with the aim to provide housing at an affordable price to the weaker sections of the society, lower income group people, urban poor, and rural poor. The Yojana involves a construction of around 20 million houses at an affordable price.

Concept note-4: -INTRODUCTION. The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana-Gramin (PMAY-G) has been devised in line with Government’s commitment to provide ‘Housing for All’ by 2022 in the rural areas. The scheme aims at providing a pucca house with basic amenities to all houseless householder living in kutcha and dilapidated houses by 2022.
