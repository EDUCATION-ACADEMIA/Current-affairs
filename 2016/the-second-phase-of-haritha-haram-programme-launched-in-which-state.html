The second phase of Haritha Haram programme launched in which state

(A) ** Telangana

(B) Andhra Pradesh

(C) Odisha

(D) Haryana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chandrshekar Rao on 8th July launched the second phase of Haritha Haram a flagship programme of the Telangana State Government at Gundrampalli Village in Nalgonda, district.

Concept note-2: -In 2020, the Chief Minister of Telangana K Chandrashekhar Rao has launched the sixth phase of the Haritha Haram Programme. In the sixth phase, Target is to plant 30 crore saplings across the state.

Concept note-3: -The program was launched by Telangana Chief Minister Kalvakuntla Chandrashekar Rao on 3 July 2015. It is one of the Telangana Flagship programmes to rejuvenate degraded forests, protecting these forests from threats such as smuggling, encroachment, fire and grazing.

Concept note-4: -Telangana Ku Haritha Haaram is a flagship programme of the Telangana Government to increase the present 24% tree cover in the State to 33% of the total geographical area of the State.

Concept note-5: -Some slogans of the program are:-Save trees, freshen up the environment.-Plant a tree, plant a life.-Plant a tree, Save nature.
