Indian Army Signed MoU for which Bank

(A) ** Induslnd Bank

(B) ICICI Bank

(C) Axis Bank

(D) HDFC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Army has signed historic MoU with 11 Banks viz State Bank of India, Punjab National Bank, Bank of Baroda, IDBI Bank, ICICI Bank, HDFC Bank, Axis Bank, Yes Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, IDFC First Bank and Bandhan Bank for providing banking facilities to Agniveers on enrollment.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Dhanlaxmi Bank.

Concept note-3: -IDFC FIRST Bank announced the signing of a Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) with the Indian Navy to offer Honour FIRST, a premium banking solution, to serving personnel and veterans of the Indian Navy.

Concept note-4: -HDFC Bank Ltd., signed an MoU with the Indian Navy to offer a salary account to Navy personnel.

Concept note-5: -The MoU was signed by Lt. Gen. Ravin Khosla (AVSM, SM, VSM), DG (MP & PS), Indian Army, and Chandra Shekhar Ghosh, Managing Director & CEO, Bandhan Bank, in New Delhi.
