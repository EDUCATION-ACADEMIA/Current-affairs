Mhow railway station to be renamed after _________ by Central Government

(A) ** Ambedkar Railway Station

(B) Lal Bahadur Shastri Railway Station

(C) Vallabhbhai Patel Railway Station

(D) Periyar E. V. Ramasamy Railway Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Dr. Ambedkar Nagar railway station-Wikipedia.

Concept note-2: -The town was renamed as Dr. Ambedkar Nagar in 2003, by the Government of Madhya Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Patalpani railway station in Indore to be renamed as Tantya Mama railway station. Decision comes following a move by Prime Minister Narendra Modi, who had earlier inaugurated the Rani Kamlapati Station which was earlier called Habibganj Railway Station.

Concept note-4: -"Centre nod for renaming of Mhow railway station after Ambedkar". Business Standard. Retrieved 31 October 2020. ↑ “Indore: Mhow railway station now Ambdekar station".
