Which of the following company won the prestigious 2016 Golden Peacock Award for Corporate Governance.

(A) Maruti Suzuki India Limited

(B) Infosys

(C) ** Steel Authority of India Limited

(D) GAIL

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The prestigious 2016 Golden Peacock Award for the ‘Excellence in Corporate Governance’ in the rubber sector is won by Goodyear India. The Golden Peacock awards, which were instituted in India by the Institute Of Directors in the year 1991, are considered as the Holy Grail of Corporate Excellence worldwide.

Concept note-2: -Delighted to share the good news that Bharti Airtel has been declared as the Winner of’Golden Peacock Award for Excellence in Corporate Governance’ for the year 2016.

Concept note-3: -Larsen & Toubro Ltd. Golden Peacock Awards, instituted by the Institute of Directors (IOD), India in 1991, are now regarded as a benchmark of Corporate Excellence worldwide.

Concept note-4: -Golden Peacock Awards, instituted by Institute of Directors, India, is regarded as an industry benchmark of Corporate Excellence worldwide to celebrate and honour the best governed companies, as recognition of their unique achievements to build their Quality and Brand.

Concept note-5: -SBI Life wins Golden Peacock National Quality Award-2015.
