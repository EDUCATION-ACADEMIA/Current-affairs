Majuli becomes India’s first island district.Majuli located in which state

(A) ** Assam

(B) Gujarat

(C) Madhya Pradesh

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Majuli district is the largest river island in the world, situated on the Brahmaputra River in Northeastern Assam. It is also the first island district of the country.

Concept note-2: -Assam is famous for “Majuli”, the world’s largest river island and “Umananda”, the smallest inhabited river island in the world.

Concept note-3: -The island situates itself is in the state of Assam, mid-stream of the Great male river Brahmaputra river, which is also one of the largest rivers in the world.

Concept note-4: -Majuli is the biggest river island in the world which is located on the Brahmaputra River in the state of Assam. This island is formed by the River Subansiri in the north and River Brahmaputra in the south.

Concept note-5: -Majuli is a district curved out of Jorhat district via govt notification no GAG (B) 27/2005/Pt.NI/59 in the north east province of Indian Sub-Continent.
