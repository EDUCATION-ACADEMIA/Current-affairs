Which country has successfully tested Akash missile System

(A) ** India

(B) China

(C) US

(D) Russia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India on Monday successfully test-fired the new-generation Akash missile from an integrated test range off the Odisha coast, an official statement said.

Concept note-2: -The Akash missile system can target aircraft up to 50 km (31 mi) away. It has the capability to neutralise aerial targets like fighter jets, cruise missiles and air-to-surface missiles as well as ballistic missiles. It is in operational service with the Indian Army and the Indian Air Force.

Concept note-3: -India test-fires surface-to-air missile Akash India today test-fired the indigenously developed surface-to-air Akash missile from the Integrated Test Range launch complex at Chandipur.

Concept note-4: -On April 27, 2022, akash prime, the advance version of akash missile, was successfully test-fired at pokhran field firing range in the border district of jaisalmer.

Concept note-5: -Earlier this year, on January 25, the DRDO conducted the successful maiden launch of Akash-NG or New Generation Missile from ITR.
