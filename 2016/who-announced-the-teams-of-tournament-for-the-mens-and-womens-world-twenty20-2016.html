Who announced the teams of tournament for the men’s and women’s World Twenty20 2016

(A) ** International Cricket Council

(B) Board of Control for Cricket in India

(C) England and Wales Cricket Board

(D) None of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In the final, played at Eden Gardens, Kolkata, the West Indies defeated England by four wickets. Indian batsman Virat Kohli was named the player of the tournament, while Bangladesh’s Tamim Iqbal and Afghanistan’s Mohammad Nabi led the tournament in runs and wickets, respectively.

Concept note-2: -INDIA. Ekta Bisht, Rajeshwari Gayakwad, Jhulan Goswami, Thirush Kamini (wk), Harmanpreet Kaur, Veda Krishnamurthy, Smriti Mandhana, Niranjana Nagarajan, Shikha Pandey, Anuja Patil, Mithali Raj (capt), Deepti Sharma, Vellaswamy Vanitha, Sushma Verma, Poonam Yadav.

Concept note-3: -The sixth tournament, the 2016 ICC World Twenty20, was hosted by India and was won by West Indies defeating England.
