Finance Minister Nirmala Sitharaman will attend the two-day meeting of G-20 Finance Ministers and central bank governors in which country?

(A) Brazil

(B) Canada

(C) ** Japan

(D) Germany

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The First G20 Finance and Central Bank Deputies Meeting, under the Indian Presidency, was held in Bengaluru, Karnataka, during December 13-14, 2022, under the co-chairship of Secretary, Economic Affairs, Shri Ajay Seth and Deputy Governor, Reserve Bank of India, Dr. Michael Patra.

Concept note-2: -Story highlights. India Budget 2023: Country’s finance minister Nirmala Sitharaman said that New Delhi’s G20 presidency presents a ‘unique opportunity’ in a larger global spotlight as it strengthens the country’s role in the world economic order when countries across the globe are facing various challenges.

Concept note-3: -The G20 Finance Track, led by Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governors of G20 countries, focuses on economic and financial issues. It provides an effective forum for global economic discourse and policy coordination.

Concept note-4: -The Fourth Finance Ministers and Central Bank Governor Meeting (FMCBG) was held on 12-13 October 2022, back-to-back with the 2022 Annual Meetings of the International Monetary Fund and the World Bank Group.
