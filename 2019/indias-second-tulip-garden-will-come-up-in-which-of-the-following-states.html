India’s second Tulip Garden will come up in which of the following states?

(A) Nagaland

(B) Jharkhand

(C) ** Uttarakhand

(D) Sikkim

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Q. India’s second Tulip Garden will come up in which of the following states? Notes: After Srinagar (J&K), India’s second Tulip Garden will come up at Pithoragarh district of Uttarakhand over 50 hectares of forest land near Chandak hill top at a cost of Rs 50 crore.

Concept note-2: -Long-awaited for opening, the Tulip garden was inaugurated by The Floriculture Department of the Government of Jammu and Kashmir, allowing thousands of visitors to get the first insight into the beautiful garden.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Srinagar. The Indira Gandhi Memorial Tulip Garden is home to around 65 varieties of tulips. It is the largest tulip garden in Asia spread over an area of about 30 hectares. It is situated on the foothills of Zabarwan Range with an overview of Dal Lake.

Concept note-4: -Srinagar’s Tulip Garden: All of the spring season’s major attractions are ready to greet visitors to Asia’s largest Tulip Garden in Srinagar. It will open its doors to the public on March 23, 2022.

Concept note-5: -Tulip Garden | District Srinagar, Government of Jammu and Kashmir | India.
