Who has been appointed head coach of the Indian men’s senior football team?

(A) Hakan Ericson

(B) ** Igor Stimac

(C) Albert Roca

(D) Lee Min-Sung

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Calling for careful planning for 2023 Asian Cup, Indian men’s football team coach Igor Stimac says he will be using the upcoming FIFA World Cup to build case studies which will help in preparing the team for the continental event.

Concept note-2: -Former Indian goalkeeper Kalyan Chaubey was elected as the new president of the All Indian Football Federation (AIFF). He will become the first ex-player to hold the top office of India’s football governing body in its 85 years of existence.

Concept note-3: -Sunil Chhetri is the greatest Indian footballer of all time and leading international goal scorer for India.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. John Wright was the first foreign coach appointed to the India cricket team.
