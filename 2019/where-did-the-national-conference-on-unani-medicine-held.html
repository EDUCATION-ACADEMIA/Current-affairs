Where did the National Conference on Unani Medicine held?

(A) Ahmedabad

(B) Pune

(C) Hyderabad

(D) ** New Delhi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Union Minister of Ayush and Ports, Shipping and Waterways Sarbananda Sonowal inaugurated the International Conference on “Diet and Nutrition in Unani Medicine for Good Health & Well-Being” in Srinagar on 10 March 2022.

Concept note-2: -ORIGIN AND DEVELOPMENT- Central Council for Research in Unani Medicine Ministry of AYUSH, Government of India. The Unani System of Medicine, as its name suggests, owes its origin to Greece. The roots of this system go to Egypt and its sister civilization Mesopotamia.

Concept note-3: -The theme of the two-day international conference is “Diet and Nutrition in Unani Medicine for Good Health & Well-being”.

Concept note-4: -In the 1920s Indian physician Ajmal Khan revolutionized Unani medicine by advocating that research be conducted on various natural products that were claimed by ancient physicians to effect miraculous cures.

Concept note-5: -In India, Unani system of Medicine was introduced by Arabs and soon it took firm roots in the soil. The Delhi Sultan, the Khiljis, the Tughlaqs and the Mughal Emperors provided state patronage to the scholars and even enrolled some as state employees and court physicians.
