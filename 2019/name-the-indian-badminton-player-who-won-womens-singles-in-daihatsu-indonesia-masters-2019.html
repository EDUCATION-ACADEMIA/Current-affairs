Name the Indian Badminton player who won Women’s Singles in DAIHATSU Indonesia Masters 2019.

(A) Sania Mirza

(B) P. V. Sindhu

(C) ** Saina Nehwal

(D) Ashwini Ponnappa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Correct Answer is Saina Nehwal . Saina Nehwal is the winner of the Women’s Singles Indonesian Masters Badminton tournament 2019. . In addition to reaching finals in Denmark, Indonesia Masters and Syed Modi International, Saina won a gold medal at the 2018 Commonwealth Games, a bronze in the Asian Games.

Concept note-2: -Indian badminton starlet P.V. Sindhu has made it to the Forbes’ annual list of highest-paid female athletes. Sindhu is the only shuttler and Indian female sportsperson to feature in the list of 25 athletes.

Concept note-3: -Indian badminton player Sai Praneeth was runner-up at the 2019 Swiss Open tournament. Shi yuqi won the men’s singles and Chen Yufei was the winner of the women’s singles in this tournament.
