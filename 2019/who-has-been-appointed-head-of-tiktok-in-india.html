(A) Rahul Sharma

(B) ** Nikhil Gandhi

(C) T.Prakashan

(D) Ved Prakash

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Based out of Mumbai, Gandhi will lead the development of TikTok’s products and operations in India.

Concept note-2: -TikTok CEO Shou Zi Chew gave a rare public interview at The New York Times’ DealBook conference today, telling host Andrew Ross Sorkin that he is “responsible for all the strategic decisions at TikTok” in response to a question about interference from the Chinese government.

Concept note-3: -India set an ‘incredibly important precedent’ by banning TikTok, FCC Commissioner says. India set an “incredibly important precedent” by banning TikTok two and a half years ago, FCC commissioner Brendan Carr said as he projected a similar fate for the Chinese giant ByteDance app in the U.S.
