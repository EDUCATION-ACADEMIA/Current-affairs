Union Government appoints ____ pvt sector experts as joint secretaries.

(A) 4

(B) 6

(C) ** 9

(D) 12

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -New Delhi: The Appointments Committee of the Cabinet (ACC) has approved the number of appointments of officers at joint Secretary and Joint Secretary equivalent level on Monday.

Concept note-2: -Joint secretary is mostly a career civil servant and is a government official of high seniority. The civil servants who hold this rank and post are either from All India Services (on deputation; on tenure, after empanelment) or Central Civil Services (Group A; on empanelment).

Concept note-3: -Joint Secretary salary in Government of India ranges between ₹ 25.7 Lakhs to ₹ 46.0 Lakhs per year.

Concept note-4: -The three joint secretaries from the 2021 batch of lateral entrants are Samuel Praveen Kumar, who is on deputation, and Balasubramanian Krishnamurthy and Manish Chadha who are on a three-year contract.
