The 2019 International conference on Guru Padmasambhava was held in which of the following cities?

(A) Gorakhpur

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Dharamshala

(D) Sanchi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The conference titled ‘Life and Legacy of Guru Padmasambhava’, was held at IIC, New Delhi from 29-30 January 2019.

Concept note-2: -Statue of Guru Rinpoche, Central Tibet, Tsang Valley, 15th-16th century.

Concept note-3: -Padmasambhava, also called Guru Rimpoche, Tibetan Slob-dpon (“Teacher”), or Padma ‘Byung-gnas (“Lotus Born”), (flourished 8th century), legendary Indian Buddhist mystic who introduced Tantric Buddhism to Tibet and who is credited with establishing the first Buddhist monastery there.

Concept note-4: -Thrunkar Tsechu marks the birth anniversary of Guru Rinpoche (Guru Padmasambhava), the founder of Tibetan Buddhism who first introduced Buddhism in Sikkim and other neighbouring Himalayan regions during 8th century.

Concept note-5: -Those places are still important and preserved as places of holy Padmasambhava Guru Rinpoche in Nepal. 22 kilometers southwest of Kathmandu, Padmashamvaba entered in a meditation retreat focused on the Tantric Buddha know as Vajrakilaya in Tibetan.
