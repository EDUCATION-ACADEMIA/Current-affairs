World Energy Conservation Day is observed on which of the following dates?

(A) 11th December

(B) 12th December

(C) ** 14th December

(D) 10th December

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated on December 14 in India to raise awareness among people about global warming, climate change and promote ways of save energy resources.

Concept note-2: -The National Energy Conservation Day is celebrated every year on 14th December 2022.

Concept note-3: -14 December - National Energy Conservation Day It is observed on 14 December to raise awareness about the need for energy and its conservation in daily life. Since 1991, it is celebrated every year on 14 December by the Bureau of Energy Efficiency (BEE), under the Ministry of Power.

Concept note-4: -Objective: The day focuses on making people aware of global warming and climate change and promotes efforts towards saving energy resources. It also highlights the achievements of the country in the fields of energy efficiency and conservation.

Concept note-5: -The awards were given away for the first time on December 14, 1991, which was declared as the ‘National Energy Conservation Day’.
