Recently, Who laid the foundation stone for Indian Institute of Skills in Mumbai (IIS-Mumbai)?

(A) Narendra Singh Tomar

(B) Prakash Javadekar

(C) ** Mahendra Nath Pandey

(D) Santosh Gangwa

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Kanpur. Prime Minister Narendra Modi laid the foundation stone of the country’s first Indian Institute of Skills (IIS) in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh to make India the Skill Capital of the World.

Concept note-2: -Assam Chief Minister Sarbananda Sonowal on Monday laid the foundation of the first skill university of eastern India in Darrang district, aimed at imparting training to over 10,000 students in 12 disciplines. The varsity would be built with a financial outlay of ₹1,000 crore.

Concept note-3: -This was conveyed during a meeting between the high powered team from Tata Group and senior officials of Gujarat Government held in Gandhinagar yesterday.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Kanpur. The construction of the country’s first Indian Institute of Skills (IIS) has started in Kanpur, Uttar Pradesh. The foundation stone of this initiative was laid by prime minister Narendra Modi.
