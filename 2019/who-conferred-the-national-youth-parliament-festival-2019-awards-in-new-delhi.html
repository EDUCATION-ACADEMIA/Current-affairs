Who conferred the National Youth Parliament Festival 2019 awards in New Delhi?

(A) Venkaiah Naidu

(B) Arun Jaitley

(C) ** Narendra Modi

(D) Nitin Gadkari

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister confers the National Youth Parliament Festival 2019 awards to the winners. Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi today conferred the National Youth Parliament Festival 2019 awards to the winners at VigyanBhawan. The National Level Finals of National Youth Parliament Festival concluded in New Delhi yesterday.

Concept note-2: -Minister of State (I/C) for Youth Affairs and Sports Col Rajyavardhan Rathore (Retd) today launched the National Youth Parliament Festival 2019 here, thereby beginning the celebration of the National Youth Day 2019.

Concept note-3: -National Youth Parliament Festival Taking inspiration from the idea of Prime Minister, the first NYPF 2019 was organised from 12 January to 27 February 2019 at District, State & National level with the theme “Be the Voice of New India and Find solutions and Contribute to Policy”.

Concept note-4: -50,000 for 2 consolation prize may also be awarded, if any. Union Minister for Youth Affairs & Sports Shri Anurag Thakur addressed the inaugural session of National round of 3rd edition of National Youth Parliament Festival (NYPF) 2022 today in the central hall of Parliament, New Delhi.

Concept note-5: -2nd edition of NYPF was organized from 23rd December, 2020 to 12th January, 2022 with the theme “YUVAAH- Utsah Naye Bharat Ka” through virtual mode which was witnessed by more than 23 lakh youth and stakeholders across the country at District, State and National level.
