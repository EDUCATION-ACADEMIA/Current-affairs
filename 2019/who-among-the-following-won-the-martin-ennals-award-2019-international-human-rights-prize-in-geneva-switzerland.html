Who among the following won the Martin Ennals Award 2019, International human rights prize in Geneva Switzerland?

(A) Anish Kapoor

(B) Widyan

(C) ** Abdul Aziz Muhamat

(D) Justine Frischmann

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jailed Kashmiri rights activist Khurram Parvez has won the Martin Ennals Award, one of the world’s most prestigious human rights prizes, along with two other campaigners from Chad and Venezuela, with the jury hailing their “courage”.

Concept note-2: -Every year, the Martin Ennals Foundation, based in Geneva, Switzerland, gives out an award that is regarded as the Nobel Prize for human rights defenders. The recipients of this year’s awards include Daouda Diallo from Burkina Faso, Pham Doan Trang from Vietnam and Abdul-Hadi Al-Khawaja of Bahrain.

Concept note-3: -Gohar Eshghi, the mother of justice and freedom, has won the 2022 Human Rights Award by the International Center for Human Rights in Canada. Gohar Eshghi Mother of Sattar Beheshti was chosen as the winner of the 2022 award.
