Which of the following cities has topped the Mercer’s 2019 Quality of Living Index?

(A) Auckland

(B) Zurich

(C) ** Vienna

(D) Munich

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Austrian capital Vienna tops the Mercer’s index of most liveable cities for the 10th year in a row. Vienna was closely followed by Zurich in Switzerland. Auckland, Munich, and Vancouver came in joint third.

Concept note-2: -Bangalore emerged as a top performer in India’s list of top livable cities. This start up capital of India will always mesmerise with its sheer perfection. Bangalore city is ranked number one among India’s best cities to live in in 2022.

Concept note-3: -The EIU’s Global Liveability Ranking Between 2004 and 2010, Vancouver, Canada, was ranked the EIU’s most livable city, with Melbourne sharing first place in the inaugural 2002 report. Vancouver has ranked third since 2015, while Vienna, Austria, ranked second until 2018 when it climbed to the top spot.

Concept note-4: -For the third time in the past five years, Vienna captured the first spot in the 2022 Global Liveability Index, the annual report published by The Economist Intelligence Unit (EIU).
