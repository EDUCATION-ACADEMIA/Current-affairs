Who was honoured with the UNICEF’s Danny Kaye Humanitarian Award 2019?

(A) David Malpass

(B) Sundar Pichai

(C) Rami Malek

(D) ** Priyanka Chopra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW YORK: Priyanka Chopra has been honoured with UNICEF’s Danny Kaye Humanitarian Award. The India star was named the 2019 recipient of the Humanitarian Award by the United Nations Children’s Fund in June this year and received the award at the Snowflake Ball Tuesday night.

Concept note-2: -Unicef honored Indian actress Priyanka Chopra with the Danny Kaye Humanitarian Award at the Unicef Snowflake Ball in December. The event was held on December 3 in New York.

Concept note-3: -At the height of his prolific career, legendary entertainer Danny Kaye made a commitment to helping the world’s children by becoming UNICEF’s first celebrity goodwill ambassador. For more than three decades, he used his influence and fame to educate millions about UNICEF’s lifesaving work.

Concept note-4: -Actress Priyanka Chopra, who became the Global UNICEF Goodwill Ambassador in 2016 and has been associated with the organisation for almost 15 years, recently spoke about children’s rights at a recently held conference at the United Nations General Assembly (UNGA) in New York.
