Virat Kohli has been confered with how many honors in the 2018 ICC Awards?

(A) 2

(B) ** 3

(C) 4

(D) 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Virat Kohli became the first cricketer in history to win all three major awards.

Concept note-2: -Kohli has received many accolades for his performances on the cricket field. He was recognized as the ICC ODI Player of the Year in 2012 and has won Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy, given to the ICC Cricketer of the Year, on two occasions, in 2017 and 2018.

Concept note-3: -India’s fast-rising wicket-keeper-batsman Rishabh Pant was named the International Cricket Council’s Emerging Player of the Year 2018 in the world body’s annual awards.

Concept note-4: -Virat Kohli has become the first player in history to win the Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year, the ICC Men’s Test Player of the Year and the ICC ODI Player of the Year awards following an extraordinary 2018.

Concept note-5: -Virat Kohli is the first player to win the top-3 ICC awards in the same year in 2018 winning Cricketer of the Year, Test Player of the Year, and ODI Player of the Year awards.
