The statistical study of elections and trends in voting is called

(A) ** Psephology

(B) Palaeontology

(C) Demography

(D) Ornithology

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Psephology is a division of political science that deals with the examination as well as the statistical analysis of elections and polls.

Concept note-2: -countable noun. A psephologist studies how people vote in elections.

Concept note-3: -The study of politics is called political science, or politology. It comprises numerous subfields, including comparative politics, political economy, international relations, political philosophy, public administration, public policy, gender and politics, and political methodology.

Concept note-4: -Systematic study of election trends : Psephology.
