Which space agency has revealed that giant asteroid 99942 Apophis is expected to fly close to Earth in a decade?

(A) ** NASA

(B) Roscosmos

(C) JAXA

(D) CNES

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -According to NASA, the Asteroid 99942 Apophis will make an extremely close trip to Earth on April 13, 2029. On that day, this space rock will come as close as 38000 kilometers to Earth. This is closer than many geostationary satellites orbiting the planet.

Concept note-2: -Although Apophis will not hit Earth anytime soon, the asteroid will make a close encounter with our planet on April 13, 2029, when it will pass within just 19,000 miles (31,000 kilometers).
