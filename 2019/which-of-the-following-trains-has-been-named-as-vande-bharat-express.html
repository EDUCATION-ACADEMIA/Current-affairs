Which of the following trains has been named as Vande Bharat Express?

(A) Deccan Odyssey

(B) Golden Chariot

(C) Lifeline Express

(D) ** Train 18

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On 27 January 2019, Train 18 was renamed to Vande Bharat Express to honour the fact that the train was manufactured entirely in India for covering distances of 500kms to 750kms. It was designed with the vision of low-cost maintenance and cost optimization in mind. It began operations on 15 February 2019.

Concept note-2: -The first Vande Bharat Express train was flagged off on February 15, 2019, on the New Delhi- Kanpur-Allahabad-Varanasi route. 1 On January 15, 2023, Prime Minister Narendra Modi flagged off the eighth Vande Bharat Express train connecting Secunderabad (Telangana) and Visakhapatnam (Andhra Pradesh).

Concept note-3: -Vande Bharat Express trains list: There are a total of 7 advanced high-speed Vande Bharat Express Trains that are operational in the country till January 12. The 8th Vande Bharat Express between Secunderabad to Visakhapatnam is flagged by Prime Minister Narendra Modi on January 15.
