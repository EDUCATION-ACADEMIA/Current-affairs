Which business tycoon emerged as India’s most searched business tycoon on Google in 2019?

(A) Neerav Modi

(B) ** Ratan Tata

(C) Anil Ambani

(D) Adi Godrej

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NEW DELHI : Tata Sons’ chairman emeritus Ratan Tata has emerged as India’s most searched business tycoon on Google in 2019.

Concept note-2: -Mukesh Ambani Scoring tenth place among the richest persons in the world in 2022, Mukesh Ambani has $91 billion in net worth. The founder and chairman of Reliance Industries has his interests across petrochemicals, oil and gas, retail and telecom.

Concept note-3: -Since April 2022, Gautam Adani has been on the list of the five richest men in the world, but one cannot see Ratan Tata or N Chandrasekharan, Chairman of Tata Group, on the list of the richest people in the world. However, this is a representation of individual wealth only.

Concept note-4: -As per the ranking, he stood at 433rd position, with Rs 3,500 crore in net wealth in 2021. This is mainly because 66 per cent of the profit earned by the Tata companies, under the holding company Tata Sons, is donated for philanthropic activities through Tata Trusts.
