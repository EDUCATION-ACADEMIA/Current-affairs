This bank recently updates rules for cash deposits of up to Rs 50, 000.

(A) Axis Bank

(B) Yes Bank

(C) HDFC Bank

(D) ** Canara Bank

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -50,000 and above only by debit to customers’ accounts or against cheques and not against cash (Circular DBOD. BP.

Concept note-2: -India’s income tax laws prohibit cash transactions above ₹ 2 lakh for any reason.

Concept note-3: -According to the new rules, PAN and Aadhaar will be required for depositing cash of Rs 20 lakh or more in a bank or post office in any one financial year. The Central Board of Direct Taxes (CBDT) has issued new rules under the Income Tax (15th Amendment) Rules, 2022.

Concept note-4: -As such there is no limit on how much money you can save in a savings bank account, but there is always a ‘but’. The maximum amount of money you can save in your account can only be set by your bank, but in most cases, there is no such limit set as the banks also earn a good amount with your deposited funds.
