First ASEAN-U.S. Maritime Exercise begins in Sattahip Naval Base in which country?

(A) Hong Kong

(B) ** Thailand

(C) Singapore

(D) Japan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first ASEAN-U.S. Maritime Exercise, co-led by the U.S. and Royal Thai navies, includes maritime forces from the U.S. and all ten ASEAN member states. AUMX promotes shared commitments to maritime partnerships, security and stability in Southeast Asia.

Concept note-2: -The 32nd edition of India-Thailand Coordinated Patrol (Indo-Thai CORPAT) between the Indian Navy and the Royal Thai Navy is being conducted from 12 – 14 November 2021.

Concept note-3: -The Indian Navy is hosting the 29th edition of the Singapore-India Maritime Bilateral Exercise (SIMBEX) from 26 to 30 October, 2022 at Visakhapatnam.

Concept note-4: -Initiated in 1994, SIMBEX is the Indian Navy’s longest uninterrupted bilateral maritime exercise with any foreign navy.
