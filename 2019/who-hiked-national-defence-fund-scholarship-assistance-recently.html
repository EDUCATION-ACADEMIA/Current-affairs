Who hiked National Defence Fund scholarship assistance, recently?

(A) Nirmala Sitharaman

(B) Arun Jaitley

(C) Rajnath Singh

(D) ** Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Modi government ups scholarships given under National Defence Fund. NEW DELHI: In its first decision of its second tenure, Narendra Modi government has enhanced the amount to be paid as part of ‘Prime Ministers Scholarship Scheme’ under the National Defence Fund.

Concept note-2: -PM Scholarship 2022: PM Scholarship or Prime Minister Scholarship Scheme (PMSS) is a scholarship scheme that strives at promoting higher professional and technical studies for the dependent wards and widows of Central Armed Police Forces & Assam Rifles (CAPFs & AR) and State Police Personnel.

Concept note-3: -However, the amount of scholarship given to girls is INR 3,000 per month and the amount of scholarship given to boys is INR 2,500 per month, paid annually. Furthermore, the duration of the scholarship may vary from one year to five years depending on the course duration.

Concept note-4: -Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme was introduced from the Academic year 2006-07 to encourage higher technical and professional education for the dependent wards of Ex Servicemen / Ex Coast Guard personnel and their widows.

Concept note-5: -The ‘Prime Minister’s Scholarship Scheme (PMSS)’ ‘ is being implemented to encourage technical and post-graduate education for the widows and wards of the deceased/ex-service personnel of Armed Forces. The scheme is funded out of National Defence Fund administered by Prime Minister’s Office.
