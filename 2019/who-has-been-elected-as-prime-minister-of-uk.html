Who has been elected as Prime Minister of UK?

(A) Michael Gove

(B) Jeremy Corbyn

(C) ** Boris Johnson

(D) Jeremy Hunt

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first prime minister of the current United Kingdom (formally the “United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland"), was Bonar Law, although the country was not renamed officially until 1927, when Stanley Baldwin was the serving prime minister. The incumbent prime minister is Rishi Sunak.

Concept note-2: -In July 2016, Theresa May became Prime Minister of the United Kingdom, having taken over from David Cameron (who had resigned in the wake of the 2016 Brexit referendum).

Concept note-3: -Notable lengths. Of the 56 past prime ministers, nine served more than 10 years while eight served less than a year.

Concept note-4: -Biography. Today often viewed as the first British Prime Minister, Sir Robert Walpole was described by contemporary opponents as the ‘Screen-Master General’, adept at pulling all the political strings.
