Which day is celebrated as an Independence day in the United States?

(A) July 2

(B) ** July 4

(C) July 3

(D) July 5

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Independence Day is celebrated in the United States on July 4. Often the holiday is called the Fourth of July.

Concept note-2: -The document, called the Declaration of Independence, was treasonous against England; and the fifty-six men who signed it were in danger of being executed. Independence Day is celebrated on July 4 because that is the day when the Continental Congress approved the Declaration of Independence.

Concept note-3: -Americans celebrate by participating in parades and going to events and concerts where patriotic songs, such as “America the Beautiful” and “This Land is Your Land,” are played. At night many people gather in parks to watch fireworks, displays of colorful lights exploding in the sky.
