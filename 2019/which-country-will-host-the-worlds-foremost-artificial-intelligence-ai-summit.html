Which country will host the world’s foremost Artificial Intelligence (AI) Summit?

(A) UK

(B) India

(C) ** UAE

(D) Malaysia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -UAE is set to host the world’s foremost Artificial Intelligence, AI Summit to empower global dialogue on the future of government, business and society.

Concept note-2: -However, some countries are known for investing heavily in AI research, including the United States, China, Canada, and several European countries, such as the United Kingdom, France, and Germany.

Concept note-3: -India secures 1st rank in AI talent concentration and overall 61st according to the recently released Network Readiness Index 2022. India’s position has improved by six slots in the latest version of 2022.

Concept note-4: -A global tech and science strategy group focused on AI4Good operating in healthcare, education, STEM and government / strategic level Artificial Intelligence. We connect the global AI ecosystem from Enterprise to BigTech, Startups, Investors and Science.

Concept note-5: -India will take over the Chair of the Global Partnership on Artificial Intelligence, GPAI from France today, November 21. Minister of State for Electronics and Information Technology Rajeev Chandrasekhar will represent the country at the GPAI meeting in Tokyo for the symbolic takeover from France.
