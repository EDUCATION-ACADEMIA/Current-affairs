Who wins Monaco Grand Prix, recently?

(A) Max Verstappen

(B) Sebastian Vettel

(C) ** Lewis Hamilton

(D) Valtteri Bottas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It returned in 2021, on 23 May, where Max Verstappen won his first Monaco Grand Prix.

Concept note-2: -Red Bull’s Sergio Perez won his first race of 2022 by 1.1 seconds after a nailbiting end to the Monaco Grand Prix, with Carlos Sainz and Max Verstappen unable to make it past the Mexican – and Charles Leclerc losing out after dropping from pole to P4.

Concept note-3: -The race started with a rolling start after a 65-minute delay. Leclerc lost the race lead to strategy error and dropped down to fourth place behind Max Verstappen and Sainz, as Pérez won the race.

Concept note-4: -PEREZ WINS THE MONACO GRAND PRIX! Red Bull’s strategy at the pit stop worked perfectly and they have claimed the victory from Ferrari. Sainz is second and Verstappen is third. Clock has his zero, so this is the last lap.

Concept note-5: -Max Verstappen claimed a record-extending 15th Formula 1 win of the season in Abu Dhabi as Charles Leclerc snatched P2 in the championship.
