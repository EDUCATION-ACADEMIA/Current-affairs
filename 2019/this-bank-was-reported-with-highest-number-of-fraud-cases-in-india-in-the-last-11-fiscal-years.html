This bank was reported with highest number of fraud cases in India in the last 11 fiscal years.

(A) ** ICICI Bank

(B) State Bank of India

(C) HDFC Bank

(D) Bank of Baroda

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The State Bank of India (SBI), ICICI Bank, and HDFC Bank had the most incidences of over 50,000 frauds that affected Indian institutions in the recent 11 fiscal years. Several international banks with operations in India have also reported scam cases totalling billions of rupees over the same period.
