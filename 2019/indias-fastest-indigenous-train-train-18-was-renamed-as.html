India’s fastest indigenous train "Train 18" was renamed as;

(A) Train 18 Express

(B) ** Vande Bharat Express

(C) Rajdhani 18 Express

(D) Train 18 Premium

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Vande Bharat Express, formerly known as Train 18, is a semi-high-speed, electric multiple-unit train operated by Indian Railways. It was designed and manufactured by the government-owned Integral Coach Factory (ICF), located in Chennai.

Concept note-2: -Fastest Train in India: The Vande Bharat Express is the fastest train in India and has the fastest top design and can reach the speed of 180 km/h (110 mph).

Concept note-3: -Train 18 will be India’s fastest train. It has recorded a top speed of 180 km/hr during test runs. However, on commercial runs, it will hit a maximum speed of 160 km/hr, which is the maximum that India’s railway tracks can safely handle.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Deccan Queen. Deccan Queen was India’s first Superfast train.

Concept note-5: -India to get third Vande Bharat train Friday; Check features of indigenous high-speed rail. New Delhi [India], September 29 (ANI): Prime Minister Narendra Modi will flag off the new and upgraded version of the indigenously-built high-speed Vande Bharat Express between Gandhinagar Capital and Mumbai Central on Friday.
