This Indian business school has been ranked among Top 10 Forbes Best International 1-Year Business Schools 2019?

(A) FMS Delhi

(B) SIBM Pune

(C) IIM Ahmedabad

(D) ** ISB Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian School of Business (ISB) secured the seventh rank globally in ‘The Best International MBAs: One-Year Programs’ rankings by Forbes. The Hyderabad based management institute is also the top-ranked B-school from Asia. This is the first time, ISB took part in the rankings.

Concept note-2: -It is ranked #151-200 in QS WUR Ranking By Subject 2022.

Concept note-3: -#7 Indian School of Business With 42 partnerships with leading schools, students can study at many other universities like Kellogg, Wharton, IE and HEC Paris. In India, there is no other B-school that comes close to the quality of faculty, networking and classroom learning that ISB provides.

Concept note-4: -1. Harvard University. Harvard Business School applicants must submit either a GMAT or GRE score to be considered for admission.
