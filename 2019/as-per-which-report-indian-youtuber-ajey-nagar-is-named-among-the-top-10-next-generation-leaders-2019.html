As per which report, Indian YouTuber Ajey Nagar is named among the Top 10 ’Next Generation Leaders 2019’?

(A) Facebook

(B) Rolling Stone

(C) ** Time Magazine

(D) Redbook

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India’s 19-year-old YouTuber Ajey Nagar, who runs the YouTube channel ‘CarryMinati’, has been named among TIME Magazine’s 10 Next Generation Leaders for 2019.

Concept note-2: -Ajey Nagar (pronounced [əˈdʒeː ˈnaːɡər]; born 12 June 1999), popularly known as CarryMinati, is an Indian YouTuber and streamer from Faridabad, India. He is known for his roasting videos, comedic skits and reactions to various online topics on his channel CarryMinati.

Concept note-3: -He is known for his roasting videos, comedic skits, and reactions to various online topics on his channel CarryMinati. He is the most subscribed non-corporate Indian YouTuber and the first in India to reach 30 million subscribers.
