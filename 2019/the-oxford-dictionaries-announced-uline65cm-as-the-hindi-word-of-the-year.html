The Oxford Dictionaries announced ______ as the Hindi Word of the Year.

(A) Yayavar

(B) Adamya

(C) Parthiv

(D) ** Nari Shakti

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Goblin mode: Oxford Word of the Year 2022 - Times of India.

Concept note-2: -Explained: How was ‘Aatmanirbharta’ chosen as the Hindi Word of the Year by Oxford Languages?

Concept note-3: -Few other words that the English dictionary picked are - Bas, Jhuggi, Funda, Nivas, Gully, Natak, Sevak, Sevika, Chup, Surya Namaskar, Desh, Diya, Bada Din among many others.

Concept note-4: -From Abba to Dadagiri and Gulab Jamun, 70 Indian words added to Oxford dictionary. From endearing words like ‘Abba’ and ‘Anna’ to Indian delicacies like ‘gulab jamun’ and ‘vada’ can now be found in the Oxford English Dictionary (OED).
