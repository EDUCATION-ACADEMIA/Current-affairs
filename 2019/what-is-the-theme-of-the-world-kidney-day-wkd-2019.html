What is the theme of the World Kidney Day (WKD-2019)?

(A) Kidney Disease and Obesity

(B) ** Kidney Health for Everyone Everywhere

(C) Kidneys for Life - Stop Kidney Attack

(D) Kidney Disease & Children: Act Early to Prevent It

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for World Kidney Day 2019 is global awareness, with a focus on disparities and inequities due to social determinants of health.

Concept note-2: -In 2019, World Kidney Day was observed on March 14, the second Thursday. The theme for 2019: “Kidney Health for Everyone Everywhere”. Chronic kidney disease is a severe public health problem in India, with 1 in 10 people estimated to be suffering from some form of chronic kidney disorder (CKD).

Concept note-3: -The WKD Joint Steering Committee has declared 2022 the year of “Kidney Health for All,” with the campaign focusing on bridging knowledge gaps to improve kidney health through increased kidney care education and awareness in the public arena.

Concept note-4: -March is National Kidney Month and March 11th is World Kidney Day.

Concept note-5: -The World Kidney Day Joint Steering Committee has declared 2022 to be the year of “Kidney Health for All”. Specifically, it calls on all of us to work to bridge the knowledge gaps to better kidney care.
