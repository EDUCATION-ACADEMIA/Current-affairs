Typhoon Phanfone hits which country recently?

(A) Indonesia

(B) ** Philippines

(C) Bangladesh

(D) Nepal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Typhoon Phanfone, known in the Philippines as Typhoon Ursula, was a strong and deadly tropical cyclone which traversed the Philippines on Christmas Eve and Christmas Day in 2019, the first typhoon to do so since Nock-ten in 2016.

Concept note-2: -1 – TROPICAL CYCLONE NALGAE, Philippines – 4 NOVEMBER 2022.

Concept note-3: -On 25 September 2022, tropical cyclone Noru (locally named Karding) undergone explosive intensification and became of super typhoon strength while it continues to move westward towards eastern seaboard of the Philippines.

Concept note-4: -Super Typhoon Hinnamnor, the strongest global storm of 2022, is sitting just south of Japan’s Okinawa Island with sustained winds of 241 kilometers per hour. According to Japan Meteorological Agency, the Hinmnamnor storm will further move northward to approach the island over the weekend.

Concept note-5: -Severe tropical storm Nalgae (locally named Paeng) which made landfall on 29 October 2022 in Catanduanes, Philippines, has brought heavy rains and strong winds and affected several hundreds of thousands of people since 27 October 2022.
