Who has been appointed as Chairman of Ordnance Factory Board (OFB)?

(A) Ajay Jain

(B) Kuldeep Saxena

(C) Hemant Bhargava

(D) ** Saurabh Kumar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -C.S. Vishwakarma takes over charge of DGOF and Chairman of Ordnance Factory Board.

Concept note-2: -E.R. Sheikh assumed charge as first Director General of the Ordnance Directorate (Co-ordination and Services). It is the successor organization of Ordnance Factory Board (OFB). An Indian Ordnance Factory Service (IOFS) officer of the 1984 batch, he has been a spearhead of modernization.

Concept note-3: -Shri Sanjeev Kishore, a 1985 batch officer of the Indian Ordnance Factory Service (IOFS), has taken over as the Director General Ordnance (C&S) with effect from 01-10-2022 upon the superannuation of Shri M K Grag.

Concept note-4: -As decided by the Union Cabinet on 16th June, 2021, the Government of India has decided to corporatise the functions of the 41 production units (Ordnance Factories) of the Ordnance Factory Board (“OFB”), functioning under the Department of Defence Production, Ministry of Defence (“DDP”).
