Bandana, who set a new Guinness World Record on ’Longest Dancing Marathon by an Individual’, is from which country?

(A) India

(B) ** Nepal

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Boosie is a Moton Park dancer. As of June 2022 she holds the Guinness World Record for “Longest heel-toe and park girl marathon by an individual", for which she danced for 126 hours continuously.

Concept note-2: -According to Official Guinness Records, The longest dance marathon by an individual was 123 hours and 15 minutes and was achieved by Kalamandalam Hemaletha (India) at the Kerala Sangeetha Nadaka Academy in Thrissur, Kerala, India from 20 to 26 September 2010. Hemaletha danced the Mohiniyattam dance.

Concept note-3: -According to guinnessworldrecords.com, the largest line dance involved 18,431 participants, and was achieved by Beijing Redance League Technology Co. Ltd., in China, in November 2015.

Concept note-4: -Kathmandu, Nepal – Bandana Nepal , 18, aNepali teenager,a resident of Dhankuta district in eastern Nepal,danced continuously for 126 hours to set a new World Record for the Longest Dancing Marathon by an Individual , according to the World Record Academy.

Concept note-5: -The Dance Marathon originated in 1923, when a woman named Alma Cummings danced for 27 hours outlasting six partners. From there it bloomed into a full-blown craze where couples competed against others to be the last pair standing.
