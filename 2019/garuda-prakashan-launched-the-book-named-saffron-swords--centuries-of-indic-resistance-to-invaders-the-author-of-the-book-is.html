Garuda Prakashan launched the book named ’Saffron Swords- Centuries of Indic Resistance to Invaders’. The author of the book is;

(A) Yogaditya Sinha Rawal

(B) ** Manoshi Sinha Rawal

(C) Anita Desai

(D) Aravind Adiga

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: The book titled “Saffron Swords- Centuries of Indic Resistance to Invaders” has been authored by Manoshi Sinha Rawal. It has been launched by India’s homegrown startup publishing houses - Garuda Prakashan Private Limited. It is a book on 52 unsung warriors of India from the last 1300 years.

Concept note-2: -Manoshi Sinha Rawal Manoshi Sinha is the author of several books, including the ‘Saffron Swords’ series. A postgraduate in English Literature from Pune University, she is from Assam. She is an independent Researcher on Indian History and ancient Temples.
