Emisat satellite, which is in news recently, is associated to which country?

(A) ** India

(B) Japan

(C) China

(D) South Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian Space Research Organisation (ISRO) launched the country’s first electronic surveillance satellite, EMISAT, from Sriharikota in coastal Andhra Pradesh on Monday morning.

Concept note-2: -Recently, PSLV-C54 was successfully launched EOS-06 satellite on 26th November 2022 along with Eight Nano-satellites including INDIA-BHUTAN SAT (INS-2B).

Concept note-3: -ISRO’s work horse PSLV-C54 has successfully launched EOS-06 satellite along with Eight Nano-satellites into two different SSPOs. The mission was accomplished from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR on 26th November 2022.

Concept note-4: -NanoMx is a multispectral optical imaging payload. The Nov. 26 launch was ISRO’s fifth and final mission for 2022.
