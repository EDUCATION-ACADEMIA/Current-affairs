Saina Nehwal has won the 2019 Indonesia Masters title against which player?

(A) Huang Sui

(B) Ratchanok Intanon

(C) Wang Shixian

(D) ** Carolina Marin

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indonesia Masters 2019 Final, Saina Nehwal vs Carolina Marin Final Highlights: Saina Nehwal wins title as Carolina Marin forced to retire.

Concept note-2: -Saina Nehwal World No 1: The year she made history Despite winning numerous trophies in her career, it was the World No 1 ranking which had eluded the Indian shuttler. That drought finally ended on April 2, 2015 when the Saina Nehwal World No 1 news made headlines.

Concept note-3: -Viktor Axelsen wins Indonesia Masters title | Badminton News - Times of India.

Concept note-4: -Selection trials were announced at a time when Nehwal was still nursing injuries, and her “aloof, stand-offish behaviour” during a past Uber Cup campaign was cited in federation circles as the reason to show her the door. Kashyap says it led to frequent breakdowns for the former World No 1.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution The Correct Answer is Saina Nehwal. Saina Nehwal is the winner of the Women’s Singles Indonesian Masters Badminton tournament 2019.
