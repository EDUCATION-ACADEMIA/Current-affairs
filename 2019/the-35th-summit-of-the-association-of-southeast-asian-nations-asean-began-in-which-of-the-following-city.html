The 35th Summit of The Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) began in which of the following city?

(A) Hong Kong

(B) Singapore

(C) ** Bangkok

(D) Mumbai

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Bangkok. The 35th ASEAN Summit began in Bangkok, Thailand from October 31 to 4 November. The Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) is a group of ten member countries that encourages political, economic, and social cooperation in the region.

Concept note-2: -The First ASEAN Summit was held in Bali, Indonesia on 23-24 February 1976.

Concept note-3: -The Association of Southeast Asian Nations, or ASEAN, was established on 8 August 1967 in Bangkok, Thailand, with the signing of the ASEAN Declaration (Bangkok Declaration) by the Founding Fathers of ASEAN: Indonesia, Malaysia, Philippines, Singapore and Thailand.
