Which is the country’s best police station according to central report?

(A) Campbell Bay police station

(B) Farakka policestation

(C) ** Aberdeen Police Station

(D) Bakani Police Station

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -BHUBANESWAR: Union home minister Amit Shah Friday awarded Aska police station in Odisha’s Ganjam district as the number one police station in the country in the annual ranking of police stations for 2022, an official release said.

Concept note-2: -Chief Minister Naveen Patnaik in a tweet has congratulated Aska police station as it has been selected as best police station in the country. Aska has also been declared as the best police station in CCTNS dashboard for 2022.

Concept note-3: -1. Police Force of England: Metropolitan (Scotland Yard Police) is the first on our top ten list, they truly exceeded the call of duty when they launched a campaign raising awareness of the police awareness number. They have successfully achieved police protection in the nooks and crannies of the UK.

Concept note-4: -Berhampur: Union home minister Amit Shah on Friday awarded Aska police station in Ganjam district as the best police station in the country for 2022.Prasant Kumar Sahoo, inspector in-charge, Aska police station, received the trophy from Shah at the annual conference of DGPs-IGPs in New Delhi.
