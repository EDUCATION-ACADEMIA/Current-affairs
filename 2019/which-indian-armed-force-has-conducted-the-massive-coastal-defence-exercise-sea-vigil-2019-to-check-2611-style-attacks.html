Which Indian armed force has conducted the massive coastal defence exercise ’Sea Vigil-2019’ to check 26/11-style attacks?

(A) Indian Army

(B) Indian Air Force

(C) ** Indian Navy

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Navy begins massive coastal defence exercise to check 26/11-style attacks.

Concept note-2: -This edition of exercise Sea Vigil saw the largest participation from all maritime security agencies. More than 500 surface assets from the Indian Navy (IN), Coast Guard (CG), States Marine/ Coastal Police, Customs, Forest Department, Port Authorities and private operators participated in the exercise.

Concept note-3: -THIRD EDITION OF LARGEST NATIONWIDE COASTAL DEFENCE EXERCISE ‘SEA VIGIL-22’ COMMENCES.

Concept note-4: -VISAKHAPATNAM: Exercise Sea Vigil-22, the two-day Coastal Defence exercise was conducted on November 15 and 16.

Concept note-5: -Exercise Sea Vigil-22 was organized by the Indian Navy on November 15 and 16 this year. This is the third edition of the Pan-India coastal defence exercise. The previous edition was held in January 2021.
