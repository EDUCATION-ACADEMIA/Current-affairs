Which of the following exercise was held between India and Africa?

(A) ** AFINDEX

(B) INDO-AR

(C) COPE 2019

(D) Yudh Abhyas

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In March 2019, India held its first-ever joint exercise with African countries, Africa-India Field Training Exercise-2019 (AFINDEX-19), which saw participation by 17 African countries. This was also held in Pune.

Concept note-2: -INDIAN ARMY SPECIAL FORCES COMMENCE JOINT EXERCISE GARUDA SHAKTI WITH INDONESIAN SPECIAL FORCES AT SANGGA BUANA TRAINING AREA, KARAWANG, INDONESIA.

Concept note-3: -IBSAMAR are a series of naval exercises between the navies of India, Brazil and South Africa. The name IBSAMAR is an abbreviation of India-Brazil-South Africa Maritime.

Concept note-4: -India–Malaysia joint military exercise “Harimau Shakti 2022” commenced at Pulai, Kluang, Malaysia on 28th November 2022. The annual training exercise between the Indian and Malaysian Army will culminate on 12th December 2022. The military exercise will be conducted for the next two weeks in Malaysia.

Concept note-5: -A major annual aero-naval event whose first edition dates back to 1983, “VARUNA” bears witness to the ability of the French and the Indian Navies to deploy and operate together and exemplifies the high level of trust between France and India, according to the statement.
