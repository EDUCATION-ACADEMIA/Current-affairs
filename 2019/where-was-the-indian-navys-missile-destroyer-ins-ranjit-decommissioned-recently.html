Where was the Indian Navy’s missile destroyer, INS Ranjit decommissioned recently?

(A) ** Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh

(B) Mumbai, Maharashtra

(C) Chennai, Tamil Nadu

(D) Balasore, Odisha

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The INS Ajay was in active naval service for more than 32 years during her illustrious journey.

Concept note-2: -INS Ajay was decommissioned on 19 September 2022 after rendering 32 years of glorious service to the nation.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is INS Nishank. The Indian Navy will bid farewell to its two ships INS Nishank and INS Akshay, at Naval Dockyard in Mumbai on 3 June 2022. Both the ships will be decommissioned at Naval Dockyard after 32 years of being at the forefront.

Concept note-4: -First of the Indian Navy’s modern destroyers was built in former USSR. Destroyer INS Rajput , built by the erstwhile USSR, was decommissioned on Friday at Naval Dockyard, Visakhapatnam after 41 years of service.
