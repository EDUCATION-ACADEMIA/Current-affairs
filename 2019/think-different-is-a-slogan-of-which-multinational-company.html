“Think Different” is a slogan of which multinational company?

(A) Microsoft

(B) Audi

(C) ** Apple

(D) Lenovo

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -08: Apple (1997) – Think Different. It’s easy to forget just what a perilous state Apple was in when Steve Jobs returned to the company in 1996.

Concept note-2: -"Think different” is an advertising slogan used from 1997 to 2002 by Apple Computer, Inc., now named Apple Inc. The campaign was created by the Los Angeles office of advertising agency. The slogan has been widely taken as a response to the IBM slogan “Think".

Concept note-3: -“Think Different” is what made Apple into the cult-like company you know today.

Concept note-4: -At the top of each image was the rainbow-colored Apple logo and the words “Think Different.” Nothing else. The creator of the work was a brilliant art director named Craig Tanimoto.

Concept note-5: -Apple’s New Motto: “Think Different - But Not Too Different”
