Which journalist won Ramon Magsayasay award 2019?

(A) Arnab Goswami

(B) Karan Thapar

(C) ** Ravish Kumar

(D) Sagarika Ghose

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indian media is in a state of “crisis” which is not accidental or random, but systemic and structural, prominent Indian journalist Ravish Kumar said here on Monday as he received the prestigious 2019 Ramon Magsaysay Award, regarded as the Asian version of the Nobel Prize.

Concept note-2: -Ravish Kumar wins 2019 Ramon Magsaysay Award Indian journalist Ravish Kumar (44) of NDTV news channel was chosen for 2019 Ramon Magsaysay Award. He has been given this prestigious award in recognition for harnessing journalism to give voice to voiceless. He is among 5 individuals who were chosen for this years’ award.

Concept note-3: -Indian journalist Ravish Kumar has been awarded the 2019 Ramon Magsaysay Award for “harnessing journalism to give voice to the voiceless”. He is among five individuals who were declared winners of the award.

Concept note-4: -In 2022, only four individuals got the award. None of them was Indian.
