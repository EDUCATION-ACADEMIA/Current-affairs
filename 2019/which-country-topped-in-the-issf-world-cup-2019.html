Which country topped in the ISSF World Cup 2019?

(A) Singapore

(B) ** India

(C) Brazil

(D) Spain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -India emerged as the most successful nation in the series, topping medal table in all Rifle-Pistol World Cups and Finals.

Concept note-2: -In the ISSF Shooting World Cup, India topped the medal tally at Changwon in South Korea. India finished the tournament with eight medals including three golds, four silvers and one bronze and was way ahead of hosts Korea and Serbia in the medal tally.

Concept note-3: -Federations decide which shooters travel to the main event by conducting trials. China topped the ISSF Rifle/Pistol World Championships 2022 medals tally with 58 medals, 27 hold, 16 silver and 15 bronze.

Concept note-4: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is 4. India has taken first place in the medal standing in International Shooting Sport Federation, ISSF World Cup 2022 in Cairo. The Indian team finished on the top of the medals tally with a total of seven medals comprising of four gold, two silver, and one bronze.
