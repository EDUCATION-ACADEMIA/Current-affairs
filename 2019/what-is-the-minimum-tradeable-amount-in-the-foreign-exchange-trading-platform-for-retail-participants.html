What is the minimum tradeable amount in the foreign exchange trading platform for retail participants?

(A) ** $1000

(B) $750

(C) $500

(D) $250

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The minimum transaction size of each unit of trade is approximately 1 million of the base currency.

Concept note-2: -No Minimum Deposit - You may find brokers who do not stipulate a minimum deposit, and require you to deposit a suitable amount to trade with the currency pairs you are considering.

Concept note-3: -Bringing in Foreign Exchange If, however, the value of foreign currency in cash exceeds US$ 5,000 and/or the cash plus TCs exceed US$ 10,000 it should be declared to the customs authorities at the airport in the currency declaration form (CDF), on arrival in India.

Concept note-4: -What is the minimum amount that I need to have? You can start trading with as low as $100 (7484.5 Indian Rupee), depending on the broker you want to trade with, but note that you will not make as much profit on each trade as someone who opened an account with $1,000 or $10,000.
