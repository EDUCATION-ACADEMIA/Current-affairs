Who among the following has been appointed as Deputy Executive Director in UN Women?

(A) Nishi Vasudeva

(B) ** Anita Bhatia

(C) Vinita D. Gupta

(D) Chitra Ramkrishna

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Importance of Gender Parity in Field Settings | Deputy Executive Director Anita Bhatia (Full)

Concept note-2: -United Nations Assistant Secretary-General and UN Women Deputy Executive Director Anita Bhatia will visit to the Republic of Korea on 10-12 October 2022.

Concept note-3: -UN Women was established by the merger of the United Nations Development Fund for Women (UNIFEM, established in 1976) and other entities, and became operational in January 2011. Concept note-4: -Secretary-General appoints Sima Sami Bahous of Jordan as Executive Director of UN Women.
