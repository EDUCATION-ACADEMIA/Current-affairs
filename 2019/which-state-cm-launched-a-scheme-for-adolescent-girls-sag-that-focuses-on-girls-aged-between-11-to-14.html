Which state CM launched a Scheme for Adolescent girls (SAG) that focuses on girls aged between 11 to 14?

(A) ** Uttar Pradesh

(B) Uttarkhand

(C) Bihar

(D) Assam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG) was devised in 2010 as a special intervention for adolescent girls of the age group 11 – 14 years to break the intergenerational life-cycle of nutritional and gender disadvantage and offer a supportive environment for the self-development of adolescent girls in the country.

Concept note-2: -The Government of India is implementing the Scheme for Adolescent Girls (SAG), a Centrally Sponsored Scheme, which focused on out of school adolescent girls in the age group of 11-14 years across the country.

Concept note-3: -Purpose of the Scheme: Sabala aims at empowering Adolescent Girls of 11 to 18 years by improving their nutritional and health status, up gradation of home skills, life skills and vocational skills.
