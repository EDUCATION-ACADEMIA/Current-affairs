Who among the following players has become the 1st Indian cricket player to play 100 T20 International matches?

(A) Mithali Raj

(B) ** Harmanpreet Kaur

(C) Virat Kohli

(D) Jhulan Goswami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Harmanpreet Kaur , the Indian Women Cricket team Captain has become the 1st Indian cricket player to play 100 T20 International matches. She is the first Indian to achieved this. Harmanpreet achieved this at the final T20 International against South Africa at Surat, India. Was this answer helpful?

Concept note-2: -India’s Rohit Sharma leads the list with four T20I centuries, followed by Czech Republic’s Sabawoon Davizi, India’s Suryakumar Yadav, Australia’s Glenn Maxwell and New Zealand’s Colin Munro with three each. A further 14 players have scored two T20I centuries including Evin Lewis of the West Indies.

Concept note-3: -Kohli also became the first player in history to play a hundred matches for India in all game formats.
