World Book and Copyright Day was observed on which date?

(A) ** 23 April

(B) 22 April

(C) 21 April

(D) 20 April

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Book and Copyright Day is a celebration to promote the enjoyment of books and reading. Each year, on 23 April, celebrations take place all over the world to recognize the scope of books - a link between the past and the future, a bridge between generations and across cultures.

Concept note-2: -History of World Book and Copyright Day Although World Book and Copyright Day was officially inaugurated in 1995, its conception is traceable to Spanish writer, Vicente Clavel Andres, who conceived the idea in 1922. He developed the holiday to pay tribute to the notable author, Miguel de Cervantes.

Concept note-3: -The theme for World Book and Copyright Day 2022 is “Read, so you never feel low”. The motive is to enhance the scope of reading.

Concept note-4: -It is the date on which several prominent authors, William Shakespeare, Miguel de Cervantes and Inca Garcilaso de la Vega all died. This date was a natural choice for UNESCO’s General Conference, held in Paris in 1995, to pay a world-wide tribute to books and authors on this date, encouraging everyone to access books.

Concept note-5: -The latter date is the one that was chosen because it was also the date that William Shakespeare died and when Inca Garcilaso de la Vega died too.
