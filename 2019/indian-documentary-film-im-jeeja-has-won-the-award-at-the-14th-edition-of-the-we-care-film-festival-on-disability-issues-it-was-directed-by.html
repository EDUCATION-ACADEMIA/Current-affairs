Indian documentary film “I’m Jeeja” has won the award at the 14th edition of the ‘We Care Film Festival on Disability Issues’. It was directed by;

(A) ** Swati Chakraborty

(B) Rakesh Sharma

(C) Anand Patwardhan

(D) Nishtha Jain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The International Film Festival of India Secretariat (Directorate of Film Festivals) under the Ministry of Information & Broadcasting is going to organize the 45th International Film Festival of India (IFFI 2014) in Goa from 20th to 30th November 2014.

Concept note-2: -The 51st International Film Festival of India has conferred Biswajit Chatterjee, veteran actor, producer of Hindi and Bengali cinema, with the Indian Personality of the Year Award. This award will be presented to him, when the National Film Awards are presented in March 2021.

Concept note-3: -The 1st edition of IFFI was organized by the Films Division, Government of India, with the patronage of the first Prime Minister of India. Held in Mumbai from 24 January to 1 February 1952, the Festival was subsequently taken to Madras, Delhi and Calcutta. In all it had about 40 features and 100 short films.
