Which of the following cricketers has officially received the knighthood at Buckingham Palace?

(A) Andrew Strauss

(B) ** Alastair Cook

(C) Kevin Pietersen

(D) Andrew Flintoff

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Frank Worrell was the first West Indian cricketer to be knighted for services to the sport. A formidable batsman, Worrell scored 3,860 runs in 51 tests.

Concept note-2: -The correct answer is Clive Lloyd. Former West Indies captain Clive Lloyd was given the knighthood for his service towards cricket. The Clive Llyod knighthood investiture was performed in January 2022 by the Duke of Cambridge at Windsor Castle.

Concept note-3: -10: Sir Alastair Cook - Cricket He was the first England cricketer to receive a knighthood since Sir Ian Botham in 2007 and the 11th player to be given the honor for services to English cricket.

Concept note-4: -The first man to receive a knighthood for services to cricket, or indeed any sport, was Francis Lacey. Knighted in 1926 (Gazette issue 33179), Lacey was most noted for his work as Secretary of Marylebone Cricket Club (MCC), a post which he held from 1898 to the year of his knighthood.
