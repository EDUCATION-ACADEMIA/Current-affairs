In which Year slave dynasty started in India?

(A) ** 1206

(B) 1210

(C) 1212

(D) 1216

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Slave dynasty, (1206–90), line of sultans at Delhi, India, that lasted for nearly a century.

Concept note-2: -Slave Dynasty Introduction. Established by Qutb ud-Din Aibak. Dynasty lasted from 1206 to 1290. It was the first of the dynasties to rule as the Delhi Sultanate.

Concept note-3: -Slave Dynasty or Ghulam Dynasty was founded by Qutub-ud-Din Aibak which ruled north India from 1206 to 1290. He was the slave of Muhammad Ghori and became the first sultan of the Delhi Sultanate.

Concept note-4: -By 1206-09, Aibak assumed the title of Sultan thus beginning the rule of the Slave dynasty and making Lahore his capital to secure the northwestern frontier of the kingdom.
