Which team win the final of the IBSF Snooker World Cup in Doha?

(A) England

(B) Scotland

(C) India

(D) ** Pakistan

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Kok Leong makes history with World Snooker Championships 2022 win. National snooker player Lim Kok Leong has created history after emerging as the first Malaysian player to win the IBSF World Championship in Antalya, Turkey, yesterday.

Concept note-2: -The IBSF & Turkish Billiards Federation are hosting the 2022 World Snooker Championships for the Men, Women and Masters categories. These championships shall be played in Antalya, Turkiye from 4th November till 17th November.

Concept note-3: -Pankaj conquers Qatar 6Reds World Cup 2021 Indian player Pankaj Advani has conquered back-to-back two major International championships.

Concept note-4: -The 2022 World Snooker Championship final begins at 13:00 local time (BST) on Sunday, May 1 at The Crucible Theatre in Sheffield. The second session is due to start six hours later. For viewers in the United States and Canada, that means a start time of 08:00 ET.
