What is the name of the new operating system that Huawei has launched?

(A) Libra

(B) Disc

(C) ** Harmony OS

(D) Symbian

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Using a multi-kernel design, HarmonyOS selects appropriate OS kernels for devices with various resource limitations. It uses the LiteOS kernel instead of the Linux kernel on low-powered devices such as smartwatches and IoT devices.

Concept note-2: -HarmonyOS 3.0 beta 2 updates are rolling out for the Huawei Smart Screen V75 and the entire V series. Check out the full Changelog. Huawei has recently released the August 2022 update in China for the Mate 40 series. Now, the flagship-grade smartphone Huawei Mate 40E is collecting the August 2022 HarmonyOS update.

Concept note-3: -Currently, the Huawei Mate 40 Pro is the best Huawei phone you can find in 2021 (although this might change soon, as the debut of the Huawei P50 is imminent).

Concept note-4: -HarmonyOS is a prime Huawei operating system that deserves to be at the top of the mobile software system world. On the other hand, EMUI 12 has been introduced with a user interface that perfectly copies HarmonyOS in many aspects including the Control Panel features.

Concept note-5: -AppGallery is a well-known application distribution platform. It is an official Huawei app that is available in more than 170 countries and is compatible with Android and HarmonyOS platforms. Besides, it supports more than 80 languages for Android users.
