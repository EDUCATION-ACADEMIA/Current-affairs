The multilateral maritime security field exercise, Asean Defence Ministers’ Meeting-Plus (ADMM-Plus) was held in ______

(A) Russia

(B) Germany

(C) ** Singapore

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 9th ASEAN Defence Ministers’ Meeting Plus (ADMM-Plus) was convened on 23 November 2022 in Siem Reap, Cambodia. The Meeting adopted the Joint Declaration by the ADMM-Plus Defence Ministers on Defence Cooperation to Strengthen Solidarity for A Harmonised Security, which can be downloaded here.

Concept note-2: -About the ASEAN Defence Ministers’ Meeting Against this background, the Inaugural meeting of the ADMM was held in Kuala Lumpur on 9 May 2006. The ADMM is the highest defence consultative and cooperative mechanism in ASEAN.

Concept note-3: -The Inaugural ADMM-Plus was convened in Ha Noi, Viet Nam, on 12 October 2010.

Concept note-4: -1. The Second ASEAN Defence Ministers’ Meeting (herein after referred to as “ADMM”) held in Singapore on 14 November 2007 approved the “ADMM-Plus Concept Paper” which provides for the ADMM’s engagement and interactions with ASEAN’s friends and Dialogue Partners.
