Who won 2019 Austrian Grand Prix, recently?

(A) Lewis Hamilton

(B) Valtteri Bottas

(C) Charles Leclerc

(D) ** Max Verstappen

Concept note-1: -The victory of Max Verstappen in the Red Bull Racing RB15 was the first win for a Honda-powered F1 car since Jenson Button in the 2006 Hungarian Grand Prix and he also became the first non-Mercedes driver to win a race in 2019.
