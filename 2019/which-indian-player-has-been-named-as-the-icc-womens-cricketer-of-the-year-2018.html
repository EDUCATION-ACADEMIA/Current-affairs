Which Indian player has been named as the ICC Women’s Cricketer of the Year 2018?

(A) Jhulan Goswami

(B) Harmanpreet Kaur

(C) Mithali Raj

(D) ** Smriti Mandhana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The women’s awards were announced on 31 December 2018, with Smriti Mandhana winning the Rachael Heyhoe Flint Award as the Women’s Cricketer of the Year. Virat Kohli became the first cricketer in history to win all three major awards.

Concept note-2: -Renuka Singh - India India’s newest fast bowling star Renuka Singh beat Australia’s Darcie Brown, England’s Alice Capsey and compatriot Yastika Bhatia to become the recipient of ICC Emerging Women’s Cricketer of the Year 2022.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Risabh Pant. India’s fast-rising wicket-keeper-batsman Rishabh Pant was named the International Cricket Council’s Emerging Player of the Year 2018 in the world body’s annual awards.

Concept note-4: -ICC Women’s T20I Cricketer of the Year Tahlia McGrath reached the summit of the T20I batter rankings back in December, and despite only having made her debut for Australia in late 2021, her rise to the top has seen plenty of individual and team success.
