Who among the following was conferred with the Bharat Ratna by President Ram Nath Kovind?

(A) K. R. Narayanan

(B) Pratibha Patil

(C) ** Pranab Mukherjee

(D) P. Chidambaram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Prior to his election as President, Mukherjee was Union Finance Minister from 2009 to 2012. He was awarded India’s highest civilian honour, the Bharat Ratna, in 2019, by his successor as president, Ram Nath Kovind. (BA, MA, LL.B.)

Concept note-2: -Q. How many Former Presidents have been awarded “Bharat Ratna” till now? Notes: The following six presidents of India have been awarded Bharat Ratna till date: Sarvepalli Radhakrishnan.

Concept note-3: -The correct answer is Baldev Singh. Baldev Singh is Minister for Development, Government of Punjab, from June 1942 to September 1946. Minister in charge of Defence, Government of India, August 1947 to May 1952; Member, First Lok Sabha,1952-57.
