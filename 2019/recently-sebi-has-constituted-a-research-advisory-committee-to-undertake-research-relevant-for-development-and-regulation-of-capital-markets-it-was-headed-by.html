Recently SEBI has constituted a "Research Advisory" committee to undertake research relevant for development and regulation of capital markets. It was headed by;

(A) Munshi Golam Mustafa

(B) ** Sankar De

(C) Prabhat Mandal

(D) Ashoke Sen

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 20-member committee will be chaired by KV Kamath, Chairperson of National Bank for Financing Infrastructure and Development, an update with the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI) showed.

Concept note-2: -Secondary Market Advisory Committee (SMAC)

Concept note-3: -The committee is chaired by S Sahoo, Professor at National Law University, Delhi, and former chairperson of the Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India (IBBI).

Concept note-4: -The committee aims to measure pertaining to areas such as securities market data access and privacy. Sebi has appointed Professor at National Law University, Delhi and former chairperson, Insolvency and Bankruptcy Board of India S Sahoo as Panel Head.

Concept note-5: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is the Narasimham Committee. The SEBI was given statutory recognition in 1992 on the recommendation of the Narasimham Committee.
