What is the theme of the 5th India International Science Festival?

(A) Science for New India

(B) ** RISEN India-Research, Innovation, and Science Empowering the Nation

(C) Science for Transformation

(D) Science for the masses

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is time to make science a Jan Andolan” . The Science and Technology Minister further informed the gathering that the theme for this year’s festival is RISEN India – Research, Innovation, and Science Empowering the Nation.

Concept note-2: -More than 8,000 delegates from across the country will participate in this science festival from January 21 to 24. The main theme of this festival being held in the Amrit kaal of independence is ‘Country moving towards Amrit kaal with science, technology and innovation’. Dr.

Concept note-3: -Union Minister of State (Independent Charge) Science & Technology, Dr Jitendra Singh inaugurated the seventh edition of India International Science Festival at Panaji, Goa today.

Concept note-4: -The first and second IISF were held in New Delhi, the third in Chennai, the fourth one in Lucknow, fifth in Kolkata, sixth through virtual mode and the last one IISF was held in Goa which witnessed a footfall from over 5 lakh people worldwide.

Concept note-5: -The eighth India International Science Festival is to be held in Bhopal. The Union Minister Jitendra Singh inaugurated the festival. According to the Government of India, the festival is a step in moving towards “AMRIT KAAL”.
