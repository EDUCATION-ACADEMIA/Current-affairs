National Agricultural Higher Education Project (NAHEP) was launched by;

(A) ** ICAR

(B) NABARD

(C) FAO

(D) IFAD

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -NAHEP has been formulated by ICAR with a total cost of US$ 165 million (Rupees 1100 crores at the exchange rate of Rs.

Concept note-2: -ICAR, with the assistance of World Bank and Govt. of India on a 50:50 cost sharing basis, has launched the National Agricultural Higher Education Project (NAHEP).

Concept note-3: -The project thus responds to objectives of Government of India as well as of World Bank, as have been expressed in their main policy documents. The project is planned for 6 years and has been launched on 26 July 2006.

Concept note-4: -The World Bank aided National Agricultural Innovation Project (NAIP) has been conceived to pilot this innovation in conducting agricultural research. To give the agricultural research and technology development system an explicit development and business perspective through innovative models.
