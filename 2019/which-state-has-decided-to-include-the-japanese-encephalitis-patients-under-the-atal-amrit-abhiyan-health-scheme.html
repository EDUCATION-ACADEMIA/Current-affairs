Which state has decided to include the Japanese encephalitis patients under the Atal Amrit Abhiyan health scheme?

(A) Manipur

(B) ** Assam

(C) Sikkim

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atal Amrit Abhiyan is a new health insurance scheme announced by the State Government of Assam.

Concept note-2: -Objective: To address this key vulnerability faced by the BPL population and Low Income households in the State, the Government of Assam has launched the health assurance scheme“Atal Amrit Abhiyan” providing cashless treatment and coverage of critical care targeted at the BPL population and Low Income households.

Concept note-3: -Various diseases like cancer, Cardiovascular diseases, Cancer, Kidney diseases, Neonatal diseases, brain and heart-related ailments and burn injuries have been included in the scheme. The beneficiary can go for the treatment only in government-aided and CGHS empaneled hospitals in the state.

Concept note-4: -Eligibility Criteria for Atal Amrit Abhiyan The scheme is open to all BPL families in Assam with an annual income lesser than ₹1.2 Lakhs.

Concept note-5: -Referral Hospitals inside the State: Baruah Cancer, Institute, Guwahati. GNRC, Guwahati. International Hospital, Guwahati (Nephrology) Sankardev Netralaya, Guahati (Opthalmology)
