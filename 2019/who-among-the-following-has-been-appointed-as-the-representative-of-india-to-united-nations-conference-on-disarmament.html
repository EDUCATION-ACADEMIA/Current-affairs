Who among the following has been appointed as the representative of India to United Nations Conference on Disarmament?

(A) Rahul Srivastava

(B) ** Pankaj Sharma

(C) Nandan Nilekani

(D) Smriti Irani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Anupam Ray. Anupam Ray has been appointed as India’s new permanent representative at UN Conference on Disarmament. Indian Foreign Service officer, Anupam Ray is currently serving as a joint secretary in the MEA’s headquarters in Delhi.

Concept note-2: -India signed the Declaration by United Nations on 1 January 1942 and was represented by Girija Shankar Bajpai who was the Indian Agent-General at the time.

Concept note-3: -India has said that it supports the full and effective implementation of the Chemical Weapons Convention and emphasises the strengthening of the OPCW to fulfill its important mandate.
