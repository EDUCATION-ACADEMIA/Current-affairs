Indira Gandhi International Airport ranks ______ in the world airport traffic rankings for 2018 released by Airports Council International.

(A) 14

(B) 13

(C) ** 12

(D) 15

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Delhi Airport ranked world’s 10th busiest airport: Report.

Concept note-2: -At the World Travel Awards 2022 held in Paris last month, Indira Gandhi International Airport was awarded ‘the best’ and ‘the cleanest’ airport in India/South Asia, and Rajiv Gandhi International Airport bagged the title of the ‘Best Airport Staff in India & South Asia’.

Concept note-3: -NEW DELHI: The capital’s IGI Airport is the world’s seventh busiest airport, according to the latest rankings issued by UK-based air consultancy firm OAG. The six airports that are ahead of IGIA are Atlanta Hartsfield-Jackson, Dubai, Tokyo Haneda, London Heathrow, Dallas Fort Worth and Denver International.
