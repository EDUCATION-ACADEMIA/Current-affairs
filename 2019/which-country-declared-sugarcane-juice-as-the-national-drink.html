Which country declared sugarcane juice as the "national drink"?

(A) Bangladesh

(B) Sri Lanka

(C) ** Pakistan

(D) India

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Government of Pakistan has recently declared sugarcane juice as the ‘national drink’ of the country. The announcement came after the government conducted a Twitter poll where people were asked to decide on the country’s national drink with two other options - orange and carrot - besides sugarcane.

Concept note-2: -Sugar cane production quantity Brazil is the top country by sugar cane production in the world. As of 2021, sugar cane production in Brazil was 715 million tonnes that accounts for 38.48% of the world’s sugar cane production.

Concept note-3: -Government of Pakistan - Sugarcane Juice is National Drink of Pakistan.

Concept note-4: -Sugarcane juice is the national juice of Pakistan, It is called “Roh” in Pakistan.

Concept note-5: -Hello buddy here is your answer :- There has been a consistent demand from the Tea Industry for declaring Tea as ‘national drink’ of India. In August 2012, a parliamentary standing committee on commerce recommended that the beverage be given the status of ‘national drink’.
