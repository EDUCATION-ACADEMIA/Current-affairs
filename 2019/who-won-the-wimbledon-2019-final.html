Who won the Wimbledon 2019 Final?

(A) Andy Murray

(B) ** Novak Djokovic

(C) Roger Federer

(D) Rafael Nadal

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -After 4 hours and 57 minutes, first seed Novak Djokovic defeated second seed Roger Federer in five sets to win the title in a repeat of the 2014 and the 2015 Wimbledon finals. It was the longest Wimbledon final in history, and the last major final of Federer’s career.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 Wimbledon Championships was a Grand Slam tier tennis tournament that took place at the All England Lawn Tennis and Croquet Club in Wimbledon, London, United Kingdom. Novak Djokovic successfully defended his gentlemen’s singles title to claim his 21st major title, defeating Nick Kyrgios in the final.

Concept note-3: -Elena Rybakina defeated Ons Jabeur in the final, 3–6, 6–2, 6–2 to win the ladies’ singles tennis title at the 2022 Wimbledon Championships. It was her first major singles title.
