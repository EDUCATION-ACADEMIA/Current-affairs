Government has approved ____ interest rate on deposits in Employees Provident Fund for 2018-19.

(A) 7.2%

(B) 7.8%

(C) ** 8.65%

(D) 8.42%

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -In March 2020, the EPFO had lowered the interest rate on provident fund deposits to a seven-year low of 8.5% for 2019-20, from 8.65% in 2018-19.

Concept note-2: -Revised EPF Interest Rate for 2019-20 : Lowered by 0.15% Earlier in the year 2016-17 and 2018-19, the EPFO had given an 8.65% rate of interest to the subscribers.

Concept note-3: -8.1% EPF interest rate approved for 2021-2022, amount to be credit soon; Here’s how to check EPF balance.

Concept note-4: -It should be noted that the current proposed PF Interest Rate for FY 2021-22 at 8.1% is the lowest of the interest rates paid by EPFO since 1977-78, i.e. at 8.5% (FY 2019-20 and 2020-21), 8.65% (FY 2018-19), 8.55% (FY 2017-18), 8.65% (FY 2016-17), and so on.

Concept note-5: -Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) interest rate has been set at 8.1 per cent for 2021-22.
