Which armed force has launched a website “RODRA” to address veterans issues?

(A) Indian Coast Guard

(B) Indian Air Force

(C) Indian Navy

(D) ** Indian Army

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Army launched an online platform, Retired Officers Digital Records Archive (RODRA), https://rodra.gov.in with an aim to have a better connection with armed forces veteran officers and family pensioners.

Concept note-2: -RODRA (Retired Officers Digital Records Archive) website hosted on civil internet is an online interface between Officers Record Office (ORO)/MP 7) and the veteran officers & their NOK/dependents.

Concept note-3: -It will enable us to connect with the veteran officers and family pensioners and address their grievances related to pension in a better manner and also update them on the relevant policies.

Concept note-4: -GENERAL MANOJ PANDE, PVSM, AVSM, VSM, ADC.

Concept note-5: -Please send whatsapp message at mobile No 83680 51743 or contact at 011-2619 5669 & 2086 3044 or email the grievance on email ID (offr.recordgov.in).
