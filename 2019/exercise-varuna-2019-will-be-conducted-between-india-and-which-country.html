(B) Germany

(C) ** France

Concept note-2: -Varuna 2023: India-France conclude joint naval exercise in Goa.

Concept note-3: -In April 2022, the French and Indian navies held the five-day 20th edition of bilateral naval exercise, Varuna 2022 in the Arabian Sea. It was held from 30 March to 3 April 2022.
