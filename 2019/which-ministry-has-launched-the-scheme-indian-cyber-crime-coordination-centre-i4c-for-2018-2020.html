Which ministry has launched the scheme ‘Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C)’ for 2018-2020?

(A) Ministry of Corporate Affairs

(B) Ministry of Human Resource Development

(C) Ministry of External Affairs

(D) ** Ministry of Home Affairs

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: - Ministry of Home Affairs.

Concept note-2: -Overview about the I4C Scheme. Outlay of Rs. 415.86 Crore. To act as a nodal point in the fight against cybercrime. Identify the research problems/needs of LEAs and take up R&D activities in developing new technologies and forensic tools in collaboration with academia / research institutes within India and abroad.

Concept note-3: -Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C) has been established under Ministry of Home affairs (MHA) to act as a nodal point at National level in the fight against cybercrime.

Concept note-4: -Although NCCC is jurisdictionally under the Ministry of Home Affairs, it coordinates with multiple security and surveillance agencies as well as with CERT-In of the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology.
