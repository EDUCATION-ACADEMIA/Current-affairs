The Central government has asked the state transport departments to make it mandatory for all EVs to use the ______ number plates.

(A) Yellow

(B) ** Green

(C) White

(D) None of the Above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Delhi. The Delhi Government announced all its departments would use only electric vehicles, transitioning over 2000 cars to EVs.

Concept note-2: -All Government vehicles to be converted to electric vehicles by 2030. After 2025, only electric vehicles will be allowed to purchase. Additionally, it also aims to phase out all fossil fuel based commercial fleets and logistics vehicles in all cities by 2030.

Concept note-3: -For electric 2-wheelers, the first 100,000 units to be sold would qualify for subsidies – an incentive of ₹5,000 per kWh of battery capacity – a maximum of ₹10,000. There was an early bird incentive of up to ₹15,000 for an electric 2-wheeler with a 3 kWh battery until the end of 2021.

Concept note-4: -The government has stated that it intends to grow electric vehicle (EV) sales in India to capture the markets of 30% of private cars, 70% of commercial vehicles and 80% of two and three-wheelers by 2030.
