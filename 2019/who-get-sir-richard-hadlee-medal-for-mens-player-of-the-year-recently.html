Who get Sir Richard Hadlee Medal for Men’s Player of the Year, recently?

(A) Yusuf Pathan

(B) ** Kane Williamson

(C) Rishabh Pant

(D) David Warner

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Southee is New Zealand’s third-highest wicket-taker in Tests, 23 wickets behind Daniel Vettori and 93 short of the record-holder the award is named after, Sir Richard Hadlee.

Concept note-2: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is New Zealand. New Zealand cricket team captain Kane Williamson has been awarded the Sir Richard Hadlee Medal for the fourth time in six years.

Concept note-3: -The four awards presented in cricket are Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna, Arjuna Award, Dhyan Chand Award and Dronacharya Award.
