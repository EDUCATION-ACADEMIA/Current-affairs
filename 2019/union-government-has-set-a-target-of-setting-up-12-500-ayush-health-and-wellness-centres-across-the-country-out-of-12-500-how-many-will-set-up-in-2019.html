Union Government has set a target of setting up 12, 500 Ayush Health and Wellness Centres across the country. Out of 12, 500, How many will set up in 2019?

(A) 5000 Centres

(B) ** 4000 Centres

(C) 6610 Centres

(D) 8200 Centres

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2. How many AYUSH HWCs are to be established in the country? As per the decision taken on 30.01. 2019 by the Government of India, 10% (12,500) of the total Health and Wellness centres (HWCs) targeted under Ayushman Bharat Scheme would be developed by Ministry of AYUSH in a phased manner from year 2019-20 to 2023-24.

Concept note-2: -RECENT INITIATIVES UNDER NPCDCS AYUSH facilities and methodologies are being integrated with NPCDCS services for prevention and management of common NCDs, wherein the practice of Yoga is an integral part of the intervention.

Concept note-3: -Ayushman Bharat (AB) is an attempt to move from a selective approach to health care to deliver comprehensive range of services spanning preventive, promotive, curative, rehabilitative and palliative care. It has two components which are complementary to each other.

Concept note-4: -Objective. The main objectives are to establish a holistic wellness model based on AYUSH principles and practices, to empower masses for ‘self-care’ to reduce the disease burden, out of pocket expenditure and to provide informed choice to the needy public.

Concept note-5: -These 14 tests can be done using Rapid Diagnostic Kits (RDKs) and the DoMHFW/NHM has already released Rs. 30,000-/- per HWC Sub Centers to the districts to ensure that these 14 tests are available within the HWC Sub Centers itself. The manpower required to conduct these 14 tests is available in the HWC Sub Centers.
