The flagship initiative Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) will be extended by two more years. In which year the mission was launched?

(A) 2017

(B) 2016

(C) ** 2015

(D) 2014

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT), first focused national water Mission was launched in June 2015 to facilitate ease of living to citizens in 500 cities by providing tap connections and sewer connections.

Concept note-2: -Atal Mission for Rejuvenation and Urban Transformation (AMRUT) was launched in June 2015 by PM Narendra Modi under the Government of India. The AMRUT scheme is an initiative to provide basic civic amenities to the urban areas to improve the quality of life with major focus to the poor and the disadvantaged.

Concept note-3: -The components of the AMRUT consist of capacity building, reform implementation, water supply, sewerage and septage management, storm water drainage, urban transport and development of green spaces and parks.
