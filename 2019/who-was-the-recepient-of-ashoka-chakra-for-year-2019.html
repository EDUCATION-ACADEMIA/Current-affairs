Who was the recepient of Ashoka Chakra for year 2019?

(A) Hangpan Dada

(B) Mohan Goswami

(C) Jyoti Prakash Nirala

(D) ** Nazir Ahmad Wani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Lance Naik Nazir Ahmad Wani, AC, SM & Bar was an Indian soldier in the Jammu and Kashmir Light Infantry of the Indian Army. He was posthumously awarded the Ashoka Chakra, India’s highest peacetime military decoration, in January 2019.

Concept note-2: -The President of India posthumously awarded the Ashok Chakra to ASI Babu Ram of the Jammu and Kashmir Police for his excellence, bravery, devotion to duty and dedication. ASI Baburam’s wife Reena Rani and son Manik were presented with the award by the President of India on Republic Day 2022.

Concept note-3: -His courage and leadership inspired his men to press on the attack even after he succumbed to his injuries. For this act of most conspicuous gallantry, leadership and exceptional devotion to duty, Lt Ram Prakash Roperia was awarded the highest gallantry award in peace ‘Ashoka Chakra’ posthumously.

Concept note-4: -Conditions of Eligibility: The Chakra is awarded for most conspicuous bravery or some act of daring or pre-eminent act of valour or self-sacrifice otherwise than in the face of the enemy.
