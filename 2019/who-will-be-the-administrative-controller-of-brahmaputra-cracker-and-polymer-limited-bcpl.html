Who will be the administrative controller of Brahmaputra Cracker and Polymer Limited (BCPL)?

(A) Department of Chemicals & Petrochemicals

(B) Union Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilisers

(C) ** Union Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas

(D) All of the above

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Cabinet Committee on Economic Affairs approved the transfer of administrative control of BCPL from Ministry of Chemicals and Fertilizers to Ministry of Petroleum & Natural Gas.

Concept note-2: -Shri Reep Hazarika, Managing Director, BCPL welcomed the Honâ€™ble minister with a gamocha & Seleng chador .

Concept note-3: -The National Flag was also hoisted by Shri Vijay Kumar Pal , Chief Operating Officer BCPL at Administrative Building BCPL.

Concept note-4: -Brahmaputra Cracker and Polymer Limited (BCPL) has set up a Petrochemical Complex at Lepetkata. The complex is spread over 3000 Bighas of land at Lepetkata and is situated approximately 15 Kilometres away from the Tea City Dibrugarh, Assam.

Concept note-5: -The Feedstock Subsidy to BCPL is a Central Sector Scheme of Ministry of Petroleum and Natural Gas. The yearly subsidy is a function of Polymer price, Gas price and Naphtha price while maintaining minimum post tax IRR of 10%. The methodology for computing yearly subsidy has been approved by the CCEA.
