Sharda corridor plan, which is in news recently, is related to which of the following countries?

(A) Nepal

(B) ** Pakistan

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Myanmar

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Sharda corridor plan, which is in news recently, is related to which of the following countries? Notes: There was a proposal of the Pakistan government to establish a corridor that will allow Hindu pilgrims from India to visit Sharda Peeth, an ancient Hindu temple and cultural site in Pakistan-occupied Kashmir (PoK).

Concept note-2: -The crossing allows devotees from India to visit the gurdwara in Kartarpur, Pakistan, 4.7 kilometres (2.9 miles) from the India–Pakistan border on the Pakistani side without a visa.

Concept note-3: -Sharda (Urdu: ), also known as Shardi, is a small Tehsil in Neelam District in Azad Kashmir, Pakistan. It is one of the two tehsils of Neelum district, and is located on the banks of the Neelum river at an altitude of 1,981 metres (6,499 ft).

Concept note-4: -Sharada Peeth is a ruined Hindu temple and ancient centre of learning located in the Neelum Valley of Pakistani-administered Kashmir. Between the 6th and 12th centuries CE, it was among the most prominent temple universities in the Indian subcontinent.
