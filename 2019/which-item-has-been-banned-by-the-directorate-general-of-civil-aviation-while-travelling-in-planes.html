Which item has been banned by the Directorate General of Civil Aviation while travelling in planes?

(A) MI Phone

(B) Samsung Charger

(C) Lenovo Laptop

(D) ** Apple Mac Book

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Liquids, Aerosols, Gels and Powders.

Concept note-2: -2.3 Prohibited Items A list of items i.e. weapons, explosives or other dangerous devices, articles or substances which may be used to commit an act of unlawful interference with Civil Aviation operations.

Concept note-3: -The Directorate General of Civil Aviation (DGCA) is the regulatory body in the field of Civil Aviation, primarily dealing with safety issues. It is responsible for regulation of air transport services to/from/within India and for enforcement of civil air regulations, air safety, and airworthiness standards.
