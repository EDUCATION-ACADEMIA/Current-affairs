The 52nd Boori Boot Yollo festival 2019 is celebrated by which tribe of Arunachal Pradesh?

(A) Singhpo

(B) Mishmi

(C) ** Nyishi

(D) Apatani

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Today at Dollungmukh during celebration of Boori-Boot festival celebrated by Nyishi tribe.

Concept note-2: -Boori-Boot Yullo is a festival celebrated by the Nyishi tribe of Arunachal Pradesh.

Concept note-3: -Boori Boot means getting together to welcome the spring and successful harvest. It is a 3-day festival celebrated by the Hill-Miris of Nyishi community in the month of February. During the festival, people invoke God to bless them with prosperity and seek protection from any kind of disease.

Concept note-4: -Nyokum is a festival celebrated by the Nyishi tribe of the Indian state of Arunachal Pradesh. The Word Nyokum has been derived from the combination of two words – Nyok means land (earth) and Kum means collectiveness or togetherness.

Concept note-5: -Rikham Pada the Folk Dance of the Nyishi Tribe They (Rikham Pada & Rinyam Yami) were attire with beautiful traditional dresses which adding up their handsomeness, the most beautiful girls of the universe were after them.
