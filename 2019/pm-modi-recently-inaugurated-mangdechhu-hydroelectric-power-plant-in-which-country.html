PM Modi recently inaugurated Mangdechhu hydroelectric power plant in which country?

(A) Nepal

(B) ** Bhutan

(C) Sri Lanka

(D) Singapore

Concept note-1: -Prime Minister Narendra Modi had inaugurated the 720 MW project. The run-of-river power plant, built on the Mangdechhu River in Trongsa Dzongkhag district of central Bhutan at a cost of Rs 4,500-crore, is seen as a symbol of friendship between the two countries.
