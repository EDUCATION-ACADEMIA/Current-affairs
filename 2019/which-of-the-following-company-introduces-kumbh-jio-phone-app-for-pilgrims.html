Which of the following company introduces Kumbh Jio Phone app for pilgrims?

(A) Paytm

(B) BSNL

(C) Bharati Airtel

(D) ** Reliance Jio

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Reliance Jio launches Kumbh app for Kumbh mela: Check out the features. It said the Kumbh is the largest human congregation in the world, with over 130 million pilgrims taking a holy dip over 55 days.

Concept note-2: -Reliance Jio Infocomm Limited, doing business as Jio, is an Indian telecommunications company and a subsidiary of Jio Platforms, headquartered in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra, India. It operates a national LTE network with coverage across all 22 telecom circles.

Concept note-3: -Mukesh Ambani will continue to be the Chairman of Jio Platforms Ltd., which owns all Jio digital services brands, including Reliance Jio Infocomm.

Concept note-4: -Jio partners Ericsson, Nokia, Samsung, Cisco for 5G network; Google for ultra-affordable 5G smartphone.

Concept note-5: -With a 30% market share, Reliance JioPhone became the leading feature phone brand in India in the first quarter of 2019, Counterpoint Research said on Friday.
