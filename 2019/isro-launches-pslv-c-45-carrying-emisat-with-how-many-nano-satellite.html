ISRO Launches PSLV C-45 Carrying EMISAT with how many Nano satellite?

(A) ** 28

(B) 12

(C) 25

(D) 32

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PSLV-C45 successfully launches EMISAT and 28 customer satellites.

Concept note-2: -India’s PSLV-C47 successfully launched Cartosat-3 and 13 commercial nanosatellites from Satish Dhawan Space Centre (SDSC) SHAR, Sriharikota.

Concept note-3: -Launch. Oceansat-2 was launched from Satish Dhawan Space Centre, First Launch Pad (FLP) on 23 September 2009, at 06:21 UTC using the Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle (PSLV-C14). India successfully launched its 16th remote-sensing satellite Oceansat-2 and six nano European satellites in 1200 seconds.

Concept note-4: -PSLV-C51/Amazonia-1. India’s Polar Satellite Launch Vehicle PSLV-C51 successfully launched Amazonia-1 along with 18 co-passenger satellites today (February 28, 2021) from Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR, Sriharikota.

Concept note-5: -The PSLV-C45 lifted off at 09:27 Hrs (IST) on April 01, 2019 from the second launch pad of Satish Dhawan Space Centre SHAR, Sriharikota in its 47th flight.
