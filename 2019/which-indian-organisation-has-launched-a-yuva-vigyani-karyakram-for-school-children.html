Which Indian organisation has launched a ’Yuva Vigyani Karyakram’ for school children?

(A) ** ISRO

(B) CSIR

(C) DRDO

(D) BARC

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Indian Space Research Organisation is organising a special programme for School Children called “Young Scientist Programme” “YUva VIgyani KAryakram” , YUVIKA, to impart basic knowledge on Space Technology, Space Science and Space Applications to the younger students with a preference to rural areas.

Concept note-2: -Which organization recently (in March’22) selected 150 school students for the “YUva VIgyani KAryakram” (YUVIKA) programme? Explanation: A special programme for school students “YUva VIgyani KAryakram” (YUVIKA) or “Young Scientist Programme” is organized by the Indian Space Research Organization (ISRO).

Concept note-3: -Dr K Sivan, Chairman, ISRO inaugurated the Young Scientist Programme (Yuvika-2019) through video mode from ISRO Headquarters, Bengaluru on May 13, 2019. A total of 110 students representing every State and Union Territory of India are participating in this programme.

Concept note-4: -YUVIKA 2022: ISRO launches young scientist programme for Class 11 students. Interested candidates must note that YUVIKA - 2022 residential programme will be of two weeks duration during the summer holidays (May 16, 2022, to May 28, 2022).
