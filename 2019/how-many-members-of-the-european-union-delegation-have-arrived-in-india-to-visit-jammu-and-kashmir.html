How many members of the European Union delegation have arrived in India to visit Jammu and Kashmir?

(A) 26

(B) ** 27

(C) 36

(D) 24

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The 45 standing delegations The European Parliament currently has 45 ‘standing’ - in other words, permanent - delegations.

Concept note-2: -A tour of inspection by Aurangzeb (1664–65) gave Bernier the opportunity to describe Kashmir, the first and for a long time the only European to do so.

Concept note-3: -Article 370 acknowledges the special status of the state of Jammu and Kashmir in terms of autonomy and its ability to formulate laws for the state’s permanent residents.

Concept note-4: -As of August 2021, there have been 105 amendments of the Constitution of India since it was first enacted in 1950. Hence, the Indian Home Minister moved a resolution in the Rajya Sabha to give the President the necessary recommendation he needs to declare Article 370 as inoperative.
