Which of the following cricket teams has clinched the 2019 Ranji Trophy?

(A) ** Vidharbha

(B) Saurashtra

(C) Bengal

(D) Railways

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The correct answer is Saurashtra . Saurashtra was the winner of Ranji Trophy 2019-20 which was fought against Vidarbha .

Concept note-2: -Vidarbha were the defending champions. The final took place between Vidarbha and Saurashtra, starting on 3 February 2019. Vidarbha defeated Saurashtra by 78 runs in the final, to become the sixth team in the tournament’s history to retain their title.

Concept note-3: -Detailed Solution. The correct answer is Saurashtra.

Concept note-4: -Mumbai (Bombay) have won the tournament the most times with 41 wins including 15 back-to-back wins from 1958–59 to 1972–73.

Concept note-5: -In the final, Madhya Pradesh beat Mumbai by six wickets to win their first Ranji Trophy title.
