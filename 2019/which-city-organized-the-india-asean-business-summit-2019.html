Which city organized the ‘India-ASEAN Business Summit 2019’?

(A) ** New Delhi

(B) Chennai

(C) Faridabad

(D) Gurugram

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -1. The 16th ASEAN-India Summit was held on 3 November 2019 in Bangkok, Kingdom of Thailand.

Concept note-2: -The 19th ASEAN-India Summit to Commemorate the 30th Anniversary of ASEAN-India Dialogue Relations was held in Phnom Penh, Kingdom of Cambodia, on 12 November 2022.

Concept note-3: -ASEAN Business and Investment Summit 2019 (ABIS2019) under the theme “Empowering ASEAN 4.0.” H.E. General Prayut Chan-o-cha (Ret.), Prime Minister of the Kingdom of Thailand, presided over the Closing Ceremony of the 35th ASEAN Summit and Related Summits.

Concept note-4: -convened the First ASEAN-India Summit in Cambodia in accordance with the decision of the ASEAN Leaders at the 7th ASEAN Summit in Brunei Darussalam in November 2001.

Concept note-5: -This year’s Association of Southeast Asian Nations (ASEAN) Summit hosted by Cambodia from 10-13 November 2022, in Phnom Penh was the first in-person meeting held after the outbreak of the COVID-19 pandemic in 2020.
