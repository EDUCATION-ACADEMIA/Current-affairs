Who will chair the 24th Meeting of Western Zonal Council at Panaji?

(A) Shri Ram Nath Kovind

(B) Shri Rajnath Singh

(C) ** Shri Amit Shah

(D) Shri Narendra Modi

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Chairman - The Union Home Minister is the Chairman of each of these Councils.

Concept note-2: -The Western Zonal Council in the 25th meeting at Diu on 11.6. 2022 and its Standing Committee in its 12th meeting at Panaji on 28.01. 2022 has discussed over a total of 36 issues.

Concept note-3: -The Western Zonal Council, comprising the States of Goa, Gujarat, Maharashtra and the Union Territories of Daman & Diu and Dadra & Nagar Haveli; The Southern Zonal Council, comprising the States of Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Kerala, Tamil Nadu and the Union Territory of Puducherry.
