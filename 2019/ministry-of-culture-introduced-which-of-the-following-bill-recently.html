Ministry of culture introduced which of the following bill recently?

(A) Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial Bill 2019

(B) Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial Bill 2018

(C) ** Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial (Amendment) Bill 2019

(D) Jallianwala Bagh National Memorial (Amendment) Bill, 2018

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -2022. Following this, in relation to the state of Karnataka, the Constitution (Scheduled Tribes) Order (Fourth Amendment) Bill, 2022, was also passed unanimously in Rajya Sabha on 22.12.

Concept note-2: -What is the latest amendment in the Indian Constitution? 105th Amendment Act of 2021 brought the latest amendment in the Constitution of India. The Constitution (One Hundred and Fifth Amendment) Act, 2021 is designed to clarify that the states can maintain the “state list” of OBCs.

Concept note-3: -104th amendment of the Indian Constitution extended the deadline for the cessation of seats for SCs and STs in the Lok Sabha and states assemblies from Seventy years to Eighty. It removed the reserved seats for the Anglo-Indian community in the Lok Sabha and state assemblies.
