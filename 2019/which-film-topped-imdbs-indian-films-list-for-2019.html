Which film topped IMDb’s Indian films list for 2019?

(A) Uri:The Surgical Strike

(B) Gully Boy

(C) Article 15

(D) ** Peranbu

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -This year, Peranbu takes the first slot in the Top 10 Indian movies. While Uri: The Surgical Strike got the second place, Gully Boy and Article 15 bagged the third and fourth places. Chhichhore, Super 30, Badla, The Tashkent Files, Kesari and Lucifer are the other films that made it to the list.”

Concept note-2: -As per the latest Box Office Report from media consulting firm Ormax Media, the gross BO across languages stood at Rs 10,948 crore in 2019. In net terms, the growth was higher, at 18.7% to Rs 9,490 crore, as the GST rates were reduced from 28% and 18% to 18% and 12%, respectively.
