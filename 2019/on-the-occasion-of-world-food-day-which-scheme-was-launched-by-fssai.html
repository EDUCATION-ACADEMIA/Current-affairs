On the occasion of World Food Day, which scheme was launched by FSSAI?

(A) Food Quality Scheme

(B) Health and Food Relation Scheme

(C) Food Supply Gram

(D) ** Food Safety Mitra

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -On the occasion of World Food Safety Day, Union Health Minister Mansukh Mandaviya released the 4th State Food Safety Index.

Concept note-2: -Important Points To promote safe and healthy food, FSSAI has launched a training toolkit for health under the scheme Ayushman Bharat.

Concept note-3: -On the occasion of World Food Day, Union Minister for Health and Family Welfare Dr Harsh Vardhan launched Food Safety Mitra Scheme, Eat Right Jacket and Eat Right Jhola.

Concept note-4: -World Food Day is observed annually on 16 October to highlight the millions of people worldwide who cannot afford a healthy diet and the need for regular access to nutritious food. The theme for 2022 is Leave NO ONE behind.

Concept note-5: -In order to strengthen food safety administration, FSSAI launched ‘Food Safety Mitra (FSMs)’ scheme, Eat Right Jacket, a training course for domestic workers and more.
