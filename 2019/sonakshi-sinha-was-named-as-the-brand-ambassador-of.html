Sonakshi Sinha was named as the Brand Ambassador of;

(A) FanMojo

(B) ** CavinKare

(C) Dabur

(D) Emami

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Female western wear brand FableStreet has announced Sonakshi Sinha, Huma Qureshi and Srinidhi Shetty as its brand ambassadors. The brand has launched its latest campaign #FitsLikeNothingElse with the famous names.

Concept note-2: -CavinKare revamps national haircare brand Nyle Naturals; ropes in Erica Fernandes as brand ambassador.

Concept note-3: -Sonakshi Sinha has been signed as brand ambassador of Riyo Herbs Skincare brand Riyo Herbs has signed on Indian actress Sonakshi Sinha as the brand ambassador for its skincare segment. As part of this partnership, Sinha will be responsible for the brand’s advertising and marketing campaigns.

Concept note-4: -FMCG giant CavinKare has announced Bollywood actor Sonakshi Sinha as the brand ambassador for its shampoo brand, Chik, with an aim to further bolster its leadership in the Indian shampoo segment. The campaign will go on air next week, the company said in a statement.

Concept note-5: -Sonakshi Sinha made her foray into Bollywood with one of the biggest hits of 2010, Dabangg. The actress couldn‘t have asked for a better start to her career than being cast alongside the superstar Salman Khan in her debut movie. Her demure character as the village belle won her the Filmfare Award for Best Female Debut.
