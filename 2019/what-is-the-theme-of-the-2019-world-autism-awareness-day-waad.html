What is the theme of the 2019 World Autism Awareness Day (WAAD)?

(A) Toward Autonomy and Self-Determination

(B) ** Assistive Technologies, Active Participation

(C) Employment: The Autism Advantage

(D) Opening Doors to Inclusive Education

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -’Assistive Technologies. Active Participation’ is the theme for WAAD 2019.

Concept note-2: -The 2022 World Autism Awareness Day observance The theme of inclusive education is intrinsically linked with the focus of last year’s WAAD observance, “Inclusion in the Workplace”.

Concept note-3: -Every April Autism Speaks celebrates World Autism Month, beginning with the United Nations-sanctioned World Autism Awareness Day on April 2.

Concept note-4: -The United Nations General Assembly unanimously declared 2 April as World Autism Awareness Day (A/RES/62/139) to highlight the need to help improve the quality of life of those with autism so they can lead full and meaningful lives as an integral part of society.

Concept note-5: -This day is observed on April 2 ever year. What is the theme of World Autism Awareness Day? The theme of World Autism Awareness Day was “Inclusive Quality Education for All” in 2022.
