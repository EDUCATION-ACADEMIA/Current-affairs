India delivered 2 Mi-24 helicopters to which of the following country?

(A) Kazakhstan

(B) ** Afghanistan

(C) Bangladesh

(D) North Korea

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -It is estimated that by 2022 around 900 of Mi-24 and Mi-35 seriers helicopters remain in service. The Russian Army being the most significant operator with around 700 helicopters. Though actual numbers of operational helicopters are unclear due to the Russian losses in Ukraine.

Concept note-2: -The Mi-24 is in service with Russia and countries of the ex-Soviet Union and has been exported to Afghanistan, Algeria, Angola, Bulgaria, Cuba, Czech Republic, East Germany, Hungary, India, Indonesia, Iraq, the Ivory Coast, Libya, Mozambique, Nicaragua, North Korea, Peru, Poland, Vietnam and South Yemen.

Concept note-3: -Indian Air Force operates 22 Apache attack helicopters and 15 Chinook heavy-lift helicopters procured from Boeing through the Foreign Military Sales programme of the U.S. government under a $3 billion deal in September 2015.
