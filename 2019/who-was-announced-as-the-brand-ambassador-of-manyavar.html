Who was announced as the brand ambassador of Manyavar?

(A) ** Ranveer Singh

(B) Amir Khan

(C) Vicky Kaushal

(D) Rajkumar Rao

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The campaign has been conceptualised and executed by Shreyansh Innovations. Representative image (iStock) Manyavar has launched a new campaign featuring brand ambassador Ranveer Singh.

Concept note-2: -Kartik Aaryan, Manyavar’s Brand Ambassador turns up as the groom in their new Ad. India’s leading celebration wear brand, Manyavar, has launched a new ad film under the ‘Naye Rishte Naye Vaade campaign featuring the Bollywood heart throb Kartik Aaryan.

Concept note-3: -Ravi Modi started Vedant Fashions in 2002 in Kolkata, West Bengal, to manufacture traditional Indian garments. The brands under the company include Manyavar, Manthan, Twamev, Mebav and Mohey. Ravi Modi, the 45-year-old founder of Vedant Fashions, is the 50th richest person in India.

Concept note-4: -Manyavar-YAARON KE NAKHRE. And when you finally do go, remember to go in style, in a Manyavar.
