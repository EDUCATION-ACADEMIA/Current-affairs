World Malaria Day is observed on April 25. The theme of 2019 World Malaria Day is;

(A) Invest in the future: defeat malaria

(B) ** Zero malaria starts with me

(C) Ready to beat malaria

(D) End Malaria For Good

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Each World Malaria Day focuses on a specific theme. Current and past themes include the following: World Malaria Day 2022: “Harness innovation to reduce the malaria disease burden and save lives.” World Malaria Day 2019-2020-2021: “Zero malaria starts with me"

Concept note-2: -World Malaria Day 2022 will be marked under the theme “Harness innovation to reduce the malaria disease burden and save lives.” No single tool that is available today will solve the problem of malaria.

Concept note-3: -World Malaria Day, annual observance held on April 25 to raise awareness of the global effort to control and ultimately eradicate malaria. World Malaria Day, which was first held in 2008, developed from Africa Malaria Day, an event that had been observed since 2001 by African governments.

Concept note-4: -Each year on April 25, World Malaria Day (WMD) is commemorated.
