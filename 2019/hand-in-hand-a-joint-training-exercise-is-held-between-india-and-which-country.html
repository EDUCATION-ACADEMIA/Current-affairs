Hand-in-Hand, a joint training exercise is held between India and which country?

(A) Japan

(B) Indonesia

(C) Nepal

(D) ** China

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Hand In Hand Exercise - Facts about Exercise Hand-in-Hand for UPSC. Hand in Hand exercise is India-China joint military exercise. The exercise takes place between the armies of both countries.

Concept note-2: -IAS Exam Latest Updates The eighth round of the India-China joint training exercise ‘Hand-in-Hand-2019’ began on December 7, 2019, at the Joint Training Node in Umroi, Meghalaya. The joint military exercise “Hand-in-Hand” began in Kunming, China in 2007, and its second edition took place in Belgaum, Karnataka, India.

Concept note-3: -Exercise Yudh Abhyas is conducted annually between India and USA with the aim of exchanging best practices, Tactics, Techniques and Procedures between the Armies of the two nations. The previous edition of the exercise was conducted at Joint Base Elmendorf Richardson, Alaska (USA) in October 2021.

Concept note-4: -Hand-in-Hand is an annual exercise that takes place as part of India’s and China’s military diplomacy and interaction. The exercise will include tactical operations in an UN-mandated multinational counterinsurgency/counterterrorism environment.
