Which country has concluded a road map on Cyber security and Digital Technology with India recently?

(A) Russia

(B) ** France

(C) United Arab Emirates

(D) Bahrain

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Netherlands has invited India to the first global conference for creating a roadmap on responsible use of artificial intelligence (AI) in the military domain even as the two sides scale up cooperation on shared challenges in cyber-security, the top Dutch diplomat for cyber issues has said.

Concept note-2: -India is placed at third position among the first 20 countries which are affected by cybercrime, according to the FBI’s Internet Crime Report.

Concept note-3: -Infinity (India-France Innovation in Information Technology) is a new platform and conference organized by the Embassy of India in Paris to connect the French and Indian ecosystems to promote cooperation and highlight cross-border opportunities between the two countries.

Concept note-4: -Cyber Security – Growing Career Opportunity The demand for cybersecurity is at an all-time high as the global business environment shifts to cloud data storage and online management. With increased internet exposure, commercial organisational data and the personal data of users are at a threat of being misused.

Concept note-5: -According to Research and Market analysis, the Indian cybersecurity market is expected to develop at a CAGR of 8.05% and is projected to reach $3543.37 million by 2027. In fact, due to the increase in the importance of cybersecurity, more specialized roles are emerging.
