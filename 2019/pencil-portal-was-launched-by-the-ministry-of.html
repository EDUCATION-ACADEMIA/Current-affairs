PENCIL Portal was launched by the ministry of;

(A) ** Ministry of Labour & Employment

(B) Ministry of Health and Family Welfare

(C) Ministry of Home Affairs

(D) Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -PENCIL is an electronic platform developed by the Ministry of Labour and Employment for effective enforcement of no child labour policy. It was launched in 2017.

Concept note-2: -In this regard, to further add impetus to establish a child labour free nation, the Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour (PENCIL) had been launched by the Ministry of Labour and Employment, Government of India.

Concept note-3: -The Ministry of Labour and Employment launched Platform has launched the Platform for Effective Enforcement for No Child Labour (PENCiL) portal to rehabilitate child labour in the country.

Concept note-4: -Under the NCLP scheme, the children in the age group of 9-14 years are rescued / withdrawn from work and enrolled in the NCLP Special Training Centres (STCs), where they are provided with bridge education, vocational training, mid-day meal, stipend, health care, etc.

Concept note-5: -NCLP Scheme has been appraised for its continuation beyond 31st March 2021 in terms of relevant instructions of Ministry of Finance and it has been decided to subsume / merge the scheme with Samagara Shiksha Abhiyan (SSA) Scheme in phased manner as under:- Project.
