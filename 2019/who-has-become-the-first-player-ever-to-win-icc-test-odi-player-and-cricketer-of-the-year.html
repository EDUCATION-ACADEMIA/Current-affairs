Who has become the first player-ever to win ICC Test, ODI Player And Cricketer Of The Year?

(A) ** Virat Kohli

(B) Kane Williamson

(C) Kumar Dharmasena

(D) M S Dhoni

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -There were match-winning knocks, spell-binding stroke-play and memorable moments from a personal perspective and his team in 2022 as Pakistan skipper Babar Azam claimed the ICC Men’s ODI Cricketer of the Year Award for a second consecutive year.

Concept note-2: -Kohli first player to win all three top ICC awards in single year.

Concept note-3: -There are only two winners of the ICC Men’s T20I Cricketer of the Year: Mohammad Rizwan in 2021 and Suryakumar Yadav in 2022.

Concept note-4: -Babar Azam wins Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Men’s Cricketer of the Year. The International Cricket Council (ICC) today unveiled the final list of winners celebrated in the individual categories of the ICC Awards 2022.
