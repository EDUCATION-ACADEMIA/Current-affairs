Which railway zone has won the “ Best innovation award” of Indian Railways 2018-19 for its strategy ‘Plan Bee’?

(A) East Coast Railway zone

(B) South East Central Railway zone

(C) ** Northeast Frontier Railway

(D) North Eastern Railway zone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Plan Bee is an amplifying system that imitates the buzz of a swarm of honey bees to keep wild elephants away from railway tracks. This strategy adopted by Northeast Frontier Railway (NFR) has won the ‘Best Innovation Award’ in Indian Railways for the 2018-19 fiscal.

Concept note-2: -The South Coast Railway zone is the 18th railway zone of the Indian railway which was introduced in the year 2019 by Piyush Goyal, the railway minister during the period. The zone has Guntur, Guntakal, Vijayawada, and Waltair divisions under it and is headquartered in Visakhapatnam.
