Name the Scheme launched by Ministry of Women and Child Development(WCD) for the rehabilitation of women in difficult circumstances.

(A) Sabla

(B) Rashtriya Mahila Kosh

(C) ** Swadhar Greh Scheme

(D) Pradhan Mantri Matritva Vandana Yojana

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Another scheme with the similar objectives namely Swadhar –A Scheme for Women in Difficult Circumstances was launched by the Department of Women and Child Development in 2001- 02.

Concept note-2: -Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Scheme.

Concept note-3: -Schemes that were introduced under the&nbsp;Beti Bachao Beti Padhao Scheme&nbsp;includes Ladli scheme, Kanyashree Prakalpa Yojana, Sukanya Samridhi Yojana, Balika Samridhi Yojana, Ladli Laxmi Yojana, Dhanalakshmi scheme and many more.

Concept note-4: -2. What are the schemes for women’s empowerment in India? Several government schemes for women, including Beti Bachao Beti Padhao, Mahila E-Haat, UJJAWAL, and many other initiatives, are launched to focus on women empowerment.
