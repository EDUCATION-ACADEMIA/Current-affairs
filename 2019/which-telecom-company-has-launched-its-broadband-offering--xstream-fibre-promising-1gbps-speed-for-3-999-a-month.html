Which telecom company has launched its broadband offering, ‘ ____ Xstream Fibre’ promising 1Gbps speed for? 3, 999 a month?

(A) Jio

(B) ** Airtel

(C) BSNL

(D) Vodafone

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The internet speed this pack assures is 1Gbps. The other benefits of this pack include unlimited data, unlimited voice calls – both local and STD, and subscription to Airtel Xstream app, Zee5 Premium, Disney+ Hotstar, Wynk Music, and Amazon Prime.

Concept note-2: -Airtel ‘All-in-One’ Xstream Fiber broadband plans In terms of difference, ₹699 offers 40 Mbps speed and one OTT platform whereas, ₹1099 offers 200 Mbps speed and it comes with Amazon Prime and Disney+ Hotstar. The ₹1599 plan gives you 300 Mbps along with Disney+ Hotstar, Netflix and Amazon Prime access.

Concept note-3: -Users will get access to Airtel Xstream application, and Wynk Music, with its Rs 499 and Rs 799 broadband plans. Furthermore, the company offers a subscription to Disney+ Hotstar, Netflix, Airtel Xstream, Wynk Music, and Amazon Prime with its Rs 999, Rs 1,499, and Rs 3,999 Xstream Fiber broadband plans.

Concept note-4: -In comparison to Airtel broadband, Jio Fiber is a newer broadband connection available to Indians. Because of its low broadband connection prices, data, and speed, it has become a popular choice among users across the country.
