Name the Malayalam actor who has won the J.C. Daniel Award, recently.

(A) Geetu Mohandas

(B) Yami Gautam

(C) Sharada

(D) ** Sheela J.C

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Filmmaker K.P. Kumaran has been chosen for the prestigious J.C. Daniel Award for 2021. The award, conferred by the Kerala government annually in recognition to an individual’s contributions to Malayalam cinema, carries a purse of ₹5 lakh, a statuette, and a citation. Minister for Cultural Affairs V.N.

Concept note-2: -The award was created to commemorate the contribution of Indian filmmaker JC Daniel, who is often regarded as the father of Malayalam cinema. Since its inception, the award has been bestowed on 27 individuals. Film distributor and producer TE Vasudevan was its first recipient.

Concept note-3: -30 September 2022, 08:34 PM IST New Delhi: The 68th National Film Awards ceremony held here on Friday marked a proud moment for Keralites as singer Nanchamma, actors Aparna Balamurali and Biju Menon received the awards.

Concept note-4: -’Ayyappanum Koshiyum’ has already shined at the 68th National Film Awards as it bagged the awards for the categories best director, best-supporting actor, best playback singer, best action direction, and best stunt choreography.
