Where did 2019 Indo Pacific Chiefs of Defence (CHOD) Conference will be held?

(A) Cambodia

(B) Bhutan

(C) ** Thailand

(D) Indonesia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Indo-Pacific Chiefs of Defense (CHODs) conference is an annual event that provides a venue for Indo-Pacific Chiefs of Defence and U.S. military leaders to address emerging challenges and security matters. This year, the conference is co-hosted with Australia and to be held from 25-27 July in Sydney.

Concept note-2: -The 24th annual Indo-Pacific Chiefs of Defense (CHODs) conference will be held in Sydney from 25 to 27 July 2022. The conference will be co-hosted by Chief of the Defence Force, General Angus J. Campbell, AO, DSC and Commander U.S. Indo-Pacific Command, Admiral John C.
