Name the country which rejected the $22 million G7 aid to fight Amazon rainforest fire.

(A) Argentina

(B) ** Brazil

(C) Chile

(D) Bolivia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-2: -Industrial agriculture is the biggest driver of deforestation in the world. In Brazil, cattle ranchers and land-grabbers set the Amazon on fire to illegally clear land and expand their destructive business.

Concept note-3: -The Amazon is a vast biome that spans eight rapidly developing countries-Brazil, Bolivia, Peru, Ecuador, Colombia, Venezuela, Guyana, and Suriname-and French Guiana, an overseas territory of France.
