Which state won 2019-20 Vijay Hazare Trophy?

(A) Bihar

(B) UP

(C) Odisha

(D) ** Karnataka

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Karnataka won a rain-affected final, beating Tamil Nadu by 60 runs, with Abhimanyu Mithun taking a hat-trick.

Concept note-2: -Mumbai won the tournament, to take their fourth title, beating Uttar Pradesh by six wickets in the final.

Concept note-3: -Tamil Nadu is the most successful team having won the trophy five times. Saurashtra cricket team are the current champions, defeating Maharashtra in the (2022-23 Vijay Hazare Trophy) to win their second title.

Concept note-4: -The 2022–23 Vijay Hazare Trophy was the 21st season of the Vijay Hazare Trophy, a List A cricket tournament in India. It was played from 12 November to 2 December 2022. Himachal Pradesh were the defending champions. Saurashtra won the final match against Maharashtra by 5 wickets to clinch their second domestic title.

Concept note-5: -Karnataka has won the 2019-20 Vijay Hazare Trophy. Karnataka defeated Tamil Nadu by 9 wickets in the finals of Vijay Hazare Trophy at Bengaluru.
