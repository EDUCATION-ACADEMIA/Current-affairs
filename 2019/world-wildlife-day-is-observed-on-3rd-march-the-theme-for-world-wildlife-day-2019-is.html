World Wildlife Day is observed on 3rd March. The theme for World Wildlife Day 2019 is;

(A) It’s time to get serious about wildlife crime

(B) ** Life below water: for people and planet

(C) Listen to the young voices

(D) Big cats - predators under threat

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme for World Wildlife Day 2019 is ‘Life below Water : for People and Planet’. The CITES Secretariat and the UN Development Programme (UNDP) are working in partnership to facilitate the global celebration of World Wildlife Day 2019.

Concept note-2: -World Wildlife Day, celebrated each year on 3 March, commemorates the day of signature of the Convention on International Trade in Endangered Species of Wild Fauna and Flora (CITES) in 1973. The theme for 2022 is “Recovering key species for ecosystem restoration".

Concept note-3: -On 20 December 2013, the Sixty-eighth session of the United Nations General Assembly decided to proclaim 3 March as World Wildlife Day to celebrate and raise awareness of the world’s wild fauna and flora.

Concept note-4: -This year’s theme for World Wildlife Day 2022 is ‘Safeguarding key species for ecosystem restoration’. The reason behind this theme is to save most of the critically endangered species of wild flora and fauna.

Concept note-5: -World Wildlife Day will be celebrated in 2023 under the theme “Partnerships for wildlife conservation", honoring the people who are making a difference.
