Which country announced that the Doctors and Nurses need not take TOEFL & IELTS exam to practice?

(A) USA

(B) ** UK

(C) Canada

(D) Australia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Doctors, nurses need not take TOEFL, IELTS exam to practice in U.K.

Concept note-2: -Germany provides Nursing Jobs in Germany without IELTS. Germany offers nursing jobs to the candidates that have skills and experience to work in hospitals. It has definitive steps, rules, and procedures that every nurse has to follow to immigrate to work and live in Germany.

Concept note-3: -There are plenty of amazing countries that offer admissions without IELTS. These include the US, Canada, the UK, France, Ireland, Sweden, China, Japan, and Italy, to name a few. You can select one based on the courses and universities aligned with your goals.

Concept note-4: -Some top countries include the United States, United Kingdom, Australia, New Zealand, Canada, and Ireland. TOEFL scores are also accepted by several European educational institutions, including those in Germany and France.

Concept note-5: -But before you can become a doctor in the UK, you’ll need to pass the IELTS with an overall score of at least 7.5, and a minimum of 7 for each section. That’s why your English skills are so important, and why you should be working on them right away.
