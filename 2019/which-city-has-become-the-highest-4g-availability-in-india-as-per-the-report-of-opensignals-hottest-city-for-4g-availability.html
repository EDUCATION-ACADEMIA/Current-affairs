Which city has become the highest 4G availability in India as per the report of OpenSignal’s hottest city for 4G Availability?

(A) Ranchi

(B) ** Dhanbad

(C) Patna

(D) Srinagars

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Jump to individual city results Jaipur, meanwhile, had the largest percentage of data use over 4G (75.3%), with Hyderabad having the lowest 4G usage and the highest percentage of data transmitted over Wi-Fi (49.4%).

Concept note-2: -Vi leads in Upload Speed Experience Vi is the winner of the Upload Speed Experience award, as our Vi users observed the fastest overall upload speeds across India, averaging 4.7 Mbps - 0.5-0.7 Mbps faster than Airtel and Jio.

Concept note-3: -India’s 4G-only operator, Jio, is victorious on 4G Availability as our users on its network spent the greatest proportion of time connected to 4G services - 99.5%. Airtel is in second-place with a 4G Availability score of 98.3%, followed by Vi’s score of 92%, while BSNL lagged further behind with 63.6%.

Concept note-4: -All villages in northeastern India and border areas will be connected with 4G network, the Centre said. New Delhi: All inhabited villages in the northeastern region and border areas will be connected with 4G network by December 2023, Union minister Kiren Rijiju said today.
