The first ever India International Cooperatives Trade Fair will be held in ____

(A) Pune

(B) Mumbai

(C) ** New Delhi

(D) Hyderabad

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The first ever ‘India International Cooperatives Trade Fair’(IICTF) is going to be held at Pragati Maidan, New Delhi from 11 till 13 October, 2019.

Concept note-2: -INDIA INTERNATIONAL TRADE FAIR 2022: VENUE The location will be Pragati Maidan, New Delhi.

Concept note-3: -The inaugural Indian International Trade Fair was held on November 14, 1980, at Delhi’s Pragati Maidan. It is a significant gathering spot for manufacturers, merchants, exporters, and importers and is sponsored by the India Trade Promotion Organisation or ITPO.

Concept note-4: -The theme of the IITF 2022 is “Vocal for Local, Local to Global” and was held from November 14 – 27, 2022. It was organized by the India Trade Promotion Organization (ITPO), a division of MoCI.

Concept note-5: -The Odisha pavilion won the prestigious ‘Special Appreciation Certificate for excellence in display’ in the category of ‘State Government and Union Territory’ on Sunday at the 41st edition of India International Trade Fair(IITF), 2022.
