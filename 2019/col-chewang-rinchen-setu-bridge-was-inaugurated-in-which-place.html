Col. Chewang Rinchen Setu Bridge was inaugurated in which place?

(A) ** Ladakh

(B) Spiti

(C) Kufri

(D) Shimla

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Defence Minister Rajnath Singh inaugurated Col. Chewang Rinchen Setu (Bridge) in Ladakh. It is named in honor of Colonel Chewang Rinchan who was one of the highly decorated officers in Indian Army from Ladakh.

Concept note-2: -On 21 October 2019 Defense Minister Rajnath Singh inaugurated the Col Chewang Rinchen Setu, India’s highest altitude all-weather permanent bridge, in eastern Ladakh just 45 km from border with China.

Concept note-3: -Colonel Chewang Rinchen MVC & bar, SM was a fearless and highly decorated officer in the Indian Army. He was born in Sumur Village on November 11, 1931. He is known as the ‘Lion of Ladakh’ for his extraordinary acts of courage in defending the Leh and Partapur sector in face of attacks by adversaries.

Concept note-4: -The Bailey Bridge is the highest bridge in the world. It is located in the Ladakh valley between Dras and Suru rivers in the Himalayan mountains.

Concept note-5: -India’s highest bridge ‘Col Chewang Rinchen Bridge’ in Ladakh Details, Features, Significance. Defence Minister Rajnath Singh inaugurated the highest bridge in India named after the “Lion of Ladakh” Col Chewang Rinchen. Col Chewang Rinchen Bridge is located at 45km from India’s border from China.
