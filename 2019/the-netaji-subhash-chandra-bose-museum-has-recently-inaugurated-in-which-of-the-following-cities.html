The Netaji Subhash Chandra Bose museum has recently inaugurated in which of the following cities?

(A) Port Blair

(B) ** New Delhi

(C) Tiruchirappalli

(D) Kollam

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Notes: On 23rd January, Prime Minister Narendra Modi inaugurated the Subhash Chandra Bose museum at the iconic Red Fort in New Delhi to mark the leader’s 122nd birth anniversary.

Concept note-2: -Netaji Bhawan or Netaji Bhavan (Bengali: ; lit. ‘Netaji’s Residence’) is a heritage building in Kolkata, West Bengal, maintained as a memorial and research center to the life of the Indian nationalist “Netaji” Subhas Chandra Bose.

Concept note-3: -Remembering the heroes of freedom struggle, Prime Minister, Narendra Modi inaugurated the Subhas Chandra Bose museum at Red Fort on Wednesday, to mark his 122nd birth anniversary. He also visited this Museum on Netaji’s life and Indian National Army.

Concept note-4: -Notes: Netaji Museum is a memorial hall of Subhash Chandra Bose (Netaji). It is located in Kolkata and owned and managed by Netaji Research Bureau. This is the ancestral house of Subhash Chandra Bose where he had lived.
