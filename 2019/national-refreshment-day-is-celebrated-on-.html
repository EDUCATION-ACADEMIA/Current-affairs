National Refreshment Day is celebrated on ____

(A) Second Thursday in July

(B) Fourth Thursday in August

(C) Second Thursday in August

(D) ** Fourth Thursday in July

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -National Refreshment Day on the fourth Thursday in July celebrates fun at the hottest time of the year. The day toasts those moments of ice, cold refreshment, especially a nice cold beer.

Concept note-2: -National Refreshment Day was first commemorated in 2015 by Traveler Beers. The U.S.-based company was established in 2012. The founders wanted to integrate European shandy flavors with American ones in order to bring a refreshing touch and look to the beer scene in America.

Concept note-3: -Answer: There is no theme for celebrating National Refreshment Day in any year. As such, National Refreshment Day 2022 will also be celebrated without any theme.

Concept note-4: -In 2022 the theme is ‘I Can’t Wait’. On World Hepatitis Day, 28 July, we call on people from across the world to take action and raise awareness of hepatitis because Hepatitis Can’t Wait. WHD is a day for the world’s hepatitis community to unite and make our voices heard.

Concept note-5: -National Refreshment Day: It is observed on the fourth Thursday of July and is primarily intended to be a day of enjoyment and of course, refreshments during the hottest time of the year. Refreshment Day 2021: The day is observed on July 22.
