7th April is observed as the World Health Day. What is the theme of 2019 World Health Day?

(A) Good health adds life to years

(B) Healthy heart beat, Healthy blood pressure

(C) ** Universal Health Coverage: Everyone, Everywhere

(D) Depression: Let’s talk

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -World Health Day 2019 was celebrated worldwide on Sunday, 7th of April. World Health Day theme 2019 was – “Health for All – Everyone, Everywhere”. I agree to the terms and conditions and privacy policy.

Concept note-2: -The 2019 World Health Day theme was “Universal Health Coverage: Everyone, Everywhere", a repeat of the 2018 theme, with an emphasis on the idea that “Universal Health Coverage is the WHO’s number one goal".

Concept note-3: -In the face of the current pandemic, a polluted planet, and an increasing incidence of diseases, the theme for World Health Day 2022 is Our Planet, Our Health.

Concept note-4: -In the midst of a pandemic, a polluted planet, increasing diseases like cancer, asthma, heart disease, on World Health Day 2022, WHO will focus global attention on urgent actions needed to keep humans and the planet healthy and foster a movement to create societies focused on well-being.
