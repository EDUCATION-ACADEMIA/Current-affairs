Which state has allowed women to work in night shifts in all factories (registered under the factories act)?

(A) ** Karnataka

(B) Maharastra

(C) Odhisa

(D) Andhra Pradesh

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Women allowed to work in night shifts in all factories in Karnataka.

Concept note-2: -According to Section 14 of the Delhi Shops and Commercial Establishment Act, 1954, â€œNo woman shall be allowed or required to work whether as an employee or otherwise in any establishment between 9 p.m. and 7 a.m. during the summer season and between 8 p.m. to 8 a.m. during the winter season."

Concept note-3: -Meeting a longstanding demand for gender parity in the workforce, the Government of India approved an amendment in the Factories Act to allow women employees to work night shifts.

Concept note-4: -Under Section 66 of the Factories Act, women are barred from working in factories between 7 pm and 6 am. However, a state government can, through a notification in the official gazette, change the time limits.
