Who among the following has been conferred with the Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2019?

(A) Neeraj Chopra

(B) Hima Das

(C) Vinesh Phogat

(D) ** Bajrang Punia

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award 2019 was awarded to Bajrang Punia for wrestling and Deepa Malik for athletics.

Concept note-2: -National Sports Awards -2019 announced Bajrang Punia and Deepa Malik to get Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award.

Concept note-3: -Deepa Malik was awarded for Para Athletics. Other National Sports Awards include Dronacharya Awards, Arjuna Awards, and Dhyan Chand Award.

Concept note-4: -Shri Sharath Kamal Achanta has won the Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 in the Table Tennis discipline. The Major Dhyan Chand Khel Ratna Award 2022 ( 2022) is given by the Indian government and the Department of Sport to those sportspersons who have excelled in their sports.

Concept note-5: -The correct answer is option 4, i.e. 1, 2, 3 and 4. The recipients of Rajiv Gandhi Khel Ratna Award for the year 2016 were, P.V. Sindhu.
