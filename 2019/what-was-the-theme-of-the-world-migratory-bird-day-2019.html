What was the theme of the World Migratory Bird day, 2019?

(A) ** Protect Birds: Be the Solution to Plastic Pollution!

(B) Unifying our Voices for Bird Conservation

(C) Their Future is our Future - A Healthy Planet for Migratory Birds and People

(D) Year of the Bird

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The theme of World Migratory Bird Day 2019 is ‘Protect Birds: Be the Solution to Plastic Pollution’! The Day is celebrated on second Saturday in May and October.

Concept note-2: -Light pollution and its impact on migratory birds is the focus of World Migratory Bird Day 2022, a global campaign that aims to raise awareness of migratory birds and the need for international cooperation to conserve them. The slogan for the 2022 campaign is “Dim the Lights for Birds at Night!”

Concept note-3: -World Migratory Bird Day (WMBD) is an awareness-raising campaign highlighting the need for the conservation of migratory birds and their habitats. It aims to draw attention to the threats faced by migratory birds, their ecological importance, and the need for international cooperation to conserve them.

Concept note-4: -World Migratory Bird Day was initiated in 2006 by the Secretariat of the Agreement on the Conservation of African-Eurasian Migratory Waterbirds (AEWA) in collaboration with the Secretariat of the Convention on the Conservation of Migratory Species of Wild Animals (CMS).

Concept note-5: -Migratory birds are those that fly out to different parts of the world in the winter to escape the cold. The Siberian Crane and Flamingoes are examples of migratory birds that flock to India during the winter.
